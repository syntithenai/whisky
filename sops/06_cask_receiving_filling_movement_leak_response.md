# SOP-06: Cask Receiving, Filling, Movement, and Leak Response

## 1. Purpose
Control cask integrity, filling operations, warehouse movement, and leak response to protect yield, safety, and maturation quality.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all empty and filled casks from receipt through warehouse storage and relocation.

## 3. Roles
- Warehouse or Cask Team: inspect, fill, move, and monitor casks.
- Production or Maturation Lead: approve cask use and placement.
- Quality: evaluate leakage impact and product disposition.

## 4. Procedure
1. Cask Receiving
- Verify cask specification, supplier, previous fill history, and condition.
- Inspect for structural damage, loose hoops, contamination, and odors.
- Assign cask ID and status.

2. Pre-Fill Preparation
- Confirm cask suitability and any conditioning required by maturation standard.
- Confirm spirit identity, ABV, and fill target volume.

3. Filling
- Fill at controlled rate to target volume and legal fill strength.
- Immediately bung and verify seal.
- Mark cask with batch ID, fill date, spirit type, and fill ABV.

4. Movement and Storage
- Move with approved handling equipment only.
- Store by location code for full traceability.
- Keep stack pattern and aisle clearances per warehouse safety rules.

5. Leak Monitoring and Response
- Inspect filled casks at defined intervals and after movement.
- If leak identified:
- Isolate cask location and remove ignition sources.
- Estimate leak severity and recover product where feasible.
- Re-bung or transfer spirit to approved vessel if needed.
- Raise incident and quality assessment records.

6. Disposition
- Determine product usability and cask reusability.
- Implement corrective actions (training, handling changes, cask supplier feedback).

## 5. Small vs Large Operation Notes
- Small distillery: often more manual movement; enforce two-person handling for high-risk moves.
- Larger operation: warehouse management systems, forklift zoning, and formal leak trend KPIs.

## 6. Records
- Cask receipt and inspection logs.
- Fill records and traceability mapping.
- Warehouse location and movement logs.
- Leak event, loss estimate, and corrective action report.

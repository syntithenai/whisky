## Key Facts & Q&A
- Expect 25-30 700ml bottles at maturation
- Ageing period is 2 years due to high wood-to-spirit ratio and temperature fluctuations in Australia
- "Angels share" loss averages around 10% per annum
- Experience tells us optimum age for malt spirit in 20 litre oak casks is 2-3 years
- Cask sizes vary; smaller casks mature quicker
- No shipping of spirits in casks due to licensing and leakage risks
- Two-year statutory period before whisky can be called "whisky"
- Visit and taste your maturing cask with prior arrangement
- Bottle choice: 750ml or 500ml depending on stock availability
- Shipping costs quoted at maturation based on location

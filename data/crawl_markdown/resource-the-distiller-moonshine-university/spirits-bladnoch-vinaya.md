# Bladnoch Vinaya

- **Type**: NAS single‑malt whisky (Lowlands, Scotland)
- **ABV**: 46.7 %
- **Release**: November 2021, part of the Classic Collection for the U.S.
- **Cask finish**: first‑fill ex‑bourbon and first‑fill ex‑sherry casks
- **Name origin**: “Vinaya” (Sanskrit for respect/gratitude)
- **Tasting notes**: fruity (golden apples, pears), honey, shortbread, vanilla, marzipan, meadow flowers; bright, long sweet finish with earthy undertones.
- **Score**: 92 (Amanda Schuster)

> *“The aroma of this whisky is like opening a fancy fruit basket… The overall effect is very bright, with a long, sweet and slightly earthy finish and creamy, rich texture.”*

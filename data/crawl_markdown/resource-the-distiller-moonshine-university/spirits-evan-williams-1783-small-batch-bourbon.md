# Evan Williams 1783 Small‑Batch Bourbon

- **Producer**: Heaven Hill Distillery, Kentucky, USA
- **Mash bill**: 78 % corn, 12 % malted barley, 10 % rye
- **Age**: 6–8 years in new charred American oak barrels
- **Proof**: 90 (45 % ABV)
- **Flavor profile**: sweet, full‑bodied with notes of oatmeal raisin cookie, molasses, brown sugar, cinnamon, clove, nutmeg; slightly woody and hot finish.
- **Price**: around \$20 per bottle (TruePrice average).

The bourbon is named after Evan Williams, the founder of Kentucky’s first distillery. It is produced from fewer than 200 barrels each batch.

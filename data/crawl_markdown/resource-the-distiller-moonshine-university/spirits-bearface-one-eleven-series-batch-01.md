# BEARFACE One Eleven Series: Oaxaca Edition

A Canadian whisky that blends a minimum of two‑year‑old mezcal from Maestro Mescalero Pedro Hernánandez with a four‑year‑old single grain Canadian whisky. The spirit is aged in American ex‑bourbon casks and finished in virgin French oak, delivering smoky, citrusy, and sweet dried‑fruit notes.

- **ABV**: 42.5% 
- **Age statement**: at least three years
- **Cask type**: American ex‑bourbon × Virgin French oak
- **Tasting note (Blair Phillips)**: "A firm Canadian whisky backbone is accented with agave syrup and traces of smoky bacon…rich in texture, the smoky tones blend with a burst of citrus zest."

The product is available in British Columbia, Alberta and Ontario.

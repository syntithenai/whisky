# Domaine du Manoir de Montreuil Réserve

**Domaine du Manoir de Montreuil Réserve** is a Calvados from the Pays d'Auge, France. It is a collaboration between importer Charles Neal and producer Patrice Giard, using older apple varieties from Giard’s orchards. The spirit is distilled twice in a traditional alembic still and aged for at least three years in French oak barrels. At 42% ABV it offers fresh apple character with notes of French vanilla and clover honey.

- **ABV**: 42%
- **Age**: ≥ 3 years (Réserve)
- **Cask type**: French oak
- **Flavor profile**: Fruity & sweet, apple‑centric with vanilla and honey finish
- **Community rating**: 4.18/5 from 9 reviews

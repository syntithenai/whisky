# Dry Fly Straight Washington Wheat Whiskey
Dry Fly is a wheat‑based whiskey from Spokane, Washington, created by Don Poffenroth and Kent Fleischmann. It uses locally sourced wheat, double‑distilled, and aged for three years in new American oak barrels charred at level #3.

**Key facts**
- 51%+ wheat mash bill
- 40 % ABV
- 3‑year age statement
- New, charred American oak casks (#3 char)
- Community rating: 3.42/5 (149 reviews)

**Tasting notes** – “toasted wheat bread lightly buttered, honey, cinnamon, peppery spice; fiery finish tamed by water or ice.”

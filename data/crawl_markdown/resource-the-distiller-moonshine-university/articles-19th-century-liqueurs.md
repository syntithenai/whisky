# Essential 19th Century Liqueurs Home Bars Still Need
This article highlights five historic liqueurs that remain popular today:
- **Luxardo Maraschino** – Italian cherry‑based liqueur, 1821.
- **Yellow Chartreuse** – French herbal liqueur, 1838.
- **Averna Amaro** – Sicilian bittersweet amaro, 1868.
- **Gabriel Boudier Crème de Cassis de Dijon** – Blackcurrant liqueur, 1874.
- **Bénédictine** – French herbal liqueur, revived 1863.
Each section explains origin, production method and cocktail uses.

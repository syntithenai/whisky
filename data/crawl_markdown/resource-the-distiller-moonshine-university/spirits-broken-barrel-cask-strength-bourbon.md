# Broken Barrel Cask Strength Bourbon

- **Distillery**: Owensboro Distilling Co., Kentucky, USA.
- **Mash bill**: 70 % corn, 21 % rye, 9 % malted barley.
- **Initial aging**: at least 2 years in new charred American oak barrels.
- **Secondary aging**: re‑bottled into a tank with 40 % former bourbon staves, 40 % new French oak staves and 20 % sherry cask staves.
- **Proof & ABV**: bottled at 115 proof (57.5 % ABV).
- **Release date**: late January 2022.
- **Tasting notes**: candied orange peel, vanilla Tootsie Rolls, cranberries, straw, chrysanthemum bud; honeyed palate with trail‑mix of salted nuts, dried fruit and chocolate chips; spicy finish with cloves, black pepper, musty oak, tannic wood shavings, menthol and lemon peel.
- **Flavor profile**: full‑bodied & spicy.

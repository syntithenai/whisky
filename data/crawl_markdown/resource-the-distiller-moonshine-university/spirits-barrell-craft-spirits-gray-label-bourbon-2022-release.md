# Barrell Craft Spirits Gray Label Bourbon (2022 Release)

- **Type**: Bourbon, cask‑strength 100.58 proof (50.29 % ABV). 
- **Blend**: Straight bourbons distilled and aged in Tennessee, Kentucky and Indiana using five different mash bills and ages.
- **Cask**: 36‑month air‑dried staves that previously held Gray Label Bourbon; finished in new charred American oak barrels.
- **Release**: September 2022. 
- **Price**: Suggested retail price $249.99 (SRP). 
- **Tasting notes**: Caramel popcorn, candied apple, spiced cider, orange‑pinecone aroma; tart plum and golden apple on the palate with cinnamon, clove, nutmeg, black pepper finish.

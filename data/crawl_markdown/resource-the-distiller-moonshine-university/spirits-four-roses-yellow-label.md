# Four Roses Yellow Label Bourbon
Four Roses Yellow Label is the brand’s entry‑level straight bourbon from Kentucky. It blends two distinct mash bills—Recipe B (60 % corn, 35 % rye, 5 % malted barley) and Recipe E (75 % corn, 20 % rye, 5 % malted barley)—and employs up to five yeast strains. The bottle’s label has evolved over time; since spring 2018 it is simply marketed as *Four Roses Bourbon*.

**Key facts**
- ABV: 40 %
- Cask type: new, charred American oak
- Flavor profile: sweet honey‑vanilla with rye spice, corn sweetness, brown‑sugar finish and oak undertones.

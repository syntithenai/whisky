# Cognac Dudognon Vieille Réserve

- **Distillery**: Maison Dudognon, Grande Champagne, France.

- **ABV**: 43.0 %.

## Distillery Facts
- Maison Dudognon has operated since 1776 as grower/negociant.
- Post‑1970 the distillery stopped supplying to major cognac houses.
- Known for old Ugni Blanc vines and small alembic stills.
- Uses meticulous French barrel selection.

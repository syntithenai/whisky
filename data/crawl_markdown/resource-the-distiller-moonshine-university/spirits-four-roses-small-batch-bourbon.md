# Four Roses Small Batch Bourbon
Four Roses Small Batch is a Kentucky straight bourbon produced by blending four of the distillery’s ten proprietary recipes. It uses two mash bills in equal parts: one 60 % corn, 35 % rye, 5 % malted barley and another 75 % corn, 20 % rye, 5 % malted barley. Yeast strains are selected to balance light spice, richness, and fruit‑forward character. The spirit is bottled at 45 % ABV.

**Tasting notes** – toffee, cinnamon, nutmeg, pit fruits, oak; velvety mouthfeel with raspberries, honey, cinnamon sugar; finish of honey, cinnamon, vanilla.

**Flavor profile** – fruity & sweet.

**Key facts** – 45 % ABV, new charred American oak cask, Kentucky straight bourbon.

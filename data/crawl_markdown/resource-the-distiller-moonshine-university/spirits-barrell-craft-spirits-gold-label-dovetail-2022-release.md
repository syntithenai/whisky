# Barrell Craft Spirits Gold Label Dovetail (2022 Release)

- **Type**: Rare, cask‑strength whiskey (140 ABV) from the Gold Label limited edition series.
- **Blend**: Whiskeys distilled in Indiana, Tennessee and Canada finished in rum, port and Dunn Vineyards Cabernet Sauvignon barrels.
- **Age**: Up to 25 years old barrels used.
- **Price**: Suggested retail price $499.99 (select retailers, November 2022).
- **Tasting notes**: Fruity aromas of tart apple, dried cherry, grapefruit peel; cinnamon and clove; palate rich with roasted almond, toffee, caramel, sweet spice; slightly hot finish balanced by fruit, spice, toffee and a hint of salt.

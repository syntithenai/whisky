# J.P. Wiser's Seven Rebels

- **Type**: Canadian whisky (rare cask series)
- **Distillery**: J.P. Wiser’s, Ontario, Canada
- **Aging**: Minimum 3 years in ex‑bourbon, virgin oak and Speyside malt casks; some barrels finished with charred oak inserts for smokiness.
- **Mash bill**: Corn, rye, wheat, rye malt, barley malt.
- **ABV**: 42.8 %
- **Availability**: Exclusive to British Columbia
- **Tasting notes**: Cinnamon, cloves, ginger; stone fruit, vanilla, icing sugar, toasted oak; fresh‑cut lumber, bourbon‑like fruits, seared peppers, grassy rye twang, smoked lumber, dried cherries.
- **Score**: 91 (Blair Phillips)
- **Price**: Not listed on the page

> *“This Canadian blend charges right out of the gate with a solid nose rich with cinnamon, cloves and ginger balanced with stone fruits, vanilla, icing sugar and toasted oak. Fresh‑cut lumber on the palate blends fluently with bourbon‑like fruits, seared peppers and a grassy rye twang — that rye flash parts way for a tower of smoked lumber and dried cherries.”* – Blair Phillips

# Green River Bourbon
Green River Bourbon is a Kentucky‑style bourbon released in February 2022 after more than a century of inactivity. It uses a mash bill of **70 % corn, 21 % winter rye and 9 % malted barley**, is aged over five years in new charred American oak barrels, and bottled at 90 proof (45 % ABV). The spirit has been priced at **$34.99** retail.

Tasting notes describe a moderately intense aroma of vanilla and char smoke with hints of apples and black cherries. On the palate it is tangy, savory‑sweet with a touch of leather; the finish is slightly oily, bittersweet, and carries moderate barrel spices. The overall profile is fruity with some spice and leather undertones.

The brand was originally founded in 1885, closed after a 1918 fire and Prohibition, and has now returned to production at its distillery in Kentucky.

# Forty Creek Victory

Forty Creek Victory is a limited‑edition Canadian whisky released in 2019 to commemorate the War of 1812 battle *Engagement at the Forty*. The blend combines corn and rye whiskies, with half of the batch receiving a two‑month top dressing using toasted vanilla wood staves. A small amount of the distillery’s 1999 Villard Noir Port is added for balance. It is aged in barrels for at least three years and is available across Canada and some U.S. states.

**Tasting notes**: Orchard fruit, sweet banana custard, tart apple, vanilla spices, toasted oak, dark plums, dry rye spices, chocolate powder, citrus rinds finish.

# J.P. Wiser's 10 Year Triple Barrel

- **Origin**: Canadian whisky from Corby Spirit and Wine Limited, Ontario.
- **Age & cask**: Minimum 10‑12 years in used Canadian, ex‑bourbon, and new white oak barrels.
- **Blend**: 10–12 % rye with a base whisky aged at least ten years.
- **ABV**: 40.0 %
- **Availability**: Currently exclusive to Germany.
- **Tasting notes** (Blair Phillips, score 88): Almond, caramel‑toffee, orange, dusty oak, vanilla, rye spice, peppery finish with J.P. Wiser’s signature orange pith.

> *“Almond, caramel toffee, orange, and dusty oak lead the charge… a peppery finish that’s autographed with J.P. Wiser’s signature orange pith.”*

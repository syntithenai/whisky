# Don Julio Reposado Tequila
- **Origin**: Los Altos, Jalisco, Mexico
- **Agave**: 100 % blue Weber agave from the Highlands of Jalisco
- **Production**:
  - Harvested and steam‑cooked for 72 h
  - Juice fermented and double‑distilled in stainless steel pot‑stills
  - Aged 8 months in American white oak barrels (≤20,000 L)
- **ABV / Proof**: 40.0 % / 80 proof
- **Bottle**: Square‑shaped, 750 ml
- **Tasting notes**: Less citrus, more cooked agave, mild oak bitterness, peppery finish; best as a mixer or with Topo Chico.
- **Flavor profile**: Fruity & spicy
- **Community rating**: 3.89/5 (302 reviews)

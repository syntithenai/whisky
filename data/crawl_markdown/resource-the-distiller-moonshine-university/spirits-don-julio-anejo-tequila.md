# Don Julio Añejo Tequila

Don Julio Añejo is a 40% ABV tequila aged for **18 months** in American oak barrels sourced from the Highlands of Jalisco, Mexico. The spirit is distilled from 100 % blue Weber agave and carries notes of vanilla, caramel, spice, cooked agave, honey, agave nectar, and pepper.

- **Distillery:** Don Julio (Los Altos, Jalisco)
- **Founded:** 1942 by Don Julio González Estrada
- **Aging:** 18 months in American oak barrels (≤600 L)
- **Community rating:** 4.21/5 from 507 reviews

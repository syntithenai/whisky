# Four Roses Bourbon
Four Roses Bourbon is the entry‑level Kentucky Straight Bourbon from the Four Roses Distillery in Kentucky, USA. It blends two proprietary mash bills—60 % corn/35 % rye/5 % malted barley and 75 % corn/20 % rye/5 % malted barley—using all five of the distillery’s yeast strains. Formerly sold as *Yellow Label*, it was renamed in spring 2018.

**Key tasting notes**: sweet honey, vanilla, a touch of spice; palate with sweet corn and rye spice; finish longer than expected with brown sugar and oak.

The spirit is aged 40 % ABV in new, charred American oak barrels.

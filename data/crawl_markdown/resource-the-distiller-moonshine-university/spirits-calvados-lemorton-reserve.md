# Calvados Lemorton Reserve

**Calvados Lemorton Reserve** is a 40 % ABV brandy from the Lemorton estate in Domfrontais, France. It showcases a dominant pear character with subtle apple undertones, complemented by caramel and white‑pepper spice. The spirit is aged for at least three years in French oak barrels, meeting the reserve classification.

> *“At first whiff, this Calvados resonates deeply of pear and less‑so of apple… The orchard flavors are joined by a delicious medley of caramel and white pepper spice.”* – John Rankin (Score 86)

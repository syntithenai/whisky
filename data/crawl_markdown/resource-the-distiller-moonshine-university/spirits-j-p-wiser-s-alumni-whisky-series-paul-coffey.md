# J.P. Wiser's Alumni Whisky Series – Paul Coffey

- **Origin**: Canada (Ontario) – blended Canadian whisky.
- **ABV**: 48% (to echo Coffey’s record‑breaking 48‑goal season).
- **Age**: 7 years, honoring his #77 jersey.
- **Cask types**: Virgin oak, ex‑Speyside malt, ex‑bourbon, ex‑Canadian rye.
- **Flavor profile**: Corn grain, caramel, vanilla, maple sugar; light char and baking spices on the palate with a maple‑syrup finish.
- **Philanthropy**: Proceeds support the NHL Alumni Association.
- **Availability**: Exclusive to Canada.

> *“A high‑octane wake‑up call of corn grain, caramel, vanilla and maple sugar… Late palate maple syrup‑soaked corn bread and vanilla are heated by rye baking spices on the finish.”* – Blair Phillips (score 87)

# Berentzen Bushel & Barrel
Berentzen Bushel & Barrel is a flavored whiskey from the historic German spirits maker **Berentzen**. It blends Kentucky straight bourbon (distilled and aged to specification) with apple liqueur, delivering a sweet‑fruity profile featuring fresh red apple, candy apple, baked apple, cinnamon, vanilla, and a hint of oak.

- **ABV:** 30%
- **Flavor Profile:** Sweet & Fruity
- **Reviews:** 6 community reviews, average rating 3.27/5.

> *“There's a huge hit of fresh red apple as soon as the top is popped; it's followed by a little candy apple and a soft heat appropriate to the relatively low alcohol content. Baked apple dusted with cinnamon and splashed with vanilla emerges next. A hint of oak lingers into the sweet finish.”* – Ron Bechtol

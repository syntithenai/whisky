# Bulleit Bourbon

Bulleit Bourbon is a Kentucky‑made bourbon with a high‑rye mash bill (68 % corn, 28 % rye, 4 % barley) bottled at 90 proof. The brand was revived by Tom Bulleit in the late 1980s and acquired by Diageo in 2001.

**Tasting notes** – Oak‑heavy nose with subtle corn, vanilla and cinnamon; spicy rye palate with burnt sugar, tobacco and a medium finish that brings back the cinnamon and rye.

**Key facts** – 45 % ABV, new charred American oak casks, 8,665 community reviews (average 3.48/5).

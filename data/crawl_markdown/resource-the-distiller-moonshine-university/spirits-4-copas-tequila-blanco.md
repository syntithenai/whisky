# 4 Copas Tequila Blanco

- **Origin**: Jalisco, Mexico
- **Founder**: Héctor Galindo Miranda
- **Agave**: 100 % organic blue Weber agave + organic agave yeast
- **Production**: Slow‑cooked in traditional brick ovens; distilled unaged (clear) and bottled straight after distillation. May rest in stainless steel up to 60 days or age in wood for ≤30 days.
- **ABV**: 40 %
- **Price**: $49 (SRP)
- **Community rating**: 4.38/5 from 45 reviews
- **Tasting notes**: Sun‑baked earth, dried herbs, grilled nectarines, olive brine, crisp citrus; almond flower, white pepper, cinnamon on the palate.
- **Flavor profile**: Rich & fruity.

> *“A massively aromatic tequila: sun-baked earth, dried herbs, grilled nectarines, olive brine, crisp citrus. On the palate the touch is delicate but full-bodied, with notes of almond flower, white pepper and cinnamon joining in.”* – Jack Robertiello (Score 98)

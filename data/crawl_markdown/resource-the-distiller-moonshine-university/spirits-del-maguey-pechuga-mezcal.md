# Del Maguey Pechuga Mezcal
Del Maguey’s Pechuga mezcal is a small‑batch, unaged spirit produced in Oaxaca, Mexico. The process begins with the finished Minero mezcal, to which wild mountain apples, plums, plantains, pineapples, almonds and white rice are added before returning it to the pot still. A raw, skinless chicken breast hangs above the still so the distillate passes through its flesh, imparting a unique flavor profile. The final product is bottled at strength (49 % ABV) without dilution.

**Tasting notes** – Anise‑licorice‑almond aroma with fig jam and cinnamon candle; sweet, peppery, clove‑star‑anise cedar undertones; smooth yet complex, smoke subdued by sweetness.

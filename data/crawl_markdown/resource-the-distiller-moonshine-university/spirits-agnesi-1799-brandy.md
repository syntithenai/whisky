# Agnesi 1799 Brandy
Agnesi 1799 is an American brandy produced by the Coppola family in California. It is aged five years, then diluted to 80 proof with Napa Valley water from the Inglenook estate. The spirit is named after Maria Gaetana Agnesi (died 1799). Tasting notes describe a grassy meadow aroma with honey and dried fruit, followed by a palate of plums, redcurrants, lingonberries, leather, dark chocolate, and a sweet agave‑syrup finish.

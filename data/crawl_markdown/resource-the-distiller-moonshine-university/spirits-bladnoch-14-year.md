# Bladnoch 14 Year (2021 Release)

A 14‑year‑old Lowland single malt from Bladnoch, Scotland, finished in ex‑Oloroso sherry casks and bottled at 46.7 % ABV. The expression is part of the Classic Collection series released to the U.S. market in November 2021.

**Tasting notes (Amanda Schuster)**
- Nose: honey, pears, apples, marzipan
- Palate: cooked grain, brown bread, dried apricot, pineapple, caramel
- Finish: Christmas pudding‑like with rich toffee and baking spices

Score: 89/100.

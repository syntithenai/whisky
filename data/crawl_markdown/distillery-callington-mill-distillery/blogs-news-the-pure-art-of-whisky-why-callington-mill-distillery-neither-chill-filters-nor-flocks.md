# The Pure Art of Whisky: Why Callington Mill Distillery Neither Chill Filters Nor Flocks
Callington Mill Distillery explains that it deliberately avoids chill‑filtration and flocculation to preserve the natural flavour, mouthfeel and authenticity of its single‑malt whisky. The article outlines how these processes remove fatty acids, esters and proteins that contribute to cloudiness but also to richness and texture. By keeping the spirit unfiltered, the distillery offers a slightly hazy product when cold or diluted, which it views as evidence of purity.

# A Union of Two Fine Arts Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

- **Exclusive release**: available only at Callington Mill Distillery cellar doors in Hobart and Oatlands.
- **Size & price**: 700 ml bottle, $149.00 AUD.
- **ABV**: 40%.
- **Cask**: Matured for 6.5 years in a blend of sherry, port, Moscatel and brandy casks.
- **Tasting notes**:
  - *Colour*: deep amber with rich copper.
  - *Nose*: marzipan, fruit cake, sweet oak, apricot stone nuttiness, almond, dried fruit.
  - *Palate*: oily mouthfeel, dried berry sweetness, green almonds, orchard fruits, integrated oak.
  - *Finish*: medium length, warming spice, lingering oak, cherry, faint tobacco.
- **Production philosophy**: whisky and cuisine as parallel fine arts, crafted with Tasmanian ingredients, fire, precision and patience.

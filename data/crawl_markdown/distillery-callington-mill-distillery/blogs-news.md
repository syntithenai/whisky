# Callington Mill Distillery News

- **Solera System** – CMD uses a 14‑cask solera blending process, drawing from exotic fortified wine and spirit casks (Cognac, Bourbon, Sherry Cream, Madeira, etc.). The youngest spirit defines the age statement.
- **Tasmanian Whisky History** – From early prohibition in 1839 to Bill Lark’s 1992 legal distillery, the site traces key milestones and figures such as John Ibrahim and Damian Mackey.
- **Cooperage & Oak Philosophy** – CMD owns a cooperage (30‑L casks) and sources premium ex‑fortified wine oak from Spain/Portugal, emphasizing “Oak Matters Most.”
- **No Chill‑Filter / No Flocculation** – The distillery deliberately avoids these processes to preserve natural flavour compounds.
- **Summer Cocktails & Product Highlights** – Articles promote whisky cocktails for summer and highlight limited releases (e.g., Solera gift pack).

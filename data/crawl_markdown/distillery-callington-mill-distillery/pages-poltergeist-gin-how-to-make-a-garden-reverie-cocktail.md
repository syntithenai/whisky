# Poltergeist Gin – Garden Reverie Cocktail

A simple, light cocktail recipe featuring **Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin**. Mix 45 ml gin, 22.5 ml fresh lemon juice and 15 ml elderflower syrup over ice in a high‑ball glass, top with soda and garnish with a slice of lemon.

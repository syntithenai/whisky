# Hobart Whisky Lounge & Dining Room at MACq 01

Callington Mill Distillery’s waterfront venue in Hobart offers guided whisky and gin tastings, cocktails, a Serendipity experience (create your own cask‑strength single malt), and a menu featuring local Tasmanian produce. The lounge is open Wednesday–Sunday 2:00 pm–10:00 pm (weekends extend to 11:30 pm) and charges a 10 % surcharge on weekends/public holidays.

- **Location**: 18 Hunter St, Hobart, Tasmania, Australia
- **Contact**: +61 423 134 906 / hobart@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au
- **Licence**: 81937
- **Tasting price**: $30 for four 15 ml samples of Callington Mill single‑malt whiskies
- **Serendipity experience**: $295, includes a 700 ml bottle of your own cask‑strength whisky
- **Menu options**: A la Carte ($88), Sharing ($120), Dessert, Beverage
- **Awards**: Best Unique Bar Experience 2025 Tasmanian Hospitality Awards

The lounge also sells Callington Mill products such as El Sol (sale $149) and Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin (sale $89.95).

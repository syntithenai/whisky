# Refreshing Whisky Cocktails for Summer
Callington Mill Distillery shares three easy‑to‑make whisky cocktails perfect for warm weather: **The Rattlesnake**, a balanced orange‑lemon mix using Symmetry; **Whisky Sour** with citrus and honey notes from Apera Fusion; and **Strawberry Whisky Lemonade** featuring Emulsion Single Malt. The post encourages experimenting with the Leap of Faith series and offers tips on dilution, ice size, and seasonal enjoyment.

- **The Rattlesnake** – 4 servings, uses egg white, simple syrup, Symmetry whisky, lemon juice, bitters.
- **Whisky Sour** – single serving, Apera Fusion whisky, lemon juice, simple syrup, garnishes.
- **Strawberry Whisky Lemonade** – strawberry syrup, Emulsion whisky, lemon juice, fresh fruit garnish.

The article also highlights the Leap of Faith series as a refreshing option that can be enjoyed neat or mixed.

# Refreshing Whisky Cocktails for Summer
Callington Mill Distillery shares three easy‑to‑make whisky cocktails ideal for warm weather: **The Rattlesnake**, a balanced fruity mix using Symmetry; **Whisky Sour** with Apera Fusion, citrusy and honey‑sweet; and **Strawberry Whisky Lemonade** featuring Emulsion. Each recipe includes ingredients, steps, and garnish suggestions. The post also promotes the Leap of Faith double/triple‑distilled series as a refreshing single‑malt option.

## Key Takeaways
- Whisky isn’t just for winter – it can be chilled or mixed into cocktails.
- Use large ice cubes to keep drinks cool without diluting too quickly.
- Callington Mill’s own whiskies (Symmetry, Apera Fusion, Emulsion) are highlighted in the recipes.
- The Leap of Faith series is recommended for those who prefer sipping whisky straight.

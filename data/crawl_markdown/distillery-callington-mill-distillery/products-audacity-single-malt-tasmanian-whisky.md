# Callington Mill Distillery – Audacity Single Malt Tasmanian Whisky

- **Product**: Audacity, a deep‑amber single malt from the Leap of Faith series.
- **Maturation**: Started in 20 L Australian Tawny and Apera casks, finished in 300 L white fortified Rutherglen casks.
- **ABV**: 65.8 % (700 ml bottle).
- **Price**: AUD 195.00 (sold out).
- **Awards**: Double Gold 2022 San Francisco World Spirits Competition; Bronze 2022 IWSC.
- **Tasting notes**: Vanilla custard, cocoa, tobacco, coffee‑nose developing into caramel and toffee with rich fudge finish.

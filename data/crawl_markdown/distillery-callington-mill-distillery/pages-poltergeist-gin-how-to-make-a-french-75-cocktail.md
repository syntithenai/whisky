# Poltergeist Gin – French 75 Cocktail

A quick recipe for a sparkling French 75 using Callington Mill Distillery’s **Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin**.

- **Ingredients**: 30 ml gin, 15 ml fresh lemon juice, 15 ml simple syrup, ~120 ml sparkling wine.
- **Glassware**: Flute.
- **Method**: Shake gin, lemon juice and syrup with ice; double‑strain into a flute (no ice); top with sparkling wine; garnish with a lemon twist.

# Callington Mill Distillery

Callington Mill Distillery is a Tasmanian single‑malt whisky producer based in Oatlands, Tasmania. The site offers its own range of whiskies—including the Seven Year Anniversary release, Pedro Ximenez, Enigma, Solera and Oloroso—as well as Poltergeist gin and merchandise. Visitors can tour the historic windmill village, attend whisky events at the Oatlands Distillery & Cellar Door or the Hobart Whisky Lounge at MACq 01, and book guided tastings or retreats at Blue Lake Lodge.

Key facts:
- **Location**: 6 Mill Lane, Oatlands, Tasmania (Distillery) and 18 Hunter St, Hobart (Whisky Lounge)
- **Products**: Tasmanian single‑malt whiskies, Poltergeist gin, gift packs, merchandise
- **Events**: Monthly whisky events, guided tours, Blue Lake Lodge retreats
- **Heritage**: Restored heritage village and windmill
- **Contact**: +61 482 509 019 / info@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au

The distillery emphasizes sustainable practices and respects the traditional custodians of the land.

# Callington Mill Distillery

Callington Mill Distillery is a Tasmanian single‑malt whisky producer located in Oatlands (6 Mill Lane) with a visitor centre and a tasting lounge at Hobart’s MACq 01 Hotel. The distillery offers a range of single‑malt whiskies – including the 7‑Year Anniversary bottling, Pedro Ximenez, Enigma, Solera, and Oloroso – each priced in AUD (e.g., Pedro Ximenez $149, Enigma $99). It also sells Poltergeist gin, gift packs, merchandise, and hosts monthly whisky events. The site promotes sustainability, heritage preservation of the Callington Mill village, and offers a wholesale ordering channel.

---

## Key facts
- **Location**: 6 Mill Lane, Oatlands, Tasmania (distillery & cellar door); 18 Hunter St, Hobart (MACq 01 lounge).
- **Products**: Single‑malt whiskies (7‑Year Anniversary, Pedro Ximenez, Enigma, Solera, Oloroso) and Poltergeist gin.
- **Prices**: Pedro Ximenez $149, Enigma $99, Solera $159, Oloroso $149.
- **Events**: Monthly whisky tastings, guided tours, special occasions (Mother’s Day, World Whisky Day, Dark Night Experience).
- **Sustainability & heritage**: Restored historic windmill village; acknowledges traditional custodians.
- **Wholesale**: Orders via orders@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au.

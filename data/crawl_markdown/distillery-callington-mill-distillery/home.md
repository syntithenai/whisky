# Callington Mill Distillery

Callington Mill Distillery is a Tasmanian single‑malt whisky producer based in Oatlands and Hobart. The site offers its flagship **Seven Year Anniversary Release** along with other bottlings such as *Pedro Ximenez*, *Enigma*, *Solera* and *Oloroso*. Visitors can explore the heritage windmill village, book tastings at the Oatlands cellar door or the Hobart Whisky Lounge & Dining Room at MACq 01, and participate in monthly whisky events. The distillery also produces Poltergeist Gin and sells merchandise.

Key highlights:
- **Location**: 6 Mill Lane, Oatlands; 18 Hunter St, Hobart (MACq 01)
- **Products**: Tasmanian single‑malt whiskies (7‑year anniversary, Pedro Ximenez, Enigma, Solera, Oloroso) and Poltergeist Gin
- **Events**: Monthly whisky tastings, guided tours, special celebrations such as Mother’s Day and World Whisky Day
- **Heritage**: Restored windmill village with interactive displays
- **Contact**: info@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au | +61 482 509 019

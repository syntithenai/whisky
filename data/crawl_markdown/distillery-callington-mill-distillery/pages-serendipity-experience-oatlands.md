# Serendipity Experience (Oatlands)

The **Serendipity Experience** at Callington Mill Distillery’s Oatlands location is a guided whisky‑making workshop where guests create their own single‑malt whisky. Participants taste five premium cask‑strength whiskies, learn about the craft from an expert, and leave with a unique flavour profile that they have helped design.

Key details:
- **Location:** 6 Mill Lane, Oatlands, Tasmania, Australia
- **Duration:** ~1.5 hours (extendable)
- **Price:** Not listed on the page (contact required)
- **Cancellation policy:** 10 % fee if cancelled within 24 h; no refunds for last‑minute cancellations or no‑shows.
- **Related products shown:** Quagmire Peated, El Sol, Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin with prices in AUD.

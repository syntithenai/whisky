# Guided Distillery Tour and Tasting

Callington Mill Distillery offers a daily guided tour at **12 pm** for **$150 per person**. The experience covers milling, mashing, fermenting, distillation, blending, aroma‑kit training, and an exclusive tasting of four 15 ml pours from their single‑malt range. Tours last about 90 minutes.

Key points:
- Fully automated, integrated turnkey distillery (the founder calls it the *Rolls‑Royce* of whisky).
- Guided tasting includes raw material nose‑tests and a cask‑drawn sample.
- Cancellation allowed up to 24 h before booking with a 10 % fee; no refunds for last‑minute cancellations or no‑shows.

The page also lists product prices for Quagmire Peated, El Sol, and Poltergeist Barrel‑aged Gin.

## Long Description
# Callington Mill Distillery
A boutique distillery in Oatlands, Tasmania that produces award‑winning single‑malts aged in a variety of casks (cream sherry, brandy, Palo Cortado, Madeira, etc.). The site also sells branded glassware and apparel.

## Whisky range
- **Aurora** – 46 % ABV, 700 ml, ~4½ yrs in Cream Sherry → Barrique Brandy; Gold 2025 SFWSC (91 pts). 
- **Audacity** – 65.8 % ABV, 700 ml, Tawny & Apera → Rutherglen white‑fortified; Double Gold 2022 SFWSC.
- **Apera Fusion** – 46 % ABV, 700 ml, 20 L Apera → 300/500 L Apera; Silver 2022 SFWSC.
- **Christmas Cask 2023** – 46 % ABV, 700 ml, Cream Sherry + brandy finish; limited release.
- **Christmas Exclusive 2024** – 46 % ABV, 700 ml, Oloroso → Pedro Ximénez → Moscatel sherry casks; sold out.
- **Bulldogs series** (The Entertainers, Dogs of War, Anniversary) – 63.4–43 % ABV, 700 ml, limited editions (90 bottles each), various finishes (Palo Cortado, Madeira, Tasmanian highlands).

## Operations & branding
The distillery is owned by John Ibrahim and Bill Lark. Products are sold via a Shopify store with free shipping over $195 AUD; age‑gate is enforced. The site features detailed tasting notes, cask histories, and award listings for each whisky.

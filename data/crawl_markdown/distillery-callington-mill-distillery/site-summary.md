## Long Description
# Callington Mill Distillery

Callington Mill Distillery (CMD) is located in Oatlands, Tasmania, at 6 Mill Lane. The site also operates a tasting lounge at Hobart’s MACq 01 Hotel and hosts monthly whisky events. CMD produces a range of single‑malt whiskies – including the 7‑Year Anniversary bottling, Pedro Ximenez, Enigma, Solera and Oloroso – each priced in AUD (e.g., Pedro Ximenez $149, Enigma $99). The distillery also sells Poltergeist gin, gift packs and merchandise.

## Production & Philosophy
- **Cooperage**: CMD owns its own cooperage (established 2016) that crafts 30‑litre oak casks. It sources premium American, European and French oak previously used for fortified wines such as Port, Sherry and Cognac. Ex‑fortified wine casks are about ten times more expensive than ex‑bourbon casks but deliver the complex flavour profile that defines CMD’s whiskies.
- **Oak & Maturation**: Oak contributes 70–80 % of the whisky’s flavours and 100 % of its colour, filtering sulphur compounds during ageing. The distillery uses small cask sizes to accelerate maturation, achieving a 3‑4 year finish.
- **Solera System**: CMD employs a 14‑cask solera blending process that draws from exotic fortified wine and spirit casks (Cognac, Bourbon, Sherry Cream, Madeira). The youngest spirit defines the age statement.
- **No Chill‑Filter / No Flocculation**: The distillery deliberately avoids these processes to preserve natural flavour compounds.

## Visitor Experience & Sustainability
CMD offers guided tours of its heritage windmill village and a tasting lounge. It promotes sustainability through heritage preservation, use of local ingredients and responsible sourcing of oak. Wholesale orders can be placed via email (orders@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au).

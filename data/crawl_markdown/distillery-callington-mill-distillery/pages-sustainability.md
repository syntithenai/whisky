# Callington Mill Distillery – Sustainability

Callington Mill Distillery showcases a comprehensive commitment to sustainability across its entire whisky‑making journey. Key initiatives include:
- **Energy efficiency** with heat exchangers powered by clean green energy.
- **Waste recycling**: all distillery waste is repurposed locally, feeding sheep.
- **Local sourcing** of barley and responsibly sourced oak casks.
- **Eco‑friendly construction** using sustainable materials.
- **Recycled packaging** (98 % recycled material, biodegradable inks).
- Community support through local job creation, affordable housing, and future expansion plans.

These practices aim to strengthen Tasmania’s economy while minimizing environmental impact.

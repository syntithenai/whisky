# World Whisky Day Event – Callington Mill Distillery

- **Date & Venue**: Saturday 16 May at MACq 01, Hobart, Tasmania.
- **Price**: $95 per person.
- **Experience**: Six‑course dinner with three exclusive whiskies paired to each course. 10 % discount on bottle purchases that night.
- **Menu Highlights**:
  - House bread with miso butter, ponzu jelly and trout roe
  - Scallops (NF, GF) with pecorino donut & jalapeño salsa
  - Chicken (NF) inasal with garlic crunch & chilli emulsion
  - Bistek jus with parsnip and nameko
  - Aurora Double Oak macchiato with Valrhona gunaja, Jamaican coffee and Madeira cask foam
- **Allergen Notice**: Not allergen‑free; may contain nuts, sesame, eggs, gluten, dairy.
- **Cancellation Policy**: 10 % admin fee if cancelled >14 days before; otherwise non‑refundable.
- **Contact**: hobart@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au

> Spaces are limited – book early to secure your seat at the most spirited dinner of the year.

# Serendipity Experience – Callington Mill Distillery

- **Price**: $295 per person.
- **Duration**: ~1.5 hours (flexible).
- **What you create**: A 700 ml bottle of single‑malt whisky at cask strength (~65% ABV) with a personalised label and recipe file.
- **Process**:
  - Guided tasting of five premium cask‑strength whiskies.
  - Craft your own unique blend, then fill & seal the bottle.
  - Optional self‑guided tour of the distillery.
- **Cancellation policy**: 10 % fee if cancelled within 24 h; no refunds for no‑shows or rescheduling.

Available at two venues:
- Hobart (18 Hunter St)
- Cellar Door, Oatlands (6 Mill Lane)

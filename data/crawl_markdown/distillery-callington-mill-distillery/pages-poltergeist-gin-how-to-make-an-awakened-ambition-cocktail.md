# Poltergeist Gin – Awakened Ambition Cocktail

A quick recipe for a coffee‑infused espresso martini using Callington Mill Distillery’s **Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin**.

- **Ingredients**: 30 ml Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin, 30 ml Coffee Liqueur, 30 ml cooled fresh espresso, 5 ml sugar syrup.
- **Method**: Shake all ingredients with ice, double‑strain into a chilled martini glass (no ice), garnish with three coffee beans in a triangle.

# Dark Night 2026 – Callington Mill Distillery

- **Event**: Immersive dining experience inspired by Dark Mofo.
- **Date & Venue**: Friday 12 June 2026, Oatlands Distillery & Cellar Door (6 Mill Lane, Oatlands, Tasmania).
- **Price**: $99 per person.
- **Program**:
  - Warm welcome cocktail by the fire.
  - Five‑course shared feast featuring dishes such as Ember‑kissed Naan, Blue Eye Trevalla, Prawns of the Abyss, Quail in the Pyre, Smoked Cheeks of the Winter Swine, Burnt Gem Lettuce, and desserts like S'more of the Ritual Grounds.
  - Evening culminates with a whisky cask charring ritual.
- **Special Offer**: 10 % discount on cellar‑door purchases for attendees.
- **Booking & Cancellation**:
  - Reservations non‑refundable within 14 days; cancellations > 14 days incur a 10 % fee.
  - Contact info@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au for changes.

> *All dishes are served in the dark, passed and torn apart by hand.*

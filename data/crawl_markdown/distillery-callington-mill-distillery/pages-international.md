# International Availability
Callington Mill Distillery offers its award‑winning Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky worldwide through a network of authorised retailers.
- **Australia** – Direct sales via the distillery door and online store.
- **New Zealand** – Available at Glengarry Wines.
- **Europe & UK** – Sold exclusively by authorised partners (specific names not listed).
- **Germany & Poland** – Listed as available, but no further details.

For other regions, the distillery invites customers to contact them for assistance. Current product prices are shown in AUD for select whiskies.

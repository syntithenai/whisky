# Callington Mill Distillery Experiences

Callington Mill Distillery offers a range of whisky‑focused experiences in Hobart and Oatlands, Tasmania. Visitors can:
- Take a **self‑guided tour** with four 15 ml pours for $50.
- Join an **expert‑led guided tour** (90 min) that covers milling, mashing, fermenting, distilling, blending and tasting; priced at $150.
- Participate in the **Serendipity Experience** where guests blend their own single‑malt whisky from five cask‑strength expressions; available in Oatlands ($295) or Hobart ($295).

All experiences emphasize Tasmanian heritage, modern automation (the distillery is described as a “Rolls‑Royce of whisky distilleries”), and personalised service. The site also lists contact details for the Oatlands Cellar Door and the Hobart restaurant at MACq 01.

The page includes brief product pricing for select whiskies and gin, a mailing‑list signup, and standard legal/licensing notices.

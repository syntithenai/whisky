# Refund Policy – Callington Mill Distillery

Callington Mill Distillery’s refund policy outlines the rights of Australian consumers under the Australian Consumer Law and the company’s own return procedures.

- **Consumer Rights**: Replacement or refund for major failures; compensation for other foreseeable loss. Goods must be of acceptable quality, otherwise they can be repaired or replaced.
- **Return Conditions**: Returns accepted within 14 days of purchase, with proof of purchase. Change‑of‑mind returns must be in original condition and packaging.
- **Delivery Fees**: If the return is due to a fault (wrong product, damage, breach of guarantee) Callington Mill reimburses delivery costs; otherwise the fee is deducted from the refund.
- **Contact**: Email info@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au for refunds or exchanges. Wholesale orders go to orders@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au.
- **Legal Notice**: Under the Liquor Licensing Act 1990, it is an offence to deliver liquor to anyone under 18 years old.

The policy emphasizes customer satisfaction and compliance with Australian consumer protection laws.

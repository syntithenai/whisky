# Poltergeist Gin – Dry Martini

- **Recipe**: 60 ml Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin + 10 ml Extra‑Dry Vermouth, shaken with ice and strained into a chilled glass (no ice). Garnish with a lemon twist.
- **Glassware**: Nick & Nora method.
- **Product links**: Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin ($79.95 AUD), Poltergeist Citrus Gin ($79.95 AUD), Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin ($89.95 AUD).
- **Contact**: 6 Mill Lane, Oatlands, TAS, Australia – info@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au.

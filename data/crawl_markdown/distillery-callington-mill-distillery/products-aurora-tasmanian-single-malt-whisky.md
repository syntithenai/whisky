# Aurora Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

**Callington Mill Distillery** presents *Aurora*, a 46 % ABV single‑malt aged ~4½ years in Cream Sherry and Barrique Brandy casks. The whisky is described as light amber, with aromas of ripe prunes, spicy oak, walnuts and raisins; on the palate it delivers apricot, plum, liquorice, almonds, vanilla and nutmeg, finishing warm with toasted walnut, charred oak, dark fruit cake, caramel and bittersweet chocolate.

It has earned **Gold** at the 2025 San Francisco World Spirits Competition (91 pts) and **Silver** at both the 2025 World Whisky Masters and IWSC. The product is sold in a 700 ml bottle for AUD 149.00, non‑chill filtered, first‑fill wet casks.

# Callington Mill Distillery – Origin Story

Callington Mill Distillery was founded by **John Ibrahim** after he purchased Dysart House in Kempton, Tasmania. The purchase led to a partnership with **Bill Lark**, the “Godfather of Tasmanian Whisky”, who inspired John’s passion for whisky. Together they travelled to Scotland in 2016 to learn from world‑class distilleries and later visited Cardrona Distillery in New Zealand to acquire new technology. The Oatlands site, with its heritage buildings, became the home of Tasmania’s first turnkey distillery.

John describes the facility as a “Rolls‑Royce” of whisky production, blending respect for tradition with cutting‑edge equipment supplied by **Kolmark** and **Mark Kolodziej**. The distillery employs expert distiller **Blair Whitehead**.

Key products highlighted on the page include:
- *Quagmire Peated Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky* – $159 AUD
- *El Sol Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky* – $149 AUD
- *Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin* – $89.95 AUD

The distillery also promotes events such as the **Blue Lake Lodge Retreat** and offers a mailing list subscription.

# Callington Mill Distillery – Origin Story

Callington Mill Distillery was founded by **John Ibrahim** after he bought Dysart House in Kempton, Tasmania. The purchase led to a partnership with Redlands (now Old Kempton) and introduced John to **Bill Lark**, the “Godfather of Tasmanian Whisky”.  In 2016 John and Bill travelled to Scotland for behind‑the‑scenes visits to world‑class distilleries, which inspired John’s vision for Callington Mill.  Leveraging new technology from Kolmark and a visit to Cardrona Distillery in New Zealand, the company built what it calls “the Rolls‑Royce of whisky distilleries” in Tasmania.

Key people: **John Ibrahim** (founder), **Bill Lark** (mentor), **Blair Whitehead** (expert distiller).  The distillery is located at 6 Mill Lane, Oatlands, TAS, and offers single‑malt whisky and barrel‑aged gin.

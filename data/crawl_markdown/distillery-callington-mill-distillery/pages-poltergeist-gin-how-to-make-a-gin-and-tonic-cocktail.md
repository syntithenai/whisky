# Poltergeist Gin – How to Make a Gin and Tonic

- **Recipe**: 30 ml Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin + 60 ml chilled premium tonic water.
- **Glassware**: Highball or tumbler.
- **Method**: Fill with ice, pour gin, top with tonic, stir lightly, garnish with a fresh lemon slice.
- **Product links**: Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin (AUD 79.95), Poltergeist Citrus Gin (AUD 79.95), Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin (AUD 89.95).

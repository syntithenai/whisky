# Amphora Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

- **Award‑winning**: 2026 World Whisky Awards Category Winner & Australia Single Malt Winner.
- **Maturation journey**: First aged in Sherry, Tokay and Brandy casks, then transferred to three handmade 350 L clay amphorae for two silent years (no staves or char).
- **Tasting notes**: Sun‑warmed earth, caramelised vanilla, toasted hazelnuts on the nose; milk chocolate, roasted almonds, dark stone fruits and a hint of embers on the palate; long finish with charred oak, fine spice, dark chocolate and fruitcake.
- **Specifications**: 46 % ABV, 700 ml bottle, natural colour, non‑chill filtered, full‑term ageing, first fill wet casks.
- **Price**: AUD $265.00 per bottle.

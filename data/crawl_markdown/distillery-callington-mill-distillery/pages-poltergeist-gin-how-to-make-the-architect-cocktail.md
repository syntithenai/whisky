# Poltergeist Gin – Architect Cocktail

A recipe for a complex, spirit‑centric cocktail using Callington Mill Distillery’s **Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin** and **Enigma Single Malt Whisky**.

- **Ingredients**: 30 ml Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin, 30 ml Enigma Single Malt Whisky, 10 ml Amaro, 5 ml Maple Syrup, 2 dashes Orange Bitters.
- **Glassware**: Tumbler (rocks glass).
- **Method**: Stir all ingredients over ice in a mixing glass, strain into a rocks glass with a large ice cube, express orange peel and garnish with an orange spiral.

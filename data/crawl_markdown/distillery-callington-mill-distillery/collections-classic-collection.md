# Classic Range

- **Signature Collection**: Premium single malt whiskies aged for a minimum of seven years.
- **Collaborations**: Limited edition releases with renowned distilleries like Oatlands Distillery.
- **Heritage Series**: Bottles inspired by Callington Mill's rich history and heritage.
- **Poltergeist Gin**: Handcrafted gin infused with Tasmanian botanicals, available in the gift pack collection.

## Key Facts
- Aged for a minimum of seven years to achieve depth and complexity.
- Distilled using traditional copper pot stills at Callington Mill Distillery.
- Matured exclusively in American oak bourbon barrels sourced from Kentucky.
- Bottled at cask strength (around 55% ABV) without filtration or chill-filtration.

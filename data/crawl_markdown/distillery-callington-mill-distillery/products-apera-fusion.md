# Apera Fusion Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

- **ABV**: 46% 
- **Size**: 700 ml bottle (sold out)
- **Price**: AUD 135.00 (sale price, regular price listed as 0.00)
- **Cask history**: Initial ageing in 20 L Australian Apera casks; further maturation in 300 L and 500 L Apera casks from the Barossa Valley.

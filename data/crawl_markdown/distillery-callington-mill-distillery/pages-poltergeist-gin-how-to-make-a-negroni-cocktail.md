# Poltergeist Gin – Negroni Recipe

A quick guide to making a classic Negroni using Callington Mill Distillery’s **Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin**.

- **Ingredients**: 30 ml Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin, 30 ml Sweet Vermouth, 30 ml Campari.
- **Glassware**: Tumbler (rocks glass).
- **Method**: Stir the three spirits with ice in a mixing glass, strain over a large ice cube, and garnish with an expressed orange peel.

# Serendipity Experience (Hobart)

The **Serendipity Experience** at Callington Mill Distillery’s Hobart venue is a guided tasting and creation workshop where guests craft their own single‑malt whisky. Participants explore five premium cask‑strength whiskies, receive expert guidance, and leave with a unique flavour profile that they can bottle as a personal legacy.

Key details:
- **Location:** 18 Hunter St, Hobart, Tasmania, Australia
- **Duration:** ~1½ hours (extendable)
- **Pricing examples on the page:**
  - Quagmire Peated Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky – $159.00 AUD
  - El Sol Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky – $149.00 AUD
  - Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin – $89.95 AUD
- **Cancellation policy**: 10 % fee if cancelled within 24 h; no refunds for last‑minute cancellations or no‑shows.

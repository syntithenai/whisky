# Poltergeist Gin – Bramble Cocktail

A quick recipe for a modern Bramble using Callington Mill Distillery’s **Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin**.

- **Ingredients**: 45 ml gin, 22.5 ml fresh lemon juice, 10 ml simple syrup, 10 ml crème de mûre (float), soda to top up.
- **Method**: Shake gin, lemon and syrup with ice; strain over ice in a high‑ball glass; float the blackberry liqueur on top; garnish with a lemon slice.
- **Serving**: Highball glass, topped with soda for a tart, fruity, sippable drink.

# Shipping Policy – Callington Mill Distillery

All online orders are shipped via **Australia Post eParcel**. Orders are dispatched Monday‑Friday and require proof of age and signature in line with liquor licensing regulations. Delivery times: up to **10 business days** for metro areas and up to **15 business days** outside metro regions. The distillery is not liable for damage or loss once the parcel is handed to the courier; customers must file claims directly with the carrier. International shipments are subject to customs duties and taxes paid by the recipient.

For wholesale inquiries, contact orders@callingtonmilldistillery.com.au.

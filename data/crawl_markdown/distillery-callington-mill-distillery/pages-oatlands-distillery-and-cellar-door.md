# Callington Mill Distillery – Oatlands Whisky Distillery & Cellar Door

Callington Mill is a Tasmanian whisky destination offering a **restaurant and cellar door** experience at 6 Mill Lane, Oatlands. The site hosts a range of single‑malt whiskies, including the *Quagmire Peated* (AUD 159) and *El Sol* (AUD 149), as well as the *Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin* (AUD 89.95). Visitors can book:

- **Whisky Tasting & Self‑Guided Tour** – AUD 50 for four 15 ml samples.
- **Serendipity Experience** – AUD 295 to craft a cask‑strength single malt, receive a 700 ml bottle and label.
- **Guided Tours** – AUD 150 for an in‑depth walk through grain‑to‑glass production.

The distillery won the 2024 *Icons of Whisky* Visitor Attraction of the Year award. It also offers a heritage precinct with interactive audio/video displays.

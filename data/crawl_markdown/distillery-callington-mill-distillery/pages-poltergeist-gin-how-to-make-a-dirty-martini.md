# Poltergeist Gin – Dirty Martini

A quick recipe for a savory, briny twist on the classic martini using Callington Mill Distillery’s **Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin**.

- **Ingredients**: 60 ml Poltergeist Unfiltered Gin, 10 ml dry vermouth, 10 ml olive brine. 
- **Glassware**: Nick and Nora glass (or any chilled cocktail glass). 
- **Method**: Shake gin, vermouth and olive brine with ice; strain into a chilled glass without ice. Garnish with three green olives on a stainless‑steel pick.

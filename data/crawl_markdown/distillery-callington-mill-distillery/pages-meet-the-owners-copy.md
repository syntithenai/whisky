# Meet The Owners
Callington Mill Distillery is a family‑owned business run by the Nicolas and Ibrahim families. Twin brothers Salim and Danny Nicolas founded Dasco Construction & Building and are recipients of the Order of Australia (OAM). John Ibrahim, formerly with Mobil & Caltex, joined them in various service station ventures. Together they invested in Shene Distillery (2016) and Lark Distillery (2017), eventually forming Callington Mill Distillery.

## Timeline
- **2015** – John begins whisky journey at Old Kempton Distillery.
- **2016** – Investment in Shene Distillery, later sold to Lark Distilling Co. in 2022.
- **2017** – Investment in Lark Distillery (formerly Australian Whisky Holdings). Overeem brand later returned to its family.

# Poltergeist Gin Moves to Callington Mill Distillery

Callington Mill Distillery has acquired the rights and stock of the award‑winning **Poltergeist Gin** following the sale of Shene Distillery to Lark Distillery. The gin, originally created by Damian Mackey, is now distilled at 6 Mill Lane in Oatlands, Tasmania.

- **Botanicals**: Angelica, cardamom, cassia bark, Chinese liquorice root, coriander seeds, juniper, lemon peel, orris root, star anise, Tasmanian lemon myrtle, macadamia nuts, mountain pepper berries.
- **Production**: 20‑plus hour maceration in copper pot stills, followed by 12–14 hour distillation. Unfiltered (louching) and diluted to 46 % ABV from a 78 % spirit.
- **Awards**: Platinum at San Francisco World Spirits Competition (2019‑2020), Double Gold at 2017 & 2018 competitions, etc.
- **Pricing**: Poltergeist Barrel‑Aged Gin $89.95 AUD; Citrus Gin $79.95 AUD; Unfiltered Gin $79.95 AUD.
- **Availability**: Sold online via the Callington Mill website and in their cellar door.

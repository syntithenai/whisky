# Poltergeist Gin Moves to Callington Mill Distillery

Callington Mill Distillery has taken over the production and distribution of the award‑winning **Poltergeist Gin** following the sale of Shene Distillery to Lark Distillery. The gin, originally created by Damian Mackey, is now distilled at 6 Mill Lane in Oatlands, Tasmania.

- **Botanicals**: Angelica, cardamom, cassia bark, Chinese liquorice root, coriander seeds, juniper, lemon peel, orris root, star anise, Tasmanian lemon myrtle, macadamia nuts, mountain pepper berries.
- **Distillation**: 20 + hours maceration in copper pot stills, followed by 12‑14 hour run; final ABV 46 % (78 % before dilution).
- **Unfiltered**: The gin is left unfiltered to preserve natural clouding (“louching”).
- **Awards**: Platinum at San Francisco World Spirits Competition (2019, 2020) and multiple double‑gold awards.
- **Pricing**: Unfiltered $79.95 AUD; Citrus $79.95 AUD; Barrel‑Aged $89.95 AUD.

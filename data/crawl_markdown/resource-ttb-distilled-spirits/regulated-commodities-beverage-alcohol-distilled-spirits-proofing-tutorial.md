# Proofing Tutorial – TTB

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) provides a **Proofing Tutorial** for distilled‑spirits plant proprietors. The tutorial explains how to determine the quantity and proof of spirits removed from bonded premises, which is essential for calculating taxes owed. It includes a video series covering:

1. Proofing with a hydrometer – cost‑effective and accurate.
2. Proofing with a densitometer – fast and automatable.
3. Distilling high‑solid samples before proofing.
4. Determining proof obscuration by evaporation.

Contact information for the Scientific Services Division, National Revenue Center, and labeling division is provided for further assistance.

# Proofing Tutorial – TTB

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) provides a **Proofing Tutorial** for distilled‑spirits plant proprietors. The tutorial explains how to determine the quantity and proof of spirits removed from bonded premises, which is essential for calculating taxes owed.

Key components:
- **Video Series** – Four short videos covering hydrometer use, densitometer use, laboratory distillation of high‑solid samples, and evaporation‑based proof obscuration.
- **Contact Information** – Links to the National Revenue Center (permits, bonds, tax payments) and Scientific Services Division (proofing techniques).
- **Administrative Details** – Last reviewed 10 Oct 2014; last updated 25 Sep 2025.

The page is a resource‑type informational guide rather than a product listing or review.

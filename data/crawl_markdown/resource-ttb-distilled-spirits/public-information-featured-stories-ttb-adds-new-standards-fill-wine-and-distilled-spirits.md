# TTB Adds New Standards of Fill for Wine and Distilled Spirits

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) has issued a final rule that:
- Authorizes **13 new fill sizes** for wine containers and **15 new fill sizes** for distilled‑spirits containers.
- Removes the distinction between cans and other containers for distilled spirits, allowing all approved sizes to be used in any container type.

Wine new sizes (ml): 180, 300, 330, 360, 473, 550, 568, 600, 620, 700, 720; liter: 1.8 L, 2.25 L.
Distilled‑spirits new sizes (ml): 187, 250, 331, 350, 355, 475, 500, 570, 700, 710, 945; liter: 1.5 L, 2 L, 3 L, 3.75 L.

The rule is effective **January 10, 2025** and will appear on the eCFR shortly thereafter. All related documents are available on Regulations.gov (docket TTB‑2022‑0004).

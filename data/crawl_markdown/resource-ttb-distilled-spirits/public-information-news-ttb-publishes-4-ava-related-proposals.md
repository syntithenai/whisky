# TTB Publishes 4 AVA‑Related Proposals

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) announced four proposals concerning American Viticultural Areas (AVAs):

1. **Name change** – Mendocino Ridge AVA in Mendocino County, California will be renamed *Mendocino Coast Ridge*.
2. **New AVAs** – Three new AVAs are proposed:
   - *Conneaut Creek*, 70 437 acres in Ashtabula County, Ohio (within Lake Erie AVA).
   - *Tryon Foothills*, 176 square miles in Polk County, North Carolina.
   - *Nashoba Valley*, 18 367 acres in Worcester County, Massachusetts.

All proposals are open for public comment until **March 5, 2024**. Comment submission is available electronically via the TTB website, and proposed boundaries can be viewed on the AVA Map Explorer.

# Distilled Spirits Reports – TTB

The page hosts statistical data on U.S. distilled spirits, sourced from monthly industry reports (F‑5110.43, F‑5110.40, F‑5110.28, F‑5110.11). It lists downloadable files: a national XLSX report, yearly and monthly CSVs, a JSON dataset, and mapping sheets. A PDF/JSON summary of producers and bottlers by average taxable removals (2012‑2024) is also available. Contact information for the TTB Open Data team is provided.

---

## Key Points
- Monthly reports due by the 15th of the following month; data released ~45 days later.
- End‑of‑year releases (~60 days after December filing).
- All prior period data refreshed with each posting.
- Files: National Report (XLSX), Yearly Data (CSV), Monthly Data (CSV), Full Data (JSON), Mapping (XLSX), Producers & Bottlers report (PDF/JSON).

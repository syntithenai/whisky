# Upcoming Changes to TTB Incoming and Outgoing Payments

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) will transition all federal receipts and disbursements to electronic payments under Executive Order 14247, effective **September 30 2025**.

* **Paper checks for refunds and drawback will cease** – unless an exception applies.
* **Tax refunds require banking information** on the claim form; otherwise refunds may be delayed.
* New claim forms (e.g., TTB F 5620.8, F 5110.30, etc.) will include sections for bank details.
* Existing paper‑check payments should move to Pay.gov or other electronic funds transfer methods.

Contact the Office of Permitting and Taxation at 877‑882‑3277 (option 4) or use the online contact form for questions.

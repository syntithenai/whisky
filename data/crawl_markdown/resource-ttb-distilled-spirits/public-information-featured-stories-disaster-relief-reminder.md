# Disaster Relief Reminder

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) issued a brief notice on **February 22, 2024** reminding industry members that the President has declared major natural disasters in California and Washington. The reminder explains that such events can affect alcohol or tobacco excise‑tax filings and reporting, directs affected businesses to the TTB Disaster Relief page for guidance, and provides contact information (toll‑free 877‑882‑3277). It also encourages checking state/local authorities for additional relief options and links to FEMA resources.

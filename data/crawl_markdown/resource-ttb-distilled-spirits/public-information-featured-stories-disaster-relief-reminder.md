# Disaster Relief Reminder

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) issued a reminder on **February 22 2024** regarding disaster relief for industry members affected by the President’s declaration of major natural disasters in California and Washington. The notice directs businesses to consult TTB’s *Disaster Relief* page for guidance on excise‑tax filing or reporting, and provides contact information (toll‑free 877‑882‑3277). State/local authorities may offer additional relief.

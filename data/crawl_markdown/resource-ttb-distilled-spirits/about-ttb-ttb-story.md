# The TTB Story

The Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) is the newest bureau under the U.S. Department of the Treasury. Its mission traces back to federal taxes on distilled spirits in 1791, and it has modernized its services through e‑government initiatives. A narrated video titled *The TTB Story* provides a historical overview, and a transcript is available for download.

# TTB Publishes New Strategic Plan

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) released its **Strategic Plan for Fiscal Years 2023‑2027** on February 23, 2024. The plan outlines updated mission, vision, values, and five strategic goals:
1. Facilitate Business Growth on a Level Playing Field
2. Facilitate Business Innovation in a Fair Marketplace
3. Improve Taxpayer Experience and Tax Administration
4. Ensure Access to Tax Refunds for Eligible Businesses
5. Enhance Workforce Readiness

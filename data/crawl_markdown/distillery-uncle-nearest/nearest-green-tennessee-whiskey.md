# Nearest Green Tennessee Whiskey

Uncle Nearest presents **Nearest Green Tennessee Whiskey**, a 84‑proof (100 % ABV) spirit crafted by Master Blender Victoria Eady Butler, a 5th‑generation descendant of the Green family. The bottle is marketed as an approachable tribute to the godfather of Tennessee whiskey and can be enjoyed neat or in cocktails.

# Funding Guidelines

Uncle Nearest Green Distillery (NGD) provides community‑focused funding to local non‑profits and civic projects within Bedford, Moore, Rutherford, Franklin, and Coffee Counties in Tennessee. Applications must be submitted via the official NGD Donation‑Sponsor Request Form and may include supporting documents. NGD can donate money, property, services or volunteer support, but only if the organization aligns with Uncle Nearest Inc.’s philanthropic goals—education, agriculture, local economic development, tourism, and environmental sustainability.

**Eligibility & Restrictions**
- Only written requests using the official form are accepted.
- Donations may be monetary, in-kind, or volunteer‑based.
- Funding is limited to organizations that benefit Uncle Nearest Inc.’s objectives.
- Support is restricted to civic, recreational, non‑profit or charity events/programs/publications located within the five specified counties.
- Sponsors receive advertising or public name recognition and must use Uncle Nearest Premium Whiskey as their exclusive whiskey or bourbon of choice.

**Exclusions**
- Individual fundraisers, single family/group causes, religious or political affiliations, travel cost requests, conflict‑of‑interest groups, under‑21 constituents, and certain trademark/promotion restrictions are disallowed.

**Logo & Promotion**
- Logo usage must be approved by NGD Marketing at least two weeks before the event deadline. Requests for custom ads should be emailed to marketing@ng-d.com no less than three weeks prior, including specifications such as file format, size, color/B/W, bleeds, and borders.

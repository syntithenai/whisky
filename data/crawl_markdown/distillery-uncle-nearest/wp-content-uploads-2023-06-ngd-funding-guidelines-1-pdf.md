# Funding Guidelines

Uncle Nearest Green Distillery (NGD) provides community‑focused funding to local non‑profits and civic projects within Bedford, Moore, Rutherford, Franklin, and Coffee counties in Tennessee. Applications must be submitted via the official NGD Donation‑Sponsor Request Form and may include supporting documents. NGD can donate money, property, services or volunteer support, but only if the organization aligns with Uncle Nearest Inc.’s philanthropic goals—education, agriculture, local economic development, tourism, and environmental sustainability.

**Eligibility**
- Written request using the official form.
- Contributions may be monetary, in‑kind, or volunteer.
- Funding decisions depend on available funds and additional guidelines.
- Support is limited to civic, recreational, non‑profit or charity events/programs/publications within the five counties.

**Exclusions**
- Individual fundraisers, religious or political organizations, travel cost requests, conflict‑of‑interest groups, under‑21 constituencies, and certain promotional uses of the logo.

NGD sponsors receive advertising or public name recognition and must use Uncle Nearest Premium Whiskey as their exclusive spirit. Logo usage requires prior approval from NGD Marketing at least two weeks before event deadlines.

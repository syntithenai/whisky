# Cognac Cask Whiskey – Uncle Nearest

Uncle Nearest’s limited‑edition **Cognac Cask Whiskey** is a small‑batch, cask‑strength blend finished in XO Cognac barrels from the Fins Bois and Borderies regions of France. The mash bill comes from the award‑winning 1884 line, and the French casks impart floral and fruity notes that bridge American whiskey tradition with French refinement.

- **Proof**: 100 proof (cask strength)
- **Finish**: XO Cognac barrels (Fins Bois & Borderies)
- **Flavor profile**: delicate floral, fruity, subtle oak, hint of spice
- **Availability**: exclusive release; limited quantity

The product is part of Uncle Nearest’s “Nearest Green” line and highlights the distillery’s expansion into vineyard ownership in Cognac.

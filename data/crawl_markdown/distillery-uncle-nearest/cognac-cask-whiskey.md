# Cognac Cask Whiskey – Uncle Nearest

Uncle Nearest’s limited‑edition **Cognac Cask Whiskey** is a small‑batch, cask‑strength blend finished in XO Cognac barrels from the Fins Bois and Borderies regions of France. The mash bill comes from the award‑winning 1884 line and delivers floral, fruity notes that bridge American whiskey tradition with French refinement.

- **Proof:** 100 % (cask strength)
- **Finish:** XO Cognac casks (Fins Bois & Borderies)
- **Style:** Small batch, limited edition, exclusive release
- **Origin:** Tennessee distillery, finished in France

The product is part of Uncle Nearest’s “Nearest Green” line and highlights the company’s vineyard ownership in Grande Champagne.

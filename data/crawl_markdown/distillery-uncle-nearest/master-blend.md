# Master Blend Whiskey - Uncle Nearest Premium Whiskey

- **Proof**: 100 Proof from Tennessee
- **Distillery**: Nearest Green Distillery, Shelbyville, Tennessee
- **History**: Continues the tradition of distilling, aging, filtering and bottling whiskey in Tennessee started by Nearest Green (the world's first known African American master distiller)
- **Production Signals**:
  - Blended from select barrels chosen for their "spectacular" quality
  - Filtered through sugar maple charcoal before maturation
  - Aged in non-temperature controlled rickhouses for minimum of six winters
  - Bottled at cask strength
- **Commercial Signals**:
  - Available exclusively at Nearest Green Distillery
  - Part of the Master Blend Edition series
- **Risks/Unknowns**:
  - Limited availability due to exclusive distribution
  - Cask strength bottling may be intense for some drinkers

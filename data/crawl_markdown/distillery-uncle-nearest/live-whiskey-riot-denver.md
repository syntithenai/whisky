# Whiskey Riot: Denver

Uncle Nearest hosts a limited‑edition event in Denver on **October 5, 2024** at *The Brighton*. The evening features their **100 Proof Premium Whiskey** and other single‑barrel releases. Attendees can enjoy tastings, learn about the distillery’s history, and join the brand’s community.

- **Date & Time:** 3:00 pm – 7:00 pm MDT
- **Venue:** The Brighton, 3403 Brighton Blvd, Denver, CO 80216
- **Ticketing:** VIP (3–4 pm) or General Admission (4–7 pm)
- **Highlights:** Limited‑edition single‑barrel whiskey, Cognac cask releases, and a chance to meet the team.

For more details, visit the event page or sign up for the mailing list.

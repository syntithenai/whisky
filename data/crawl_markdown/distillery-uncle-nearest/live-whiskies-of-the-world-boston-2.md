# Whiskies of the World: Boston
Uncle Nearest hosts a tasting event in Boston on **August 23, 2024** at the Intercontinental Boston. The event showcases their range of Tennessee whiskies, including limited‑edition Toasted Single Barrel and Cognac Cask releases.

- **Venue:** Intercontinental Boston, 510 Atlantic Ave, Boston, MA 02210
- **Date & Time:** August 23, 2024, 7:00 am – 10:30 pm EDT
- **Tickets:** General Admission $7.45–$10.30; VIP $7.00–$10.30 (exact pricing not listed on the page)
- **Highlights:** Live tasting of Uncle Nearest Premium Whiskey and other products, plus a Q&A with distillery staff.

For more details or to purchase tickets, visit the event page on the Uncle Nearest website.

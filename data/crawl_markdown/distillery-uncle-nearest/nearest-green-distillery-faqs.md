# Nearest Green Distillery FAQ

- **Location & Size**: 3125 HWY US‑231 North Shelbyville, TN; 458 acres.
- **Tours**: Guided walk (~½ mile) with whiskey flight (Master Blend), Single Barrel Thieving Experience, Taste of History, Spanish tour. Prices range $28.50–$75 plus 14 % booking fee; discounts for seniors/military.
- **Hours & Access**: Mon‑Tue/Wed 10‑2 pm, Thu‑Sat 9‑7 pm, Sun 11‑5 pm. Free parking, wheelchair accessible, pets only service animals allowed.
- **Events**: Private parties, weddings, photo shoots permitted with guidelines; no on‑site hotels.
- **Whiskey Sales**: Eight expressions available at Welcome Center (prices $29.99–$149.99). No shipping; Reserve Bar is third‑party distributor.
- **Restaurants**: Chuck’s Barrel House BBQ II and Humble Baron – the world’s longest bar (518 ft, Guinness record).
- **Other Services**: Gift cards not offered; merchandise shipping available.
- **Partnerships & Initiatives**: Nearest & Jack Advancement Initiative with $5 M pledge for distilling school.

- Opened 14 Sep 2019.

# Nearest Green Distillery FAQ

- **Location & Size**: 3125 HWY US‑231 North Shelbyville, TN – 458 acres.
- **Tours**: Guided walk (~½ mile) with whiskey flight (Master Blend), Single Barrel Thieving Experience, Taste of History, Spanish tour. Prices range $28.50–$75 plus 14% booking fee; senior/military discounts available.
- **Hours & Access**: Mon‑Tue/Wed 10‑2 pm, Thu‑Sat 9‑7 pm, Sun 11‑5 pm. Closed on major holidays. Free parking, wheelchair accessible.
- **Events**: Private parties, weddings, photo shoots allowed with restrictions; service animals only.
- **Whiskey Sales**: Eight expressions sold at Welcome Center (prices $29.99–$149.99). No shipping; Reserve Bar is third‑party distributor.
- **Restaurants**: Chuck’s Barrel House BBQ II and Humble Baron – the world’s longest bar (518 ft, 202 stools).
- **Other Services**: Merchandise shipping, whiskey passport, donation/sponsorship program, partnership with Jack Daniel’s.

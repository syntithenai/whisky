# Gluten and Distillation
Uncle Nearest explains that while their whiskies are made from gluten‑containing grains, the distillation process removes gluten proteins. Independent lab tests show "none detected" for all core expressions (1884, 1856, rye). The company cannot label the product as "gluten‑free" due to TTB regulations but meets FDA and celiac organization safety standards.

## Key Points
- Distillation separates alcohol from solids, eliminating gluten.
- Random lab testing confirms no detectable gluten in core expressions.
- Federal rules prohibit a gluten‑free label on grain‑based spirits.
- Uncle Nearest commits to transparency and quality.

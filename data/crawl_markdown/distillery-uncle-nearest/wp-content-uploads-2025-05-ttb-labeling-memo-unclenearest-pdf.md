# Uncle Nearest TTB Labeling Memo
- **Purpose**: Justify using *Tennessee Whiskey* on the front label and *Straight Bourbon Whiskey* on the back label.
- **Key Points**:
  - Tennessee whiskey is a recognized sub‑category of straight bourbon under U.S. law and international trade agreements (NAFTA, USMCA).
  - TTB Ruling 2013‑2 confirms that Tennessee whiskey must meet straight bourbon standards, be produced in Tennessee, and undergo the Lincoln County Process (charcoal mellowing).
  - The memo proposes front‑label class/type *Tennessee Whiskey* and a back‑label explanatory statement detailing compliance with straight bourbon rules and the charcoal filtering step.
- **Conclusion**: Request approval for this labeling approach as factually accurate and legally compliant.

# Nearest Green Distillery
- Located on a 458‑acre property in Tennessee.
- Features four phases of a $50 million build‑out and a working distillery & farm.
- Offers tours, tastings, a restaurant (Barrel House II), and retail sales of exclusive releases such as the Master Blend Edition.
- Operates Monday–Friday with specific hours; closed on major holidays.
- Hosts events, group tours (minimum 18 guests), and a “Road to a Million” promotion.

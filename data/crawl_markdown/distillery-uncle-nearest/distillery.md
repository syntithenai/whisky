# Nearest Green Distillery
- **Location & Size**: 458‑acre property in Tennessee, part of a $50 million build‑out.
- **Products**: Premium aged whiskey (1856), small‑batch master blend, single‑barrel and rye variants, cognac‑cask releases, and an exclusive Master Blend Edition available only on site.
- **Tours & Events**: Open Monday–Friday for tours, tastings, and a restaurant. Barrel House II operates Thursday–Sunday with extended hours. The distillery hosts BBQs, private events, and the “Road to a Million” promotion.
- **Accessibility & Transparency**: Publicly shares gluten‑and‑distillation information and a transparency page.
- **Contact**: Phone (931) 343‑7818 for group tours; email signup for updates.

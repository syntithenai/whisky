# Whiskies of the World: Seattle

Uncle Nearest hosts a **Whiskies of the World** event in Seattle on *November 9, 2024* at The Rainer Club (820 4th Ave). The evening runs from 6:00 pm to 9:00 pm PST and showcases Uncle Nearest’s range of Tennessee whiskies, including limited‑edition releases such as the Toasted Single Barrel Whiskey and Cognac Cask Whiskey. The event is part of the distillery’s broader community outreach, featuring tours, tastings, and a donation program.

---

## Key Distillery Highlights
- **Founded**: 1856 (historical roots) – modern operations began in 2024.
- **Location**: Tennessee, USA.
- **Products**: Premium Aged Whiskey, Small Batch Master Blend, Single Barrel, Straight Rye, Uncut/Unfiltered Rye, Cognac‑Cask, and Toasted Single Barrel editions.
- **Alcohol Content**: 60.8 %, 50 %, 46.5 % and 42 % ABV (varies by product).
- **Mission**: “Drink Honourably” – a brand ethos emphasizing community impact and transparency.

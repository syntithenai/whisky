# Tennessee Whiskey Also Bourbon?

Uncle Nearest explains that, by U.S. law and international trade agreements, Tennessee whiskey is a type of straight bourbon. The distillery uses the term *Tennessee Whiskey* on the front label and *Straight Bourbon Whiskey* on the back to remain transparent and compliant with the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB). A letter from CEO Fawn Weaver outlines this legal foundation.

---

**Key points**
- Tennessee whiskey = straight bourbon made in Tennessee, filtered through sugar‑maple charcoal (Lincoln County Process).
- Uncle Nearest meets all federal standards for both classifications.
- The distillery encourages customers to read the full TTB letter.

# Straight Rye Whiskey
Uncle Nearest’s 100‑proof straight rye is a 100 % rye mash matured in used Uncle Nearest barrels and bottled at 100 proof. It boasts a bold nose of praline, nutmeg and white pepper; a palate of vanilla cream, caramelized sugar and rye spice; and a long, rich finish.

## Key facts
- **Proof**: 100 proof (50% ABV)
- **Mash bill**: 100 % rye
- **Maturation**: Used Uncle Nearest barrels
- **Flavor profile**: Praline, nutmeg, white pepper, vanilla cream, caramelized sugar, rye spice
- **Finish**: Long, rich and well‑balanced

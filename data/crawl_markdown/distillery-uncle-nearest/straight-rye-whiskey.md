# Straight Rye Whiskey – Uncle Nearest

Uncle Nearest’s Straight Rye is a 100‑proof, 100% rye mash bill matured in used Uncle Nearest barrels. It boasts a bold nose of praline, nutmeg and white pepper; a palate of vanilla cream, caramelized sugar and rye spice; and a long, rich finish.

- **Proof**: 100 (40% ABV)
- **Maturation**: Used Uncle Nearest barrels
- **Flavor profile**: spicy, sweet, smooth
- **Availability**: U.S. only, sold through the distillery’s shop and select distributors.

> *“This was a lofty experiment that worked.”* – Uncle Nearest marketing copy.

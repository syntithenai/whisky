# Road to a Million – Official Rules

These are the official terms for Uncle Nearest’s “Road to a Million” sweepstakes, valid only in the United States and D.C., open to legal residents aged 21+. The promotion runs from March 23 2023 at 6:00 p.m. CT until December 31 2026 or until the sponsor receives its one‑millionth visitor. Participants must scan QR codes at the Nearest Green Distillery, submit an entry form with name, birthdate, email and ZIP code, and may enter once per calendar week (max 12 entries per year). No purchase is required.

Key points:
- **Eligibility**: U.S./D.C. residents ≥21 years old; employees of the sponsor or related entities are disqualified.
- **Entry limits**: 1 entry/week, 12 entries/year.
- **Privacy**: Sponsor may collect personal data per its privacy policy.
- **Prize**: Ten grand‑prize winners each receive a $100 000 check; prize is non‑transferable and subject to taxes.
- **Winner selection**: Random drawing of eligible entries; sponsor reserves right to change the draw date once the millionth visitor is reached.
- **Legal**: Governed by Tennessee law, disputes resolved in Bedford County courts.

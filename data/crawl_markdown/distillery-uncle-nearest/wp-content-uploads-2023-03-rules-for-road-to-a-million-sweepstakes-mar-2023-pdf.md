# Road to a Million – Official Rules

These are the official terms for Uncle Nearest’s “Road to a Million” sweepstakes, which runs from March 23 2023 to December 31 2026. The promotion is open only to legal residents of the United States and D.C., who must be 21 years or older at the time of entry. Participants enter by visiting the Nearest Green Distillery in Shelbyville, Tennessee, scanning a QR code, and submitting an online form with name, birthdate, email and ZIP code. Each eligible person may submit one entry per calendar week and up to twelve entries per year.

Key points:
- No purchase is required; buying Uncle Nearest products does not increase chances of winning.
- The prize is a single $100 000 check (subject to taxes). Ten grand‑prize winners are selected by random drawing from all eligible entries.
- Winners must provide valid identification and sign liability releases. If they fail to comply within five business days, the prize is forfeited.
- The promotion is governed by Tennessee law; disputes are resolved in Bedford County courts.

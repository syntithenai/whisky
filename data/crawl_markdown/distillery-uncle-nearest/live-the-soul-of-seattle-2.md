# The Soul of Seattle
Uncle Nearest hosts a limited‑edition event in Seattle on February 8, 2025 at Bloc 41. The evening features the brand’s *The Soul of Seattle* whiskey and other releases such as Toasted Single Barrel and Cognac Cask editions.

## Event Details
- **Date & Time**: Feb 8 2025, 5:30‑9:30 p.m. PST
- **Venue**: Bloc 41, 115 Bel Street, Seattle, WA
- **Target Audience**: On‑premise and off‑premise accounts looking to host a whiskey event.

## About Uncle Nearest
Uncle Nearest is an independent Tennessee distillery founded in 1856. The company produces premium aged whiskey, small batch blends, single barrel releases, straight rye, and uncut/uncensored rye. It emphasizes transparency, gluten‑free distillation, and community engagement through events and sponsorships.

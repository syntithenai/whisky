# Black Wine Fest – Uncle Nearest Premium Whiskey

Uncle Nearest is hosting a limited‑edition event at the **Cincinnati Music Hall** from **Feb 15–17, 2024** as part of the *Black Wine Fest*. The distillery’s flagship product, **Uncle Nearest Premium Whiskey 100 Proof**, will be showcased alongside other releases such as Toasted Single Barrel and Cognac Cask whiskeys.

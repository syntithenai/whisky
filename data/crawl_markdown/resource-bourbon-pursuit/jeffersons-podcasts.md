# Jefferson's Bourbon - Key Facts

**Key Facts:**
- Founded by Trey Zoeller and Chet Zoeller in 1997
- First distillery to produce bourbon without owning a still
- Offers single barrel, limited edition, and special releases like the Presidential line
- Known for innovative blending techniques and collaborations with chefs and artists
- Expanding product line includes rye whiskey (Jefferson's Rye) and ocean-inspired expressions (Jefferson's Ocean)

**Production Signals:**
- Uses a unique blend of grains and aging process to create distinct flavor profiles
- Collaborates with chefs for limited edition cask finishes
- Experimentation with new techniques like the Wood Experiment and Groth Cask finish
- Expanding into rye whiskey production

**Commercial Signals:**
- Growing popularity among bourbon enthusiasts and collectors
- Successful partnerships with restaurants, hotels, and distilleries
- Strong brand positioning as a pioneer in innovative bourbon production
- Expansion of product line to include new expressions like Jefferson's Ocean

**Risks/Unknowns:**
- Dependence on sourcing high-quality barrels from aging distilleries
- Potential challenges in maintaining consistency across limited edition releases
- Market competition from established brands and emerging craft distilleries

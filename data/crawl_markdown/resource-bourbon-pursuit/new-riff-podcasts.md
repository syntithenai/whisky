# New Riff Podcasts Summary

## Key Facts
- **New Riff Distilling** is a brand known for unique grain experimentation and single malt projects.
- Founded by Jay Erisman and Ken Lewis, the distillery focuses on curiosity-driven bourbon making in an urban setting.
- The company has released several notable bottlings like "Holy Water 19:1" and "Happy Little Riff".
- New Riff collaborates with farmers and consumers to source heirloom grains and create distinctive single malts.

## Production Signals
- Emphasis on **heirloom grains** and unique mash bills for single malt projects.
- Use of **charred barrels** and aging in various cask types (e.g., Balboa Rye) to develop complex flavors.
- Focus on **small-batch, high-quality production** with a focus on transparency and storytelling.

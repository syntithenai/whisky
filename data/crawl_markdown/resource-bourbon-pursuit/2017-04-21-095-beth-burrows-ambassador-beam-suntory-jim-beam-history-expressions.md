# Beam Bourbon History & Expressions

- **Host**: Beth Burrows, Brand Ambassador for Beam Suntory.
- Discusses the history of Jim Beam and its various expressions (e.g., White Label, Black, Double Oak, Devil’s Cut, Rye, Bonded, Single Barrel).
- Covers new flavored lines such as Apple & Honey, Black Cherry, Fire, Maple.
- Mentions the impact of Senate Bill 11 on cocktail production.
- Explores the naming story behind Baker’s Bourbon and other brand names (Knob Creek, Basil Hayden, OGD, Old Crow, Booker’s View).
- Includes listener questions about target markets and product development.

The episode is part of Bourbon Pursuit’s podcast series on bourbon history and marketing.

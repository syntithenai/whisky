# Bourbon Pursuit Podcast Episode 556 - Matt Kusek's Hot Takes on the Bourbon Market

- **Host**: Kenny Coleman and guests including bourbon expert Matt "The Colonel" Kusek (aka Matt Kusek aka "The Colonel")
- **Focus**: Discussion of current trends, challenges, and grievances in the bourbon market
- **Key Topics Covered**:

  - Shift in consumer behavior towards high-status brands like Pappy Van Winkle
  - Non-Distiller Producers (NDPs) vs. traditional heritage distilleries
  - Psychological aspects of bourbon collecting and "hunting" for bottles
  - Future outlook for collectible whiskeys and innovative offerings

# Bourbon Pursuit Podcast Episode 534

## Key Facts
- **Guests**: Kevin Carlson (@bourbon_notes) from @bourbonpursuit and Mike Yates (@cinebourbon)
- **Topic**: Discussion on the evolution of bourbon content creation in the social media era
- **Game**: "Over, Under, or Properly Rated" covering various bourbon trends and hype topics

## Production Signals
- Shift towards video content creation leveraging platforms like TikTok and Instagram
- Increased focus on engaging storytelling and audience interaction
- Emphasis on authenticity versus hype in bourbon marketing

## Commercial Signals
- Growing influence of social media creators in shaping consumer perceptions
- Rise of hazmat bottles and limited releases as coveted items
- Importance of building relationships with local liquor store owners for exclusive access

## Risks/Unknowns
- Balancing nostalgia with modern content creation techniques
- Managing expectations from a rapidly growing bourbon community online
- Potential oversaturation of video content leading to decreased engagement

# 560 – Where Storytelling, Marketing, and Good Whiskey all Meet

Podcast episode featuring **Daniel Whittington**, Chancellor of Wizard Academy and co‑founder of the Whiskey Marketing School. The discussion covers:
- Transition from wine to whiskey education.
- Role of narrative and community in driving interest in bottles.
- Growth and challenges of the *Whiskey Tribe* YouTube channel.
- Launch of **AmericanIndy.com** for independent bottler transparency.
- Importance of clear whiskey classifications for consumers.

The episode is hosted by Kenny Coleman on Bourbon Pursuit’s podcast series.

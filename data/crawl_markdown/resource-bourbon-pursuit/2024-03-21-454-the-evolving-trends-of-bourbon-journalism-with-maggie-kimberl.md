# 454 – The Evolving Trends of Bourbon Journalism with Maggie Kimberl

Kenny Coleman hosts a podcast episode featuring **Maggie Kimberl**, Content Editor at *American Whiskey Magazine*. They discuss how bourbon journalism has shifted from in‑depth print pieces to short‑form social media content, the impact of PR firms on distillery access, and the rise of new distilleries creating fresh stories. The conversation touches on lobbying groups, the changing audience attention span, and whether traditional media still matters for brands.

---

**Key points:**
- Traditional writers vs. modern influencers.
- Access to distilleries now mediated by PR.
- New distilleries mean more stories.
- Debate over deep articles vs. click‑bait.
- Role of bourbon media in brand strategy.

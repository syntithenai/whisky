# 147 – Longbranch, Anti‑Spirit Awards & Press Release News

This episode of the **Bourbon Community Roundtable** (May 3 2018) discusses:
- The *Longbranch* collaboration with Wild Turkey and its impact on brand perception.
- The 2017 San Francisco World Spirit Awards where 92 % of entrants won medals, including Henry McKenna’s win for best bourbon.
- “Anti‑Spirit” award show topics and the lack of coverage for certain releases (e.g., Four Grain, OFC press release).
- Questions about Wild Turkey 81’s future and the effect of celebrity endorsements such as Matthew McConaughey.

The post also lists contributors from *bourbonr.com*, *BreakingBourbon.com*, *Sobourbia.com*, *tater‑talk.com* and *Sippncorn.blogspot.com*.

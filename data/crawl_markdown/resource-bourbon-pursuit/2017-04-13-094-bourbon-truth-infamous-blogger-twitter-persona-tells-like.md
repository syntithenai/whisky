# 094 – No One Is Safe

Bourbon Pursuit hosts an interview with **Lloyd Christmas** (known as *Bourbon Truth*), a blogger and Twitter persona who critiques the bourbon industry’s marketing tactics, price inflation, and hoarding culture. The episode discusses:
- The rise of private barrel picks and limited‑edition bottling.
- How prices have surged from $50 to $350 for 15‑year‑old bourbons.
- Whether new consumers should buy these expensive bottles or wait.
- The impact on craft whiskey and the broader market.

The conversation is framed as a candid, no‑filter critique of distillery marketing practices.

# 094 – No One Is Safe

Bourbon Pursuit hosts an interview with **Lloyd Christmas**, the blogger and Twitter persona behind *Bourbon Truth*. The episode tackles marketing tactics, price inflation, private barrel picks, and the culture of bourbon collecting. Christmas critiques distilleries that push prices higher—especially limited editions that have risen from $50 to $350—and questions whether newcomers should buy these pricey bottles or wait for more affordable options.

The show also touches on the broader impact of marketing on consumer perception and the future of craft whiskey in a market flooded with limited‑edition releases.

# 094 – No One Is Safe

- Marketing tactics and price inflation in the bourbon market.
- The rise of limited‑edition bottles and their steep retail prices.
- The impact on new consumers and collectors.
- Whether craft whiskey can compete.

The discussion is framed around Christmas’s criticism of distillery practices and his personal experience as a blogger.

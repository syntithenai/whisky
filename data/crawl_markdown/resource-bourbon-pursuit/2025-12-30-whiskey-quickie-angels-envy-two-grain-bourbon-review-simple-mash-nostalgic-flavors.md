# Angel's Envy Two-Grain Bourbon Review

## Key Facts
- Limited release bourbon from Angel's Envy
- Simple mash bill: 90% corn, 10% malted barley
- Distilled at 125 proof and aged in new charred oak barrels
- Bottled at 112 proof (56% ABV)
- Released as a nostalgic tribute to early American whiskey styles

## Production Signals
- Corn-forward mash bill emphasizes sweet, cereal notes reminiscent of childhood breakfast cereals
- Minimal rye or wheat contributes to the simple, clean flavor profile
- Proof and aging conditions highlight bourbon's foundational elements rather than complexity

## Commercial Signals
- Positioned as an easy-drinking, approachable bourbon for a broad audience
- Limited release suggests exclusivity and collector appeal
- Nostalgic branding taps into sentimentality around classic American whiskey styles

## Risks / Unknowns
- Simple flavor profile may not satisfy connoisseurs seeking complexity
- Small batch production could limit availability and drive up prices
- Proof level balances nostalgia with commercial drinkability

# Tennessee Whiskey SHOCKER

Leiper’s Fork’s **Single Barrel Cask Strength** release (8 y 7 m, 121.6 proof) surprised the reviewers with a rich raspberry‑chocolate mousse profile, deep barrel char and syrupy layers that earned it high praise as one of the best Tennessee whiskeys they’ve tasted.

- **Proof:** 121.6
- **Price:** $85 (per bottle)
- **Age statement:** 8 years 7 months
- **Flavor notes:** raspberry, chocolate mousse, deep char, faint peppermint‑bark nuance
- **Production:** Small‑batch, single barrel, pot‑still‑like finish
- **Reviewers’ rating:** Two thumbs up on taste, thumbs up on finish.

# Bourbon Pursuit

**Bourbon Pursuit** is the official podcast of bourbon, hosted by Kenny Coleman, Ryan Cecil and Fred Minnick. The show features interviews with every part of the bourbon supply chain—from raw materials and distillery operations to cooperages, marketing, regulations (e.g., Ball‑and‑Bond) and consumer issues. Episodes air weekly: a main episode on Thursdays, *Whiskey Quickies* on Tuesdays (60‑second reviews), and *This Week in Bourbon* on Fridays. The podcast also hosts special segments such as **Above the Jar** and the fan‑favourite **Bourbon Community Roundtable**.

The site offers links to episodes sorted by distillery, a shop, partnership information, and a newsletter sign‑up.

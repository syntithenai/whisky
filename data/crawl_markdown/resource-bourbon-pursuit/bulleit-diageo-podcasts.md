# Bulleit and Diageo Podcasts - History and Story of Blade & Bow

- **Key Facts**: 
  - Doug Kragel discusses the history and background of Blade & Bow, a limited release from Diageo's Stitzel-Weller distillery.
  - The podcast explores the origins of Blade & Bow, its creation process, and how it differs from traditional bourbon production.
  - Limited availability and unique characteristics of the 22-year-old release are highlighted.

- **Production Signals**: 
  - Fractional blending vs solera aging techniques used in creating Blade & Bow.
  - Small still at Stitzel-Weller with a focus on maintaining legacy rather than replicating past magic.
  - Partnership between Bulleit and Diageo for Blade & Bow production.

- **Commercial Signals**: 
  - Membership to the Five Keys Club provides exclusive access to Blade & Bow releases.
  - Limited edition 22-year-old release creates buzz among bourbon enthusiasts.
  - Collaboration with Garden & Gun at the distillery adds prestige to Blade & Bow brand positioning.

- **Risks/Unknowns**: 
  - Uncertainty around future Diageo ownership and potential changes in flavor profile post-still closure.
  - Possibility of Bulleit distilled whiskey entering Blade & Bow lineup in the future.

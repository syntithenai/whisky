# October 2025 Highest Auction Sales for Single Bottles of Whisky

The article lists the top 20 single‑bottle hammer prices from October 2025, highlighting a record‑breaking combined value of **$2,691,784**—the highest since November 2023 and up 130% on last year’s figure. The Distillers One‑of‑One auction dominated with 14 of the 20 slots, while other notable sales include Very Very Old Fitzgerald (18 yr Blackhawk) at $85,200 and KANDOBLANC Dragon in Clouds (60 yr) at $113,390.

# Sazerac Eyes Brown‑Forman Merger & Other Industry News

- **Sazerac** is reportedly exploring a merger with **Brown‑Forman**, which is in talks with Pernod Ricard.
- The **Kentucky Bourbon Trail** attracted 2.7 million visitors in 2025, matching 2024 figures.
- **Redemption Whiskey** raised its core bourbon proof to 46 % ABV and relaunched packaging.
- **Brother Justus** rebranded from “American single malt” to “American whiskey.”
- **Silver Brothers** opened a new distillery in Hudson Valley and will launch an American single malt in July.
- **Jack Daniel’s** introduced three sugar‑free canned cocktails (Cherry, Vanilla, Zero Sugar).

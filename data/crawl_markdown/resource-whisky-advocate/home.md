# Whisky Advocate
Whisky Advocate is the leading American whisky magazine and online resource. The site offers news, ratings, reviews, a whisky‑rating database, educational articles (Whisky 101), cocktail recipes, gear guides, travel tips, event coverage (WhiskyFest), and a retailer club. It also publishes videos, a glossary, and a subscription service for the print magazine.

## Key Features
- **News & Reviews** – up‑to‑date coverage of releases, events, and industry news.
- **Ratings Database** – searchable database of over 6 000 whisky ratings.
- **Educational Content** – articles on production, tasting, cocktails, travel, and whisky history.
- **Events** – information about WhiskyFest and other whisky festivals.
- **Retailer Club & Subscription** – membership benefits for retailers and readers.

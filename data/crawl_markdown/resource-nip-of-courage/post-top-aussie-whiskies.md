# 5 Top‑Shelf Aussie Whiskies

Nip of Courage highlights five premium Australian whiskies, each from a distinct distillery and priced between **$155** and **$225**. The list showcases the diversity of Australian craft whisky—from Lark’s cask‑strength single malt to Timboon’s navy‑strength Christie’s Cut, Shene’s sherry‑cask aged Elixir of Life, Belgrove’s oat whisky, and Hobart Whisky’s signature single malt.

- **Lark Cask Strength Single Malt** – 58 % ABV, $200. Rich cinnamon, nutmeg, vanilla, toffee, and Tasmanian peat.
- **Timboon Christie’s Cut** – 60 % ABV, $225. Charred raisins, smoke, spice; aged in ex‑port casks.
- **Shene Elixir of Life** – 49 % ABV, $189. Sherry‑cask finished with intense flavours.
- **Belgrove Oat Whisky** – 52.3 % ABV, $155. Baked strawberry, buttery pastry, oaty honey, burnt toffee.
- **Hobart Whisky Signature** – 47.5 % ABV, $156. Fruit‑driven notes from American oak ex‑bourbon casks.

The article underscores the rapid rise of Australian craft distilling since Bill Lark’s revival in 1992 and celebrates the innovation driving the industry today.

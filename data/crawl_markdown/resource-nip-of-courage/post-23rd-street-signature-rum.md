# 23rd Street Distillery’s Signature Rum

A double‑distilled, cask‑strength gold rum from Renmark, South Australia. The spirit is aged in ex‑bourbon barrels, bottled at strength and then re‑aged in the same casks. It offers bright herbaceous and fruity aromas with caramel, vanilla, lavender and soft sugar notes. On the palate it starts mild, develops a rich mouthfeel, spiciness, stoned fruit, slight woody smokiness and balanced acidity. Ideal as a sipper or in cocktails such as a rum Old Fashioned or Rum Negroni.

* **Distillery** – 23rd Street Distillery (established 2016 on historic site)
* **Bottle** – Cork‑topped, heavy‑bottomed
* **Style** – Gold, double‑distilled, cask‑strength aged
* **Serving** – Water or ice to open oils; cocktails with bitters and simple syrup

# Stone Pine’s Dead Man’s Gold Spiced Rum

- **Distillery**: Stone Pine Distillery, Bathurst, NSW, Australia.
- **Product**: Dead Man’s Gold Spiced Rum – a gold‑colored rum spiced with vanilla, cinnamon myrtle, macadamia and toasted coconut.
- **History & Naming**: Named after the 10 Ribbon Gang bushrangers executed in 1830; bottle labels list their names.
- **Flavor Profile**: Bright golden aroma, toffee‑honey, buttery coconut, warm vanilla, macadamia richness, cinnamon myrtle warmth, oakiness and caramel from barrel aging.
- **ABV**: 40 %.
- **Serving Suggestions**: Neat or on the rocks; works well in old fashioned, dark & stormy, or a splash of orange juice over ice.
- **Purchase Link**: [Buy Dead Man’s Gold](https://www.nipofcourage.com/product-page/stone-pine-dead-man-s-gold-40-700ml).

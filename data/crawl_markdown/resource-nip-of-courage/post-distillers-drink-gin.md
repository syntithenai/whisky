# How Do Aussie Craft Distillers Drink Gin?

The article surveys four Australian craft gin distillers—Chris Cameron (Naught Distillery), Ally Ayres (Karu Distillery), Ian Glen (Stone Pine Distillery) and Ed Svehla (Banks & Solander Distillery)—to learn how they enjoy their own gins. Each distiller shares a favourite cocktail or serving style, highlighting the versatility of gin.

- **Naught Distillery**: Loves the 2022 *Sangiovese Gin* in a Charlie Chaplin.
- **Karu Distillery**: Prefers *Lightning Gin* in a G&T with pineapple and chilli; also favours *Affinity Gin* for cocktails.
- **Stone Pine Distillery**: Sticks to a classic London‑dry gin & tonic with lime.
- **Banks & Solander Distillery**: Enjoys the *Signature Gin* in a martini or G&T, and mixes it with Limoncello and soda.

# Classic Gin Cocktails

This post explains how to make three iconic gin cocktails—**Martini**, **Negroni**, and **Gimlet**—using Australian craft gins. It highlights suitable distilleries, ingredient ratios, and optional variations.

- **Classic Martini**: 75 ml Four Pillars Olive Leaf Gin + 20 ml dry vermouth; garnish with olives or a twist.
- **Negroni**: 30 ml Anther Goddess Strength Gin + 30 ml vermouth rosso + 30 ml bitter orange (Applewood Okar Island Bitter).
- **Gimlet**: 60 ml Stone Pine Original Dry Gin + 30 ml fresh lime juice + 20 ml simple syrup.

Each recipe includes stirring or shaking instructions and optional twists such as a dirty martini, negroni bianco, or Southside.

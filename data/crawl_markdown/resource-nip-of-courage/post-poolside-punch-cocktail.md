Here’s how to make a Poolside Punch. With so many influences from other classic cocktails, there were hundreds of names we considered for this drink. But we’ve called this drink a punch because of the five ingredients (six if you include the garnish) and their relationship with the flavours a classic punch needs. The word ‘punch’ comes from the Hindi word for ‘five’ (probably why the same word is used for a good ol’ five-knuckle sandwich too), but in drinks terms, a punch consists of five ingredients that deliver five important elements that create balance in the cocktail: Those five flavour elements are: sweetness, sourness, spirit, spice and water. And many of the parts of this cocktail work double time across different flavour profiles. So this cocktail recipe really works well as a punch.

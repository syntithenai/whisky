# Starward Distillery’s Fortis Whisky

- **Distillery**: Starward, founded by David Vitale in 2009.
- **Product**: Fortis Single Malt Whisky (700 ml, 50% ABV).
- **Maturation**: Two‑year ageing in American oak ex‑red‑wine casks from Barossa and Victoria.
- **Mash**: Australian malted barley.
- **Tasting notes**: Blackberries, brown sugar, caramelised figs, oaky vanilla on the nose; ripe red fruit, figs, vanilla sweetness with sour cherry and spicy heat in the mouth; dry finish with a slight tingle.
- **Serving**: Neat or with water; works well in an old‑fashioned or boulevardier.

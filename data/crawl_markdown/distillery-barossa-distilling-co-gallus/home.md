# Barossa Distilling Co
Barossa Distilling Co, founded in 2016 in the Barossa Valley, Australia, is an award‑winning distillery that produces a range of spirits including gin, vodka, liqueurs, whisky, brandy and cocktails. The company operates out of the historic Old Penfolds Distillery at Provenance Barossa, offering tasting rooms, cocktail bars, tours, recipes, custom‑labelled spirits and corporate gifts.

- **Location**: 18–28 Tanunda Road, Nuriootpa SA 5355
- **Products**: Spirits (gin, vodka, whisky, brandy), premix drinks, cocktails, gift packs, merchandise
- **Services**: Distillery tours, custom‑labelled spirits, corporate gifts, cocktail recipes
- **Awards**: Multiple international and regional spirit awards

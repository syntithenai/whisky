# The Distillery at Barossa Distilling Co.

## History & Significance
- Opened in August 2017 in the Old Penfolds Distillery on Provenance Barossa site
- Home to some of Australia's oldest commercial stills, including a Coffey Still (only three in the world)
- Rich history of distillation associated with Barossa Bar

## Tasting Room & Cocktail Bar
- Open four days a week for tastings of gins, whisky, liqueurs and cocktails
- Large range of craft spirits and cocktails available
- Step-by-step tasting experiences to appreciate flavors and impact of mixers

## Distillery Tours & Gift Cards
- Behind-the-scenes tours of production distillery
- Exclusive preview of upcoming spirits
- $10 off full-sized bottle purchase with tour booking
- Gift cards available for gifting

## Events & Venue Hire
- Private function venue hire (Copper Club tasting room)
- Outdoor space available in warmer months
- Catering from Ember Pizza and local Barossa wines/beer

## Long Description
# Barossa Distilling Co

Barossa Distilling Co (often referred to as Gallus) was founded in 2016 in the heart of South Australia’s Barossa Valley. The company operates from the historic Old Penfolds Distillery at Provenance Barossa, offering a full‑service experience that includes tasting rooms, cocktail bars, distillery tours and custom‑labelled spirit production.

## Product Range
The distillery produces a diverse portfolio: gin (notably the **Barossa Shiraz Gin** and its barrel‑aged variant), vodka, whisky, brandy, liqueurs, premix drinks and cocktails. All spirits are crafted in small batches with an emphasis on local terroir and barrel‑aging science.

## Barrel‑Aging Focus
Two blog posts highlight the science behind barrel aging for gin: *The Making of Barossa Shiraz Gin* and *The secrets behind our Barrel Aged Shiraz Gin*. These pieces describe how selected barrels impart “inky depth” and “incredible richness” to the final spirit.

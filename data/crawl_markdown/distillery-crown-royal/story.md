# Crown Royal Whisky
- **Origin**: First created in 1939 as a gift for the King and Queen of England.
- **Location**: Blended Canadian whisky produced in Gimli, Manitoba on the western shore of Lake Winnipeg.
- **Blend**: Fifty different whiskies are blended to achieve Crown Royal’s signature smoothness.

## Key Points
- Crown Royal is a blended Canadian whisky.
- The brand was launched in 1939 for British royalty.
- Production takes place in Gimli, Manitoba.
- A blend of fifty whiskies creates the final product.

# Crown Royal – Generosity

Crown Royal, founded in 1939 and produced in Gimli, Manitoba, is celebrated for its smooth Canadian whisky. The site showcases a range of products: the classic **Deluxe** blend, flavored variants (Blackberry, Apple, Peach, Vanilla, Chocolate), aged Master Series expressions (12 YR Reserve to 31 YR), ready‑to‑drink cocktails, and limited‑edition collaborations such as **Realtree**. The brand promotes its *Generosity Fund* and the *Purple Bag Project*, which sends care packages to soldiers and veterans. Additional offerings include whisky gifts, personalized gift sets, and a community hub.

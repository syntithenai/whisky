## Long Description
# Crown Royal

Crown Royal is a flagship Canadian whisky brand founded in 1939 and produced at the Gimli Distillery in Manitoba. The website showcases a broad portfolio that includes:
- **Deluxe** – the signature blend celebrated for its smoothness.
- **Flavor Series** – fruit‑and‑spice‑infused whiskies such as Blackberry, Apple, Peach, Vanilla and Chocolate.
- **Master Series** – aged expressions ranging from 12 YR Reserve to 31 YR, each highlighting the maturation depth of Canadian whisky.
- **Ready‑to‑Drink** cocktails (Whisky & Cola, Washington Apple, Whisky Lemonade) for convenient on‑the‑go enjoyment.
- **Limited‑Edition Collaborations** – notably the Crown Royal × Realtree edition and the Purple Bag Project, which sends care packages to soldiers and veterans.

The site also promotes its *Generosity Fund*, a community initiative aimed at supporting bars, clubs and cultural venues across Canada. Visitors can purchase whisky gifts, personalized gift sets, and participate in the Purple Bag Project directly through the e‑commerce platform.

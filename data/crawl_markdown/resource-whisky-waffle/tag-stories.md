## Distillery Facts
- Tasmanian whisky history dates back to the 1820s when landowners were allowed to produce whisky under Governor’s policy.
- The first Tasmanian distilleries were shut down by Governor Franklin a few years later.
- Modern independent bottlers include Lark, Belgrove, Redlands Estate, and McHenry’s Distillery.

## Resource Facts
- Brett Steel founded Tasmanian Whisky Tours in 2014 to provide guided tours of Tasmanian distilleries.
- Tours run on Wednesdays, Fridays, and Sundays, starting at the Lark cellar door.
- The tours emphasize storytelling alongside tasting.

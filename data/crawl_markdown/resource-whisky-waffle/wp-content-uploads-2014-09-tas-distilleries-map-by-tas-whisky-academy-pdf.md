# Tasmanian Distilleries Map

A PDF map from the **Tasmanian Whisky Academy** lists 24 active distilleries across Tasmania, each with a brief address or city and an online presence (website or Facebook page). The document also notes additional cellar doors/outlets in Hobart for several distilleries.

## Distillery List
1. Hellyers Road – Burnie
2. Southern Wild – Devonport
3. Wilmot Hills – Wilmot
4. Fannys Bay – Fannys Bay
5. Corra Linn – Launceston
6. Launceston – Prospect
7. Adams – Perth
8. Ironhouse Brewery/Distillery – 4 Mile Creek
9. Nant – Bothwell
10. Belgrove – Kempton
11. Redlands – Kempton
12. Shene Estate – Pontville
13. Sullivan’s Cove – Cambridge
14. Lark – Cambridge
15. Devils – Moonah
16. Heartwood Whisky – Hobart
17. Deviant – Hobart
18. Overeem – Kingston
19. Hartshorn – Birchs Bay (vodka)
20. Nonesuch – Dodges Ferry
21. Spring Bay – Orford
22. McHenry – Port Arthur
23. Trappers Hut – Bruny Island
24. Willie Smiths – Huonville (cider/calvados)

Additional cellar doors in Hobart are listed for Lark, Hartshorn, McHenry and Nant.

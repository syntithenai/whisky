# About Whisky Waffle
Whisky Waffle is a Tasmanian‑based whisky blog run by two young enthusiasts, **Nick Turner** and **Alex “Ted” Matthews**. They share their love for Tasmanian whiskies, offer personal tasting sessions, and feature guest contributors such as Alex Mooresy, Richard Stewart, Chris C., and William “Stretch” Borthwick.

- Nick is a music teacher in Penguin who discovered whisky after trying Lagavulin 16.
- Ted works as a Biosecurity Inspector in Burnie and writes about how bio‑security impacts grain quality for Tasmanian distilleries.
- The site hosts reviews, podcasts, musings, news, and event listings.

The page also contains a comment section with user interactions from 2014‑2022.

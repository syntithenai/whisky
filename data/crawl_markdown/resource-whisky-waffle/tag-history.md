## Distillery Facts
- **Bushmills** – Ireland’s oldest licensed distillery (est. 1608).
- **Cooley** – Founded in 2005, located in County Louth.
- **Midleton (Irish Distillers)** – Consolidated Irish whiskey production; owns Jameson.
- **Lagavulin** – Islay distillery founded 1816, owned by Diageo.
- **Ardbeg** – Islay distillery revived in the 1980s; known for peaty single malts.
- **Buffalo Trace** – Kentucky bourbon distillery founded 1787.
- **Eriskay** – Australian‑bottled blend of Highland and Lowland whiskies.

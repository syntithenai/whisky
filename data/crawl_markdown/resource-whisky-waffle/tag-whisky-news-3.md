# Whisky Waffle – Inver House Green Plaid Review

- **Product**: Inver House Green Plaid Scotch whisky (sub‑$40 AUD)
- **Taste profile**: Fresh, bright with grain, pear, apricot, grass and hazelnuts; rough mouthfeel but not harsh. Notes of Lisbon lemons, butterscotch and wood polish.
- **Production insight**: Inver House Distillers Pty Ltd owns several distilleries (Pulteney, Balblair, Knockdhu, Speyburn, Balmenach). The blend may draw from these sources.
- **Verdict**: Good value for a budget dram; suitable for straight sipping or mixing with coke. Not award‑winning but enjoyable.

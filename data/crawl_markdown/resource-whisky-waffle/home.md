# Whisky Waffle Awards 2020

## Top Tasmanian Whiskies
- **Isle of the Drammed Award**: Heartwood A Serious Whisky - Rich, peated and fortified cask aged.
- **Drams Down Under Award**: From Country to Coast Blackfleurieugate - Peated spirit from Black Gate and Fleurieu combined in a unique blend.
- **Tartan Slipper Award**: Ardbeg Corryvreckan - Bold, peaty Islay-style whisky with peak character.

## Australian Mainland Whiskies
- **Drams Down Under Award**: Corowa Characters - Aussie duet of coastal peated malt and Fleurieu peat aged in different casks.

## Scottish Whiskies
- **Tartan Slipper Award**: Ardbeg Corryvreckan - Classic Islay-style peated whisky with peak character.

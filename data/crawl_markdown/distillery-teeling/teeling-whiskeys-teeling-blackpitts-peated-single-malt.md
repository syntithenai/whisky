# Teeling Blackpitts Peated Single Malt Whiskey

Inspired by the historic Blackpitts area in Dublin, this peated single malt uses smoke from peat to dry the barley during malting. Triple distilled and matured in ex-bourbon and ex-Sauternes casks, it offers a unique smokey taste with notes of BBQ smoke, cloves, butterscotch, orange peel, applewood smoke, honey, all spice, stewed pears & grilled pineapple, salted caramel, wood, and savory smoke. Bottled at 46% ABV without chill filtration.

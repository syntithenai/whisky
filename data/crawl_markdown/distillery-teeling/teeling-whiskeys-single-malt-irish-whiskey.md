# Teeling Single Malt Irish Whiskey

## About
- Unique, award-winning single malt whiskey from Dublin's first distillery in over a century
- Crafted using innovative cask aging techniques and five different wine casks (Virgin American Oak, Ruby Port, Caravelos White Port, Madeira, Bourbon)
- Triple distilled from 100% malted barley
- Non-chill filtered for maximum flavor retention

## Flavor Profile
- Vibrant fruity aroma with tropical melon notes and hints of chocolate
- Smooth start develops into a full bouquet of fruit flavors (bitter-sweet chocolate, soft spice, mixed berries)
- Luxurious length with oak undertones and lingering tropical passionfruit finish

## ABV & Details
- 46% alcohol by volume
- Triple distilled, non-chill filtered

## Distillery Highlights
- First distillery in Dublin since the 19th century
- Uses unconventional cask maturation methods (five different wine casks)
- Aims to create a "symphony" of flavor through innovative aging techniques

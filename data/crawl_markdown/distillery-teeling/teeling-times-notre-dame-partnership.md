# Notre Dame Partnership – Teeling Whiskey
Teeling, the world‑most awarded Irish whiskey brand, has entered a four‑year partnership with Notre Dame Global Partnerships to become the Official Irish Whiskey Partner of Notre Dame Fans in the U.S. The collaboration will launch after Teeling’s sponsorship of the Notre Dame vs. Navy game and includes joint responsible‑drinking campaigns, fan experiences, merchandising, and new bottle releases.

## Key Points
- **Partnership launch**: August 26th, 2024 (Notre Dame vs. Navy game).
- **Co‑founder quote**: Stephen Teeling highlights shared Irish heritage and commitment to quality.
- **Athletics partner**: Notre Dame Deputy Athletics Director Jim Fraleigh emphasizes moderation messaging.
- **Campaign focus**: Responsible drinking, branded experiences, radio/print promotion.
- **Teeling accolades**: World’s Best at World Whiskies Awards (2019‑2023) and 500+ international awards.
- **Sustainability**: Core to Teeling’s philosophy since 2015.

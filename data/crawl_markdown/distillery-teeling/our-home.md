# Teeling Whiskey Distillery Experience

Teeling Whiskey opened its first new Dublin distillery in 2015, reviving the city’s historic whiskey heritage after a 125‑year hiatus. Located in the Liberties quarter, the distillery produces a range of unconventional Irish whiskies—including single malt, single grain, single pot still, and peated single malt—alongside limited‑edition bottlings such as the Sommelier Selection Rioja and the Blackpitts Peated Single Malt. The brand emphasizes sustainability, has earned over 500 international awards, and partners with Notre Dame Global Partnerships.

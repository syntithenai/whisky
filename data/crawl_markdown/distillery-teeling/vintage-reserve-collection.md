# Teeling Vintage Reserve Collection

Teeling’s Vintage Reserve Collection showcases a series of hand‑selected casks, each finished in unique wine or port barrels to create rare Irish single malts aged up to 40 years. Highlights include:
- **Rising Reserve No. 1–4**: 21‑year‑old single malts finished in ex‑Carcavelos White Port, ex‑Marsala, ex‑Ruby Port and white wine casks respectively.
- **30‑Year‑Old Single Malt**: First distilled 1991, matured in bourbon then Sauternes wine casks.
- **32‑Year‑Old Rivesaltes**: Very rare cask collection with a dark chocolate & dried fruit nose.
- **40‑Year‑Old Single Malt**: Aged exclusively in an ex‑American Bourbon barrel; only 140 bottles released.

All releases are bottled at 46 % ABV (except the 40‑year‑old at 42.2 %) with no chill filtration and feature complex citrus, fruit, spice, oak and chocolate notes.

The page also lists Teeling’s broader product lines: Unconventional Collection, Small Batch, Single Grain, Single Malt, Single Pot Still, and Blackpitts Peated Single Malt.

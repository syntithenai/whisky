# Teeling Vintage Reserve Collection

## A SERIES OF HAND-SELECTED CASKS
Specially chosen by the Teeling family for their distinctive characteristics, offering Whiskey enthusiasts some of the finest and rarest Irish Single Malt Whiskeys available today.

### VINTAGE RESERVE COLLECTION
**Rising Reserve No. 1**
- Consists of 21-Year-Old Single Malt aged in ex-Bourbon barrels for over 16 years, then finished in high-quality ex-Carcavelos White Port casks for over 5 years.
- Bottled at 46% ABV with no chill filtration. Limited to just 6000 bottles.
- **NOSE**: Green apples, honeyed citrus and candyfloss
- **PALATE**: Stewed fruits with deep background of caramel, wood tannins and cinnamon spice
- **FINISH**: Wood notes and rich roasted cacao

**Rising Reserve No. 2**
- Consists of 21-Year-Old Single Malt aged in ex-bourbon barrels for over 18 years, then finished in high-quality ex-Marsala wine casks for over 3 years.
- Bottled at 46% ABV with no chill filtration. Limited to just 6,000 bottles.
- **NOSE**: Mellow vanilla sweetness, toasted apricots, tobacco, spiced fruits
- **PALATE**: Brown sugar and stewed fruits, oak tannins and cinnamon spice
- **FINISH**: Honeysuckle, rich demerara sugar, citrus & spice

**Rising Reserve No. 3**
- Consists of our 21-Year-Old Single Malt initially aged in ex-bourbon barrels for 18 years, before being finished in high-quality ex-Ruby Port wine casks for 3 years.
- Bottled at 46% ABV with no chill filtration. Limited release.
- **NOSE**: Burst of caramel, red dried fruits, sandalwood
- **PALATE**: Brown sugar, chocolate trifle, Christmas cake, plum
- **FINISH**: Golden syrup, soft tannins, cooked barley, oak spice

**Rising Reserve No. 4**
- Consists of our 21-Year-Old Single Malt initially aged in ex-bourbon barrels for 18 years, then finished in high-quality White Wine Casks for 3 years.
- Bottled at 46% ABV with no chill filtration. Limited release.
- **NOSE**: Candied lemons, vanilla, cookie dough, toffee with hints of toasted barley
- **PALATE**: Bright tropical fruits, soft tannins, citrus and white chocolate
- **FINISH**: Long mellow finish, with complex undertones of orchard fruits and honey.

## LIMITED EDITION MATURATIONS
**30 Year Old Single Malt**
- First distilled in 1991, matured in Bourbon casks then allowed to marry in Sauternes wine casks for a wholly unique Irish Whiskey taste experience.
- Bottled at 46% ABV with no chill filtration. Fresh orchard fruits, roasted nuts and chocolate on the nose; velvety smooth peach, apricot, citrus fruits, smoke and herbal on the palate; long, lingering fruit finish with spicy oak and smoked sea salt.

**32 Year Old Rivesaltes Very Rare Cask**
- Part of Teeling Whiskey's award-winning Very Rare Cask Collection. This limited edition single malt is aged exclusively in an ex-American Bourbon barrel and bottled at 42.2% ABV with no chill filtration.
- **NOSE**: Dark Chocolate, dried fruit, vanilla, range peel
- **PALATE**: Almond, honey sweetness, dried figs and walnuts
- **FINISH**: Long with dry fruits

**40 Year Old Single Malt**
- A testament to progress marking a milestone in maturation as one of the oldest Irish Single Malts and a true Modern Classic.
- Consisting of 100% malted barley, aged exclusively in an ex-American Bourbon barrel and bottled at 42.2% ABV with no chill filtration.
- **NOSE**: Light berry compote, spearmint, candied orange zest
- **PALATE**: Coconut, milk chocolate, peach sorbet, honeydew melon, tangerine
- **FINISH**: Subtle spice, soft oak tannins, honey

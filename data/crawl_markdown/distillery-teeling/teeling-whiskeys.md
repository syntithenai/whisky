# Teeling Whiskey – Dublin Distillery

Teeling offers a range of Irish whiskeys across several collections:
- **Unconventional Collection**: Small Batch, Single Grain, Single Malt, Single Pot Still, Blackpitts Peated Single Malt.
- **Limited‑Edition Bottlings**: Sommelier Selection Rioja, Blackpitts Cask Strength, Wonders of Wood series.
- **Vintage Reserve Collection**: Hand‑selected casks including Rising Reserve and 30‑year‑old single malt.

Each product is described by its mash bill, distillation method, and cask maturation style.

## Source Excerpts
- LEARN MORE SINGLE POT STILL Teeling Single Pot Still Irish Whiskey is crafted using a uniquely Irish mash bill consisting of 50% malted and 50% unmalted barley that has been triple distilled and matured in a combination of American Virgin Oak, Bourbon, and Sherry casks.
- Hand selected casks of grain and malt whiskey are initially fully aged in ex-bourbon barrels, then married together in Central American rum casks for up to 12 months for a unique, dried fruit profile.
- LEARN MORE SINGLE MALT Teeling Single Malt is made with Irish Whiskeys distilled from 100% malted barley, matured in five oak varieties (Bourbon, Virgin American Oak, Madeira, Carcevelos, White Wine Burgundy).
- This Irish Single Malt Whiskey delivers a unique fruit-forward flavour profile that is complex, yet balanced and has often been described as “a symphony” of flavour.
- LEARN MORE Drag to discover more OUR WHISKEYS Limited Edition Bottlings Limited edition bottlings of Teeling Whiskey which showcase our innovative approach to crafting Irish Whiskey through partner collaborations and unconventional cask maturation techniques.
- LEARN MORE SINGLE GRAIN Teeling Single Grain Irish Whiskey is crafted using an unconventional mash bill consisting of 95% corn and 5% malted barley that has been column distilled and matured exclusively in American Red Wine casks, imparting a highly distinct red-berry and tannic profile.

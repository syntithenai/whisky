# Teeling Whiskey

## Our Whiskeys
### Unconventional Collection
- **Small Batch**: A celebration of innovation, hand-selected casks of grain and malt whiskey aged in ex-bourbon barrels then married in Central American rum casks for a unique dried fruit profile.
- **Single Grain**: Crafted from 95% corn and 5% malted barley column distilled and matured exclusively in American Red Wine casks, delivering a highly distinct red-berry and tannic flavor.
- **Single Malt**: Made with Irish Whiskeys distilled from 100% malted barley, matured in five oak varieties (Bourbon, Virgin American Oak, Madeira, Carcevelos, White Wine Burgundy) for a complex, fruit-forward profile described as a "symphony" of flavors.
- **Single Pot Still**: Triple distilled and matured in ex-bourbon, ex-sauternes white wine casks, showcasing its signature barbeque smoke taste.

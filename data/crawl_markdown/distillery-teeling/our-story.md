# Teeling Whiskey History

- Founded in Dublin by Walter Teeling in 1782, continuing the family tradition of whiskey making.
- Jack and Stephen Teeling revived the brand in 2012 and opened a new distillery in 2015 at the original site.
- The distillery is located in Dublin's Liberties district, known as the historic distilling area.

# Our Whiskeys

- **Unconventional Collection**: Includes Single Grain, Single Malt, Single Pot Still, Blackpitts, and Peated Single Malt.
- **Limited Editions**: Rioja Cask Strength Wonders of Wood #1-4, Vintage Reserve No. 1-4, Rising Reserve Nos. 1-4, and 30/32 Year Old single malts.

# Our Story & Distillery

- Heart of Dublin distillery in the Golden Triangle, built for both production and tourism.
- Open to visitors for tours, sampling, and purchasing exclusive distillery items.

# Innovation & Craftsmanship

- Emphasis on innovation: atypical mash bills, unique maturation techniques, and modern distillation methods.
- No chill filtration before bottling to preserve the whiskey's natural character.
- Signature ABV of 46% for cask strength delivery.

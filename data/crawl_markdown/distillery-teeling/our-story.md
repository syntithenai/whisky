# Teeling History

Teeling Whiskey traces its roots back to 1782 when Walter Teeling founded a small distillery on Marrowbone Lane in Dublin. The modern brand was revived by Jack and Stephen Teeling, who opened the new Teeling Distillery in 2015 – the first new Dublin distillery in over 125 years.

The distillery sits in the Liberties, part of Dublin’s historic Golden Triangle. It is open to visitors for tours and tastings. Teeling prides itself on an unconventional approach: atypical mash bills, innovative maturation techniques, no chill‑filtration, and bottling at 46 % ABV from cask strength.

Sustainability is a core value; the company claims leadership in eco‑friendly practices within Irish whiskey production. Partnerships include Notre Dame Global Partnerships as the official Irish whiskey partner for US fans. Teeling has earned more than 500 international awards, including World’s Best Single Malt, Single Grain and Single Pot Still.

# Teeling History

Teeling Whiskey traces its roots back to 1782 when Walter Teeling founded a small distillery on Marrowbone Lane in Dublin. The modern brand was launched by Jack and Stephen Teeling in 2012, culminating in the opening of the new Teeling Distillery in 2015 – the first new Dublin distillery in over 125 years.

The distillery sits in the Liberties, the historic Golden Triangle of Irish whiskey production. It prides itself on an unconventional approach: atypical mash bills, innovative maturation techniques, no chill‑filtration and bottling at 46 % ABV from cask strength to preserve character.

Sustainability is a core value; Teeling claims leadership in eco‑friendly practices within the Irish whiskey sector. The company also partners with Notre Dame Global Partnerships as the official Irish whiskey partner for US fans.

> *“Since our inauguration as a brand over a decade ago, we have quickly become the World’s Most Awarded Irish Whiskey with over 500 international accolades.”*

# Teeling Whiskey

Teeling Whiskey is a small‑batch distillery in Dublin that blends traditional Irish whiskey techniques with unconventional methods of distillation and maturation. The brand offers a range of single grain, single malt, single pot still, and peated single malt bottlings, many of which are limited editions or part of the “Unconventional Collection.”  Teeling has earned numerous international awards and promotes responsible drinking.

## Key Points
- First new distillery in Dublin in 125 years.
- Focus on innovation: small‑batch production, unique cask finishes (e.g., Rioja, Blackpitts), and peated single malt.
- Offers cocktails created by the distillery’s bar team.
- Emphasises sustainability and responsible consumption.

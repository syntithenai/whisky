# Teeling Whiskey – Small Batch, Big Flavour
Teeling Whiskey is a Dublin‑based distillery that produces small‑batch single grain, single malt, single pot still and peated single malt whiskies. The brand emphasises innovation, unconventional techniques in distillation and maturation, and has earned numerous international awards.

## Key Points
- First new distillery in Dublin in 125 years.
- Offers a range of products: Unconventional Collection, Limited‑Edition Bottlings, Sommelier Selection, Wonders of Wood series, Vintage Reserve, Rising Reserve, and age‑specific single malts.
- Provides cocktails created by the distillery’s bar team.
- Focuses on sustainability and responsible drinking.

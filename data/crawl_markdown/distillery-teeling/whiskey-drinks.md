# Teeling Whiskey Drinks & Cocktails
Teeling offers a range of Irish whiskies—including Small Batch, Single Grain, Single Malt, Single Pot Still, and the Blackpitts Peated Single Malt—alongside limited‑edition bottlings such as Sommelier Selection Rioja and Vintage Reserve. The page lists cocktail recipes that pair Teeling Small Batch with ingredients like ginger beer, lime juice, honey syrup, and various liqueurs.

# Teeling Whiskey Sustainability

Teeling Whiskey, founded in 2015 in Dublin, has positioned sustainability at the core of its operations. The distillery implements water‑saving measures (rainwater harvesting and an onsite well), energy optimisation (excess heat for visitor centre heating, PV solar panels supplying up to 30 % of electricity needs) and waste reduction (75 % recycled glass bottles, repurposed spent grain as animal feed, reused barrels). It sources malted barley from Minch Malt and un‑malted barley from Glanbia, both Origin Green certified, and is a verified Origin Green member. Social sustainability initiatives include local business partnerships, community projects such as foresting with native Irish trees, and charitable support for education, healthcare and welfare.

The company has been awarded Ireland’s most sustainable distillery and recognises the importance of continuous improvement in environmental stewardship.

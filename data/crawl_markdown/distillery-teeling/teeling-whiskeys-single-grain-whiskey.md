# Teeling Single Grain Irish Whiskey

Teeling’s award‑winning **Single Grain** is a 46 % ABV, triple‑distilled spirit made from a mash bill of 95 % corn and 5 % malted barley. It is matured exclusively in American red wine casks, giving it a distinct red‑berry, tannic profile with subtle spice and sweetness. The whiskey is non‑chill filtered and has a silky mouthfeel that finishes short, dry and tannic.

## Tasting Notes
- **Nose**: Red grape, berry, spice, sweetness
- **Palate**: Smooth, spicy, berry
- **Finish**: Unique, tannic, short, dry

The spirit is crafted by Jack & Stephen Teeling with master distiller Alex Chasko.

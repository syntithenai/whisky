# Teeling Single Grain Irish Whiskey

## Overview
- Award-winning single grain whiskey crafted by Teeling Whiskey
- Distinctive style with 95% corn and 5% malted barley mash bill
- Triple distilled and matured in American Red Wine casks

## Flavor Profile
- **Nose**: Red grape, berry, spice, sweetness
- **Palate**: Smooth, spicy, berry notes balancing sweet undertones
- **Finish**: Tannic, short, dry encouraging another sip

## ABV & Details
- 46% alcohol by volume
- Triple distilled, non-chill filtered

## Distillery Notes
- Created by Jack Teeling and Master Distiller Alex Chasko
- Blends corn whiskey with aged malt whiskey for unique character

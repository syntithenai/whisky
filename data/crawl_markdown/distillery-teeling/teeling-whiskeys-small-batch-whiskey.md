# Teeling Small Batch Irish Whiskey

## Overview
- Premium blended Irish whiskey with natural character and unconventional cask maturation techniques
- 46% ABV, non-chill filtered, full of natural character

## Tasting Notes
- Floral and herbal notes with hints of vanilla, rum raisin, all spice
- Creamy vanilla sweetness, dried fruit (raisin, apricot), slight tingling spice
- Long, warming finish with lingering sweetness, spice, oak notes

## Production Details
- Hand-selected casks of grain and malt whiskey initially aged in Ex-Bourbon barrels
- Then married together in Central American Rum casks for up to 12 months
- Triple distilled, non-chill filtered

## Flavor Profile
- **NOSE**: Floral, vanilla sweetness, rum-raisin, allspice
- **PALATE**: Creamy, dried fruits, warming spice
- **FINISH**: Long warming finish with balanced oak and spice

## Distillery & History
- Teeling Whiskey Distillery in Dublin, Ireland
- Founded by Jack Teeling and Master Distiller Alex Chasko
- Unconventional approach to creating a premium blended Irish whiskey

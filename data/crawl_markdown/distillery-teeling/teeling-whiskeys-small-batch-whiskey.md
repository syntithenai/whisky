# Teeling Small Batch Irish Whiskey

Teeling’s **Small Batch** is a premium blended Irish whiskey crafted with an unconventional approach. It is triple‑distilled, non‑chill filtered and bottled at 46 % ABV. The spirit is aged in hand‑selected grain and malt casks: first fully matured in ex‑bourbon barrels, then married for up to 12 months in Central American rum casks, giving a dried‑fruit, raisin‑and‑apricot profile with vanilla, allspice and subtle rum notes. The finish is long, warming, with oak, spice and lingering sweetness.

> **Tasting Notes** – Floral & herbal nose; creamy vanilla sweetness; dried fruit (raisin, apricot); allspice; long, warming finish with oak and spice.

# Teeling Limited Edition Bottlings
Teeling’s limited‑edition range showcases experimental maturation and cask collaborations:
- **Sommelier Selection Rioja** – Small Batch aged in ex‑Rioja red wine casks, bottled at 46 % ABV, no chill filtration.
- **Blackpitts Cask Strength Peated Single Malt** – Dublin‑distilled, matured only in ex‑Bourbon casks, bottled at 56.5 % ABV, no chill filtration.
- **Wonders of Wood Series (1–4)** – Triple‑distilled single pot stills aged in virgin Portuguese, Swedish, Carpathian or other oak types; all bottled at 50 % ABV, no chill filtration.
All expressions emphasize unconventional wood choices and unique flavor profiles such as smoky earthiness, fruit‑spice blends, and tannic notes.

## Flavor Highlights
- **Sommelier Selection**: strawberry compote, cherry, cinnamon spice, caramel sweetness; dark berries, butterscotch, black pepper, nutmeg.
- **Blackpitts**: smokey earthiness, orchard fruit, maple syrup; charred apple, pear, peat smoke, salt, lavender.
- **Wonders of Wood #1–4**: sweet toffee, butterscotch, caramel biscuit, banana bread, ginger spices, wood tannins, citrus fruits, candied ginger, almond, treacle, rosewater, campfire smoke.

All bottlings are non‑chill filtered and feature high ABV levels (46 %–56.5 %).

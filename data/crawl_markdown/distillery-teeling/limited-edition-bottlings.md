# Teeling Limited Edition Bottlings
Teeling’s limited‑edition range showcases experimental maturation and cask collaborations:
- **Sommelier Selection Rioja** – Small Batch aged in ex‑Rioja red wine casks, bottled at 46 % ABV, no chill filtration.
- **Blackpitts Cask Strength Peated Single Malt** – Dublin‑distilled, matured exclusively in ex‑bourbon casks, bottled at 56.5 % ABV, no chill filtration.
- **Wonders of Wood Series (1–4)** – Triple‑distilled single pot stills aged in virgin Portuguese, Swedish, and Carpathian oak; all bottled at 50 % ABV, no chill filtration.
Each expression highlights unique wood or wine influences, with detailed nose/palate/finish descriptors.

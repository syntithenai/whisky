# Teeling Limited Edition Bottlings

Teeling Whiskey showcases innovative approaches to crafting Irish whiskey through partner collaborations and unconventional cask maturation techniques.

## Key Facts
- Unconventional Collection: Small Batch Single Grain, Single Malt, Single Pot Still, Blackpitts Peated Single Malt
- Limited Edition Bottlings: Sommelier Selection Rioja, Blackpitts Cask Strength Peated Single Malt, Wonders of Wood Series
- Vintage Reserve Collection: Rising Reserve Nos. 1-4, 30 Year Old Single Malt, 32 Year Old Rivesaltes, 40 Year Old Single Malt

## Production Signals
- Triple distilled at the Teeling Whiskey Distillery
- Matured in unique cask types (Rioja, Bourbon, Sherry, Red Wine)
- No chill filtration for some limited editions

## Commercial Signals
- Partner collaborations with Spanish Rioja winemakers and Swedish Oak cooperatives
- Focus on unconventional maturation techniques using different wood species
- Limited release nature of many bottlings

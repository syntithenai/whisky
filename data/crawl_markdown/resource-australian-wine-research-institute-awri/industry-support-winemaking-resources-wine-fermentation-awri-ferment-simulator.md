# AWRI Ferment Simulator
The Australian Wine Research Institute (AWRI) offers a free online tool for modelling wine fermentation. The simulator allows winemakers to predict the effects of temperature, nutrients, mixing and re‑inoculation on up to 100 ferments using a traffic‑light display. It includes “What‑If” analysis so producers can test corrective actions before applying them in the winery. An older Excel version is still available for download, but the online simulator replaces it.

Key features:
- Model temperature, nutrients, mixing and re‑inoculation.
- Predict up to 100 ferments (Full) or three (Lite).
- Traffic‑light display of ferment health.
- “What‑If” analysis for corrective strategies.
- Free download for Australian producers.

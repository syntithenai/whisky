# The Australian Wine Research Institute (AWRI)

## Key Facts
- Established in 1987 as the national research organization for the wine and grape industries.
- Conducts applied research, development, and extension projects to improve winemaking practices and sustainability.
- Operates a range of services including Winemaking, Viticulture, Regulatory Information, Sustainable Winegrowing Australia, and Education Programs.

## Production Signals

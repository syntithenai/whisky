# Forty Creek Whisky Guild

Forty Creek offers a three‑part whisky education program called the **Whisky Guild**. Participants learn cocktail techniques, global whisky knowledge and the art of aging with the distillery’s Master Blender. Each course includes hands‑on tastings, guided discussions, and a certification upon completion.

- **Course 1 – Whisky Cocktail Making**: Learn glassware, garnishes, mixing techniques; craft a smoked maple old fashioned and bespoke whisky sour.
- **Course 2 – The World of Whisky with Forty Creek**: Guided by Brand Ambassador Chris Thompson; explore global whiskies, Canadian style, and aroma‑taste identification.
- **Course 3 – The Art of Aging**: Master Blender Bill Ashburn explains wood treatment, tasting of Unity, Victory, Resolve, Double Barrel and Confederation Oak, plus a distillery tour.

All courses require participants to be of legal drinking age (19+). Tickets are available online via the site.

# Winter Sour Cocktail

A seasonal take on the classic whisky sour, made with **Forty Creek Double Barrel**. The recipe balances citrus, subtle sweetness and deep oak-forward whisky notes.

## Ingredients
- 2 oz Forty Creek Double Barrel
- 1 oz orange juice
- 0.75 oz lemon juice
- 0.75 oz cranberry rosemary syrup
- 1 egg white (optional)
- Fresh rosemary (optional)

## Method
1. Shake all ingredients without ice.
2. Add ice and shake until chilled (~20 s).
3. Double‑strain into a coupe glass.
4. Garnish with a fresh rosemary sprig if desired.

*Difficulty: Medium | Glass: Coupe | Taste: Bright & herbal | Season: Winter.*

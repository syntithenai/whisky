# Forty Creek Whisky
Forty Creek is a Canadian whisky distillery based in Niagara that has earned multiple awards, including *Whisky Maker of the Decade* (2020) and *Canadian Distillery of the Year* (2024). The site showcases its core product line—**Niagara Triple Reserve**, **Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor**, **Confederation Oak**, **Barrel Select**, **Double Barrel**, **Copper Bold**, **Honey Spiced**, and **Original Cream Liquor**—alongside a range of cocktails, creams, gifts, and corporate gifting options. Visitors can book tasting flights at the distillery or order whiskies for delivery (Ontario only). The brand emphasizes responsible drinking and offers free shipping on orders over $149.

## Key Facts
- **Location**: Niagara, Ontario, Canada

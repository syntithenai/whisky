# Forty Creek Whisky
Forty Creek is an award‑winning Canadian whisky distillery based in Ontario. The site showcases its core product line—various blended whiskies and specialty cream liquors—as well as cocktails, gifts, and a tasting experience at the Niagara region distillery.

## Key Highlights
- **Core products**: *Forty Creek Whisky*, *Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor*, *Confederation Oak Reserve*, *Barrel Select*, *Double Barrel Reserve*, *Copper Bold (Copper Pot)*, *Original Cream Liquor*, *Honey Spiced*, and *Niagara Triple Reserve*.
- **Awards**: 2024 Canadian Distillery of the Year; 2020 Whisky Maker of the Decade; multiple gold medals at Canadian Whisky Awards and San Francisco Spirits Awards.
- **Tasting & Events**: Visitors can book a tasting flight or cocktail session at the Niagara distillery.
- **Retail**: Online shop (Ontario‑only delivery) with free shipping on orders over $149.

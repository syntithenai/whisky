# Forty Creek Whisky Blog

The blog is a hub for Canadian whisky enthusiasts, featuring articles on cocktail recipes, grain types, collection tips, tasting guides, and the distillery’s history and innovations.

- **Key Topics**: Winter cocktails, grains behind whiskies, favourite cocktails, starting a collection, hosting tastings, production process, whisky stones, mixing basics, whisky sour history, whiskey style comparisons, beginner guide.
- **Distillery Background**: Founded 1972 by Otto Rieder; key milestones include 1992 launch of Barrel Select (2000) and Copper Bold (2007); joined Campari Group in 2014; named Whisky Maker of the Decade 2020.
- **Audience**: Both novices and seasoned whisky lovers, with a focus on Canadian whisky culture and innovation.

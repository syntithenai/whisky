# Forty Creek Whisky Blog

The blog is a hub for Canadian whisky enthusiasts, featuring articles on cocktail recipes, grain types, collection tips, tasting guides, and the distillery’s history and innovations. It highlights the brand’s commitment to quality, its Master Blender Bill Ashburn, and key milestones such as the 2000 Barrel Select launch and joining the Campari Group in 2014.

**Distillery background:** Founded 1972 by Otto Rieder; key figures include John Hall and Master Blender Bill Ashburn. Milestones: Barrel Select (2000), Copper Bold (2007), Campari Group acquisition (2014). Named Whisky Maker of the Decade in 2020.

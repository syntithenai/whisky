# Beginners Guide to Drinking Whisky – Forty Creek

Forty Creek’s blog post explains how newcomers can appreciate whisky, focusing on the distillery’s flagship expressions. It covers flavour profiles, tasting techniques (neat, with ice or water), and cocktail options, while highlighting key products such as Barrel Select, Copper Bold, Double Barrel Reserve, Honey Spiced, Confederation Oak Reserve, and Foxheart.

The guide also touches on whisky terminology, the science behind adding ice or water, and practical tips for glassware and dilution. Finally, it invites readers to visit the distillery for a complimentary tasting flight and directs them to shop online (Ontario‑only).

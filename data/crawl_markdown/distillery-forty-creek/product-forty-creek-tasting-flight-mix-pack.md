# Forty Creek Tasting Flight Mix Pack

The pack lets you sample four of Forty Creek’s flagship whiskies—**Barrel Select**, **Copper Bold**, **Honey Spiced**, and **Original Cream**—in a branded tasting set that includes a 750 ml bottle of each, a tasting flight set, and an instructional card. The total price is **$189.95**.

# Forty Creek Cream Liqueurs
Forty Creek offers two award‑winning cream liqueurs: **Original Cream Liquor** ($32.95–$45.95) and the newer **Cinnamon Bun (Nanaimo Bar) Cream Liquor** ($33.95). Both are whisky‑forward, made with fresh Canadian dairy cream and Forty Creek whisky. The Original has a spicy rye profile with a velvety caramel finish; the Cinnamon Bun blends vanilla, chocolate and coconut notes. They can be enjoyed neat, on ice or as brunch mixers with coffee.

The brand’s history dates back to 2013 when Bill Ashburn and John Hall launched the first cream liqueur after pioneering Canadian whisky innovations such as Barrel Select and Double Barrel. The products have earned Gold Medals at the Canadian Whisky Awards and the Beverage Tasting Institute.

**Key facts:**
- Two flavours: Original Cream Liquor, Cinnamon Bun (Nanaimo Bar) Cream Liquor.
- Prices: $32.95–$45.95 for Original; $33.95 for Cinnamon Bun.
- Whisky‑forward flavour profile.
- Awarded Gold Medal for Flavoured Whisky of the Year (2017) and Best Cream Liqueur (2022).

Order directly from the distillery or find a retailer.

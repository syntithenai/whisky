# How to Host a Whisky Tasting Night
Forty Creek’s guide walks readers through planning a whisky‑tasting evening, covering:
- **Whisky selection**: start with Canadian whisky and add single‑malt Scotch, bourbon, Irish whisky for variety.
- **Ambience & décor**: cozy seating, soft lighting, rustic or vintage accents.
- **Glassware**: tulip‑shaped glasses to concentrate aromas; optional large ice cubes and water.
- **Snacks**: cheeses, crackers, dark chocolate, nuts, charcuterie to complement flavours.
- **Tasting notes & storytelling**: share origin, flavour profile, anecdotes; encourage guests to record impressions.
- **Engagement**: blind tastings, discussion prompts, games.
- **Parting gifts**: mini‑tasting kits, glasses, journals, or themed accessories.
- **Local visit**: invites readers to the Niagara distillery for an in‑person experience.

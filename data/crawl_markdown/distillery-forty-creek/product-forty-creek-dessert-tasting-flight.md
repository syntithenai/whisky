# Forty Creek Dessert Tasting Flight

Forty Creek’s dessert tasting flight is a curated set of four sweet liqueurs:
- **Cinnamon Bun Cream** – a creamy, cinnamon‑spiced liquid.
- **Butter Tart Cream** – a buttery, decadent butter‑tart inspired liqueur.
- **Honey Spiced Whisky** – smooth whisky blended with honey and a hint of spice.
- **Oh Canada Maple Liqueur** – a maple‑flavoured exclusive from the distillery.

The set comes in a branded tasting flight box, each bottle 750 ml, priced at **$174.95** (currently out of stock). The page also promotes related products such as the Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor and the Forty Creek Tasting Flight Mix Pack.

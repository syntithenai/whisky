# Forty Creek – Our Story

Forty Creek is a Canadian whisky brand that has consistently pushed the boundaries of what Canadian whisky can be. Founded in 1972 by Otto Rieder at Grimsby, the distillery originally produced eau de vie and Alpenbitter before shifting focus to grain whiskies under founder John Hall and master blender Bill Ashburn. Key milestones include:

- **2000** – *Barrel Select* launched as a blend of grain whiskies aged in seasoned ex‑bourbon barrels and new American white oak.
- **2007** – *Copper Pot* (later renamed Copper Bold) introduced, blending three individually aged grain whiskies for an intense spirit.
- **2014** – Acquisition by Campari Group.
- **2020** – Named Whisky Maker of the Decade at the Canadian Whisky Awards.
- **2022** – Brand relaunch with new transparent bottles that honour Niagara heritage.

The distillery remains committed to innovation, quality and responsible drinking.

# Cocktail and Drink Recipes

Forty Creek Whisky offers a selection of cocktail recipes that pair their Canadian whisky with classic mixers such as ginger ale, coffee, and maple syrup. The page lists seasonal drinks (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter) and provides brief descriptions for each recipe.

- **Forty & Ginger** – whisky + ginger ale
- **Spiced Canadian Mule** – whisky + honey‑spiced ginger
- **Cream & Coffee** – coffee with Forty Creek Original Cream
- **Cinnamon Bun Espresso Martini** – espresso martini with vanilla, cinnamon and raisin notes
- **Bespoke Whisky Sour** – classic whisky sour
- **Honey Spiced Lemonade** – whisky + honey‑spiced lemonade
- **Forty Neat** – straight whisky tasting guide
- **A Bold Ginger** – rye whisky + ginger ale

The page also promotes the distillery’s direct shipping and invites visitors to book a complimentary tasting flight.

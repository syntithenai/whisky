# Forty & Ginger

A simple high‑ball cocktail that pairs **Forty Creek Barrel Select** Canadian whisky with ginger ale (or beer). The drink is described as refreshing, spicy and semi‑sweet, featuring vanilla, honey and toasted cocoa notes from the whisky. Optional garnishes include a lime wedge and walnut bitters.

## Ingredients
- 2 oz Forty Creek Barrel Select
- Ginger Ale (top up)
- Garnish: lime wedge (optional)
- Walnut Bitters (optional)

## Method
1. Fill a rocks glass with ice.
2. Add whisky, ginger ale and stir.
3. Garnish with lime wedge.

The recipe is easy to make and suitable for spring/summer or winter gatherings.

# Alpenbitter
Alpenbitter is a locally crafted, complex bitter from Forty Creek. It blends honey, vanilla, orange peel, apricot, toasted oak, black walnut and spice to create a warm gold‑coloured spirit at 40% ABV. The flavour profile is robust, rich and bold with hints of rye spice and cocoa, finished by a smooth, lingering finish. It can be served neat or over ice and works well in cocktails.

# Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor

Forty Creek’s **Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor** is a velvety, dessert‑style liqueur inspired by the classic cinnamon bun. It blends rich vanilla with warm cinnamon and subtle raisin undertones, offering an indulgent flavor that works well straight, on ice, or in cocktails such as the Cinnamon Bun Espresso Martini.

- **Size:** 750 mL
- **Price:** $33.95 (Canada)
- **ABV:** 15%
- **Appearance:** Opaque, milk‑chocolate color
- **Aromas:** Cinnamon, flaky pastries, vanilla, freshly baked oatmeal cookies
- **Flavor profile:** Vanilla with pops of cinnamon and underlying raisin notes
- **Finish:** Lengthy dairy‑cream finish with lingering cinnamon and raisin
- **Suggested uses:** Straight up, over ice, or in coffee/espresso martini cocktails.

Other related creams: Butter Tart Cream Liquor ($33.45), Original Cream Liquor (varies $32.95–$45.95), Maplewood Select ($90.20), Oh Canada Maple Liqueur ($24.95).

# A Bold Ginger
A high‑ball cocktail featuring **Forty Creek Copper Bold®** whisky and ginger beer, garnished with lime. The recipe calls for 2 oz of Copper Bold, 1 oz ginger beer, a slice of lime, and ice in a rocks glass.

- **Glass:** Highball
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Season:** Spring & Summer
- **Taste:** Bold & Refreshing

The drink showcases the whisky’s toffee aroma, rye spice, and higher proof, paired with the sharp zest of ginger beer for an invigorating experience.

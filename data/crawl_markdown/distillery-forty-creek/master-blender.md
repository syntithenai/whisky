# Bill Ashburn – Emeritus Master Blender

Forty Creek’s long‑time master blender, Bill Ashburn, joined the distillery in 1987 and steered its creative direction for 38 years. He pioneered innovative blends, botanically infused whiskies, and limited releases that earned him Canadian Master Blender of the Year (2020) and Whisky Maker of the Decade (2020). In July 2025 he retired, leaving a legacy celebrated by the distillery’s General Manager, Doug Costantini.

The page lists his career highlights, major awards, and an extensive catalogue of releases from 2000 to 2025, including flagship blends, botanical infusions, and specialty cask finishes. It also contains a brief Q&A explaining whisky production, tasting, food pairings, and sugar content.

# Bespoke Whisky Sour – Forty Creek

- Classic whisky sour recipe using **Forty Creek Barrel Select**.
- Ingredients: 2 oz whisky, 1 oz fresh lemon juice, 1 oz simple syrup (or sweetener), 2 dashes bitters, 1 large egg white.
- Method: dry‑shake with egg white, then shake with ice until cold; strain into a coupe and garnish with bitters.
- Flavor profile: tangy & smooth, suitable for spring/summer.
- Historical note: first printed in 1862 by Jerry Thomas.
- Variations mentioned: New York Sour (red‑wine float) and pasteurized egg white substitution.

> Fresh lemon juice is essential for brightness; pre‑made mixes dilute the flavor.

## Key Takeaways
- **Barrel Select** whisky provides a balanced base.
- Egg white adds silky texture; safety can be ensured with pasteurised eggs.
- The recipe is simple yet versatile, fitting into classic cocktail culture.

# Forty Creek Cream and Coffee

Forty Creek’s **Original Cream Liquor®** is a dairy‑cream based whisky liqueur that pairs perfectly with coffee. The recipe calls for 1 oz of the cream liquor topped with your favourite brewed coffee, creating a rich, vanilla‑caramel chocolate flavour profile.

## Key facts
- **Product**: Original Cream Liquor® (cream‑whisky liqueur)
- **Serving suggestion**: 1 oz over brewed coffee, iced coffee, or cold brew
- **Flavor notes**: vanilla, caramel, chocolate, subtle creaminess
- **Origin**: Canadian whisky blended with fresh dairy cream
- **Related cocktails**: Forty & Ginger, Spiced Canadian Mule, Cinnamon Bun Espresso Martini, etc.
- **Historical note**: Irish coffee invented in 1942 by Joe Sheridan at Foynes air base.

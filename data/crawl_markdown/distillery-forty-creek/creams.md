# The Forty Creek Cream Range

Forty Creek offers a line of cream liquors inspired by classic Canadian desserts. The range includes **Cinnamon Bun Cream**, **Butter Tart Cream**, and the original **Cream Liquor**. Each product is made with dairy cream and features flavour notes that echo its dessert inspiration:
- *Cinnamon Bun*: vanilla‑rich, cinnamon‑spiced, subtle raisin undertones.
- *Butter Tart*: buttery, caramel‑laden, ideal for after‑dinner or on ice.
- *Original Cream Liquor*: vanilla‑caramel profile, perfect with coffee.

The site also promotes cocktail recipes that incorporate these creams, such as the **Cinnamon Bun Espresso Martini** and a simple **Cream & Coffee** mix. No retail pricing or direct purchase links are displayed on this page.

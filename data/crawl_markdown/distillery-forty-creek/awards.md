# Forty Creek Awards

Forty Creek, Canada's original craft whisky from Niagara, has earned a long list of accolades across Canadian and international competitions. Highlights include:
- **Whisky Maker of the Decade** (2024)
- Multiple medals for its core whiskies, flavored whiskies, and cream liqueurs (Gold, Silver, Bronze, Double Gold, etc.)
- Awards span 2017‑2026 in categories such as *Best Buy*, *Best Canadian Whisky*, *Cream Whisky of the Year*, and *Flavored Whisky of the Year*.

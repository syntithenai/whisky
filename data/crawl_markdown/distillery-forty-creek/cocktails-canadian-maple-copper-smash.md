# Canadian Maple Copper Smash
A Canadian‑inspired cocktail featuring **Forty Creek Copper Bold** whisky, apple cider, maple syrup and lemon juice. The recipe calls for a neat glass, optional torched cinnamon garnish, and is best served in the fall or winter.

## Ingredients
- 1.5 oz Forty Creek Copper Bold®
- 1 oz apple cider
- 0.75 oz Canadian maple syrup
- 0.75 oz fresh lemon juice
- Torched cinnamon stick (optional)

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.
2. Shake ~20 s until chilled.
3. Strain into a neat glass over ice.
4. Garnish with torched cinnamon if desired.

The drink is described as *smooth, bold and beautifully crafted*, evoking campfire evenings and crisp autumn air.

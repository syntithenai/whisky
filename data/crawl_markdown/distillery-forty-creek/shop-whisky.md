# Forty Creek Whisky Shop

Forty Creek offers a range of Canadian whiskies, from the flagship **Barrel Select** to limited‑run expressions such as **Foxheart** and **Chronology**. Prices start at $18.45 for Barrel Select and rise to $299.95 for Chronology. The distillery emphasizes experimentation: each grain is aged separately before blending, producing distinct flavor profiles (spicy rye in Copper Bold, smooth oak‑aged notes in Confederation Oak). Awards include *Whisky Maker of the Decade* (Bill Ashburn) and multiple medals at Canadian Whisky Awards.

The shop also sells related products—creams, cocktails, gifts—and offers free shipping on orders over $149. The site encourages drinking whisky neat or with ice, but provides cocktail recipes for those who prefer mixed drinks.

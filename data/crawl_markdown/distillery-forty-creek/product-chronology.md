# Chronology – Forty Creek

Chronology is a limited‑edition rare blend crafted by Master Blender Bill Ashburn. It combines four distinct whiskies dating back to 1992, delivering aromas of sweet corn and toffee, a rich barley‑rye palate, and a long, warming finish with toasted oak, spicy rye, and dried fruit notes. The spirit is bottled at 45 % ABV, presented in a rich amber bottle, and sold only in select LCBO stores (starting November 1) and the Grimsby retail shop.

## Key facts
- **Price**: $299.95
- **Availability**: Limited quantities at the distillery store; rolling out to LCBO from Nov 1.
- **Bottle**: Rich amber with golden highlights.
- **ABV**: 45 %.
- **Tasting notes**: Sweet corn, toffee, rye bread, barley, toasted oak, spicy rye, dried fruit.

## Where to buy
- Grimsby retail store (297 S Service Rd, Grimsby, ON).
- LCBO stores (starting Nov 1).

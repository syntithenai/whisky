# Forty Creek Whisky Neat

- **What it is**: 2 oz of a chosen Forty Creek whisky served straight in a rocks glass (no ice or mixers). The term *NEAT* stands for “Naturally Enhanced Aroma Technology” and refers to the glass shape that concentrates lighter ethanol molecules.
- **Recommended starter**: Double Barrel Reserve Neat, followed by Rocks if desired. 
- **How to drink**: Pour, sniff, swish, sip slowly; cleanse palate with water or add ice cubes for a milder experience.
- **Flavor profile**: Caramel & spice notes, easy to taste the whisky’s nuances.
- **Related cocktails**: Forty & Ginger, Spiced Canadian Mule, Butter Tart Espresso Martini, etc.

> *“The concept of a ‘Whisky Neat’ might be a bit strange at first but… it is the way to experience the full nuance of our Forty Creek Whiskies without any distractions.”* – Master Blender

# Cinnamon Bun Espresso Martini

Forty Creek’s **Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor** is a dairy‑cream based liqueur blended with the distillery’s award‑winning whisky. It adds vanilla, cinnamon and subtle raisin notes to an espresso martini.

## Recipe
- 1 oz Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor
- ½ oz Frangelico®
- ½ oz espresso or cold‑brew coffee

Shake with ice, strain into a chilled coupe, top with three coffee beans (La Mosca).

The drink is best served in fall and winter.

## About the liqueur
* Dominant vanilla & cinnamon flavors
* Made with fresh dairy cream
* Uses Forty Creek whisky as base

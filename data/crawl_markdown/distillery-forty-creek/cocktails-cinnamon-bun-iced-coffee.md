# Cinnamon Bun Iced Coffee

A coffee‑based cocktail that blends brewed or cold‑brew coffee with Forty Creek’s Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor. The drink is served over ice in a rocks glass, optionally topped with whipped cream. It’s marketed as a brunch, dessert, or anytime treat.

## Ingredients
- 1 cup coffee (brewed or cold brew)
- 2 oz Forty Creek Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor
- 1 cup ice cubes
- Whipped cream (optional)

## Preparation
1. Cool brewed coffee to room temperature, then refrigerate.
2. Add ice to a rocks glass.
3. Pour chilled coffee over the ice.
4. Slowly add Cinnamon Bun Cream Liquor.
5. Top with whipped cream if desired.

The cocktail is described as *rich & creamy* and suitable for spring or summer.

## Context
Forty Creek’s Cream Liquor line uses fresh dairy cream and award‑winning Canadian whisky, positioning the product as a premium coffee‑whisky pairing reminiscent of Irish coffee traditions.

# Forty Creek Cold Brew Martini Kit

A ready‑to‑drink kit that pairs **Forty Creek Cream Liquor** (Original, Cinnamon Bun or Butter Tart) with a 70 g bag of dark roast cold brew coffee. The kit includes two coupe glasses stamped with the Forty Creek logo and is priced at **$54.95–$55.95**.

## Contents
- 70 g ground dark‑roast coffee
- 1 × 750 ml Forty Creek Cream Liquor (choose Original, Cinnamon Bun or Butter Tart)
- 2 × Forty Creek Coupe glasses

The kit is sold on the official site with free shipping for orders over $149.

---

**Note:** The product is marketed as a *cold brew martini* and is intended for consumers aged 19+ in Canada. The brand is owned by Davide Campari N.V.

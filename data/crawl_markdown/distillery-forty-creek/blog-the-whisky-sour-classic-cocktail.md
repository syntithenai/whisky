# Whisky Sour – The Classic Cocktail

Forty Creek explains the whisky sour as a simple blend of **whisky, fresh lemon juice, and sugar** (with optional egg white for froth). It traces the drink’s origins to 18th‑century naval sailors who mixed citrus with rum to prevent scurvy, later replacing rum with whisky. The post offers a step‑by‑step recipe: 2 oz whisky, ¾ oz lemon juice, ½ oz simple syrup, optional egg white; shake, strain over ice, garnish.

The author recommends using high‑quality whiskies such as **Double Barrel Select** or **Honey Spiced**, and stresses fresh lemons. A brief “Bespoke Whisky Sour” variant is mentioned for a premium experience.

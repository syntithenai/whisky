# After Nine Cocktail
A sophisticated nightcap featuring **Forty Creek Double Barrel** whisky blended with chocolate liqueur, crème de menthe, Averna Amaro and chocolate bitters. The recipe calls for a mint‑chocolate twist and can be finished with nutmeg.

## Ingredients & Method
- 1 oz Forty Creek Double Barrel
- 0.5 oz chocolate liqueur
- 0.5 oz crème de menthe
- 0.25 oz Averna Amaro
- 2 dashes chocolate bitters
- Optional: nutmeg

Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a rocks glass and optionally top with fresh nutmeg.

## Flavor Profile
Minty, creamy, chocolate‑rich with herbal bitterness and a subtle nutty finish.

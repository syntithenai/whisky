# Forty Creek Whisky Weekend 2025
- **Event**: 19th annual open‑house for whisky lovers.
- **Date & Time**: Saturday 20 Sep 10 AM–5 PM, Sunday 21 Sep 10 AM–5 PM.
- **Highlights**:
  - Guided distillery tours (starting 10:15 AM hourly) – $10 per person.
  - Whisky seminars led by Master Blender Bill Ashburn and others – $60 per person.
  - Live music (Lucas Trio, Feverish Lemons).
  - Exclusive tasting of the limited‑edition *Niagara Triple Reserve*.
- **Tickets**: Must be booked online in advance via AnyRoad.

> *Niagara Triple Reserve*: triple‑barrel finished in Niagara Cabernet casks; 51 % ABV, notes of sweetness, spice, oak, fruit and tannins.

## Source Excerpts
- About the Release: Crafted through a triple-barrel process and finished in Niagara Cabernet casks, Niagara Triple Reserve is a masterful blend that pulls rich, layered flavours from the wood.
- Expect an opening burst of sweetness and spice, followed by deep notes of oak, fruit, and tannins.
- This years seminar explores some of our most innovative releases, culminating with a before and after taste of Niagara Triple Barrel Reserve!
- Visit the still house, bottling line and the highlight of every tour, our main barrel aging warehouse where you will experience the sights and aromas of thousands of barrels aging your favourite Forty Creek whiskies!

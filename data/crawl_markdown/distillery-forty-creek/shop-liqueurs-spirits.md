# Forty Creek Liqueurs & Spirits
Forty Creek offers a range of non‑whisky spirits including maple liqueur, spiced rum, Prince Igor vodka (standard and extreme), Bolivar coffee liqueur, small cask brandy, Alpenbitter, and the new pickle‑flavoured vodka. The site highlights Canadian innovation in spirit production, citing historical experimentation with local botanicals and modern artisan blends.

# Forty Old Fashioned

Forty Old Fashioned is a classic whisky cocktail featuring **Forty Creek Copper Bold** rye whisky. The recipe calls for 2 oz of the whisky, ½ oz simple syrup, 2 dashes Angostura bitters, and an orange peel garnish. It’s served over ice in a rocks glass with a rim rubbed with orange peel.

The page explains the historical background of the Old Fashioned, its resurgence, and why Copper Bold—extra oak‑aged for a deep copper hue—is ideal for this drink. The cocktail is positioned as a fall/winter staple that pairs well with friends or vinyl records.

Other cocktails listed on the site include *Forty & Ginger*, *Spiced Canadian Mule*, *Cream & Coffee*, and *Nanaimo Bar Espresso Martini*.

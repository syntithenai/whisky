## Glossary
- **Cask Strength** – whisky bottled directly from the barrel without dilution.
- **Triple‑Barrel Process** – aging in three successive casks to develop complexity.
- **Port‑Wood Finish** – finishing whisky in barrels that previously held port wine.
- **Maplewood Finish** – using kiln‑dried maple wood for flavor infusion.
- **Cherrywood Reserve** – steeping whisky with cherrywood chips.

# Spiced Canadian Mule

A cocktail recipe from Forty Creek that swaps vodka for their Honey‑Spiced whisky. The drink uses 1.5 oz of the whisky, 1.5 oz lemonade, ginger beer (optional), and a dash of orange or lime bitters. Serve in a copper mug with ice, garnish with lime and mint.

The page explains the history of the mule family, notes that the copper mug is optional but ideal for chilling, and mentions alternative citrus options.

## Key Ingredients
- 1.5 oz Forty Creek Honey Spiced Whisky
- 1.5 oz Lemonade
- Ginger beer (optional)
- Orange or lime bitters

## Preparation
Fill a copper mug with ice, add all ingredients, stir, and garnish.

# Honey Spiced Lemonade

A summer‑ready cocktail that pairs **Forty Creek Honey Spiced whisky** with freshly squeezed lemonade, a splash of soda and a dehydrated lemon garnish. The drink balances the warm, honey‑spice notes of the whisky with bright citrus, making it an easy‑to‑make, refreshing option for warm evenings.

## Ingredients
- 1 oz Forty Creek Honey Spiced
- 2 oz lemonade
- Splash of soda
- 1 dehydrated lemon (garnish)

## Method
Fill a rocks glass with ice, add all ingredients, stir and garnish with the dehydrated lemon.

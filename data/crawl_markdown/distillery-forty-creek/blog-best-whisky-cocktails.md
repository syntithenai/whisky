# Forty Creek’s Essential Whisky Cocktails

Forty Creek presents a curated list of whisky cocktails suitable for any occasion—from camping trips to romantic date nights. The page highlights six core recipes:
- **Forty Ginger** (whisky + ginger ale)
- **Bespoke Whisky Sour** (whisky, lemon juice, simple syrup)
- **Forty Old Fashioned** (whisky, sugar cube, bitters)
- **Spiced Canadian Mule** (whisky, ginger beer, lime juice)
- **Forty Creek Whisky Neat** (pure whisky)
- **A Bold Ginger** (whisky + bold ginger flavours)

Each cocktail is paired with suggested food pairings and links to full recipes on the site. The page also promotes the distillery’s broader product range and encourages responsible drinking.

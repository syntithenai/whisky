# Butter Tart Cream Liquor

Forty Creek’s newest cream liquor blends their whisky with dairy cream to evoke the classic Canadian butter tart. The 750 mL bottle (15% ABV) is opaque, caramel‑brown in colour and offers aromas of browned butter, flavours of fresh baked brown sugar, vanilla and butterscotch, finishing velvety smooth.

**Serving suggestions:** straight up, over ice or mixed into coffee.

Price: **$33.45** (currently out of stock).

> *“Our Master Blender, Bill Ashburn, has perfectly paired our whisky with dairy cream to produce a Butter Tart Cream worthy of the namesake that pays homage to our Canadian heritage.”*

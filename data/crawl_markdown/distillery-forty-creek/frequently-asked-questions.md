# Frequently Asked Questions – Forty Creek Whisky

- **Shipping**: Flat $15 for orders under $149; free shipping in Ontario on orders over $149 (excluding certain postal codes). Shipping via third‑party couriers (ICS, Canpar) with delivery times of 2–5 business days.
- **Pickup**: Same‑day pickup available at the Grimsby distillery for orders placed before noon Monday‑Friday. Confirmation email required.
- **Pricing**: Set by LCBO to comply with Ontario liquor laws.
- **Age Verification**: Mandatory; must be 19+ and sign for delivery.
- **Payment**: Visa, MasterCard, American Express (including debit).
- **Refunds/Exchanges**: Not accepted currently due to COVID‑19 safety concerns.
- **Gluten**: Generally gluten‑free; cross‑contamination possible but rare. Products containing gluten are labeled.

> *“Forty Creek is committed to not selling alcohol to minors.”*

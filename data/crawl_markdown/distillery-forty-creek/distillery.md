# Forty Creek Distillery – Grimsby, Ontario

Forty Creek is a Canadian craft whisky distillery located in Grimsby, Ontario. The site offers guided tasting flights of its award‑winning blends, including **Barrel Select**, **Double Barrel**, and **Copper Bold** (formerly Copper Pot Whisky). Visitors can also purchase limited‑release products and merchandise at the on‑site retail store.

Key highlights:
- Master Blender: Bill Ashburn
- Tasting flights available by appointment or walk‑in for small groups
- Distillery address: 297 S Service Rd, Grimsby, ON L3M 1Y6
- Contact: [email protected] | (905) 945‑9225
- Hours: Mon‑Sat 10 am–5 pm; Sun 11 am–5 pm
- Parking available on site

The distillery’s flagship whiskies are noted for honey, vanilla, cocoa, and spice notes.

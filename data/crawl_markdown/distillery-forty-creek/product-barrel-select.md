# Barrel Select Whisky

Forty Creek’s flagship **Barrel Select** whisky, first released in 2000, is a blend of rye, barley and corn whiskies aged in seasoned ex‑bourbon barrels and new American white oak. It offers a warm gold colour with aromas of honey, vanilla, orange peel, apricot, toasted oak, black walnut and spice. The flavour profile is robust, complex and rich, featuring vanilla, honey, rye spice, cocoa and toasted earthiness, finishing smooth and lingering at 40 % ABV.

Available in 375 ml, 750 ml, 1140 ml and 1750 ml bottles, the price ranges from **$18.45 to $70.95** depending on size. It can be served neat or over ice and is also suitable for cocktails.

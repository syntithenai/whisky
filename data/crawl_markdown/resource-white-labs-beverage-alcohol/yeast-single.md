# WLP037 Yorkshire Square Ale Yeast

- **Attenuation**: 68 %‑72 %
- **Flocculation**: High
- **Alcohol tolerance**: Medium (5–10 %)
- **Fermentation temperature**: 65 °F – 70 °F (18 °C – 21 °C)
- **Key flavor notes**: toasty, malt‑driven esters; good for English‑style pale ales, brown ales, milds.
- **Beta‑lyase activity**: releases bound thiols from hops, adding passionfruit/guava aromas.

> *“This yeast produces a beer that is malty and well‑balanced. Expect toasty flavors with malt‑driven esters.”*

# Standard Tours – Springbank
Springbank offers two standard tours:
- **Springbank Tour**: £15 per person, ~1 hour.
- **Kilkerran Tour**: £12 per person, ~1 hour.
Both are led by the distillery’s knowledgeable team and cover the history, sights and flavours of Springbank Whisky. The site lists opening hours (Mon‑Sat 10am–6pm, closed Sunday) and contact details.

The page is part of the broader *Tours & Tastings* section on the Springbank website.

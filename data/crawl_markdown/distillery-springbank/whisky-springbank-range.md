# Springbank Distillery

Springbank is a small distillery located on the Isle of Barra in Scotland, known for its unique whiskies that showcase the island's maritime environment and traditional production methods.

## Key Facts
- Founded: 1888
- Owner: Diageo
- Annual Production: ~30,000 cases
- Notable Whiskies: Springbank 10 Year Old, Springbank 21 Year Old, Springbank Single Cask

## Production Signals
- **Barrel Aging**: Uses a mix of American white oak and ex-bourbon barrels for maturation.
- **Cask Types**: Ex-bourbon, sherry casks, and refill bourbon casks are used to impart different flavors.
- **Maturation Environment**: The distillery's maritime climate contributes to unique flavor development.

## Commercial Signals
- **Popularity**: Consistently ranked among the top whiskies in international competitions.
- **Awards**: Multiple gold medals at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition and other major events.
- **Retail Presence**: Widely available in premium liquor stores globally.

## Risks / Unknowns
- **Limited Availability**: Small production makes some releases scarce.
- **Price Sensitivity**: Higher-end expressions can be expensive, potentially limiting mass-market appeal.
- **Environmental Factors**: Climate change may impact the unique characteristics of whiskies produced on Barra.

# Springbank Distillery Overview
- Located on the Isle of Barra in Scotland
- One of the oldest distilleries, founded in 1817
- Produces single malt whisky using traditional methods

## Key Facts
- **Malt Whisky Only**: No grain whiskies produced
- **Barra's Finest**: Known for exceptional quality due to island location
- **Limited Production**: Small batch production

## Production Signals
- Uses peated barley and copper pot stills
- Maturation in ex-bourbon casks
- Aged for at least 12 years

## Commercial Signals
- Highly sought after by collectors
- Limited releases create buzz among whisky enthusiasts

## Risks / Unknowns
- Small scale production makes sourcing challenging
- Exact aging times kept proprietary

# Springbank Single Malt Whisky

## Introduction
- Springbank is a historic distillery located in Campbeltown, Scotland.
- Founded in 1828 by John Murdoch, it has been family-owned for five generations.
- The distillery produces three single malts: Longrow, Hazelburn, and Campbeltown.

## Distillery & Production
- **Location**: Campbeltown, Scotland - the whisky capital of the world.
- **Process**: Triple distilled in copper pot stills using traditional methods.
- **Maturation**: Aged exclusively in ex-bourbon casks for a minimum of 12 years.
- **Key Facts**:
  - Distills approximately 30,000 litres annually.
  - Produces around 60 bottles per day.
  - Maturation time: Minimum 12 years (Longrow), Hazelburn matures longer due to cooler conditions.

## Tasting Notes & Flavour Profile
- **Longrow**: Fruity and floral with notes of honey, citrus peel, and vanilla. Spicy finish with hints of cinnamon and nutmeg.
- **Hazelburn**: Heavier peat character with smoked salmon, seaweed, and iodine. Rich oak and caramel notes.
- **Campbeltown**: Balanced blend showcasing coastal influences, briny sea air, and a touch of smoke.

## Commercial Signals & Risks
- **Commercial Signals**:
  - Strong brand recognition in the UK market.
  - Successful bottlings for Springbank Society and Cadenhead's Warehouse.
- **Risks/Unknowns**:
  - Limited availability due to small production volumes.
  - Dependence on Campbeltown market trends.

## Production Signals
- **Key Facts**: Distills ~30,000 litres annually; produces ~60 bottles/day; minimum 12-year maturation for Longrow.
- **Production Signals**:
  - Exclusively uses ex-bourbon casks for maturation.
  - Triple distillation enhances purity and flavor complexity.

## Sensory & Brand Positioning
- **Sensory**: Fruity, floral, and spicy notes in Longrow; peaty, smoky, and iodine-rich in Hazelburn; balanced coastal profile in Campbeltown.
- **Brand Positioning**: Authentic Highland single malt with distinct Campbeltown character; premium yet accessible to whisky enthusiasts seeking unique flavors.

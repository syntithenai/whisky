# Springbank Malts Festival 2026
Springbank’s 2026 Campbeltown Malts Festival runs **18‑23 May** and features a mix of tasting events, open days, barbecues, and a special dinner. Tickets are sold via Eventbrite or the distillery website; all events are strictly 18+ with no refunds.

## Key Events
- **Cadenhead’s Warehouse Tasting – Festival Edition (1‑6)**: six whiskies drawn straight from casks, priced £5 each day, three available for purchase at the end. 
- **Local Barley Farm Tour**: 1½ h tour of Glencraigs Farm with barley sampling and new‑make spirit (£40). 
- **Springbank Open Day (Thu 21 May)**: multiple tastings (Longrow, Art & Science of Malting, Warehouse, Cadenhead’s Archive, Cage Bottles), live malting demo (free) and a charity BBQ & Tasting (£500). 
- **Kilkerran Open Day (Fri 22 May)**: peated tasting, blending sessions, warehouse tastings, Cadenhead’s tasting, cocktail clash. 
- **Old Bottling Hall Dinner (Wed 20 May)**: £300 dinner with a champagne reception and hand‑drawn whisky selection.

All events are booked in advance; the Old Bottling Hall dinner is ballot‑only.

## Practical Details
- Tickets sold via Eventbrite from 18 Feb 14:00 GMT or directly on the Springbank site. 
- No refunds, strict age limit (18+). 
- Bring reusable water bottles – water stations available.

# Springbank Malts Festival 2026
Springbank’s 2026 Campbeltown Malts Festival runs **18‑23 May** and features a mix of tasting events, open days, barbecues, and a charity dinner. Tickets are sold via Eventbrite or the Springbank website; all events are 18+ with a strict no‑refund policy.

## Key Events
- **Cadenhead’s Warehouse Tasting – Festival Edition** (Mon‑Sat) – £10 per tasting, six whiskies drawn straight from casks. Three are available for purchase after the session.
- **Local Barley Farm Tour** (Mon‑Tue) – £4, 1½ h tour of Glencraigs Farm with new‑make spirit and three drams.
- **Springbank Charity BBQ & Tasting** – £50, limited to 30 tickets; proceeds go to local charities.
- **Old Bottling Hall Dinner** – £300, ballot only, guests receive a 70 cl bottle of the winning dram.
- **Open Days** (Thu at Springbank, Fri at Kilkerran) – multiple tastings, live malting demos, blending sessions, and exclusive bottlings.

All events are booked on Eventbrite from 18 Feb 2026 14:00 GMT; some Thursday‑only tickets must be purchased in‑person.

## Practical Info
- **Location**: Springbank Distillery, Campbeltown (Bolgam Street) and Kilkerran Distillery (Glengyle). 
- **Opening Hours**: Mon‑Sat 10 am–6 pm; Sunday closed. 
- **Contact**: +44 (0)1586 552009 or [email protected].

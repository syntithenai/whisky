# Springbank Tasting Room Menu – November 2023

The PDF is a printed menu for the Springbank tasting room, listing whisky selections by category (Best Sellers, First Editions, World Books, Local Authors, Flights, etc.), along with prices and cask types. It also contains a full drinks list (hot beverages, soft drinks, gin, rum, cognac, wine, beer) and a brief description of the tasting flights.

The whisky section is organized into:
- **Best Sellers** – popular releases from Cadenhead’s Authentic Collection.
- **First Editions** – single‑cask, cask‑strength bottlings from various Scottish distilleries.
- **World Books** – international whiskies (Canadian, Irish, etc.).
- **Local Authors** – Campbeltown‑based expressions.
- **Tasting Flights** – curated flights and special releases.

Each entry lists the name, age statement, ABV, cask type, and price in GBP.

The drinks menu provides standard prices for hot drinks, soft drinks, gin, rum, cognac, wine, beer, and a bar‑specific whisky (Glencairn).

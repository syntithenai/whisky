# Countdown Collection 2024: The Whisky
Springbank’s 2024 release is a **limited edition of 500 bottles** made from three refill Bourbon hogshead casks that spent 26 years aging in Campbeltown warehouses. It is a 70 cl bottle at 51.2 % ABV.

## Tasting Notes
- **Nose:** Rich, fruity, syrupy with nectarines, key lime pie, pineapple juice; subtle marzipan and coconut.
- **Palate:** Creamy, oily mouthfeel; citrus, ginger, sesame oil, brazil nuts, pistachio; dunnage‑style earth/soot finish.
- **Finish:** Coastal influence – ripe banana, green apples, coconut, oak, banana bread, liquorice.

## Availability
Only 500 bottles worldwide. The ballot has finished and the release is now available for purchase through Springbank’s channels.

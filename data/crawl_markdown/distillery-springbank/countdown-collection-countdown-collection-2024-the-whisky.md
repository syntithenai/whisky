# Countdown Collection 2024: The Whisky
Springbank’s 2024 release is a limited‑run single malt aged in three refill Bourbon hogshead casks that spent 26 years in Campbeltown warehouses. Only **500 bottles** are available worldwide.

- **ABV:** 51.2% (70 cl bottle)
- **Tasting notes:** Rich, fruity nose with nectarines, key lime pie and pineapple; creamy, oily palate with citrus, ginger, sesame oil, brazil & pistachio nuts; finish featuring coastal banana, green apples, coconut, oak and liquorice.

The page also lists contact details, opening hours, and links to tours, tastings and the Springbank Society.

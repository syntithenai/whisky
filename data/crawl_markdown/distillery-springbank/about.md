# Springbank Distillery

## Key Facts
- Located on the Isle of Barra, Scotland
- Founded in 1888
- Produces single malt whisky using traditional methods
- Known for peated whiskies and unique maritime terroir

## Production Signals
- Small batch distillery with limited production capacity
- Focuses on quality over quantity
- Uses copper pot stills to create rich, flavorful spirit
- Maturation in ex-bourbon barrels for distinctive flavors

## Commercial Signals
- Limited availability due to small-scale production
- Strong brand identity centered around heritage and craftsmanship
- Popular among whisky enthusiasts for its unique character

## Risks / Unknowns
- Potential supply chain challenges due to remote location

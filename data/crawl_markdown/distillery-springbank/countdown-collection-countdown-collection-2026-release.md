# Countdown Collection: 2026 Release
Springbank announces a **29‑year‑old single malt** that has spent time in two refill sherry hogsheads and one fresh rum barrel. Only **500 bottles** will be available by ballot (16–18 March GMT) with shipping to follow; shop pickup starts 30 March.

> *Nose*: gentle creaminess, peach, apricot, almonds, tropical notes of papaya, watermelon, coconut shavings.
> 
> *Palate*: honeycomb sweetness, sea‑salt background, fig jam, muscovado sugar, cocoa powder.
> 
> *Finish*: salty note, burst of sweetness and soft peat smoke.

Price: **£25.00** (incl. VAT) with a Certificate of Authenticity.

---

## Key facts
- 29‑year age
- Maturation: 2 refill sherry hogsheads + 1 fresh rum barrel
- Limited to 500 bottles by ballot
- Ballot window: 16 March – 18 March (midday GMT)
- Shipping begins after ballot closes; shop pickup from 30 March
- Price: £25.00 incl. VAT
- Certificate of Authenticity included
- Part of a five‑part Countdown Collection leading to Springbank’s 200th anniversary in 2028

## Tasting notes (full)
**Nose:** gentle creaminess, peach, apricot, almonds; tropical hints of papaya, watermelon, coconut shavings.

**Palate:** honeycomb sweetness, sea‑salt note building in background; sherry influence with fig jam, muscovado sugar, cocoa powder.

**Finish:** salty note continues, burst of sweetness and soft peat smoke emerges.

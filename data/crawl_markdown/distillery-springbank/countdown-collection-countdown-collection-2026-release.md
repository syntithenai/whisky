# Countdown Collection: 2026 Release
Springbank announces a 29‑year‑old single malt finished in two refill sherry hogsheads and one fresh rum barrel. Only **500 bottles** will be available by ballot (open 16 Mar – 18 Mar GMT). The release is part of a five‑part series celebrating the distillery’s 200th anniversary in 2028.

## Tasting notes
- **Nose:** gentle creaminess, peach, apricot, almonds; tropical hints of papaya, watermelon, coconut shavings.
- **Palate:** honeycomb sweetness, sea salt background, fig jam, muscovado sugar, cocoa powder.
- **Finish:** salty note, burst of sweetness and soft peat smoke.

Price: £50.8 % vol – £25 00 (incl. VAT) with Certificate of Authenticity.

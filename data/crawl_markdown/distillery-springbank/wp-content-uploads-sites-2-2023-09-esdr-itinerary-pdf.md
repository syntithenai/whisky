# September 2023 Springbank Itinerary

- **Arrival** – Check‑in at The Still Guesthouse, dinner & whisky tasting.
- **Day 1** – Breakfast, full‑access tour of Springbank Distillery, new‑tasting in the Washback Bar, lunch, Cadenhead’s Warehouse tasting + shop visit, historic walking tour of Campbeltown, dinner and cocktails.
- **Day 2** – Breakfast, full‑access tour of Glengyle Distillery, Springbank & Kilkerran warehouse tasting, lunch at the Tasting Room, free time in Campbeltown, Springbank blending session, dinner with whisky pairing.
- **Departure** – Breakfast, checkout by 11:00. Itinerary subject to change.

> *All activities are scheduled within The Still Guesthouse and surrounding distillery sites.*

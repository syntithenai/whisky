# Kilkerran Warehouse Tasting
- **Price**: £40.00 for a 1‑hour session.
- **Location**: Springbank Distillery warehouse, Campbeltown.
- **What you get**: Sample six cask‑strength Kilkerran whiskies with a complimentary glass; post‑tasting 35 cl bottles from the same casks are available to purchase (limited to one per person per cask).
- **Booking**: Online or by email/phone. Advance booking recommended.
- **Restrictions**: No under‑18s, no alcohol consumption if driving, zero‑tolerance policy for drinkers on the premises.
- **Other notes**: Separate Kilkerran Tour available; opening hours Monday–Saturday 10 am–6 pm.

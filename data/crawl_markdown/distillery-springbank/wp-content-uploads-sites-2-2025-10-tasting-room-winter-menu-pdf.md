# Springbank Tasting‑Room Winter Menu

A concise menu from Springbank’s tasting room for the winter season, featuring soups, sandwiches, wraps, ciabatta/toasties, sweet options, and filled baked potatoes. Prices are listed in GBP.

- **Soup of the Day** – £5.00 (served with crusty bread)
- **Sandwich Platter** – £12.50 (choose 3 from the list)
- **Individual Sandwiches** – £7.50 each (various fillings including vegan options)
- **Ciabatta / Toasties & Wraps** – £8.50 each (spicy chicken, Philly cheese steak, etc.)
- **Sweet of the Day** – £2.95‑£4.50
- **Filled Baked Potatoes** – various fillings such as haggis whisky sauce, pulled beef madras, and vegan options
- **Hot & Cold Fillings** – £10.50 / £9.95 respectively (soup with ciabatta, sausage rolls, etc.)
- **Homemade Pastries** – £5.00 (served with side salad & whisky chutney)

All items are available daily from 12 pm to 2:30 pm.

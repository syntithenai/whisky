# Springbank Distillery

Springbank is a small distillery located on the Isle of Barra in Scotland, known for its unique whiskies that reflect the island's rugged landscape and maritime climate.

## Key Facts
- **Founded**: 1888
- **Owner**: Diageo
- **Malt Whisky Production**: Yes
- **Single Grain Whisky Production**: No
- **Barrel Capacity**: Approximately 200 barrels per batch
- **Maturation Time**: Typically aged for 12-15 years, with some expressions aged up to 25 years

# Campbeltown Loch Blended Malt
Campbeltown Loch is a blended malt produced by Springbank using all five malts from the Campbeltown region’s three distilleries. It matures in ex‑bourbon and ex‑sherry casks, is vatted and bottled at Springbank Distillery, and has an ABV of 46 %. The dram opens with buttery, sea‑salted notes, ginger loaf and pecan pie; the palate delivers creamy coffee, salted caramel, date syrup and crystallised brown sugar with a subtle peat smoke. The finish builds peat smoke into a leathery note.

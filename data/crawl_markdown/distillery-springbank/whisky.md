# Springbank Distillery

## Key Facts
- Located on the Isle of Barra, Scotland
- Founded in 1888
- Produces single malt whisky using traditional methods
- Known for peated whiskies and unique maritime terroir

## Production Signals
- Uses peat to smoke barley during malting process
- Distills in copper pot stills
- Maturation occurs in oak casks on the island
- Limited production due to remote location and small scale operations

## Commercial Signals
- Small distillery with limited bottlings
- Focuses on quality over quantity
- Popular among whisky enthusiasts for its distinct peated flavors
- Brand positioning emphasizes heritage, tradition, and maritime character

## Risks / Unknowns
- Remote location makes sourcing ingredients challenging
- Limited distribution due to island's isolation
- Potential climate change impacts on peat availability

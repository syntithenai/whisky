# Fair Work First Policy – Springbank
Springbank Distillers Ltd, operating under J&A Mitchell & Co Ltd, outlines its commitment to the Scottish Government’s Fair Work First policy. The page explains how the company applies the five dimensions of Fair Work—Security, Respect, Effective Voice, Opportunity and Fulfilment—to benefit employees, the business and society.

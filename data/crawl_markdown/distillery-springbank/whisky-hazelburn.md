# Hazelburn – Springbank

Hazelburn is a triple‑distilled, unpeated single malt range from Springbank that celebrates Campbeltown’s lost distillery of the same name. The flagship **Hazelburn 10 Year Old** (46% ABV, 100% Bourbon cask) offers sweet tropical aromas and a creamy finish with subtle earthiness. Limited editions include the **Sherry Wood 2025 Edition** (8 yrs, 48.2% ABV, 100% Oloroso Sherry casks) and the **Hazelburn 21 Year Old 2023 Edition** (43.2% natural cask strength, a blend of Bourbon, Sherry and Port). Prices range from £35 to £375.

The distillery also offers tours, tastings and membership programmes.

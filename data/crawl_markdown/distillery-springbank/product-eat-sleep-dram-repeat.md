# Eat, Sleep, Dram, Repeat – Springbank

- **Price**: £950 per person.
- **Duration**: 3 nights (Monday‑Thursday) in the Springbank Guesthouse.
- **Highlights**:
  - Accommodation in the distillery guesthouse.
  - Tours of Springbank and Glengyle distilleries, Cadenhead’s Warehouse, Blending Lab, and the Still.
  - Guided historic walking tour of Campbeltown.
  - Exclusive tastings (including a chance to blend your own bottle).
  - All meals provided.
- **Availability**: September‑February; only 7 spaces per week.
- **Production note**: Springbank Distillery is closed for production from mid‑September to end‑January, but tours continue. Glengyle operates late‑September to mid‑December.

> *“Delve into the depths of Springbank, Kilkerran and Cadenhead’s with our exclusive package offering 3 nights of accommodation, tours and tastings in our most in-depth experience ever.”*

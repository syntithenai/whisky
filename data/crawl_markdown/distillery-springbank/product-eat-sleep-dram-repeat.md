# Eat, Sleep, Dram, Repeat
A premium three‑night package at Springbank that includes:
- Guesthouse accommodation (4 double, 1 twin, 1 single)
- Tours of Springbank, Glengyle and Cadenhead’s Warehouse
- Guided historic walk of Campbeltown
- Exclusive tastings and a blending session to create your own bottle
- All meals provided
Price: **£950 per person**

The itinerary is available for download (PDF). The package runs September‑February, with 7 spots each week. Production at Springbank stops mid‑September to end of January; Glengyle operates late‑September to mid‑December.

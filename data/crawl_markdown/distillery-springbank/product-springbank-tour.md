# Springbank Tour
- **Price:** £15.00 for a 1‑hour guided walk through the historic distillery (1828‑present). 
- **Highlights:** Full on‑site whisky production, tasting of a core range whisky, complimentary glass and miniature.
- **Availability:** Monday–Saturday; no production from early September to December but tours continue. 
- **Booking:** Online or by email/phone; free for Springbank Society members. 
- **Extras:** Children under 18 allowed (free) with adult supervision; assistance dogs permitted.
- **Refunds:** Full refund if cancelled ≥48 h before the tour.

# Springbank Distillery

Springbank is a small, family-run distillery located on the Isle of Barra in Scotland.

## Key Facts
- Founded: 1888
- Owner: Springbank Distillers Ltd (family-owned)
- Production: Small batch single malt whisky
- Cask Types: Ex-bourbon, ex-sherry, and other casks
- Bottlings: Various limited editions and annual releases

## Production Signals
- **Small Batch**: Limited production runs of 1,000 to 2,500 bottles per release.
- **Cask Variation**: Uses a mix of bourbon barrels (new and toasted) and sherry casks for maturation.
- **Maturation**: Aged in Scottish oak for varying periods before bottling.

## Commercial Signals
- **Limited Releases**: Focuses on small batch, limited edition releases to maintain exclusivity.
- **Boutique Bottlings**: Annual releases and special editions under the "Springbank Collection" label.
- **Whisky Clubs**: Offers exclusive membership programs for collectors.

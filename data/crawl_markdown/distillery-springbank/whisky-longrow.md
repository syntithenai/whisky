# Longrow – Springbank

Longrow is a heavily peated, double‑distilled whisky first produced in 1973 to supply a robust component for blends. It has become a staple of the distillery’s core range since the 1990s.

## Current Editions
- **Longrow Peated** – 46 % ABV, matured in Bourbon and Sherry casks; price £50.00.
- **Longrow 18‑Year‑Old (2024 Edition)** – 90 % Sherry / 10 % Port maturation; price £110.00.
- **Longrow 100 ° Proof (2026 Edition)** – 57.1 % ABV, aged in refill Bourbon and Pinot Noir casks; price £60.00.

Each edition offers a distinct smoky profile with complementary fruit, spice or oak notes.

## Tasting Notes
- **Nose**: creamy vanilla custard, toasted marshmallows, herbs, rich fruits, subtle smokiness.
- **Palate**: well‑balanced, rich and creamy, medicinal hint, persistent smoke waves.
- **Finish**: lingering gentle smoke with sea salt or coffee bean nuances depending on edition.

# Cadenhead’s Creation – Blending Session

- **Duration**: 1 h 30 min
- **Location**: Cadenhead’s Blending Lab, Campbeltown (meeting point at the Whisky Shop)
- **Experience**: Blind blending of 8 pre‑selected Cadenhead’s whiskies from various cask types to create a personalised blended malt or blended Scotch.
- **Take‑home**: Participants hand‑fill a 70 cl bottle of their own blend.
- **Booking**: Online via Experience.Cadenhead.scot (on‑day availability may exist).
- **Ballot & Purchase**: One per person, random draw, 3 % amendment fee, shipping to many countries (see list), VAT/DAP/DDP rules apply.
- **Other services**: Tours, tastings, Cadenhead’s Warehouse Tasting, Cadenhead’s Tasting Room, The Washback Bar.

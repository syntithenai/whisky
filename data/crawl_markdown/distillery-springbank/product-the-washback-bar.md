# The Washback Bar – Springbank

The Washback Bar is a tasting venue inside the Springbank distillery, housed in historic washbacks from Springbank and Glengyle. It offers:
- Flights of new releases, core range whiskies, and rare archive bottles by the glass.
- Light bites, homemade soup and hot drinks between 12‑2:30 pm daily.
- No pre‑booking for small groups; groups of 10+ must email in advance.

The page also contains detailed **ballot entry & purchase** rules for a limited release, shipping restrictions (including a long list of eligible countries), payment via Stripe, and return/​authenticity policies. It ends with links to related tasting events and the distillery’s opening hours.

## Key facts
- Location: Springbank Distillery, Well Close, Campbeltown, Argyll, Scotland.
- Opening hours: Mon‑Sat 10 am–6 pm; Sunday closed.
- Food service: 12 pm–2:30 pm daily.
- Pre‑booking required for groups of 10+.
- Ballot entry limits one bottle per person; duplicate orders cancelled.
- Shipping destinations include most EU countries, Australia, New Zealand, USA (with restrictions), and others listed.
- Payment via Stripe; VAT applied to UK shipments.
- Returns accepted within 14 days if damaged or unsatisfactory.

## Distillery facts
- Springbank is a Campbeltown distillery founded in 1828.
- The Washback Bar uses old washbacks from Springbank and Glengyle.
- The bar is part of the broader Springbank tours & tastings offering.

# Cadenhead’s Warehouse Tasting
- **Price**: £45.00 for a 1‑hour session.
- **Location**: Cadenhead’s Warehouse, Bolgam Street, Campbeltown.
- **Experience**: Sample six exclusive whiskies straight from the cask (various distilleries), then choose a seventh at the Tasting Room bar.
- **Extras**: Whisky can be purchased on‑site and filled, labelled and sealed in front of you; free Cadenhead’s glass provided.
- **Booking**: Available Monday–Saturday in Campbeltown. Group bookings (20+) require email contact.
- **Notes**: Does not include a Springbank or Kilkerran distillery tour; separate booking needed.

# Cadenhead’s Warehouse Tasting

- **Price:** £45.00 for a 1‑hour session.
- **Location:** Cadenhead’s Warehouse, Bolgam Street, Campbeltown.
- **Experience:** Sample six exclusive whiskies straight from the cask (various Scottish distilleries and possibly others). After tasting you can purchase the drams, watch them be filled, labelled and sealed in front of you, and take home a complimentary Cadenhead’s glass. A seventh whisky is available at the adjacent Tasting Room bar.
- **Availability:** Monday‑Saturday (Campbeltown only). Group bookings over 20 people require email contact.
- **Notes:** Does not include a Springbank or Kilkerran distillery tour; those must be booked separately.

> *“Experience drams from all over the world in a Cadenhead’s Warehouse Tasting.”*

# Japanese Whisky Labelling Standards (PDF)

- **Document**: 0019012‑155.pdf, published by the Japan National Tax Agency.
- **Date**: 2011‑02‑16.
- **Purpose**: Provides rules for applying to use of “whisky” on labels for products produced in specified Japanese prefectures and towns.
- **Scope**: Covers a list of prefectures (e.g., Fukui, Ishikawa, Kyoto, etc.) and numerous municipalities within them. The document specifies that the application period is automatically extended for all relevant cases.

The PDF contains a detailed table of the affected areas but no product‑specific information or pricing.

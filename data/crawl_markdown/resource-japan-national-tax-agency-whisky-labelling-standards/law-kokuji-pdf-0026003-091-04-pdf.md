# Japan National Tax Agency – Whisky Labelling Standards

This document outlines the regulatory framework for using information‑communication technology (ICT) in the promotion of whisky and related products under Japanese law. It specifies how prefectural orders, particularly Article 5, Item 3 of the *Prefectural Ordinance on Information‑Communication Technology*, are to be amended and applied. The text also details the roles of national officials, legal authorities, and the procedures for electronic registration and notification in relation to these regulations.

The PDF is largely a legal memorandum that explains:
- The amendment process for prefectural orders concerning ICT use in whisky promotion.
- The responsibilities of the National Tax Agency’s Director‑General and other officials.
- The procedural steps for updating the relevant articles, including electronic filing requirements.
- The interaction between national and local regulations, especially regarding legal authority and registration numbers.

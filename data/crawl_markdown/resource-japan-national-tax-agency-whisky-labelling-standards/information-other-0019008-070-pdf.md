# Japan National Tax Agency – Employee Insurance Status (2018‑08)

This PDF explains the legal basis for determining the number of employees covered by compulsory insurance under Japanese law (Act No. 123, 35th year). It presents a table with:
- Total employee count
- Number of insured employees
- Insured rate
- Shortfall amount

The document also contains footnotes clarifying how the figures are calculated and the treatment of various categories such as temporary workers, part‑time staff, and special cases.

## Key points
1. **Legal basis** – Act No. 123 (35th year) on compulsory insurance for employees.
2. **Data snapshot** – As of 2018‑08‑11: 58,987 total employees, 1,285 insured, 2.18% insured rate, shortfall of 189.
3. **Calculation method** – Insured count excludes non‑regular staff and applies a specific conversion factor to regular employee numbers.
4. **Special categories** – Notes on temporary workers, part‑time employees, and special cases (e.g., those with health insurance benefits).

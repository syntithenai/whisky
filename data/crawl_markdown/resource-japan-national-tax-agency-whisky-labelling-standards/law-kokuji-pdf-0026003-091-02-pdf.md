# Japan National Tax Agency – Whisky Labelling Standards

This PDF (0026003‑091_02.pdf) is a legal notice from the Japanese National Tax Agency outlining amendments to the **National Tax Administration Act** concerning the use of information communication technology in public policy. It specifically addresses:

* The definition and application of *国税局長* (Director General of the National Tax Agency) for payment‑related documents.
* The procedural changes effective from 25 May 2025, including updated forms and guidelines for provincial tax notices.
* References to related statutes such as the **Special Measures Act** and the **Insurance Special Measures Law**.

The document is a formal regulatory update rather than a product‑specific guide.

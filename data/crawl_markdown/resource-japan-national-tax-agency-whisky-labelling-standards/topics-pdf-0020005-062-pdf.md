# Whisky Labeling Notification – Japan National Tax Agency

The PDF explains the procedure for notifying the Japanese tax authorities about whisky that contains land within specified prefectures (e.g., Shizuoka, Aichi, Gifu, Mie, etc.). It details:
- The notification must be sent to individuals who own land in the designated areas.
- The deadline is 10 Oct 12 for the year 2024, with automatic extensions for all tax items.
- Special provisions apply to whisky that has been awarded or received special recognition.
- If there are uncertainties, the agency can be contacted for clarification.

The document lists the prefectures and municipalities covered under the “designated area” and provides contact information for the relevant tax office.

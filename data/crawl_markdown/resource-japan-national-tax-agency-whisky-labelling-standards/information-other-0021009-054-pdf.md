# Japanese National Tax Agency Disability Statistics (2023‑04‑24)

This PDF contains the 2023 statistical report on disability among employees in Japan, prepared under the *Act on Employment of Persons with Disabilities* (Article 35‑123). It lists detailed counts of full‑time and part‑time workers by disability type, calculates the overall disability rate, and provides a breakdown by specific categories such as visual, hearing, speech, mobility, and mental disabilities. The report also includes a table of recommended employment measures for each category.

Key figures:
- Total employees considered: **62 007.5** (full‑time + part‑time)
- Total disabled employees reported: **1 699.5**
- Disability rate: **2.74 %**
- Visual disability: 11; Hearing: 23; Speech: 12; etc.

The document is a government statistical release and does not contain product or commercial information.

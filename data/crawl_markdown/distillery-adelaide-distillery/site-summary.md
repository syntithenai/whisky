# Adelaide Distillery

Adelaide Distillery is a family‑founded, small‑batch spirit producer based in Adelaide, South Australia. The distillery crafts three core products: a premium whisky (priced at **$165**), the award‑winning Adelaide Gin (**$85**) and a Greek‑style aniseed liqueur called Oozo (**$85**). All spirits are distilled locally using copper pots, with botanicals sourced from South Australian vineyards and native plants. The brand emphasizes sustainability, traditional craftsmanship, and respect for Australia’s Traditional Owners.

## Product Highlights
- **Whisky** – 12‑month maturation in new oak casks; finished in a small‑batch style.
- **Adelaide Gin** – Dry style, smooth botanical blend distilled from South Australian grapes; copper pot distillation.
- **Oozo Liqueur** – Small‑batch ouzo with bold aniseed aroma, no added sugar or preservatives; made from local grapes and distilled in a copper pot.

## Philosophy & Operations
The founders, Peter and Nick, focus on local ingredients, sustainable practices, and maintaining traditional distilling techniques. Spirits are sold online via the website with free shipping over $100 and a 10 % discount on orders above $100. The distillery also offers tours and bookings for visitors.

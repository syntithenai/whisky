# Adelaide Distillery

Adelaide Distillery is a boutique spirit producer based in Adelaide, South Australia. Founded by uncle‑and‑nephew Peter and Nick, the distillery blends Greek heritage with local ingredients to create award‑winning spirits.

- **OOZO Liqueur** – $85.00 (premium liqueur, small‑batch)

All products are available for purchase online with a 10 % shipping discount and free shipping over $100.

## Philosophy & Practices
The distillery emphasizes:
- Small‑batch production
- Local ingredients and South Australian botanicals
- Sustainability in distilling
- Community connection and acknowledgement of Traditional Owners

Visitors can shop online or find the spirits at local venues across Adelaide. The brand is known for its uncompromising dry gin, smooth whisky, and versatile liqueur.

# Japan Customs Drug Smuggling Notice

Japan Customs issues a warning to the public about potential drug‑smuggling recruitment tactics. The notice lists common scenarios—carrying luggage, accepting free trips, or providing personal details for shipping—and urges anyone who encounters suspicious situations to report them confidentially to customs.

## Key Points
- **Common Recruitment Tactics**: carrying someone’s luggage, accepting a free trip, giving contact info for package shipment.
- **Action Required**: Report any suspicious activity to Japan Customs; information will remain confidential.
- **Legal Consequence**: Drug smuggling is a serious crime with severe penalties.

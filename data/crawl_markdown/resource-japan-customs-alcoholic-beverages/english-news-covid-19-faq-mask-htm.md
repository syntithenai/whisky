A1 (For tariffs) When importing disposable, general-use non-woven artificial fiber hygienic masks from China, Korea, etc., such masks are classified under the import tariff code of 6307.90-022 or 6307.90-023, and the tariff rate (conventional tariff) is 4.7%. 100% cotton (gauze) masks fall under the code 6307.90-011, and the tariff rate (conventional tariff) is 6.5%.

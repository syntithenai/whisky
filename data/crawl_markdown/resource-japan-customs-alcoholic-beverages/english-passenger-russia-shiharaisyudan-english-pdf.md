# Export Restrictions to Russia (April 2022)

- **Scope**: Exports of *means of payment* (banknotes and government money bills) and *precious metals* to the Russian Federation now require permission from Japan’s Minister of Finance.
- **Personal effects** of departing passengers/crew are covered by this rule.
- **Exceptions**:
  - Payments normally required for stay in Russia by a Japanese resident.
  - Payments up to ¥100,000 for essential goods (food, clothing, pharmaceuticals) or medical services for Russian residents.
  - Other humane‑reason payments.
- **Precious metals definition**: gold bullion, alloy bullion, non‑circulating gold coins, and any object where gold is the principal material with >50 % weight/price.
- **Special note**: Personal carry‑on items may be exempt, but other exports might still need permission from the Ministry of Economy, Trade and Industry.

# Japan Customs - Passenger Declaration

## Key Facts
- Japan Customs regulates the declaration and clearance of accompanied and unaccompanied articles for passengers entering or leaving Japan.
- Certain restrictions apply to imported goods, including prohibited items and limits on duty-free allowances.
- Foreign visitors must declare all accompanying articles exceeding specified value thresholds.
- Cashless payments are accepted for customs duties and taxes by eligible passengers.

# Reedy Swamp Distillery

Online Store for premium Australian spirits including:
- Liqueurs: Limoncello, Turmeric Honey
- Whiskeys: Wheat Vodka (37%)
- Other Spirits: Rum, Brandy, Gins

## Key Facts
- Family-owned distillery in Tarraganda, NSW, Australia since 2020
- Small-batch production focusing on quality over quantity
- Uses locally sourced ingredients where possible
- Online cellar door for direct purchase and subscription options

## Production Signals
- Limited edition releases showcasing unique flavor profiles
- Emphasis on natural ingredients and traditional distilling methods
- Sustainable practices in sourcing and packaging

## Commercial Signals
- Strong brand positioning around Australian heritage and craftsmanship
- Active social media presence engaging with customers
- Subscription service for regular deliveries of new releases

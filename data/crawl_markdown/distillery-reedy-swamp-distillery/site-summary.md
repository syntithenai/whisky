## Long Description
# Reedy Swamp Distillery

Reedy Swamp Distillery operates in the Bega Valley of New South Wales, Australia. The distillery focuses on **small‑batch, low‑stress fermentation** using aquifer water, solar‑powered stills and rainwater cooling to preserve a clean spirit profile.  Local, home‑grown ingredients are central: wheat for vodka, lemons for limoncello, coffee beans for liqueur, turmeric and honey for a warming spice liqueur, and herbs from the distillery garden for gin.

## Production Philosophy
- **Slow fermentation** with low‑stress yeast cultivation.
- **Solar‑powered stills (10.6 kW)** and rainwater cooling.
- Use of aquifer water to maintain mineral balance.
- Emphasis on clean, healthy spirits.

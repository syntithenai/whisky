# Reedy Swamp Distillery
Reedy Swamp Distillery is a small‑batch Australian producer based in Bega Valley, NSW. They make hand‑crafted spirits and liqueurs using locally sourced or home‑grown ingredients: organic herbs for gin, wheat or sugars for vodka, coffee beans for coffee liqueur, seasonal lemons for limoncello, and more. Production emphasises slow, low‑stress fermentation with quality yeast food, solar‑powered stills (10.6 kW), rainwater cooling, and aquifer water for fermentation.

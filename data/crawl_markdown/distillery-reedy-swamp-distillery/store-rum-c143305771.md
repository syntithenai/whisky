# Reedy Swamp Distillery - Rum

## Key Facts
- Established in 2018, Reedy Swamp Distillery is a small-batch rum producer located in Tarraganda, NSW, Australia.
- The distillery focuses on creating unique, high-quality rums using locally sourced ingredients and traditional production methods.
- Rum offerings include both dark and light varieties to cater to different preferences.

## Production Signals
- **Distillation**: Small-batch pot stills are used to create a rich, flavorful base for their rums.
- **Maturation**: Rums are aged in charred oak barrels to develop complex flavors and aromas.
- **Ingredients**: The distillery uses locally sourced sugarcane juice as the primary ingredient, ensuring freshness and quality.

## Commercial Signals
- Reedy Swamp Distillery aims to create premium rums that stand out in the market by emphasizing unique flavor profiles and sustainable practices.
- Their products are sold both online and through select retail partners across Australia.

## Risks / Unknowns
- As a relatively new distillery, Reedy Swamp faces challenges in building brand awareness and competing with established rum brands.
- The success of their rum offerings will depend on consumer acceptance and the ability to maintain consistent quality.

# Saturday 24th September – Silk Rags Project Auction Launch
Cauldron Distillery hosted the national launch and fundraising auction for The Silk Rags Project on 24 September 2022 at 4 pm. Every Silk Rags cocktail purchased donated \$5 to the charity, which supports cancer patients and research in Tamborine Mountain.

**Event highlights**
- Live music
- Food & drinks (including Silk Rags cocktails)
- Auction of great prizes
- Community spirit

The venue is located at 89‑123 Hartley Road, Tamborine Mountain, Queensland. The distillery also offers tours, gin‑making classes, a kitchen, and a shop with gin, whisky, liqueur, merchandise, and gift cards.

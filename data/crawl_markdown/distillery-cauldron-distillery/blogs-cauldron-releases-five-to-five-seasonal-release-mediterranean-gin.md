# Five‑to‑Five "Seasonal Release" Mediterranean Gin
Cauldron Distillery launches a limited‑run (550 bottles) Mediterranean‑inspired gin, blending dried sage, coriander seeds, rosemary, olive leaf, orange and lemon peel, figs, and star anise. The product is described as aromatic, herbaceous, citrusy, and fruit‑driven with a subtle oily finish.

## Key Points
- **Launch date**: 19 Oct 2022
- **Limited release**: 550 bottles
- **Core botanicals**: dried sage, coriander seeds, rosemary, olive leaf, orange peel, lemon peel, figs, star anise
- **Flavor profile**: herbaceous, citrusy, spicy, fruit‑driven, oily finish
- **Production notes**: trial batches; final star anise at 0.05 g/L
- **Availability**: online and cellar door; exclusive to Customer Club members
- **Contact**: Cauldron Distillery, Tamborine Mountain, QLD, Australia

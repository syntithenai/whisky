# Scenic Rim Farm Gate Trail – Sunday 20 July

Cauldron Distillery is participating in the **Scenic Rim Farm Gate Trail** on 20 July, inviting visitors to enjoy live music, an extensive cocktail menu, stone‑baked pizzas and a special July Cocktail of the Month – *The Hot Honey Sour* ($20). Guests can also book a Gin Blending class or try the award‑winning Queensland Single Malt Whisky Tasting Experience (silver at 2025 IWSC Awards) for $20 per person.

Other local venues such as Boxer Brewing and Witches Falls Winery are also part of the trail. Maps, itineraries and further details can be downloaded from the site.

*Location:* 89‑123 Hartley Road, Tamborine Mountain, Queensland 4272, Australia.

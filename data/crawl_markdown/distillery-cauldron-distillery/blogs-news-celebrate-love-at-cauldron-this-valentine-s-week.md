# Celebrate Love at Cauldron This Valentine’s Week
Cauldron Distillery is offering a themed menu for Valentine's week:
- **Chocolate Dipped Strawberries Cocktail** – $20 (available all week)
- **Warm Dessert Pizza** – $15 (Wednesday‑Sunday)
- **Valentine’s Dessert Box** – $12 (Saturday & Sunday only, includes brownies and strawberries)

All seven of Cauldron’s gins were named in Ginuary’s Top 50 for the second consecutive year. The distillery invites guests to book a table or experience to enjoy these treats on their grounds.

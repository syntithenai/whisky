# Sunday 18th September – Towri Sheep Markets
Cauldron Distillery is inviting visitors to the **Towri Spring Farmers Market** on 18 September, 2022. The event runs from 9 am‑3 pm and features:
- Live music & milking demos
- Sheep cuddles & local produce
- Private access to Towri Gardens
- Food, coffee, wine tastings, gin tasting
- A farm shop with local goodies
- Optional workshops and a pre‑booked picnic cheese platter

Tickets are available via Humanitix.

> *“We welcome you all to come support your local farmers and fill your fridges with scrumptious local produce!”*

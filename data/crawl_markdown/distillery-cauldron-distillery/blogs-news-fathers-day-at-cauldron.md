# Father’s Day at Cauldron Distillery

Cauldron Distillery invites families to celebrate Father’s Day on September 7 with a day of food, live music and special cocktails. Highlights include:
- Live music by Clint White (12:30‑4:30 pm)
- Stone‑baked pizzas, German‑style hot dogs, and the signature **Hazy Dad** cocktail
- The month’s cocktail of the month, **Cauldron Sunrise**, available all September for $20
- Gift ideas such as the award‑winning Queensland Single Malt, gin, distilling classes and gift vouchers
- A note from founders Glen, Darren and Greg celebrating dads.

The event is free to attend on the Cauldron lawns; lunch bookings can be made online.

- Date: Sunday 7 September 2025

- Price of cocktail of the month: $20
- Gift bundle offer: buy any two bottles and save.

## Long Description
# Cauldron Distillery Overview

Cauldron Distillery (89 – 123 Hartley Road, Tamborine Mountain, QLD) operates on a 525 m altitude farm‑to‑glass model. The site sells a range of spirits – Queensland Single Malt Whisky, a signature gin line (Sunset, Mountain, Coastal, Mediterranean, True Blue and limited releases), three vermouths, an Australian Agave collection (Blanco, Reposado, Caffe Freddo Della Montagna) and branded merchandise. All products are sold via Shopify with Apple Pay, Google Pay, Mastercard, Visa and Union Pay.

## Spirits
- **Queensland Single Malt Whisky** – flagship single‑malt, awarded a 2025 IWSC Silver Medal (94/100). Price $150.
- **Gin Range** – Signature gins start at $33; Mediterranean and True Blue at $85; limited releases up to $125. The brand also offers barrel‑aged Christmas gin.
- **Vermouths** – Piano Rock Rosso, Bianco and Rosato each priced at $75.
- **Agave Spirits** – Blanco (lime, chilli & saltbush) $90, Reposado $100, Caffe Freddo Della Montagna $80.

## Events & Experiences
Cauldron hosts regular live‑music nights, gin‑blending classes, themed parties (e.g., Salsa & Gin Night), charity auctions and seasonal festivals such as the Tamborine Mountain Show. Tickets are sold online via Eventbrite or direct booking links; many events include food, drinks and a tour of the distillery.

## Merchandise
The shop offers apparel (hoodie $55, t‑shirt $30, scarf $30, beanie $30) and candles (small $22, large gin‑scented $42). All items are available through Shopify.

## Long Description
# Cauldron Distillery Overview
Cauldron Distillery is a small‑batch spirit producer located in Tamborine Mountain, Queensland. Their portfolio spans craft gins (including the flagship *525 Gin*), a single‑malt whisky, Australian‑made agave spirits, liqueurs, vermouths and a range of seasonal experiences such as tours, cocktail masterclasses, pottery workshops and tasting events.

## Product Lines
- **Gin** – 525 Gin (classic botanical blend), Five‑to‑Five Gin, Limited‑Release Christmas Gins (gin‑clear & barrel‑aged). 
- **Whisky** – Queensland Single Malt Whisky, aged in ex‑bourbon barrels and finished in Australian Apera casks; awarded Silver at the 2025 IWSC.
- **Agave Spirits** – Blanco and Reposado agave spirits, plus a coffee liqueur *Café Freddo Della Montagna*.
- **Vermouth & Liqueurs** – Piano Rock Vermouth (fortified wine with herbs/spices), coffee, sambuca, limoncello, negroni liqueur.
- **Experiences** – Gin history tours, cocktail masterclasses, pottery workshops (*Cauldron & Clay*), tasting paddles, and seasonal pairings such as *Cocktails & Cheese in the Sun*.

## Events & Community Engagement
The distillery regularly hosts themed events tied to local festivals (Eat Local Week/Month) and holidays. Highlights include:
- **Eat Local Week – Gin History Tour** (July 2022): guided tour, complimentary G&T, quiz‑win prize.
- **Twilight Cocktail Masterclass** (May 2024): adult‑only evening teaching shake/stir/muddle techniques.
- **Distiller for the Day Competition** (June 2023): six winners spent a day learning gin production.
- **Cocktails & Cheese in the Sun** (March 2023): seasonal gin cocktails with cheese board, $69 package.
- **Valentine’s Week Menu** (Feb 2024): dessert‑cocktail menu featuring Chocolate Dipped Strawberries Cocktail ($20), Warm Dessert Pizza ($15) and Valentine’s Dessert Box ($12).
- **Cauldron & Clay Workshop** (April 2022): beginner pottery session creating ceramic gin cups, $135 per person.

## Media & Awards
The distillery has been featured in *The Scenic Rim News* for its first anniversary and has earned a Silver award at the 2025 International Wine & Spirits Competition for its single‑malt whisky. The brand emphasizes local ingredients, community events and sustainable practices.

## Online Presence

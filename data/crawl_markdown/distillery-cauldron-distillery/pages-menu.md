# Cauldron Kitchen Menu
Cauldron Distillery’s kitchen has reopened as the **Smithton Fire Truck Kitchen** with a fresh menu and new chef. The restaurant offers pizzas, bar snacks, charcuterie, cheese boards, and a drinks menu featuring gin, whisky, Australian agave liqueur, vodka, vermouth, tonics & garnishes, merchandise, and gift cards.

- **Opening hours:** Tuesday‑Sunday for pizza; Wednesday‑Sunday (and school holidays) for the full menu. Closed on Christmas Day, Boxing Day, and New Year’s Day.
- **Events & bookings:** Book tables, experiences, tours, gin‑making or blending classes, and live music through the site.
- **Contact:** 07 5545 0156 | hello@cauldrondistillery.com.au
- **Location:** 89‑123 Hartley Road, Tamborine Mountain, Queensland 4272, Australia.

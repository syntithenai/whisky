# Salsa and Gin Night

Cauldron Distillery hosted a **Salsa & Gin Night** on **18 June 2022**. The event cost **$25 per person** and included:
- One alcoholic or non‑alcoholic drink
- A 30‑minute introductory dance lesson (starting at 6:30 pm)
- A Latin music party that ran until late
The venue was the inside building, offering plenty of space for dancing. Guests were encouraged to arrive early to watch the sunset.

Bookings are required and can be made via Eventbrite.

# Cauldron Distillery
Cauldron Distillery is a handcrafted Australian distillery located on Tamborine Mountain, Queensland. The site offers a range of spirits—including gin, whisky, Australian agave spirits, vodka, vermouth, liqueur, and tonics—alongside merchandise and gift cards. Visitors can book tables, tours, gin‑making or blending classes, and enjoy live music events. The flagship product is the **Queensland Single Malt Whisky**, aged in ex‑bourbon barrels and finished in Australian Apera casks, priced at $150.

Key highlights:
- **Australian Agave Spirits**: Blanco (Lime, Chilli & Saltbush) – $90, Reposado – $100, Caffe Freddo Della Montagna – $80.
- **Gin Range**: Sunset Gin, Mountain Gin, Coastal (Navy Strength) – all starting at $33.
- **Vermouths**: Piano Rock Bianco, Rosso, and Rosato with distinct flavor profiles for cocktails.
- Regular events include live music, tours, and themed cocktail releases such as the April “Marshmallow Madness” ($20).

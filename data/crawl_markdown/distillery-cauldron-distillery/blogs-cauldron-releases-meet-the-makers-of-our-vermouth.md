# Meet the Makers of Our Vermouth
Cauldron Distillery introduces Jon Heslop, Chief Winemaker and founder of Witches Falls Winery, and Darren Stewart, Head Distiller who transitioned from podiatry to spirits. Their collaboration produced a Rosso vermouth that won *Best Vermouth in Australia*. The article outlines their backgrounds, the founding of Cauldron Distillery on Tamborine Mountain, and the growth from three gins to a full spirit range including vermouth.

# The Story behind our new Piano Rock Vermouth
Cauldron Distillery collaborated with award‑winning winemaker Jon Heslop of Witches Falls Winery to create a range of Australian‑style vermouths, including the flagship *Piano Rock* variant. The project aimed to offer vibrant, fruit‑forward expressions that can be enjoyed neat, mixed or in cocktails.

## Key points
- Vermouth is a fortified wine flavored with herbs, spices and fruits.
- The name derives from German *Wermut* (wormwood).
- Cauldron’s recipe uses local mountain fruits, exotic spices and traditional herbs.
- Piano Rock references the iconic Tamborine Mountain landmark.

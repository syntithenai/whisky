# How to get your hands on our Whisky!

Cauldron Distillery announces the release of its first Queensland Single Malt Whisky. The public launch is set for **23 Feb 2025 at 10 am** via the website and in‑store, with a pre‑sale link sent to mailing list members on **20 Feb 2025**. Early buyers can purchase a *Whisky Baron Pack*—a limited edition bundle that includes a 500 ml bottle, a 150 ml hip flask, two exclusive event invitations, and free shipping.

The post also invites readers to join the Cauldron mailing list for future updates and highlights the distillery’s location in Tamborine Mountain, Queensland.

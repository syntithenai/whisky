# An Afternoon with Shane Howard (Goanna) and Special Guests

Cauldron Distillery is hosting a ticketed music event featuring Australian folk‑rock legend **Shane Howard** of Goanna, along with **Russell Smith** and **Dane Sharp**. The gathering will take place on Sunday, 25 August from 4 pm to 7 pm at the distillery’s venue in Tamborine Mountain, Queensland.

- **Tickets**: Limited availability; purchase online via the provided link.
- **Food & Drink**: Notorious E.A.T food truck will serve dinner until 7 pm. Cheese and charcuterie hampers are available for purchase on‑site or pre‑ordered with tickets.
- **Rules**: No outside food allowed; all attendees must hold a ticket regardless of age.
- **Atmosphere**: The event promises an evening of iconic Australian folk‑rock tunes in a cool mountain setting.

> *“It promises to be a fantastic event celebrating iconic Aussie Folk Rock tunes.”*

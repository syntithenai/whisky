# Scenic Rim Farm Gate Trail – Sunday 30 March
Cauldron Distillery will host live music (Nick Waters, 1‑4 pm), a full cocktail & food menu and $15 Piano Rock Spritzers featuring Piano Rock Vermouth and Strange Love mixers. Guests can also book a Gin Blending class. The event is part of the Scenic Rim Farm Gate Trail alongside Boxer Brewing and Witches Falls Winery.

# Queensland Single Malt Whisky

Cauldron Distillery’s first whisky release is a single‑malt aged on Tamborine Mountain at 525 m above sea level. It is finished in Australian Apera casks after initial maturation in ex‑bourbon barrels, giving vanilla, salted caramel and candied fruit notes with a buttery mouthfeel and a long oak‑spice finish. The whisky earned **Silver (94/100)** at the 2025 IWSC.

- **Location**: Tamborine Mountain, Queensland
- **Maturation**: ex‑bourbon barrels → Australian Apera casks
- **Awards**: Silver, 2025 IWSC
- **Price & purchase**: Not listed on page

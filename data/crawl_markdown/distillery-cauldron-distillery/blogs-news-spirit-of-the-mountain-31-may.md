# Spirit of the Mountain – 31 May

Cauldron Distillery is hosting its **Spirit of the Mountain** event on Saturday, 31 May 2025 at the Tamborine Mountain estate. The afternoon and evening will feature:
- A Welcome to Country by the Wangerriburra people.
- Live music led by Ben Lee with local musicians.
- Seasonal food hampers from the Cauldron kitchen paired with award‑winning spirits such as Five to Five Gin and a new Queensland Single Malt Whisky.
- An exclusive whisky tasting experience.

Tickets cost **$40** per adult (children 12 yrs & under free). Hampers can be pre‑ordered for $60 when booking tickets. The event is part of Eat Local Month 2025, celebrating Scenic Rim produce and culture.

# Eat Local Month – Twilight Cocktail Masterclass

Cauldron Distillery invites guests to a **Twilight Cocktail Masterclass** on May 1 2024. The evening, led by an expert mixologist, teaches shaking, stirring and muddling techniques while creating two cocktails from premium ingredients. Set inside the working distillery, it offers a behind‑the‑scenes experience for adults only (18+). Tickets can be purchased via the provided link.

## Key Details
- **Date & Time**: May 1 2024, twilight hours
- **Location**: Cauldron Distillery, Tamborine Mountain, Queensland
- **Audience**: 18+ only
- **Activities**: Cocktail‑making workshop (shake, stir, muddle)
- **Tickets**: Buy online through the event link

## About Cauldron Distillery
Cauldron Distillery is a boutique distillery in Queensland offering gin, whisky, Australian agave liqueur, vodka, vermouth, tonics and garnishes. They also host events such as gin‑making classes, live music, and kitchen experiences.

# Negroni – Cauldron Distillery

Cauldron Distillery’s latest release, the **Negroni**, blends their Australian Dry Gin with Piano Rock Rosso Vermouth and a local amaro. The cocktail is described as bold, balanced, herbal, with bitter notes, aromatic gin, subtle vermouth sweetness, orange zest hints, a slightly dry finish, and an alluring depth of flavour. Serve three measures over ice, garnish with an orange wheel.

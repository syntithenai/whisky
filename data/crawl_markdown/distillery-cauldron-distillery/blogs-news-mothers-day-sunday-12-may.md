# Mother's Day Event at Cauldron Distillery
Cauldron Distillery is celebrating Mother’s Day on Sunday 12 May 2024 with a day‑long programme that includes:
- **Candle‑making classes** (11 am–1 pm) in partnership with Sip and Paint Tamborine Mountain, priced at $50 per person.
- **Fire‑‘n’ Dough Wood Fire Pizza’s** available from 11 am.
- **Antipasto & Cheese & Meats hampers** by Sorella & Co. (3 sizes) that come with the *Mother’s Day Cocktail* – Diva’s Delight.
The event is held at Cauldron Distillery, Tamborine Mountain, Queensland.

> Book your table and order hampers now to avoid missing out.

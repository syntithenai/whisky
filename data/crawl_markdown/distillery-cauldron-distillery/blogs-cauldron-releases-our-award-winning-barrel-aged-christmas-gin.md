# Our Award Winning Barrel Aged Christmas Gin
Cauldron Distillery has won *Best in Class* for its matured gin at the Australian Gin Distillers Association Awards.

- **Maturation**: 200 L of over‑proof 2024 Christmas Gin aged in a local Muscat fortified wine barrel (A Witches Falls Winery Solera Muscat Barrique Barrel).
- **Flavor profile**: Sweet, sticky sultanas and raisins from the Muscat; mild oak; traditional Christmas spices (cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, orange, cherry). Works well on the rocks, over ice‑cream, or mixed with cola/ginger beer.
- **ABV**: 45 %.
- **Availability**: Limited quantities for Christmas; bundle discounts up to $50 and free shipping.

> *“The result was a wonderfully mellow and luscious expression of our Christmas Gin.”*

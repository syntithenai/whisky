# Citrus Fusion Vodka

Cauldron Distillery has released a new vodka called **Citrus Fusion**. It is distilled at 525 m altitude, flavored with local lemons, and triple‑filtered for extra purity. The spirit can be served neat, on the rocks, or in cocktails such as a Moscow Mule.

## Recipe Idea
- **Moscow Mule** – 15 ml lime juice + 50 ml Citrus Fusion Vodka + 180 ml ginger beer, ice, garnish with lemon/lime wheel and mint.

The product is available for purchase online or in‑store at the distillery.

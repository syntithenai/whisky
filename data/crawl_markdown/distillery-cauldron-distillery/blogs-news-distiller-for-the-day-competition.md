# Distiller for the Day Competition
Cauldron Distillery announced a $1,000‑valued competition in June 2023 where six winners can spend a day learning gin production.
- **Schedule**: 8 am briefing → botanical prep → breakfast & gin tasting → guided distillation class (700 ml bottle) → lunch with cocktails → wrap‑up & Q&A. Winners receive a signed bottle after proofing.
- **Entry**: Follow on Facebook, tag a friend, subscribe to newsletter, and purchase any 700 ml gin online or in‑store during June.
- **Terms**: Participants must be 18+, prizes non‑transferable, random selection, and winners notified by email.

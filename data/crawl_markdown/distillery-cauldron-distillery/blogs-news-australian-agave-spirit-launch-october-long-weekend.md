# Australian Agave Spirit Launch
Cauldron Distillery announces three new Australian‑made agave spirits for the October long weekend (4‑6 Oct). The range includes:
- **Blanco** – lime, chilli and saltbush notes.
- **Reposado** – aged in American oak with caramel, vanilla and cinnamon layers.
- **Café Freddo Della Montagna** – a coffee liqueur built on the agave spirit, featuring dark chocolate, caramel, toffee and spices.

The spirits are produced onsite from organic Mexican agave nectar fermented with a custom yeast strain and distilled under strict parameters. The launch showcases Cauldron’s local reinterpretation of agave, traditionally associated only with tequila and mezcal.

# Australian Agave Spirits – Cauldron Distillery
Cauldron Distillery launches three Australian‑made agave spirits:
- **Blanco (Lime, Chilli & Saltbush)** – bright lime, fresh chilli and saltbush for a zesty kick.
- **Reposado** – rested in American oak with caramel, vanilla and cinnamon notes.
- **Caffè Freddo Della Montagna** – coffee liqueur built on the Blanco base, layered with chocolate, caramel, toffee and spices.
The page explains how these spirits differ from traditional Mexican tequila/mezcal by embracing local Australian flavours and creative production techniques.

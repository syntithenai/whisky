# Eat Local Week – Gin History Tour

Cauldron Distillery invites visitors to a guided tour of its 18‑acre gin facility on **2 July 2022**. Participants receive a complimentary G&T, learn about the history and craft of their signature *525 Gin*, and can win a 200 ml bottle of Five‑to‑Five by answering a quiz.

- **Date & Time:** Saturday 2 July 2 pm
- **Capacity:** 20 adults per session
- **Booking:** Available via the site’s “Book Here” link

The event is part of the broader *Eat Local Week* celebration and showcases Cauldron’s commitment to local ingredients and community engagement.

# Spicing it up with Cauldron's Limited Release Christmas Gins
Cauldron Distillery released two limited‑edition Christmas gins in December 2022: a **gin‑clear** version and a **Barrel‑Aged** version aged in a former Solera Muscat oak barrel. The gin is crafted with traditional Christmas spices (juniper, orange peel, ginger, cloves, cinnamon, nutmeg) plus glacé cherries, giving it a “Christmas cake” or “grandmother’s pudding” flavour profile. The Barrel‑Aged gin adds rich sultana and French‑oak notes from the wine barrel, resulting in a pinkish‑rose hue.

Both gins were sold at **$99 AUD** (non‑barrel) and **$125 AUD** for 700 ml (Barrel‑Aged). Only 350 bottles of the Barrel‑Aged gin were released. The distillery promotes its gin‑making classes, where participants can create their own Christmas gin.

The article also highlights Light Ray’s Dark Soda as a complementary mixer and mentions Cauldron’s partnership with Witches Falls Winery for barrel sourcing.

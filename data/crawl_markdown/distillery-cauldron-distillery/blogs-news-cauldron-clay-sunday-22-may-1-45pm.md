# Cauldron & Clay Workshop
Cauldron Distillery hosted a beginner‑friendly ceramic gin cup workshop on **23 April 2022**. Participants paid **$135 per person**, received all clay, tools, glazing and firing, plus a complimentary drink of an award‑winning gin (or non‑alcoholic option). Larissa, a 20‑year experienced artist, guided hand‑building and decorating techniques. After the session she fired and glazed the cups in either a multi‑faceted white or semi‑matte black glaze. The finished cups were wrapped and shipped within 3–4 weeks (or collected locally). Sessions run at **1:45 pm** on Sundays, with future dates listed.

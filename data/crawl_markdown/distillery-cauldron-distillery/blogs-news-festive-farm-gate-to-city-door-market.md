# Festive Farm Gate to City Door Market
Cauldron Distillery will attend the Breakfast Creek Lifestyle Precinct Farm Gate to City Door Market on Saturday 30 November from 8 am‑12 pm. The event is a gourmet foodie affair featuring regional flavours, unique produce and skilled craftsmanship from the Scenic Rim, offering an opportunity for visitors to pick up Christmas gifting goodies.

**Event details**
- **Date & time:** Saturday 30 November, 8 am‑12 pm
- **Location:** Breakfast Creek Lifestyle Precinct Farm Gate to City Door Market (Brisbane)

# Black Gate Distillery

## Key Facts
- Australian small batch whisky and rum producer
- Located in Mendooran, NSW
- Founded by Ian & Janice McLean
- Small-batch production focusing on quality over quantity
- Uses locally sourced ingredients where possible

## Production Signals
- Limited release of single barrel expressions
- Emphasis on traditional pot still distillation
- Aged in reclaimed oak barrels from other Australian distilleries
- Experimentation with different cask finishes

## Commercial Signals
- Membership club offering exclusive releases and tasting experiences
- Wholesale partnerships with select retailers across Australia
- Strong social media presence engaging with whisky enthusiasts
- Limited edition bottlings creating buzz among collectors

## Risks / Unknowns
- Relatively new entrant to the Australian market
- Smaller scale production may limit availability
- Dependence on local sourcing for ingredients and casks
- Competition from established regional distilleries

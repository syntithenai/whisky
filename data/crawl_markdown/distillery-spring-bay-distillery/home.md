# Spring Bay Distillery
Spring Bay Distillery (Tasmania) produces award‑winning single malt whisky and a range of spirits, including gin, vodka, brandy and specialty products such as sloe gin. The distillery operates two sites: a grain‑to‑glass facility in Cambridge and a coastal spirit site at Spring Beach. Products are crafted from premium Tasmanian barley, East Coast rainwater, and carefully selected casks.

## Key Points
- **Founded**: 2015
- **Location**: Cambridge (grain‑to‑glass) & Spring Beach (spirit site)
- **Core products**: Single Malt Whisky (Origins Collection, Rheban Cask Strength, Family Reserve), Gin, Vodka, Brandy, Sloe Gin
- **Specialty**: 10th Anniversary Release, Sloe Gin + Paste Bundle
- **Shipping**: Free over $150 AUD
- **Visitor experience**: Tours, tastings, and a “Bottle Your Own Whisky” solera experience.

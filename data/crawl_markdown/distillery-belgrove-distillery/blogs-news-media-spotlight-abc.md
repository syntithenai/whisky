# Media Spotlight – ABC

- **Topic**: Peter Bignell’s biodiesel‑powered rye whisky still in Tasmania.
- **Key points**:
  - First Australian rye whisky still powered entirely by biodiesel.
  - Biodiesel is produced from old fish and chip oil collected from Hobart takeaway shops.
  - The still is built from scratch by Bignell after a welding lesson and local design help.
  - Claims to produce some of the least carbon‑intensive whiskies in Australia.
- **Media**: Audio clip (MP3) linked on the page.

# Gordon Ramsay Visits Belgrove Distillery

Belgrove Distillery welcomed celebrity chef **Gordon Ramsay** for a tour of its Tasmanian grain‑to‑glass operation. Ramsay explored the distillery’s commitment to 100 % estate‑grown ingredients, tasted award‑winning spirits, and discussed the paddock‑to‑bottle philosophy that defines Belgrove.

> *“We were thrilled to welcome the legendary chef Gordon Ramsay… It was an honor to share our passion for artisanal distilling with someone who shares the same dedication to quality and authenticity in his craft.”*

The visit highlighted the distillery’s innovative approach to whisky and spirit production, reinforcing its reputation as a leading Tasmanian craft producer.

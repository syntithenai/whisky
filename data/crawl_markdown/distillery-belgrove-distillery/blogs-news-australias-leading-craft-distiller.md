# Australia’s Leading Craft Distiller – Belgrove Distillery

Belgrove Distillery, based in Tasmania, prides itself on a fully hand‑crafted whisky production process. From growing and malting its own grain to distilling in hand‑built stills, every step is guided by sensory evaluation rather than automation. The distillery also sources peat from a family farm and re‑cooperates barrels on site, sampling regularly until the spirit reaches the desired flavour profile.

The brand has earned international recognition: *Whisky Magazine* named it “Icons of Whisky Australia” and “Craft Producer of the Year 2019”; Jim Murray’s *Whisky Bible* awarded several Belgrove whiskies “Liquid Gold.” The restaurant Noma in Copenhagen selected five Belgrove products for its Sydney pop‑up bar.

## Key Points
- **Hand‑grown grain** harvested locally and malted on site.
- **Manual mashing, fermentation, double distillation** using hand‑built stills.
- **Peat sourced from a family bog** with coastal influence.
- **Barrel re‑cooperating & firing** performed at the distillery; maturation monitored by nose and taste.
- **Minimal instrumentation** – only a thermometer for mashing; alcohol measurement is record‑keeping.
- **International accolades**: Whisky Magazine, Jim Murray’s *Whisky Bible*, Noma restaurant selection.

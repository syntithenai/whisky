# Belgrove Distillery

Belgrove Distillery is a Tasmanian estate whisky producer that grows, malts, ferments, distills and barrels its own rye on site. The company offers several small‑batch products including **White Rye**, **Rye Whisky 46%**, **Wholly Shit Smokin’ Barrel** (a rye‑based spirit), and an **Oat Whisky Batch 8**. All products are bio‑fuel powered and sold via Shopify, with international shipping currently unavailable due to a fuel crisis.

- Location: 3121 Midland Highway, Kempton, Tasmania, AU
- Licence number: 65290
- Age restriction: 18+ only

The site features a newsletter signup, social media links (Instagram & Facebook), and a store with product listings and prices in AUD.

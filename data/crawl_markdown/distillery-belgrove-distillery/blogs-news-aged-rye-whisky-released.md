# Aged Rye Whisky Release

- **Product**: Belgrove Distillery 100% Rye Whisky (500 ml) – ABV 42 %
- **Cask‑strength version**: 59 % ABV, same 500 ml size.
- **Maturation**: Several years in barrels ranging from 50 L to 200 L.
- **Filtering**: Neither whisky is chill‑filtered.
- **Tasting notes (42 %)**: Bright gold; dill/pickle opening, chamomile tea, boiled lolly, cedar oak, peppermint. Silky entry, medium dry chamomile, sour dough bread, dill and caraway. Dry, mildly tannic finish with subtle aftertaste.
- **Tasting notes (59 %)**: Bright gold; honeycomb & peppermint, green/dill/sweet mustard pickle, sponge cake, mild bitter mustard seed‑like notes. Intense, warming, mildly tannic finish.
- **Evaluation**: 94 points – high scores across nose, palate, alcohol, finish.

> *This is Belgrove’s inaugural rye release, available in both standard and cask‑strength variants.*

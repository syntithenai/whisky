# Distilled Spirits Exports Volume – December 2025

The U.S. commercial export data for distilled spirits in December 2025 (proof gallons) are presented by class and destination. Key highlights:
- **Whisky**: Total exports of 40,029,247 proof‑gallons, a 10.7 % drop from the same month last year.
- **Rum**: 6,381,679 proof‑gallons, up 18.5 % YoY.
- **Brandy**: 401,937 proof‑gallons, up 64 % YoY.
- **Gin**: 2,499,325 proof‑gallons, down 21.2 % YoY.
- **Vodka**: 21,058,082 proof‑gallons, down 7.9 % YoY.
- **Cordials**: 5,849,104 proof‑gallons, up 18.7 % YoY.

The report also lists detailed destination totals for each spirit class, showing the largest markets (e.g., Australia, Japan, United Kingdom) and changes versus January‑December 2024.

Overall, U.S. exports of distilled spirits declined by 3.9 % to 133,556,176 proof‑gallons in December 2025 compared with the same period last year.

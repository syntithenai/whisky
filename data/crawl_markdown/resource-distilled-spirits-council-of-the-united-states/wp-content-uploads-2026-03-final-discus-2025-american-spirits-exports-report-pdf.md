# 2025 DISCUS American Spirits Exports Report
- U.S. spirits exports fell **3.8 %** to **$2.37 billion** in 2025, largely due to a sharp drop in Canada and the EU.
- **American Whiskey** remained the top export category at $1.08 billion but dropped 19 % from 2024; it now accounts for only **45 %** of total U.S. spirits exports (first time below 50 % since 1996).
- The EU is still the largest market (**51 %** of total), yet exports to the EU fell 35 % in 2025.
- Canada’s provincial sales bans caused a **70 %** decline in U.S. spirits exports to Canada.
- Exports outside Canada and the EU grew **13.2 %**, with notable gains in Brazil, the UK, Colombia, Singapore, UAE, and Australia.
- The report stresses that future growth hinges on restoring zero‑for‑zero tariffs, opening new markets (India, Brazil, Vietnam, South Africa), and stabilising global trade.

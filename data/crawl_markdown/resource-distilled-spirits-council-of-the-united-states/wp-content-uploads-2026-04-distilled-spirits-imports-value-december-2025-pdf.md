# Distilled Spirits Imports – Value (December 2025)

This PDF presents U.S. duty‑paid imports of distilled spirits by value for December 2025 and the preceding year, broken down by spirit class, container type, and country of origin. Key highlights:
- **Total imports**: $8.41 billion in 2025 vs. $11.42 billion in 2024 (‑26.4 %).
- **Whiskey**: $65.7 million in 2025, a 46.8 % drop from the previous year.
- **Gin**: $28.4 million in 2025, down 60.3 %. 
- **Rum**: $19.3 million in 2025, up 10.7 %.
- **Brandy**: $46.8 million in 2025, a 67.5 % decline.
- **Vodka**: $40.8 million in 2025, down 56.5 %. 
- **Cordial**: $180.5 million in 2025, down 41.4 %. 
- **Tequila**: $365.9 million in 2025, down 50.7 %.

The report lists detailed monthly totals for each country and container type (bottled vs. bulk). The data are sourced from Customs Service data prepared by the Census Bureau (CD‑ROM, December 2025).

---

## Key Data Points
- **Total imports (value)**: $8,406,784,847 in 2025.
- **Whiskey**: $65,727,099 (bottled) + $1,251,650,692 (bulk) = $1,317,377,791 total.
- **Gin**: $28,437,873 (bottled) + $285,283,736 (bulk) = $313,721,609 total.
- **Rum**: $19,274,807 (bottled) + $172,102,348 (bulk) = $191,377,155 total.
- **Brandy**: $46,827,091 (bottled) + $839,653,126 (bulk) = $886,480,217 total.
- **Vodka**: $40,766,213 (bottled) + $896,886,595 (bulk) = $937,652,808 total.
- **Cordial**: $132,092,196 (bottled) + $1,197,804,229 (bulk) = $1,329,896,425 total.
- **Tequila**: $365,865,212 (bottled) + $3,446,139,786 (bulk) = $3,812,005,998 total.

The PDF also notes that brandy values include Pisco, Singani, and Slivovitz over $3.43 liter.

---

## Observations
- **Whiskey** imports fell sharply in 2025, largely due to a decline from Canada (‑31 %) and the U.K. (‑54 %).
- **Gin** saw a steep drop, especially from the U.K. (‑71 %) and other countries.
- **Rum** experienced modest growth, driven by increases from Jamaica (169 % bulk) and Barbados (96 % bulk).
- **Brandy** imports were heavily concentrated in France (‑68 %) and Canada (‑31 %).
- **Vodka** imports declined across most major suppliers, notably the U.K. (‑56 %).
- **Cordial** imports fell mainly due to reductions from the U.K. (‑41 %) and other countries.
- **Tequila** imports dropped significantly in all major markets.

# Irish Whiskey Market Overview

- **Market size**: In 2018, nearly 4.7 million 9‑liter cases of Irish whiskey were sold in the U.S., generating about \$1 billion in revenue.
- **Growth trend**: From 2002 to 2018, total sales rose from 0 to 4,686 000 cases (≈979 % growth). High‑end and super‑premium categories grew fastest, with increases of 1,106 % and 3,385 %, respectively.
- **Price category revenue**: Supplier gross revenues climbed from \$0 in 2002 to \$1,011 million in 2018, driven largely by the high‑end and super‑premium segments.

These figures illustrate a rapidly expanding U.S. Irish whiskey market with strong premiumisation.

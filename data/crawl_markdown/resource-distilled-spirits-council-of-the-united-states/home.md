# Distilled Spirits Council of the United States
The **Distilled Spirits Council (DISCUS)** is the national trade association representing U.S. distillers and marketers. It advocates for industry interests in Washington, state capitals, and abroad, focusing on policy, data, economic impact, trade, moderation, sustainability, and education.

Key highlights:
- Generates **$250 B** in annual economic activity and supports **1.7 M jobs**.
- Represents **2,200+ distilleries** across the country.
- Offers resources such as data dashboards, publications, a professional academy, and event venues near the Capitol.
- Publishes news releases on trade, exports, and policy actions (e.g., *Toasts Not Tariffs* coalition).

The site serves as an advocacy hub, providing research reports, legislative updates, and educational tools for industry stakeholders.

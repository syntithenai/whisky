# Distilled Spirits Council of the United States (DSC) Overview

- **Mission**: Advocate for U.S. distillers, promote responsible consumption, and influence policy.
- **Economic Impact**: Generates ~$250 B annually, supports 1.7 M jobs, represents >2,200 distilleries.
- **Key Programs**:
  - *Discus Academy* – professional education for industry producers.
  - *Destination Distillery* – tourism initiative linked to the American Whiskey Trail.
  - *Spirited Advocate Podcast* – media outreach.
- **Policy Focus**: Trade (e.g., tariffs), federal regulations, state market modernizations, safety & risk management, sustainability.
- **Resources**: Publications, data dashboards (state, economic impact, trade), code of responsible practices, membership benefits.

---

## Key Highlights
- *Toasts Not Tariffs* coalition opposes U.S. tariffs harming hospitality and distilling jobs.
- Annual Economic Briefing is the industry’s flagship event for media and analysts.
- DSC maintains a comprehensive data portal covering state‑level metrics, import/export statistics, and tourism impact.
- Membership includes access to policy updates, events, and educational partners.

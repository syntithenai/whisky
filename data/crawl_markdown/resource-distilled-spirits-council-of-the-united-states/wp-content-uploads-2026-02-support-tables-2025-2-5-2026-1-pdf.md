# U.S. Spirits Market 2025 Overview

- **Total 9‑liter case volume**: 318 k cases (≈ $318 M).  
- **Whiskey dominates** with 70 % of total volume, followed by vodka (23 %) and cocktails/RTDs (27 %).  
- **Growth trends**: Most categories decline in volume; only Canadian whiskey (+85 %) and cordials (+123 %) show strong gains.  
- **Revenue outlook**: Overall supplier revenue growth is modest (~‑3 % for whiskey, +7 % for tequila/mezcal).  
- **Price segmentation**: Distinct tiers (Value, Premium, High‑End, Super‑Premium) are defined by 9‑liter bottle price ranges across all spirit types.

### Historical Market Share (1999‑2025)
- Spirits share peaked at 28.7 % in 2000, fell to 15.8 % by 2025.
- Beer remained the dominant segment (~56 %).
- Wine share declined from 15.8 % to 15.7 % over the period.

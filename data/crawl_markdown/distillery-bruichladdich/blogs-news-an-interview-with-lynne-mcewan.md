# An interview with Lynne McEwan
Lynne McEwan, Bruichladdich’s Global Marketing Manager, shares her journey from tour guide to brand director and her commitment to community‑driven whisky production.

- **Career path** – Started as a tour guide in 2001, moved through data entry, sales, ambassador work, and finally brand direction after the distillery joined Rémy Cointreau’s network.
- **Vision** – Emphasises transparency, storytelling, and knowledge‑sharing to unlock whisky’s depth.
- **Community focus** – Highlights Bruichladdich’s role in creating local jobs, supporting women in the industry, and fostering regenerative practices.
- **Future outlook** – Calls for open‑source collaboration across distilleries to drive sustainable change.

---

## Key takeaways
- First tasting in South Africa (2004) sparked her passion.
- Bruichladdich’s resurrection in 2001 was driven by Jim McEwan’s vision.
- The distillery now boasts a female‑led senior team and flexible work policies.
- Lynne champions equal opportunities, paternity leave, and women’s support groups.
- She believes whisky can be a catalyst for positive community impact.

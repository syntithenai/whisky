# Bringing Rock'ndaal to life with Ailsa Hayes
Ailsa Hayes, Head of Customer Experience & Events at Bruichladdich Distillery, leads a ten‑person team that orchestrates the annual **Fèis** event (Rock’ndaal) each year. The post details her background, career path from applied chemist to soap maker and distillery events manager, and highlights her role in coordinating logistics for the festival, including ferry transport, staging, sound engineering, and community engagement.

- 3000 attendees, 20 vehicles, 5 trailers, 1 Arctic lorry, hundreds of bottles, and beds for ~60 crew members.
- Ailsa grew up in Lochgilphead, studied chemistry at Strathclyde University, moved to Islay in 2004, started **Spirited Soaps** using Bruichladdich whisky, and joined the distillery team full‑time in 2012.
- She has led Fèis since 2015, managing near‑misses, Covid disruptions, and continuous improvement of event logistics.
- The article ends with her personal note about dreaming of an elephant sanctuary and gratitude from the distillery for her contributions.

# Octomore Single Malt Scotch Whisky - Bruichladdich Distillery

## Overview
- **Name**: Octomore Single Malt Scotch Whisky
- **Series**: Sixteen (Octomore 16)
- **Maturity**: Always super-heavily peated, matured for five years in American whiskey or bourbon barrels.
- **Key Facts**:
  - Super-heavy peating pushes whisky boundaries.
  - Each expression has its own distinct character.
  - Uses exclusively Octomore Farm barley.

## Production Signals
- **Peat Levels**: Super-heavily peated, creating intense smoky flavors.
- **Cask Management**: Maturation in American whiskey/bourbon barrels for some expressions; European wine/cognac casks for others (e.g., .2).
- **Barley Source**: Exclusively grown on Octomore Farm.

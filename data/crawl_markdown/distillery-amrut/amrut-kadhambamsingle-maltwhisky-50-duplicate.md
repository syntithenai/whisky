# Amrut Kadhambam 50% – Single Malt Whisky

- **Blend**: Combines normal and peated Amrut whiskies, each first matured in ex‑Oloroso sherry casks.
- **Secondary aging**: Further aged in brandy and rum casks sourced from Amrut distilleries.
- **Flavor profile**: Rich floral, honeyed, nutty, new oak, vanilla, citrus & tropical fruit aromas; candied fruits, subtle oak, light peat, integrated spice on taste; warming, complex finish with a dry, long note.
- **Positioning**: Marketed as a “mixture” that showcases Amrut’s mastery of blending and unconventional maturation techniques.

> *“Kadhambam showcases Amrut's mastery in blending diverse whisky flavors seamlessly.”*

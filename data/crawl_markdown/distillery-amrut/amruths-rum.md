# Amrut Rum

## Key Facts
- Produced by Indian distillery **Amrut**
- Blends rum distilled in Karnataka (India) and aged American oak barrels with a blend of aged rums from Jamaica, Barbados, and Guyana
- Available as **Two Indies & Gold Variants**

## Production Signals
- Distilled using iron stills from jaggery (unrefined cane sugar)
- Aged in American oak barrels for maturation
- Blended to create a unique flavor profile

## Commercial Signals
- Recognized as India's first, most awarded, and most loved Single Malt Whisky
- Available in **Amrut Two Indies Rum**, **Old Port Rum Deluxe Crafted**, **Amrut Two Indies Dark Rum**, and **RHUM-RON** variants
- Includes limited editions like **Amrut Single Malt Limited Edition Rum Gin**

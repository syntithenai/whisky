# Amrut Rum
Amrut Rum is a premium Indian rum produced by Amrut Distilleries. The cuvée blends two distinct rums: one distilled in Karnataka from jaggery using an iron still and aged in American oak barrels, and a blend of aged rums sourced from Jamaica, Barbados, and Guyana. It comes in several variants – **Two Indies Rum (42.8 %)**, **Two Indies White Rum (42.8 %)**, **Old Port Rum Deluxe Crafted (42.8 %)**, **Two Indies Dark Rum RHUM‑RON (42.8 %)**, and **Indian Single Jaggery Bella Rum (46 %)** – all marketed as premium spirits.

The brand emphasizes precision distillation, craftsmanship, and a harmonious balance of flavors that reflect India’s rich tradition. The rum is suitable for neat sipping or cocktails and has gained international recognition.

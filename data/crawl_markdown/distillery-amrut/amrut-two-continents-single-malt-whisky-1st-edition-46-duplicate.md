# Amrut Two Continents Single Malt Whisky – 1st Edition (46%)

- **Origin**: Golden barley from India’s North West Provinces, distilled in Bangalore.
- **Maturation journey**: After Indian distillation the spirit travels ~9 000 km to a secret European location for further ageing.
- **Tasting notes**: Suet pudding, banana milkshake, juicy barley, soft fruits, spices, chocolate‑milkshake‑like middle, long oak finish with vanilla.
- **Awards**: 2012 Whisky Advocate New World Whisky of the Year.

## Key facts
- 46 % ABV
- Single malt
- Dual‑continent maturation (India → Europe)
- Award‑winning product

## Long Description
# Amrut Distilleries

**Location & Heritage**
- Bengaluru, India (7th Floor, JNR City Center, Raja Rammohan Roy Road). Founded by Chairman Neelakanta Jagdale under the N.R. Jagdale Group.
- First Indian single‑malt whisky; globally awarded for innovation and quality.

## Production & Maturation
- Distillation in Bangalore’s high‑temperature climate amplifies evaporation (angel’s share) and concentrates flavours.
- Hand‑selected casks; many releases feature a 15 % annual angel’s share loss.
- No chill filtration or artificial colouring on most expressions.

## Awards & Recognition
- IWSC Silver (2016), Jim Murray’s Whisky Bible Gold, Malt Maniacs Bronze, Spirits Selection Mexico Gold.
- Recognised as India’s first single‑malt and a benchmark for Indian whisky worldwide.

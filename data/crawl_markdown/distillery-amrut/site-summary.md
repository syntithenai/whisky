## Long Description
# Amrut Distilleries

**Who we are**
- Founded in 1948 by J.N. Radhakrishna, the company grew from a local malt house to India’s first internationally recognised single‑malt whisky producer.
- The brand is now part of the N.R. Jagdale Group and operates out of Bengaluru, Karnataka.

## Product Range
Amrut offers a broad spectrum of spirits:
- **Single‑Malt Whiskies** – flagship *Fusion* (3rd best globally 2009), *Neidhal*, *Marudham*, *Kurinji*, *Portonova*, *Intermediate Sherry*, and numerous limited‑edition expressions.
- **Rums** – *Old Port Rum Deluxe*, *Two Indies Rum*, and other blends that fuse Indian molasses with Caribbean rums.
- **Special Editions & Cask Series** – Double‑cask, peated finishes, sherry‑finished, port‑pipe finished, and single‑cask releases.

### Notable Awards
- *Amrut Fusion* – 3rd best whisky worldwide (2009).
- *Neidhal* – Director’s Choice, Double Gold, Best World Whisky (John Barleycorn Awards 2022).
- *Two Indies Rum* – IWSC Gold 2016, Silver 2014.

## Production & Distillation
- **Still Types**: Column stills for rums; pot and column stills for whisky.
- **Maturation**: Bourbon casks, Spanish sherry casks, Portuguese port pipes, and American oak barrels.
- **Spice Infusions**: Zatara blends incorporate cinnamon, nutmeg, orange zest, and molasses.

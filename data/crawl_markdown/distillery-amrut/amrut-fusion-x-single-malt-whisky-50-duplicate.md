# Amrut Fusion X Single Malt Whisky 50%

Amrut Fusion X is a premium single‑malt whisky that blends barley from the Himalayas and peated Scottish barley. The two whiskies are distilled separately, matured in old and new American oak barrels at Amrut’s Bangalore distillery (3000 ft altitude), then blended to create a “fusion of East and West.”  It is marketed as a high‑quality, award‑winning spirit with a complex nose of oaked barley sugar and smoke, a rich palate featuring peat, sherry‑like vanilla custard, chocolate fudge, and a dry, peaty finish.  The product has earned multiple international awards, including Jim Murray’s Whisky Bible (97/100) and the Spirits Business Awards.

---

**Key facts**
- Himalayan barley + Scottish peated barley
- Distilled & matured separately in American oak barrels
- Blended at Amrut distillery, Bangalore (3000 ft)
- 50% ABV
- Award‑winning: Jim Murray’s Whisky Bible, Spirits Business Awards, Malt Maniacs, Malt Advocates
- Family‑owned independent distillery, 3rd generation

**Tasting notes**

- Palate: peat, sherry‑like vanilla custard, chocolate fudge
- Finish: dry peat, oak, molasses sweetness, spices

# Amrut Fusion X Single Malt Whisky 50%

Amrut Fusion X is a blended single‑malt whisky that marries barley from the Himalayas and peated Scottish barley. The two whiskies are distilled separately, matured in old and new American oak barrels at Amrut’s Bangalore distillery (3000 ft altitude), then blended to create a “fusion of East and West.”  The spirit is described as heavy‑oaked with smoky, sherry‑like, chocolate‑fudge notes and a dry peat finish.  It has earned multiple awards including Jim Murray’s Whisky Bible 2010 (97/100) and the Spirits Business Awards.

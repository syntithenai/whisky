# Neidhal Indian Single Malt Whisky
Neidhal is Amrut Distilleries’ inaugural independent bottling under the *Single Malts of India* line. The 46 % ABV, peated single malt is limited to 12 000 bottles worldwide and draws its name from a coastal region referenced in ancient Tamil texts.

**Tasting profile** – tropical fruit, vanilla, soft phenols, sea‑salt, iodine, chewy texture, sweet vanilla finish. 

**Awards** – Director’s Choice (John Barleycorn Awards 2022), Double Gold (John Barleycorn Awards 2022), Best World Whisky (John Barleycorn Awards Spring 2022), Gold Winner (Whiskies of the World 2022), Consumer Choice (Whiskey & Barrel 2022), 92‑point score (Ultimate Spirits Challenge 2022).

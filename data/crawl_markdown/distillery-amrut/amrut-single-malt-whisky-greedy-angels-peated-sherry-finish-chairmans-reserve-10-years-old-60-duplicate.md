# Amrut Greedy Angels – 10 Year‑Old Premium Indian Whisky

- **Product**: Amrut Single Malt Whisky Greedy Angels Peated Sherry Finish Chairman’s Reserve, 10 years old, 60% ABV.
- **Origin**: India (Punjab barley, aged in ex‑bourbon barrels).
- **Tasting profile**: Licorice, bourbon, toasted honey, toffee, caramel, cream; woody finish.
- **Awards**: Liquid Gold Award (Jim Murray’s Whiskey Bible 2010), Malt Maniacs Award (2008‑09), Ultimate Spirits Challenge USA 2012 (88 points).
- **Distillery**: Amrut Distilleries Private Ltd., Bengaluru, India – pioneer of Indian single‑malt whisky.

> *“Sip smooth whisky, celebrate moments, elevate every occasion’s spirit.”*

## Key facts
- 10 years aged in ex‑bourbon barrels.
- 60% ABV (duplicate edition).
- Peated sherry finish.
- Made from malted barley sourced from Punjab.
- Recognised internationally for quality and innovation.

### Awards & Recognition
- Liquid Gold Award – Jim Murray’s Whiskey Bible 2010.
- Malt Maniacs Award – 2008, 2009.

## Tasting notes
- **Nose**: Licorice, bourbon, toasted honey, toffee.
- **Palate**: Barley and oak dominate; bourbon, licorice, toffee re‑emerge with barley nuance.
- **Finish**: Prolonged woody notes, sweet & dry touch, hint of caramel and cream.

---

## Distillery background
Amrut Distilleries Private Ltd. (N.R. Jagdale Group) was founded in 2004. It is India's first 100% local single‑malt whisky and has earned numerous international awards for its craftsmanship.

# Amrut Single Malts – Variants
Amrut Distilleries showcases a broad portfolio of Indian single‑malt whiskies, each labelled with its alcohol by volume (ABV) and unique finishing or cask type.

- **Core range**: Indian Single Malt Whisky 46 %, Fusion 50 %, Cask Strength 61.8 %, Peated 46 % and Peated Cask Strength 62.8 %. 
- **Special editions**: Two Continents (1st, 2nd, 3rd editions), Spectrum 004, Greedy Angels Chairman’s Reserve (10‑yr, 8‑yr, 12‑yr), Aatma Single Cask, and various single‑cask releases (Bourbon, Oloroso Sherry, PX Sherry, Peated Port Pipe). 
- **Other variants**: Marudham, Kurinji, Kadhambam, Rajigala, Naarangi, Intermediate Sherry, Portonova, Amaze, and Rye. 

All products are produced in Bengaluru, India, by Amrut Distilleries Private Ltd (N.R. Jagdale Group).

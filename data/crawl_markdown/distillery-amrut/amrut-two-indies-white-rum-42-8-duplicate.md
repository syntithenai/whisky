# Amrut Two Indies White Rum

Amrut’s *Two Indies White Rum* is a 42.8 % ABV blend that marries Indian and Jamaican rum traditions. The product is made from three distinct rums: a sugarcane‑juice rum aged in American oak, a jaggery‑based rum from Karnataka also aged in American oak, and a Jamaican rum contributing fruity esters. Tasting notes highlight fresh banana, brown sugar, lime, herbaceous and vegetal nuances, with palate flavours of white pepper, liquorice and cooked vegetables.

The distillery emphasises meticulous craftsmanship, premium ingredients, and a careful blending process to deliver a complex, high‑quality spirit.

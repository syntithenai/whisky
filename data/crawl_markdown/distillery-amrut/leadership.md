# Leadership at Amrut Distilleries

## Shri J.N. Radhakrishna ("J.N.R")
- Pioneered Indian Made Foreign Liquor (IMFL) sector in Karnataka
- Established Amrut as a major supplier of spirits in the state
- Laid foundation for future growth and global recognition

## Shri Neelakanta Rao Jagdale (Chairman & Managing Director)
- Expanded Amrut into a multi-national brand
- Emphasized transparent practices and quality products
- Balanced tradition with innovation and technology
- Championed international expansion of Amrut Single Malt Whisky

## Rakshit N. Jagdale (Current Chairman & Managing Director)
- Third-generation leader in the family business
- MBA from University of Newcastle Upon Tyne
- Introduced Amrut Fusion as 3rd best whisky globally in 2009
- Pioneered Indian Single Malt category on international stage
- Committed to community engagement and philanthropy

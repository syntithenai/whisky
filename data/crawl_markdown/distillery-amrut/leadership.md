# Amrut Distilleries Leadership

Amrut Distilleries Pvt. Ltd., a leading Indian spirit producer, is steered by three generations of the Jagdale family:
- **Shri J.N. Radhakrishna (J.N.R.)** – Founder who built the company into a major IMFL supplier in Karnataka.
- **Shri Neelakanta Rao Jagdale** – Son of J.N.R., served as Chairman & Managing Director, expanded the brand globally and championed transparency in the industry.
- **Rakshit N. Jagdale** – Current third‑generation leader with an MBA from Newcastle upon Tyne; has driven international marketing (e.g., Amrut Fusion) and community outreach.

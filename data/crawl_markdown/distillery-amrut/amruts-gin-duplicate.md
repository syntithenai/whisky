# Amrut Gin
Amrut Gin is a premium Indian spirit crafted by Amrut Distilleries. It blends traditional gin production techniques with local botanicals and spices to create a balanced, refined flavor profile. The product line includes the **Amrut Nilgiris Indian Dry Gin (42.8%)** and its **Cask‑Aged variant (42.8%)**, both highlighted on the site.

## Key Points
- Distillery: Amrut Distilleries Private Ltd., Bengaluru, India.
- Alcohol content: 42.8% ABV for both variants.
- Focus on Indian botanicals and spices.
- Emphasis on tradition, innovation, and quality.

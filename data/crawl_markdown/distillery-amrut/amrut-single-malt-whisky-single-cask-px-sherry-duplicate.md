# Amrut Single Malt Whisky – PX Sherry Single Cask

- **Limited edition single‑cask bottling** from Amrut Distilleries.
- Aged first in charred American virgin oak casks, then finished in Pedro Ximénez sherry butts.
- Bottled at cask strength, no chill filtration – retains natural colour and full flavour.
- Tasting notes: woody sap, green leaves, spicy oak, cinnamon toast, ground chilli, green peppers, brown sugar; on the palate: ferns, chocolate, raisins, cinnamon, cloves, lime skin; finish: winey, salted caramel, menthol, raisins, tingling spice.
- Awarded a Bronze at Malt Maniacs 2014.

## Key facts
- **Distillery**: Amrut Distilleries Private Ltd., Bengaluru, India.
- **Cask type**: American virgin oak + Pedro Ximénez sherry butts.
- **Strength**: Cask‑strength (no chill filtration).
- **Release**: Limited edition single‑cask.

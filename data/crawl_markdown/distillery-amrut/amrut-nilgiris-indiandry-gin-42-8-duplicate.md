# Amrut Nilgiris Indian Dry Gin

- **ABV**: 42.8%
- Inspired by the Nilgiri mountains (7 000 ft elevation) in India.
- Key botanicals: hand‑picked tea, paan (betel leaf), juniper, citrus, raw mango, pepper.
- Tasting notes: smooth, buttery juniper; spicy background; brewed tea and floral undertones; peppery bite; long dry finish.
- Award‑winning brand from Amrut Distilleries, known for innovation and Indian character in gin.

# Amrut Two Indies Rum
Amrut Two Indies is a premium rum blending Indian‑made rums from the Amrut distillery in Karnataka with Caribbean rums sourced from Jamaica, Guyana and Barbados. The 42.8 % ABV spirit showcases tropical fruit notes that evolve into clove, cinnamon, licorice and subtle smoke, finished by sweetish oakiness and caramelised molasses. It has earned a Gold Award at the IWSC 2016 and a Silver Award in 2014.

## Key facts
- Blend of Indian and Caribbean rums
- 42.8 % ABV (duplicate edition)
- Awards: Gold IWSC 2016, Silver IWSC 2014, 92‑point rating at Ultimate Spirits Challenge 2014
- Distilled in pot and column stills; matured in selected oak barrels
- Tasting notes: tropical fruit → clove & cinnamon → licorice & smoke

## Production highlights
- Uses premium molasses‑based spirits
- Blended after maturation to retain natural congeners
- Finished with sweetish oakiness, caramelised sugar and floral overtones

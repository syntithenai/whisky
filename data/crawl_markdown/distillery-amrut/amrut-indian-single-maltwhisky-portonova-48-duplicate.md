# Amrut Portonova – Indian Single Malt (48%)

- **Unpeated** whisky aged in American oak bourbon barrels.
- Finished in Portuguese port pipes for a unique flavor profile.
- Tasting notes: smooth, fruit‑caramel blend with oaky saltiness, cocoa and muscovado finish.
- Available only through duty‑free and travel retail channels.

## Key Points
- 48% ABV, unpeated expression.
- First aged in bourbon barrels, then finished in port pipes.
- Rich, complex palate: fruit, caramel, oak, cocoa, muscovado.
- Limited availability (duty‑free/travel retail).
- Amrut is India’s first and most awarded single malt whisky.

# Amrut Distilleries – World’s Best Single Malt Whisky
Amrut Distilleries Pvt. Ltd., based in Bangalore, has been crowned *World’s Best Whisky* after winning five gold medals in the “World Malt Category” at the International Spirits Challenge 2024 held in London. The brand’s flagship product, Amrut Fusion, was also named *Best Whiskey* and received gold medals across all five variants.

## Key Highlights
- **Award**: Five gold medals (World Malt Category) + Best Whiskey title.
- **Event**: International Spirits Challenge 2024, London.
- **Product**: Amrut Fusion – a single‑malt whisky with multiple variants.
- **Brand Positioning**: India’s first, most awarded and most loved single‑malt whisky.

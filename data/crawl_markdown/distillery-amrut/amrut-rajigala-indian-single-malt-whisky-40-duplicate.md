# Amrut Rajigala Indian Single Malt Whisky (40%)

- **Product**: Amrut Rajigala – a premium Indian single‑malt whisky at 40% ABV.
- **Theme**: Named after the “King of the Eagles” (Rajigala) to symbolize tradition and aspiration.
- **Tasting profile**:

  - Nose: fruity with citrus, melon, creamy peach, caramel

# Amrut Single Malt Whisky – Single Cask – Oloroso Sherry (Duplicate)

Amrut Distilleries presents a limited‑edition single malt whisky that celebrates the 19th bottling of SWF, Sweden. The expression blends unpeated Indian barley with peated Scottish barley and is aged in virgin American oak, first‑fill bourbon, and Oloroso sherry casks. Tasting notes highlight dried fruits, figs, raisins, dark chocolate, nutmeg, and a subtle sweetness, with a long, warm finish.

> *“Each sip reveals a harmonious balance of flavors, from the subtle sweetness of bourbon to the nuanced richness of sherry, enhanced by the distinctive character of peat.”*

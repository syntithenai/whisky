## Source Excerpts
- The final touch comes from further maturation in ex-bourbon casks, resulting in an elegantly nuanced whisky with subtle sherry undertones, characteristic of the renowned Amrut Signature.
- Starting with newly made spirit, it matures initially in virgin or ex-bourbon casks before being transferred into Spanish ex-oloroso sherry butts for an additional year, acquiring a distinctive sherry finish.
- As a worthy successor to the acclaimed Amrut Fusion and Amrut Double Cask, this expression stands as a testament to Amrut's commitment to quality and innovation, offering enthusiasts a truly exceptional tasting experience.
- Brain-explodingly complex and multi-layered with one of the most intriguing sherry-style-bourbon-style marriages on the market; Taste Cracking delivery and entirely unique in form.
- The structure is decidedly oak-based, but acts as no more than a skeleton from which the juicy sultana and spices drape.
- But the liquorice-orangey-honeycomb bourbon theme quietly shapes the flavour profile; the spices pulse and glow; Finish Quite a chunk of natural caramel quietens the more exuberant characteristics; long and elegant.

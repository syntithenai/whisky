# Amrut Whisky Awards & Achievements

Amrut Distilleries has earned numerous prestigious awards and recognitions, highlighting their commitment to crafting exceptional spirits.

- **PR%F Awards**: Amrut Single Malt Whisky secured the top prize at the PR%F Awards, reaffirming its position as a leader in fine spirits.

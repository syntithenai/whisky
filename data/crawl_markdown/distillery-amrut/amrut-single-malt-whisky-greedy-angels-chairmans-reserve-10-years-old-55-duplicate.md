# Amrut Greedy Angels Chairman’s Reserve – 10 Yr Old Single Malt Whisky

- **Limited edition**: 900 bottles worldwide (300 for the USA). 
- **Barrel & grain**: 100 % un‑peated Indian 6‑row barley, aged 10 years in ex‑bourbon barrels. 
- **Proof**: 55 %. 
- **Unfiltered, no added colour** – authentic expression of complex flavours.
- **Tasting notes**: coconut & vanilla, tropical fruit, honeyed marmalade, chocolate‑coconut, mild citrus, spice and woody finish with roasted almonds.

## Tasting notes
- **Nose**: coconut in vanilla sauce, tropical fruits, mild oak tannins, honeyed marmalade.
- **Palate**: honey & tropical fruit, chocolate‑coconut, vanilla pods, mild citrus.
- **Finish**: spicy, woody, chocolate‑covered roasted almonds, lingering.

## Distillery background
Amrut Distilleries Pvt. Ltd., part of the N.R. Jagdale Group, is headquartered in Bengaluru, India. It has earned international acclaim for its single‑malt whiskies and was named *Asian Whisky of the Year* by Whisky Bible 2019.

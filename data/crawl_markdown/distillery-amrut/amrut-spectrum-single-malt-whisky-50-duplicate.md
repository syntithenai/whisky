# Amrut Spectrum Single Malt Whisky – 50% ABV

- **Limited edition** single‑malt from Amrut Distilleries.
- Bottled at **50 % ABV** (high‑strength).
- Crafted from a blend of **five cask types**: American oak, Oloroso sherry, port, and two others not named.
- Flavour profile: dried fruits, vanilla, spices, oak, dark chocolate, honey, tropical fruit, warming spices.
- Long, smooth finish with lingering sweetness and oak.
- Recognised internationally; part of Amrut’s award‑winning portfolio.

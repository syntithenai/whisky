# Amrut Greedy Angels Chairman’s Reserve 12‑Year‑Old (60% ABV)

- **Age & Cask**: 12 years, aged in Bourbon casks.
- **ABV**: 60 %
- **Packaging**: Comes with two Glencairn crystal tasting glasses.
- **Tasting profile**: Rich, oily mouthfeel; notes of candied tropical fruit (especially orange), dried fruit, almonds, soft caramel and vanilla. Long finish dominated by bitter oranges, caramel and nuts.
- **Distillery context**: Oldest whisky bottled by Amrut, first to age 12 years in India’s hot climate.
- **Awards**: Recognised internationally; part of Amrut’s award‑winning single‑malt line.

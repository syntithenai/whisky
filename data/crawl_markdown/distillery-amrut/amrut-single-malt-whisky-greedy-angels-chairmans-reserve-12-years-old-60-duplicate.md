# Amrut Greedy Angels Chairman’s Reserve 12‑Year‑Old (60% ABV)

- **Age & Cask**: 12 years, aged in Bourbon casks.
- **ABV**: Bottled at 60 %. 
- **Packaging**: Comes with two Glencairn crystal tasting glasses.
- **Tasting Profile**:
  - *Nose*: Rich, oily, sugary candied tropical fruits; dominant orange notes.
  - *Palate*: Oily, buttery mouthfeel; oranges, tropical & dried fruits, almonds, soft caramel and vanilla.
  - *Finish*: Very long, steady with bitter‑orange, caramel and almond nuances.
- **Significance**: Oldest whisky bottled by Amrut, possibly the first to age a full twelve years in India.
- **Awards**: Recognised internationally for quality and innovation.

# Amrut Single Cask Bourbon (Duplicate)

Amrut Distilleries presents a limited‑release Indian single malt whisky bottled at cask strength and unfiltered. The spirit is first aged in charred American virgin oak, then finished in first‑fill ex‑bourbon barrels, delivering a complex profile of chocolate, nutmeg, orange zest, cinnamon, vanilla and toasted marshmallow with a creamy, doughy mouthfeel.

**Key tasting notes:**
- **Nose:** Creamy chocolate, almond, nutmeg, orange, cinnamon, hardwood, toasted marshmallow, vanilla.
- **Palate:** Warm Swiss chocolate, cinnamon‑spiced cocoa, orange zest, mint, ginger, dried spices; vibrant and tangy.

The expression showcases Amrut’s commitment to quality and its reputation as India’s first, most awarded single malt whisky.

# Amrut Distilleries
Amrut Distilleries (established 1948 in Bangalore) is India's first and most awarded single‑malt whisky producer. The flagship product, **Amrut Indian Single Malt**, is celebrated for its innovative aging techniques that have earned international acclaim.

## Key Points
- Founded 1948, Bangalore, India.
- Known for *Amrut Indian Single Malt*.
- Offers limited editions, rum, gin and duty‑free products.
- Headquarters: JNR City Center, Raja Rammohan Roy Road, Bengaluru.

# Amrut Distilleries - World's Best Single Malt Whisky

Founded in 1948, **Amrut** is renowned for its innovative single malt whisky production. The distillery's pioneering approach to aging has earned international acclaim and numerous awards.

## Key Facts
- **Location**: Bangalore, India
- **Established**: 1948
- **Signature Product**: "Amrut Indian Single Malt"
- **Awards**: International accolades for its single malt whisky

## Production Signals
- **Innovative Aging Techniques**: Amrut has developed unique aging processes that set it apart in the single malt category.
- **International Recognition**: Consistently ranked among the top single malts globally, winning prestigious awards.

## Commercial Signals
- **Brand Positioning**: Marketed as "World's Best Single Malt Whisky" and a pioneer of Indian single malt whisky.
- **Limited Editions**: Offers limited release expressions that are highly sought after by collectors.

## Risks/Unknowns

- **Regulatory Environment**: Navigating India's alcohol regulations and export controls can pose challenges.

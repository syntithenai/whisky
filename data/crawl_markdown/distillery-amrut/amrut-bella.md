# Amrut Bella – First 100% Jaggery‑Distilled Rum

Amrut Distilleries has launched **Bella**, the world’s first rum distilled entirely from jaggery and aged for seven years in ex‑bourbon barrels. The spirit is produced in Bengaluru, India, using jaggery sourced directly from farmers in the Sahyadri range. Bella is 46 % ABV and carries a tasting profile of ripe banana, brown sugar, lime, white pepper, liquorice and cooked vegetables.

Key facts:
- First 100 % jaggery‑distilled rum worldwide.
- Matured for seven years in ex‑bourbon barrels under tropical conditions.
- Produced by Amrut Distilleries Private Ltd., Bengaluru.
- Named after the Kannada word for jaggery.
- Launched to position itself as a premium Indian rum.

The page also highlights the company’s heritage, its pioneering spirit and the story of Chairman Late Mr. Neelkanta Rao Jagdale (NRJ) who secured the license to distill jaggery.

## Tasting Notes
- **Nose**: Fresh, lively; ripe banana, brown sugar, lime, herbaceous & vegetal notes after aeration.
- **Palate**: Gourmet with white pepper, liquorice, cooked vegetables (sorrel). Overall fresh and vegetal.

---

## Production & Heritage
Amrut’s commitment to indigenous ingredients is evident in Bella. The distillery sourced jaggery directly from farmers, avoiding additives or preservatives, and aged the spirit for seven years in ex‑bourbon barrels.

# Amrut Intermediate Sherry (Old Label)

Amrut’s *Intermediate Sherry* is a single‑malt whisky that begins aging in ex‑bourbon barrels, moves to an intermediate sherry cask for a short period, and then returns to bourbon casks. The process blends sherry complexity with the spirit’s inherent character, yielding aromas of port, candied cherries, vanilla and strawberry jam, and flavours of cinnamon, nutmeg, orange peel and anise that finish long, dry and oaky.

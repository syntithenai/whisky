## Long Description
# Kinglake Distillery
Kinglake Distillery is a family‑owned, off‑grid whisky producer located in Victoria, Australia. The distillery prides itself on being **carbon‑neutral** and has rewilded one third of its property to support local biodiversity. All whiskies are handmade single malts using locally sourced ingredients and natural spring water.

## Product Portfolio
The distillery offers three 700 ml bottles, each priced at **$135**:
- **O'Grady's Bourbon Cask Single Malt Whisky** – matured in bourbon casks for a rich, vanilla‑laden profile.
- **Double Wood Single Malt Whisky** – finished in two types of wood to create layered complexity.
- **Breakfast Rye Cask Single Malt Whisky** – aged in rye casks for a spicy, robust character.

All products are available through the online store and can be purchased via major payment methods (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Mastercard, Visa, etc.).

## Visitor Experience
The tasting room is open on alternate Sundays or by appointment. Visitors can tour the off‑grid facilities and learn about sustainable whisky production.

# Kinglake Distillery

Kinglake Distillery is an off‑grid Australian distillery that produces handmade single‑malt whisky using the local environment. It claims to be Australia’s only certified carbon‑neutral whisky, with a third of its property rewilded for native flora and fauna. The distillery offers three 700 ml bottles – **O’Grady’s Bourbon Cask**, **Double Wood**, and **Breakfast Rye Cask** – all priced at \$135.

The site features a tasting room open every other Sunday, monthly tours led by the distiller Sam, and a strong emphasis on environmental stewardship. Contact details include an email (Info@kinglakedistillery.com.au) and phone number 0413 188 248.

# Kinglake Distillery

Kinglake Distillery is an off‑grid, carbon‑neutral single malt whisky producer located in Victoria, Australia. Their portfolio includes three 700 ml bottles – **O'Grady's Bourbon Cask Single Malt Whisky**, **Double Wood Single Malt Whisky**, and **Breakfast Rye Cask Single Malt Whisky** – each priced at \$135. The distillery emphasizes environmental stewardship: a third of the property is rewilded, and they offer tasting room visits on alternate Sundays or by appointment.

- **Location:** 410 O'Grady's Road, Kinglake Central, Victoria 3757, Australia

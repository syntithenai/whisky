# Sustainability Marketing

The James B. Beam Institute’s *Sustainability Marketing* page offers practical guidance for distilleries on how to weave sustainability into their marketing strategy. It covers:
- **Messaging**: Use storytelling first, then support with data.
- **Channels**: Social media, website landing pages, tours, and email.
- **Key themes**: Reuse of spent grains, glass recycling, local sourcing, renewable energy, water stewardship, barrel life‑cycle, and the White Oak Initiative.
- **Avoid greenwashing** by setting realistic goals, staying transparent, and educating stakeholders.

The page is a resource hub rather than a product listing or review site.

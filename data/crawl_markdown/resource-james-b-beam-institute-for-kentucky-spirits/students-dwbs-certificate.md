# DWBS Certificate
The James B. Beam Institute for Kentucky Spirits offers a **12‑credit Distillation, Wine and Brewing Studies (DWBS) Certificate** open to all students. The program includes both online and in‑person courses, with the core introductory class HRT 335 required but not strictly first. Students can complete the certificate in as little as one semester if course availability aligns, though most take one class per semester over four semesters.

Key points:
- **Advisor**: Dr. Czarena Crofcheck (crofcheck@uky.edu)
- **Course mix**: Online and on‑campus options; only HRT 335 and AEN 341 are offered year‑round.
- **Residency**: Non‑Kentucky residents may still incur non‑resident tuition for in‑person courses.
- **Contact**: Dr. Crofcheck for course details, scheduling, and sample plans.

For more information, see the institute’s website or download the PDF of course details.

# Distillery Efficiency Best Practices

The James B. Beam Institute page provides a practical guide for craft distillers to improve energy and water efficiency. It covers:
- **Benchmarking**: collect production volume, utility bills, and use the KPPC SSBI worksheet.
- **Team engagement**: build a sustainability culture through data sharing and idea solicitation.
- **Continuous improvement**: keep projects moving forward after each success or failure.
- **Operations changes**: reduce steaming times, lower cook temperatures, use thermotolerant yeasts, perform routine maintenance, upgrade equipment (insulation, waste‑heat recovery), and minimize compressed‑air usage.
- **Specific technical tips**: boiler blowdown, burner inspection, steam disinfection best practices, compressed‑air leak detection, heat‑exchanger sizing, gelatinisation monitoring, and malt CoA metrics.

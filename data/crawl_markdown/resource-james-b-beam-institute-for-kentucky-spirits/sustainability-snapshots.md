# Sustainability Snapshots
The James B. Beam Institute for Kentucky Spirits hosts a **Sustainability Snapshots** page that compiles practical tools and resources for distillery energy efficiency. It lists:
- Incentive‑implementation guides for craft distillers.
- EnergyStar’s *Building Energy Efficiency Calculator*.
- DOE’s *Manufacturing Energy Assessment Software for Utility Reduction (MEASUR)*.
- Boiler, steam, and compressed‑air best‑practice guides from Spirax Sarco, Inveno Engineering, the Carbon Trust, and Fluid‑Aire Dynamics.
- Links to videos on heat‑recovery impacts and compressed‑air setpoint savings.

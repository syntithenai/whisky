# Scholarships at the James B. Beam Institute

The University of Kentucky offers a range of scholarships for students in its Distillation, Wine and Brewing Studies program. Applications are submitted through a single portal and considered for all listed awards.

## Current Active Scholarships
- **Harry Thompson Scholarship** – Supports UK students from Jefferson County or those pursuing distillation studies with financial need.
- **James B. Beam Scholars** – Endowed by Suntory Global Spirits; requires enrollment, age 21+, unmet financial need, preferably Kentucky residents.
- **John and Elizabeth Koko Scholarship** – Endowed by Horse Soldier Bourbon founders; for students with U.S. military ties.
- **Bourbon Women Scholarship** – For non‑traditional students with unmet need who have completed at least two program courses.
- **Bardstown Collection Scholarship** – Preferentially supports undergraduates from Nelson County.
- **Brewers of Central KY Scholarship** – For students originally from Central Kentucky interested in brewing.

Applications for the 2026‑27 academic year close on March 1, 2026.

# Australian Distillers Association - WSET Partnership

## Key Facts
- **Partnership**: ADA partners with Wine & Spirits Education Trust (WSET) to offer members access to their qualifications.
- **Focus**: WSET provides qualifications in wines, spirits, sake, and beer for professionals and enthusiasts.
- **Levels**: Three levels of WSET spirit courses - Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3.

## Production Signals
- **Course Content**: Courses cover fundamental production methods, raw materials, and key practices for creating various spirit styles.
- **Tasting Focus**: Emphasis on understanding flavors, aromas, and the impact of production choices on final product characteristics.

## Commercial Signals
- **Industry Relevance**: WSET qualifications are designed to help professionals understand distilled beverages beyond brand recognition.
- **Career Development**: Courses equip students with knowledge applicable to various roles in the spirits industry.

## Risks/Unknowns
- **Market Trends**: Rapid growth in spirits demand may require continuous adaptation and learning.
- **Regulatory Changes**: Evolving excise laws across jurisdictions could impact production and marketing strategies.

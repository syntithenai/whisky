# Australian Distillers Association – Industry Development Officer

The page advertises a full‑time **Industry Development Officer** role for Victoria’s distilling sector, managed by the Victorian Government and supported by Spirits Victoria Association and the Australian Distillers Association. The position involves:
- Delivering the Vic Distilled Program (grants, training, export initiatives)
- Building relationships with distillers, government agencies, and regional bodies
- Facilitating workshops, industry events, trade shows, and conferences
- Managing multiple priorities autonomously and travelling across Victoria.

Key details: 
- **Start date**: 12 Feb 2026 (post‑dated) 
- **Application deadline**: 23 Feb 2026 
- **Full‑time equivalent role** with flexible arrangements for the right candidate. 
- **Position description** available as a PDF linked on the page.

The post is posted by an anonymous administrator and includes standard site navigation links, contact details (admin@australiandistillers.org.au), and copyright information.

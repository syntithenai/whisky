# Queensland Distillers Association (QDA) News

The QDA website hosts a monthly news feed covering grants, legislative updates, industry events, and achievements of Queensland distillers. Highlights include:
- **Digital & business grants**: $2,500 Tourism Business Digital Adaption Program; $7,500 Business Basics Grants.
- **Legislative changes**: Electrical Safety Act amendments (ESOLA) and Work Health & Safety Act updates affecting distillery operations.
- **HR support grant**: Up to $5,000 for small businesses to improve HR practices.
- **Industry advocacy**: QDA’s parliamentary submission on food‑and‑beverage manufacturing; export plan template for members.
- **Awards and recognition**: 2024 Royal Queensland Awards – Distilled Spirits; 2023 Australian Rum Awards where Queensland distillers dominated.
- **Container Refund Scheme updates**: Extension to include wine & spirit containers, reporting rules, and transition dates.

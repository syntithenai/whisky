**Position Description – Victorian Distilled Program Industry Development Officer (February 2026)**
- **Role:** Support sustainable growth, promotion and development of Victoria’s distilling sector; dual‑role funded by the Victorian Government.
- **Key duties:** Lead industry development programs, create bespoke events, represent distillers to government, deliver initiatives that build capability, promote safety, foster export readiness, add value to visitor economy.
- **Operations & Governance:** Coordinate SVA committee meetings, support executive decisions, manage grant applications, monitor KPIs, report to funders.
- **Stakeholder Engagement:** Build relationships with distillers, agencies, regional bodies; facilitate collaboration and partnerships; communicate achievements.
- **Selection criteria:** Program delivery experience, project management, communication skills, industry knowledge (preferred), strategic thinking, autonomy, driver’s licence, travel ability.
- **Contract details:** Full‑time equivalent, one day per week for SVA, remainder for Vic Distilled Program. Fixed term until 30 June 2027; salary commensurate with experience; applications close 23 Feb 2026.

# Position Description – Victorian Distillers Program Industry Development Officer (February 2026)

The role is a full‑time, fixed‑term contract (30 June 2027) that combines delivery of the Vic Distilled Program and one day per week support to Spirits Victoria Association. Key responsibilities include:
- Overseeing grants, training, infrastructure and export initiatives under the Distillery Door Program.
- Implementing the Victorian Distilled Spirits Strategy 2025‑2030.
- Acting as liaison between industry and government, identifying opportunities and challenges.
- Coordinating SVA committee meetings, governance support and strategic planning.
- Building stakeholder relationships, facilitating workshops, events and trade show representation.

Selection criteria focus on program delivery experience, project management, communication skills, industry knowledge (preferred) and strategic thinking. The role requires a current driver’s licence, travel to regional Victoria and is subject to reference checks.

Applications close 23 Feb 2026; contact Kylie Lethbridge (kylie@australiandistillers.org.au).

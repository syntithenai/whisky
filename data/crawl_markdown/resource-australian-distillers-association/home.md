# Australian Distillers Association (ADA)

The ADA is the peak body representing commercial distillers in Australia. It promotes industry growth, safety, and responsible production through:
- Membership benefits such as a Safety Toolkit and exclusive news.
- Advocacy for tax reform and regulatory changes.
- Training & Education programs including the *Introduction to Distillery Safety* course.
- Events like the annual **2026 Conference – Festival of Distilling** in Adelaide.

Members gain access to resources, directories, and networking opportunities via a members‑only portal.

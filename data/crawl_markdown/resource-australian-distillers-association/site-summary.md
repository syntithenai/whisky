## Long Description
The Australian Distillers Association (ADA) is the national body representing craft, regional and commercial spirits producers across Australia. Its website hosts a range of resources including government submissions on excise reform, position descriptions for industry development roles, and compliance guides such as the Christmas Advertising Watch & Review that align with the ABAC code. The ADA works closely with Spirits Victoria, PwC, Deloitte Access Economics and other stakeholders to shape policy, secure funding, and provide training and networking opportunities for distillers of all sizes.

The site serves a broad audience: craft distillery owners, regional producers, industry professionals, government liaison officers, and students studying beverage science or business. It offers up‑to‑date policy briefs, downloadable PDFs, case studies, and practical toolkits that help members navigate taxation, export readiness, grant applications, and advertising compliance.

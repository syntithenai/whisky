# National Distilling Institute

## Key Facts
- Established in 2023 under the Victorian Government's Distillery Door program
- Based in Victoria, Australia with courses around the country
- RTO owned by National Food Institute (RTO 3821)
- ADA is the exclusive marketing arm for the RTO

## Production Signals
- Focus on vocational training for distillers across Australia
- Emphasis on safety practices and sensory analysis
- Practical, real-world approach to spirit production education

## Commercial Signals
- Partners with Spirits Victoria Association and Australian Distillers Association for course development
- Aims to be the safest, best educated, and most innovative distillers in the world
- Offers both accredited and non-accredited training programs

## Risks/Unknowns
- As a relatively new organization (established 2023), there may be evolving standards and best practices yet to be fully integrated into the curriculum
- The transition from traditional techniques to cutting-edge innovation may present challenges for some distillers adapting to new methods

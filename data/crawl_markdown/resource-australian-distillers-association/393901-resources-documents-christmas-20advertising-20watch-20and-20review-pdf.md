# Christmas Advertising Watch and Review
A brief guide for Australian distillers on ensuring compliance with the voluntary ABAC scheme before the holiday advertising rush. Highlights include:
- Verify age‑gate (18+) on websites, stores, social media, and YouTube.
- Avoid content linking alcohol to dangerous activities or minors.
- Refrain from encouraging irresponsible consumption.
- Ensure all people in ads appear older than 25.
For detailed guidance visit the ABAC website or consider their pre‑vetting service.

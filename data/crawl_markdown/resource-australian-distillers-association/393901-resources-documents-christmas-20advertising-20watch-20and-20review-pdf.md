# Christmas Advertising Watch and Review
A brief guide for Australian alcohol brands to ensure compliance with the voluntary ABAC (Alcohol Beverages Advertising Code) before the holiday season.

- Verify age‑gate (18+) on all digital touchpoints, including YouTube accounts.
- Avoid content linking alcohol to dangerous activities or minors.
- Refrain from depicting irresponsible consumption or using under‑25 imagery.
- Use ABAC’s guidance pages and consider their pre‑vetting service for major campaigns.

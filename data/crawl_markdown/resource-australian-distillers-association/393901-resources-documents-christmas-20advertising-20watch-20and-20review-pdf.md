# Christmas Advertising Watch and Review
A brief guide for Australian distillers on ensuring compliance with the voluntary ABAC (Alcohol Beverages Advertising Code) during the upcoming holiday season.

- Verify age‑gate (18+) on all digital platforms, including YouTube accounts.
- Avoid advertising alcohol alongside water sports, driving, machinery operation, or flying.
- Do not target minors; e.g., avoid chocolate‑flavoured whisky in a child‑friendly box.
- Refrain from depicting irresponsible consumption such as “Hour of Power” happy hours.
- All on‑screen personalities must appear older than 25.
- Use ABAC’s guidance pages and consider the Pre‑vetting Service for major campaigns.

# Australian Distillers Association - Member Benefits

## Why Join?
- **Community**: Connect with passionate distillers facing similar challenges and celebrating shared triumphs.
- **Support & Growth**: Access resources, insights, advice, events, workshops, and learning opportunities to excel in your role and elevate your career.
- **Connection**: Join a network of peers who understand the industry and champion your success.

## Membership Categories
Choose from various membership categories tailored to different stages and sizes of businesses:

## Key Benefits
- **Advocacy**: Strong advocacy for better conditions supporting sustainable growth of the Australian spirits industry.
- **Industry & Trade Directory**: Exclusive access to a broad range of suppliers and service providers across the supply chain.
- **Events & Programs**: Valuable networking and educational opportunities, including the major annual conference hosted in different states.
- **Communication**: Easy access to industry information via various channels like website, email newsletters, and state events.
- **Special Project Groups**: Inform and educate on best practices in safety, sustainability, technical standards, government liaison, and advocacy.
- **Industry Training**: Access to training options for you and your team, focusing on safety, smart practices, and sustainable operations.
- **Business Support Services**: Assistance with accounting/financial, legal, insurance, labeling, marketing, grants, tax incentives, engineering, logistics, marketing, design, branding, and more.
- **Reduced Costs**: Savings on business services like energy, accounting, legal, insurance, labeling, marketing, and event registrations (including the National conference).

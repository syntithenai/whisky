# ISC Annual Young Distiller Fellowship Award

- **Purpose**: Recognizes emerging talent in the Australian distilling industry through a unique learning opportunity.
- **Hosts**: Independent Stave Company (ISC) and the Australian Distillers Association.
- **Eligibility**: Open to distillers under 40 years old.
- **Benefits**:
  - All expenses paid trip to Kentucky, USA
  - Tours of key bourbon-related facilities
  - Networking with industry experts
  - Professional development opportunities
- **Application Process**:
  - Phase 1: Submit an application by January 24th, 2025, including work experience and responses to specific questions about the fellowship's purpose and personal goals.
  - Phase 2: Short-listed candidates are interviewed in February 2025.
- **Key Dates**: Nominations open now; applications due January 24th, 2025; interviews in February 2025; announcement at the 2026 ADA conference.

# Risks/Unknowns
- The competitive nature of the application process may discourage some eligible candidates from applying.
- The trip's expenses and limited duration could restrict comprehensive learning experiences for all participants.

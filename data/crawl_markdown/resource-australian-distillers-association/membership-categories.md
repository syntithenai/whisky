# Australian Distillers Association Membership Categories

## Distiller Membership Categories
- **Establishing**: New distilleries with sales under $1,000 and still in the establishment phase. ($300 pa + GST)
- **Small Producer**: Existing voting members producing less than $350,000 annually (Remission). ($850 pa + GST)
- **Medium Producer**: Producers exceeding Remission but not over 50,000 LALs. ($1,250 pa + GST)
- **Large Producer**: Commercial producers surpassing 50,000 LALs. ($2,300 pa + GST)

## Industry and Trade Membership Categories
- **Industry and Trade**: Non-voting membership for related supply chain companies sharing the association's philosophies. ($750 pa + GST)
- **Commercial Partners**: Recognized strategic relationships with non-voting status. (Non-Voting)

## Benefits of Membership
- Connect with key decision-makers in Australian distilling sectors.
- Expand business networks through directory listings and articles.
- Showcase services to influential buyers and industry leaders.
- Gain recognition as a preferred supplier and attend exclusive events.

# Australian Distillers Association - Training & Education

## Key Facts
- Focus on safety, education and innovation in the distilling industry
- Partnering with National Distilling Institute (RTO 3821) to develop Certificates IV in Artisan Fermented Products
- Offering introductory courses like Introduction to Distillery Safety and WSET level 2 award in spirits
- Working with third-party providers for additional training packages

## Production Signals
- Developing new Certificates IV course tailored to distilling industry needs
- FBP40621 - Certificate IV in Artisan Fermented Products launched March 2024

## Commercial Signals
- Partnering with WSET, The Distillers Institute, University of Adelaide and other reputable organizations for training courses
- Promoting safer, smarter and more innovative distilleries through education initiatives

## Risks/Unknowns

# Member Action Pack
A concise guide for Australian distillers to engage their local MPs in four simple steps:
1. Personalise a ready‑made invitation email.
2. Send it to the MP using the provided contact tool.
3. CC or forward the email to the Association’s admin address.
4. Prepare to host the visit with up‑to‑date talking points.
The document stresses that such visits have historically helped secure industry support, citing the creation of the Alcohol Manufacturer’s Remission (AMR) and the push for Spirits Australia as outcomes of grassroots engagement.

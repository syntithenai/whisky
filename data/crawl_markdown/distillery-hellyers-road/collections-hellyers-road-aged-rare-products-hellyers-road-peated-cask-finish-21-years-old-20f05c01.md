# Hellyers Road 21 Year‑Old Peated Cask Finish
- **Age & Maturation**: 17 years in first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon barrels + 4 years in freshly tipped 2004 richly peated casks (52.5 % ALC/VOL, 700 mL). 
- **Price**: AUD 1,350.00 (free shipping on orders over $200). 
- **Limited Edition**: 301 bottles, each boxed in a hand‑crafted presentation box made from Tasmanian Blackheart Sassafras and Myrtle woods.
- **Awards**: Winner of *Best Australian Single Malt Whisky* at the 2025 World Whiskies Awards.
- **Tasting Notes**: Powerful nose with barbecued red meat, orange, strawberry, apple; palate shows chestnut mushroom, sea‑salt caramel, charred nectarines, apricots, smoked vanilla pods, coastal botanicals (kunzea, blue gum, lavender); finish long with dark chocolate and leather.
- **Head Distiller Quote**: Emphasises maritime influence, sweet smoke, salinity, coastal botanicals and layered complexity.

> *“The Hellyers Road 21 Year Old Peated Cask Finish is a true testament to the quality of the peated whiskies crafted at Hellyers Road…”* – Fiona Coutts

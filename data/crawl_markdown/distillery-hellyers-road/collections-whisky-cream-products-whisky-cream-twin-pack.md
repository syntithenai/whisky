# Whisky Cream Twin Pack

Hellyers Road Distillery offers a **Twin Pack** of its award‑winning *Whisky Cream* liqueur, available in six flavour combinations:
- Two Original
- Two Salted Caramel
- Two Coffee
- Original & Salted Caramel
- Original & Coffee
- Coffee & Salted Caramel

Each pack contains two 700 mL bottles (17 % ABV) and is presented in a custom gold‑and‑black gift box with a Hellyers Road postcard. The liqueur blends single‑malt whisky with fresh Tasmanian cream, delivering a creamy, caramelised, or coffee‑infused profile depending on the variant.

Price: **$85 AUD** per twin pack (free shipping on Australian orders over $200).

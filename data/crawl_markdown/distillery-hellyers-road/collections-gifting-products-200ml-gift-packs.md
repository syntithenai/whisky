# Hellyers Road Gift Pack Duos

Hellyers Road Distillery offers **200 mL Tasmanian single‑malt whisky gift packs** featuring two of their finest whiskies in a custom box with a vintage North West Tasmania map. Each duo is available in several age and cask combinations, priced from AUD 95 to AUD 109.

- **Pack options**: Double Cask & Peated 7 yr, Double Cask & Journeyman 8 yr, Double Cask & Oloroso de Jerez 10 yr, Peated 7 yr & Journeyman 8 yr, Peated 7 yr & Oloroso de Jerez 10 yr, Journeyman 8 yr & Oloroso de Jerez 10 yr.
- **Price range**: AUD 95–109 per pack (AUD 105 for the Double Cask & Oloroso de Jerez 10 yr).
- **Shipping**: Free on Australian orders over $200.

These packs are marketed as a perfect gift for whisky enthusiasts, with an option to add a postcard note via order notes.

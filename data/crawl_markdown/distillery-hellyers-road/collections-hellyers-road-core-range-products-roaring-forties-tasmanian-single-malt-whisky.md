# Roaring Forties Tasmanian Single‑Malt Whisky

- **Origin**: Hellyers Road Distillery, Tasmania, Australia.
- **ABV**: 40% ALC/VOL in a 700 mL bottle.
- **Price**: AUD $99.00 (free shipping on orders over $200).
- **Name inspiration**: Named after the Roaring Forties westerly winds that bring clean air from Patagonia to North‑West Tasmania.
- **Tasting notes**:
  - *Nose*: subtle oak, luscious sweetness, lemon peel twist, floral hints, oatmeal, cream, dried apricots, cacao nibs.
  - *Palate*: vanilla ice‑cream with strawberry syrup, plump sultanas, sweet mid‑palate, whisper of smoke.
  - *Finish*: medium length, smooth, moreish.
- **Distillery background**: nearly 30 years of whisky‑making experience; emphasis on maritime origins and pristine Tasmanian environment.

> “Roaring Forties is powerful and bold, yet complex and impeccably balanced.”

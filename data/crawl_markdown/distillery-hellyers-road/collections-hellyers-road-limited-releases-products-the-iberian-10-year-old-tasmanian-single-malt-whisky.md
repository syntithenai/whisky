# The Iberian 10 Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

- **Age & Cask**: 10 years, matured in Spanish oloroso sherry casks and 50+ year old Australian port casks.
- **ABV**: 48.3%.
- **Price**: AUD 220.00 (700 mL) – free shipping on orders over $200.
- **Flavor Profile**: Warm, sweet with sultanas, stewed fruits, spice, orange zest, florals; nuttiness, leather, tobacco from sherry; dark chocolate, mandarin oil from port; wood‑sugar sweetness on finish.
- **Notable Review**: Christopher Coates praises its uniqueness and compares it to a marmalade cask experience.

## Product Highlights
- Rich nose of brioche, stewed fruits, orange, cocoa, Jaffa cake, date.
- Palate: dark chocolate, treacle, spice, tangy mandarin, lasting Christmas‑cake finish.
- Finish: textural, nutty with spicy marmalade and restrained wood.

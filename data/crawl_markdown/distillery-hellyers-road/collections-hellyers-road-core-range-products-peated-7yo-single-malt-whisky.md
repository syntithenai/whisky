# Peated 7 Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky
- **ABV**: 46.2% 
- **Barley**: Peated barley from Inverness, Scotland (Bairds) 
- **Cask**: First‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon casks (Heaven Hill Distillery)
- **Price**: AUD 150 for a 700 mL bottle
- **Award**: Gold Medal – 2024 San Francisco World Spirits Competition
- **Tasting notes**: Sweet tea, brine, sea salt, oyster shell, green leaf, fresh bark, campfire embers, medicinal undertones; smooth peat on the palate with herbal and smoked leaf nuances, honey sweetness, medium‑length finish.

This release marks Hellyers Road’s return to single malt production, emphasising its reputation as Australia’s leading peated whisky producer.

# Hellyers Road Distillery – Gold‑Award Winning Peated 7 YO Single Malt Whisky

Hellyers Road Distillery (Tasmania) has released a **Peated 7 Year Old Single Malt** that earned a gold medal at the 2024 San Francisco World Spirits Competition. The whisky is finished in first‑fill American Oak ex‑bourbon casks from Heaven Hill and uses peated barley sourced directly from Inverness, Scotland. Bottled at 46.2 % ABV, it showcases sweet tea, brine, sea salt, oyster shell, green leaf, fresh bark, campfire embers, medicinal notes, peat, herbal tones, smoked leaf, honey sweetness and a medium‑length finish.

The distillery partners with Salty Lips Oysters to promote pairing videos that highlight the complementary smoky–salty profile. Head Distiller Fiona Coutts emphasizes the unique Tasmanian maturation climate and aims to broaden peated whisky appeal.

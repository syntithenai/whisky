# Voyager Cask 20 Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt
- **Age & Maturation**: 16 years in first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon casks, then 4 years in a French oak cask that previously held Pinot Noir, Christmas fruit and Ruby Pinot Noir.
- **Flavor Profile**: Vanilla, caramel, toffee apples, pecan pie, stewed apples, orange jubes, creamy vanilla, toasted coconut, lemon oil, aniseed, milk chocolate fudge, peach iced tea, floral notes of orange blossom & rose, subtle chili & ginger, bright citrus.
- **Awards**: 2025 World Whiskies Awards – Gold Medal (Small Batch Single Malt Whisky 13‑20 yrs).
- **Price**: AUD 690.00 for a 700 mL bottle.

> *“Voyager” is apt for this special cask release – the whisky was moved through several world‑travelling vessels, culminating in a French oak cask that had aged Pinot Noir and fortified Ruby Pinot Noir.*

## Source Excerpts
- Showcasing meticulous attention to detail in flavour and cask management, this outstanding single malt whisky epitomises the distillery’s commitment to producing aged expressions of distinction that are unrivalled in quality, intensity and character.
- Crafted at the Distillery on Tasmania’s North West coast, after 16 years of careful nurturing in first fill American Oak ex-Bourbon casks, the whisky was beautifully matured, with incredible notes of noble woods and butterscotch, yet destined for more.
- The whisky was then moved into the most worldly of vessels: a French oak cask that started its life ageing Pinot Noir in Tasmania's south.
- Head Distiller Fiona Coutts used this well-travelled cask for the final four years of maturation for this stunning 20 Year Old dram.
- The cask was then used to macerate Christmas fruit, giving the whisky a decadent richness.
- The journey did not stop there, with the cask then used to age local fortified Ruby Pinot Noir, before making its way to Hellyers Road.

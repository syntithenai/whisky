# Oloroso de Jerez 10‑Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky (200 ml)

- **Age & cask**: 10 years in very old 250 L Oloroso sherry casks from Jerez, Spain.
- **ABV**: 46.2 %.
- **Price**: AUD 60.00 per bottle (200 ml).
- **Flavor profile**: toffee, treacle, orange zest, brioche, clove; rich caramel, honey, subtle chocolate, stewed fruits; smooth finish with morish sweet notes.
- **Distillery note**: Small‑batch release from Hellyers Road’s bond store of over 6 500 full‑size casks – the largest in Australia.

> *“Reaping the rewards of 10 years of patience we invite you to savour this sweet and savoury dram, a melange of flavours to delight the senses, you will not be disappointed!”* – Fiona Coutts, Head Distiller.

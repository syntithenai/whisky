# Hellyers Road 21‑Year‑Old American Oak

Hellyers Road Distillery celebrates the release of **American Oak 21 Year Old Single Cask 2283.01** – a single‑malt aged in ex‑bourbon casks from Jack Daniel’s and Wild Turkey for two decades. The whisky was laid down on 10 Oct 2002, selected from over 5,700 casks for its balance and readiness, and is now the distillery’s most awarded Australian spirit.

**Tasting notes:**
- Nose: leatherwood honey, vanilla sweetness, citrus, light floral, butteriness.
- Palate: warm, refined elegance with lingering aftertaste.

The release showcases Hellyers Road’s heritage of quality and its commitment to innovation in Tasmanian whisky making.

# Peated 7 Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky (200 ml)

- **Age & style**: 7 years, peated single malt.
- **Barley**: Sourced from Inverness, Scotland (Bairds).
- **Cask**: First‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon casks from Heaven Hill Distillery.
- **ABV**: 46.2 %.
- **Price**: AUD 50.00 per 200 ml bottle.
- **Accolades**: Gold Medal at the 2024 San Francisco World Spirits Competition.
- **Tasting notes** (from Head Distiller Fiona Coutts):
  - *Nose*: sweet tea, brine, sea salt, oyster shell, green leaf, fresh bark, campfire embers, medicinal nuance.
  - *Palate*: smooth peat, herbal tones, smoked leaf, oyster essence, honey sweetness.
  - *Finish*: medium‑length, effortless smoothness.
- **Special**: First return to Hellyers Road’s single‑malt portfolio; highlighted as Australia’s leading peated whisky producer.

> “Each sip reveals layers of subtle sweetness, vanilla, and savoury peat, underscored by a nuanced salinity that is unique to Hellyers Road Distillery's cool maritime climate in Northwest Tasmania.” – Fiona Coutts, Head Distiller.

# Dark Harmony Limited 2024 Release

Hellyers Road Distillery’s second entry in the *Dark Harmony* series marries a seven‑year‑old whisky finished in American ex‑bourbon casks with a Doppelbock beer‑cask finish. The result is a sweet, chocolate‑laden spirit that evolves to reveal strawberry shortcake and raspberry jam notes.

- **Collaboration**: Hellyers Road Distillery + Communion Brewing Co.
- **Process**: Whisky → barrel → beer fermentation → bottle → whisky re‑fill → final product
- **Tasting notes**: Milk chocolate, strawberry shortcake, raspberry jam, caramelised malt, deep red colour
- **Awards**: Dark Harmony No. 1 won Gold at the 2024 World Whiskies Awards.
- **Availability**: Limited release; sold via Hellyers Road online shop.

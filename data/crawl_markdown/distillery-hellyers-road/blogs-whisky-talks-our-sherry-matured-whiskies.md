# Hellyers Road – Sherry‑Matured Whiskies

Hellyers Road Distillery showcases two flagship sherry‑cask expressions:

* **Oloroso 10 Year Old** – A full‑life maturation in Spanish Oloroso casks, noted as Australia’s oldest example of this style. Available in a small bottle or gift pack.
* **Oloroso Cask Finish 20 Year Old** – First barrelled in 2002, aged 20 years in American oak (ex‑bourbon) then finished for 5–6 months in a 40‑year‑old Oloroso sherry cask. Part of the Aged & Rare collection.

Both whiskies derive their character from dry, nutty, textural Oloroso sherries produced from Palomino grapes and aged oxidatively in a solera system.

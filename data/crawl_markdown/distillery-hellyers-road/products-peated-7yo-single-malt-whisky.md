# Peated 7 Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky
- **ABV**: 46.2% | **Volume**: 700 mL
- **Price**: AUD 150.00 (free shipping on orders over AUD 200)
- **Barley**: Peated malt sourced from Inverness, Scotland (Bairds)
- **Cask**: First‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon casks from Heaven Hill Distillery
- **Awards**: Gold Medal – 2024 San Francisco World Spirits Competition
- **Tasting notes**: Sweet tea & brine, sea salt, oyster shell, green leaf, fresh bark, campfire embers, medicinal undertones; smooth peat on the palate with herbal and smoked leaf tones, honey sweetness, medium‑length finish.
- **Distiller**: Fiona Coutts, Head Distiller

> *“Each sip reveals layers of subtle sweetness, vanilla, and savoury peat, underscored by a nuanced salinity that is unique to Hellyers Road Distillery's cool maritime climate in Northwest Tasmania.”* – Fiona Coutts

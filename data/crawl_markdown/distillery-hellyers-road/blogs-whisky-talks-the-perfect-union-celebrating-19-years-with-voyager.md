# Hellyers Road 19‑Year Old Voyager

Hellyers Road celebrates a 19‑year anniversary with the limited‑edition **19 Year Old Voyager Cask 20F10.03** single malt. Only 350 bottles are released, bottled at 57% ABV after 19 years of maturation in American oak followed by a secondary finish in a French oak cask that previously held wine and liqueur. The whisky is described as rich fruit, noble wood, citrus hints, and a long, comforting finish – a metaphor for the journey of marriage.

The post frames the bottle as a symbol of partnership, maturity and exploration, encouraging gifting on anniversaries and celebrating Hellyers Road’s commitment to distinction.

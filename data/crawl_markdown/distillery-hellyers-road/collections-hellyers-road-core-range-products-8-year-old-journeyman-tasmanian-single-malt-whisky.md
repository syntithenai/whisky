# Journeyman 8‑Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky
- **Age & Maturation**: 8 years, first in American oak ex‑bourbon barrels, then in fine old Australian tawny casks.
- **ABV**: 46.2 % | Volume: 700 ml.
- **Price**: AUD 150.00 (free shipping on orders over AUD 200).
- **Flavor Profile**: Ginger snaps, baking spice, buttery fruit toast, tobacco, sandalwood, toffee apples, fresh plums, dark chocolate, medjool dates, oak tannins.
- **Concept**: Named after the “Journeyman” program that brings whisky makers worldwide for six‑month residencies, fostering knowledge exchange.

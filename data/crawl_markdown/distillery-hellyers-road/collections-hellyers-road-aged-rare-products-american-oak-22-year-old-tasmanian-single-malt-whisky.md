**American Oak 22 Year Old Tasmanian Single‑Malt Whisky – Hellyers Road Distillery**

- 22‑year maturation in a single American oak ex‑bourbon cask (first fill, formerly Jack Daniel’s). 
- Laid down 21 Nov 2002, drawn 18 Mar 2025; 62 L drawn from a 200 L cask, yielding 80 bottles. 
- Bottle size: 700 mL, 56.5 % ALC/VOL. 
- Price: AUD 1,450 (free shipping on orders over $200). 
- Each bottle comes in a handcrafted presentation box made from Tasmanian Huon Pine, Myrtle and Celery Top Pine, with a secret lock and an authenticity letter signed by Head Distiller Fiona Coutts. 
- Awarded Gold for Single Cask Single Malt (Australia) 21 Years & Over at the World Whiskies Awards 2025.

**Tasting notes** – complex, fruit‑forward with peach, apricot, apple compote, baking spice, marzipan, orange oil, stone fruits, honeydew, lemon curd, sticky ginger treacle cake, toasty charred oak and a lingering herbaceous Australian gum tree finish.

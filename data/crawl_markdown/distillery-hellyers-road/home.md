# Hellyers Road Distillery

Hellyers Road Distillery is Tasmania’s oldest operating whisky distillery, continuously producing single‑malt whiskies since the late 1990s. The site showcases its award‑winning range, including the Iberian 10‑Year‑Old and Sherry Cask A Fruit Bowl, as well as whisky cream products. Visitors can explore the distillery, view awards, read blog posts, and shop online.

---

## Key Points
- **Location**: North West Tasmania (Lutruwita)
- **History**: Operating since the late 1990s; oldest continuous distillery in Australia
- **Products**: Iberian 10‑Year‑Old, Sherry Cask A Fruit Bowl, Whisky Cream (six‑pack), and other single‑malt releases
- **Awards**: Multiple national awards for Tasmanian single malt whisky
- **Visitor Experience**: Tours available; off‑season 2026 schedule posted
- **Legal & Compliance**: Age restrictions under Liquor Licensing Act 1990, ABN 81 080 055 269, Licence No. 52260
- **Cultural Acknowledgement**: Respect for the Traditional Custodians of Lutruwita (Palawa/Pakana people)

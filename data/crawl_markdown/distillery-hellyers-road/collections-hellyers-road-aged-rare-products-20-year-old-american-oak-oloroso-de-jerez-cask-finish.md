# American Oak Oloroso de Jerez Cask Finish – 20 Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt

- **Age & casks**: 20 years in American oak (ex‑bourbon) followed by a finish in 40‑year‑old Oloroso sherry casks.
- **ABV**: 54.2 %.
- **Price**: AUD 890.00 (700 mL, free shipping on orders over $200).
- **Presentation**: Hand‑crafted frame made from sustainably sourced Tasmanian Oak off‑cuts.
- **Tasting notes**: Biscotti, caramel, almond; ripe peach, marmalade, oak; ginger snap, cherry, raisin bread; spicy chocolate finish.

This release is one of the oldest Australian whiskies and a collector’s item.

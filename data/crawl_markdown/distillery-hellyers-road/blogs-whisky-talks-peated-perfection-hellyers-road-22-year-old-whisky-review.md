# Peated Perfection: Hellyers Road 22 Year‑Old Whisky Review

A brief blog post by Tim Grant from the *Peated Perfection* series, reviewing Hellyers Road’s 22‑year‑old single malt in American oak. The author notes it is the distillery’s oldest release and one of Tasmania’s oldest single malts, comparing it to a previously reviewed 21‑year‑old peated cask finish.

---

**Key points**
- 22‑year‑old unpeated single cask in American oak.
- Oldest Hellyers Road single malt released to date.
- Second‑oldest Australian whisky tasted by the reviewer.
- Compared favourably to a 21‑year‑old peated finish.

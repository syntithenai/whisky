# Hellyers Road Distillery – Our Story
Hellyers Road Distillery, founded in 1997 on the historic Hellyers Road in North West Tasmania, is Australia’s oldest operating whisky distillery. The site sits amid pristine air, pure rainfall and volcanic soil, surrounded by the Tarkine wilderness and a temperate rainforest climate that shapes its single‑malt whiskies. The distillery remains largely owned by descendants of the original dairy cooperative families and employs around 20 staff, including Head Distiller **Fiona Coutts**, a former microbiologist who blends scientific rigor with creative innovation.

Key points:
- Rooted in an eight‑decade dairy heritage.
- Produces award‑winning single‑malt whisky using local barley, pure water and natural climate for maturation.
- Emphasises “sense of place” and community involvement.
- Operates within a strict regulatory framework (Liquor Licensing Act 1990).

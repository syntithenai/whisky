# The Spaniard 8‑Year‑Old Tasmanian Single Malt
- **Age & Origin**: 8 years, Tasmanian single malt.
- **Maturation**: Started in American oak ex‑bourbon casks, finished one year each in Pedro Ximénez and Oloroso sherry casks (Jerez, Spain).
- **ABV**: 46.2 %.
- **Volume**: 700 mL bottle.
- **Price**: AUD $170.00 (free shipping on Australian orders over $200).
- **Tasting notes**: Rich fruit‑forward nose with toasted nuts, hazelnuts and milk‑chocolate almonds; palate of dried fruits (raisins, figs), raspberry compote, lemon zest; finish dark chocolate, espresso, lingering.

> *“The Spaniard brings together two casks… a rich, yet vibrant whisky, full of fresh and dried fruit, chocolate, coffee and a nuttiness that could only come from a sherry cask.”*

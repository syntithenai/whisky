# 21 Year‑Old Peated Cask Finish Wins “Best Australian Single Malt”

Hellyers Road Distillery announced that its **21 Year‑Old Peated Cask Finish** (product code 20F05c01) captured the *Best Australian Single Malt* award at the World Whiskies Awards 2025 in London. The whisky, aged 17 years in first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon barrels and finished 4 years in a freshly tipped 2004 peated cask, was judged against other top Australian expressions.

The win follows earlier category victories for Hellyers Road that year, including four gold medals. CEO Derek Charge and Head Distiller Fiona Coutts highlighted the distillery’s Tasmanian terroir, maritime influence and meticulous maturation process as key to the whisky’s success.

# Off Season 2026 – Hellyers Road Distillery

Hellyers Road launches an exclusive tasting event tied to the launch of its fifth single‑malt, **Dark Harmony** (a collaboration with Communion Brewing). The event offers two cask‑aged Dark Harmonies and two “B‑side” casks that were used by Communion for beer maturation but are now left to mature in Hellyers Road’s Bond Store. All tastings take place at the Cellar Door.

- **Price**: $22 per tasting (4 × 15 mL). 
- **Cask details**:
  - *Direct from cask*: 23F009c01 – Oat Stout Finish, 9‑year‑old; 24F060c01 – Black IPA Tawny Finish, 8‑year‑old.
  - *Direct from bottle*: Dark Harmony 4 (American Oak Oyster Stout) and a preview of Dark Harmony 5.

The page also includes standard site navigation, acknowledgements to Traditional Custodians, legal warnings, and contact information.

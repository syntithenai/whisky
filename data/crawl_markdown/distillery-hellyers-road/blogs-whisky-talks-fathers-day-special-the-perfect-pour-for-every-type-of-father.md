# Father's Day Special: The Perfect Pour for Every Type of Father
Hellyers Road Distillery celebrates Father’s Day with a curated selection of award‑winning single malt whiskies and whisky cream liqueurs, each paired to a different father archetype. Highlights include:
- **Double Cask** – 5 yrs American Oak + up to 2 yrs Tasmanian Pinot Noir French Oak.
- **7 yr Peated** – smoky, two‑time Gold Award winner.
- **Journeyman** – 12 yr American Oak + 5 yr Port casks.
- **21 yr American Oak Single Cask #2283.01** – hand‑crafted box, signed by Head Distiller.
- **12 yr & 16 yr American Oak** – classic and award‑winning single‑cask expressions.
- **Whisky Cream Liqueur** – World’s Best Cream Liqueur (2024).
The post invites gifting ideas and encourages treating dads to a whisky that matches their personality.

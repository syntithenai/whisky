# Dark Harmony No. 4 – Oyster Stout‑Finished American Oak

- **Type**: Tasmanian single‑malt whisky finished in an oyster stout cask.
- **Age**: 8 years, aged in American oak then finished in a beer‑cask.
- **Production**: 310 bottles made; brewed with oyster shells, barrel‑aged for 6 weeks before whisky finishing.
- **Price**: AUD 245.00 (bottle) / AUD 255.00 (bottle + can of oyster stout).
- **Key flavours**: maritime umami, seaweed/miso, chocolate, coffee, herbaceous hops, anise, citrus oil, milk chocolate, Ovaltine‑like sweetness.
- **Notes**: Deep oily mouthfeel with long viscous legs; finish echoes the barrel‑aged oyster stout.

> *“A complex whisky that will leave you pondering the wonders of both whisky and beer…”* – Head Distiller Fiona Coutts

## Long Description
# Hellyers Road Distillery

Hellyers Road Distillery, located on Tasmania’s northwest coast, is the state’s oldest operating whisky distillery. Since the late 1990s it has produced a range of single‑malt whiskies that have earned multiple international awards, including Best Australian Single Malt at the 2025 World Whiskies Awards.

## Product Highlights
- **21 Year‑Old Peated Cask Finish** – Awarded Best Australian Single Malt (2025). Aged in peated casks for 21 years.
- **10 Year Old Oloroso de Jerez** – Released October 2024, matured in traditional 250‑litre Oloroso sherry casks.
- **7 Year Old Peated Single Malt** – Gold‑award winning release (May 2024).
- **Iberian 10 Year Old** – Sherry‑cask aged single malt celebrating Iberian fortified wine heritage.
- **Barnbougle Edition 1 – 8 Year Old** – Co‑produced with New Zealand’s Barnbougle, selected by founder Richard Sattler.
- **Roaring Forties** – Clean, airy character named after Southern Ocean winds.
- **Whisky Cream Six Pack** – Three flavours (Original, Salted Caramel, Coffee) priced at $249 per pack.

## Visitor Experience
The distillery offers guided tours of the visitor centre, tastings, and a shop for online orders. Visitors can also book private tours or attend special events such as Tasmanian Whisky Week.

## Industry Presence
Hellyers Road maintains an active blog (“Whisky Talks”) that covers product launches, award news, reviews, and industry insights. The distillery has been featured in podcasts (e.g., Christopher Coates interview) and reviewed by external publications like Peated Perfection.

## Awards & Recognition
- 2025 World Whiskies Awards – Best Australian Single Malt (21 Year‑Old Peated Cask Finish)
- Multiple other international awards for peated and sherry‑cask releases.

The distillery’s unique flavour profile blends peat, sherry, oak, and fruit notes, delivering a complex yet smooth experience that reflects Tasmania’s maritime climate.

## Long Description
# Hellyers Road Distillery

Hellyers Road Distillery (North West Tasmania, Australia) is the country’s oldest operating whisky distillery.  Founded in 1997, it produces a small‑batch Tasmanian single‑malt portfolio that is frequently finished or matured in exotic casks – American oak ex‑bourbon, Spanish sherry and Australian port – and also offers a line of award‑winning whisky‑cream liqueurs.

## Core Product Lines
- **Single Malt Whisky** – 700 ml bottles (46.2–54.7 % ABV) aged in American oak, French oak, sherry or stout casks.  Highlights include *The Journeyman*, *Peated 7‑Year Old*, *Double Cask*, *Dark Harmony No. 4* and limited releases such as *The Spaniard* (8 y) and *The Iberian* (10 y).
- **Whisky Cream Liqueur** – 700 ml bottles (17 % ABV) blended with fresh Tasmanian cream and sea salt.  Variants are Original, Salted Caramel, Coffee and a twin‑pack gift set.

## Production & Distillery Features
All whisky is mashed, fermented, triple distilled and bottled on site.  The distillery uses only locally sourced barley (including peated malt from Inverness) and employs first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon barrels, French oak barriques, Spanish sherry casks and even beer casks for finishing.  Products are non‑chill filtered, non‑coloured and free of preservatives.

## Visitor Experience & Commerce
Hellyers Road offers guided tours, tastings and a visitor centre on the North West Tasmania coast.  The website sells all products directly (eBay‑style cart) with free shipping for orders over AUD 200.  Age‑gate checks are present; the site complies with Australian liquor licensing.

## Awards & Recognition
The distillery’s whiskies have earned Gold Medals at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition and other international accolades, while its whisky‑cream liqueur has been crowned *World’s Best Cream Liqueur*.

**Hellyers Road Distillery – Production Overview**

- Founded by dairy farmers who applied their expertise to create *non‑traditional pot stills*.
- Uses **Tasmanian barley**, malted locally, and rainwater for mashing.
- Wash: ~7 % ABV, cloudy/clear mix; fermented at ≤32 °C for 50 h with high‑performance yeast (85 % attenuation in first 16 h).
- Distillation:
  - *Wash still*: 42 kL working capacity, 60 kL total; tall cylindrical design gives high reflux → 70 % ABV distillate.
  - *Spirit still*: 20 kL working, 30 kL total; slow runs allow precise cuts and copper‑rich condensers (32 tubes).
- Maturation in Tasmanian bond stores near Bass Strait: cool, humid climate accelerates aging (~2× faster than Scotland) with sea‑salt influence.
- Oak program includes American Bourbon casks, French wine casks, Pinot Noir Tasmanian wine casks, and fortified wine casks (Port, Sherry, etc.).
- Peated expressions sourced from Bairds Maltings, Scotland.

*Key flavour notes*: light, fruity new‑make; complex cereal, nutty, floral, smoky (peated).

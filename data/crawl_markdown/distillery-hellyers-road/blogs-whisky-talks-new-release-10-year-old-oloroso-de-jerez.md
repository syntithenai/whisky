# Hellyers Road – 10 Year‑Old Oloroso de Jerez

Hellyers Road Distillery announces its newest sherry‑cask release: a 10‑year‑old single malt aged in traditional 250 L Oloroso casks from Jerez, Spain. The whisky is bottled at 46.2 % ABV in 700 mL bottles and presented in a white‑framed box with a North West Tasmania map.

**Tasting notes** – toffee, treacle, orange zest, ginger snaps, stewed apricots, brioche, caramel, clove, dark chocolate, honey‑glazed walnut, nutmeg, vanilla, dried fruits and sherry. The palate is full‑bodied, smooth, satin‑like with rich sweetness and a lingering finish of caramel, stewed fruit and dark chocolate.

The release is the oldest fully matured sherry‑cask whisky from any Australian distillery and reflects Hellyers Road’s legacy of quality sherry casks.

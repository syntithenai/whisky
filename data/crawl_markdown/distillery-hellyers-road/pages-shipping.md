# Shipping – Hellyers Road Distillery

**Domestic shipping**: Daily dispatch from North West Tasmania via Australia Post. Standard and Express options are available; rates vary by state:
- TAS, VIC, NSW, ACT, QLD, SA: $15 (Standard), $23 (Express)
- WA, NT: $20 (Standard), $30 (Express)

All orders receive tracking via email and the Australia Post app. Orders over **$200** qualify for free standard shipping; Express can be upgraded.

**International shipping**: Direct to New Zealand, Singapore, United Kingdom. Prices are in destination currency with no Australian GST. Import duties/taxes are paid by the recipient. Customers must comply with local import regulations and accept responsibility for any additional charges.

The distillery is not liable for delays or stops due to customs or tax rules abroad.

**Legal & compliance**: The site acknowledges traditional custodians, includes liquor licensing warnings, and provides ABN and licence details.

# Woodlands Distillery FAQ

- **Location**: 1455 O'Neil Road, Porongurup, WA (base of the Porongurup Range). 
- **Hours**: Friday‑Saturday & Monday, 11 am–4 pm; closed Tuesday‑Thursday. 
- **Visiting**: Open to walk‑ins; group bookings recommended via email. Tasting paddles available for gin, vodka and whisky.
- **Accessibility**: On‑site parking (including accessible bay), ramp access, disability bathrooms.
- **Pets & Families**: Dogs allowed on lead after prior confirmation; children welcome with supervision.
- **Products**: Five whiskies – Devils Slide Rye, Wheat Whisky Batch 1, and Single Malt Batches 2, 3, 5. All single‑cask, small batch releases (2–4 yr ageing in ex‑bourbon, Port French Oak, French Oak Muscat or Shiraz barrels).
- **Sustainability**: Solar‑powered spring water pump; spent grain used for cattle feed; nutrient‑enriched distillery water recycled on 450 acres.
- **Shipping**: Australia‑wide and international (fee applies). Free shipping over $100.
- **Contact**: info@woodlandsdistillery.com.au

## Key Facts
- Single‑cask, small batch whisky.

- Distillery building repurposed from old shearing shed.

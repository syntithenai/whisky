# Woodlands Distillery FAQ

- **Location**: 1455 O'Neil Road, Porongurup, WA (base of the Porongurup Range). 
- **Hours**: Friday‑Saturday & Monday, 11 am–4 pm. Closed Tuesday‑Thursday.
- **Visits**: Open to walk‑ins; group bookings recommended via email. Private tours can be arranged outside normal hours.
- **Tasting**: Three‑shot tasting paddle available for spirits including whisky, gin and vodka.
- **Accessibility**: On‑site parking (including accessible bay), ramp access, disability bathrooms.
- **Pets & Families**: Dogs allowed on lead after prior email; children welcome with supervision.
- **Products**: Five whisky batches – Devils Slide Rye, Wheat Whisky Batch 1, and Single Malt Batches 2, 3, 5. All single‑cask, limited release.
- **Shipping**: Australia‑wide shipping; international orders possible (extra fee). Free shipping on orders over $100.
- **Production**: Ethanol made from scratch using local grain and spring water; aged 2–4 years in ex‑bourbon, Port French Oak, French Oak Muscat or Shiraz barrels.
- **Sustainability**: Solar‑powered water pump, spent grain used for cattle feed, nutrient‑enriched distillery water recycled on farm.

For more details contact info@woodlandsdistillery.com.au.

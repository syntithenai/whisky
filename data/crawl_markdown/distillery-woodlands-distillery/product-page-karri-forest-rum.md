# Karri Forest Rum

A rich and unique Australian rum, the **Karri Forest Rum** combines sweet and decadent flavors with the sophisticated influence of the Shiraz barrel, making it a crowd-pleasing choice for any occasion.

## Tasting Notes
- Smooth and easy to enjoy
- Creamy coconut
- Sweet caramel
- Rich dark chocolate taking center stage
- The Shiraz barrel adds a touch of fruity sweetness and a light hint of spiced oak, giving it a nice depth of flavor

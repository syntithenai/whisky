# Sustainability at Woodlands Distillery
Woodlands Distillery highlights its eco‑friendly practices:
- **Waste**: Spent grain from vodka production is fed to local cattle.
- **Water**: On‑site spring water pumped by solar power; distillation by‑product used as fertigation on 450 acres of farmland.
- **Up‑cycling**: Old shearing shed and hardwood timbers repurposed for the distillery’s architecture.

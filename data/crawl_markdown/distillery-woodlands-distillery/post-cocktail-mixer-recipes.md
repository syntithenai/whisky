# Cocktail & Mixer Recipes
Woodlands Distillery shares a handful of simple martini‑style drinks and a strawberry daiquiri, all using their own wheat vodka, classic vodka or Nancy Peak Botanical Gin. The page lists ingredients, proportions (in ml), preparation steps, and a quick simple syrup recipe.

## Key recipes
- **Wheat Vodka Martini** – 60 ml wheat vodka + 1 Tbsp dry vermouth + soda/tonic, served with lemon peel.
- **Limoncello Martini** – 30 ml classic vodka + 15 ml limoncello + soda/tonic + lime juice, garnished with a lime wheel.
- **Gin Mojito** – 30 ml Nancy Peak gin + half‑lemon juice + simple syrup + mint + soda water, topped with lemon and mint.
- **Vodka Strawberry Daiquiri** – 60 ml classic vodka + 30 ml sugar syrup + 30 ml lime juice + strawberries + ice, finished with soda water and a strawberry garnish.

A quick simple‑syrup recipe (1 cup water + ½ cup honey) is also provided.

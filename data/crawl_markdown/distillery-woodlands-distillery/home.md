# Woodlands Distillery – Australian Spirits

Woodlands Distillery is a grain‑to‑glass craft distillery located at the base of the Porongurup Range in Western Australia. They grow their own grains, use natural spring water from their land, and produce ethanol from scratch—an uncommon practice among Australian distilleries.

## Product Range
- **Whiskies**: 5 varieties – Devils Slide Rye, Wheat Whisky (Batch 1), and three single‑malt batches (2, 3, 5).
- **Other Spirits**: Gins, vodkas, rum, liqueur.
- Prices for the highlighted whiskies are $180 (Devils Slide Rye) and $100 for each of the other batches.

## Operations & Sustainability
- All ingredients sourced locally; botanicals reflect Porongurup character.
- By‑products: water returned to paddocks, mash waste fed to cattle.
- Building repurposed from an old shearing shed; bar made from a fallen Marri tree.

## Visiting & Purchasing
- Open Friday–Monday 11 am–4 pm (closed Tuesday‑Thursday). Good Friday closed.
- Spirits available online with free shipping over $100 and nationwide delivery.

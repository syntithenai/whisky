# Woodlands Distillery – Australian Spirits

Woodlands Distillery is a grain‑to‑glass craft distillery located at the base of the Porongurup Range in Western Australia. They grow their own grains, use natural spring water and produce ethanol from scratch. The site offers five whiskies (Devils Slide Rye, Wheat Whisky Batch 1, Single Malt Batch 2, 3 & 5), a range of gins, vodkas, rum and liqueurs. All products are available for online purchase with free shipping on orders over $100.

Key highlights:
- **Locally sourced ingredients** – grains, botanicals, spring water.
- **Sustainability focus** – waste water used to irrigate crops; mash waste fed to cattle; building materials recycled.
- **Visitor experience** – open Friday‑Monday 11 am–4 pm with tasting paddles and tours.

For more details visit the shop or the distillery’s online store.

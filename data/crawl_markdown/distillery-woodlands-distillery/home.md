# Woodlands Distillery - Australian Spirits from the Porongurup Range

## Key Facts
- Craft distillery nestled at the base of the Porongurup Range in Western Australia's Great Southern
- Grow their own grains, use natural spring water and make ethanol from scratch
- Award-winning Single Malt Whiskies: Batch 2, Batch 3 & Batch 5
- Handcrafted Gins, Vodkas, Rum and other spirits available online with nationwide shipping

## Production Signals
- Grown their own cereal grains used in alcohol production
- Spring water sourced from natural spring at base of Porongurup mountain range
- Ethanol made from scratch on-site
- Botanicals for gins chosen to reflect unique character of the Porongurup region

## Commercial Signals
- Five different whiskies available online including bold Devils Slide Rye and three Single Malts (Batch 2, Batch 3 & Batch 5)
- Handcrafted Gins, Vodkas and Rum in addition to whisky range
- Nationwide shipping Australia wide

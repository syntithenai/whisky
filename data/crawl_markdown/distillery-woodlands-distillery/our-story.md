# Woodlands Distillery – Our Story

Woodlands Distillery was founded in 2018 by four friends who wanted to showcase Western Australia’s potential for premium spirits. Located on a 450‑acre farm in the Great Southern region, the distillery operates out of an old shearing shed at the base of the Porongurup Range. The company focuses on Australian‑made gin, vodka and whiskey, using locally grown grains, native botanicals, and water sourced from granite rock to create distinct flavours.

The site offers free shipping on orders over $100 and provides contact details, FAQs, a shop page, subscription service, and legal information under the Liquor Control Act 1988.

# Woodlands Distillery – Our Story
Woodlands Distillery was founded in 2018 by four friends who wanted to showcase Western Australia’s potential for premium spirits. Located on a 450‑acre farm in the Great Southern region, the distillery operates out of an old shearing shed at the base of the Porongurup Range. The company focuses on Australian‑made gin, vodka and whiskey, using locally sourced grains, granite‑filtered water and native botanicals to give each spirit a distinct regional character.

The site offers free shipping on orders over $100 and provides information on stockists, subscription options, shipping & returns, licensing, FAQs, contact details and legal warnings under the Liquor Control Act 1988.

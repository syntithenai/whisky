# Woodlands Distillery - About Us

- Founded in **2018** by four friends passionate about promoting the Great Southern region of Western Australia as a high-quality liquor-producing area.
- Located on a **450-acre farm** in the Porongurup Range, using native water sourced from above granite rock and locally grown cereal grains to create unique gins, vodkas, and whiskeys.
- Emphasizes Australian heritage by sourcing native botanics for gin infusions.

# Production Signals

- **Gin**: Infused with native Western Australian botanicals like kangaroo apple, wattleseed, and finger lime.
- **Vodka**: Made from locally sourced wheat and barley, distilled in small batches.
- **Whiskey**: Crafted using the region's pristine water and locally grown grains, aged in reclaimed oak barrels.

# Commercial Signals

- Proudly Australian products with a distinct flavor profile inspired by native ingredients.
- Emphasis on sustainability through local sourcing and traditional distilling methods.

# Risks / Unknowns

- Limited production scale due to small farm size and artisanal approach.
- Dependence on regional climate for consistent water supply and botanical availability.

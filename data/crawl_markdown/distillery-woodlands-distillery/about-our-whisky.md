# Woodlands Distillery - Australian Single Malt Whisky

Crafted with dedication since 2021 in the Porongurup region of Western Australia's Great Southern, Woodlands Distillery produces award-winning single malt whiskies that tell their own story through the barrel they were aged in.

## Key Facts
- **Small Batch**: Each batch is unique and handcrafted with care.
- **Award Winning**: Gold and Bronze medals at the 2025 Perth Royal Show.
- **Grain to Glass**: Distilled, matured, and bottled on-site.
- **Variety**: Single Malt Batch 2, Batch 3, Batch 5, Rye Devils Slide, Wheat Whisky Batch 1.

## Production Signals
- **Barrel Aging**: Ex-bourbon, Port French Oak, Muscat, Shiraz barrels used for distinct flavors.
- **Small Batch**: Limited production ensures quality and uniqueness.
- **Local Ingredients**: Rye grain sourced locally; Mosquito wheat grown on the farm.

## Commercial Signals
- **Recognition**: Multiple awards at Perth Royal Show including Champion Whisky.
- **Best Seller Online**: Batch 5 is their top-selling single malt.
- **Award Winning**: Gold and Bronze medals highlight quality and appeal.

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited availability of small batch releases may affect supply for collectors.
- Regional focus on Western Australia could limit broader market reach initially.

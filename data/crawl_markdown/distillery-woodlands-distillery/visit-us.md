# Visit Us – Woodlands Distillery
Woodlands Distillery welcomes visitors Friday‑Monday (11 am–4 pm) at 1455 O'Neil Road, Porongurup, WA. Guests can sample a three‑shot tasting of local spirits (vodka, gin, liqueur), enjoy food such as grazing boards, and view the surrounding vineyards and mountain scenery. The distillery offers online sales with free shipping on orders over $100.

- **Opening hours**: Friday–Monday 11 am–4 pm; closed Tuesday‑Thursday.
- **Location**: Porongurup, Western Australia.
- **Contact**: info@woodlandsdistillery.com.au.
- **Group bookings**: email in advance.
- **Online shop**: available with nationwide shipping.

For more details visit the [FAQ](https://www.woodlandsdistillery.com/faq) or the [contact page](https://www.woodlandsdistillery.com/contact).

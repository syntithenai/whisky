# Nancy Peak Botanical Gin

Woodlands Distillery’s **Nancy Peak Botanical Gin** is a 40 % ABV, 500 ml bottle named after the nearby Porongurup Range peak. It offers a classic botanical profile with prominent floral notes, gentle citrus and spice undertones, and a smooth juniper base. The gin can be enjoyed neat, with elderflower tonic, or in cocktails.

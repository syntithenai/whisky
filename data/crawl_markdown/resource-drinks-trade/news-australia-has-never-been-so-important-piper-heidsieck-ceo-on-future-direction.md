**Piper‑Heidsieck CEO on Australia’s importance**

Champagne house Piper‑Heidsieck, led by new CEO Stéphane Decaux, sees Australia as a key growth market amid global contraction in the Champagne sector. Decaux attributes Australian success to strategic partnerships—most notably the Australian Open sponsorship and collaborations with Oatley Fine Wine Merchants—which boost brand visibility and premium positioning. He outlines three priorities: elevating brand experience, balancing premium retail/off‑premise expansion with on‑trade growth, and enhancing profitability while maintaining sustainability commitments (including B Corp certification). The article also notes EPI Group’s appointment of Nicolas Beckers as CEO of its Champagne Division.

---

**Key takeaways**
- Australia is a bright spot for Piper‑Heidsieck despite global volume decline.
- Partnerships, especially the Australian Open deal, drive brand awareness and new accounts.
- Decaux’s strategy focuses on experience, trade balance, and sustainable profitability.
- EPI Group has reshuffled leadership in its Champagne division.

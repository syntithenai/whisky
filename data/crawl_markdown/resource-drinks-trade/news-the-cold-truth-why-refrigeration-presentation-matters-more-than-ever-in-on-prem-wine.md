# The Cold Truth: Why refrigeration presentation matters more than ever in on‑prem wine

In 2026, Drinks Trade highlights how a well‑designed, temperature‑controlled wine fridge is now a key element of an on‑prem venue’s ambience and commercial strategy. A glass‑fronted cabinet that keeps bottles at the right temperature (≈13 °C for reds, ≈24 °C for whites) not only preserves quality but also signals care to guests, boosting confidence and sales. Brands like **LeCavist** produce purpose‑built cabinets with precision zones, humidity control, vibration dampening and UV protection—features that domestic fridges lack.

The article stresses the Australian climate’s impact: ambient heat can over‑warm reds, accelerating ageing and damaging corks. Proper refrigeration protects margins by preventing temperature‑related spoilage and meeting increasingly educated consumer expectations for optimal serving conditions.

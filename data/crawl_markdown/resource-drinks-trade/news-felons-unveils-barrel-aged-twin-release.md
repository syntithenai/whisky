# Felons Brewing Co. Unveils Barrel‑Aged Twin Release
Felons Brewing Co. has launched a limited‑edition twinpack of two barrel‑aged beers:
- **Amber Plum Cola (Oude Bruin)** – a modern Belgian style aged in American oak barrels, including ex‑red wine, ex‑Starward whisky and ex‑crisp lager casks.
- **Yuzu Mist Fruited Saison** – a bright, citrusy saison finished in Slavonian white oak and ex‑Chardonnay barrels, with locally grown Willyama yuzu.
Both beers are aged up to 22 months at Felons’ Brisbane Barrel Hall and are available in bottles, on tap at Felons venues (Brisbane Barrel Hall & Manly Wharf) and through select national stockists.

> *“These beers are an expression of patience and possibility… each release allows us to further explore how flavour can evolve through time.”* – Dean Romeo, Brand Director

> *“We’ve taken a style known for its sharpness and refined it into something more rounded, layered and approachable…”* – Tom Champion, Brewery Director

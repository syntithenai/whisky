# Future Proof Melbourne 2026
Future Proof Melbourne expanded to over 30 suppliers, added interactive activations and a broader discussion program. Key brands included William Grant & Sons, Lion, Sazerac, Brown‑Forman, Diageo, Asahi Beverages, Campari, Red Bull, Altus Brands and more.
Panel sessions hosted by Samantha Payne tackled business strategy, guest experience and consumer expectations with panellists such as Sarah Dawn Mars, Pete Hollands, Dani & Oska Whitehart, Rob Libecans, Jack Connor and Priscilla Leong. Paramount Liquor Director Leigh Rowe praised the event’s collaborative spirit.
Brand activations featured Lion’s Guinness Lovely Day (live music), Monkey Shoulder’s Waxflowers Vinyl Bar Masterclasses on Speyside whisky, live vinyl sets, roaming samples, food from EzTenda, Fassta & Frntlne, and The Notetakers wine tasting. Future Proof will move to Adelaide next.

# Taylors Wines Appoints New GM of Sales

Taylors Wines has named **Mikaela Noonan** as its new General Manager of Sales for Australia, bringing over 15 years of experience from Moët Hennessy and other major retailers. The appointment aligns with the company’s strategic pivot toward a premium‑led growth model that focuses on simplifying operations, reducing complexity, and investing in brand health.

The move follows CEO **John Freeman**’s announcement of a new strategy centred on premiumisation, addressing oversupply, targeting younger consumers, and removing trade barriers. Noonan is expected to drive growth in the premium wine segment by unlocking opportunities and executing with precision.

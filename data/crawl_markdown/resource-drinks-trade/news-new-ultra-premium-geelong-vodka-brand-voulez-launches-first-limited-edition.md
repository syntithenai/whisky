# Voulez Vodka – Ultra‑Premium Geelong Launch

- **Brand**: Voulez Vodka, a small‑batch, ultra‑premium vodka from Geelong.
- **Founders & Makers**: French entrepreneur Tamara Craig and Anther Distillery (lead chemist Dervilla McGowan).
- **Ingredients**: Blend of locally sourced potatoes and Australian wheat chosen for texture, purity and appeal to a female palate; no filtration needed.
- **Profile**: Textural, delicately sweet with a crisp, clean finish suitable for martinis or on‑ice.
- **Packaging & Pricing**: 700 ml bottles at 40% ABV, priced at $200 each (limited release).
- **Story Angle**: Craig’s French upbringing and emphasis on elegance add an experiential narrative to the spirit.

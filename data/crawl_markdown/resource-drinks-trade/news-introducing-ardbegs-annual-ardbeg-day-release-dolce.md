# Introducing Ardbeg's annual Ardbeg Day release – Dolce

Ardbeg has released its 2026 limited‑edition **Dolce** in Australia ahead of the global launch. The whisky is a blend of rare malt aged in *Marsala dolce* casks and classic bourbon‑cask matured spirit, marking the distillery’s first use of Marsala since 2012. It offers aromas of sun‑ripened orange and apricot with flavours of dark chocolate, olive grove, soft smoke and salted nuts.

- **Release date**: 30 April (24‑hour preview) via Ardbeg Committee; from 1 May through The Whisky Club.
- **Price**: $180 per bottle.
- **ABV**: 47.8%.
- **Events**: “Peat‑Zzeria” celebrations across Melbourne, Brisbane, Perth, Adelaide and Canberra, culminating in a Sydney Ardbeg Day event featuring Dr Bill Lumsden.

The launch is themed around *la dolce vita* and celebrates peat and pizza with the unique name *Peat‑Zzeria*.

**Maison Perrier‑Jouët unveils latest artistic collaboration**

- Maison Perrier‑Jouët partners with Polish artist and designer Marcin Rusak for its 14th annual art series.
- The collaboration is inspired by laboratory research that translates plant signals into visual and audible forms, conducted at the University of Kraków.
- Limited‑edition bottle designs feature both *Perrier‑Jouët Blanc de Blancs* and *Belle Époque 2016*, incorporating a spiral motif, botanical elements from the Épernay region, human senses, and the house’s colour palette.
- Each set includes two wine glasses with the iconic anemone motif.
- The gift sets are available to Australian trade via existing Perrier‑Jouët and Pernod Ricard distribution channels.

**Calabria Family Wine Group expands distribution in Australia**

- Secured national distribution rights for *Groupe Bollinger*, *Villa Maria* and *Tahbilk*.
- Added House of Fine Wine and The Wine Company to its network.
- Deal announced Feb 23 2026 by Sales & Marketing Director Andrew Calabria.
- Indevin Group CEO Simon Limmer praised the partnership.
- New portfolio will roll out from April 2026.

This move positions Calabria as a leading national distributor in the premium and luxury wine segment.

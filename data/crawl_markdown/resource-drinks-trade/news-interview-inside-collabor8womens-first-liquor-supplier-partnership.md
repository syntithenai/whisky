# Interview: Inside Collabor8Women’s first liquor supplier partnership

- **Collabor8Women** is an invitation‑only networking collective for women in hospitality, founded Dec 2021 and now with ~2,000 members across Australia.
- The group has supported charity events (e.g., $18k raised for love.fish) and recently announced its first liquor supplier partnership with **Joval Wines**.
- The partnership was launched at Collabor8Women’s inaugural Founding Members event in March 2026 as part of the new Gold Access premium series.
- Co‑founders Karen Eck and Samantha Theron highlighted Joval’s focus on quality, integrity and professional service. Marketing Director Bree Richmond noted the partnership will showcase Joval wines to a highly engaged professional audience.
- The first partner spotlight is **Narelle King**, winemaker at Tar & Roses (Victoria). An interview with Drinks Trade covers her career path, winemaking philosophy, challenges in building a brand, and the importance of teamwork and trust.

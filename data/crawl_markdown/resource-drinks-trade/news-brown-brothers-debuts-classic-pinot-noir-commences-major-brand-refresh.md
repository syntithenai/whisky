# Brown Brothers Classic Pinot Noir Launch
- **Brand refresh**: Over 40 wines updated in 2026.
- **New product**: First pinot noir at scale from the 137‑year‑old family winery.
- **Price**: $20 per bottle.
- **Target**: Lighter, fresher red wine for everyday and social occasions.
- **Packaging**: Cleaner, contemporary design across the Classic range.
- **Grapes**: Sourced from Victoria; 2025 vintage released.

The refresh aims to strengthen brand cohesion and provide retailers with a unified, impactful product block.

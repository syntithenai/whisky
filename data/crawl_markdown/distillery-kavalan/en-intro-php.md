# Kavalan Single Malt Whisky

Kavalan is a Taiwanese single malt whisky brand known for its unique, Japanese-inspired distilling techniques and high-quality ingredients.

## Key Facts
- Distilled at the Kavalan Old Still Distillery in Kenting, Taiwan
- Uses locally sourced sugarcane molasses as the primary base ingredient
- Aged in American white oak barrels previously used for bourbon
- Offers a range of expressions with varying ages and flavors

## Production Signals
- Triple distilled using traditional copper pot stills
- Matured in charred American oak barrels for 3-12 years depending on expression
- Emphasizes the use of sugarcane molasses over grain spirits
- Focuses on achieving a balance between Japanese whisky traditions and Taiwanese terroir

## Commercial Signals
- Gained international recognition, winning multiple awards at global whisky competitions
- Limited release expressions create buzz among collectors and enthusiasts
- Collaborations with other distilleries and brands showcase Kavalan's versatility

## Risks / Unknowns
- As a relatively new brand (established in 2013), some aspects of the production process may still be evolving
- Availability can be limited due to high demand and small-scale production
- Pricing reflects the premium quality and unique Taiwanese origin

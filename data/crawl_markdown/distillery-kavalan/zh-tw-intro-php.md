# Kavalan Whisky - Gold Reserve Single Cask

Kavalan's flagship expression, the **Gold Reserve Single Cask**, is a limited edition Japanese whisky that has been aging in oak casks for over two decades. This dram showcases the distillery's commitment to craftsmanship and quality.

## Key Facts
- Limited Edition: Only 1,000 bottles produced annually
- Aged in Japan: Distilled at Kavalan Distillery, aged in Japan
- Single Cask: Each bottle is a unique expression from one cask
- No Fillers: Pure whisky with no added sugars or flavorings
- Premium Price: Reflects the rarity and quality of the product

## Production Signals
- **Distillation**: Double distilled using traditional copper pot stills
- **Maturation**: Aged in Japanese oak barrels for 20+ years
- **Finishing**: Some bottles may have a sherry cask finishing
- **Proof**: Bottled at 46% ABV (92 proof)

## Commercial Signals
- Highly sought after by collectors and connoisseurs
- Limited availability, making it a prized possession
- Frequently featured in whisky auctions and private sales

## Risks / Unknowns
- Exact age of each bottle is unknown due to the single cask nature
- Availability is limited, leading to speculation and high prices on the secondary market

# How to Choose the Right Distilling Equipment

This article explains how craft distillery owners should select durable equipment. It covers:
1. **Distillation basics** – vapor rises through a column and condenses on plates.
2. **Number of plates** – more plates give higher purity (e.g., vodka) but longer runs; fewer plates keep flavor for whiskey.
3. **Still types** – pot stills, reflux columns with liquid‑management, vapor‑management or cooling‑management options.
4. **Budget considerations** – balance upfront cost against per‑liter production costs and quality.
5. **Choosing a supplier** – evaluate longevity, reputation, customer service, and technical knowledge.

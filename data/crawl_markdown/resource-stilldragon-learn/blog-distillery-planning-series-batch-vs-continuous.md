# Distillery Planning Series: Batch vs. Continuous

StillDragon explains the two main distillation methods—batch and continuous—highlighting their operational differences, strengths, and ideal use cases for small‑scale versus large‑scale production.

- **Batch distillation** is simple, offers fine control over flavor extraction, but can be time‑consuming and variable between runs.
- **Continuous distillation** handles high volumes efficiently, delivers consistent quality, yet demands more expertise and automation.

The article advises choosing the method that best matches your production volume, desired consistency, and level of automation.

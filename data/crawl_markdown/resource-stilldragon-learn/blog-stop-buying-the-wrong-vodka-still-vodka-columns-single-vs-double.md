# Stop Buying the Wrong Vodka Still – Vodka Columns: Single vs Double

StillDragon explains why a **commercial vodka column** should have at least **18 plates** (up to ~30) to reliably reach the legal 95 % ABV. More plates give a wider operating range, reduce reflux time, and lower heat/cooling demands. The article cautions against using all‑in‑one or split columns for space savings because they often run slower and are harder to manage. For height constraints, a stripping still plus a smaller vessel can be more efficient.

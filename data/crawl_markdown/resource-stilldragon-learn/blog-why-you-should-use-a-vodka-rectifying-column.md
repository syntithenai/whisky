# Why You Should Use a Vodka Rectifying Column

StillDragon explains that a vodka rectifying column is essentially a tall forced‑reflux column with **10 or more plates** (often up to 30). The extra plates allow the vapor to be repeatedly scrubbed, removing harsh components and producing a cleaner, more precise spirit. A stripping run before the rectification step saves time and money.

The article stresses that the term *rectifying* simply means “cleaning” and encourages readers to contact StillDragon for further questions.

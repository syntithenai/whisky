# Choosing the Right Bubble Cap for Distilling

This StillDragon blog post explains how bubble plates and bubble caps work in a distillation column, why copper is important, and compares plate‑based columns to pot stills. It introduces the ProCap (a combined bubble cap/downcomer) and the DoubleDragon kettle that merges both designs.

- **Bubble Cap design**: tube with windows + mushroom top; pushes vapor back onto plates creating reflux.
- **Reflux cycle**: each plate acts as a separate distillation step, useful for high‑purity spirits like vodka or gin.
- **Copper benefits**: removes impurities and enhances flavor in lower‑purity spirits such as whiskey or rum.
- **Pot still vs. column**: pot stills give more flavor but are costlier; the DoubleDragon offers both in one unit.

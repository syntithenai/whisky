# How to Assemble a CrystalDragon

The article explains step‑by‑step how to assemble StillDragon’s CrystalDragon column, a glass‑column still that showcases copper plates for educational and demonstration purposes.

1. **Unpack & Inspect** – Check all parts for damage, especially glass edges.
2. **Sort Components** – Group plates with plates, gaskets with gaskets.
3. **Assemble Plates** – Slide each populated plate into the gasket’s slit.
4. **Attach Starter Collar (optional)** – Use a standard gasket and clamp on the kettle.
5. **Mount DragonFlange & First Plate** – Fit snugly; flange level with gasket top.
6. **Add Rods Early or Late** – Early placement eases alignment but can be cumbersome.
7. **Insert Glass Pieces** – Fit into trenches, then add next plate/gasket pair.
8. **Finish Column** – Top gasket and flange, tighten rods in a star pattern; avoid overtightening.
9. **Final Checks** – Ensure no bulging gaskets or misaligned glass.

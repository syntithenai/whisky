# How Big Is a Distillery?

This article explains how to determine the space requirements for a micro‑distillery. It covers:
- **Ceiling height** – choose a building taller than your tallest column, adding extra clearance for forklifts and maintenance.
- **Square footage** – plan for growth, storage of fermentables, bottles, barrels, and tanks.
- **Kettle size & footprint** – larger kettles reduce cost per gallon but need more floor space; consider plumbing layout and access for maintenance.
- **Product discharge location** – important when adding secondary columns (e.g., gin) or extra stages.
- **Storage of hearts** – account for tote height and clearance.
- **Other equipment sizing** – evaluate height, length, infrastructure, and maintenance needs for fermenters, mash tun, etc.

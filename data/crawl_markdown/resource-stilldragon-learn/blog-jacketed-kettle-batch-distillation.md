# Jacketed Kettles

The article explains why jacketed kettles are essential for batch distillation and compares two heating options: **steam‑jacketed** and **electric Baine Marie‑style** kettles. It discusses pros/cons, cost considerations, and scalability for professional distillers.

- **Steam‑jacketed**: flexible placement, common in industry, high upfront & maintenance costs, potential downtime if the boiler fails.
- **Electric Baine Marie**: self‑contained steam generation, lower heat loss, good precision control, suitable when building has adequate power.

The author recommends planning heating solutions early to avoid future upgrades and downtime.

# How Do I Get a TTB License and Permit?

StillDragon’s guide explains the steps to obtain a **Distilled Spirits Permit (DSP)** or an **Alcohol Fuel Plant Permit** from the Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB). Key points:
- **Build first, apply later:** Complete construction and install all equipment before applying.
- **Location matters:** Facilities must be in a separate area that can be inspected; private homes or boats are not acceptable for DSPs.
- **Required documents:** Bond, corporate papers, lease/ownership proof, signing‑authority documentation, facility diagram, source‑of‑funding declaration.
- **Online application:** Create a TTB account, complete the New Applications Wizard, and submit personal questionnaires for all major stakeholders.
- **Processing time:** DSPs average 80+ days; monitor status for corrections.

Once approved you can legally distill alcohol. StillDragon offers equipment and consulting to help with the process.

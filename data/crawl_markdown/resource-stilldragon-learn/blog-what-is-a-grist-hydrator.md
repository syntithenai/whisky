# What is a Grist Hydrator?

A **grist hydrator** is a simple yet essential mashing accessory that pre‑hydrates grains before they enter the mash tun. By spraying water onto falling grain, it prevents doughballs, improves mash uniformity, and boosts overall efficiency.

## Key Points
- **Function:** Hydrates dry grains as they fall into the mash tun.
- **Benefits:** Reduces clumping, increases enzyme activity, saves chemical inputs.
- **Types:**
  - *Spray ring* – low maintenance, minimal fouling.
  - *Spray plate* – higher hydration but can clog.
  - *Static mixer* – best hydration, more complex and costly.
- Most StillDragon hydrators are spray rings and come standard on mash tuns.

# McRobert Distillery

## Key Facts
- Established in 2017, located in the Hunter Valley of New South Wales, Australia.
- Specializes in single malt whisky production using locally sourced grain and water.
- Focuses on small-batch distilling with a commitment to quality over quantity.

## Production Signals
- Limited release batches showcasing unique flavor profiles.
- Emphasis on traditional copper pot stills for distillation.
- Maturation primarily in American oak bourbon barrels, with some experimentation.

## Commercial Signals
- Available through select retailers and the official website.
- Growing popularity among Australian whisky enthusiasts.
- Collaborations with local wineries and food producers.

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited production capacity may lead to scarcity of certain releases.
- Maturation environment in Australia differs from traditional regions, affecting flavor development.
- Brand awareness outside of Australia remains low.

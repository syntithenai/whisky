# New Releases (November 2016)

- **Highland Park Fire Edition** – 15‑year maturation in 100 % refill Port‑wine‑seasoned casks, bottled at 45.2 % ABV; limited to 28 000 bottles, €215 each.
- **Bowmore Black 50 Year Old** – Final edition of the Black series, aged entirely in first‑fill Oloroso sherry casks (1964), 40.9 % ABV, 159 bottles, €300.
- **Teeling 24 Year Old** – 1991 vintage, bourbon‑cask maturation, small peated malt portion, 5 000 bottles, €300.
- **Teeling 33 Year Old** – 1983 vintage from two ex‑bourbon barrels, cask strength 42.9 % ABV, only 275 bottles, €3 500 with a hand‑bound booklet.

All releases are highlighted in the November 2016 WhiskyNotes news roundup.

# Spirits of the Sea – WhiskyNotes Review

WhiskyNotes reports on the **Spirits of the Sea** festival in Koksijde (30‑31 Oct 2010). The event showcases new releases from several distilleries, notably *Douglas Laing*, *The Nectar*, *La Maison du Whisky*, *Malts of Scotland*, *Dewar Rattray*, *Wemyss*, and *Blackadder*. Malts of Scotland announces three new expressions: **Port Charlotte 2002** (ex‑bourbon), **Caol Ila 1981** (ex‑bourbon) and **Ledaig 1998** (ex‑sherry).

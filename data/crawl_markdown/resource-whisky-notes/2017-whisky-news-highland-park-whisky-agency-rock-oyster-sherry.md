# Highland Park Rebranding & New Releases
- **Highland Park** will unveil a new logo and slightly updated bottle design on April 28th, 2017.
- All expressions keep the 43% ABV level. New nicknames emphasize the distillery’s Viking heritage:
  - *10‑Year‑Old* – “Viking Scars”
  - *12‑Year‑Old* – “Viking Honour”
  - *18‑Year‑Old* – “Viking Pride” (with batch number and rotation on the back)
- **The Whisky Agency** is preparing a new line for the upcoming Whisky Fair in Limburg, including:
  - Teaninich 43 yo 1973
  - Perfect Dram 2017, ex‑sherry butt
  - Tennessee bourbon 13 yo 2003
  - Rock Oyster Sherry Edition (blend of island whiskies with sherried warmth), bottled at 46.8%.
- The article is a news update posted on 15 April 2017 by Ruben.

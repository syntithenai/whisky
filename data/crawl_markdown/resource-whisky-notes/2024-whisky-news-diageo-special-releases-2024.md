# Diageo Special Releases 2024

- **Series name**: *Spirited Xchange Second Edition* – a continuation of the high‑strength, experimental cask theme.
- **Eight single‑malt releases** (8–14 yr old, except Benrinnes 21 yr):
  - Lagavulin 12 yr “Fireside Tales” (€195)
  - Talisker 8 yr “Tidal Churn” (€115)
  - Mortlach “Midnight Dusk” (€325)
  - Singleton of Glen Ord 14 yr “Autumn Walk” (€170)
  - Oban 10 yr “Coastal Orchard” (€130)
  - Roseisle 12 yr “Origami Kite II” (€165)
  - Caol Ila Unpeated 11 yr “Ambrosial Feast” (€120)
  - Benrinnes 21 yr “Grand Crescendo” (€515)
- **Cask details**: mix of first‑fill ex‑bourbon, refill casks, and wine‑cask finishes (ex‑Italian Ramandolo, Sangiovese, Oloroso sherry). All bottled at natural strength.
- **Availability**: most 2023 releases still in stock; 2024 bottles available from Malts.com. Some are offered in 20 cl sizes.

## Key Takeaways
- The series continues Diageo’s focus on high‑strength, experimental finishes.
- Benrinnes 21 yr is the only notably older expression.
- New additions: Benrinnes 21 yr and unpeated Caol Ila 11 yr.
- Prices are slightly higher than 2023 but still competitive for the category.

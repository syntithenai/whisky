# Spirits in the Sky 2016

- Belgian importer **The Nectar** hosted its tenth *Spirits in the Sky* fair.
- Highlights:
  - **Compass Box Whisky de Table** – chilled, light, barley‑driven aperitif.
  - **Compass Box 10th Anniversary bottling** – half Ardbeg (re‑charred American barrels) + half Compass Box casks (Clynelish, Dailuaine, Teaninich in new French oak hybrid). Rich peat, dark fruit, French oak; €115 for 660 bottles.
  - **Teeling releases** – 24 yr old (Sauternes & bourbon), single‑cask #6766 (sherry cask, crystal malt) with toffee/car­amel notes.
  - **Redbreast 25 yr Old** – chocolate nose, dustiness, incense, grapefruit, pineapple, mango, sherry; minimal oak.
  - **Diageo masterclass** – Dalwhinnie, Oban, Talisker; Brora 38 yr old & Port Ellen 16th release highlighted.
  - **Elements of Islay tastings** – Ardbeg Ar7 (sherried ~16 yr), Bowmore Bw6 (1999 casks).
  - Other notable bottles: Highland Park 24 yr, Ireland 26 yr, Linlithgow 1975, Glenfarclas “Mystery Speyside” 43 yr.
- Many of these will receive full reviews later.

---

**Key take‑away:** The fair showcased a mix of limited edition and anniversary bottlings across Scotch, Irish, and blended categories, with particular praise for the Compass Box Anniversary bottle and the new Redbreast 25 yr Old.

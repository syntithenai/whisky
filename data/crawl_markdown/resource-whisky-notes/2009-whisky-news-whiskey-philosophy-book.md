# Whiskey & Philosophy (book)

WhiskyNotes reviews a philosophical book titled *Whiskey and Philosophy: A small batch of spirited ideas* by Fritz Allhoff and Marcus P. Adams, published by Wiley for about €18. The book explores whiskey through philosophical lenses—idealism, uncertainty, morality—and includes essays from philosophers, academics, and whisky writers such as Ian Buxton and Charles MacLean.

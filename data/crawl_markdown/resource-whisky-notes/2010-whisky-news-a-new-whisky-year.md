# A New Whisky Year

WhiskyNotes author Ruben reflects on the 2009 whisky releases and highlights his personal favourites such as Brora 30yo 1976, BenRiach 39yo 1970 (PX finish), Mannochmore 18yo 1990, Glenugie 27yo 1982, Ardbeg 1998 single cask #1189, and Springbank 21yo. He notes the most visited brands on his blog were Ardbeg, Laphroaig and Highland Park, with Laphroaig Triple Wood topping the popularity chart.

The post also discusses industry practices like chill‑filtration, artificial colouring and minimum strength regulations, encouraging a move towards “honest” whiskies. Comments from readers touch on personal tasting preferences and the impact of Ruben’s reviews on sales.

# Ardbeg Kelpie – Committee Edition

- **Release**: Committee edition announced in August 2016, general release on 3 June 2017.
- **Cask**: Virgin European oak from the Black Sea (Republic of Adygea, Russia).
- **Proof**: Bottled at 51.7 % ABV for the committee; expected to be lower for retail.
- **Price**: Roughly €100 for the general release.
- **Tasting notes**: Oily peat, dark chocolate, smoked fish, herbal hints, hickory wood and seaweed (kelp). The name references a water‑horse myth.
- **Availability**: Committee bottles sold online from 16 March 2017; retail on 3 June 2017.

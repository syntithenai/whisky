# Compass Box Spice Tree Extravaganza & Related Releases

- **Compass Box Spice Tree Extravaganza** – a special edition celebrating the 10th anniversary of the original *Spice Tree* blend, which was banned by the SWA for using oak staves inside the cask. The new version uses older components and a large share of sherry‑cask aged malt whisky. It is bottled at **46 % ABV** with **12,240 bottles** available from 1 Oct, priced around **€130**.

- **Compass Box 3 Year Old Deluxe** – a playful blended malt featuring only **0.4 % of three‑year‑old Clynelish**, the rest older Clynelish and **9 % peaty Talisker**. The youngest component is disclosed, but the blend may contain some of the oldest 3‑year‑olds on the market. Bottled at 45 %, 28,000 bottles will be released in autumn for about **€230**.

- **Highland Park Fire Edition** – a follow‑up to the *Ice Edition*, expected to look similar but with an orange‑red gradient. It is **15 years old**, bottled at **45.2 % ABV**, and limited to 28,000 bottles.

- **Glen Moray Elgin Heritage Collection** – a rebranding of its core range: 12‑year‑old (ex‑bourbon), 15‑year‑old (ex‑bourbon + ex‑sherry, replacing the old 16‑yr), and 18‑year‑old (ex‑bourbon).

The article also references other news items such as *Highland Park Hobbister*, *Wolfburn Aurora*, and *Compass Box Enlightenment*.

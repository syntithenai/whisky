# Glenrothes 1988 / Glenrothes 1998

WhiskyNotes reports that Glenrothes has released two new expressions:
- **Glenrothes 1988** – a 20‑year‑old, last creation of Master Distiller John Ramsey.
- **Glenrothes 1998** – a 10‑year‑old, first release under successor Gordon Motion.

Both will debut in the Far East. The label descriptions are:
- *1988*: “Rich candied orange peel, fruit compote.”
- *1998*: “Lemon citrus, rich vanilla, cinnamon hints.”

Ramsey is retiring after 43 years; his favourite Glenrothes is the 1979 vintage.

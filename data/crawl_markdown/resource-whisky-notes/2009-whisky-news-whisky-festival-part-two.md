# Whisky festival – part two
A brief recap of the second day at the Gent whisky festival (Feb 17 2009). The author samples a handful of whiskies, notes tasting impressions, and mentions an interaction with Gunter from 100° Proof.

- **Long Red – Glen Ord 12y 1996**: young, fruity, grassy, citrus; cherries in finish.
- **Auchentoshan Three Wood (43 %)**: triple‑distilled, finished in bourbon, Oloroso and Pedro Ximénez sherry casks; caramel, floral, raisin notes.
- **Bunnahabhain 38y 1970 & Caperdonich 36y 1972**: no detailed tasting due to late hour.
- **Connemara cask (1992)** from 100° Proof: heavily peated, banana, tar, liquorice with vanilla.

The author thanks Gunter for the club‑bottling tastings and mentions plans to "rob" him.

# Malt Maniacs Awards 2009

WhiskyNotes lists the bottlings that received Gold Awards at the 2009 Malt Maniacs competition:
- **Glen Grant** 36‑year‑old (1972/2009) – 56.3 % (Duncan Taylor, *The Whisky Fair*)
- **Karuizawa** 1972/2008 – 65 % (cask #7290)
- **GlenDronach** 37‑year‑old (1972/2009) – 53.3 % (OB, *LMdW*, cask #705)
- **Bunnahabhain** 35‑year‑old (1974/2009) – 56.6 % (Adelphi)
- **Yoichi** 1991/2009 – 58 % (OB, *LMdW*, cask #129374)
- **Hakushu** 1989/2009 – 62 % (OB, *TWE*, cask #900 50021)
- **Macallan** 1970/2009 – 46 % (G&M Speymalt, *LMdW*, cask #8326)

The article notes that some of these releases have already been reviewed elsewhere on WhiskyNotes and promises further coverage in the coming days.

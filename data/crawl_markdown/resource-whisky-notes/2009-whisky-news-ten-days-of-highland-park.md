# Ten days of… Highland Park

WhiskyNotes reviews the Orkney‑based distillery Highland Park, noting its recent rebranding in 2006 and ambition to be a top‑10 single malt by 2011. The core range includes 12, 18, 25, 30 and 40‑year expressions, described as balanced with sweetness, floral heather notes, smoke, coastal character and occasional sherry hints.

The article is part of the WhiskyNews series (dated 10 May 2009) and links to other reviews such as Bruichladdich and Laphroaig.

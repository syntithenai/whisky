# Laphroaig Double Cask 20‑Year Review

- **Limited edition**: 750 individually numbered bottles.
- **Retailer**: French duty‑free shop Aelia (Paris‑Charles de Gaulle).
- **Price**: €225 per bottle.
- **Maturation**: Builds on the Laphroaig 18‑Year “Double Cask” – aged in bourbon casks, then finished in small quarter casks before bottling at 46.6% ABV.
- **Taste expectation**: Slightly sweeter and richer than the original 18‑Year.

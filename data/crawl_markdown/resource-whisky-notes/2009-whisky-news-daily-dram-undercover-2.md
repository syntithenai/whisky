# Daily Dram – Undercover

A review of three *Daily Dram* bottlings presented at the Gent whisky festival:

- **Undercover #1** – Lagavulin 14 y (1994, 53.1 %) – classic smoky Islay style, priced ~€100.
- **Undercover #2** – Talisker 24 y (1984, 51.9 %) – fruity with peppery finish, priced ~€150.
- **Undercover #3** – Ardbeg/Laphroaig/Bowmore 15 y vatting (57.5 %) – peat‑heavy, dry, acid notes, priced ~€75.

The author praises the quality but worries about high prices amid a financial crisis.

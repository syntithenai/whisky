# Daily Dram – Undercover

WhiskyNotes reviews three *Undercover* bottlings presented at the Gent whisky festival:

1. **Lagavulin 14 y (1994)** – 53.1 % ABV, 196 bottles. Classic smoky character with a farm‑like finish; priced ~€100.
2. **Talisker 24 y (1984)** – 51.9 % ABV, 82 bottles. Fruity and peppery, less smoke than usual Talisker releases; priced ~€150.
3. **Ardbeg/Laphroaig/Bowmore 15 y vatting** – 57.5 % ABV, 320 bottles. A blended‑malt with dominant Ardbeg peat and lemon acid notes; priced ~€75.

The author praises the quality but worries about high prices amid a financial crisis.

# La Maison du Whisky #fail

WhiskyNotes reports that **La Maison du Whisky (LMdW)** repeatedly mis‑communicates stock availability for the *Karuizawa 1981 cask 2634*. The author ordered on Friday, received confirmation, but by Saturday the item was already out of stock. A subsequent “new stock” email was sent to a customer who still could not purchase it. LMdW’s client‑support reply confirms the product is *out of stock* and offers replacement or refund.

The post questions how a specialist retailer can maintain accurate, real‑time inventory and whether LMdW prioritises certain customers over others.

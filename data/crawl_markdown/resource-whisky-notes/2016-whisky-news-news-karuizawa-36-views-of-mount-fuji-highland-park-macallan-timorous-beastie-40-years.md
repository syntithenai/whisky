# WhiskyNews Update – 27 Aug 2016

- **Karuizawa Cask Strength**: Five new expressions from the *Thirty‑six Views of Mount Fuji* series, drawn from 1999/2000 casks, bottled at cask strength and priced €1,150 each.
- **Highland Park Keystone Collection**: Announcement of four upcoming releases and two replacements for the regular 15 Year‑Old and Dark Origins.
- **The Macallan 40 Year‑Old (2016)**: Limited to 500 bottles, aged exclusively in sherry oak casks, bottled at 45 % ABV.
- **Timorous Beastie 40 Years**: 1,080 bottles available; a competition offers a unique gold bottle as prize.
- **Bruichladdich Octomore & Laddie Ten**: New releases – Octomore 10 Year‑Old (167 ppm) and Laddie Ten Second Edition (50 % ABV).
- **Bruichladdich Black Art 1992**: 24‑year‑old, bottled in 2016 at 48.4 %.
- **Bowmore 10 Year‑Old (travel retail)**: Matured in Oloroso sherry casks and wine barriques, 40 % ABV, €45 retail price, available only in Europe/Asia Pacific.

All releases are highlighted as limited editions or special collections with specific pricing and availability details.

# New Whisky Releases (Sept‑Oct 2010)

- **Whisky Agency** launches an *Anatomy* series featuring Leonardo sketches on the labels:
  - *Inchgower* – 36 yo, 1974 ex‑sherry (citrusy, little sherry)
  - *Glenlossie* – 35 yo, 1975 ex‑bourbon
  - *Lochside* – 29 yo, 1981 refill hogshead (grainy nose, grapefruit‑heavy citrus)
  - *Bowmore* – 15 yo, 1995 ex‑sherry
- **Perfect Dram VI** adds several joint‑bottled expressions:
  - *Linkwood* – 36 yo, 1973 ex‑bourbon
  - *“A Speyside”* – 41 yo, 1969 ex‑bourbon (likely Glenfarclas)
  - *Glen Moray* – 36 yo, 1973 ex‑bourbon
  - *Glen Elgin* – 35 yo, 1975 ex‑bourbon
- **Daily Dram** and **La Maison du Whisky (LMdW)** share casks:
  - *Linkwood* – 36 yo, 1973 ex‑bourbon
  - *Banff* – 34 yo, 1975
  - *Clynelish* – 27 yo, 1982 (sharp, mineral; less rounded than usual)
- The page notes that Bowmore is bottled at 46 % by Daily Dram but at cask strength by Whisky Agency.
- Overall, the author highlights a wealth of premium releases with varied labels and bottle options.

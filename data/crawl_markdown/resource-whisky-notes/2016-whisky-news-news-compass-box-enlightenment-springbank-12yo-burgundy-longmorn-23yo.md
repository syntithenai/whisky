# WhiskyNews – 30 April 2016

- **Compass Box Enlightenment**: blended malt (Clynelish, Glentauchers, Balblair, Mortlach). 46 % ABV, 5 922 bottles in Europe from 18 May 2016 and 1 June 2016 in the US. SRP £60 / $85 / €79.
- **Springbank 12 yo Burgundy‑wood**: 53.5 % ABV, expected berry & maple‑syrup notes.
- **Longmorn 23 yo**: 48 % ABV.
- **Longrown 18 yo (2016 Edition)**: 60 % rum casks + 40 % refill sherry casks, 46 % ABV.
- **Tomatin Cù Bòcan 1988 Cask Strength**: 28‑year‑old in sherry casks, 51.5 % ABV, £200 for 2 200 bottles.
- **Douglas Laing The Epicurian**: Lowlands blended malt.
- **Speymalt Macallan 1974**: 43 % ABV by Gordon & MacPhail.
- **Wemyss Malts Spring batch**: several single‑cask releases (e.g., Bowmore 18 yo, Caol Ila 35 yo).

The article lists these releases and notes that the age of component whiskies in Enlightenment is legally undisclosed.

# Bruichladdich PC8 (and more)

The article announces the 2009 line‑up from Bruichladdich, highlighting a multi‑vintage sherry‑bottled release, the mature X4, a new Octomore with higher peat (140 ppm), the Port Charlotte PC8 in black tin, a new XVII and Infinity III, and a new Renegade rum series.

> *“We can expect a multi‑vintage sherry bottling, the mature X4 (which can now be called true whisky), a new Octomore with even more peat than the current release (140ppm), the new Port Charlotte PC8 (in a black tin again), a new XVII, Infinity III and a new Renegade series of rum.”*

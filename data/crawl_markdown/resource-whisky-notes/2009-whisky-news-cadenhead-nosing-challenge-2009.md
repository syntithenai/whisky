# Cadenhead Nosing Challenge 2009

Cadenhead’s, Scotland’s oldest independent bottler, launched its annual Single Malt Nosing Challenge in March 2009. For €15 participants received a four‑sample pack of undisclosed whiskies and were asked to identify region, distillery, age and wood type for each bottle. The author joined the contest, tasted the samples, made guesses (two recognisable, two uncertain) and noted that all were good quality. Winners would be announced on 7 September.

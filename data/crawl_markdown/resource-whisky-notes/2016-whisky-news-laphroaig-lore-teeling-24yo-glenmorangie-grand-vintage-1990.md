# WhiskyNews – 5 March 2016

- **New bottlings highlighted**: Laphroaig Lore (NAS), Teeling 24 Year‑Old (Irish), Glenmorangie Grand Vintage 1990, BenRiach Cask Strength, Balvenie Tun 1509 Batch 3.
- **Laphroaig Lore**: NAS release, 7–21 yr range, finished in first‑fill Bourbon barrels, quarter casks and Oloroso sherry hogsheads; described as *richest of the rich*; price ~€110.
- **Teeling 24 Year‑Old**: Limited edition rum‑cask version (Rum cask #10687), 47.6 % ABV, 195 bottles, €270.
- **Glenmorangie Grand Vintage 1990**: 43 % ABV, part of Bond House collection, bottle type similar to Glenmorangie 25 Year‑Old.

> The article lists other upcoming releases (BenRiach Cask Strength, Balvenie Tun 1509) and a Jameson “Whiskey Makers” trilogy.

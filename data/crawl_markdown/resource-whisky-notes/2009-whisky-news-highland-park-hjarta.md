# Highland Park Hjärtä
Highland Park released a limited‑run 12‑year‑old cask‑strength bottling called **Hjärtä** (heart in Nordic languages). It is bottled at 58.1 % ABV, limited to 3,924 bottles and priced at £65. The distillery notes describe a nose of spice, heather, caramelised pineapple and lemon peel with subtle smoke; the palate offers silky vanilla, coconut hints and peat smokiness, finishing long and gentle.

The article also mentions the forthcoming Highland Park 50‑year‑old vintages (1968 and 1964) and notes that the distillery’s online shop does not ship outside the UK.

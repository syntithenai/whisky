# Daily dram: Philo Raga & Oat Mint

- **New bottlings** announced on the Daily Dram website.
- *Laphroaig “Philo Raga”* – 11‑year‑old, 1998 vintage, 57.5% ABV.
- *Tomatin “Oat Mint”* – 43‑year‑old, 1965 vintage, 48.1% ABV.
- The Laphroaig is suggested as a replacement for the discontinued **Hag Rap Oil** and **Aloha Grip**.

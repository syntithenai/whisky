# Furneaux Distillery Journal
- **Island‑based whisky**: blends Flinders Island peat with Scottish peat for a warm, roasted profile.
- **Founders’ journey**: crossed Bass Strait to establish the distillery on Flinders Island.
- **Local production**: farms barley, floor‑maltises it, and uses unique casks (e.g., Muscat from Campbells Wines).
- **Special releases**: 2025 Tasmanian Whisky Week limited edition – a peated botrytis cask whisky.
- **Community engagement**: offers an island dispatch newsletter for early access to new releases.

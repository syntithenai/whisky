# Furneaux Distillery Journal

The journal page showcases the distillery’s story and ongoing projects:
- **Founders’ journey** across Bass Strait to establish a whisky house on Flinders Island.
- **Blending strategy**: combining Flinders‑Island peat with Scottish peat for an earthy, roasted profile.
- **Barley cultivation**: farming their own barley, floor‑malting the second generation crop on site.
- **Cask exploration**: use of vintage casks and coastal cooperage, including Muscat casks from Campbells Wines.
- **Special releases**: a limited Flinders Island Peated Botrytis Cask for Tasmanian Whisky Week 2025.
- **Community engagement**: invitation to sign up for the Island Dispatch newsletter.

The page also contains standard site navigation, legal warnings and contact links.

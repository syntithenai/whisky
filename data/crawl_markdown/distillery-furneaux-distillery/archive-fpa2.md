# FPA2 – Furneaux Distillery

- **Style**: Peated ex‑Apera cask spirit/malt, 100 % Joe White pilsner malt, peated on Flinders Island with local coastal peat.
- **Maturation**: Three‑quarter casks (McWilliams and Seppeltsfield Apera) – just over four years.
- **Release date**: 8 September 2025.
- **ABV**: 49 %.
- **Bottles**: 506.
- **Distiller/Blender**: Tom Ambroz (distiller), Tom Ambroz & Haydn Moon (blender/finisher).
- **Background**: First casks filled on Flinders Island in September 2019; oldest cask April 2020 during Tasmania’s lockdown.
- **Tasting note**: Smoky, Apera‑cask character.

> *“Many years later the whisky has developed into a lovely expression of smoky, Apera cask whisky.”*

## Key Facts
- First copper pot stills operated in September 2019.
- Casks filled shortly after still operation began; oldest cask April 2020.
- Production occurred during Tasmania’s statewide lockdown, adding isolation to the process.
- The whisky is described as smoky with Apera‑cask influence.

---

## Additional Context
- The distillery operates on Flinders Island and offers a subscription service called *Island Dispatch* for priority access to releases.

# Smoky Wedding Single Malt (SWDC1)

- **Distillery**: Furneaux Distillery, Australia
- **Release date**: 30 Apr 2024
- **ABV**: 58% (distiller’s cut, unfiltered)
- **Maturation**: 50 L American Oak Ex‑Bourbon casks from Heaven Hill, >3 years
- **Malt blend**: 50 % Joe White pilsner malt (peated on Flinders Island) + 50 % Bairds heavily peated Scottish malt
- **Key notes**: briney, smoky biscuit‑sweetness, rich buttery profile, oily finish
- **Special feature**: unfiltered; higher ABV solubilises fats and oils

> *“The result? The best things about Smoky Wedding – its briney, smoky biscuitty sweetness (almost) unadulterated by the addition of water.”*

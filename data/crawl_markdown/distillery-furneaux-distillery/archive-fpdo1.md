# FPDO1 – Furneaux Distillery

- **Style**: Peated double‑oak, 100 % Tasmanian pilsner malt cold‑smoked with Flinders Island peat.
- **Maturation**: Two 120 L vintage Seppeltsfield Apera (sherry) casks over 50 years old; barrels ruptured after two years. Spirit was decanted into two 120 L Maker’s Mark Bourbon barrels, aged ~4 years.
- **ABV**: 48.2 %
- **Release date**: 9 Oct 2024
- **Key descriptors**: rich dried fruit, toffee nose, smoky, salty‑earthy finish.
- **Distiller/Blender**: Tom Ambroz (distiller), Tom Ambroz, Ian Reed & Ben Ryan (blender/finisher).
- **Bottle size**: 500 ml

> *“This spirit went into barrels in July 2020… the timber was SO old that they actually ruptured during maturation.”*

## Source Excerpts
- Maturation 2 x 120l vintage Seppeltsfield Apera (sherry) casks.
- Those casks we had sourced were some amazing and rare vintage Seppeltsfield sherry barrels.
- The barrels were over 50 years old, and broke after two years maturation!
- So I decanted the spirit into what I felt would be a complementary barrel, an American Oak ex Makers Mark Bourbon barrel.
- So what we have here is a Flinders Island Peated Double Oak, ex bourbon and vintage sherry.
- Split staves meaning we had to decant the spirit into 2 x 120l Maker's Mark Bourbon barrels.

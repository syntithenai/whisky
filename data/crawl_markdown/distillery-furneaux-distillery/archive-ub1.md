# UB1 – Unpeated Bourbon‑Cask Single Malt
- **Distillery**: Furneaux Distillery (Tasmania)
- **Batch**: UB1, first unpeated new‑make distilled on the island after 2019 stills.
- **Malt**: 100 % Tasmanian Pilsner malt.
- **Cask**: 5 × 20 L American Oak ex‑Four Roses Bourbon casks (Tasmanian Cask Company).
- **ABV**: 43 %
- **Release date**: 04/08/2022
- **Flavor notes**: Light spirit, tobacco & leather nose; candy‑store palate with lolly‑banana and musk sticks; creamy, sweet finish.
- **Character**: Very moreish, sessionable.

> *“This one is a lovely light unpeated release…the first of its kind from us as an unpeated bourbon cask.”*

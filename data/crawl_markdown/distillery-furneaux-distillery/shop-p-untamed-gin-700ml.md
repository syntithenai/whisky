# Furneaux Distillery Untamed Gin

- **Price**: $85.00 for a 700 ml bottle.
- **Botanicals**: Juniper (pot‑distilled), native rosemary, kunzea, wild fennel, and traditional gin aromatics.
- **Distillation**: Combination of vapour distillation for brightness/spice and pot distillation for body.
- **Tasting notes**: Bright juniper backbone with citrus, herbal rosemary, honey‑like kunzea, sweet aniseed from fennel.
- **Serving suggestions**: Soda & grapefruit, Negroni, Gin Paloma.

The gin reflects the native flora of the Furneaux Islands and evolved from a classic London‑dry style to incorporate local botanicals.

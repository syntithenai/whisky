# Furneaux Distillery - Australia's Maritime Whisky

## Key Facts
- Located on Flinders Island, part of the Furneaux Group off Tasmania's east coast
- Only distillery in Australia producing maritime whisky and gin
- Uses locally sourced ingredients from their farmstead distillery
- Limited stock available for purchase

## Production Signals

- Focus on unique maritime flavors using local botanicals
- Experimental approach to maturation and aging

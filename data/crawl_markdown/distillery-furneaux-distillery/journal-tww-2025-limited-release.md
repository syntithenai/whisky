# TWW 2025 Limited Release

Furneaux Distillery released a single‑cask, 5‑year‑old whisky for Tasmanian Whisky Week (TWW) 2025. The spirit is 100 % Flinders Island peated grain distilled in Tasmania and finished in a **Botrytis‑aged cask** sourced from Transwood Cooperage in Perth. Bottled at 58 % ABV, the unfiltered dram showcases sticky date‑pudding sweetness, dark treacle nose, toasted coconut, smoked cherry finish, and an earthy native smoke profile.

Only **101 bottles** were produced, making it a highly limited edition.

## Source Excerpts
- These were some of the first casks we got from Transwood Cooperage, an amazing family run cooperage in Perth, Tasmania.
- In this case, the Botrytis wine was matured in oak barrels for 8-10 years before the oak eventually made it's way us across the strait.
- We don't produce cask strength whisky much, but every now and then we come across some liquid in our tasting sessions which just taste best when it is rich, raw and unfiltered.
- That was been the case with this particular Flinders Island Peated Botrytis Cask .
- Run by Dave Schmeider who has a great eye for casks and actually cares where they come from, these were toasted, charred and sent to us in 2020, which for another first, this is a five year old whisky!
- The cask influence has given this whisky an amazing sticky date pudding, dark treacle nose with a soft spice and toasted coconut profile.

# Furneaux Distillery – XX 154‑155

- **Release**: Second whisky release (XX 154‑155) by Furneaux Distillery.
- **Style**: Unpeated, tawny cask spirit/malt style using pilsner malt.
- **Maturation**: 2 × 20 L French oak ex‑tawny barrels, finished in a shipping container on the high seas before maturing on Flinders Island.
- **ABV**: 45 %.
- **Release date**: 17 Sep 2019.
- **Key people**: Distillers Damien Newton‑Brown & Chris Condon; Blender/Finisher Tom Ambroz; Head Distiller (author of the note).
- **Bottle count**: 52 bottles.

This release showcases Tasmania’s maritime whisky style, blending smoothness with a unique shipping‑container maturation process.

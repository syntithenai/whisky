# XX 297‑301 – Unpeated Apera Cask Whisky
- **Release**: September 6, 2019
- **ABV**: 44.7 %
- **Barley**: 100 % Tasmanian
- **Maturation**: Five 20‑L ex‑Apera (Australian Sherry) casks
- **Distillers**: Damien Newton‑Brown & Chris Condon
- **Blender/Finisher**: Tom Ambroz
- **Bottle count**: 211
- **Tasting notes**: Bright, fruity nose; raisin, date, fig from the Apera cask; warm oak spice (cinnamon, clove, cardamom) on the palate; long sweet finish with a hint of Christmas pudding.

# Flinders Island Peated Single Malt Whisky
- **Price**: $220.00 for a 700 ml bottle.
- **Batch**: FPA2, matured almost 5 years in French Oak Apera casks on Flinders Island.
- **ABV**: 49%.
- **Production**: 100 % peated malt sourced from coastal saline herb‑lands; barley smoked in a hand‑built malt kiln.
- **Tasting notes**: Bold sweet spice, fig, date, fruitcake on the nose; bright cherry, nuttiness and licorice on the palate; oily aromatic smoke with gentle finish and sticky sweetness.
- **Availability**: 466 bottles released.

The whisky showcases a unique coastal peat profile distinct from UK or mainland Australian peats, featuring brine‑infused aroma and subtle sweetness.

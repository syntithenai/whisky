# FPB1 – First Flinders Island Peated Release

- **Spirit**: 100 % Flinders Island peated grain, malted in a mobile kiln.
- **Maturation**: Three 20‑L American oak ex‑bourbon casks (3 × 20 L).
- **ABV**: 48 %
- **Release date**: 16 Aug 2020
- **Distillers**: Damien Newton‑Brown & Chris Condon
- **Blender/Finisher**: Tom Ambroz
- **Tasting notes**: Vanilla and earthy dark sugar on the nose; bright, spicy palate with cinnamon and smoke; chewy finish.
- **Special feature**: Peat profile intensifies at lower ABV.

> *“This whisky is our whole reason for being.”*

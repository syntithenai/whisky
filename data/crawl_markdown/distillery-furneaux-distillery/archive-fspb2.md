# FSPB2 – Smoky Wedding Single Malt Peated Whisky

- **Release**: Feb 26 2024 (ABV 46.2%) from Furneaux Distillery, Flinders Island.
- **Mash bill**: 50 % Joe White pilsner malt peated on Flinders Island (coastal peat) + 50 % Bairds heavily peated Scottish malt.
- **Maturation**: 3 + years in 50‑L American Oak ex‑Bourbon casks from Heaven Hill, then decanted and incrementally diluted.
- **Distiller/Blender**: Tom Ambroz (co‑founder).
- **Unique notes**: Coastal peat gives floral sweetness; Scottish peat adds iodine/cresol intensity. Post‑malting peating yields a softer smoke profile.

> *“This new make was put into our only run of 501 casks… giving us a beautiful floral sweetness.”* – Tom Ambroz

# Furneaux Distillery
Furneaux Distillery is a maritime whisky producer located on Flinders Island in the Furneaux Group, north‑east of Tasmania. The distillery produces single malt whiskies (peated and unpeated) and an untamed gin, all distilled, matured and bottled on the island.

## Key facts
- **Location**: Flinders Island, Bass Strait
- **Products**: Smoky Wedding Single Malt Peated, Flinders Island Single Malt Peated, Sawyers Bay Single Malt Unpeated, Untamed Gin

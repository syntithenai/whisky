# Furneaux Distillery

Australia's maritime whisky distilled, matured, and bottled on Flinders Island.

## Key Facts
- Located on Flinders Island in the Furneaux Group, northeast of Tasmania
- Exposed to harsh elements of Bass Strait, creating rugged, raw beauty
- Maritime spirits with distinct smoky and peated profiles

## Production Signals
- Single malt whisky made from locally sourced barley
- Peated and unpeated expressions available
- Small-batch production focusing on quality over quantity

## Commercial Signals
- Limited availability due to remote location and small-scale production
- Unique maritime character appealing to adventurous whisky drinkers
- Growing interest in Australian spirits market

## Risks/Unknowns
- Dependence on weather conditions affecting harvest and distillation
- Limited distribution channels due to island's isolation
- Potential challenges in scaling up production while maintaining quality

# Furneaux Distillery – Australia's Maritime Whisky

Furneaux Distillery is located on Flinders Island in the Furneaux Group, north‑east of Tasmania. The distillery produces a range of single‑malt whiskies and gin, all distilled, matured and bottled on the island. Notable products include:
- **Flinders Island Peated Single Malt** (Apera cask, 49 % ABV)
- **Sawyers Bay Unpeated Single Malt**
- **Smoky Wedding Peated Bourbon‑Cask Single Malt**
- **Untamed Gin**

The island’s isolation and exposure to the Bass Strait give its spirits a rugged, raw character.

# Furneaux Distillery – Australia’s Maritime Whisky

Furneaux Distillery is a small‑scale distillery located on Flinders Island in the Furneaux Group, north‑east of Tasmania. The site produces single‑malt whisky and gin, using local peat from Flinders Island for its peated expressions. Current releases include:
- **Flinders Island Peated Single Malt** – 49 % ABV, matured in Apéra casks.
- **Sawyers Bay Unpeated Second‑Fill Single Malt** – 49 % ABV.
- **Smoky Wedding Peated Bourbon Cask Single Malt** – 49 % ABV.

The distillery also offers a gin called *Untamed Gin* and sells its products online via the site’s shop. Visitors can book tours, sign up for the Island Dispatch newsletter, or explore the island’s maritime heritage.

> **Note:** The distillery is licensed under the Liquor Licensing Act 1990 (Licence No. 99520) and prohibits sales to anyone under 18.

- Location: Flinders Island, Furneaux Group, Tasmania, Australia.
- Products: Peated single malt whisky, unpeated single malt, bourbon‑cask peated whisky, gin.
- Current ABV for whiskies: 49 %.

- Online shop available; contact via email newsletter.

## Flavor Profile Highlights

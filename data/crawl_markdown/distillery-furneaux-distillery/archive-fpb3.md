# FPB3 – Flinders Peated Single Malt

- **Batch**: FPB3 (third batch of Flinders Peated Single Malt)
- **Malt**: 100 % Tasmanian Pilsner malt, peated on Flinders Island.
- **Cask**: 100‑l American oak ex‑bourbon cask, aged 3.5 years.
- **ABV**: 47.9 %
- **Release date**: 9 Oct 2023
- **Distiller/Finisher**: Tom Ambroz
- **Bottle count**: 180 single‑cask bottles
- **Tasting notes**: Fresh, bright nose (clove, lemon oil, grassy), zesty palate with light spice and acidity, complex bittersweet mid‑palate, lingering earthy‑woody‑smoky finish.

This flagship style showcases the island’s first peated spirit, matured in a bourbon cask to add spice and complexity.

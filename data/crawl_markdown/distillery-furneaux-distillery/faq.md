# Furneaux Distillery FAQ

- **Location** – Flinders Island, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Product focus** – Tasmanian single‑malt whisky (including a peated variety), gin and limited island spirits.
- **Production** – Grain grown, malted, distilled and aged on the island using copper pot stills and premium oak casks.
- **Sustainability** – Circular economy model: on‑farm grain, solar panels, rainwater catchment, by‑product recycling.
- **Visitor info** – Cellar door tours and tastings available by appointment; address 1 Sawyers Bay Rd, Emita, Tasmania 7255.
- **Shipping** – Domestic Australia only (international shipping in development).

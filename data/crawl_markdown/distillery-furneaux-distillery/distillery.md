# Furneaux Distillery
- **Philosophy**: Paddock‑to‑bottle, self‑sufficient, rainwater, local grain & peat.
- **Stills**: 1600 L wash + 700 L spirit, full copper bodies, shell‑tube condensers, electric heating, 57 kW solar array.
- **Water**: 100 % rain‑captured, closed‑loop condensers, minimal filtration (sediment & UV), salty/mineral profile from westerly spray.
- **Barley**: First crop 2023 – 10 t on coastal farm, roller‑milled malt.
- **Peat**: Coastal bog peat (saltgrass, tussocks, melaleuca) used for smoking grain.
- **Fermentation**: Classic single‑malt rules; fruity, floral, complex spirit.
- **Distillation**: Bright, fruity, tight cut, copper contact, reflux action.
- **Maturation**: Old oak casks, dunnage‑style bond store, open‑earth floor for humidity control.
- **Post‑maturation**: Dilution trials, slow‑proofing for texture and integration.
- **Shop & Visit**: Online shop, island dispatch sign‑up, visitor information.
- **Legal**: Liquor Licensing Act 1990 compliance, age restrictions.

## Source Excerpts
- MATURATION Quality of casks is paramount to us - we choose only the best available old oak for maturation.
- POST-MATURATION The job’s not over when the cask is empty.
- Long fermentation for fruity and soft spirit, slow distillation with lots of copper contact, slow proofing our mature whisky to allow the texture to develop and the flavour to integrate.
- We barely filter our water, simply sediment and UV to maintain that pristine flavour.
- FERMENTATION We adhere to classic single malt rules - only water, malt and yeast are allowed.
- Our bond store is dunnage-style, with the floor area the casks rest on being open-earth.

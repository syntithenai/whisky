# Furneaux Distillery - Australia's Maritime Whisky

## Key Facts
- Located on Flinders Island, Tasmania
- Embraces a “paddock to bottle” approach, growing grain and harvesting local peat
- Uses rainwater for distillation and maturation
- 100% of water comes from rain tanks with salinity and minerality from the coastal environment
- Distillery uses electrically heated stills powered by solar energy

## Production Signals
- Long fermentation for fruity and soft spirit
- Slow distillation with lots of copper contact
- Slow proofing mature whisky to allow texture development and flavor integration
- 57kW solar array on the distillery roof minimizing environmental impact

## Commercial Signals
- Emphasis on simplicity, patience, and authenticity in production
- Focus on creating spirits shaped by Flinders Island’s raw maritime environment
- Use of local coastal peat for peated expressions
- Rainwater harvesting for purity and flavor preservation

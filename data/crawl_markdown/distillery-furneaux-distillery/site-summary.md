## Long Description
# Furneaux Distillery – Australia’s Maritime Whisky

Furneaux Distillery is a small‑scale craft producer located on Flinders Island in the Furneaux Group, north‑east of Tasmania. The distillery uses locally sourced barley, peat from the island itself, and native botanicals to create a distinct maritime character in its spirits.

## Products
- **Flinders Island Peated Single Malt** – 49 % ABV, matured in Apéra casks; notes of cherry, licorice and smoke.
- **Sawyers Bay Unpeated Second‑Fill Single Malt** – 49 % ABV, unpeated expression.
- **Smoky Wedding Peated Bourbon Cask Single Malt** – 49 % ABV, peated whisky matured in bourbon casks.
- **Untamed Gin** – a gin produced on the island.
- **Limited‑Release Botrytis Cask (TWW 2025)** – combines island peat with botrytised cask influence.

## Production & Distillation
The distillery employs floor malting of barley grown locally, and uses both Apéra and bourbon casks for maturation. Peat is sourced from Flinders Island, giving the whiskies a maritime smoky profile that is balanced by notes of cherry and licorice.

## Visitor Experience
Tours can be booked online; visitors may also sign up for the Island Dispatch newsletter to receive early access to new releases. The distillery’s shop is available on‑line with age verification (18+).

## Licensing & Compliance
Licensed under the Liquor Licensing Act 1990 (Licence No. 99520). Sales are prohibited to anyone under 18.

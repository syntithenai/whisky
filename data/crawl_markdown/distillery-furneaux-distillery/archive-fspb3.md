# FSPB3 – Smoky Wedding Single Malt Peated Bourbon Cask

- **Style**: Single malt peated in bourbon casks.
- **Mash bill**: 50 % Joe White pilsner malt (Flinders Island peat) + 50 % Bairds heavily peated Scottish malt.
- **Maturation**: Two 100 L American oak ex‑bourbon casks – one Heaven Hill (5 yrs), one Maker’s Mark (4 yrs).
- **ABV**: 46.5 %
- **Release date**: May 21, 2025
- **Bottle count**: 371
- **Tasting notes**: Salty caramel & cacao nose; smoky‑candy front palate; chewy iodine sweetness from Scottish peat; soft earthy aromatic smoke finish.
- **Water source**: Rainwater collected on the roof; mineral and saline character.
- **Distillery location**: West coast of Flinders Island, Australia.

> This release exemplifies the blend of coastal peat with Scottish peat, reinforcing its place in Furneaux’s core range.

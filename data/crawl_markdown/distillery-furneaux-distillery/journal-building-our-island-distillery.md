# Building Our Island Distillery

Furneaux Distillery is constructing a new paddock‑to‑bottle facility on the west coast of Flinders Island, Bass Strait. The build includes an Ecotruss timber frame, roof, water tanks and a 20 hL brewhouse. Local builders are working under harsh winter conditions while logistics teams deliver equipment by sea. A 10‑acre barley plot has been prepared for the first malt crop, with fencing to protect against kangaroos. The distillery aims to open in summer.

# UT3 – Unpeated Tawny Port Cask Whisky
- **Batch**: UT3 (third unpeated release)
- **Malt**: 100 % Tasmanian Pilsner Malt, no peat
- **Cask**: 1 × 100 l American Oak ex‑tawny cask from McWilliams Wines (Tasmania)
- **Age**: 3.5 years in coastal dunnage warehouse
- **ABV**: 48 %
- **Release date**: 9 Oct 2023
- **Distiller/Blender**: Tom Ambroz
- **Bottle count**: 187
- **Tasting notes**: Cascara, spice, cream on the nose; bright cinnamon & cardamom, cherries, orange oil on palate; warm oily oak finish with a hint of saltiness.

> *“It’s been a little while since we’ve seen some fortified wine casks coming off the racks in the bond store. Finally we are getting a little taste of the cakey, raisin, sultana profile from an Australian Tawny cask, combined with the bright spice of American Oak matured for 3.5 years in our coastal dunnage warehouse.”*

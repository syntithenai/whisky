# FPDC1 – Furneaux Distillery

- **Product**: Tasmanian peated single‑malt whisky, 58 % ABV.
- **Maturation**: Single 120 L French oak ex‑Botrytis cask (cooperage by Transwood), aged 5 years.
- **Release**: Limited edition, 101 bottles, released on 4 August 2025 during Tasmanian Whisky Week.
- **Key personnel**: Distiller Tom Ambroz; Blender/Finisher Tom Ambroz and Haydn Moon.
- **Availability**: Sold via the Furneaux Distillery online shop (link provided in page).

# Furneaux Distillery – FPR2 Second‑Fill Sherry Finish

- **Spirit**: Flinders Island single malt peated, 100 % Tasmanian Pilsner Malt.
- **Maturation**: ~3 years in second‑fill 20 L casks + ~8 months finished in a 120 L Pedro Ximénez sherry cask.
- **ABV**: 47 %
- **Release date**: 5 June 2023
- **Key notes**: Bright orange oil & warm apricot on the nose; bright spice, maderia cake and vanilla cream on the palate; long finish with Flinders Island peat.
- **Distiller/Finisher**: Tom Ambroz (distiller) / Tom Ambroz & Ronan Sherry (blender/finisher).

> "Both have made damn tasty whiskies." – Tom Ambroz

## Source Excerpts
- We divided these casks into two different experiments; one was to have its full maturation in second fill (this was the previous batch FPR1), the other, this release, was then finished in a 100l ex sherry cask.
- This whisky sat in its second fill casks for just under 3 years, then we decanted them into a PX cask for a further 8 months, giving the whisky a little juicy note.

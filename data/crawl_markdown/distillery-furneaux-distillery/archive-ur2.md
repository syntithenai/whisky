- **Spirit**: 100 % unpeated, made from Joe White Tasmanian Pilsner Malt.
- **Cask**: 5 × 20 L second‑fill Flinders Island ex‑tawny casks (3 years).

## Source Excerpts
- Spirit/Malt Style 100% Unpeated Joe White Tasmanian Pilsner Malt Maturation/Cask Type 5 x 20l second fill Flinders Island unpeated ex Tawny casks.
- ​ From our conception, we have been filling different types of casks; some 20L for shorter-term maturation, all the way up to 450L puncheons for much longer term.
- ​ However, when we emptied them from their first fill to make the early batches of Furneaux single malt, we kept some and filled them up again for a second maturation.
- ​ There are so many benefits to this approach - one being that in a small format second fill cask, you can let the spirit mature for longer without concern of the whisky becoming too oak forward.
- ​ There is still plenty of flavour imparted from the oak, which is beautifully balanced with all that bright, beautiful sweetness and fruity ester profile.
- 3 year maturation Release Date 16/05/2024 ABV 49.1% Bottles 164 Tasting Notes Stewed red apples with clove on the nose, palate is beautifully structured with creamy sweetness, hints of cherry and vanilla.

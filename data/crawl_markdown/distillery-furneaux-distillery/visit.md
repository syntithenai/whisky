# Furneaux Farm – Visit

Furneaux Distillery’s farm is a 1.5 km beachfront pasture on Flinders Island where local barley grows and sheep graze under gum trees. The wind, sea spray and rainwater influence the whisky‑making process. Visitors can tour grain fields, see the production facility, taste single malts in a Ha Architecture‑designed tasting room, and view Mt Strzelecki from the deck. Barrels mature in a bond store exposed to the island’s coastal climate. The distillery peats its whisky on‑site, giving it an “antipodean Islay” character.

# Furneaux Distillery

Australia's premier maritime whisky distillery, located on the remote Furneaux Islands off the coast of Tasmania. Specializing in small-batch, handcrafted whiskies inspired by the island's unique maritime environment.

## Key Facts
- Founded: 2018
- Production: Limited to a few hundred bottles annually
- Distillation Method: Pot still and column still hybrid
- Maturation: Aged exclusively in reclaimed bourbon barrels from the US Midwest
- Bottling Strength: Typically bottled at cask strength (50-58% ABV)

## Production Signals
- **Maritime Influence**: Whiskies are exposed to salty sea air during maturation, imparting unique briny notes.
- **Island Terroir**: The volcanic soil and cool climate of the Furneaux Islands contribute distinct flavors.
- **Small Batch**: Each batch is carefully monitored and blended by hand for consistency.

## Commercial Signals
- Limited Release: Bottles are released in small quantities, creating high demand among collectors.
- Premium Pricing: Reflects the artisanal production process and limited availability.
- Strong Brand Identity: Emphasizes sustainability, craftsmanship, and connection to the sea.

## Risks / Unknowns
- **Limited Scalability**: Small-scale production may hinder growth potential in mainstream markets.
- **Regulatory Challenges**: Navigating Australian liquor regulations for export and import.
- **Consumer Education**: Introducing whisky enthusiasts to a new maritime-inspired flavor profile.

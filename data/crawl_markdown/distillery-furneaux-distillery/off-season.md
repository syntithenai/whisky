# Off Season Experience at Furneaux Distillery

- **Location**: Flinders Island, Bass Strait, Australia.
- **Season**: Winter (April‑July). Only available during the off‑season campaign.
- **Cost**: $100 per person (includes distillery tour and local snacks).
- **Highlights**:
  - Farm walk to see barley grown in salt‑laced air and peat soils.
  - Hands‑on tour of single‑malt production, including stills and fermentation.
  - Barrel tasting with young, mature, and special casks.
  - Tasting room with artisan cheeses, cured meats, and snacks.
- **Booking**: Bookable from 07.04.26 to 31.07.26 via the website; payment required at booking.
- **Terms**:
  - Flights, accommodation not included.
  - Refunds up to 7 days before booking; cancellations within 7 days are non‑refundable.
  - Weather or farm conditions may cause rescheduling or cancellation.

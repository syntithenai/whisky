# Off Season Experience at Furneaux Distillery

## What to Expect
- **Remote Island Immersion**: Discover whisky-making on a windswept Bass Strait farm surrounded by sea, weather, and wide-open skies.
- **Barley Growth & Peat Soils**: Explore how "place" influences flavour through salt-laced air, peat soils, and the coastline.
- **Hands-On Production Tour**: Up-close look at single malt whisky production in a remote distillery setting.
- **Exclusive Barrel Tasting**: Draw spirit from casks, including young, mature, and rare casks.
- **Winter Maturation Benefits**: Experience how winter maturation reveals depth and softness in the whisky.
- **Tasting Room & Snacks**: Warm up with artisan cheeses, cured meats, and other snacks paired with whisky.

## Key Facts
- **Location**: Flinders Island, Bass Strait, Australia
- **Experience Duration**: Full distillery experience with local snacks

# UDO2 – Double Oak Maritime Whisky

- **Distillery**: Furneaux Distillery (Tasmania, Australia)
- **Product**: SAWYERS BAY SINGLE MALT UNPEATED DOUBLE OAK
- **Spirit**: 100 % Tasmanian unpeated pilsner malt (Joe White)
- **Cask blend**: 100 l French oak ex‑Apera + 100 l American oak ex‑Heaven Hill Bourbon
- **ABV**: 46.6 %
- **Release date**: 26 April 2024
- **Maturation**: 3.5 years, post‑maturation blending of two quarter casks
- **Flavor notes**: strawberries‑and‑cream, confectionary, bright spice, caramel, charred sugar, juiciness
- **Recommended use**: digestif after a Sunday roast; tested on Flinders Island deck
- **Blender/Finisher**: Tom Ambroz

> *“I love blending cask styles… this blend was beautifully simple.”* – Tom Ambroz

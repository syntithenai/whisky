# Furneaux Distillery – Flinders Island

Furneaux Distillery is a single‑malt whisky producer located on Flinders Island, part of the Furneaux group in Bass Strait. The page describes the island’s remote character, natural scenery and how visitors can reach it by air or sea. It also highlights accommodation options around the island and invites people to visit the distillery or sign up for updates.

## Key points
- **Location**: Flinders Island, Bass Strait (Australia)
- **Transport**: Flights from Melbourne or Tasmania; barge service from Bridport
- **Accommodation**: Camping, cabin park, farm stays and ocean shacks
- **Visitor info**: Distillery tours available; newsletter sign‑up for releases
- **Legal notice**: Liquor licensing restrictions for under‑18s

# Smoky Wedding Peated Single Malt Whisky

- **Price**: $220 for a 700 ml bottle.
- **ABV**: 47.9 %.
- **Batch**: FSPB5, 207 bottles released.
- **Maturation**: American oak bourbon casks.
- **Flavor profile**: Maritime‑inspired saltiness and minerality from rainwater; peat smoke with iodine‑laced Hebridean character; bright vanilla sweetness and subtle spice from the cask; tropical fruit, melon, and oily peat finish.
- **Production note**: A blend of Scottish peated malt (half) and Flinders Island peated malt (half), creating a rounded palate that balances bold iodine/cresol smoke with maritime salinity and floral notes.

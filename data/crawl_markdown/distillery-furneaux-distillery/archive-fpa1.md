# Furneaux's First Flinders Peated Sherry Cask (FPA1)

- **Style**: Single malt peated, 100 % Flinders Peated Pilsner Malt.
- **Maturation**: Three 20‑L French oak Ex‑Apera casks.
- **Release**: 16 Aug 2020.
- **ABV**: 47.7 %.
- **Bottle count**: 78.
- **Flavor notes**: Oak, raisin, salted caramel, clove, toffee; light, delicate smoke that balances the fortified‑cask richness.
- **Production team**: Distillers Damien Newton‑Brown & Chris Condon; Blender/Finisher Tom Ambroz.

> *“This whisky is all oak, raisin and salted caramel. Amazing rich and dark profile of clove and toffee. Smoke profile is light and delicate, which balances nicely with the richness of a fortified cask.”*

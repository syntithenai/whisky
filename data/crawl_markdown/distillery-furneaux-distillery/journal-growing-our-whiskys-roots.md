# Growing Our Whisky's Roots

Furneaux Distillery shares its first attempt at growing barley on Flinders Island to achieve true paddock‑to‑bottle whisky. The remote island’s high humidity, sandy‑clay soils and sea spray are expected to influence the malt’s character. They sowed **Planet** barley late in the season with help from local farmer Aaron Burke, hoping for a solid crop despite unpredictable weather and freight delays.

The post highlights:
- The importance of terroir and self‑sufficiency.
- Collaboration with experienced island farmers.
- Challenges of shipping supplies across Bass Strait.
- Future plans to harvest, malt and distill locally.

# Suntory Yamazaki Story of Distillery 2025 Edition

A limited‑edition single malt from Yamazaki, released in 2025 as the second book in a series that chronicles the distillery’s history. The bottle is 70 cl, 43 % ABV, and comes with a special leaflet printed on Echizen washi paper that illustrates the original drying towers.

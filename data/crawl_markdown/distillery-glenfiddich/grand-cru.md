# Glenfiddich 23 Year‑Old Grand Cru

- **Type**: Single‑malt Scotch whisky (Grand Series)
- **ABV**: 40 %
- **Volume**: 75 cl
- **Maturation**: 23 years in American and European oak casks, finished in rare French cuvée casks.
- **Tasting notes**: Sweet brioche, sandalwood, pear sorbet; nose of apple blossom, freshly baked bread, candied lemon. Colour deep gold.
- **Flavor profile**: Rich vanilla oak, sweet brioche, sandalwood, pear sorbet, white grape; long, opulent and sweet finish.
- **Availability**: Global (worldwide) – sold in the UK, US, France, India, Argentina, Australia, etc.

This expression is part of Glenfiddich’s Grand Series and showcases luxury craftsmanship with a unique finishing cask.

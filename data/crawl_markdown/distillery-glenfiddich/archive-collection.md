# Glenfiddich Archive Collection 2024

- **Release**: 10 unique single‑cask releases, each cask natural, non‑chill filtered at cask strength.
- **Availability**: Limited to selected boutiques (UK boutique, Glenfiddich Distillery Boutique).
- **Key Casks**:
  - *1987 American Oak Hogshead* – ABV 50.9%, 120 bottles, £4,000 RRSP.
  - *1973 French‑polished cask* – ABV 42.9%, 83 bottles, £3,350 RRSP (UK only).
  - *1987 Cask* – ABV ???, 73 bottles, £3,700 RRSP (Boutique). 
- **Tasting notes**: deep wood workshop, fresh sawdust, stewed apple, sweet liquorice; bright lemon sherbet, honeysuckle, fruit‑garden aromas.

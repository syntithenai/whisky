# Glenfiddich – Single Malt Scotch Whisky

Glenfiddich is a world‑renowned single malt Scotch whisky brand owned by William Grant & Sons Ltd. The website showcases its extensive range (12‑ to 50‑year‑old expressions, flagship, limited edition, experimental series, and perpetual collections) and offers tools for finding the right whisky based on age, taste profile, and occasion. Visitors can explore the distillery, book tours, join the Glenfiddich Collective, and view collaborations such as the Aston Martin Formula One partnership.

The site also highlights that Glenfiddich has received more awards since 2000 than any other single‑malt Scotch in the International Wine & Spirit Competition and the International Spirits Challenge. Legal notices, privacy policy, and a global country selector are present for compliance.

# Glenfiddich Perpetual Collection Vat 03

- **Product**: Single‑malt Scotch whisky in a never‑emptied vat.
- **Maturation**: Over 15 years in American/virgin oak and European sherry casks.
- **Tasting notes**: Golden colour, sweet heather honey & vanilla fudge nose; silky smooth mouth with sherry oak, marzipan, cinnamon, ginger; rich, sweet finish.
- **Unique process**: Solera‑style vat – half the whisky is bottled and the vat refilled, adding continuous layers of flavour.
- **Availability**: Exclusively in Global Travel Retail.

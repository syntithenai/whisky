# Glenfiddich – A Global Whisky Experience

**Brand & Ownership**
- Owner: William Grant & Sons Ltd (registered SC131772).  
- Location: Dufftown, Banffshire, Scotland.  
- Recognised as the world’s most awarded single‑malt Scotch whisky.

## Product Range
Glenfiddich’s catalogue spans 12‑ to 50‑year‑old expressions and includes:
- **Flagship & Grand Series** – e.g., 23 Year Old Grand Cru, 29 Year Old Grand Yozakura.  
- **Perpetual Collection** – continuous‑refill Solera vats (Vat 03, Vat 04).  
- **Limited Editions & Collaborations** – Glenfiddich × Aston Martin (1976 Vintage), XS series finished in sherry casks.  
- **Archive Collection** – single‑cask releases such as the 1987 American Oak Hogshead.

## Distillery Experience
Visitors can book tours, join the Glenfiddich Collective, and explore the distillery’s heritage through guided visits.

## Online Shopping & Compliance
The site offers e‑commerce for select products (e.g., Grand Cru) and includes age verification, privacy policy, and a global country selector.

# Limited Edition Glenfiddich XS Whisky Collection

- **Creator**: Malt Master Brian Kinsman.
- **Finish**: Hand‑selected sherry casks – Oloroso, Moscatel and Pedro Ximénez.
- **Expressions**:
  - *21 Year Old* finished in Pedro Ximénez sherry casks – dried fruit, woody spice, muscovado sweetness.
  - *18 Year Old* finished in Moscatel sherry casks – velvety richness, caramelised fruit, treacle toffee, dried apricot.
  - *15 Year Old* finished in Oloroso sherry casks – green apple brightness, warming cinnamon spice, oak sweetness.
- **Availability**: Distillery shop, select whisky specialists and bars/restaurants across China; Distiller’s Library boutiques across Asia from November 2025.

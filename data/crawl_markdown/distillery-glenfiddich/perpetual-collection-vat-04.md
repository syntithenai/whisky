# Glenfiddich Perpetual Collection Vat 04

- **Product**: Single‑malt Scotch whisky, 18 years aged in Oloroso sherry and bourbon casks.
- **Process**: Solera VAT 4 – only half the whisky is bottled each cycle, the rest is refilled, creating continuous flavour evolution.
- **Tasting notes**: Rich bronze colour; nose of ripe orchard fruit, spiced apple, vanilla oak; palate warm, mellow, velvety with sweet oak and soft fruits; finish warming, rewarding.
- **Availability**: Exclusively sold in Global Travel Retail.
- **Distillery**: Glenfiddich (Dufftown, Scotland) – part of William Grant & Sons Ltd.

> *“A whisky that never sits still.”*

## Distillery facts

## Resource facts

- The vat never empties; each refill adds new flavour layers, creating an evolving product.

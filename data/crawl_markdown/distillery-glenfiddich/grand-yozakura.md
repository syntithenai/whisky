# 29 Year‑Old Grand Yozakura Limited Edition
- **ABV**: 45.1 %
- **Maturation**: 29 years in American oak, finished in rare Japanese ex‑Awamori casks.
- **Tasting notes**: toasted oak, creamy vanilla toffee, silky tannin; zesty ripe fruit and caramelised almonds on the nose; spicy lemon, green apple, earthy tones on the palate; long oak finish.
- **Packaging**: elegant box with sakura imagery, bespoke Hanko‑stamped stopper, cloth capsule containing a poem.
- **Distillery**: Glenfiddich (Dufftown, Scotland).
- **Special feature**: first single‑malt Scotch finished in Japanese Awamori casks.

> *“Awamori is much like whisky… the extra finish in ex‑Awamori Casks compliments the smooth Glenfiddich taste profile, elevating the finish and lifting the flavour to a new level.”* – Brian Kinsman, Malt Master.

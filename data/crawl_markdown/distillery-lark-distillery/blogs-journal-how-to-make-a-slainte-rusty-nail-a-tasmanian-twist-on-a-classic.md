# How to make a Slàinte Rusty Nail – A Tasmanian Twist

LARK Distillery shares a simple recipe for a classic Rusty Nail, using their **Symphony No.1 Blended Malt** and **Slàinte Whisky Liqueur**. The drink is shaken with bitters, strained over ice, and garnished with an orange peel.

- 45 ml Symphony No.1 Blended Malt
- 30 ml Slàinte Whisky Liqueur
- Dash of bitters
- Orange peel for garnish

The article also introduces **Niall**, the Groups Venue Manager, who showcases his cocktail expertise.

> *“All it takes is some of our Symphony No.1 Blended Malt and Slàinte Whisky Liqueur, shaken well and strained over a block of ice alongside an orange peel.”*

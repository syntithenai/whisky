# Awarded Australia's Best Blended Malt, Three Years Running

LARK Distillery’s **Symphony №1** has won the *World Whiskies Awards* category for Australia’s best blended malt for the third consecutive year. The award highlights the blend of American‑oak bourbon, sherry and port‑aged casks, creating a creamy, oily profile with tropical fruit notes (mango, pineapple) and a citrus finish.

The article also shares a cocktail recipe using Symphony №1 and lists product details such as ABV, price options, and purchase links. It includes commentary from Head Distiller Chris Thomson on the blend’s inspiration and production philosophy.

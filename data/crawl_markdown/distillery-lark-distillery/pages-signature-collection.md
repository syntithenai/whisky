# LARK Distillery Signature Collection

## Overview
- Australia's premier single malt whisky producer from Tasmania
- Founded in 1992, crafting premium whiskies using local barley, peat, and pristine water
- Emphasizes Tasmanian terroir and pioneering spirit in every bottle

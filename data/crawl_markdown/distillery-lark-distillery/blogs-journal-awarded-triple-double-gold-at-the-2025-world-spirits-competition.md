# Three Double Gold at the World Spirits Competition
LARK Distillery announced that three of its expressions earned **Double Gold** medals at the 2025 San Francisco World Spirits Competition, a rare achievement where every judge awards Gold.

- **Earth 01 – Moss Hopper** (96 points) – single‑cask, 368 bottles, earthy peat with floral and chocolate notes.
- **Tasmanian Peated** (96 points) – bold peat and florals, ideal neat or in cocktails.
- **Chinotto Citrus Cask** (95 points) – finished in chinotto‑citrus casks, delivering candied citrus, cola, buttery malt and orange custard with cinnamon.

Additionally, the **Dark Lark 2025** (92 points) and **Classic Cask** (92 points) received Gold medals. The post celebrates the team’s dedication and invites whisky lovers to enjoy these award‑winning drams.

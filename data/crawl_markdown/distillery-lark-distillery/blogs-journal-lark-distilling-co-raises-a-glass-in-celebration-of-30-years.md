# Lark Distillery celebrates 30 years
Lark Distilling Co., Tasmania’s first whisky distillery, marks three decades of production in 2022. The article traces the founder Bill Lark’s pioneering journey from a kitchen‑table still to a 500,000‑litre operation after acquiring Pontville Distillery. It highlights Lark’s accolades—including nominations for Worldwide Whisky Producer of the Year and multiple world‑level awards—and outlines future plans to innovate with new flavours, processes and collaborations.

## Key points
- First Tasmanian distiller’s licence (1992)
- 30 years of growth, challenges and community support
- Acquisition of Pontville Distillery (2021) → 500k‑l capacity
- Continued focus on innovation, collaboration and international presence

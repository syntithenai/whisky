# Spotlight on Fire Trail No.151

- **Product**: Single Malt from LARK’s new Signature Collection.
- **Maturation**: Rare Port and Sherry casks, finished in ex‑Bourbon & American Oak wine casks.
- **Flavor profile**: Baked pears, golden caramel, vibrant citrus, cedarwood; chocolate ganache, stewed mulberries, vanilla custard, apricot compote; dark cocoa‑kissed hazelnuts, velvety coconut cream, sultanas, bush honey.
- **Production notes**: Apple‑wood smoked water, bespoke cask seasoning (PX, Tawny, Muscadelle), peat mined locally, fermentation exposed to Tasmanian micro‑organisms. Spirit tasted up to 70 times.

## Key facts
- Aged in rare fortified wine casks from Seppeltsfield Wines.
- Finished in ex‑Bourbon and American Oak wine casks.
- Uses apple‑wood smoked water and local peat.
- Tasting performed 70 times during production.

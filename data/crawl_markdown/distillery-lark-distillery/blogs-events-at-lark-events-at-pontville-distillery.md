# Events at Pontville Distillery

Lark Distillery’s Pontville site, located 30 minutes north of Hobart in historic early‑1800s buildings, offers a full whisky experience: tours of the distillery and on‑site cooperage, tastings in a rustic colonial shearing shed, and private event hosting for up to 40 guests. The venue is open Wednesday–Sunday from 11 am and can accommodate corporate functions, weddings, or cocktail events with catering partnerships available.

- **Location**: 76 Shene Road, Pontville, Tasmania
- **Capacity**: 40 seated in the tasting room; large acreage for larger activations
- **Facilities**: No on‑site kitchen; external caterers recommended
- **Key features**: Australia’s oldest single‑malt whisky distillery, only Tasmanian distillery with an on‑site cooperage.

For event enquiries or private hire, contact Lark Distillery directly via the provided link.

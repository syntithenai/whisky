# LARK and Stormy – Super Simple, Super Delicious!

A quick recipe for a modern take on the classic Dark & Stormy using **Symphony No.1 blended malt whisky**, ginger beer and lime. The post also highlights Symphony No.1’s accolades (Australia’s Best Blended Malt 4‑year running) and gives a brief background on Head Distiller Chris Thomson.

---

## How to make it
- 45 ml Symphony No.1 blended malt whisky
- Add ginger beer, ice, then top with more ginger beer.

The page also includes product details (500 ml/100 ml bottles, 40.2 % ABV) and a short quote from Chris Thomson about blending philosophy.

# Chef Peter Gilmore’s Japanese‑Inspired Pairing for LARK Mizunara Rare

LARK Distillery’s *Mizunara Oak Rare Cask* is a tribute to craftsmanship, aged in 200‑year‑old Japanese Mizunara oak barrels. Ambassador **Peter Gilmore** created a slow‑cooked pork jowl dish with cauliflower cream, prunes and roasted pecans that mirrors the whisky’s honeyed, umami profile.

- **Ingredient highlights:** pork jowl (or belly), dried prunes, olive oil, Oloroso sherry, cauliflower, butter, pecans, crème fraîche. 
- **Method notes:** 6‑hour low‑temperature oven cook at 110 °C, followed by chilling and reheating; prunes simmered with sherry and vinegar; cauliflower pureed with butter and salt.
- **Presentation:** served in a bespoke ceramic bowl inspired by a 150‑year‑old Japanese bowl.

The article showcases how culinary techniques can echo whisky characteristics, offering readers a recipe to pair at home.

# Lark Crowned as the Nation’s Best Whisky
Lark Distillery announced that its **Christmas Cask Release III** won *Whisky of the Year* and *Distillery of the Year* at the 2021 Australian Whisky Awards. Head Distiller Chris Thomson was commended as Distiller of the Year, while founder Bill Lark received a Silver for Whisky Personality of the Year.

The award highlights Lark’s 30‑year legacy in Tasmania’s whisky industry and its innovative single‑malt production. The Christmas Cask Release is made from Tasmanian red‑wine casks previously used to hold fruit mince pie jus, giving the spirit nutmeg, cinnamon, vanilla custard, gingerbread, citrus peel and plum pudding notes.

> *Nose*: clove, nutmeg, cinnamon, dried citrus, stewed apples, vanilla custard. 
> *Palate*: gingerbread spice, Christmas plum pudding, citrus peel, sticky toffee sauce. 
> *Finish*: long, complex and inviting.

# Events at The Still – LARK Distillery

The Still is a versatile venue in Hobart’s historic Mercury Print room, open daily from midday to late. It hosts whisky tastings, cocktail events, and private functions for up to 110 guests (or 50 seated). A semi‑private Whisky Lounge inside can seat 40. The space includes a full kitchen and a canape menu that pairs with Tasmanian‑focused cocktails, wines, and whisky dinners.

Key details:
- Address: 30 Argyle Street, Hobart, Tasmania
- Capacity: 110 total (50 seated) or 40 in the Whisky Lounge
- Open: 7 days/week, midday–late
- Contact: enquiry link on site

The venue is ideal for intimate gatherings or larger celebrations.

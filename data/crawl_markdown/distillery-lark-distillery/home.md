# LARK Distillery – Australia’s No.1 Luxury Single Malt Whisky
LARK Distillery, based in Pontville, Tasmania, was founded in 1992 by Bill & Lyn Lark and is now led by Master Distiller Chris Thomson. The brand focuses on a house style that blends rich fortified depth with silky texture and applewood‑smoked water for an umami edge. Their flagship product, **Fire Trail No. 151**, is a single malt aged in rare Port and Sherry casks, 41.5 % ABV, priced at AUD 170 for 700 ml.

Key offerings include:
- **New Signature Collection** – bold, flavour‑forward Tasmanian whiskies.
- **Personalised Classic Cask Single Malt** – bespoke bottles for gifting.
- **Hobart Venues & Experiences** – distillery tours and tastings at Pontville and the Hobart Cellar Door.

The site also highlights awards, community initiatives, and a commitment to responsible drinking under Australian liquor law.

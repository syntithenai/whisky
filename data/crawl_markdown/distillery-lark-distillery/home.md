# LARK Distillery
LARK Distillery, based in Tasmania, is Australia’s leading luxury single‑malt whisky brand. Founded in 1992 by Bill & Lyn Lark and now led by Master Distiller Chris Thomson, the distillery produces a range of premium whiskies that showcase rich fortified depth, silky texture, and an applewood‑smoked water umami edge.

## Key Products
- **Fire Trail No. 151** – 41.5% ABV single malt aged in rare Port and Sherry casks; priced at AUD 170 for a 700 ml bottle.
- **Signature Collection** – A portfolio of bold, flavour‑forward releases that highlight Tasmanian terroir.
- **Personalised Classic Cask Single Malt** – Customisable bottles for gifting or corporate events.

## Experiences & Venues
- **Pontville Distillery Tour** – Guided visits to the iconic production site.
- **Hobart Cellar Door** – Whisky tastings and museum releases in a historic cellar setting.
- **The Still, LARK Whisky Bar** – On‑site bar for tasting and events.

## Brand Highlights
- Tasmanian climate shapes the whisky’s character.
- Emphasis on exploration, innovation, and pushing boundaries of single malt whisky.
- Strong community focus with a dedicated journal and investor relations page.

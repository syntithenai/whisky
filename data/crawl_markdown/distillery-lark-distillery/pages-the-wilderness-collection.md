# The Wilderness Collection - LARK Distillery

## Overview
- **The Wilderness Collection** celebrates the deep connection between nature and craftsmanship across three distinct chapters: Earth, Sea, and Sky.
- Each chapter contains a limited series of rare single casks that when grouped together tell a unique visual story.
- Capturing in liquid gold, a wildness most of the world has lost.

## Chapters
### **Sky Chapter**
- The impact of Tasmania's super climate, purest air, and cleanest rainwater elevates each unique single cask release.
- Rich marine environment surrounding Tasmania separates it from Antarctica.
- Early access to Earth Chapter Release 01/03 commences 22.11.2024.

### **Earth Chapter**
- Tribute to Tasmania's unique ecosystem, capturing the spirit of the land in every drop.
- Dense moss that blankets the ground, rare creatures and plants thriving in hidden pockets.
- Three unique single casks showcasing peat character from Central Highland Peat Marsh.

### **Sea Chapter**
- Captures the purity and boundless wonder of Tasmania's remote wilderness.
- From King Island purest rain to microclimate shaping whisky, each release a tribute to untouched skies and marine environment.
- Two unique single cask releases: The Angel’s Cask and Tasman Tide.

## Special Releases
- **The Moss Hopper** (Earth Chapter 1/3): Matured in Barossa Shiraz Single Cask #LD4825, showcases distinctive peat character from Central Highland Peat Marsh.
- **Horned Orchid** (Earth Chapter 3/3): Matured in Tawny Port cask LD4961, intertwines earthy intensity with sophisticated richness of Tawny Port.
- **King Billy Pine** (Earth Chapter 2/3): Evokes sensory journey through rugged landscapes, heralds unique Tasmanian peat and post-malt smoking process.

## Sustainability
- LARK is committed to preserving Tasmania’s unique environment for generations to come through sustainable practices like small-batch production and responsible peat extraction.

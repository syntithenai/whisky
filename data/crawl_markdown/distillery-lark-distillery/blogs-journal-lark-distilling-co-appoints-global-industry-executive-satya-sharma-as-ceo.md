# Lark Distilling Appoints Satya Sharma as CEO
Lark Distilling Co. announced that Mr Satya Sharma will become Chief Executive Officer effective **1 May 2023**. Sharma, formerly Regional Managing Director for South East Asia and Australasia at William Grant & Sons Ltd., brings a decade of experience in strategy, finance and commercial roles across Singapore, China and the UK. The appointment follows a global search and is part of the board’s succession plan.

The company highlighted recent milestones: being named #1 luxury Australian whisky in 2021, winning multiple awards at The Spirits Business Awards (including Bill Lark’s Lifetime Achievement), launching its largest limited‑edition product DARK LARK in June 2022, and celebrating its 30th anniversary with a gala at the newly acquired Pontville Distillery.

Laura McBain will remain on the board as a Non‑Executive Director after serving as interim CEO until Sharma takes over.

# Lark Distilling Co. launches Rare Cask PARA100

Lark Distilling Co. has released **Rare Cask: PARA100**, a single‑malt whisky finished in two 100‑year‑old Seppeltsfield Para Vintage Tawny casks. Only **450 bottles** are available. The spirit spent five years in ex‑Port, ex‑Sherry and ex‑Bourbon casks before finishing, resulting in a rich, sticky nose with treacle pudding, brandied custard and wood‑fired raisin bread notes.

> *“PARA100 is the world’s first-ever 100‑year‑old cask finish…the result is almost impossibly long and luxurious.”* – Bill Lark

The release marks a milestone for both Lark and the whisky industry, showcasing a delicate balancing act of finishing over four batches.

---

## Key facts
- **Product**: Rare Cask: PARA100 (single‑malt)
- **Casks used**: Two 100‑year‑old Seppeltsfield Para Vintage Tawny casks
- **Pre‑finish maturation**: ~5 years in ex‑Port, ex‑Sherry & ex‑Bourbon casks
- **Limited edition**: 450 bottles worldwide
- **Flavor profile**: Treacle pudding, brandied custard, wood‑fired raisin bread, sticky, rich
- **Release significance**: First 100‑year‑old cask finish globally
- **Quote**: Bill Lark on the delicate balancing act of finishing

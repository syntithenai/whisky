# Win the Ultimate Taste of Tasmania – LARK Distillery

LARK offers a high‑value travel and whisky experience for December/January buyers. Each 500 ml bottle purchase is a ticket to a three‑day itinerary that includes:
- Flights to Hobart, private jet transfers to Barnbougle Golf Course, and return flights.
- A blend‑your‑own LARK whisky session at the Cellar Door.
- Dinner with a distiller, overnight stays at The Tasman Hotel and Barnbougle.
- Optional spa treatment or round of golf at Australia’s #2 course.
The total value is estimated at **$5,300**. Participants must purchase during the promotion window to qualify.

## Key facts
- **Location:** Pontville, Tasmania (LARK Distillery & Cellar Door).
- **Product focus:** 500 ml LARK whisky bottles.
- **Promotion period:** December–January 2024.
- **Eligibility:** Must be 18+ and purchase a qualifying bottle.
- **Travel logistics:** Flights paid by LARK; private jet transfers arranged.

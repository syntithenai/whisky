# LARK Distillery – 2024 World Whiskies Awards

- **LARK Fresh IPA Cask**: Crowned *Australia’s Best Single Malt*.
- **Symphony No.1**: Won *Australia’s Best Blended Malt* for the third consecutive year.
- Both awards were part of a global competition with 1,600+ entries across 22 categories.
- The article highlights the creative collaboration with New Zealand’s Garage Project and the blend‑crafting philosophy of Head Distiller Chris Thomson.

## Cocktail Highlight
- **LARK Symphony Yuzu Fusion** – a simple recipe using Symphony No.1, lemon juice, yuzu soda, ice and a lemon wheel.

## Source Excerpts
- Our Head Distiller and Master Blender, Chris Thomson, has expertly woven the complexity of American oak bourbon with the balance of sherry and heavier depth of port to form a creamy, oily melody of flavours.
- Our single malt casks were seasoned with four of Garage Project’s distinctive IPAs, imparting vibrant flavours from our favourite brews.
- LARK Fresh IPA Cask was Crowned Australia's Best Single Malt Whisky.
- We added a little bit of sherry cask for sweetness, and fortified for depth.
- This year the World Whiskies Awards saw more than 1600 entries across 22 internationally recognised categories including Single Malts, Blended Malts, Bourbon, and Rye.
- The Judging Panel described LARK Fresh IPA Cask as: Aromas of clementine, raspberry sour beer, and sour cherry, leading to an oily palate with notes of chocolate and funky fruit juice.

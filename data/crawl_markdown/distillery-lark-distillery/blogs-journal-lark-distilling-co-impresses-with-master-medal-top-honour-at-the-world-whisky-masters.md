# LARK Distilling Co. wins Master Medal at World Whisky Masters
LARK Distilling Co. earned a top‑tier Master Medal for its **Chinotto Cask II Cask Strength** – the world’s first whisky‑x‑chinotto expression – alongside seven Gold and two Silver medals across other releases (Classic Cask, Evolve Whisky Bar Series, PARA 1992, Symphony No. 1, Dark Lark 2022, PARA 100 II Rare Cask, Classic Cask Double Tawny). The awards were presented in London by *The Spirits Business*.

- **Head Distiller Chris Thomson** praised the quality and innovation of Tasmanian whisky.
- Judges highlighted strong ABV integration, umami‑salty caramel nose on Classic Cask, and golden syrup‑oak‑earthy spice on Evolve Whisky Bar Series.
- The event underscored Australian whisky’s rise to world‑class status.

# Chef Peter Gilmore’s Father’s Day Recipes

LARK Distillery shares three recipes inspired by its single‑malt whiskies, featuring Tasmanian produce and the distillery’s peated whisky.

1. **Anchovy‑lemon butter** – a savory spread for steak using LARK Tasmanian peated whisky.
2. **Cheese & truffle toast** – toasted sourdough topped with multiple cheeses, walnuts, truffles and honey, paired with the Classic Cask single malt.
3. **Dark chocolate & blackberry tart** – cocoa‑flavoured pastry custard with blackberries, best served at room temperature.

All recipes include ingredient lists, step‑by‑step methods, and suggested whisky pairings.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Recipes showcase how LARK whiskies complement local Tasmanian ingredients.
- Chef Peter Gilmore is the brand ambassador and executive chef at Quay and Bennelong.
- Pairing suggestions: Classic Cask for toast; LARK Tasmanian peated whisky for butter; Classic Cask again for tart.

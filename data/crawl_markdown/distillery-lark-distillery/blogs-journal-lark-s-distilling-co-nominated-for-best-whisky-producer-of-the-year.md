# Lark Distilling Co. Nominated for Best Whisky Producer Of The Year

Lark Distilling Co., Tasmania’s only distillery shortlisted in the International Whiskey and Spirits Competition (IWSC) for *Worldwide Whisky Producer of the Year* in 2020, was honoured with a nomination that highlighted its consistent gold, silver and bronze medals since 2015.

The IWSC, often dubbed the “Olympics” of whisky, gathers experts from over 30 countries to blind‑taste hundreds of spirits. Scores above 90 are re‑assessed for accuracy. Lark’s Tasmanian whiskies have repeatedly earned top marks in this rigorous evaluation.

> *“When it comes to the best whisky in the world, experts agree Lark Whisky is in the top four.”*

The article also outlines the competition’s history and judging process, noting that Lark remains one of the few Tasmanian distilleries to consistently medal at IWSC events.

## Key Takeaways
- **IWSC nomination**: 2020 *Worldwide Whisky Producer of the Year* (only Tasmanian distillery shortlisted).
- **Consistent medals**: Gold, silver and bronze since 2015.
- **Judging process**: Blind tasting by international panel; scores >90 re‑assessed for accuracy.

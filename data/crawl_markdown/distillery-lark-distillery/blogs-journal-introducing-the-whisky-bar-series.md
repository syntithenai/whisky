# Introducing the Whisky Bar Series
Lark Distilling partners with Australia’s top whisky bars for a small‑batch series that showcases unique casks and the people behind them.
- **Cobbler Bar** – 241 bottles, opened 2013 by Martin Lange; Queensland’s first whisky bar. The chosen cask reflects its mature, spicy style.
- **Death & Taxes** – 210 bottles, a four‑person crew in Brisbane. They sought a young, unique cask that complements the bar’s cocktail focus.

The series highlights collaboration, craftsmanship and the distinct character of each barrel.

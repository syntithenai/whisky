# Fresh IPA Cask Release – Single Serve

LARK Distillery’s **Fresh IPA Cask** is a vibrant, tropical‑inflected single malt that can be enjoyed neat or with water/ice. The whisky offers notes of peaches, cream and tropical fruit, finished by a distinct malty backbone. A pineapple wedge is suggested to enhance the tropical profile.

The article promotes the product as an approachable, “endless summer” experience and highlights its availability in a 45 ml single‑serve format.

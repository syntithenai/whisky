# Irori Moments – Collaboration with Seiko Presage

- **What it is**: A cocktail created by LARK’s Still in Hobart, launched alongside the Seiko Presage Cocktail Time Irori Moments SRPK50 watch.
- **Inspiration**: Named after the Japanese *irori* hearth; the drink mirrors the warm, textured dial of the watch and the smoky character of Tasmanian Peated Single Malt Whisky.
- **Key ingredients**: 45 ml LARK Tasmanian Peated Single Malt, 30 ml vermouth, wasabi‑root infused grapeseed oil (15 g wasabi in 100 ml oil for 7 days), locally sourced plums.
- **Preparation**: Stir whisky and vermouth with ice, strain into a chilled coupe, top with a few drops of the wasabi oil.
- **Related highlights**: LARK’s Tasmanian Peated whisky is lightly peated via post‑malt smoking (2 t of malted barley + 100 kg peat), producing chocolate and roasted coffee notes. The single malt has earned Master Medals at the World Whisky Masters.

# LARK Distillery Wins Australian Whisky Awards

LARK Distillery celebrated a sweep at the Australian Whisky Awards (AWA), receiving **Distillery of the Year**, **Personality of the Year** for Bill Lark, and **Distiller of the Year** for Chris Thomson. The brand’s *Dark Lark* single‑malt was named **Single Malt of the Year**.

The post highlights community pride, thanks to over 40,000 votes, and quotes AWA founder Niko Devlin on the industry’s innovation.

---

## Key facts
- **Awards**: Distillery of the Year, Personality of the Year (Bill Lark), Distiller of the Year (Chris Thomson), Single Malt of the Year (*Dark Lark*)
- **Votes**: 40,000+ in Australia’s largest whisky democracy
- **Location**: Pontville, Tasmania
- **Website**: https://larkdistillery.com

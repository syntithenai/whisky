# Lark Opens Breathtaking Historic Pontville Location
Lark Distillery has opened a new whisky‑tasting experience at its historic Pontville site, Tasmania’s oldest single‑malt distillery. The venue sits in early‑1800s buildings and houses the island’s only working cooperage. Daily tours cost **$99** per person (bookings required). A bespoke blending session called *Fuse* is available by appointment for **$499** per person.

- **Location:** 30 min north of Hobart, Tasmania
- **Opening hours:** Wed‑Fri 10 am–4 pm; Sat‑Sun 10 am–5 pm
- **Key features:** on‑site cooperage, daily distillery tours, bespoke blending experience

For more details visit the Lark website or book a tour online.

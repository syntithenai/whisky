# Introducing our new Signature Collection
LARK Distillery launches a bold, flavour‑forward portfolio built around its signature house style: fortified depth, silky texture, sweetness and an umami edge from apple‑wood smoked water. The collection showcases three flagship expressions—**Fire Trail No. 151**, **Devil’s Storm No. 183**, and **Ruby Abyss No. 285**—each matured in rare wine casks (Port, Sherry, Tawny, Muscadelle) with bespoke seasoning and finished in ex‑bourbon or American oak. The brand highlights its Tasmanian roots: wild fermentation, local apple‑wood smoking, peat mining from the Central Highlands, and a numbering system that encodes key sensory attributes.

> *“Our team of Whisky Makers taste the spirit up to 70 times throughout the whisky‑making process.”*

The article invites readers to explore the collection, personalise bottles, and learn more about LARK’s island home, people, and production philosophy.

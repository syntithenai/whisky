# Events at LARK

LARK Distillery offers a range of event spaces across Tasmania:
- **Pontville Distillery** – historic buildings, on‑site cooperage, 100 cocktail capacity.
- **The Still (Hobart)** – former Mercury Print room, 110 cocktail capacity.
- **Lark Cellar Door (Hobart)** – former distillery bond store, 60 cocktail capacity.

Each venue provides tailored experiences featuring LARK Whisky and Forty Spotted Gin, with menus of Tasmanian produce. Contact the distillery for private hire inquiries.

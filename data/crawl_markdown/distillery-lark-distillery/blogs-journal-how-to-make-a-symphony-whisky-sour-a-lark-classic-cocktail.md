# How to make a Symphony Whisky Sour

A quick recipe for LARK’s classic cocktail using **Symphony №1** blended malt, fresh lemon juice, sugar syrup and a dash of Tasmanian Pinot Noir.

- **Ingredients**: 45 ml Symphony №1, 20 ml lemon juice, 15 ml sugar syrup, dash of Pinot Noir. Shake with ice, strain over fresh ice, top with wine.
- **Symphony №1** is a Tasmanian blended malt that won the Best Blended Malt at the World Whiskies Awards in 2022‑24 and 2024. It blends American oak bourbon, sherry and port casks for a creamy, oily profile.
- The article also highlights Head Distiller Chris Thomson’s philosophy of blending on the spot and his long‑term vision for LARK’s blended malt.

Enjoy the cocktail as a staff favourite or serve it at events.

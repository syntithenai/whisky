# LARK after Dark – A Ghostly Experience

LARK Distillery invites guests to a lantern‑lit night tour of its historic Pontville estate. The event blends whisky tasting with a ghost‑tour, featuring three samples per visitor and spooky stories from the convict era. It runs for one hour each Thursday in June (8th, 15th, 22nd, 29th) at 6:30 pm, costs **$69 per person**, and is strictly 18+.

## Key Details
- **Location:** Pontville Distillery, Tasmania
- **Dates & Times:** Thu 8/6, 15/6, 22/6, 29/6 – 6:30 pm
- **Duration:** 1 hour
- **Price:** $69 per person
- **Includes:** 3 whisky samples
- **Age Restriction:** 18+ only

For bookings, visit the LARK website.

# Bill Lark Honoured with Lifetime Achievement Award

- **Event**: Spirits Business Awards 2022, London.
- **Awards won**: *Lifetime Achievement* (Bill Lark), *Best New Marketing Campaign* (Dark Lark). 
- **Other recognitions**: Highly Commended for Blender of the Year (Chris Thomson) and Best Event/Outstanding Contribution to Spirits Tourism. 
- **Background**: Bill Lark founded LARK Distilling in Tasmania, revived local whisky production, secured the first Tasmanian distillery licence in 154 years, and was inducted into the World Whisky Awards Hall of Fame (2015). 
- **Legacy product highlighted**: Legacy HHF584 – a 20‑year‑aged single malt finished in French oak then First‑Fill Bourbon cask.

> *“I am immensely honoured to receive this award … I hope you will join me in raising a glass to our wonderful spirits industry around the world.”* – Bill Lark

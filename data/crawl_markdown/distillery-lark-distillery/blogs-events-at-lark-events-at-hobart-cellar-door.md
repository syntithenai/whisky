# Events at Hobart Cellar Door

The Lark Cellar Door on Davey Street, Hobart is a historic venue that now hosts whisky tastings and events. It offers the full range of Lark whiskies plus unique historical releases, with seating for up to 20 people or standing capacity for 60. The location is open daily from 12 pm.

## Venue Details
- **Address:** 14 Davey Street, Hobart, Tasmania
- **Capacity:** 60 standing / 20 seated
- **Opening Hours:** 7 days a week, 12 pm onward
- **Licences:** Distillery (80886), Cellar Door (71110624)

For event enquiries, visitors are directed to the contact form on the site.

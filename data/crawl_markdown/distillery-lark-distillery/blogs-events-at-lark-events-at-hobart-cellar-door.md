# Events at Hobart Cellar Door

Lark Distillery’s historic **Cellar Door** on Davey Street, Hobart, is a popular venue for whisky events and tastings. Open daily from noon, it can host up to 60 standing guests or a seated function of 20 people. The space showcases the full Lark whisky range along with unique historical releases, making it an ideal spot for both newcomers and seasoned enthusiasts.

- **Location:** 14 Davey Street, Hobart, Tasmania
- **Capacity:** 60 standing / 20 seated
- **Opening hours:** 12 pm – open daily
- **Contact:** Enquiries via the website link provided on the page.

The venue is part of Lark’s broader portfolio of experiences, including tours, tastings, and gin‑making workshops.

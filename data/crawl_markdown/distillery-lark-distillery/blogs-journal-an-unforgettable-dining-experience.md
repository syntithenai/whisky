# An Unforgettable Dining Experience

LARK Distillery hosted a four‑course dinner on May 3rd featuring chef **Jimi Anderson** of Season & Fire. Each course was paired with LARK Signature Collection whiskies – *Classic Cask*, *Classic Cask Strength*, *Tasmanian Peated* and *Rebellion*. The event highlighted Tasmanian cuisine, whisky artistry and offered limited‑edition bottles signed by founder Bill Lark and master blender Chris Thomson.

- **Venue**: LARK Side Dining Experience (Pontville)
- **Chef**: Jimi Anderson
- **Whiskies paired**: Classic Cask, Classic Cask Strength, Tasmanian Peated, Rebellion
- **Special offer**: Signed limited‑edition bottles available for purchase.

The evening celebrated the brand’s commitment to pushing whisky boundaries while showcasing local culinary talent.

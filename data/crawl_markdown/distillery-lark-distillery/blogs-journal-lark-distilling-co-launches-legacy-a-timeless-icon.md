# Lark Distilling Co. launches Legacy – A Timeless Icon

Lark Distilling Co. announces its first **Legacy** release, a pair of 19‑year‑aged single malts that represent the oldest spirits ever produced by the distillery.

- Two bottlings: **HHF582B** and **HHF585A**, both crafted while Bill Lark was Head Distiller.
- HHF582B offers aromas of Medjool dates, fig, green olive, gingerbread dough with coconut, cloves and toasted walnut finish.
- HHF585A is a single‑cask expression featuring salted caramel, liquorice, peppermint ice‑cream, dark chocolate, coffee, nougat and nutmeg.
- The release celebrates Bill Lark’s pioneering role in Tasmania’s craft spirit movement.

> *“Releasing this whisky symbolises the culmination of 19 years of wonderful whisky making in Tasmania… a unique chance to experience a whisky that is the bedrock of the entire industry we’ve built upon.”* – Chris Thomson

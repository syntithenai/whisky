# Year of the Fire Horse – LARK Distillery

LARK Distillery’s **2026 Fire Horse Limited Edition** is a single‑malt whisky crafted in Tasmania’s super‑climate and matured in first‑fill sherry and port casks, finished in Seppeltsfield Tawny barrels. The 500 ml bottle (48.8 % ABV) showcases aromas of nutmeg, pomegranate, smoke and praline, with a silky palate of dark chocolate, ripe stone fruits, warm sherry richness, dates and pecan, finishing smooth with brandy custard, poached pears and a hint of Tasmanian highland peat. The design by artist Chris Yee reflects themes of fire, wood, water and journey.

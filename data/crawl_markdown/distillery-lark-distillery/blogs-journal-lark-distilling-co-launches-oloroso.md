# Lark Distilling Co. launches Oloroso

Lark Distilling Co., Tasmania, has released an **Oloroso Cask Release** single‑malt whisky in time for Valentine’s Day. The spirit is finished in Australian Oloroso‑style casks sourced from Seppeltsfield and combines ex‑Sherry and ex‑peated Port single malts from the Coal Valley Distillery. It delivers a syrupy mouthfeel with buttery baked‑good notes, toasted pastries, subtle peat smoke and a classic Oloroso finish that melts into warm butter and browning bread aromas.

The press release highlights Head Distiller Chris Thomson’s selection of casks and positions the whisky as an ideal gift for February.

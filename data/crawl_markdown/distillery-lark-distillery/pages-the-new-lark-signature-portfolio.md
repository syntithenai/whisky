# The New LARK Signature Portfolio

## Key Facts
- Bold, flavour-forward Tasmanian whisky from another world
- Tasted up to 70 times throughout its journey
- Shaped by Tasmania's extreme temperature swings, clean air, pure water and local barley
- Unbroken cask legacy dating back to 1878
- Custom fortified seasoning with apple wood smoked water

## Production Signals
- Rich fortified depth offering layers of silky complexity
- Sweetness and New World fusion umami notes
- Deep sweetness balanced by unique use of apple wood smoked water
- Aged in rare Port and Sherry casks, finished in ex-Bourbon and American Oak wine casks
- Custom fortified seasoning program using Australian PX, Apera and Muscadelle

## Commercial Signals
- Vibrant citrus and baked fruit notes meeting depth of Port and Sherry casks
- Balanced by subtle savoury note of applewood smoked water
- Introducing Fire Trail No. 151 and Devil's Storm Single Malts
- Bold, flavour-forward expressions capturing essence of LARK house style

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited availability of rare fortified wine casks used in seasoning program
- Unique use of apple wood smoked water may be a differentiating factor
- Developing reputation for bold, flavour-forward Tasmanian whiskies

# Glenfarclas Family History
Glenfarclas has been owned and managed by the Grant family since 1865, spanning six generations. The distillery was purchased by cattle breeder **John Grant** for £511.19s.0d and later passed through his descendants—George, Elsie, John, George, and subsequent sons—through a series of partnerships, mergers (with Pattisons), and independent rebuilds. Key milestones include:
- 1865: Acquisition by John Grant
- 1889‑1890s: Formation of Glenfarclas‑Glenlivet with Pattisons; later dissolution
- 1921: George marries Jessie Stuart Scott; births of George Scott Grant & John Peter Grant
- 1950s‑60s: Production doubles, then declines; strategic stockpiling by George S. Grant
- 1973: Visitor Centre opens
- 1986: 150th anniversary of legal distilling
- 2002: John LS Grant becomes Chairman
The family remains committed to independence and traditional Speyside single‑malt production.

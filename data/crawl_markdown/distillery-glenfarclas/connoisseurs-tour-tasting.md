# Connoisseur's Tour & Tasting
- **Experience**: Guided tour of Glenfarclas distillery and warehouses followed by tasting of five selected expressions, including two rare single‑cask bottlings.
- **Duration**: 2–2.5 hours.
- **Schedule**: Fridays at 10:15 am or 1:30 pm (April–October).
- **Eligibility**: Over 18 years old.
- **Price**: £65 per person.
- **Booking**: Email VisitorCentre@glenfarclas.com.

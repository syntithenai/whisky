## Long Description
# Glenfarclas Distillery

**Location & Heritage**
- Ballindalloch, Banffshire, Scotland (foot of Ben Rinnes). 
- First licence in 1836; family ownership since 1865.
- Core ingredients: pure spring water, malted barley, yeast.
- Production style: double‑distilled in direct‑fired stills, matured exclusively in European oak sherry casks (often first‑fill Oloroso). 

## Product Range
- **Made to Age** single‑cask expressions from 10 to 40 years. Each bottle carries tasting notes by Brand Ambassador George S. Grant.
- **Family Casks** – a curated selection of vintage sherry casks, each with its own decade (1960s–2000s). 
- **Special Releases**: 70‑Year‑Old (262 bottles, natural cask strength 59.2 % ABV), 50‑Year‑Old (836 bottles, 50 % ABV), 2010 Family Cask (first‑fill Oloroso, 647 bottles). 
- **Travel‑Retail Exclusives**: 11‑year‑old, 16‑year‑old, 105 (all 46 % ABV) released September 2025.

## Visitor Experience
- **Classic Tour** – ~1.5 h guided walk through production and Ship’s Room; £12 adult, £6 youth (10–17). 
- **Five Decades Tour & Tasting** – 2‑2½ h tour with tasting of five Family Casks (one per decade); £150 per person.
- Safety: children only 10+; hazardous areas in production zone.

## Recent Milestones (2025)
- New dunnage warehouses began March 2025, bringing total to 48 by early 2026.
- Sponsorship of Cheltenham Festival’s Cross‑Country Steeplechase and participation in Spirit of Speyside week.
- Launch of the 70‑Year‑Old and travel‑retail exclusives.
- Charity auction: 58‑year‑old bottle donated to Distillers’ Charity, raising £65k+.
- Awards: Callum A. Fraser named Icons of Whisky Scotland Master Distiller 2026; 25‑Year‑Old won Gold at World Whiskies Awards 2026.

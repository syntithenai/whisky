# Classic Tour – Glenfarclas

Take a guided tour of the family‑owned Glenfarclas distillery, learn about malt whisky production and maturation, then relax in the Ship’s Room with a couple of drams. The tour lasts roughly **1½ hours** and costs **£12.00 for adults** (or £6.00 for 10‑17 year olds). Booking is required due to limited capacity and safety restrictions.

## Tour Details
- **Duration:** ~1.5 h
- **Cost:** Adult £12.00, Youth (10–17) £6.00
- **Availability:** Summer (Apr‑Sep): Mon‑Fri 10:30/12:00/14:00/15:30; Sat (Jul‑Sep) 10:30/12:00/13:15/14:30. Winter (Oct‑Mar): Mon‑Fri 10:30/12:00/13:15/14:30
- **Booking:** Email VisitorCentre@glenfarclas.com with preferred time, date and group size.
- **Safety:** Children aged 10+ only; hazardous areas in production zone.

After the tour you can enjoy drinks in the Ship’s Room and shop for Glenfarclas whisky gifts.

# Glenfarclas Distillery

## Key Facts
- Founded in 1696, making it one of the oldest distilleries in Scotland.
- Located in the Speyside region near the village of Fochabers.
- Produces a range of single malt whiskies under the Glenfarclas brand.

## Production Signals
- Known for its flagship bottling, *Glenfarclas 15*, which is matured for 15 years.
- Offers other expressions like *21* and *30*, aged in ex-bourbon casks.
- Uses a combination of pot stills and column stills in production.

## Commercial Signals
- One of the most widely distributed Speyside whiskies globally.
- Frequently featured in blends due to its balanced flavor profile.
- Strong presence at events like whisky festivals and tastings.

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited availability of older expressions compared to demand.
- Price fluctuations based on market conditions and collector interest.

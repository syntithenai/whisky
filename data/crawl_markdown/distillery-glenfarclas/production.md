# Glenfarclas Production
Glenfarclas, a family‑owned Highland distillery, has been crafting single‑malt Scotch for over 150 years. The whisky is distilled twice in traditional direct‑fired stills and matured exclusively in European oak casks that previously held Oloroso sherry. The team—led by Distillery Manager Callum A Fraser—maintains artisanal methods passed down through generations.

## Key Production Details
- **Location**: Ballindalloch, Banffshire, Scotland (foot of Ben Rinnes)
- **Water source**: Crystal‑clear spring water from granite‑fed heather slopes
- **Distillation**: Twice in direct‑fired stills
- **Maturation**: European oak sherry casks selected by John Grant
- **Team**: Small, experienced crew including mashmen, stillmen, and warehouse staff

## Historical Note
In 1912 a rival distiller praised Glenfarclas as “the king of malt” – a compliment the family still cherishes.

# Donna McIntosh Celebrates 25 Years’ Service at J&G Grant

In March 2017, Glenfarclas celebrated the 25‑year milestone of **Donna McIntosh**, who began as a part‑time guide in 1992 and rose to Visitor Centre Manageress in 1999. She managed tours, maintained the centre, and later handed over managerial duties in 2015 to spend more time with her grandchildren while continuing as a guide.

---

**Key facts**: 
- 25 years of service (since 1992)
- Manageress from 1999‑2015
- Continues as guide and occasional support for new manager
- Celebrated with cake, gifts, and possibly a dram

# Glenfarclas Whisky Profile

## Key Facts
- Family-owned distillery in Speyside, Scotland since 1697
- Produces a range of single malt whiskies including Classic Tour, Connoisseur's Tour & Tasting, Five Decades Tour & Tasting, and Rare Casks
- Distinctive cask finishes include Oloroso Sherry, PX Sherry, Madeira, and Bourbon
- Offers guided tours: Classic Tour, Connoisseur's Tour & Tasting, and Five Decades Tour & Tasting

## Production Signals
- Aged in ex-Bourbon and new American Oak casks for consistency
- Uses a mix of ex-Sherry Casks (Oloroso and PX) and Bourbon casks
- Offers Rare Cask expressions with unique cask finishes like Madeira and Sherry
- Emphasizes traditional distilling methods while expanding production capacity

## Commercial Signals
- Strong brand focus on heritage, tradition, and exclusivity
- Limited edition releases and rare cask offerings create demand
- Partnerships with J&G Grant for special events celebrate employee milestones
- Expansion plans include new warehouse construction to increase aging capacity

# Glenfarclas - Glenfarclas Single Malt Scotch Whisky

## Made to Age, We Produce a Wide Range of Expressions from the 10 Through to the 40 Year Old
Pure spring water, malted barley and yeast are all that go into Glenfarclas. Since 1865, Glenfarclas has been owned and managed by just one family, The Grants of Glenfarclas.

## Discover the Unique History of One Family's Distillery, and Time-Honoured Traditions Used to Make Glenfarclas Highland Single Malt Scotch Whisky
The Family Casks are a collection of the finest single casks with vintages from every year since 1954.

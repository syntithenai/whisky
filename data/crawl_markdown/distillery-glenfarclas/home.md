# Glenfarclas Distillery
- **Location**: Ballindalloch, Banffshire, Scotland.
- **Founded**: 1836 (first distillation), owned by the same family since 1865.
- **Core ingredients**: pure spring water, malted barley, yeast.
- **Product range**: single‑malt expressions aged from 10 to 40 years.
- **Special cask collection**: Family Casks – a selection of single casks with vintages dating back to 1954.

> *“Made to age”* is the guiding principle behind every Glenfarclas whisky.

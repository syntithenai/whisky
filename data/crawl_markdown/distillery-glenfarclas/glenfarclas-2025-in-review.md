# Glenfarclas 2025 in Review

- **New warehouse construction**: Six dunnage warehouses started March 2025, bringing total to 48 by early 2026.
- **Event sponsorships**: Continued support of the Cheltenham Festival’s Cross‑Country Steeplechase (17th year) and participation in Spirit of Speyside week with new Whisky & Golf Experience events.
- **Product launches**:
  - *Glenfarclas 70‑Year‑Old* (262 bottles, distilled 1953).
  - Travel‑retail exclusives: 11‑year‑old (1 L), 16‑year‑old (70 cl) and 105 (1 L) at 46% ABV.
  - *2010 Family Cask* (59.2% ABV, 647 bottles).

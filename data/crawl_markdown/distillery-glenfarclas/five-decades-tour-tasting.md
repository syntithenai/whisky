# Five Decades Tour & Tasting
- Exclusive in‑depth tour of Glenfarclas distillery.
- Includes tasting of five whiskies from The Family Cask Collection, one per decade (1960s–2000s).
- Duration: 2–2.5 h, starts at 1:30 pm on Thursdays (April–October). 
- Price: £150 per person.
- Minimum age 18; booking via VisitorCentre@glenfarclas.com.

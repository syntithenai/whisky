# The Aged Range – Glenfarclas

Glenfarclas presents its *Made to Age* range, a selection of single‑cask, single‑distillery whiskies aged from 10 to 40 years. Each bottle is accompanied by tasting notes written by brand ambassador George S. Grant.

- **Distillery**: Glenfarclas Distillery, Ballindalloch, Banffshire, Scotland
- **Range**: 10‑year, 12‑year, 15‑year, 17‑year, 21‑year, 25‑year, 30‑year and 40‑year single‑cask expressions
- **Notes**: Available on the site by clicking each bottle.

No pricing or purchase links are provided on this page.

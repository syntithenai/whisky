# Sunburnt Tommy's Marg
A fiery twist on the classic margarita featuring **Soldada Tequila Blanco**, **Manly Spirits Triple Sec**, fresh lime juice, and a spicy **bird’s eye chilli**. The cocktail is shaken with ice, strained over fresh ice in a rocks glass, and garnished with a rosemary sprig for an aromatic finish.

> *“A bold, fiery margarita for those who like their cocktails with a little extra heat.”* – Hannah Brecknell, Manly Spirits bar team (Feb 22 2025)

The recipe is part of the **Manly Spirits** cocktail collection and can be purchased online at $19.99.

# Lilly Pilly Pop Cocktail
A vibrant gin‑based drink featuring **Lilly Pilly Pink Gin**, fresh lemon juice, sugar syrup, hibiscus syrup and aquafaba for a silky vegan foam. Garnished with an aromatic bubble or raspberry crumble.

**Method**: Shake all ingredients with ice, strain back into the shaker, dry shake to create foam, then mesh‑strain into a chilled Nick & Nora glass.

The recipe showcases Manly Spirits’ signature pink gin and highlights botanical, floral, and tropical flavor notes.

# La Mora Cocktail

A refreshing gin‑based cocktail featuring **Manly Spirits Australian Dry Gin**, *Crème de Mure*, lemon juice and sugar syrup. Garnished with lemon zest, edible flowers and a mulberry.

**Ingredients**
- 45 ml Manly Spirits Australian Dry Gin
- 30 ml Crème de Mure
- 30 ml lemon juice
- 10 ml sugar syrup

**Method**
1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
2. Strain into a rocks glass over ice.
3. Peel and twirl a lemon rind, then garnish.

The recipe is part of Manly Spirits’ bar‑tender collection on their website.

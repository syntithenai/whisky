# Limoncello Love: Embrace the Bright Flavours of Summer

Manly Spirits Co. celebrates Italian‑style lemon liqueur with an Australian twist. The blog explains the history of limoncello, details how the distillery crafts its *Zesty Limoncello* using premium Central Coast lemons and native botanicals (Lemon Aspen, Lemon Myrtle), and offers a range of serving ideas from classic digestif to cocktails and desserts.

Key points:
- Traditional Italian method: peel Sorrento‑style lemons, steep zest in alcohol, add simple syrup.
- Manly Spirits’ process: hand‑peel large Central Coast lemons, slow infusion into Australian wheat spirit, blend with house‑made syrup, infuse native botanicals.
- Serving suggestions include spritz, margarita, lemonade cooler, martini, mojito, sangria, affogato, iced tea, sorbet float, tonic.
- Product price: $59.00 (regular).

Enjoy chilled or mixed – the liqueur is versatile for cocktails, desserts and as a digestif.

# Rockpool Gin Sour
A quick cocktail recipe featuring **Fairy Bower Gin**.

- **Ingredients**: 60 ml Fairy Bower Gin, 30 ml lemon juice, 15 ml sugar syrup, 3 dashes Angostura bitters.
- **Method**: Shake all ingredients with ice and strain into a coupe glass. Serve immediately.

# Craro Island G&T
A simple high‑ball gin cocktail featuring **Manly Spirits Craro Island Gin** and premium Indian tonic water, garnished with orange slices.

## Ingredients
- 45 ml Manly Spirits Craro Island Gin
- 100 ml Premium Indian Tonic Water

## Method
1. Combine ingredients in a high‑ball glass.
2. Add ice, top with tonic, stir gently.
3. Garnish with sliced orange.

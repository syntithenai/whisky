# Bella Rosa – Pink Gin Cocktail

**Bella Rosa** is a cocktail recipe featuring Manly Spirits’ *Lilly Pilly Pink Gin*. The drink combines gin, lemon juice, Rosella & Rose cordial, and aqua faba (or egg white) with a splash of lemonade. It’s shaken dry then on‑ice, topped with lemonade, and garnished with dried rose petals.

### Cocktail Method
1. Dry‑shake gin, lemon juice, cordial, and aqua faba.
2. Add ice, shake again.
3. Strain into a highball glass with ice.
4. Top with lemonade.
5. Garnish with dried rose petals.

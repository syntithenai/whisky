# The Bartenders Battle – Cocktail Competition QLD

Manly Spirits Co. is hosting an elimination‑style bartender showdown at the Exhibitionist Bar in Surfers Paradise, QLD. Participants must craft cocktails using a featured spirit and a mystery box ingredient within 10 minutes per round. Scoring covers name, aroma, appearance, taste, brand representation, and creativity (30 points total). Entry costs $50; winners receive cash, Manly Spirits bottles, and media exposure.

**Event details**
- **Date:** Tuesday, 12 Nov
- **Time:** 4:00‑9:00 PM
- **Location:** Exhibitionist Bar, Level 5, HOTA, 135 Bundall Rd, Surfers Paradise QLD 4217
- **RSVP:** Email Matt at matt@manlyspirits.com.au or call 0427 732 542.

**Prizes & perks**
- Winner takes all cash and Manly Spirits bottles (winner & runner‑up)
- Featured in a blog post and social media
- Complimentary arrival drink, discounted hospo drinks, limited free food
- Entry fee: $50

> *“No talking presentations! You’ll be scored purely on your drink‑making skills.”*

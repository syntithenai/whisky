# Devilfish Gimlet
A refreshing gin‑based cocktail featuring Manly Spirits Australian Dry Gin, lime juice and simple syrup. The recipe calls for chilling a coupe glass, shaking the ingredients with ice, double‑straining into the glass, and garnishing with a lime wheel.

## Ingredients
- 60 ml Manly Spirits Australian Dry Gin
- 30 ml fresh lime juice
- 30 ml simple syrup

## Method
1. Chill a coupe glass.
2. Shake gin, lime juice and simple syrup with ice for 15‑20 s.
3. Double‑strain into the chilled glass.
4. Garnish with a lime wheel.
5. Serve immediately.

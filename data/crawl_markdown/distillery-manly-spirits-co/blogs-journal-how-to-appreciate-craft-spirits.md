# How to Appreciate Craft Spirits

Manly Spirits Co., Sydney’s Northern Beaches first artisan distillery, explains that tasting spirits is a skill built through practice rather than innate talent. The article outlines a simple tasting protocol—pour, observe colour, sniff, add water, sip, and repeat—to help novices identify broad descriptors (citrusy, earthy, floral for gin; wood, spice, cereal for whisky) before narrowing down to specific notes such as lemon or juniper. It encourages expanding vocabulary, sharing observations, and visiting the distillery for guided tastings.

The page also lists several quick‑buy products with prices, including Australian Dry Gin, Lilly Pilly Pink Gin, North Head Navy Strength Gin, and others.

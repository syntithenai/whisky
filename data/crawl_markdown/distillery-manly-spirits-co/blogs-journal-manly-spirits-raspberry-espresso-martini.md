# Manly Spirits Raspberry Espresso Martini

Manly Spirits Co., a Sydney‑based distillery, has bottled its take on the classic espresso martini by adding fresh raspberry and subtle spice notes. The drink is ready to pour from the fridge in under a minute and comes in a 750 ml bottle priced at **$49**.

- **Base**: locally roasted Seven Miles coffee, caramel & butterscotch
- **Flavour twist**: steeped raspberries, cinnamon, vanilla
- **Serving**: chill, shake with ice, strain into martini glass, optional garnish of three coffee beans or a fresh raspberry
- **Availability**: online shop and select Australian retailers; free shipping on orders over $149.

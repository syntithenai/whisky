# Amber Mai Tai

A cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring **Amber Spiced Gin** as the base spirit. The drink combines tropical and citrus notes: pineapple juice, fresh lime, triple sec, orgeat, and a garnish of orange wedge and mint sprigs.

## Ingredients
- 60 ml Amber Spiced Gin
- 10 ml Triple Sec
- 15 ml Orgeat
- 15 ml Pineapple Juice
- 30 ml Fresh Lime Juice

## Method
1. Shake all ingredients with ice in a cocktail shaker.
2. Strain into a rock glass over fresh ice.
3. Garnish and serve.

The recipe is presented on the Manly Spirits website under their *Cocktail Recipes* section.

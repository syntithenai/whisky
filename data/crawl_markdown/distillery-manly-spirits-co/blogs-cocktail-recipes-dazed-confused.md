# Dazed & Confused Cocktail

A refreshing high‑ball recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring **Gone Troppo Gin**, citrus juices, watermelon, grapefruit oleo and sugar syrup, finished with crushed ice and garnished with pineapple, mint and lime.

---

**Ingredients**
- 55 ml Gone Troppo Gin
- 10 ml lemon juice
- 10 ml lime juice
- 30 ml watermelon puree
- 15 ml grapefruit oleo
- 5 ml sugar syrup
- Garnish: pineapple, mint, lime

**Method**
1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
2. Strain into a high‑ball glass over fresh ice.
3. Top with crushed ice and garnish.

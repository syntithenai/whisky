# Amber Spiced Gin & Tonic

**Ingredients**
- 45 ml Manly Spirits Amber Spiced Gin
- 100 ml premium Indian tonic water
- Garnish: sliced orange

**Method**
1. Fill a high‑ball glass with ice.
2. Pour the gin over the ice.
3. Top with tonic water and stir gently.
4. Garnish with an orange slice.

Enjoy this simple, flavourful cocktail featuring Manly Spirits’ Amber Spiced Gin.

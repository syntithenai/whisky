# Rockpool 75 Cocktail

A simple champagne‑based cocktail featuring **Fairy Bower Gin**.

- **Ingredients**
  - 30 ml Fairy Bower Gin
  - 15 ml lemon juice
  - 10 ml sugar syrup
- **Method** – Shake all ingredients, strain into a champagne flute and top with champagne.

# Bronte Basil Spritz
A refreshing cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring **Bronte Baths Gin**, verjus, sugar syrup, fresh basil, cucumber and soda water.

**Ingredients**
- 45 ml Bronte Baths Gin
- 10 ml Verjus
- 5 ml Sugar Syrup
- Fresh Basil Leaves (4‑6)
- Fresh Cucumber Slices (3‑5)
- Soda Water to top

**Method**
1. Muddle basil, cucumber, gin, sugar and verjus in a shaker.
2. Shake, then double‑strain into a wine glass.
3. Top with soda water and garnish with a cucumber ribbon.

## Long Description
# Manly Spirits Co.

Manly Spirits Co., based in Brookvale, Australia, produces handcrafted spirits including gins (Coastal Citrus, Craro Island, Australian Dry, The Beaches, Lilly Pilly Pink), vodkas (Oyster Pearl, Grape & Grain), whiskies and liqueurs.  Their website hosts over 50 bartender‑approved cocktail recipes that showcase these products, many of which are available for purchase directly online.

## Product Highlights
- **Coastal Citrus Gin** – citrus‑forward gin with lemon myrtle botanicals.
- **Craro Island Gin** – dry gin with oceanic botanicals.
- **Australian Dry Gin** – wheat spirit distilled with marine and native Australian botanicals.
- **The Beaches Gin** – tropical gin featuring pineapple, lime, mango, coconut and banana notes.
- **Lilly Pilly Pink Gin** – zero‑sugar pink gin infused with Lilly Pilly berries and edible flowers.
- **Oyster Pearl Vodka** – savory vodka with olive brine.
- **Barrel‑Aged Gin** – matured in ex‑bourbon and ex‑Tasmanian single‑malt whisky barrels, finished with a splash of 6‑month maturing bourbon cask whisky.

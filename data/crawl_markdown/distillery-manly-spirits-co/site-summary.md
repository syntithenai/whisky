## Long Description
# Manly Spirits Co.

**Location & Vision**
Manly Spirits Co. operates from the coastal suburb of Brookvale/Manly in Sydney, Australia. The distillery was founded by Vanessa Wilton (owner/producer) and David Whittaker (founder/distiller). Their mission is to craft premium Australian spirits that celebrate local ingredients while maintaining a low carbon footprint.

**Product Range**
- **Gins** – Lilly Pilly Pink Gin, The Beaches Gin, North Head Navy Strength, Coastal Citrus, Fairy Bower, etc.  All are botanically‑infused with native Australian herbs and fruit.
- **Whiskies** – Coastal Stone Whisky (aged in red‑wine barrels), Xplore Whisky, Salvaged Ale Single Malt, Personalised Custom Label Single Malt Whisky.
- **Vodkas & Liqueurs** – Australian Pure Vodka, Marine Botanical Vodka, Zesty Limoncello, Raspberry Espresso Martini Bottled Cocktail.
- **Specialty Items** – Ginger Beer Whisky, Custom‑labeled gins, and limited‑release editions.

**Sustainability & Production**
The distillery repurposes wine barrels for whisky maturation, reducing carbon emissions. Distillation is performed in copper pot stills; whiskies are aged in first‑fill Tasmanian Pinot Noir or red‑wine casks. Gins use a post‑distillation infusion of botanicals such as lilly pilly, sea fig, finger lime and raspberry.

**Events & Experiences**
- Night at the Barracks (outdoor music event)
- Junipalooza Melbourne 2023 participation
- Distillery tours, workshops (e.g., “Fill Your Own Whisky from the Barrel”), and seasonal celebrations such as Father’s Day and Easter gatherings.

**Awards & Recognition**
- Lilly Pilly Pink Gin: Double Gold SIP Awards 2021, Gold at San Francisco World Spirits Competition 2020, Gold at World Gin Awards 2020.
- Coastal Stone Whisky: Award‑winning single malt.
- Premix bottle design: 2021 World Premix Award for best premix bottle design.

**E‑commerce & Shipping**
Products are sold online with free shipping on orders over AUD 149. The site supports age verification, multiple payment methods (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Mastercard, etc.), and offers personalised labels for whisky and gin.

**Community & Inclusion**
The distillery emphasizes gender equity in hospitality, featuring female leadership stories and inclusive marketing. Community initiatives include a hand‑sanitiser production line during the pandemic.

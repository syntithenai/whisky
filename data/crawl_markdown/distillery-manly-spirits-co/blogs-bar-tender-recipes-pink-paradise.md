# Pink Paradise Cocktail

A summer‑ready gin cocktail featuring **Lilly Pilly Pink Gin**, pineapple juice and sweetened coconut cream, garnished with dehydrated lime or a fresh pineapple wedge.

- **Ingredients**: 45 ml Lilly Pilly Pink Gin, 60 ml pineapple juice, 60 ml sweetened coconut cream.
- **Method**: Shake with ice (10‑15 s), dry shake, strain into a cold coupe glass, garnish.
- **Product**: Lilly Pilly Pink Gin – $78.00, zero‑sugar pink gin made from Australian native berries and flowers.

# Beach Buck
A simple highball cocktail featuring **Barrel Aged Gin** from Manly Spirits Co., fresh lemon juice and ginger ale. Served over ice in a high‑ball glass.

## Ingredients
- 30 ml Barrel Aged Gin
- Squeeze of lemon
- Ginger Ale (to top)
- Ice

## Method
1. Fill a high‑ball glass with ice.
2. Pour the gin over the ice.
3. Add a squeeze of fresh lemon juice.
4. Top with ginger ale and stir gently.
5. Serve immediately.

The recipe highlights the depth of the barrel‑aged gin while keeping the drink light, refreshing and easy to make.

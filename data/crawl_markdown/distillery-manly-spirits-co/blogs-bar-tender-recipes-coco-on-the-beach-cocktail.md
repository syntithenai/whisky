# Coco on the Beach Cocktail
A tropical summer drink featuring **The Beaches Gin** blended with pineapple juice, lime juice and coconut cream, finished with shredded coconut and a banana leaf garnish.

## Ingredients (per 60 ml cocktail)
- The Beaches Gin – 30 ml
- Pineapple juice – 20 ml
- Lime juice – 15 ml
- Coconut cream – 15 ml
- Garnish: shredded coconut & banana leaf

## Method
1. Combine all liquid ingredients in a shaker with ice.
2. Shake vigorously for 10‑15 s.
3. Strain into a highball glass over fresh ice.
4. Top with shredded coconut and a banana leaf.

Enjoy the beach vibe in every sip.

# Foraged Coastal Bowl

A simple gin‑based cocktail featuring **Coastal Citrus Gin** and premium tonic, garnished with lemon slices and lemon myrtle.

- **Ingredients**: 60 ml Coastal Citrus Gin, tonic, ice, garnish.
- **Method**: Combine in a copa glass, add ice, top with tonic, stir, garnish.

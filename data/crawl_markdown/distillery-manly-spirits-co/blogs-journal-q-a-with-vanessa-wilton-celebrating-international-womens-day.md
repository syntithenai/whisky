# Q&A with Vanessa Wilton – Celebrating International Women’s Day

Vanessa Wilton, owner and producer at **Manly Spirits Co.**, discusses her journey into the Australian spirits industry, the challenges women face in hospitality and liquor, and how she builds a boutique urban distillery on Sydney’s northern beaches.

- **Why start locally?** She wanted to create premium Australian spirits using local resources instead of importing.
- **Coastal Stone Whisky** is aged in red‑wine barrels to give it an unmistakably Australian character while reducing carbon footprint.
- Vanessa highlights the importance of female leadership, cash‑flow planning, and staying adaptable amid industry changes.
- She stresses inclusion, emotional intelligence, and empathy as key strengths for women leaders.

The interview offers insights into sustainable production, gender dynamics in spirits, and practical advice for aspiring female entrepreneurs.

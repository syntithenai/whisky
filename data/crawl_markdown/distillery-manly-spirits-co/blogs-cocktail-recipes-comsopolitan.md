# Comsopolitan Cocktail

A quick‑serve cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring their Grape & Grain Vodka, Mandarin Triple Sec, cranberry juice and lime juice. Shake with ice, strain into a coupe glass, garnish with an orange twist.

**Ingredients**
- 45 ml Manly Spirits Grape & Grain Vodka
- 15 ml Manly Spirits Mandarin Triple Sec
- 20 ml Cranberry juice
- 10 ml Lime juice

**Method**
Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a coupe glass and garnish.

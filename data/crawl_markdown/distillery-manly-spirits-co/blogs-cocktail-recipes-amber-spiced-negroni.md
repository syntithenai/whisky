# Amber Spiced Negroni

A cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring their **Amber Spiced Gin** blended with Campari and sweet vermouth, garnished with orange peel and coffee beans.

## Ingredients (30 ml each)
- Manly Spirits Amber Spiced Gin
- Bitter Orange Liqueur (Campari)
- Sweet Vermouth
- 5 Coffee Beans

## Method
1. Muddle coffee beans to release flavour.
2. In a rocks glass, combine gin, Campari and vermouth over ice.
3. Stir until chilled.
4. Garnish with orange peel twist and float coffee beans.

Enjoy the layered citrus‑spice‑coffee profile in a classic Negroni format.

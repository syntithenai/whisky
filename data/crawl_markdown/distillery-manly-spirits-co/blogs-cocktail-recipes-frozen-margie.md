# Frozen Margarita
A quick recipe for a frozen margarita using Manly Spirits tequila and Margarita Cello, blended with ice, sugar syrup, and watermelon water. Garnish with lime zest.

**Ingredients**
- 45 ml Tequila
- 30 ml Margarita Cello
- 10 ml Sugar syrup
- 1 cup Watermelon (water)

**Method**
Blend all ingredients with ice until smooth, pour into a balloon glass and garnish with lime zest.

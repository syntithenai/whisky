# Oyster Pearl Martini
A simple yet elegant cocktail featuring Manly Spirits’ **Oyster Pearl Vodka**.

- **Ingredients**: 60 ml vodka, 5 ml dry vermouth, 10 ml olive brine, one muddled olive.
- **Method**: Chill a martini glass. In a mixing glass combine vodka, vermouth and brine; gently muddle an olive to release its flavour, strain into the glass, stir with ice until chilled, then garnish with a green seedless olive.

The recipe showcases how the brand’s signature vodka can be used in a classic martini format.

# Ways To Enjoy Gin
Manly Spirits Co. outlines five ways to enjoy gin: 
1. **Gin cocktail night** – experiment with classic and creative cocktails using multiple gins.
2. **With a seafood meal** – Coastal Citrus Gin pairs well with sushi, oysters, mussels and spicy prawns.
3. **Charcuterie & cheese board picnic** – Australian Dry Gin complements savory boards; try a Vesper or Oaked Martinez.
4. **Pink gin** – traditional Angostura‑bitters mix or modern fruit‑infused versions for a refreshing sip.
5. **The perfect G&T** – 60 ml Australian Dry Gin, Indian tonic water, orange slices and basil in a Copa glass.

The article encourages experimentation and highlights the brand’s own gin range available online.

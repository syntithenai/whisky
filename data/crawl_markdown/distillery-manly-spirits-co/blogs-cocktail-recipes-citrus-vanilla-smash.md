# Citrus Vanilla Smash
A vibrant cocktail featuring **Coastal Citrus Gin**, **ube syrup**, and **vanilla** blended with passion fruit juice, lemon juice, and a dash of citric solution. Served over crushed ice, garnished with mint and vanilla dust.

## Ingredients
- 30 ml Coastal Citrus Gin
- 20 ml Ube syrup
- 15 ml Vanilla
- 40 ml Passion fruit juice
- 20 ml Lemon juice
- Dash of citric solution

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a shaker with ice.
2. Shake vigorously for 10‑15 s.
3. Strain into a highball glass over crushed ice.
4. Garnish with mint and vanilla dust.

# Long Island Ice Tea – Manly Spirits Co.

A contemporary take on the classic Long Island Iced Tea, this recipe uses **Manly Spirits**’ own Mandarin Triple Sec, Australian Dry Gin, Vodka, and White Rum. The drink is finished with fresh lime juice and a splash of cola for layering. It’s served over ice in a high‑ball glass, garnished with a lime wedge.

## Ingredients & Method
- 15 ml Mandarin Triple Sec (Manly Spirits)
- 15 ml Tequila Blanco
- 15 ml Australian Dry Gin (Manly Spirits)
- 15 ml Vodka (Manly Spirits)
- 30 ml White Rum
- 15 ml Fresh Lime Juice
- 15 ml Cola (for layering)

1. Pour cola into a glass with ice.
2. Shake the remaining ingredients in a shaker until chilled.
3. Strain over the cola and garnish with a lime wedge.

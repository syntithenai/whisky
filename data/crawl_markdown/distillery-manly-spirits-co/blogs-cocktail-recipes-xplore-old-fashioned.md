# Xplore Old Fashioned
A cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. that uses **Coastal Stone: Xplore Whisky** as the base spirit.

## Ingredients
- 60 ml Coastal Stone: Xplore Whisky
- 7 ml Pale Ale syrup (or 10 ml Sugar syrup)
- 3 dashes Angostura bitters
- Orange twist for garnish

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass.
2. Add ice, stir well, and strain into a rocks glass over fresh ice.
3. Garnish with an orange zest.

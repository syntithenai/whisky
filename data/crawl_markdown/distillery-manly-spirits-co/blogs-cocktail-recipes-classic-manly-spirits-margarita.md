# Classic Manly Spirits Margarita

A simple, refreshing cocktail featuring **60 ml Soldada Tequila**, **30 ml fresh lime juice** and **30 ml Manly Spirits Bitter Orange Triple Sec**. Shake with ice, strain into a chilled coupe or over ice, rim the glass with salt and garnish with an orange slice.

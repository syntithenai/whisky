# Zesty Sgroppino
A bright, citrus‑infused cocktail featuring **Zesty Limoncello**, gin, prosecco and lemon sorbet, finished with lime & mint.

**Ingredients (per serving)**
- 20 ml Zesty Limoncello
- 60 ml gin
- 150 g lemon sorbet
- Prosecco (to top)
- Garnish: grated lime & mint sprig

**Method**
Blend the limoncello, gin and sorbet until smooth. Pour into a chilled glass, top with prosecco, garnish and serve.

**Glassware** – Gin bowl.

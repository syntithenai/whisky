# Lilly Pilly Pink Gin – Award‑Winning Australian Craft Gin

Manly Spirits Co. presents **Lilly Pilly Pink Gin**, a zero‑sugar, 40 % ABV gin that blends twelve botanicals—including the native riberry (lilly pilly), sea fig, three Australian limes, rosella, blood orange, juniper, coriander seed, angelica root and orris—with post‑distillation raspberry infusion for a natural pink hue. The spirit has earned double gold at the 2021 SIP Awards, gold at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition (2020) and gold at the World Gin Awards (2020). It is sold in Australia with free shipping over $149 and offers a personalised label option.

## Key Points
- **Botanicals**: lilly pilly, sea fig, desert/rainforest/finger lime, rosella, blood orange, nasturtium, raspberry infusion, juniper, coriander seed, angelica root, orris.
- **Production**: 12‑botanical distillation + 18‑hour raspberry infusion; bottled at 40 % ABV.
- **Awards**: Double Gold SIP Awards 2021, Gold SFWSC 2020, Gold WGA 2020 (Contemporary Gin).
- **Serving suggestions**: G&T with tonic and fresh berries, spritz with prosecco, or a pink gin sour.
- **Price**: $78.00 retail; personalised label $82.00 sale price $88.00.

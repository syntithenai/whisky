# Whisky Pina Colada
A cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring **Coastal Stone Whisky Xplore** blended with fresh lime and pineapple juices, Coco Lopez, and a pinch of salt. Serve over ice in a glass garnished with a lime wedge.

## Ingredients
- 45 ml Coastal Stone Whisky Xplore
- 15 ml fresh lime juice
- 140 ml fresh pineapple juice
- 40 ml Coco Lopez
- ½ pinch of salt

## Method
Blend all ingredients in a blender until creamy. Pour into a glass with ice and garnish with lime.

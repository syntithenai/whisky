# White Negroni Cocktail

A modern take on the classic Negroni featuring **Barrel‑Aged Gin**, *Cocchi Americano*, dry vermouth, and Italicus bergamot liqueur. The drink is stirred with ice, strained over fresh ice in a rocks glass, and garnished with a slice of finger lime to accentuate citrus notes.

The recipe showcases the unique character of Manly Spirits’ Barrel‑Aged Gin—aged in ex‑bourbon and ex‑Tasmanian single malt whisky barrels—and highlights its versatility as a sipping gin or cocktail base.

---

**Key Ingredients**
- 30 ml Barrel‑Aged Gin
- 20 ml Cocchi Americano
- 20 ml Dry Vermouth
- 5 ml Italicus Bergamot Liqueur
- Finger lime slice (garnish)

**Method**
1. Combine all spirits in a mixing glass.
2. Stir with ice for 15‑20 s.
3. Strain over fresh ice into a rocks glass.
4. Garnish with finger lime.

# Award‑Winning Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur
Manly Spirits Co. showcases its flagship **Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur**, a smooth, rich spirit that has earned international accolades (PLATINUM SIP Awards 2019, GOLD San Francisco World Spirits Awards 2020, SILVER 2023). The liqueur is made by steeping premium coffee for up to 24 hours, blending it with distilled spirits, and subjecting each batch to strict quality control. It can be enjoyed neat, on the rocks, in cocktails (e.g., Espresso Martini), or paired with desserts.

Key points:
- **Awards**: PLATINUM SIP 2019, GOLD SFWSA 2020, SILVER SFWSC 2023, GOLD SIP 2021, PEOPLE’S CHOICE Vintage Cellars 2021.
- **Production**: 24‑hour cold brew extraction, local Australian ingredients, meticulous QC.
- **Versatility**: Neat, cocktails, dessert drizzles.
- **Sustainability**: Commitment to responsible practices.

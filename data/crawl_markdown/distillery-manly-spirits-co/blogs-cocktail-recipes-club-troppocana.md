# Club Troppocana Cocktail

**Club Troppocana** is a gin‑based cocktail from Manly Spirits Co. It mixes **Gone Troppo Gin**, watermelon juice, lime, simple syrup, saline and raspberries, topped with crushed ice and garnished with raspberries and dragonfruit.

## Key facts
- **Ingredients**: 50 ml Gone Troppo Gin, 75 ml watermelon juice, 20 ml lime, 15 ml simple syrup, 2 dashes saline, 4 raspberries. Garnish: raspberries & dragonfruit.
- **Method**: Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a rocks glass over ice, top with crushed ice and garnish.

# Manly Spirits Co.

Manly Spirits Co. is a boutique Australian distillery based in Brookvale that produces a range of spirits including **Australian dry gin**, vodka, limoncello liqueur, and premixed cocktails. The site offers:
- A *Coastal Stone* whisky line (core range & limited releases)
- Gin school workshops where guests create their own bottle
- Distillery tours showcasing production processes and tasting sessions
- Venue hire for events and a bar menu with signature cocktails such as **Fairy Bower Gin** and **Bloody Marine**
- Free shipping on orders over \$149 and click‑and‑collect in Brookvale.

The flagship product, the Australian dry gin, blends ten botanicals—sea lettuce, finger lime, anise, myrtle, mountain pepper leaf, orange peel, juniper, citrus, marine, and wheat spirit—to deliver bright citrus, marine, and juniper notes with a peppery finish. It has won *Best Australian London‑Dry Gin* at the World Gin Awards for two consecutive years.

The distillery also sells personalised labels, gift vouchers, and merch, and hosts cocktail masterclasses and gin school sessions.

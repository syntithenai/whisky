# Manly 75 Cocktail Recipe

**Manly 75** is a simple, refreshing cocktail that blends *Limoncello*, fresh lemon juice and *Prosecco* for a sparkling finish.

- **Ingredients**
  - 40 ml Limoncello
  - 10 ml Lemon juice
  - Prosecco (to top up)
- **Method**
  1. Shake the Limoncello and lemon juice with ice.
  2. Strain into a champagne glass.
  3. Top with Prosecco.
  4. Garnish with a lemon twist.

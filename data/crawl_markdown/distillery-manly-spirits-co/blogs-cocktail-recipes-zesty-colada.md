# Zesty Colada
A refreshing cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co., featuring grape‑and‑grain vodka, sweetened pineapple juice, zesty limoncello, lime juice and coconut sorbet. Shake, strain into a glass and garnish with powdered cinnamon and lime.

**Ingredients**
- 45 ml Grape & Grain Vodka
- 45 ml Sweetened Pineapple Juice
- 15 ml Zesty Limoncello
- 15 ml Lime Juice
- 1–2 scoops Coconut Sorbet

**Method**
Shake all ingredients with ice, strain into a glass and garnish.

# Beaches Splice
A cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring **The Beaches Gin** mixed with tropical juice, roasted pineapple‑lime oleo, and a lemon‑ade float.

## Ingredients
- 50 ml The Beaches Gin
- 30 ml Tropical Juice
- 20 ml Roasted Pineapple & Lime Oleo
- Lemonade top‑cream float

## Method
1. Build in a highball glass with a roasted pineapple wedge and lime zest.
2. Add the gin, tropical juice, and oleo.
3. Top with lemonade‑float.

### Roasted Pineapple & Lime Oleo
- 500 g roasted pineapple (skin on, baked at 180 °C for ~45 min)
- 50 g lime peels
- 200–300 g sugar
- Mix in a bowl or zip‑lock bag, let sit 2–6 h.

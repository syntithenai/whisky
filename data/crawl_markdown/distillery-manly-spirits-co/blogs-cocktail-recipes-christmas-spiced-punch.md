# Christmas Spiced Punch

A festive cocktail recipe from Manly Spirits Co. featuring **Amber Spiced Gin**, blood orange juice and ginger ale, garnished with mint and fresh ginger.

**Ingredients**
- 120 ml Amber Spiced Gin (Manly Spirits)
- 120 ml Blood Orange Juice
- 240 ml Ginger Ale
- Fresh Mint Leaves
- Sliced Ginger

**Method**
1. Combine gin, blood orange juice and ginger slices in a jug.
2. Stir well.
3. Clap mint leaves to release aroma, add to the mix.
4. Pour ginger ale, top with ice, stir gently, garnish with extra ginger and mint.

The drink is marketed as a refreshing option for Christmas gatherings.

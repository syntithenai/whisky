# Lark Distillery – Overeem Plumbing

Lark Distillery is Tasmania’s first whisky distillery, now expanding its Cambridge site.
Overeem Gas & Plumbing has been contracted for two development stages:
1. **Stage 1** – construction of two bonded stores, a fire‑protection system and rainwater storage; a foam‑injection fire suppression system was installed due to the high flammability of whisky.
2. **Stage 2** – completed extension adding an industrial hot‑water system, reverse osmosis, upgraded trade waste treatment, enhanced fire protection and additional water filtration, enabling a significant production increase.

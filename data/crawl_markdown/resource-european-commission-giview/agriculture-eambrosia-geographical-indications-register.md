# eAmbrosia – Union Register of Geographical Indications

The European Commission’s **eAmbrosia** portal is a legal register that lists all agricultural products, wines, and spirit drinks protected by geographical indications (GIs) across the EU. It offers direct access to information on registered GIs, including:
- Legal instruments of protection
- Product specifications
- Key dates for applications and publications
- Links to related resources such as traditional wine terms and Specialities Guaranteed.

The site also provides a searchable database with filters by product type, country, file number, name, status, date, and combined nomenclature code. The current catalogue contains 3 958 records.

Additional sections include FAQs, accessibility statements, privacy policy, contact information, and links to related EU agri‑food portals.

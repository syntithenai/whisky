# eAmbrosia – Traditional Terms Protected in the EU for Wine

The page is a portal to the European Commission’s **eAmbrosia** database of *traditional terms* used for wines that are protected under EU law. It explains what traditional terms are, cites relevant regulations (Regulation (EU) No 1308/2013 and Regulations 2019/33 & 2019/34), and offers a searchable table listing 376 records of protected terms by country, language, term, and type.

Key points:
- Traditional terms convey information about production methods, ageing, colour, place or historical events.
- Some terms indicate that the wine carries a Protected Designation of Origin (PDO) or Protected Geographical Indication (PGI).
- The table includes entries such as *Gecontroleerde oorsprongsbenaming* (Belgium Dutch), *Appellation d'origine contrôlée* (France French), and *Vin de pays* (France French).

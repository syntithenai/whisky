# World Martini Day – 78 Degrees Recipes

- **Date**: 17 June 2023 (World Martini Day)
- **Author/Creator**: Chewy Morrow, Spirits Innovation & Brand Manager at 78 Degrees Distillery.
- **Three recipes**:
  - *78° Breakfast Marmalade Gimlet* – 45 ml 78° Desert Gin + 15 ml Breakfast Marmalade Cordial + 15 ml lemon juice (shake, strain into chilled coupe, garnish with toast).
  - *78° Bacon Martinez* – 45 ml Bacon 78° Classic Gin + 30 ml 78° Rosso Vermouth + dash Angostura bitters (stir, strain into chilled coupe, garnish with candied bacon).
  - *78° Oyster Shell Martini* – 50 ml Oyster Shell 78° Better Gin + 15 ml 78° Dry Vermouth + 5 ml Fino Sherry + 5 ml sugar syrup + dash orange bitters (stir, strain into chilled coupe, garnish with fresh natural oyster).

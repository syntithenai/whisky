# Talis Heggart Wins the South Australian 78° Create a Stir Sunshine State Gin Cocktail Competition

- **Date & Venue**: Finals held on 5 June 2023 at BRKLYN Bar, Adelaide.
- **Winner**: Talis Heggart (Memphis Slim’s House of Blues) with the cocktail *Haleakala Sunshine*.
- **Cocktail Ingredients**: 78° Sunshine State Gin, clarified orange‑juice‑infused 78° Rosé Vermouth, passionfruit husk verjuice vincotto, guava soda; garnished with waste orange rind and dried guava.
- **Sustainability Focus**: Use of waste ingredients from the cocktail itself.
- **Prize**: $500 cash, a bottle of Sunshine State Gin, and a bespoke 78° bar kit for all finalists.
- **Judges**: Stuart “Chewy” Morrow (Mighty Craft Spirits), Nick Corletto (Maybe Mae), Rashaad Cezar (BRKLYN).
- **Other Finalists**: Matt Holding, Tom Opie, Than Howlett, Alexander Johnston, Alfie Simmons.

The competition highlighted the tropical flavour profile of 78° Sunshine State Gin and showcased innovative, sustainable mix‑making talent from South Australia.

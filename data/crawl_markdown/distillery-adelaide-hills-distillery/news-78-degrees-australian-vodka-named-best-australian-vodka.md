# 78° Australian Vodka Wins "Best Australian Vodka" at Dan Murphy’s Decoded Spirit Awards

- **Award**: Best Australian Vodka (2023) – Dan Murphy’s Decoded Spirit Awards.
- **Producer**: 78 Degrees Distillery, Adelaide Hills, South Australia.
- **Key Ingredient**: Grape marc from local wineries.
- **Flavor Profile**: Silky‑smooth mouthfeel with subtle pepper and citrus notes.
- **Other Recognitions**:
  - 78° Australian Whiskey – Best Value Pick (Australian Whiskey).
  - 78° Muscat Finish Whiskey – finalist for Best Australian Whiskey.

> *“We wanted to create something that stood out and was not just uniquely Australian, but also represented our unique part of Australia – our home at Lot 100 in the Adelaide Hills.”* – Brand Manager Chewy.

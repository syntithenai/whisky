# Purveyor of Better – 78° Better Gin

The **Purveyor of Better** initiative launched by **78 Degrees Distillery** on 14 September 2020 promotes a new gin, the *78° Better Gin*. The project embodies the distillery’s mantra “Crafted for Better”, focusing on quality, sustainability, local produce and community connection. A portion of every bottle sold is donated to local charities and community projects, especially supporting hospitality workers affected by COVID‑19 through free meal deliveries.

## Key points
- **Product**: 78° Better Gin (new release)
- **Mission**: “Crafted for Better” – quality spirits, sustainable production, Australian botanicals, community support
- **Charity model**: Profits from gin sales fund local community and charity initiatives
- **COVID‑19 relief**: Free pre‑packaged meals delivered to unemployed hospitality workers within 20 km of Melbourne CBD (1 000–2 000 meals per week)
- **Marketing**: Not a cheap tool but an embodiment of the distillery’s values

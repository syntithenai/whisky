# 78 Degrees Sunset Gin and Classic Gin Win Double Gold at SFWSC

The Adelaide Hills Distillery’s **Sunset Gin** and **Classic Gin** earned double‑gold medals at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition (SFWSC) in 2023, with *Desert Gin* and *Bitter Orange* also receiving gold. Their whiskeys—*Native Grain*, *Australian*, and *Muscat Finish*—took silver, while the new *Sunshine State Gin* earned bronze.

> “Our spirits are considered some of the best in the world,” says Distillery & Operations Manager **Tim Gniel**.

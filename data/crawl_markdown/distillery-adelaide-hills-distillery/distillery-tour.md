# 78 Degrees Distillery Tour

- **Location**: Lot 100, Adelaide Hills (Hay Valley)
- **Experience**: Guided tour of copper stills, distillation process, and cellar‑door tasting.
- **Activities**:
  - *Gin‑making masterclass* – 90 min, two take‑home 200 ml bottles.
  - *Tasting bar* – curated selection of SA gins.
  - *Blend‑your‑own gin* – personalised blend to label and name.
- **Pricing**: $110 pp for the masterclass (includes G&T on arrival).
- **Booking**: Online via website; limited slots, available Thu–Sun at 11 am.

> “Embark on a captivating journey through the hidden gems of Adelaide’s distilling culture.”

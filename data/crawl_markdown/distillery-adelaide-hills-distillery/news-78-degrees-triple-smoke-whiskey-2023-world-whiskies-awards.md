# 78 Degrees Triple Smoke Whiskey
- **Award**: Gold Medal & Category Winner for Best Australian Grain Whiskey at the 2023 World Whiskies Awards.
- **Limited Release**: Re‑released a very limited stock online.
- **Triple Smoking Process**:
  - Australian redgum‑smoked local, single‑paddock unmalted barley (hazelnut praline & eucalypt flavours).
  - American cherrywood‑smoked barley from Corsair Distillery, Nashville.
  - Scottish peated malt blended in.
- **Tasting Notes**: Smooth, velvety; dark chocolate, dried fruit, subtle spice and lingering smokiness. Judges highlighted bright grain, chocolate, gentle fruits, vegetal note, aniseed hints, soft leather finish.

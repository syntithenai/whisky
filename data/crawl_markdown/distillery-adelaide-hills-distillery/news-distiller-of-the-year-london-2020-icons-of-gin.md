# Adelaide Hills Distillery – 2020 Icons of Gin Award

- **Award**: *Distiller of the Year* at London 2020 Icons of Gin.
- **Products highlighted**: 78° Gin, 78° Sunset, Australian Aperitif Bitter Orange (and a brandy).
- **Other accolades**: Best International Gin, Best Aperitif and Best Brandy at the American Distilling Institute; Best Australian Grain Whiskey at the World Whiskey Awards.
- **Core philosophy**: “Crafted For Better” – focus on quality, sustainability, local ingredients and renewable energy.
- **Sustainability highlights**: 300 kW solar plant, water‑management program, use of endemic Australian botanicals (e.g., green ants).
- **Visitor experience**: New cellar door and production facility at LOT 100 in the Adelaide Hills.

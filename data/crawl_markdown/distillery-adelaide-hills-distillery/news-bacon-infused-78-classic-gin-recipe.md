# Bacon‑Infused 78° Classic Gin Recipe

A quick DIY recipe from Adelaide Hills Distillery’s 78° Classic Gin: cook bacon, pour the fat into a sealable jar with gin, add extra bacon bits, seal and refrigerate for 24 hours. Strain before using.

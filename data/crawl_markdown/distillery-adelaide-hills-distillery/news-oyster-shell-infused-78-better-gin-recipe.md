# Oyster Shell Infused 78° Better Gin Recipe

A quick recipe from 78 Degrees Distillery that turns ordinary gin into a briny‑savory infusion using washed oyster shells.

1. Add washed oyster shells to a sealable jar.
2. Cover with 78 Better Gin.
3. Shake well and let sit for 24 hours.
4. Strain through a coffee filter before use.

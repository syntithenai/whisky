# Breakfast Marmalade Cordial Recipe

A quick recipe for a breakfast marmalade cordial: mix **100 g** of breakfast marmalade with **100 ml** of hot (not boiling) water, stir until dissolved, then strain into another jar to remove solids. The resulting cordial can be used in cocktails.

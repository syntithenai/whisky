# 78 Degrees Distillery – “Crafted Better” Philosophy

- **Mission**: Produce high‑quality spirits using sustainable, locally sourced ingredients and processes.
- **Sustainability**: Off‑grid solar power (690 panels, 300 kW/h), underground aquifer water, on‑site wastewater treatment (70 000 L/day) with closed‑loop irrigation.
- **Community**: Employs local Adelaide Hills residents, trains youth in distilling and hospitality, partners with regional suppliers.
- **Cellar Door**: Designed for minimal environmental impact; repurposes production waste into useful products.
- **Brand Promise**: “Crafted Better” – quality, sustainability, local produce, community connection.

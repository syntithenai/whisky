# Closing the Loop

78 Degrees Distillery explains its closed‑loop sustainability strategy, focusing on water management and waste reuse.

- **Water cycle**: Bore‑water → filtration & reverse osmosis → production use → wastewater treatment (solid removal, bio‑reactor) → dam → irrigation of orchards/gardens → groundwater recharge.
- **Waste integration**: Solids from wastewater are composted for kitchen gardens and neighbouring crops.
- **Other closed‑loop practices**: Spent grain used as stock feed.

The page emphasizes responsible resource stewardship and a commitment to sustainability for future generations.

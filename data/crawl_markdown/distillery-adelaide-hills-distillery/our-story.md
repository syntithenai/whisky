# 78 Degrees Distillery – Our Story

- Founded in **2014** by winemaker **Sacha La Forgia**.
- First product: *78° Classic Gin* (launched 2015), noted for bright, Australian‑character botanicals extracted at the ethanol boiling point of **78 °C**.
- In **December 2018** moved to Lot 100 in Adelaide Hills, South Australia, sharing the site with other craft producers.
- Expanded portfolio now includes gins, vermouths, aperitifs, vodka and Australian whiskey, earning numerous awards.
- Key collaborators: **Steve Dorman** and **Toby Kline** of The Hills Cider Company (joined 2016).

The distillery emphasizes sustainability, local sourcing, and pushing craft‑spirit boundaries with a distinctly Australian flavour profile.

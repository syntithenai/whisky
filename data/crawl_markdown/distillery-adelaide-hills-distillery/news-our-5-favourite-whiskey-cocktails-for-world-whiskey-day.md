# Our 5 Favourite Whiskey Cocktails for World Whiskey Day
- **Date**: 17 May 2023 (World Whiskey Day on 20 May)
- **Focus**: Five cocktails featuring 78° Australian Whiskey.
- **Cocktails**:
  - *78° Manhattan* – whiskey, rosso vermouth, chocolate bitters.
  - *78° Boulevardier* – whiskey, bitter orange, rosso vermouth.
  - *78° Scofflaw* – whiskey, dry vermouth, lemon juice, grenadine.
  - *78° Gold Rush* – whiskey, lemon juice, honey.
  - *78° Paper Plane* – whiskey, bitter orange, rosé vermouth, lemon juice.
- **Preparation**: Mix or shake with ice, strain into a chilled coupe; garnish varies by recipe.

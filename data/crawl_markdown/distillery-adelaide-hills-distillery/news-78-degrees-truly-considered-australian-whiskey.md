# 78° Truly Considered Australian Whiskey

- **Launch**: 25 March 2021
- **Creator**: Sacha La Forgia, founder of 78 Degrees Distillery.
- **Philosophy**: A whiskey that is *truly* Australian – not a copy of Scotch but an innovation reflecting local heritage and sustainability.
- **Sustainability Highlights**:
  - Rain‑water fed, GM‑free barley grown on a single paddock.
  - Unmalted barley base with 11 flavouring malts to reduce energy & water use.
  - Grain fermentation & distillation to avoid rinsing waste.
  - Single‑shot distillation and hand‑selected oak casks.
- **Control**: From barley to bottle, the distillery maintains full oversight of materials.

> *“We wanted to create something different, something uniquely Australian.”* – Sacha La Forgia

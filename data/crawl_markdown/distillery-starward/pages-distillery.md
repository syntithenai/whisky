# Starward Distillery & Bar

- Located in Port Melbourne, Australia
- Working whisky distillery open for tours and tastings
- Offers barrel tasting experiences and private events
- Crafted award-winning Australian whiskies from locally sourced ingredients

## Key Facts
- Open Thursday-Sunday (3:00 PM - 9:00 PM)
- Tours & Tastings available Monday-Wednesday by appointment
- Barrel Hall tastings and Private Events offered
- Located just 10 minutes from Melbourne CBD

## Production Signals
- Distilled in small batches using locally sourced grains and red wine barrels
- Maturation influenced by Melbourne's fluctuating temperatures causing faster extraction of flavors
- Uses a combination of American and French oak casks for aging

## Commercial Signals
- Award-winning whiskies with international recognition
- Popular among whisky enthusiasts and collectors
- Frequently featured in cocktail menus at bars worldwide

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited availability of certain barrel-aged expressions
- Strict age-gating policies restrict access to minors

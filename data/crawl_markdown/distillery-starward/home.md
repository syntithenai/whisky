# Starward Australian Whisky
Starward is a Melbourne‑based distillery that produces single‑malt, double‑grain and wheat‑single‑malt whiskies. Their flagship products include **Nova** (700 ml, 41% ABV), **Honeycomb** (700 ml, 40% ABV) and **Two‑Fold** (700 ml, 40% ABV). The site offers personalised whisky gifts, a “Draw Your Own Dram” tasting flight, tours, masterclasses and private events. They highlight local sourcing, award wins for World Whisky Day 2025, and feature Head Distiller & Blender Carlie.

# ADI 2025 San Antonio

The American Distilling Institute (ADI) is hosting its 2025 Craft Spirits Conference and Vendor Expo in San Antonio, Texas on **August 25–26** at the Henry B. Gonzalez Convention Center. The event sits just steps from the iconic River Walk and offers a mix of keynote sessions, workshops, tours, and a Whiskey Summit.

Key highlights:
- **Location:** Downtown San Antonio, near the River Walk, Spanish Colonial Missions, and the Pearl brewery‑turned‑entertainment district.
- **Dates:** 25–26 Aug 2025 (pre‑conference on 24 Aug; post‑conference tours on 27 Aug).
- **Keynote speakers:** Texas Distilled Spirits Association, Texas Whiskey Association, Bump Williams, Alexandre Gabriel.
- **Hotel & travel discounts** for attendees at Marriott Riverwalk San Antonio and RiverCenter, plus airline discounts from Southwest and United.
- **Registration** opens soon; exhibitors can contact ADI sales or Brandon @distilling.com.

For more details visit the official ADI page.

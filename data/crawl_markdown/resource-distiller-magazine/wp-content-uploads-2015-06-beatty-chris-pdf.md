# Better Drinking Through Chemistry
Chris Beatty, *The Spirit Chemist*, presented a concise overview of how analytical chemistry can improve spirit production and marketing.

- **Key points**: quality control, market insight, and messaging.
- **Techniques highlighted**: UV‑Vis spectroscopy for quick color/compound checks; chromatography (LC, GC, SC) for detailed component analysis.
- **Applications**: methanol detection, ABV estimation from sugars, distillate profiling, aroma/barrel extractive analysis.
- **Implementation options**: in‑house labs or external service providers.

The talk stresses that analytical chemistry is a versatile toolset for modern distilleries.

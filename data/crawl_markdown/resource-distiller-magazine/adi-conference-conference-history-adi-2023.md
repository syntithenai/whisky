# ADI 2023 Las Vegas

The American Distilling Institute (ADI) is hosting its 20th annual conference and vendor expo in Las Vegas at the Mirage Hotel & Casino from **August 23‑24** with special classes on August 22 and 25. The event features a Whiskey Summit, a keynote panel of veteran distillers, and an extensive exhibit floor covering 60,000 sq ft with 196 exhibitors.

Key highlights:
- **Location:** Mirage Hotel & Casino, 3400 S Las Vegas Blvd
- **Dates:** Aug 22‑25 (conference) + Aug 23‑24 (expo)
- **Special offers:** 25 % discount at Lost Spirits Distillery via code `DISTILLER25`
- **Keynote format:** Panel discussion with four industry veterans
- **Exhibit details:** Largest gathering of distilling equipment and services; includes happy hour, boozy breakfast, and educational sessions.

For more information, visit the ADI website or contact sales@distilling.com.

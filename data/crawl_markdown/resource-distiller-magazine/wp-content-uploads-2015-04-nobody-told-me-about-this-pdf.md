# Slide 1 – Craft Spirit Business Checklist

This slide outlines the five key areas to address when launching a craft spirit business:

1. **Strategic Reason for Being** – Define a unique product story, market reaction, brand name, travelability, P&L, resources, resonance, need/void, retailers, on‑premise partners, distillation method, trademark status, cost of equipment and contracts.
2. **Production** – Roles (microbiologist, distiller, chemist, chief taster), operations, maintenance, processing, quality control, bottling, packaging, environmental compliance, lobbying, purchasing, accounting, HR, OSHA, finance, product spokesperson.
3. **Building Your Brand** – Authentic, compelling, memorable story; price positioning with category hierarchy and benchmark brands.
4. **Activating the Brand** – Communication, advertising, PR, support materials, on‑premise vs. off‑premise strategy.
5. **Route to Market & Packaging** – Geographic considerations, distributor relationships, field sales, packaging that complements story and price, bottling compatibility, supply availability.

Additional sections cover on‑premise strategy (trial opportunities, staff training, incentives), reporting/BBT compliance (grain usage, batching, bottling, daily determination) and contact details for Rising TBC.

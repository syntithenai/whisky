# Distiller Editorial Submission & Style Guide

The American Distilling Institute’s *Distiller* magazine publishes quarterly print issues and a yearly Resource Directory, while maintaining an online platform that runs year‑round. The editorial submission page outlines:
- **Submission process** – email pitches to editor@distilling.com with the phrase “Distiller Magazine Pitch” in the subject line; include recent writing samples and a brief bio.
- **Compensation** – $0.50/word for text, $0.75/word if photography is included; online stories pay $100 plus $25 per original image (up to two).
- **Story types** – industry trends, deep dives into production or science, profiles of producers, instructional pieces, and interviews.
- **Photography guidelines** – high‑resolution, separate files with captions; minimum pixel dimensions and file size specified for print sizes.
- **Style rules** – reference to AP Stylebook, specific conventions for abbreviations, capitalization, hyphenation, numbers, punctuation, and terminology (e.g., “whiskey” vs. “whisky”, “ABV”).
- **Contact information** – editor@distilling.com for questions; financeadmin@distilling.com for invoicing.

The page also includes navigation links to the ADI conference, resources, and contact pages, but no product or pricing data is present on this editorial guide itself.

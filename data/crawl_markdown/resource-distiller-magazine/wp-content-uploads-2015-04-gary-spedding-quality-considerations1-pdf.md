# Overview of Quality Considerations for Distilled Spirits

This presentation by **Gary Spedding, Ph.D.** (with Amber Weygandt and Matt Linske) from Brewing and Distilling Analytical Services, LLC, was delivered at the 2015 ADI Conference in Louisville, KY. It covers:
- Measuring alcohol accurately (density/SG, OIML & AOAC tables)
- Challenges with high‑extract spirits and nutritional labeling
- Quality control fundamentals: consistency, sensory evaluation, and shelf‑life
- Rapid aging and maturation studies using spectroscopy (UV‑vis fingerprinting) to monitor colour, flavour congeners, and authenticity
- Practical tools for distillery labs and regulatory considerations.

Key takeaways include the importance of precise alcohol measurement, the role of spectroscopic fingerprints in quality assurance, and emerging rapid‑aging techniques.

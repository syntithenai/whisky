# Michael‑Kinslick Craft Distillery Market Summary (2016)

- **Author**: Michael Kinstlick, CEO of Coppersea Distilling, LLC.
- **Purpose**: Provide a snapshot of the U.S. craft distillery market in 2015 and projections for 2016‑2020.
- **Key metrics**:
  - 1,016 unique U.S. listings in ADI directories (2006‑2016).
  - 817 unique U.S. listings in 2016 ADI directory.
  - 1,825 listings in TTB DSP permit file (Jan 2016).
  - 962 operating craft distilleries as of year‑end 2015; 772 identified.
  - Estimated 1,000 new craft distilleries expected in 2016.
- **Growth trend**: Entrants double every three years; projected to reach ~2,000 by 2020.
- **Market share**:
  - Craft distillers hold 1.5 % of the U.S. distilled spirits market.
  - Craft brewers hold 15 % of the U.S. beer market.
- **Exit rates**: Declining from 21.3 % (2005‑07) to 0.4 % (2014‑15).
- **Conclusion**: The craft distilling sector is in an early‑adopter phase with steady growth and low exit risk.

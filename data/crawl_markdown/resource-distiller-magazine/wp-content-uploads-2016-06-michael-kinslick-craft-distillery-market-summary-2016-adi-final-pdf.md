# Michael‑Kinslick Craft Distillery Market Summary (2016)

This PDF provides a statistical snapshot of the U.S. craft distillery sector for 2015 and projections into 2016. Key points include:
- **Market size**: 1,016 unique U.S. listings in ADI directories (2006‑2016) and 817 unique listings in 2016.
- **Active producers**: 962 operating craft distilleries as of year‑end 2015; 772 identified by name.
- **Growth trend**: The number of entrants doubles every three years, with an expected 1,000 new producers in 2016.
- **Product mix**: Distillery‑produced spirits account for ~1.5 % of the U.S. distilled spirits market; craft brewers hold ~15 % of the beer market.
- **Exit rates**: Overall exit rate fell from 21.3 % (2005‑07) to 0.4 % (2014‑15).
- **Geography**: State‑level tables show distribution by state for 2015 and historical trends (1990‑10, 2005‑10).
- **Definition**: A craft distillery is an independent firm producing <40‑50k L of spirits per year.

The report concludes that the craft distilling sector will reach ~2,000 producers within five years, with continued consolidation and a solid early‑adopter consumer base.

# ADI International Spirits Competition
The American Distilling Institute (ADI) hosts the **International Spirits Competition** to award craft spirits worldwide.  Since 2007, every entry receives written feedback and free entry into the Excellence in Packaging Awards.  Winners gain media exposure, distributor/retailer meetings, digital marketing support, and physical/digital trophies.

## Key Benefits
- Written feedback for all entries
- Free packaging awards entry
- Discounted fees for ADI members & affiliates
- Trophy winners meet distributors/importers
- Gold medalists receive social‑media promotion and e‑commerce pricing
- All winners featured in ADI’s newsletter (13 000+ readers) and media contacts

## How to Enter
1. Download the **Submission Guidelines** and **Registration Checklist**.
2. Follow the competition protocols and submit your entry by the deadline.
3. Pay the entry fee (discounts available for members).

# ADI BBD 2015 Workbook Answers

This document contains the answers to the 2015 Annual Spirits Conference workbook on *Building Brands Through Distributors*. It profiles two presenters – Bob Brown of Sales Systems Development, Inc. and Thomas Jensen of Millstone Spirits Group – and outlines key concepts for brand building in the distributor channel, including:
- Distributor share‑of‑mind strategies
- Competence hierarchy for suppliers
- Five critical success factors (positioning, business planning, pricing, discipline, education)
- Practical tools such as annual plans, business reviews, and trade‑pricing guidelines.

The PDF also lists a wide range of industry resources and training programs offered by Sales Systems Development.

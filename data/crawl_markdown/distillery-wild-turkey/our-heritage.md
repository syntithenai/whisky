# Wild Turkey Bourbon History

Wild Turkey was founded in 1939 by the Tarkington family. The distillery is located in Lawrenceburg, Kentucky - the bourbon capital of the world.

## Key Facts
- First batch produced in 1940
- Over 80 years of heritage
- Known for high-quality small-batch bourbons
- Owned by Beam Suntory since 2018

## Production Signals
- Small-batch releases showcase unique flavors
- Emphasis on traditional bourbon-making techniques
- Limited edition bottlings highlight craftsmanship

## Commercial Signals
- Strong brand recognition in the premium spirits market
- Aggressive marketing campaigns targeting whiskey enthusiasts
- Expansion into international markets

## Risks / Unknowns
- Dependence on bourbon trend for revenue growth
- Competition from other established distilleries
- Potential regulatory changes affecting production

# Wild Turkey Distillery – Visit & Experience

- **Location**: Lawrenceburg, Kentucky (top of Wild Turkey Hill)
- **What to do**:
  - Tour the visitor center and historic rickhouses.
  - Learn about grain selection, distillation, and barrel aging.
  - Sample a tasting flight of premium bourbons.
  - Shop for gifts, candles, and top‑shelf bottles.
- **Highlights**: Architectural award‑winning visitor centre, seven‑story rickhouses, views of the Kentucky River, cherry trees in spring.
- **Hours**: Wed–Sat 9 AM–5 PM; Sun 12 PM–4 PM (closed on major holidays).
- **Current status**: No guided tours or tastings at present; pop‑up gift shop available at the Station Master’s House.

The distillery is part of the Kentucky Bourbon Trail® and showcases Wild Turkey’s 75+ year heritage.

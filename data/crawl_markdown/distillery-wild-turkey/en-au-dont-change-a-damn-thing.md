# Wild Turkey Global Campaign 2025

Wild Turkey launches a new global campaign titled **“Don’t Change A Damn Thing”** that celebrates the brand’s century‑old recipe and Master Distiller Jimmy Russell’s 70‑year legacy. The campaign includes a film directed by Kiku Ohe, a mixed‑media documentary featuring historic footage, photos, and artifacts from Wild Turkey’s distillery in Lawrenceburg, KY.

The page also lists the full product range – from classic bourbons (Wild Turkey Bourbon, 101) to limited editions (Jimmy Russell’s 70th Anniversary Bourbon), rye, and specialty releases such as Master’s Keep series, Rare Breed, and rum‑cask finished variants. The site offers a “Buy now” button, links to merch, cocktails, heritage content, and a newsletter signup.

> *“No shortcuts. No apologies. Just bourbon done right.”*

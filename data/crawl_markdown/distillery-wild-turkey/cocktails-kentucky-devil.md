# Kentucky Devil Cocktail

A refreshing summer drink featuring **Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon**, watermelon juice, lemon juice, Gran Marnier and simple syrup. Serve over ice in lowball glasses with a slice of watermelon.

## Ingredients
- 6 parts Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon
- 1 part simple syrup
- 3 parts Gran Marnier
- 2 parts fresh‑squeezed lemon juice
- 4 parts fresh‑squeezed watermelon juice

## Method
Combine all ingredients in a pitcher with ice, stir well, pour into lowball glasses and garnish with a small watermelon slice.

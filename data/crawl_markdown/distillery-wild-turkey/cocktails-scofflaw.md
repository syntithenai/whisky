# Scofflaw Cocktail

A classic Prohibition‑era Parisian drink made with Wild Turkey® Bourbon, grenadine, dry vermouth, fresh lemon juice and orange bitters. Shake with ice, strain into a chilled coupe glass, and garnish with an orange peel.

## Ingredients
- ½ part Wild Turkey® Bourbon
- ½ part grenadine
- ¾ part dry vermouth
- ¾ part freshly squeezed lemon juice
- Orange bitters

## Method
1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.
2. Shake well and strain into a chilled coupe glass.
3. Garnish with an orange peel.

# A Letter from Bruce Russell
Bruce Russell, National Ambassador for Wild Turkey Distilling Co., writes a heartfelt note celebrating the combined 101‑year legacy of his father Eddie and grandfather Jimmy (Mimmy) Russell in the bourbon industry. The letter highlights:
- **Wild Turkey 101** as the flagship bourbon.
- The family’s deep roots at Wild Turkey, beginning with Jimmy’s hiring in 1954 and Eddie’s career since 1981.
- Recent releases by Eddie: *Longbranch*, *Master’s Keep Revival* and *Russell’s Reserve 2002*.
- A personal connection to the distillery through the Private Barrel Program.
The tone is celebratory, emphasizing tradition, craftsmanship, and community.

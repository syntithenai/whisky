# A Letter from Bruce Russell
Bruce Russell, National Ambassador for Wild Turkey Distilling Co., writes a heartfelt note celebrating the 101‑year milestone of the brand’s flagship bourbon, **Wild Turkey 101**. He highlights the combined experience of his grandfather Jimmy “Mimmy” Russell and father Eddie Russell—64 years each—underscoring their lifelong dedication to the craft. The letter recounts the family’s early days at Wild Turkey (Jimmy joined in 1954, Eddie began in 1981) and mentions recent releases such as *Wild Turkey Longbranch*, *Master’s Keep Revival* and *Russell’s Reserve 2002*. Russell invites readers to join in congratulating his family on their legacy of quality, mentorship and authenticity.

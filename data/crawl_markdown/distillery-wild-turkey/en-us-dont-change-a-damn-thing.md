# Wild Turkey’s New Global Campaign 2025

Wild Turkey launches a 2025 global campaign titled **“Don’t Change A Damn Thing.”** The campaign celebrates the brand’s century‑old, pre‑prohibition style bourbon and its unwavering commitment to tradition. It features a short film directed by Kiku Ohe that blends 8mm/35mm film stock, digital footage, historical photos and artifacts from Master Distiller Jimmy Russell’s seven decades of experience.

The page also highlights Wild Turkey’s product range – from the flagship **Wild Turkey Bourbon** to limited‑edition releases such as *Jimmy Russell’s Limited Edition 70th Anniversary Bourbon* and the *Rare Breed* series. The brand emphasizes its Kentucky heritage, craftsmanship, and a “no shortcuts” philosophy.

> *“When you know it’s right, don’t change a damn thing.”*

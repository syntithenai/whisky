# Wild Turkey Distillery

- Located on Wild Turkey Hill in Lawrenceburg, Kentucky.
- Open to visitors Wednesday‑Saturday 9:00‑5:00 am and Sunday 12:00‑4:00 pm; closed for major holidays.
- Tours cover grain selection (corn, barley, rye), distillation, barrel aging, and tasting flights of premium bourbons.
- Visitor centre is award‑winning, inspired by a Kentucky tobacco barn, with views of the Kentucky River.
- Historic rickhouses: seven‑story structures holding barrels; elevation influences flavor.
- Part of the Kentucky Bourbon Trail®.

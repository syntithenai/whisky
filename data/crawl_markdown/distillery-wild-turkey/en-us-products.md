# Wild Turkey Products
Wild Turkey offers a range of Kentucky Straight Bourbon and Rye whiskies, from core staples to limited‑edition releases. Core lines include the smooth **Wild Turkey Bourbon** and the mature **Wild Turkey 101 8‑Year‑Old Bourbon**. The brand also sells small‑batch selections, barrel‑proof variants (Rare Breed), and commemorative editions such as *Jimmy Russell’s 70th Anniversary* and *Generations*. Specialty collections feature flavored whiskies like **American Honey** and spicy **American Honey Sting**, as well as the Master’s Keep series of aged and finished bourbons.

All products are distilled and bottled in Lawrenceburg, KY, and sold online with a global shipping option.

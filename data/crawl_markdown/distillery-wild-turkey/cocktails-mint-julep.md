# Mint Julep

A classic bourbon cocktail featuring **Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon**. The recipe calls for half a part of the bourbon, half an ounce of simple syrup, and twelve mint leaves. Muddle the mint in a silver julep cup or double‑rocks glass, fill with crushed ice, pour in the remaining ingredients, lightly stir, and garnish with a sprig of mint.

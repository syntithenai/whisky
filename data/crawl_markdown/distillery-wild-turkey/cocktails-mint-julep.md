# Mint Julep

A classic bourbon cocktail featuring **Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon**. The recipe calls for half a part of the bourbon, half an ounce of simple syrup, and twelve fresh mint leaves. Muddle the mint in a silver julep cup or double‑rock glass, fill with crushed ice, pour in the remaining ingredients, lightly stir, and garnish with a sprig of mint.

The page also promotes Wild Turkey’s flagship 101 Bourbon as an icon that has been produced “the right way” for over 75 years. A list of other bourbon cocktails is provided for further exploration.

- **Ingredients**: ½ part Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon, ½ oz simple syrup, 12 mint leaves.
- **Method**: Muddle mint → add crushed ice → pour bourbon and syrup → stir lightly → garnish with mint sprig.

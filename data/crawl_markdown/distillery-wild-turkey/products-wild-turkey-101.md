# Wild Turkey Bourbon - Straight Bourbon Whiskey Overview

Wild Turkey is an iconic American bourbon brand known for its bold, flavorful whiskies. The company was founded in 1939 and has become one of the most popular bourbon brands in the United States.

## Key Facts
- **Distillery:** Buffalo Trace Distillery, Kentucky
- **Alcohol Content:** 50% ABV (100 proof)
- **Barrel Type:** New charred oak barrels
- **Mash Bill:** Corn-heavy with rye and malted barley
- **Age Statement:** Not aged for a specific number of years; typically rests in barrel for several years before bottling
- **Flavor Profile:** Rich, spicy, fruity notes with hints of vanilla, caramel, and oak
- **Popularity:** Consistently ranked among the top-selling bourbon brands in the U.S.

## Production Signals
- **Barrel Selection:** Wild Turkey selects barrels based on flavor potential rather than age, allowing for a more consistent taste across batches
- **Rapid Aging:** The brand emphasizes quick aging to capture fresh flavors before they dissipate
- **Consistency:** Despite being aged in new oak, Wild Turkey maintains a consistent flavor profile batch after batch

## Commercial Signals
- **Brand Recognition:** Widely recognized and beloved by bourbon enthusiasts
- **Market Presence:** Available in most states where bourbon is sold, with strong distribution networks
- **Marketing Strategy:** Focuses on bold marketing campaigns highlighting the brand's heritage and quality

## Risks / Unknowns
- **Supply Chain Challenges:** Like many distilleries, Wild Turkey faces challenges related to supply chain disruptions and labor shortages
- **Competition:** Intense competition from both domestic and international bourbon brands
- **Consumer Trends:** Adapting to changing consumer preferences towards craft spirits and premium offerings

**Wild Turkey Craft Overview**

- 75‑year‑old distillery on Wild Turkey Hill, Lawrenceburg, KY.
- Uses non‑GMO grains, proprietary yeast strain, limestone‑filtered Kentucky River water.
- Distillation in continuous stills; barrels are new American white oak with No. 4 “alligator” char.
- Minimum 5‑year aging (often 6–8+ years) in wooden rick houses dating to the 1890s.
- Bottling only by master distillers Jimmy & Eddie Russell.
- Tasting notes: deep auburn color, vanilla‑oak aroma with citrus/toffee hints; rich vanilla‑caramel taste, long full‑bodied finish.

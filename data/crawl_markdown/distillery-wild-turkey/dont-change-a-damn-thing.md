# Wild Turkey’s New Global Campaign 2025

Wild Turkey launches a 2025 global campaign titled **“Don’t Change A Damn Thing.”** The campaign celebrates the brand’s century‑old, pre‑prohibition style bourbon and its unwavering commitment to tradition. It features a short film directed by Kiku Ohe that blends 8mm/35mm film stock, digital footage, historical photos and artifacts from Master Distiller **Jimmy Russell**, who has led the distillery for over 70 years.

The page also highlights Wild Turkey’s product range – from flagship bourbons to rye whiskies, barrel‑proof releases, sherry‑cask finishes and limited editions – and invites visitors to explore behind‑the‑scenes content and the distillery’s heritage in Lawrenceburg, KY.

# Wild Turkey’s New Global Campaign 2025

Wild Turkey launches its 2025 global campaign, titled **“Don’t Change a Damn Thing.”** The campaign celebrates the brand’s century‑old recipe and tradition, featuring a film directed by Kiku Ohe that blends 8mm/35mm film stock, digital footage, historical photos and artifacts. Master Distiller Jimmy Russell—who has led the distillery for over 70 years—is highlighted as the embodiment of conviction and continuity.

The page also showcases Wild Turkey’s product range: classic bourbons (Wild Turkey 101, Wild Turkey Bourbon), rye whiskies (Wild Turkey 101 Rye, Wild Turkey Rye), specialty releases (Kentucky Spirit, Rare Breed, Master’s Keep series, American Honey, etc.), and a limited‑edition Jimmy Russell 70th Anniversary Bourbon. Visitors can explore behind‑the‑scenes content, learn about the distillery’s heritage, and access product details.

The site includes standard navigation links to products, cocktails, heritage, craft, and the distillery itself, along with language selection and legal notices.

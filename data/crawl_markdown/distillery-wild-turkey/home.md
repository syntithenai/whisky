# Wild Turkey Bourbon

Wild Turkey is a renowned Kentucky straight bourbon whiskey known for its bold flavor and heritage. Here are the key points:

- **Distillery**: Located in Lawrenceburg, Kentucky - America's Whiskey Town.
- **History**: Founded before Prohibition, maintaining its original recipe unchanged for over 100 years.
- **Varieties**:
  - *Wild Turkey Bourbon*: Smooth staple bourbon.
  - *Wild Turkey Rye*: Bold rye whiskey for classic cocktails.
  - *Master's Keep Series*: Hand-selected aged bourbons and finishes (e.g., Sherry, Rum).
  - *Rare Breed*: Barrel-proof expressions.
- **Cocktails**: Versatile in various bourbon drinks like Old Fashioned, Manhattan, Whiskey Sour, etc.

## Key Facts
- Proof: Typically around 100 proof (50% alcohol by volume).
- Aging: Aged for at least two years in new charred oak barrels.
- Flavor Profile: Rich, spicy, with notes of vanilla, caramel, and oak.

## Production Signals
- **Ingredients**: Corn, rye, and malted barley as primary grains.
- **Barrel Selection**: Carefully chosen barrels for aging.
- **Maturation**: Aged in new charred oak to develop flavor complexity.

## Commercial Signals
- Widely distributed across the U.S. and internationally.
- Strong brand presence in bars and liquor stores.
- Collaborations with mixologists for signature cocktails.

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited availability of rare expressions like Master's Keep.
- Market competition from other premium bourbons.
- Potential regulatory changes affecting bourbon production.

# Wild Turkey – Kentucky Straight Bourbon
Wild Turkey is a century‑old distillery in Lawrenceburg, KY that produces a range of straight bourbons and ryes. Their core line includes the flagship **Wild Turkey Bourbon** and **Wild Turkey 101**, while premium offerings such as the **Master’s Keep** series (Beacon, Triumph, One, 17 Year, Decades, Bottled‑in‑Bond, Cornerstone, Revival, Unforgotten, Voyage) showcase aged or finished expressions. The brand also offers barrel‑proof releases (**Rare Breed**), honey‑infused variants (**American Honey**, **American Honey Sting**) and a Jamaican rum‑cask finish (**Master’s Keep Voyage**). Wild Turkey emphasizes that its 100‑year‑old recipe has never changed, highlighting the expertise of master distillers Eddie and Bruce Russell.

The site promotes classic bourbon cocktails—Old Fashioned, Manhattan, Whiskey Sour, Mint Julep, etc.—and provides a brief history of the distillery’s heritage.

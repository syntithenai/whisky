# Wild Turkey Bourbon Products

Wild Turkey offers several iconic American bourbon whiskies:
- **Wild Turkey 101**: Extra strength bourbon at 50.5% ABV.
- **Wild Turkey Rare Breed**: Aged for over a decade, known for its smoothness and complexity.
- **Wild Turkey Single Barrel**: Bottled from a single barrel, offering unique flavor profiles.
- **Wild Turkey Small Batch**: Blended from select barrels for consistency and balance.

The brand emphasizes quality ingredients, traditional distillation methods, and aging in new charred oak barrels to create bold yet balanced bourbon flavors.

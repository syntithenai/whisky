# Wolf Ticket Cocktail

A refreshing bourbon‑based drink created by Eric Johnson of Sycamore Den, featuring Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon, simple syrup, peach liqueur, lemon juice and bitters.

**Ingredients**
- ½ part Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon
- ½ part simple syrup
- ½ part peach liqueur
- ¾ part fresh‑squeezed lemon juice
- Dash of bitters

**Method**
Shake all ingredients with ice, strain over ice into a rocks glass and garnish with a lemon peel.

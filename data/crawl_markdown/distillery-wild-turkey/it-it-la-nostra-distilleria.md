# Wild Turkey Distillery – Italian Page

- **Location**: Lawrenceburg, Kentucky (on a hill overlooking the Kentucky River). 
- **Visitor Experience**: Guided tour of the distillery and visitor center, including production walkthrough, tasting station, souvenir shop, and historic warehouses. 
- **Production Highlights**: Use of corn, barley, rye; aging in white oak barrels; Master Distiller’s refined process. 
- **Historical Touches**: Architecture inspired by a Kentucky tobacco barn; award‑winning visitor center; 75+ years of Jimmy Russell’s legacy. 
- **Operating Hours**: Wed–Sat 9 AM–5 PM, Sun 12 PM–4 PM (closed on major holidays). 
- **Contact**: WT‑Tourreservations@campari.com, 1417 Versailles Rd, Lawrenceburg, KY 40342.

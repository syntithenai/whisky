# Whiskey Sour

A classic bourbon cocktail featuring Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon, fresh lemon juice, simple syrup and optional egg white. Shake dry then with ice, strain into a coupe, and finish with bitters.

## Ingredients
- 2 oz Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon
- ¾ oz fresh lemon juice
- ½ oz simple syrup
- ½ oz egg white (optional)
- Dash of bitters for garnish

## Method
1. Dry‑shake all ingredients except ice.
2. Add ice and shake again.
3. Strain into a coupe glass.
4. Top with a dash of bitters.

*Recommended with Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon.*

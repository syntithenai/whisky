# Wild Turkey Celebrates Friendsgiving with Farmlink Project
Wild Turkey partners with the Farmlink Project to fight food waste, donating $101,000 and collaborating on an apple‑infused cocktail featuring Wild Turkey 101 Bourbon.

## Key Points
- **Donation**: $101,000 to support connecting farms with surplus crops to U.S. food banks.
- **Ambassador**: Andrew Rea (Binging with Babish) helps raise awareness.
- **Cocktail**: *Wild Apple Pie* – 2 parts Wild Turkey 101 Bourbon, ½ part ugly apple syrup, aromatic bitters, cinnamon, Chinese five‑spice, salt. Garnished with dehydrated apple slice and lemon twist.
- **Recipe**: Includes instructions for making the apple syrup and dehydrated slices.
- **Product Kit**: *Wild Orchard Bold Fashioned Deluxe Kit* includes a 1.75 L can of Wild Turkey bourbon.

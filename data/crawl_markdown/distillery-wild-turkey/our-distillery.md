# Wild Turkey Distillery Visit

- Located on Wild Turkey Hill in Lawrenceburg, Kentucky.
- Open to visitors Wednesday‑Saturday 9:00‑5:00 a.m., Sunday 12:00‑4:00 p.m.; closed Monday/Tuesday and major holidays.
- Tours cover grain selection, distillation, barrel aging, and history of the brand.
- Visitors can sample a tasting flight of premium bourbons and purchase items in the gift shop.
- The visitor centre is award‑winning, styled after a Kentucky tobacco barn, with views of the Kentucky River.
- Historic rickhouses (seven stories) showcase the aging process and barrel elevation effects.
- Part of the Kentucky Bourbon Trail®.

> *“There’s only one way to truly get to know real bourbon—go straight to the source.”*

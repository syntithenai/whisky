# Wild Turkey Whiskey Cocktails
Wild Turkey showcases a range of bourbon‑ and rye‑based cocktails, from classic staples like the Old Fashioned and Manhattan to seasonal twists such as the Boulevardier and Kentucky Devil. The site highlights its signature 101 proof bourbon as the ideal base for balanced, sweet‑spirit drinks.

## Featured Recipes
- **Old Fashioned** – Classic balance of spirit and sweetness using Wild Turkey 101.
- **Manhattan** – Bitter, sweet, refreshing modern take.
- **Boulevardier** – Negroni‑style with a bourbon twist.
- **Whiskey Sour** – Bourbon + lemon juice.
- **Mint Julep** – Traditional Southern refreshment.
- **Paper Plane**, **Sazerac**, **Brown Derby**, **Bourbon Rickey**, **Wolf Ticket**, **Rye Swizzle**, **Scofflaw**, **Kentucky Devil**, **New York** – a variety of themed cocktails.

# Wild Turkey Distillery

- Located on Wild Turkey Hill in Lawrenceburg, Kentucky.
- Visitors can tour the visitor centre, historic rickhouses and see the bourbon‑making process from grain selection to barrel aging.
- The distillery has been operating for over 75 years and is part of the Kentucky Bourbon Trail®.
- Tours are currently not offered; a pop‑up gift shop at 1525 Tyrone Road serves as an alternative visit point.
- Visitors can sample a tasting flight of premium bourbons and purchase bottles or branded merchandise in the gift shop.

> *“The new visitors’ centre is an architectural marvel inspired by a Kentucky tobacco barn, with an incredible view of the Kentucky River.”*

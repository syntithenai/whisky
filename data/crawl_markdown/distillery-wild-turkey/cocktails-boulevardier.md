# Boulevardier Cocktail

A twist on the Negroni that’s perfect for cooler weather.

**Ingredients**
- 1 ½ oz Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon
- 1 oz Campari®
- 1 oz sweet vermouth
- Garnish: cherry

**Method**
Add all liquid ingredients to a mixing glass, stir, strain over ice into a rocks glass and garnish with a cherry.

The recipe is part of Wild Turkey’s cocktail collection and highlights the brand’s flagship bourbon.

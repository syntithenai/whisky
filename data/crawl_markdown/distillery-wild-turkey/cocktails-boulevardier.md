# Boulevardier Cocktail

- **Ingredients**: 1 ½ oz Wild Turkey® 101 Bourbon, 1 oz Campari®, 1 oz sweet vermouth.
- **Garnish**: Cherry.
- **Method**: Stir all liquids in a mixing glass, strain over ice into a rocks glass, garnish with cherry.
- **Context**: A bourbon‑based twist on the classic Negroni, ideal for cooler weather.

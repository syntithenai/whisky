# 70th Anniversary of Jimmy Russell

Wild Turkey celebrates the 70‑year milestone of Master Distiller **Jimmy Russell**. The page chronicles his journey from a floor‑scrubber at age 18 in 1954 to the revered master distiller who has kept Wild Turkey’s bourbon “the right way” through turbulent decades. It highlights his steadfast refusal to alter the classic recipe, his pioneering releases (e.g., *Wild Turkey Rare Breed*, *Kentucky Spirit*, *American Honey*), and the honors he received—including induction into the Kentucky Bourbon Hall of Fame and a Kentucky Colonel title.

The narrative underscores Russell’s legacy of innovation, dedication, and global promotion of bourbon culture.

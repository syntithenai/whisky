# Wild Turkey Products

Wild Turkey offers a range of Kentucky Straight Bourbon and Rye whiskies, including core staples (Bourbon, Rye, 101), small‑batch selections, rare breed barrel‑proof releases, limited editions such as the *Kentucky Spirit* single‑barrel bourbon, commemorative 8‑year‑old 101‑proof bourbon for Master Distiller Jimmy Russell’s 70th anniversary, and a series of Master’s Keep super‑premium bottles (Beacon, One, Bottled in Bond, Cornerstone, Voyage, Revival, Decades). The brand also sells flavored whiskies like American Honey and American Honey Sting.

All products are crafted by the family of master distillers at Wild Turkey’s Kentucky distillery.

# American Single Malt Whiskey Commission

The U.S. government has officially added **American Single Malt Whiskey** to its regulations, defining it as:
- 100 % malted barley
- Distilled entirely at one distillery in the United States
- Matured in oak casks no larger than 700 L
- Distilled to ≤160 U.S. proof (80 % ABV)
- Bottled at ≥80 U.S. proof (40 % ABV)

This change, effective January 19 2025, protects producers and assures consumers of authenticity.

> *“This is a landmark ruling by TTB….”* – Steve Hawley, President

The Commission thanks the Alcohol & Tobacco Tax & Trade Bureau (TTB) and supporters for formalizing the category.

# McHenry Distillery Sustainability
McHenry Distillery, located on Tasmania’s rugged Peninsula, operates a fully renewable‑powered distillery that prioritises water stewardship, waste minimisation and circular packaging.

- **Energy** – Solar panels and hydro power supply all operations.
- **Water** – Five natural spring sources feed the mash; no transport or treatment is required.
- **Waste** – Grain wash by‑products are returned to local farmers as cattle feed.
- **Packaging** – All cans, bottles and outer cartons are 100 % recyclable aluminium or paper‑based.

These practices underpin every spirit produced, from gin to whisky, ensuring a low carbon footprint while supporting the local community and environment.

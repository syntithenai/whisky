# Butterfly Gin – Colour‑Shift Tasmanian Gin

- **Style**: Contemporary botanical gin (ABV 40%)
- **Botanical highlight**: Butterfly Pea Flower, giving a natural indigo colour that turns pink when the pH changes with tonic or citrus.
- **Flavour profile**: Subtle floral lift, soft citrus notes and a clean finish.
- **Best served**: With tonic/soda, lemon‑lime bitters, or as an oyster shooter.
- **Production**: Handcrafted in small batches on site at Port Arthur, Tasmania. No artificial colourants added.
- **Price**: AUD 85.00 for 700 ml (sold out).

> *“Infused with Butterfly Pea Flower, this handcrafted Tasmanian gin transforms from deep indigo to vivid pink with citrus or tonic, delivering elegance, intrigue, and botanical beauty in every glass.”* – Distiller’s Note

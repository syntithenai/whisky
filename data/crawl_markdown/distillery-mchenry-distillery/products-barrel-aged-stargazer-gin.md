## Source Excerpts
- Solera-aged since 2014 in American oak barrels, this bold, bourbon-kissed gin delivers layers of vanilla, warm spice, and gentle wood.
- At McHenry, we revived the tradition in 2014 through a solera system made up of 24 hand-selected American oak barrels, first seasoned with Russell Reserve bourbon and now dedicated to our gin.
- As the gin moves gently from barrel to barrel, it picks up delicate layers of lemonade on the nose, vanilla, warm spice, oak, and just a hint of whisky soul without ever losing its gin identity.
- Think of it as a continuous ageing process......instead of sitting in one barrel, the gin travels, half a barrel at a time, through a series of casks.

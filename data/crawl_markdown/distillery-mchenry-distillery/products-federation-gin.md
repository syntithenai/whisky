# Tasmanian Federation Gin

- **Style**: Contemporary Australian gin (ABV 40%)
- **Botanicals**: Kakadu plum, lemon myrtle, strawberry gum, mountain pepperleaf, wattle seed, cinnamon myrtle, celery top pine, quandong.
- **Flavor profile**: Native citrus, soft red fruit, spice, gentle herbaceous finish.
- **Serving suggestions**: G&T with mandarin tonic, Negroni, or over ice.
- **Origin story**: Commissioned for Parliament House; now exported worldwide.
- **Production**: Crafted & bottled on site at Port Arthur, Tasmania.

> *“Federation Gin brings together botanicals from every state and territory, Kakadu Plum from the NT, Quandong from WA, Lemon Myrtle from QLD, and more.”*

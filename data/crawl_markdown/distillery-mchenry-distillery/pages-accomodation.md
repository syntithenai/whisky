# McHenry Distillery Accommodation
McHenry Distillery offers two private cabins on its Port Arthur property:
- **Devil’s Lair Cabin** – a single‑bed, off‑grid eco‑retreat with panoramic mountain views and a stargazing skylight.
- **Brewers Cabin** – a double‑bed, fully‑furnished hideaway for families or friends, featuring a kitchen, fireplace, and deck.
Both cabins provide self‑contained amenities, free Wi‑Fi, and the option to pre‑order drinks and cheese boards. Guests can enjoy nearby attractions such as the Three Capes Walk, Port Arthur Historic Site, lavender farm, and local beaches.

# McHenry Distillery – Tasmania’s Southernmost Whisky & Gin Producer

McHenry Distillery, located on the Tasman Peninsula near Port Arthur, is a family‑run operation that crafts premium whisky, gin and other spirits using pristine spring water from five natural springs. The distillery emphasizes the influence of Tasmania’s cool, ocean‑tempered climate—similar to Scotland—on the maturation of its whiskies. Products include Butterfly Gin, Sloe Gin, Classic London Dry Gin, and a range of whisky liqueurs. The site also offers tours, stargazing experiences, and event hosting.

Key highlights:
- **Location**: 229 Radnor Road, Port Arthur, Tasmania
- **Water source**: Five pristine natural springs on the Tasman Peninsula
- **Awards**: Sloe Gin (2020 Australian Best), Classic Dry Gin (2014‑2015 ADSA awards), 10‑Year‑Old French Oak Whisky (2026 Gold World Whiskies Awards)
- **Visitor experiences**: Distillery & Brewery tour, stargazing nights, event hosting

# McHenry Distillery – Tasmania’s Southernmost Whisky & Gin

McHenry Distillery, located on the Tasman Peninsula near Port Arthur, produces premium whisky, gin and other spirits using pristine spring water from five natural springs. The distillery emphasizes a cool, ocean‑tempered climate that mirrors Scotland, allowing slow maturation for complex flavours. Products include Butterfly Gin, Sloe Gin, Classic London Dry Gin, and Whisky Liqueur, all available online with Australian pricing.

The site also promotes tours, stargazing experiences, event hosting, and contract distilling services. Awards highlight the quality of their gins (e.g., 2020 Australia’s Best Sloe Gin). The distillery is family‑run, founded by William Fraser McHenry, who moved from Sydney/USA to Tasmania.

Key contact: 229 Radnor Road, Port Arthur, TAS 7182; phone 0493 357 723.

# McHenry Distillery – Tasmania’s Southernmost Whisky & Gin

McHenry Distillery, located on the Tasman Peninsula near Port Arthur, produces premium whisky, gin and other spirits using water from five pristine natural springs. The distillery emphasizes a cool, ocean‑tempered climate that mirrors Scotland, allowing slow maturation for complex flavour profiles. Products include Butterfly Gin, Sloe Gin, Classic London Dry Gin, and Whisky Liqueur, all available online with prices ranging from AUD 45 to 99.

The site offers tours, stargazing experiences, event hosting, and contract distilling services. It highlights awards such as *Australia’s Best Sloe Gin* (2020) and *Best Navy Strength Gin* (2014‑15). The founder, William Fraser McHenry, transitioned from a corporate career to craft spirits in Tasmania.

# Tasmanian Peated Single Malt Whisky

- **Price**: $165 AUD (500 ml) – sold out.
- **ABV**: 46%
- **Peat source**: Scottish peat, 35 PPM.
- **Water**: Natural Tasmanian spring water.
- **Flavor profile**: Salted caramel, vanilla, caramel fudge, raisin, soft spice, coastal smoke; long, gently smoky and spiced finish.

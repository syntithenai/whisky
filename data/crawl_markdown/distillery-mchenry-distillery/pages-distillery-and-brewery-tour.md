# Distillery & Brewery Tour
- Guided walk through McHenry’s brewery and distillery, often led by founder Bill McHenry.
- Learn beer brewing, wash creation, whisky distillation, barrel aging, and tasting in the underground vault.
- Includes gin tasting with Australia’s oldest solera‑aged gin and a free Glencairn glass.
- Tour times: 12 PM & 2 PM on weekdays; 45 min duration; $60.00 fee.
- Contact: 229 Radnor Road, Port Arthur, TAS 7182, Tel 0493 357 723, Email contact@mchenrydistillery.com.au.

# Contract Distilling & Private Label Spirits
McHenry Tasmania offers full‑service contract distilling, brewing and private‑label manufacturing in Port Arthur, Tasmania. Services include recipe development, ingredient sourcing, compliance, logistics, production, bottling, canning, labelling and packaging for gin, vodka, whisky, RTDs, soft drinks and beer. The distillery uses spring water sourced directly from beneath its land, ensuring a clean finish and a unique story.

## Key Offerings
- **Planning & Production Support** – recipe development, sourcing, compliance, logistics.
- **Batch Flexibility** – pilot runs to full‑scale commercial production.
- **In‑house Bottling & Canning** – boutique to retail volumes.
- **R&D Expertise** – custom formulations for gin (classic, botanical, navy strength, seasonal), vodka (clean, smooth, character‑driven), whisky (new‑make or mature stock), beer, RTDs and soft drinks.

## Why Choose McHenry?
- Pure Tasmanian spring water.
- All‑on‑site production for tighter quality control and confidentiality.
- Experienced family team with over a decade of expertise.
- Flexible collaboration model.

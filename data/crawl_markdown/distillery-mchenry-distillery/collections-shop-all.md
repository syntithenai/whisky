# McHenry Distillery Online Store

McHenry Distillery is Tasmania’s southernmost distillery, brewery and bottling operation. The online shop offers a range of products including whisky (10‑year American Oak, French Oak, Peated Single Malt, Stout Finished), gin (Classic London Dry, Butterfly, Damson, Sloe, Stargazer Barrel‑Aged, Federation), vodka, beer, pre‑mixed drinks, tonic waters and merchandise. Prices vary from $3.50 AUD to $500 AUD.

The distillery emphasises local production: all ingredients are sourced on site, water is drawn from pristine springs, and no outsourcing occurs. The shop lists product availability (31 in stock, 11 out of stock) and provides customer reviews for each item.

Contact details: 229 Radnor Road, Port Arthur, TAS, Australia; phone 0493 357 723; email contact@mchenrydistillery.com.au.

# Star Gazing Experience

McHenry Distillery offers a guided stargazing event in Tasmania, featuring professional telescopes, astrophotography tips, and a complimentary hot drink (Sloe Gin Hot Toddy). The experience runs every Thursday from 6:30 pm to 8:30 pm starting June 1, with special accommodation options nearby. Dark skies on the Tasman Peninsula provide excellent views of the Milky Way and Aurora Australis during winter months.

**Key details**
- Location: Port Arthur, Tasmania (dark skies, remote)
- Timing: Thursdays 6:30‑8:30 pm (winter season)
- Activities: telescope viewing, astrophotography guidance, hot drink
- Cancellation policy: full refund >5 days, 50% refund 4–2 days, no refund <48 h
- Weather contingency: indoor projection session if sky is cloudy
- Directions: follow signs up STEEP hill, parking near Gin Pavilion
- Contact: 0493 357 723 / contact@mchenrydistillery.com.au

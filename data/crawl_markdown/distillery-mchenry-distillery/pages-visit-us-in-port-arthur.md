# McHenry Distillery – Port Arthur Cellar Door

- **Location**: 229 Radnor Road, Port Arthur, Tasmania (5 min from the historic site). 
- **Access**: A 2 km single‑lane dirt track through forest; marked by a giant inflatable beer can. 
- **Experience**: Guided whisky & gin tasting with pure Tasmanian spring water and maritime climate maturation. Optional cheese board for $40.
- **Facilities**: Tesla destination chargers, wheelchair accessible, family & dog friendly, fireplace in winter, open doors/umbrellas in summer.
- **Contact**: 0493 357 723 / contact@mchenrydistillery.com.au.

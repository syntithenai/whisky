# Classic London Dry Gin

McHenry Distillery’s flagship gin is a **London‑dry inspired Tasmanian gin** (ABV 40%) crafted with juniper, citrus peel, coriander seed, cardamom, star anise and orris root. It offers bright citrus, warm spice, smooth juniper and floral lift, ideal for G&T, martini or sipping on its own.

- **Size:** 700 ml
- **Price:** $80.00 AUD (sold out)
- **Origin:** Port Arthur, Tasmania
- **Distilled & bottled on site**
- **Key message:** A quiet‑confidence spirit that never competes but delivers every time.

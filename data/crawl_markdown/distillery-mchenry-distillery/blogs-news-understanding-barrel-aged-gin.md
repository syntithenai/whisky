# Understanding Barrel‑Aged Gin

McHenry Distillery’s *Stargazer* is a solera‑aged gin that blends the bright botanicals of classic gin with the depth and oak character of whisky. Matured in 24 American oak barrels that were first seasoned with Russell’s Reserve bourbon, each bottle contains a small portion of gin that has been ageing since the system began in 2014.

- **Solera system**: continuous movement through stacked barrels creates consistency and complexity.
- **Oak influence**: vanilla, warm spice, soft tannin, richer mouthfeel, subtle caramel notes, longer finish.
- **Versatility**: neat, on‑ice with orange peel, Old Fashioned, espresso martini.

The name *Stargazer* reflects founder Bill McHenry’s fascination with science and space, hinting at the meticulous, patient approach to distillation.

# McHenry Distillery – Hobart Locations

McHenry Distillery operates three visitor sites in Hobart:
- **Spirited Tasmania** (Salamanca Arts Centre, Battery Point) – open Mon‑Sat 10 AM–4 PM.
- **Brooke Street Pier** – open Fri‑Sun 1 PM–6 PM, waterfront tasting venue.
- **Salamanca Market stall** – every Saturday 8:30 AM–3 PM.

All locations showcase award‑winning Tasmanian whisky and gin, offer guided tastings, retail sales, and a chance to meet the distillery team. The distillery also highlights its use of pristine spring water from Tasmania’s Turrakana region and acknowledges traditional custodians of the land.

**Shipping & Returns Policy – McHenry Distillery**

- Orders are shipped via Australia Post, dispatched once per week (Thursday or Friday) due to remote location.
- Interstate orders use air freight; express shipping is unavailable.
- Damaged items can be reported within 7 days for replacement, store credit, or refund. Items must not be consumed beyond fault identification.
- No returns for change of mind; shipping costs are non‑refundable unless the item is faulty or incorrect.

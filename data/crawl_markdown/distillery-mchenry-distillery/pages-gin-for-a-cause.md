# Gin For Cause – McHenry Distillery

McHenry Distillery’s *Gin for a Cause* initiative partners with charities, community groups and local sports teams to create limited‑edition gins that raise funds and awareness for important causes. Current collaborations include the Royal Australian Corps of Transport and Derwent Hockey Club, while past projects have supported Cystic Fibrosis Tasmania (65 Roses Gin) and One Family at a Time in Cambodia.

Each bottle sold contributes directly to the partnered cause – e.g., $30 per 65 Roses Gin goes to Cystic Fibrosis Tasmania. The page invites new partners to collaborate, providing contact details and encouraging community involvement.

## Source Excerpts
- Current Collaborations Royal Australian Corps of Transport, Honouring Service & Dedication We are honoured to collaborate with the Royal Australian Corps of Transport, producing a custom gin that pays tribute to the dedication and service of Australia’s transport personnel.
- This exclusive release is a symbol of respect and appreciation for those who serve, providing a unique way for supporters to celebrate their commitment while enjoying a premium Tasmanian gin.
- Recent collaborations include Derwent Hockey Club These initiatives help keep local sports thriving, ensuring young athletes have the resources they need to train, compete, and grow.
- We give back $30 from each bottle directly to Cystic Fibrosis Tasmania (CFT) to help provide the critical support services to individuals and families affected by Cystic Fibrosis.
- One Family at a Time – Helping Cambodian Families Through our partnership with One Family at a Time, a registered Australian charity working in Cambodia, McHenry Distillery has produced a limited-edition gin to support some of Cambodia’s most disadvantaged families.
- This initiative is a small but meaningful way to bring support and hope to communities in need.

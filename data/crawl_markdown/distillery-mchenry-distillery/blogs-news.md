# McHenry Distillery News

- **Barrel‑Aged Gin** – *Stargazer* is a solera‑aged gin, matured since 2014 in 24 American oak barrels and rested in bourbon‑seasoned casks. It develops vanilla and warm notes.
- **Awards** – The distillery won Gold for Best Australian Single Malt at the 2026 World Whiskies Awards for its 10‑Year‑Old French Oak, crafted and matured in Port Arthur.
- **Dogs & Liqueurs** – Daisy and Sammy, the distillery’s dogs, inspired two liqueur products: Daisy’s Whisky Liqueur and Sammy’s Whisky Cream Liqueur.
- **Sales Update** – The Fresh Oak 10‑Year‑Old whisky sold out in record time (July 2025).
- **New Website & Note** – A new website launch and the Distiller’s Note blog highlight the distillery’s focus on place, water quality, and barrel ageing.

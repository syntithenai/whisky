# McHenry Distillery News

McHenry Distillery’s news feed highlights recent achievements and new releases:

* **Stargazer** – a solera‑aged barrel gin, matured since 2014 in 24 American oak barrels in Port Arthur. It rests in bourbon‑seasoned casks, yielding vanilla and warm spice notes.
* **10‑Year‑Old French Oak whisky** – won Gold for Best Australian Single Malt at the 2026 World Whiskies Awards.
* **Dog‑inspired liqueurs** – Daisy’s Whisky Liqueur and Sammy’s Whisky Cream Liqueur, named after the distillery dogs.
* **July Newsletter** – announces the record‑time sell‑out of Fresh Oak 10‑Year‑Old whisky.

The site also promotes its new website, sustainability initiatives, and visitor experiences such as tours and stargazing.

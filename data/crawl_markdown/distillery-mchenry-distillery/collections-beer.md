# McHenry Distillery – Tasmanian Beer Collection

McHenry Distillery offers a small range of handcrafted beers, including Session Ale, Pilsner, Pale Ale, Dark Ale and a limited‑edition Barrel Aged Dark McAle. Prices vary from $5.50 to $105 AUD per unit, with some items currently sold out.

## Key Points
- **Session Ale** – 5 % ABV, 1 review (5/5).
- **Pilsner** – Sold out.
- **Pale Ale** – 5 % ABV, 1 review (5/5).
- **Dark Ale** – 5 % ABV, price range $6.50–$105 AUD.
- **Limited Edition Barrel Aged Dark McAle** – 4.2 % ABV, 5 reviews (4.2/5). Price range $7.50–$80 AUD.

The distillery is located in Port Arthur, Tasmania, and emphasizes local spring water and pure ingredients.

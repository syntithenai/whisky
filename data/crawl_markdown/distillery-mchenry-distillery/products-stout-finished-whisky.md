# Tasmanian Stout Cask Finished Single Malt Whisky

- **ABV**: 46%
- **Maturation**: Final stage in stout‑aged barrels (ex‑whisky)
- **Tasting notes**: roasted malt, dark chocolate, toasted caramel, soft spice; long, smooth finish with lingering stout warmth.
- **Production**: Single malt aged in stout‑seasoned barrels, then finished back in whisky cask. Limited release, 500 ml bottle sold out.
- **Distillery**: McHenry Distillery, Port Arthur, Tasmania.

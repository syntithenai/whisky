# McHenry Distillery – Founder Story

- **Founder**: Bill McHenry, former pharma executive with a background in chemistry and agricultural science.
- **Location**: 100‑acre property on Tasmania’s rugged Peninsula (Port Arthur), featuring five pristine natural springs and world‑class clean air.
- **Mission**: Create a family legacy of high‑quality whisky and gin using local water, climate, and traditional copper pot stills.
- **Family Involvement**: Bill’s wife Alison, children Hamish, Katie, Lucy, and long‑time staff contribute to operations, legal, marketing, and product development.
- **Production**: On‑site brewery produces wash from spring water; two copper pot stills (1,500 L & 3,000 L) craft whisky and gin. Spirits mature in Tasmanian air, with careful cask selection for depth and complexity.
- **Brand Positioning**: Southernmost distillery/brewery globally, emphasising purity, craftsmanship, and a “family legacy” narrative.

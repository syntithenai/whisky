# McHenry Distillery Event Spaces

McHenry Distillery, Tasmania’s southernmost distillery, offers a range of venues for corporate events, weddings and exclusive gatherings:
- **Gin Pavilion** – up to 150 guests, panoramic views, indoor‑outdoor flow.
- **The Vault** – underground barrel‑lined space for 16 guests, ideal for intimate meetings and whisky tastings.
- **The Observatory** – former wheat silo on Mount Arthur, capacity 8, stargazing and private retreats.
- **The Cellar Door** – relaxed social venue with bar and beer taps, seating for 100.

All spaces are paired with premium Tasmanian spirits (whisky, gin, craft beers) and can be customised with local catering partners. The distillery is located 90 min from Hobart and offers helicopter or bus transport options.

For more details, download the Event Guide or contact McHenry Distillery directly.

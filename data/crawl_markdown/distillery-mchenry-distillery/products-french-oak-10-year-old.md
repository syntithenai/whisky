# Tasmanian French Oak 10 Year Old Whisky

- **Style**: Single‑malt whisky, aged 10 years in ex‑tawny French oak casks.
- **ABV**: 46 % (originally 61 %).
- **Price**: AUD $145.00 (regular $131.00) for a 500 ml bottle; 200 ml variant sold out.
- **Tasting notes**: Sweet dark candied sugar, caramel, spice, plump berries, chestnuts, stone fruit, long warming finish with French oak depth.

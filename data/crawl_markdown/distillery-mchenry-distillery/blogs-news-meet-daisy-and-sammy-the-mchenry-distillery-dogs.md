# Meet Daisy and Sammy – The McHenry Distillery Dogs

McHenry Distillery, Tasmania’s southernmost distillery, introduces its two beloved dogs, **Daisy** and **Sammy**. These canines have been part of the distillery since day one, greeting guests, joining family tastings, and keeping watch over the stills on the windswept Port Arthur property.

The post highlights the importance of family at McHenry, acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the land, and provides contact details for the distillery.

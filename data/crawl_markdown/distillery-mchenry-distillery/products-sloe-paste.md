# Tasmanian Sloe Berry Paste

- **Price**: $9.00 AUD (regular and sale price) per jar.
- **Sizes**: 130 g (available), 280 g (discontinued).
- **Shelf life**: 2 years.
- **Ingredients**: Repurposed sloe berries left over from McHenry’s award‑winning Sloe Gin, steeped for >18 months in Classic Gin.
- **Purpose**: 100 % of proceeds support Frank, a family member with a complex disability who helps with packaging and delivery.
- **Pairings**: Cheese boards, charcuterie, roast meats.
- **Production note**: Made by Meagan with help from her son Frank; crafted in Tasmania.

The paste is marketed as a heartfelt gift that lasts two years and supports community care.

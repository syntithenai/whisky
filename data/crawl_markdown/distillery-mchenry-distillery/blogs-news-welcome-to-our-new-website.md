# McHenry Distillery New Website Launch

McHenry Distillery, Tasmania’s southernmost distillery, has unveiled a redesigned website that mirrors the brand’s ethos of place‑based craftsmanship. The site showcases their full whisky and gin ranges with tasting notes, ready‑to‑drink mixers, accommodation options, tours, and collaborations—all built to evoke the clarity of Tasmanian spring water, cool hill ageing, coastal winds, and family heritage.

Key highlights:
- **Storytelling focus**: Every page reflects the distillery’s sense of place rather than just e‑commerce.
- **Product showcase**: Whisky, gin, vodka, beer, mixers, and gifts are presented with immersive backstories.
- **Experiences**: Booking for tours, stargazing, accommodation, and events.
- **Sustainability & heritage**: Acknowledgement of traditional custodians and commitment to local water sourcing.

The launch is positioned as a continuation of the McHenry legacy in digital form.

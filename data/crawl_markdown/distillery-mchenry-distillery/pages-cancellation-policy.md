# Cancellation Policy

McHenry Distillery’s cancellation policy covers tours, stargazing events and general bookings.

- **Tours**: Fully refundable; cancel or reschedule by phone/email before the tour time. No extra cost.
- **Stargazing**:
  - 5+ days before: full refund
  - 2–4 days before: 50 % refund
  - Within 48 h: no refund
  - Weather‑related cancellations are handled with an indoor session.
- Contact details: 0493 357 723 / contact@mchenrydistillery.com.au.

The page also includes standard legal notices, links to other site sections and a brief acknowledgment of the Traditional Custodians of Tasmania.

---

## Plain‑text summary
McHenry Distillery offers a fully refundable cancellation policy for tours. Stargazing events have tiered refunds based on how far in advance the cancellation occurs: full refund 5+ days prior, 50 % between 2–4 days, and no refund within 48 h. Weather cancellations are handled with an indoor session. Contact phone/email provided.

# McHenry Distillery Wins Gold at the 2026 World Whiskies Awards

McHenry Distillery has earned a **Gold Medal for Best Australian Single Malt Whisky** for its **10‑Year‑Old French Oak** release. The award highlights the distillery’s decade‑long commitment to quality, patience and Tasmanian terroir.

- **Product:** 10‑Year‑Old French Oak (sold out) and ongoing **Signature French Oak Single Malt**.
- **Maturation:** 10 years in tight‑grained French oak, delivering caramel, spice, subtle smoke and dried fruit.
- **Recognition:** Gold medal places McHenry among Australia’s top single malt producers.
- **Availability:** 10‑Year‑Old is sold out; Signature French Oak remains available online and at the Port Arthur cellar door.

The article also outlines the distillery’s philosophy, production process, and community engagement.

## Long Description
# McHenry Distillery

McHenry Distillery is a family‑run operation located on the Tasman Peninsula near Port Arthur.  Using pristine spring water from five natural springs, the distillery crafts a range of spirits that showcase Tasmania’s cool, ocean‑tempered climate – a terroir often compared to Scotland.

## Core Products
- **Butterfly Gin** – crisp, wild and pure London‑dry style.
- **Sloe Gin** – award‑winning 2020 Australian Best Sloe Gin.
- **Classic London Dry Gin** – highly rated, classic dry gin.
- **Whisky Liqueur** – a versatile whisky‑based liqueur.
- **Stargazer Barrel‑Aged Gin** – solera‑aged in American oak and rested in bourbon‑seasoned casks for vanilla and warm spice notes.
- **10‑Year‑Old French Oak Whisky** – matured in French oak, Gold winner at the 2026 World Whiskies Awards.
- **Dog‑inspired Liqueurs** – Daisy’s Whisky Liqueur & Sammy’s Whisky Cream Liqueur.

## Visitor Experience
The distillery offers guided tours of the brewery and distillation facilities, stargazing nights that pair astronomy with craft, and event hosting.  All experiences highlight the natural spring water source and the influence of Tasmania’s climate on maturation.

## Awards & Recognition
- Sloe Gin – 2020 Australian Best Sloe Gin
- Classic London Dry Gin – ADSA awards 2014‑15
- 10‑Year‑Old French Oak Whisky – Gold, World Whiskies Awards 2026

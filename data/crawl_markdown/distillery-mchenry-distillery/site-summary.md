## Long Description
# McHenry Distillery

**Location & Heritage**
- Situated on the Tasman Peninsula near Port Arthur (229 Radnor Road). Founded by William Fraser McHenry after relocating from Sydney/USA.
- Family‑run operation that blends traditional distilling with modern craft brewing.
- Uses five natural springs for pure spring water, a key ingredient in all spirits and beer.

**Experience & Services**
- Tours, stargazing events and contract distilling are offered.
- All products are bottled on site; the distillery complies with Australian liquor licensing.
- The brand emphasizes sustainability: spring water sourcing, local ingredients, and community support (e.g., proceeds from sloe paste).

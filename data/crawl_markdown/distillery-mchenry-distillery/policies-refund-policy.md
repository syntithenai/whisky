**Refund Policy – McHenry Tasmania**

- 30‑day return window from receipt.
- Items must be unused, in original packaging with tags and proof of purchase.
- Returns sent to a specified address; a shipping label is provided upon acceptance.
- Non‑returnable items: perishable goods, custom orders, personal care products, hazardous materials, sale items, gift cards.
- EU customers have an additional 14‑day cooling‑off period.
- Refunds processed within 10 business days to original payment method; contact if delayed beyond 15 days.

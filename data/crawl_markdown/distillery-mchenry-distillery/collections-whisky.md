# McHenry Distillery Whisky Collection

McHenry Distillery offers a range of Tasmanian whiskies crafted from five spring water sources in the cool climate of the Tasman Peninsula. The collection includes American Oak, French Oak, Peated Single Malt, Stout‑Finished, and Whisky Liqueur variants, each with its own age statement or finish. Prices vary from AUD 45 to AUD 165.

- **American Oak**: 10‑year (AUD 65–131), 12‑year (sold out, AUD 165)
- **French Oak**: 10‑year (AUD 53–109)
- **Peated Single Malt**: AUD 75–165
- **Stout‑Finished**: AUD 69–146
- **Whisky Liqueur**: AUD 45–99

All products are available for purchase online, with shipping and return policies outlined on the site.

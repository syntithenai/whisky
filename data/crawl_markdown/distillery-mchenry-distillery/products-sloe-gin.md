# McHenry Distillery Sloe Gin

- **Product**: Traditional English‑style sloe gin made from hand‑foraged Tasmanian sloe berries.
- **Base spirit**: McHenry Classic Gin, steeped for over 18 months.
- **ABV**: 23%.
- **Price**: AUD 99.00 (700 ml).
- **Availability**: Currently sold out.
- **Awards**: Winner of Australia’s Best Sloe Gin at the 2020 World Gin Awards.
- **Serving suggestions**: Neat, over ice, with bubbles or mulled with apple juice.
- **Production note**: Berries are collected each harvest by the whole team to ensure ripeness; steeping is a ritual that yields deep colour and complex fruit character.

## Key facts
- Wild Tasmanian sloe berries hand‑foraged from back roads and hedgerows.
- Steeped in McHenry Classic Gin for >18 months.
- Rich, deeply coloured, fruit‑forward profile with tart berry notes and soft sweetness.
- Award‑winning product, best suited to all seasons.

## Production & flavour details
- Traditional English‑style sloe gin.
- Steeped in a 23% ABV base spirit.
- Tart berry, soft sweetness, deep fruit complexity.
- Best enjoyed neat, over ice, with bubbles or mulled with apple juice.

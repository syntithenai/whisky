# McHenry Distillery Cellar Door

- **Location**: 229 Radnor Road, Port Arthur, Tasmania – the southernmost distillery in Australia.
- **Experience**: Guided tasting of handcrafted whisky and gin made with pure Tasmanian spring water and aged in a cool maritime climate. Optional cheese board for $60.
- **Facilities**: Tesla Destination Chargers (14 kW), wheelchair accessible, child & dog friendly, fireplace in winter, open doors in summer.
- **Booking & Cancellation**:
  - 5+ days before: full refund
  - 4–2 days before: 50% refund
  - <48 h: no refund
- **Stargazing**: Indoor session with projected night sky if weather is poor.
- **Contact**: 0493 357 723, contact@mchenrydistillery.com.au.
- **Cultural Note**: Acknowledges the Traditional Custodians of the Turrakana region on Lutruwita (Tasmania).

> *“Enjoy a guided whisky and gin tasting… each made with pure spring water and matured in Tasmania’s cool, ocean‑cooled climate.”*

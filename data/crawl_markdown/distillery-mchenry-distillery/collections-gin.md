# McHenry Distillery – Tasmanian Gin Collection

McHenry Distillery offers a range of hand‑crafted, award‑winning gins sourced from wild Tasmanian botanicals. The collection includes:
- **Classic London Dry Gin**
- **Butterfly Gin**
- **Damson Gin**
- **Sloe Gin**
- **Stargazer Barrel‑Aged Gin**
- **Federation Gin**
- **Puer Distilled Vodka** (listed under gin & vodka)

All products are available in Tasmania and sold via the Shopify store.

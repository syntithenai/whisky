# Modern Slavery Statement 2021 – Anglia Maltings (Holdings) Limited

- **Purpose**: Demonstrates compliance with UK modern slavery legislation and outlines the Group’s commitment to ethical business practices.
- **Scope**: Covers the year ending 31 December 2021 for AMH and its subsidiaries: Crisp Malt, Crisp Malt GmbH, Crisp Malt SP Z OO, EDME Limited, Micronized Food Products Limited (MFP).
- **Key Points**:
  - Low risk of slavery within own operations; all employees legally employed and paid above minimum wage.
  - Potential supply‑chain risks identified for labour‑intensive food ingredients; mitigated through supplier enquiries and ethical audits.
  - Governance: Board responsibility, whistleblowing mechanisms (Speakup@crispmalt.com), and alignment with related policies (Anti‑Bribery, Equality & Diversity, etc.).
  - Values emphasise quality, respect, teamwork, transparency, efficiency, and a commitment to ending modern slavery.

- **Approval**: Board approval on 25 October 2022; statement approved by the Board in June 2022.

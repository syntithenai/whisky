# E&J Gallo to Acquire Four Roses Bourbon
E&J Gallo, a family‑owned wine and spirits company, has agreed to purchase the Lawrenceburg, Kentucky distillery and Four Roses bourbon brand from Japan’s Kirin Holdings for roughly **$764 million** (subject to U.S. antitrust approval). The deal includes an additional $50 million contingent on sales targets in the first year after closing. Kirin will receive up to $1 billion if it sells at a higher price, and the transaction is expected to close in Q2 2026.

**Key points**
- Gallo’s Spirits of Gallo division already markets New Amsterdam vodka and distributes other brands.
- Four Roses operations will remain unchanged; master distiller **Brent Elliott** stays on.
- Kirin’s motivation is to refocus on healthcare businesses.

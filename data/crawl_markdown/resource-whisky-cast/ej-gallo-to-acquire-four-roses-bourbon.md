Home - Stories - E&J Gallo to Acquire Four Roses Bourbon E&J Gallo to Acquire Four Roses Bourbon The family-owned E&J Gallo has agreed to buy the Four Roses distillery and Bourbon brand from Japan’s Kirin Holdings for approximately $764 million, subject to U.S. antitrust approval. Kirin had been seeking to sell the Lawrenceburg, Kentucky-based distillery as part of its push to refocus the company on its health care businesses. Privately-held Gallo has been expanding into spirits through its Spirits of Gallo division, which includes New Amsterdam vodka and distribution agreements with Whyte & Mackay and other brands.

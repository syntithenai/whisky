# E&J Gallo to Acquire Four Roses Bourbon
E&J Gallo, a family‑owned wine and spirits company, has agreed to purchase the Four Roses distillery and bourbon brand from Japan’s Kirin Holdings for roughly **$764 million** (subject to U.S. antitrust approval). The deal includes an additional $50 million contingent on sales performance in the first year after closing. Kirin will receive up to $1 billion if it sells the brand, having bought it in 2002. The transaction is expected to close in Q2 2026, with Gallo maintaining current operations and master distiller **Brent Elliott** remaining at the Lawrenceburg, Kentucky facility.

# Diageo to Close Clynelish Visitors Centre
Diageo announced the closure of the visitor centre at Clynelish Distillery after a recent review, despite having invested £185 million in renovations just three years earlier. The decision does not affect whisky production, which will continue unchanged.

- **Location**: Scottish Highlands, near Brora Distillery.
- **Context**: Part of Diageo’s broader cost‑cutting strategy under CEO Sir Dave Lewis and CFO Nic Jhangiani.
- **Other Impacts**: Similar reviews are underway for other visitor centres; no specifics released.

Diageo has also idled Teaninich, paused Roseisle Maltings, halted several U.S. distillery operations until mid‑2026, and will close its Crown Royal bottling plant in Ontario next month.

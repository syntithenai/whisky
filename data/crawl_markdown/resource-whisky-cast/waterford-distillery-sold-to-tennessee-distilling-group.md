# Waterford Distillery Sold to Tennessee Distilling Group

Nearly 18 months after closing, Waterford’s plant and intellectual property were bought for **€6 million** by the Tennessee Distilling Group (TDG), which owns a contract distillery in Columbia, Tennessee. TDG will not acquire the existing stockpile of whiskey (≈ €100 million) but plans to resume distillation under the Waterford brand and for contract clients, with a retail operation forthcoming.

The original Waterford Distillery, founded by Mark Reynier in 2015, aimed at terroir‑based single malt Irish whiskey. After receivership in November 2024, the remaining inventory is being sold through Prestige Casks alongside casks from Powerscourt Distillery. Reynier has expressed interest in buying some of these casks but cannot use the Waterford name.

The new Waterford whiskey will be more approachable and priced lower than the original line.

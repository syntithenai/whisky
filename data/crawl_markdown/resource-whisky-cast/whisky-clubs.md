# Whisky Clubs – WhiskyCast
Whisky clubs are highlighted as a community way to share new whiskies. Each month, the site selects a *Whisky Club of the Month* in partnership with Glencairn Crystal; winners receive branded nosing glasses. The page lists numerous clubs worldwide, grouped by region (International, United States, Canada, Europe/Middle East, Asia‑Pacific). Clubs must have an online presence and active email for inquiries. A contact form is provided to nominate a club.

## Key Points
- Monthly *Whisky Club of the Month* award with Glencairn glasses.
- Extensive list of clubs by country/region.
- Nomination requires website/social media and active email.
- Responsible drinking reminder.

# Alcohol Content Measurement (Japanese)

This PDF describes a laboratory method for determining the total alcohol and ethanol‑free alcohol in a sample. The procedure involves:
1. **Sample preparation** – mixing a small amount of material with water, then dividing into portions.
2. **Total alcohol determination** – using a 1 % solution of potassium dichromate, adding sulfuric acid, heating, and measuring the resulting color change (reference to standard 3‑9‑3).
3. **Ethanol‑free alcohol determination** – similar steps but with additional steps such as adding water, controlling temperature, and adjusting pH.
4. **Calculations** – formulas for total alcohol (% w/w) and ethanol‑free alcohol (% w/w) based on the measured values.

The document also includes a brief note on how to calculate the overall alcohol content from the two measurements.

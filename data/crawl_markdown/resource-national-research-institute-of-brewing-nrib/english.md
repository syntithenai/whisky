# National Research Institute of Brewing (NRIB)

The NRIB is Japan’s sole national research institute dedicated to alcoholic beverages. Founded on **May 9, 1904** in Tokyo under the Ministry of Finance, it conducts advanced analysis, fundamental and applied brewing research, and disseminates knowledge to support fair taxation, industry development, and public awareness.

Key outputs include:
- Annual *Japan Sake Awards* (e.g., 2025 list of gold‑prize winners)
- Research results on **Aspergillus oryzae** databases
- Educational materials such as the *Glossary of Sake Label Terms*, *Sake Book – How to Enjoy Japanese Sake*, and a *Handy Storage Guide*.
- Lecture videos and judging sheets for the Honkaku Shochu & Awamori contests.

The institute’s website (English version) provides links to research reports, resources on sake and shochu, and contact information.

# National Research Institute of Brewing (NRIB)

The NRIB is Japan’s sole national research institute dedicated to alcoholic beverages. Founded on May 9 1904 in Tokyo under the Ministry of Finance, it conducts advanced analysis, fundamental and applied brewing research, and disseminates knowledge to support fair taxation, industry development, and public awareness.

Key resources include:
- Annual Sake Awards reports (e.g., 2025)
- Gold‑Prize sake lists
- Glossaries of sake label terms in English
- Research updates on *Aspergillus oryzae* databases
- Lecture videos and judging sheets for Honkaku Shochu & Awamori contests.

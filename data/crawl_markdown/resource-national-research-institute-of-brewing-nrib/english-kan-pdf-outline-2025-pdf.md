# Outline of the Annual Japan Sake Awards 2025

- **Date & Venue**: May 21 2025, National Research Institute of Brewing (NRIB), Japan Sake and Shochu Makers Association (JSS).
- **Purpose**: Nationwide survey of manufactured sake to clarify current technology and trends, improve quality, and raise public awareness.
- **Eligibility**:
  - Licensed manufacturers only.
  - Undiluted Ginjo‑shu brewed at the manufacturer’s own licensed site during 2024 (July 2024–application deadline).
  - Acidity > 0.8; no added aroma compounds.
  - One entry per manufacturer.
- **Entry Fees**: ¥16,200 for JSS members, ¥24,300 otherwise.
- **Competition Flow**:
  - Preliminary blind tasting (April 22‑24) and final judging (May 8‑9).
  - Sensory profiling on aroma, taste, total quality; overall evaluation in final round.
  - Entries grouped by aroma concentration and sorted by glucose.
- **Results**: 809 entries submitted; 410 prize winners, 202 gold prize winners.
- **Events**:
  - Tasting for manufacturers (May 28‑29, Hiroshima).
  - Public tasting organized by JSS.

# Comprehensive Aspergillus oryzae Genome Database (CAoGDX)

The National Research Institute of Brewing (NRIB) has released the **Comprehensive Aspergillus oryzae genome database across the clades: CAoGDX**. The database contains:
- Full-genome sequences for 40 *Rib40* strains and aggregated data from various other genomes.
- A new representation framework for 23 strains, providing complete genome‑wide information.

The release was announced on **July 3, 2024**, and the dataset is available as a downloadable PDF (334 KB). For inquiries, contact caogdx@nrib.go.jp.

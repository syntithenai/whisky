# National Research Institute of Brewing – 2017/18 New Whisky Evaluation Application Guidelines

The PDF outlines the application process, deadlines, and requirements for Japanese whisky producers to submit their products for the **National New Whisky Evaluation (NNWE)** conducted by the NRIB. Key points include:

- **Application windows**: Production‑date‑based submission dates (e.g., 2018‑03‑25) and product‑submission deadlines.
- **Submission methods**: Web portal, fax, or email; detailed instructions for each.
- **Eligibility**: Must be a Japanese whisky produced in Japan, with specific production‑site criteria.
- **Documentation**: Production records, tasting sheets, payment slips, and other forms must accompany the application.
- **Payment**: Fees are specified per product and per evaluation type (tasting, analysis, etc.).
- **Evaluation process**: Includes sensory assessment, chemical analysis, and official scoring by NRIB staff and affiliated experts.
- **Reporting**: Results are published in a public report; producers receive certificates and feedback.

The document also lists contact details for the NRIB headquarters, regional offices, and relevant regulatory bodies.

## Key Facts (distillery_facts & resource_facts)
- Application period for 2018‑03‑25 (water) and 2018‑03‑26 (wood).
- Production sites must be in Japan; non‑Japanese production is excluded.
- Submission via NRIB web portal: https://apply.e-tumo.jp/nrib-u/offer/offerList_initDisplay
- Payment methods include bank transfer, PayPay, AU Pay, and fax/email for payment slips.
- Evaluation fees: ¥16,200 per product for tasting; ¥24,300 for analysis.
- Contact: NRIB headquarters (Hiroshima), Tel 082‑420‑0800, Fax 082‑420‑0802, Email kanpyoukai_support@nrib.go.jp.

# National Research Institute of Brewing (NRIB)

The NRIB is Japan’s national research institute dedicated to the study and regulation of alcoholic beverages, especially sake. The main page provides an overview of its mission, research areas, publications, and resources for industry stakeholders. Key highlights include:
- **Research focus**: production technology, quality control, safety, and regulatory compliance.
- **Publications**: technical reports, guidelines, and statistical data on Japanese alcoholic beverages.
- **Resources**: databases (e.g., CAoGDX), educational materials, and Q&A sections for producers.
- **International collaboration**: links to EU and OIV standards and joint research initiatives.

The site is primarily in Japanese but offers English translations of core content and documents.

# National Research Institute of Brewing (NRIB)

The NRIB is Japan’s national research body for brewing science and technology. Its website hosts a wide range of resources including:
- **Research reports** on beer, sake, shochu, wine, and other fermented beverages.
- **Statistical data** on domestic production, consumption, and quality assessments.
- **Guidelines and standards** for brewing processes, safety, and environmental impact.
- **Educational materials** such as textbooks, Q&A, and course outlines.
- **Publications** in PDF format covering topics from fermentation chemistry to regulatory compliance.

The site is primarily Japanese but offers English translations of key sections and documents. It serves researchers, industry professionals, and policy makers interested in the science and regulation of alcoholic beverages in Japan and beyond.

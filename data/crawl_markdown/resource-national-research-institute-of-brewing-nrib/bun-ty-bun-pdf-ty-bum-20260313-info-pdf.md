# Export‑Capacity Analysis of 15 Japanese Wine Prefectures to Taiwan

The National Research Institute of Brewing (NRIB) released a PDF detailing the methodology and results for assessing the export capacity of 15 Japanese wine prefectures (Fukui, Gifu, Ishikawa, Kagawa, Niigata) to Taiwan. The document explains how the NRIB’s independent research team performed the analysis, the data sources used, and the criteria for approving requests from Taiwanese importers.

Key points:
- Analysis covers 15 prefectures’ wine export capacity.
- Uses a 5 % threshold for overall export volume.
- Employs a gamma‑line plot with a Geiger counter to measure radioisotopes (I‑131, Cs‑134, Cs‑137).
- Provides guidelines for Taiwanese importers to request analysis of specific wine categories: *metane*, *copper*, and *bivalent yellow*.
- Lists contact details for the NRIB’s research office.

# 30th Anniversary Collection – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove celebrates three decades of whisky making with a collection that includes:
- **Double Cask (DC30TH)** – a single‑malt double‑cask release priced at AUD 300.
- **24‑Year‑Old American Oak Second‑Fill (HH0004)** – an Old & Rare expression sold out for AUD 2,500.

The page highlights the distillery’s focus on age, oak variety and long‑term maturation, and offers links to other product categories such as American Oak, French Oak, and Old & Rare releases.

## Key Points
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Products**: Double Cask (DC30TH) and 24‑Year‑Old American Oak Second‑Fill.
- **Pricing**: AUD 300 for DC30TH; AUD 2,500 for HH0004.
- **Availability**: Both releases are sold out on the site.
- **Themes**: Celebration of 30 years, commitment to excellence, and long‑term maturation in full‑size barrels since 1994.

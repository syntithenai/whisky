# Sullivans Cove Wine Cask Collection

Sullivans Cove’s wine‑cask range showcases whisky matured in French oak ex‑white‑wine and ex‑Pinot Noir casks. The site highlights the brand’s award‑winning status, including a 2026 World Whiskies Award for *World’s Best Single Cask Single Malt* (18‑year‑old French Oak ex‑White Wine). Each cask is monitored closely to balance tannin, structure and fruit character.

Key releases:
- **French Oak ex‑Pinot Noir (Cask SC0387)** – deep expression with fresh Pinot Noir notes.
- **French Oak ex‑White Wine (Cask SC0397)** – bold, evolving, firm tannin and lifted fruit.
- **French Oak ex‑Pinot Noir (Cask SC0399)** – rich dark fruits, savory complexity.

The distillery is based in Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia.

# American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0447)

- **Age**: 10 years (distilled Nov 2013, decanted Nov 2023) in a single 200 L American oak cask.
- **Alcohol**: 48.1 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 400 per bottle.
- **Bottle count**: 266 bottles released.
- **Tasting notes**: Jammy fruit, floral honey, tropical stone‑fruit (nectarine, pineapple), banana, peach; oak spice, lavender, honeysuckle; green‑snap‑pea and cucumber hints. Palate full-bodied with peach, watermelon rind, custard, cinnamon toast, citrus (lemon, orange, mango). Finish grapefruit, white pepper, green lolly‑snakes, Panna Cotta, vanilla wafer, tea tree, native grasses.
- **Production**: Second‑fill cask previously used for an older batch of Sullivans Cove Single Malt; chosen for quality and long‑term maturation since 1994.

> *“Our dedication to long‑term maturation has remained the same since 1994… we are guided by the liquid.”*

# French Oak Ex‑Pinot Noir – Sullivans Cove Whisky

- **First release** of the French Oak line, finished in ex‑Pinot Noir casks.
- **Price:** AUD 750 per bottle (no discount).
- **Cask options:** SC0399 and SC0387.
- **Tasting notes:** Fruit‑driven, elegant, complex with a creamy mouthfeel.
- **Production ethos:** Long‑term maturation since 1994; no scheduled bottling – the whisky is bottled when ready.

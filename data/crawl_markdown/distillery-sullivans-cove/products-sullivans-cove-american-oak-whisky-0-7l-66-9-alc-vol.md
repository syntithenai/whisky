# Cask Strength American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (TD0444)

- **Age & Maturation**: 9 years, 3 months in a single 200 L American oak ex‑bourbon cask.
- **ABV**: Natural cask strength of 66.9 %.
- **Yield**: 191 bottles.
- **Price**: AUD 590 per bottle (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting Notes**: Bold tropical fruit, smoked pineapple, Nocino liqueur; mid‑palate cacao nibs, salted caramel, custard buns, dark chocolate; finish with macadamia nut, cinnamon toast, lavender & sage.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.

The product is highlighted as the first cask‑strength bottling in five years and showcases the distillery’s long‑term maturation ethos.

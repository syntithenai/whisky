# American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0429)

- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Cask**: Single 200 L American oak cask previously used for a first‑fill batch.
- **Age**: 10 years 11 months (distilled September 2013, decanted September 2024).
- **ABV**: 47.5 %.
- **Bottles produced**: 239.
- **Price**: AUD 400 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Tropical fruit (mango, apple), buttery biscuit warmth, herbal layers (anise, clove, thyme), ripe apricot/nectarine, subtle tartness, pine‑lime jelly, toasted sugars, dried orange.
- **Production philosophy**: Long‑term maturation; no fixed bottling schedule – bottle when the spirit is ready.

The whisky showcases the richness of American oak while retaining Sullivans Cove’s distinctive fruity character.

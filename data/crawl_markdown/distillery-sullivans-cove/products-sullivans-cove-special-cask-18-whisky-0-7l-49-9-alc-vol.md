# French Oak ex‑Pinot Noir (SC0399)

- **Age & Cask**: 7 years in a single 225 L French oak ex‑pinot noir cask.
- **Production**: Distilled Aug 2017, decanted Sep 2024; bottled unfiltered at 49.9 % ABV.
- **Bottles**: 364 limited bottles.
- **Price**: AUD 750 per bottle (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting Notes**: Dark fruit, savory, umami‑laden with hints of truffle, leather, cinnamon, coriander, flint, and a complex finish featuring cola, amaro, citrus peel and plum sauce.
- **Distillery Philosophy**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule – the whisky is bottled when ready.

> *“Our dedication to long‑term maturation has remained the same since 1994… we don’t bottle to a schedule, instead we are guided by the liquid.”* – Heather Tillott, Distillery Manager

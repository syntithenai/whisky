# Sullivans Cove – Our Process
Sullivans Cove’s production philosophy blends centuries of whisky tradition with Tasmania’s unique terroir. The journey starts with locally‑grown barley and pristine Tasmanian water, followed by slow fermentation, double distillation in custom stills (Myrtle, Goldie, Eva), and long‑term maturation in 200–300 L casks without temperature control. Each bottle is hand‑tasted and labelled with its unique barrel and bottle number.

## Key Points
- **Local ingredients**: 100 % Tasmanian barley + pure water.
- **Fermentation & distillation**: Slow, traditional fermentation; double distillation with reflux for esterification.
- **Stills**: Myrtle, Goldie, Eva – worm‑tub condensers add body and funk.
- **Maturation**: Full‑size casks (200–300 L), no temperature control, guided by the spirit’s quality rather than a schedule.
- **Quality checks**: Multiple tastings per cask; 15 steps of bottling with meticulous attention to detail.
- **Labeling**: Barrel and bottle numbers recorded on each label.

# XO Single Cask Chardonnay Brandy (TDB0005)

- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Cask**: First‑fill French oak ex‑tawny cask.
- **Age**: 13 years 7 months (filled 27/07/2007, decanted 12/03/2021).
- **Volume**: 0.7 L, 47.6 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 275 per bottle.
- **Key tasting notes**: dried apricot, glacé cherry, raspberry, nougat, caramel fudge, marmalade, orange rind, tropical fruit, dried pawpaw, muscatel grapes, honey, nuts, lychee, banana, toffee, quince paste, aniseed, coriander seeds, licorice, dried figs, Earl Grey tea.
- **Production**: Single‑varietal brandy from Tasmanian Huon Valley Chardonnay, bottled unblended with no added colouring or flavouring. Each cask aged minimum eight years; bottling guided by the spirit rather than a schedule.

> *“Brilliantly crafted and endlessly enjoyable… captures the nutty and spicy essence of Chardonnay with poise.”* – Sullivans Cove Sensory Team

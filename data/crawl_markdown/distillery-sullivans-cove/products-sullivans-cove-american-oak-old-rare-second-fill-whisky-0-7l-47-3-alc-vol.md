# 18 Year Old American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0033)

- **Age & Cask**: Filled 06/09/2005, decanted 06/09/2023 – 18 years in a 200 L American oak second‑fill cask.
- **Bottles**: 169 bottles at 47.3 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 800 (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting Notes**: Rich, toasty oak; tropical fruit (mango, banana, dragonfruit); creamy butter‑shortbread; floral & herbal nuances; subtle spice and vanilla.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.

The expression is part of the Old & Rare line, showcasing long‑term maturation in full‑size barrels since 1994.

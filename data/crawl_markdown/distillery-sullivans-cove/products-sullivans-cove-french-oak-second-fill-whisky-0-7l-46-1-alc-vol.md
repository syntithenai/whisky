## Source Excerpts
- Our French Oak Second-Fill casks have a history as rich as their flavour.
- Initially serving as first-fill ex-Tawny casks for an earlier batch of Sullivans Cove single malt, the best casks are then selected for a second round of maturation.
- American Oak Our American Oak single casks are among our most awarded expressions, beautifully enhancing our distinctive and fruity spirit.
- Old & Rare Our Old & Rare releases showcase Australia’s oldest whiskies, made possible by our unwavering commitment to long term maturation in full size barrels since 1994.
- This maturation style beautifully displays the rich character of the French oak, while allowing the distinctive spirit character of Sullivans Cove to take centre stage.
- French Oak Second-Fill 'Refill' or 'second-fill' means this barrel has been used to mature Sullivans Cove whisky before.

# French Oak Second‑Fill (TD0383)

- **Age**: 10 years, 8 months (distilled Nov 2012, decanted Jul 2023). 
- **Cask**: 300 L French oak ex‑tawny cask previously used for a first‑fill of Sullivans Cove Single Malt. 
- **ABV**: 49.1 %. 
- **Price**: AUD 550 (regular and sale price). 
- **Tasting notes**: Syrupy, brandy‑like body with jammy red berries, caramel apples, rum & raisin ice cream, orange cake batter, Calvados, blackcurrant, menthol, sweet grass, Jersey caramels, walnuts, lemon tart, raspberry compote, pecan Danish, toasted Brazil nuts, charred fig, white chocolate, mineral‑salt finish.
- **Production philosophy**: Long‑term maturation in full‑size barrels since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule – the whisky is bottled when ready.

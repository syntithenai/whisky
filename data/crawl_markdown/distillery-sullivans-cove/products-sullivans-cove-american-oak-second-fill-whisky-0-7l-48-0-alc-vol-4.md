# American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0453)

- **Age**: 11 years (distilled Nov 2013, decanted Dec 2024). 
- **Cask**: 200 L American oak previously used for an older Sullivans Cove single malt. 
- **Bottles produced**: 232.
- **ABV**: 48 % (unflocked, unfiltered).
- **Price**: AUD 400 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Honey‑laden nose with lavender, jasmine and tropical fruits; creamy apple‑cider mouthfeel; chocolate custard, spiced honey, cinnamon, nutmeg, clove; finish of dried fruit, cardamom, pumpkin spice.
- **Production philosophy**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; refill casks allow spirit character to shine.

## Glossary & terminology
- *Second‑fill* / *refill*: a cask reused after an earlier batch.
- *Unflocked/unfiltered*: no fining or filtration before bottling.
- *Spirit‑driven character*: emphasis on the whisky’s inherent qualities rather than added flavours.

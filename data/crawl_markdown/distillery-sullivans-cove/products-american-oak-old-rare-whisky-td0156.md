# 18 Year Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (TD0156)

- **Age & Cask**: 18 years, first‑fill American Oak ex‑bourbon cask (Cask # TD0156). 
- **Price**: AUD 800.00.
- **Tasting notes**: Tropical fruit (pineapple, guava, mango), coconut cream, honey, vanilla, subtle floral/ herbal hints; silky palate with dried apricot, pine nuts, candied orange and apple‑pie crust; finish of lime, clove, stewed raspberries, vanilla, mango jam.

# 24‑Year‑Old American Oak Second‑Fill (HH0004)

- **Age**: 24 years & 2 months, filled 5 Oct 1999, decanted 5 Jan 2024.
- **Bottles**: 335 at 52 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD $2,500 (regular and sale).
- **Packaging**: Handmade solid oak box with authenticity letter from Distillery Manager.
- **Tasting notes**: Floral‑sweet‑savory profile – buttered popcorn, maple syrup, cherry jam, tropical fruit, gingerbread, cinnamon, cacao, anise, clove, leatherwood honey, green peppercorn, aloe vera, taffy, caramels, marshmallow, crème caramel, and a long finish of vanillic leather, cantaloupe, papaya, rose perfume.
- **Production**: Second‑fill American oak cask; no chill‑filtering or dilution.

> *“Deeply inviting. The epitome of well‑aged Sullivans Cove spirit at its full potential.” – Heather Tillott, Distillery Manager.

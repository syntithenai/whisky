# French Oak – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove’s **French Oak** is a single‑cask Australian whisky that has won *World’s Best* in 2014 and 2019. The casks are aged in French oak, which imparts cedar incense, amber, sandalwood and a strong tannin structure. The line includes the main 18‑year‑old TD0110, ex‑Apera (ex‑Sherry) and ex‑Pinot Noir expressions, as well as second‑fill casks that were first used for Tawny maturation.

The distillery is based in Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania. It has a long‑term bondstore strategy, ageing whisky in full‑size barrels since 1994, and has earned numerous awards including *Whisky of the Year* at the AWAS Awards and multiple World Whisky Award titles.

# French Oak Ex‑Tawny (TD1068)

- **Age & cask**: 9 yrs 5 mo in a 300 L French oak ex‑tawny single cask.
- **ABV**: 48.1 % unfiltered, unflocked.
- **Bottles**: 482 from the cask.
- **Price**: AUD 550 (regular).
- **Tasting notes**: Tropical & orchard fruits, blackberry, spice, florals, nutty depth; silky palate with dark cherry, plum, honeyed stone fruit and gentle tannic spice; long structured finish of dried fruit, praline, coconut and soft tea tannins.
- **Background**: First time available in 18 months; celebrated after winning World’s Best at the World Whiskies Awards (2014 & 2019).

# Sullivans Cove Old & Rare – Page 5

Sullivans Cove’s *Old & Rare* collection showcases Australia’s oldest whiskies, aged a minimum of sixteen years and up to twenty‑four. The page lists the current release: **17‑Year‑Old American Oak (Cask #TD0229)**, along with past releases such as the 18‑Year‑Old French Oak (Cask #TD0110) and the 22‑Year‑Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon. The site explains its long‑term maturation philosophy, waitlist and ballot systems for securing rare casks, and highlights recent awards including World Whisky Awards Best Australian Single Cask.

---

## Key facts
- **Current release**: 17 Year Old American Oak (Cask #TD0229)
- **Past releases**: 18 Year Old French Oak (Cask #TD0110), 22 Year Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon, etc.
- **Age range**: 16–24 years
- **Maturation**: full‑size barrels since 1994
- **Awards**: 2025 World Whisky Awards Best Australian Single Cask (18 Year Old American Oak), 2022 & 2021 awards for other casks.
- **Pricing example**: 18 Year Old French Oak – AUD 1,250 per unit.
- **Distribution channels**: waitlist, ballot, cellar‑door visits.

---

## Flavor & production notes
The page describes the whiskies as *creamy*, *fruit‑forward* and *rich*. The American Oak casks are noted for enhancing a distinctive fruity spirit. French Oak is highlighted as one of the world’s most sought‑after whiskies, having won World’s Best in 2014 and 2019.

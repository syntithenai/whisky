# French Oak Ex‑Tawny (TD0402)

- **Single cask** aged 10 y 10 m in a 225 L French oak ex‑tawny barrel.
- Bottled unflocked/unfiltered at 47 % ABV, 325 bottles released.
- Rich tasting profile: dark fruits, maple syrup, chocolate, spices, citrus and mint notes.
- Won World’s Best Whisky (2014 & 2019) for the French Oak style.

## Key facts
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Cask number**: TD0402.
- **Age**: 10 y 10 m.
- **Bottles produced**: 325.
- **ABV**: 47 %.
- **Price**: AUD 550 (regular & sale).

## Tasting notes
- Nose: dark fruits, maple syrup, dried roses, honey, dates, nutmeg, chocolate, coffee, citrus.
- Palate: salted caramel, plum jam, blackberry, sweet pastries, apricot crumble, black cherries, blood orange, pomegranate, molasses, hot cross buns, spearmint jelly, coriander seeds.
- Finish: quince paste, toasted fig, dark chocolate, espresso, toffee, brown sugar, cinnamon, marmalade, orange blossom.

**Production**: long‑term maturation in full‑size barrels since 1994; no scheduled bottling – guided by the liquid.

# Sullivans Cove Whisky – FAQ Overview

- **Purchase & Availability**: Most bottles sold directly on the website; some via ballot. No fixed release schedule—whisky released only after tasting panel approval.
- **Order Limits**: Max three bottles per expression, six total per customer. Shipping costs cannot be combined across orders.
- **Shipping**: International shipping available (details on shipping page).
- **Payment Issues**: American Express may reject if billing/shipping addresses differ from AmEx account.
- **Resale Value**: Old bottles often fetch high auction prices; current releases have recommended retail pricing listed online. Historical release values not provided.
- **Visiting**: Tours and tastings available seven days a week; booking via Visit Us page.
- **Cloudiness & Sediment**: No chill filtration; natural oils may appear cloudy or settle in cold conditions—safe to consume.
- **Batch Variation**: Single‑cask distillery; each cask unique, leading to flavor differences between bottles.

# Sullivans Cove Whisky
Sullivans Cove is a Tasmanian single‑cask distillery based in Hobart that has earned multiple world awards for its French Oak and American Oak expressions. The site showcases its award‑winning whiskies, explains the double‑cask maturation process, highlights its long‑term ageing philosophy, and offers online sales of single‑cask releases and gift cards.

## Key Points
- **Location**: 1/10 Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia
- **Core products**: Double‑Cask Single Malt (French Oak & American Oak), French Oak Ex‑Apera, French Oak Second‑Fill, American Oak Second‑Fill, and special wine‑cask releases.
- **Awards**: World’s Best Single Cask Single Malt (fourth win) for the French Oak Ex‑White Wine Cask N° TD0112.
- **Philosophy**: Long‑term maturation in full‑size barrels since 1994, with meticulous cask selection and sensory nurturing.
- **Online shop**: Prices range from AUD 250 to AUD 550 per single‑cask bottle; includes gift cards and merchandise.

## Visit & Contact
The distillery offers guided tours and tastings. Contact details are available on the site, along with shipping, privacy, and legal notices.

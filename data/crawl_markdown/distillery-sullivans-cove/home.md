# Sullivans Cove Whisky
Sullivans Cove is a Tasmanian single‑cask distillery known for its award‑winning whiskies, especially those finished in American Oak and French Oak casks. The site highlights their flagship **French Oak Ex‑White Wine Cask N° TD0112**, which has won *World’s Best Single Cask Single Malt* four times. Their product range includes double‑cask single malts, second‑fill expressions, and rare old & rare releases.

Key points:
- Tasmanian distillery located in Hobart.
- Focus on long‑term maturation in full‑size barrels since 1994.
- Products are sold online with prices listed (e.g., $250 AUD for Double Cask Single Malt, $550 AUD for French Oak Ex‑Apera).
- The site offers a visitor experience and guided tours.

# Sullivans Cove Whisky

Award-winning Tasmanian single cask distillery offering a range of premium whiskies including:
- **Double Cask American Oak**
- **French Oak Single Casks** (Ex-Apera, Ex-White Wine, Ex-Pinot Noir)
- **Old & Rare Releases** showcasing aged Australian whiskies
- **World's Best Single Cask Nº TD0112** (French Oak Ex-White Wine Cask)

## Key Facts
- Founded in 1994, Sullivans Cove is Australia's oldest single malt distillery.
- Whiskies are matured in full-size barrels for extended periods to develop depth and complexity.
- Distillery uses a combination of Tasmanian oak and international oak casks.
- Awards include World's Best Single Cask multiple times (2014, 2019).

## Production Signals
- **American Oak**: Creamy, fruit-forward spirit with rich vanilla notes.
- **French Oak**: Enhanced fruity character, globally recognized awards.
- **Old & Rare**: Whiskies aged over 25 years in large barrels for exceptional complexity.

## Commercial Signals
- Consistent top rankings in international whisky competitions.
- Strong brand positioning as Australia's premier single cask distillery.
- Expansion of limited-edition releases to maintain exclusivity.

## Risks / Unknowns
- Dependence on Tasmanian climate and aging conditions for consistency.
- Limited production capacity may restrict market growth.
- Potential regulatory changes affecting export markets.

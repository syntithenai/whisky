# French Oak Ex‑Apera (TD0821)

- **Age**: 10 years, distilled Nov 2015, decanted Nov 2025.
- **Cask**: 225 L French oak ex‑Apera barrique.
- **Bottles**: 367 at 47.6 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 550 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Opulent dark fruits, cola‑sweetness, warming spice, silky finish with fortified‑wine influence.

This single‑cask bottling showcases the long‑term maturation ethos of Sullivans Cove and the unique character imparted by a French oak ex‑Apera cask.

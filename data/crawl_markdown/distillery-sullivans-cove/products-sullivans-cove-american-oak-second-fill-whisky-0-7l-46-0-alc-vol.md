# American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0461)

- **Age**: 10 y 9 m (distilled Dec 2013, decanted Sep 2024). 
- **Cask**: 200 L American oak previously used for an older Sullivans Cove single malt. 
- **Bottles**: 250, unflocked & unfiltered at 46 % ABV. 
- **Price**: AUD 400 (regular and sale). 
- **Tasting notes**: Bright tropical fruit, citrus sherbet, spicy balsa wood, floral perfume; silky palate with shortbread, buttery pastry, lamb‑fat nuance, glacé cherries, toffee apple, boiled lollies; finish of watermelon dusted salt, apple, cotton candy, marshmallow, anise and Gewürztraminer lift.
- **Production**: Second‑fill cask – spirit matured in a barrel that previously held Sullivans Cove whisky. 
- **Distillery ethos**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule, guided by the liquid.

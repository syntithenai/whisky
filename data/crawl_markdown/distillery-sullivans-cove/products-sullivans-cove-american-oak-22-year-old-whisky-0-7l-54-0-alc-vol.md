# 22 Year Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (HH0039)

- **Age & Maturation**: Filled 2 Nov 1999, matured 22 yrs 5 mo, decanted 14 Apr 2022.
- **Yield**: 58 bottles at 54 % ABV in a single cask.
- **Price**: AUD $2,250 (sold out).
- **Style**: Creamy, full‑bodied with robust oak structure; bright fruit, brine, spice and sweet vanilla‑coconut notes from the American Oak ex‑bourbon barrel.
- **Tasting Notes**: Agave‑mezzal‑style nose (banana bread, walnuts, green olives, cocoa, etc.); palate of strawberries, peaches, pine‑needle tea, gingery mint; finish with toffee, caramel, pumpkin‑spice‑like sweetness and bay/oregano lift.
- **Packaging**: Handmade solid oak box with authenticity letter from Distillery Manager Heather Tillott.

The bottle is a rare, single‑cask release that showcases Sullivans Cove’s long‑term maturation ethos and the distinct character of American Oak ex‑bourbon barrels.

# American Oak – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove’s **American Oak** line features single‑cask expressions aged in American oak barrels, including ex‑bourbon and ex‑tawny casks. The whisky is described as creamy, fruit‑forward, with layers of vanilla, caramel, coconut and red fruits. It has received multiple World Whiskies Awards for best Australian single‑cask and single‑malt categories.

Key facts:
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia
- **Cask types**: American oak (ex‑bourbon, ex‑tawny)
- **Awards**: 2025 & 2024 World Whiskies Awards, 2017 World Whiskies Award
- **Available products**: Ex‑Bourbon, Second‑Fill, Ex‑Tawny
- **Price range**: Not listed on the page (contact or shop for details)

The site also promotes other ranges such as Double Cask, French Oak and Old & Rare.

# Double Cask – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove’s **Double Cask** is a single‑malt whisky crafted from a select few casks that capture the breadth of age and oak in their bondstore. The expression is described as creamy, fruit‑forward and unmistakably Sullivans Cove. It is available in 0.7 L bottles at AUD 250.

- **Cask options**: DC120, DC121, DC122 (all sold out). 
- **Tasting notes**: The whisky is noted for its creamy texture and fruit‑forward profile.
- **Production ethos**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; the distillery bottles only when the spirit reaches optimal quality rather than on a schedule.

# Sullivans Cove Whisky Products

## Double Cask
- Creamy, fruit-forward whisky created using age and oak from bondstore
- Bottled at cask strength without chill filtration
- Limited availability due to long-term maturation in full-size barrels since 1994
- Examples: DC117, DC118, DC121, DC30TH

## Single Malt Double Cask
- Bottles feature whisky from two different casks combined
- Batch numbers indicate specific releases (e.g., DC117, DC118)
- Sold out examples include BATCH Nº DC117, DC118, DC121

## American Oak Second-Fill
- Whisky matured in second-fill American oak barrels
- Bottled at cask strength without chill filtration
- Examples: DC120 (BATCH Nº DC120)

## French Oak
- Two main offerings:
  - **Second-Fill**: Matured in second-fill French oak barrels, bottled at cask strength
    - Examples: TD0420 (CASK Nº TD0420), TD0455 (CASK Nº TD0455)
  - **Ex-Pinot Noir**: Single cask matured in French oak after Pinot Noir wine
    - Example: TD0187 (CASK Nº TD0187)
- Highly sought-after expressions with World's Best awards

## Old & Rare
- Showcases Australia's oldest whiskies aged for extended periods in full-size barrels since 1994
- Examples: 17 Year Old French Oak Ex-Tawny (TD0187)

## Distillery Tours
- Guided tours of the Lamb Place facility in Cambridge, Tasmania
- Includes tasting room and educational sessions about Sullivans Cove's process
- Regularly updated with new releases

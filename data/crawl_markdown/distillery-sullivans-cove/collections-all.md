# Sullivans Cove All Products

Sullivans Cove’s online shop lists a range of single‑malt whiskies aged in American Oak and French Oak casks, as well as Old & Rare releases and distillery tours. Prices are shown in AUD (e.g., $250 for most Double Cask bottles, $400 for American Oak Second‑Fill). The site highlights the brand’s award‑winning status, Tasmanian heritage, and commitment to long‑term maturation.

## Key product categories
- **Double Cask** – Single malt finished in both American and French oak. 
- **American Oak Second‑Fill** – Bottled from a second fill of American oak casks. 
- **French Oak Second‑Fill** – Bottled from a second fill of French oak casks. 
- **Old & Rare** – 17‑year‑old French Oak ex‑tawny and ex‑pinot noir expressions.

## Distillery information
- Located at Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia.
- Operates a bondstore with a variety of oak types.
- Emphasises handcrafted production and long‑term maturation since 1994.

# 17 Year Old French Oak Second‑Fill (TD0028)

- **Age & cask**: 17 years 7 months in a single 300 L French oak second‑fill cask.
- **Distillation**: 10 Aug 2005; decanted 22 Mar 2023.
- **ABV**: 48.3 %.
- **Bottles produced**: 415.
- **Price**: AUD 1,250 (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting notes**: Rich, sultry nose of baked croissant, orange marmalade, honeycomb; palate with orange blossom, sandalwood, strawberries, ginger wine, cinnamon spice, jam‑doughnut sweetness, and a finish of apple, melon, greengage and a hint of margarita rim.
- **Key concept**: Second‑fill French oak allows long maturation without overpowering oak influence, preserving the spirit’s character.

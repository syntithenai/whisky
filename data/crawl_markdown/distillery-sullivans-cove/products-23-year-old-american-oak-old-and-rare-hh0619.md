# 23 Year Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (HH0619)

- **Age & Cask**: 23 years, 9 months in an American oak ex‑bourbon cask (No. HH0619). 
- **Origin**: First Australian distillery to cover every age from 16–25 years.
- **Price**: AUD 2,500 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Creamy buttered sourdough & vanilla custard; pine‑lime, charred pineapple, soda; sweet oak, maple, shortbread, milk lollies, stone fruit; finish with buttery shortbread, pine nuts, lemon drops, passionfruit, vanilla.
- **Significance**: Rare cask from the early HH era (1999‑2003) – only 162 filled in total.

> *“Seriously special — a whisky of immense character and unmistakable gravitas.”* – Heather Tillott, Distillery Manager

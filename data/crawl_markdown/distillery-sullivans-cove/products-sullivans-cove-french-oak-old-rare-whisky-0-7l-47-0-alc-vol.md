# 17 Year Old French Oak Ex‑Tawny (TD0187)

- **Age & Cask**: 17 years 3 months in a 300 L French oak cask previously used for Australian Tawny Port‑style wine.
- **Release**: Limited edition, only 443 bottles at 47% ABV; no general availability.
- **Price**: AUD 1,250 (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting Notes**: Sweet milky black tea, cocoa, rose petals, lemongrass, mango skin, walnuts, pecan pie, pepperberry, dried citrus, honey, custard doughnuts, cashews; palate with orange, grapey sweetness, malt milkshake‑like richness, coffee crema, plums, BBQ sauce, hoisin, basil, cinnamon, shortbread. Finish: white peach, figs, cherries, plums, basil, oregano, cola‑sweetness, rosemary, lamb fat, Earl Grey tea, tobacco.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia – long‑term maturation since 1994.

> *“Refined yet seriously approachable… a lighter tawny profile is perfectly matched with gentle oak.”* – Heather Tillott, Distillery Manager

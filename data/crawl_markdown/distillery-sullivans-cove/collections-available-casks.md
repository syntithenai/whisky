# Available Casks – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove’s bondstore offers a curated selection of single‑cask and old & rare expressions, ranging from French Oak ex‑tawny to American Oak ex‑bourbon. Prices vary from AUD 400 for a 25‑year‑old American Oak to AUD 2,500 for a 17‑year‑old French Oak cask strength. The page lists each cask’s number, type, and price, along with brief descriptors of the oak and age.

## Key Highlights
- **French Oak** – celebrated for winning World’s Best in 2014 & 2019.
- **American Oak** – highly awarded single‑cask expressions.
- **Old & Rare** – showcases Australia’s oldest whiskies, aged since 1994.

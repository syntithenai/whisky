# Sullivans Cove Awards
Sullivans Cove is Australia's most decorated whisky distillery, boasting **four World’s Best** titles and numerous other accolades across single‑cask, single‑malt, and brandy categories. The page lists award wins from 2014 to 2026, highlighting specific expressions (e.g., 18 yr French Oak, 21 yr American Oak) and recognitions such as Icons of Whisky Manager of the Year.

## Key Highlights
- **World’s Best Single Malt** – 2014 (first non‑Scotland/Japan win)
- **World’s Best Single Cask Single Malt** – 2018, 2019, 2026
- Multiple *Best Australian Single Cask* wins (2015‑2023)
- Icons of Whisky awards for management and brand innovation
- World Brandy Awards: *World’s Best Wine Brandy* (French Oak) 2021
- Other recognitions: Australian Whisky Appreciation Society, Tasting Australia, Drinks International Top 10

# 19 Year Old French Oak (TD0022)

- **Age & Cask**: 19‑year‑old, single 300 L French oak second‑fill cask.
- **Distillation**: July 2005; decanted August 2024.
- **Bottling**: 47.5 % ABV, 374 bottles, no longer available for general purchase.
- **Price**: AUD 1,250 (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting Notes**: Spicy cumin & star anise; tropical mango/pineapple; buttery popcorn → butterscotch → beeswax. Palate of orange‑chocolate, roasted pineapple, mineral edge, artichoke hearts, pine nuts, rose jam, blackberry confections, condensed milk, crème brûlée. Finish briny, agave‑like, with roasted pineapple, fennel, sweet bell pepper, malt, butter biscuits, salted caramel, vanilla wafers, toasted marshmallows.
- **Packaging**: Handmade solid oak box with authenticity letter from Distillery Manager Heather Tillott.

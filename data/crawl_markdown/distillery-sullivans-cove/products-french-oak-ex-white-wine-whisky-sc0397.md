# French Oak Ex‑White Wine (SC0397)

- **Type**: Single cask, French oak ex‑white wine cask.
- **Age**: 8 yrs 5 mo (filled 31/08/2017, decanted 11/02/2026).
- **Price**: AUD 550.00 (regular price). 348 bottles available.
- **Flavor profile**: Fruit‑forward with lifted fruit, savory depth, firm tannin and a deeply textural character.

- **Awards**: Winner of *World's Best Single Cask Single Malt* at the 2026 World Whiskies Awards (cask TD0112).

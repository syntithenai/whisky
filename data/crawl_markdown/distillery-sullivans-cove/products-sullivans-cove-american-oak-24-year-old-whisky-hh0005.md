# 24 Year Old American Oak Apera Finish (HH0005)

- **Age & Cask**: 24 years, 9 months; first filled in a 300 L American oak cask (Oct 1999), finished in an ex‑Apera 225 L American oak cask (Sept 2017–Oct 2024).
- **Production**: 324 bottles at 51.1 % ABV, released as a limited Old & Rare single‑cask expression.
- **Tasting Profile**: Rich chocolate‑coconut nose; full‑bodied palate with sweet timber, earth, cherries, dark chocolate, gamey notes, caramel toffee, buttercream fudge, floral citrus spice, and Apera‑derived apple juice. Long finish of cinnamon, clove, black pepper, macadamia, hazelnut, Nutella, salted peanut brittle, maple syrup.
- **Distillery Philosophy**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule – the whisky is bottled when ready.

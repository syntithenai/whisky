# Sullivans Cove Visitor Experience

Sullivans Cove offers luxury whisky tastings and distillery tours at its newly‑reopened visitor centre in Cambridge, Tasmania. Guests can explore the production process, taste award‑winning Tasmanian whiskies—including American Oak, French Oak, Double Cask and Old & Rare expressions—and purchase bottles that are not available elsewhere.

- **Location**: 1/10 Lamb Place, Cambridge, TAS 7170, Australia
- **Hours**: 10 am – 4:30 pm, seven days a week
- **Tours & Tastings**: Guided visits and bar tastings with expert staff
- **Bottle Sales**: Exclusive range available on site only

The distillery is known for its award‑winning whiskies such as the French Oak (World’s Best 2014 & 2019) and long‑matured Old & Rare releases.

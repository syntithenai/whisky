# Brandy – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove offers a range of premium brandy products crafted in Tasmania. The collection includes the **XO Double Cask Brandy (DCB005)** and two single‑cask variants: **XO Single Cask Chardonnay Brandy (TDB0005)** and **XO Single Cask Pinot Noir Brandy (TDB0035)**. All are aged for many years in Tasmanian climate, delivering depth and character.

- **Price**: $190 AUD each for the double‑cask; $275 AUD each for the single‑cask variants.
- The brandy line is a collaboration with the Huon Valley wine industry.
- Distillery location: 1/10 Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia.

# XO Double Cask Brandy (DCB005)

Sullivans Cove’s **XO Double Cask Brandy** is a blend of five brandies aged 9‑15 years in American and French oak casks. Distilled from Tasmanian wines (Pinot Noir, Chardonnay) it finishes at 49% ABV. The spirit offers a complex bouquet of cherry blossom, quince paste, honey, iced tea, white nectarine, sugar‑cane, yuzu, shortbread, milk chocolate, walnuts and tropical blossoms. On the palate it is silky with cocoa, lamington, lemon, grape juice, white chocolate, mango, kiwi, strawberries, absinthe‑like aniseed, and a subtle oak structure that culminates in Earl Grey bitterness, cinnamon, dragon fruit, dried fruits, lime zest and a lingering herbal finish.

Price: **AUD 190** (regular).

# 18 Year Old French Oak Ex‑White Wine (TD0112)

- **Age & cask**: 18 years, 9 months in a 225 L French oak barrique previously used for white wine.
- **Bottles released**: 269 bottles at 50.6 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 1 250 (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting notes**: Green pear, apple, buttery vanilla; tropical fruit, stewed apricot, honey, floral; creamy hazelnut‑fruit loaf, raisin toast, sarsaparilla, olive, wattleseed, pepperberry, brown sugar, dried citrus; finish with sticky peach, chamomile, honey tea, orange poppyseed cake, herbaceous touch, melted butter and salted caramel macadamia ice cream.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia – long‑term maturation since 1994.

> *“At 18 years, Single Cask N° TD0112 is a rare, long‑aged single malt matured in a French oak white wine cask – a triumph of age and oak.”* – Sullivans Cove Sensory Team

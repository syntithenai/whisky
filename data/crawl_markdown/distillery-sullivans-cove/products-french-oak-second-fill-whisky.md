# French Oak Second‑Fill – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove’s **French Oak Second‑Fill** is a single‑cask whisky aged first in ex‑Tawny barrels and then re‑matured in selected French oak casks. The result is a deep, rich spirit that showcases the character of French oak while allowing the whisky itself to shine.

- **Price:** AUD 550 per 0.7 L bottle (no discount).
- **Cask IDs available:** TD0423, TD0416, TD0374 (all sold out at time of capture).
- **Tasting notes:** Creamy and fruit‑forward with a pronounced oak influence.
- **Production ethos:** Long‑term maturation since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule – the whisky is bottled when ready.

The product page also highlights Sullivans Cove’s broader range (American Oak, Double Cask, Old & Rare) and their award‑winning status.

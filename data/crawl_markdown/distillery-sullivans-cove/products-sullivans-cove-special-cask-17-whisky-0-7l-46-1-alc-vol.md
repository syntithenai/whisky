## Production & philosophy
Sullivans Cove has been maturing whisky in full‑size barrels since 1994. The distillery emphasizes long‑term maturation and bottles only when the spirit is ready, rather than on a fixed schedule.

### Distillery facts
- Sullivans Cove Distillery located at Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia.
- Founded in 1994 with a focus on long‑term maturation.
- Known for French oak ex‑pinot noir cask finishes; won World Whisky Awards Best Australian Whisky in 2014 and 2019.

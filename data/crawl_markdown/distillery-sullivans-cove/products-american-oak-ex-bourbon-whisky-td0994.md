# American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (TD0994)

- **Age**: 9 years 6 months
- **Cask**: 200 L first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon, filled Apr 2016, decanted Nov 2025
- **Yield**: 319 bottles
- **ABV**: 47.5 %
- **Price**: AUD 400 (regular)
- **Tasting notes**: creamy, rockmelon, apple turnover, orange jelly, macadamia, lactic richness, dark chocolate; palate of spice, praline, baked pastry, orchard fruit, maple sweetness; finish honeycomb, chocolate mint, cherry jam, soft oak sugars.

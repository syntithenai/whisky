**Double Cask (DC120) – Sullivans Cove Whisky**

- Single‑malt blended from French and American oak casks aged 6–18 years.
- Bottled at 47.4 % ABV, 1,328 bottles produced.
- Price: AUD 250 per bottle (regular & sale).
- Tasting notes: creamy, bright nose with spice (clove, anise, cinnamon, nutmeg), fruit (macadamia, strawberry jam, raspberry liquorice), honeycomb and spearmint. Palate full, waxy mouthfeel; layers of herbal, brandy‑soaked fruit, chocolate, vanilla custard. Finish silky with raspberry tart, maple waffles, hazelnut, pistachio.
- Distillery: Sullivans Cove, Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia.
- Release philosophy: long‑term maturation since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule.

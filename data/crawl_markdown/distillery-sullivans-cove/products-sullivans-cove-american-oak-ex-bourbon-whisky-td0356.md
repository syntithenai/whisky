# American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (TD0356)

- **Age**: 15 yrs 11 mos, first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon cask.
- **Fill/Decant**: Filled Jan 2009, decanted Dec 2024.
- **Yield**: 190 bottles, bottled at 51.2 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 400 (sold out).
- **Tasting notes**: Creamy custard opening; buttery biscuit, strawberry cheesecake, caramel éclair on the palate; mint‑lifted finish with hazelnut syrup and toasted rye.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.

The bottle is a single‑cask expression from an American oak barrel that previously held bourbon. It showcases vanilla, coconut, fruit salad, and a robust malt base, balanced by citrus and herbal lift.

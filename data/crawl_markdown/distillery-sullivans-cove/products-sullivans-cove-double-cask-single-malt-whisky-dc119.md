# Sullivans Cove Double Cask (DC119)

- **Type**: Single‑malt double‑cask whisky
- **Casks**: French oak & American oak, youngest 6 yrs, oldest 17 yrs
- **ABV**: 47.5 %
- **Bottled**: 24 Jul 2017 – 2 Sep 2023 (948 bottles)
- **Price**: AUD 250 per bottle
- **Tasting notes**: Berries, buttery malt, citrus, soft oak, caramel, vanilla, cinnamon, nutmeg, floral & herbal nuances.
- **Production**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; blended by Distillery Manager Heather Tillott and the Sensory Team.
- **Key descriptors**: creamy, fruit‑forward, silky, playful elegance, complex, integrated oak.

> *“Our dedication to long‑term maturation has remained the same since 1994… we don’t bottle to a schedule, instead we are guided by the liquid.”* – Sullivans Cove Sensory Team

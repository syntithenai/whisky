# 24 Year Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (HB0085)

Sullivans Cove’s last of the “lost casks” – a single‑cask, 24‑year‑old whisky finished in an American oak ex‑bourbon barrel. Bottled at 49.6 % ABV, only **132 bottles** were released and sold via ballot.

- **Age:** 24 y 5 m (distilled Sep 1997 – decanted Sep 2022)
- **Price:** AUD $2,500 per bottle
- **Tasting notes:** ginger‑spice, strawberry musk, watermelon, artichoke/olive brine, pink licorice, grapefruit, shortbread, cinnamon, clove, white pepper, panettone, raspberry coulis; palate of apple, pear, kiwi, guava, raspberries, banana, mango milkshake, custard tart, vanilla slice with passionfruit; finish of candied orange peel, whole‑grain cereal, honey‑milk, banana liqueur, anise seeds, coffee crema.
- **Production:** The cask was transferred to a bourbon‑finished American oak barrel for the final maturation stage. Sullivans Cove’s long‑term maturation ethos (since 1994) guided release.

> *“This whisky is remarkably well‑rounded and complex…the harmonious blend of fruit, sweet grain, and a briny richness showcases the spirit's depth.”* – Heather Tillott, Distillery Manager

# American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (TD0327)

- **Age**: 15 years, 1 month (filled June 2008, decanted December 2023). 
- **Cask**: 200 L first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon single cask. 
- **Bottles**: 193 total; ABV 47.6 %. 
- **Price**: AUD 400 (regular and sale price). 
- **Tasting notes**: biscuity richness, custard sweetness, vanilla, caramel, coconut, fruit, citrus zest, jasmine, green tea, mango milkshake, dragon‑fruit, black pepper, cherries, honey, condensed milk, nutmeg, lemon tart, mandarin, white chocolate. 
- **Production philosophy**: long‑term maturation since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule.

## Key facts
- Single cask, first‑fill American oak ex‑bourbon.
- 15 years of ageing in Tasmania.
- 193 bottles released at 47.6 % ABV.
- Awarded for its distinctive fruity spirit and complex flavour profile.

## Production & philosophy
- Matured in barrels previously used for bourbon.
- Long‑term maturation ethos: "the whisky comes good when the whisky comes good; we don’t bottle to a schedule, instead we are guided by the liquid."

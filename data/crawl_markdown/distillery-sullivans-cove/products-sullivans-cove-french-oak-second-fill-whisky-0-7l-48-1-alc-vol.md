# French Oak Second‑Fill (TD0455)

- **Age**: 11 years, distilled Nov 2013, decanted Dec 2024.
- **Cask**: Single 300 L French oak ex‑tawny cask (first‑fill for earlier single malt).
- **ABV**: 48.1 %.
- **Bottles**: 496.
- **Price**: AUD 550 per 0.7 L bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Earthy musk, dried figs, honeyed carrots, parsnip, caramelised sugars, liquorice, sweet herbs, dill, maritime brininess, toasted marshmallow, crème brûlée, golden syrup dumplings, toasted oat biscuits, treacle, dark chocolate, cranberries, cherries, waffles, maple syrup, ice cream, ripe banana.
- **Production**: Second‑fill French oak emphasises spirit character; long‑term maturation since 1994.

> *“This is a whisky that builds in intensity – opening gently before surging into deep, caramelised richness. The influence of second‑fill French oak lingers in its refined structure and depth, allowing our distillery character to shine as brine and anise weave throughout.”* – Sullivans Cove Sensory Team

## Source Excerpts
- Our French oak second-fill casks have a history as rich as their flavour.
- Initially serving as first-fill ex-tawny casks for an earlier batch of Sullivans Cove single malt, the best casks are then selected for a second round of maturation.
- American Oak Our American Oak single casks are among our most awarded expressions, beautifully enhancing our distinctive and fruity spirit.
- Old & Rare Our Old & Rare releases showcase Australia’s oldest whiskies, made possible by our unwavering commitment to long term maturation in full size barrels since 1994.
- This maturation style beautifully displays the rich character of the French oak, while allowing the distinctive spirit character of Sullivans Cove to take centre stage.
- French Oak Second-Fill 'Refill' or 'second-fill' means this barrel has been used to mature Sullivans Cove whisky before.

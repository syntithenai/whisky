# 19 Year Old American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0018)

- **Age & Cask**: 19 years 3 months, filled Feb 2005, decanted May 2024.
- **Bottles**: 228 from a 200 L American oak cask.
- **ABV**: 47.5 %.
- **Price**: AUD 800 (regular and sale).
- **Packaging**: Hand‑made solid oak box with authenticity letter signed by Distillery Manager Heather Tillott.
- **Tasting notes**: Rich chocolate, rum‑soaked fruit, tropical passionfruit & mango; vanilla cream, sultanas, dates, black tea; finish of cocoa, rum, raisin chocolate, honey, mint, soy sauce, raspberries, banana chips, walnuts, tobacco, licorice and rooibos.
- **Production**: Second‑fill American oak cask used previously for older single malt; long‑term maturation since 1994.

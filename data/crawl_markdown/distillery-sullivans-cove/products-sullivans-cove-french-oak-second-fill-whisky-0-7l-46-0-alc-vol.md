# French Oak Second‑Fill (TD0420)

- **Age**: 11 years (distilled Sep 2013, decanted Sep 2024)
- **Cask**: Single 300 L French oak ex‑tawny cask, second‑fill of an earlier Sullivans Cove single malt batch.
- **ABV**: 46 %
- **Price**: AUD 550 (regular & sale price)
- **Tasting notes**: Bright fruit (lychee, apple, pineapple), zesty lemon skin, floral bottlebrush/geranium, honey‑sweet vanilla custard, malt biscuit, waxy finish. Palate: toffee apple, sherbet, liquorice, briny artichoke, glacé cherries, coconut water, tropical harmony. Finish: cherries, saltwater, green capsicum, pink musk, strawberry, vanilla seeds, light tannin.
- **Production**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; refill casks allow spirit character to shine.

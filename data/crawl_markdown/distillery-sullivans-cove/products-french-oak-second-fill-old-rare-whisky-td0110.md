# 18 Year Old French Oak (TD0110)

- **Age & Cask**: 18 years, 9 months in a single 300 L French‑oak second‑fill cask.
- **Distillation/Decanting**: Distilled June 2006, decanted March 2025.
- **ABV**: 48.2 %.
- **Bottles**: 417 limited release.
- **Price**: AUD 1,250 (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting Notes**: Orchard‑fruit, herbal sage/thyme, spice, caramel, dark berries, syrupy finish with rose water & Turkish delight.
- **Production Insight**: Second‑fill casks allow long maturation without overpowering oak; the spirit’s character shines through.

> *“A deep and textural whisky with a striking interplay of sweet and savoury. From dense orchard fruits and herbal warmth to syrupy richness and exotic spice…”* – Sullivans Cove Sensory Team

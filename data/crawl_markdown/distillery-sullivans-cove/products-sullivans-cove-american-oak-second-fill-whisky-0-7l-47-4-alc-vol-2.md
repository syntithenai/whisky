**American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0360)** – A 47.4 % ABV single‑cask whisky aged 11 y 11 m in a 200 L American oak cask previously used for an older Sullivans Cove batch. Bottled unflocked/unfiltered, 228 bottles produced.

*Key tasting notes:* sticky entry of lolly‑snakes and limoncello; layers of apple pie, peach Danish, whipped cream, raw honey; earthy balsa wood, pepper, nutmeg; vanilla bean, dates, prunes, buttery biscuits; subtle mango skin. Palate: creamy texture with apricot jam, starfruit, red apples, ginger beer spice, walnuts, thyme, white chocolate, lactic richness, marmalade, mixed spice. Finish: white chocolate, jammy sweetness, sweet apples, Moscato, Amaro, malty midsection, herbal note, liquorice, salted caramel, cocoa, blackberry tart.

*Production:* second‑fill cask; long‑term maturation ethos since 1994; no scheduled bottling.

*Price:* AUD 400 per bottle (regular and sale price).

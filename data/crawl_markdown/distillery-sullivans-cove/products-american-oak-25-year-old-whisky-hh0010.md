# 25 Year Old American Oak (HH0010)

- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Cask**: #HH0010, American Oak refill, 25 years & 6 months.
- **Release**: Limited‑edition, no longer available for general purchase.
- **Price**: AUD 4,500 per bottle (regular price).
- **Alcohol**: 47.8 % ABV.
- **Tasting notes**:
  - *Nose*: mandarin, fig, chocolate, cherry jam, coconut, peppermint.
  - *Palate*: tropical fruits, spiced pastries, nutty depth, umami richness.
  - *Finish*: citrus, dried mango, honeyed malt, strawberries & cream, jasmine, lavender, Turkish delight.
- **Key quotes**: “A benchmark of balancing the interesting and delicious… a living record of our history.” – Heather Tillot (Distillery Manager).

This cask marks a milestone for Australian whisky, being the first 25‑year‑old single‑cask release from a New‑World distillery.

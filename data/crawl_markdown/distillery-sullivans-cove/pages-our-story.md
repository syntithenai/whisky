# Sullivans Cove – Our Story
Sullivans Cove is a Tasmanian distillery that has turned the world of whisky on its head by producing handcrafted single‑cask, single‑malt expressions. Founded in 1994, it has earned three World Whisky Awards (2014, 2018, 2019) and is known for its American Oak, French Oak and Old & Rare releases.

Key points:
- **Location**: Hobart, Tasmania, Australia
- **Philosophy**: Slow, meticulous hand‑crafting; no two batches identical
- **Awards**: World’s Best Whisky (2014, 2018, 2019)
- **Product focus**: American Oak single casks, French Oak, Old & Rare aged in full‑size barrels
- **Mission**: Promote Australian whisky globally and nurture a new generation of whisky lovers

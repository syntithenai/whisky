# Sullivans Cove – Our Story

Sullivans Cove is a Tasmanian distillery that has turned the world of whisky on its head by crafting single‑cask, single‑malt whiskies with patience and precision. Founded in Hobart, the company has earned three World’s Best Whisky awards (2014, 2018, 2019) and is known for its American Oak, French Oak, and Old & Rare releases.

The distillery emphasizes handcrafted production, a deep respect for nature, and an intimate approach that results in rare, non‑replicable whiskies. Their mission extends beyond producing award‑winning spirits to inspiring a new generation of whisky lovers worldwide.

# 16 Year Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (TD0252)

- **Age & Cask**: Filled March 2008, decanted April 2024 – 16 years 1 month in a 200 L American oak ex‑bourbon cask.
- **Yield**: 195 bottles at 48.2 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 800 (regular and sale price).
- **Tasting notes**: Tropical fruit, herbal marjoram, apricot/blackberry jam, kaffir lime leaf, cedar, coffee, black pepper, cinnamon, leather, charred corn, paprika; silky mouthfeel with liquorice, clove, bay leaf, black pepper, toasted sourdough, biscuits, roasted red capsicum, ripe apples, juicy pomelo, vanilla‑spiced dessert, cola‑like finish.
- **Packaging**: Handmade solid oak box with authenticity letter from Distillery Manager.

The expression showcases the depth and complexity that long maturation in American oak ex‑bourbon casks can deliver, balancing sweet fruit, spice, and a pronounced oak presence.

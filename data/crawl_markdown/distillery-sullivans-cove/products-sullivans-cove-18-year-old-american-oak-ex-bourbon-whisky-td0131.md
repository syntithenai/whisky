# 18 Year Old American Oak ex‑Bourbon (TD0131)

- **Age & cask**: 18 years, 3 months in a 200 L American oak bourbon cask (filled Aug 2006, decanted Dec 2024). 172 bottles released at 47.5 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 800 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Rich fruit (peach, cherry, blackberry), vanilla‑caramel sweetness, earthy umami, dried herbs, dark chocolate finish with a hint of olive brine.
- **Production philosophy**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; no fixed bottling schedule – the whisky is bottled when ready.

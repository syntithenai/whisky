# American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0424)

- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Cask**: Single 200 L American oak cask previously used for an older batch of Sullivans Cove single malt.
- **Age**: 11 years (distilled September 2013, decanted October 2024).
- **ABV**: 48.1% unfiltered, unflocked.
- **Bottles produced**: 259.
- **Price**: AUD 400 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Silky, briny core with citrus, licorice, honey, vanilla custard, nougat, and a mineral saline edge (squid‑ink/oyster shell). Sweet coconut, toffee, marshmallow, Parma violets, candied apple, coffee, cocoa, sage, buttery scones, creamy porridge.
- **Production philosophy**: Long‑term maturation since 1994; “refill” barrels allow spirit character to shine.

- **Palate**: Silky, smooth, sweet musky, shredded coconut, vanilla sponge, Madeira cake, toffee, marshmallow, Parma violets, candied apple, coffee, spice.

## Production & philosophy
- Refill barrels used to mature whisky before; staff favourite.

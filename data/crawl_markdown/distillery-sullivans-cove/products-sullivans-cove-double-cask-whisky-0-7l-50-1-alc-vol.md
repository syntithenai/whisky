# Sullivans Cove Double Cask (DC117)

- **Type**: Single‑malt double‑cask whisky
- **Casks**: French oak & American oak, youngest 6 yrs, oldest 17 yrs
- **ABV**: 50.1%
- **Bottles produced**: 1,286
- **Price**: AUD 250 (regular)
- **Tasting notes**: floral, fruity (apricot, peach), sweet (caramel, vanilla, chocolate chip cookies), spicy (cinnamon, nutmeg), herbal (wormwood, wild anise), and a complex finish with orange blossom and white pepper.

The whisky is crafted by Distillery Manager Heather Tillott and the Sensory Team, emphasising long‑term maturation since 1994.

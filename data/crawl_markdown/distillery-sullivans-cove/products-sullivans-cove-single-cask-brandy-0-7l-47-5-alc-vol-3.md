# XO Single Cask Pinot Noir Brandy (TDB0035)

- **Distilled** from 100 % Tasmanian Pinot Noir.
- **Matured** for 13 years, 11 months in a 300‑L French oak ex‑tawny cask.
- **Bottled** at 47.5 % ABV, unblended and without added colour or flavour.
- **Price**: AUD 275 per 0.7 L bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: chocolate, ganache, tonka bean, balsamic vinegar, violet, pomelo rind, walnuts, raspberries, rum‑raisin ice cream, cinnamon, marshmallow, strawberries & cream, mint lollies, orange, bell pepper, dried mango, lychee, rose petals, Comice pear, dried apricot, leather, prune, gamey richness, nutmeg, cigar box, clove, truffle, toasted sourdough, boysenberries, and Pinot Noir grape.
- **Production philosophy**: long‑term maturation guided by the spirit itself rather than a schedule.

# 17 Year Old French Oak Cask Strength (TD0297)

- **Age & cask**: 17 years, 3 months in a 300‑L French oak cask previously used for tawny port.
- **ABV**: 61.3 % at bottling.
- **Bottles produced**: 343 from the original cask.
- **Tasting notes**: Grapefruit skin, Barossa tawny depth, glacé pineapple, candied apple, juicy dark fruit, chocolate, spice, nuts, earthy herbal nuances; palate of kumquat, green apple, lychee, nougat, cantaloupe, cola, curry leaf; finish with choc‑orange, jasmine, borage, shortbread, Pu’erh tea.
- **Packaging**: Hand‑made Tasmanian Blackwood box with a letter from Distillery Manager Heather Tillott.

This is Sullivans Cove’s first age‑stated whisky bottled at cask strength and the only French Oak ex‑tawny released in this format.

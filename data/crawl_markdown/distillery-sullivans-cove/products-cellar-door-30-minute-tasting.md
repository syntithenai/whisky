# Sullivans Cove Whisky Tastings

Sullivans Cove offers guided tastings of its award‑winning single‑cask whiskies. The experience is open to newcomers and aficionados alike, with pricing starting at **$30 per hour**. Participants under 18 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

The tasting showcases the distillery’s range of casks – Double Cask American Oak, French Oak, and Old & Rare releases – each celebrated for their fruit‑forward, creamy profiles and international accolades.

> *“Join us for a guided tasting of our award‑winning single cask whiskies – an experience to remember for newcomers and aficionados alike.”*

## Key Details
- **Location:** 1/10 Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia
- **Price:** $30 per hour (tasting experience)
- **Age Restriction:** Minors must be accompanied by a parent or guardian.

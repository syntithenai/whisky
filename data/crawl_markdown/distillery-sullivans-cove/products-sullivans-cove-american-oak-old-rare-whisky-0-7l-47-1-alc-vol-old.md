# 18 Year Old American Oak ex‑Bourbon (TD0125)

- **Age & Cask**: 18 years, single cask TD0125 filled Aug 2006, decanted Sep 2024.
- **Yield**: 212 bottles at 47.1 % ABV from a 200 L American oak cask that had once held bourbon.
- **Price**: AUD 800 (regular and sale price).
- **Availability**: No longer available for general purchase; limited‑edition, contact concierge or subscribe to mailing list.
- **Tasting notes**: Soft creamy nose of marshmallow, dried apricot, brown sugar, milk chocolate, browned butter, cheddar‑like savory, pine nuts, daisies, jasmine and beach sand. Palate: honey, buttery pastry, apple tea cake, toasted muesli, biscuity grainy finish with cinnamon, clove, anise, cranberries, mulberries and orange marmalade.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia – long‑term maturation ethos since 1994.

> *“This whisky is wonderfully full‑bodied and fruit‑forward, showcasing incredible depth of spirit and a complexity that evolves beautifully in the glass.”* – Sensory Team

# French Oak Ex‑Apera (TD0820)

- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia.
- **Cask**: Single 225 L French oak ex‑Apera barrique.
- **Age**: 9 years 10 months (distilled Nov 2015, decanted Sep 2025).
- **ABV**: 47.6 %.
- **Bottles**: 260 from the cask.
- **Price**: AUD 550 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Citrus‑dense nose (blood orange, lemon, lime), chocolate‑orange, candied fruit; full‑bodied palate with honeycomb, rum, raisin, caramel, sticky cherry and mint; long finish of dark chocolate, roasted peaches, butterscotch, grapefruit, lime soda, caramelised banana and praline.
- **Production philosophy**: Long‑term maturation since 1994, no fixed bottling schedule – “the whisky comes good when the whisky comes good.”

The product is a single‑cask release that showcases the influence of French oak on a naturally full Tasmanian spirit, with layers of bright sticky fruits and herbal lifts.

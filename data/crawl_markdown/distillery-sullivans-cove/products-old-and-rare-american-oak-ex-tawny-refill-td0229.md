# 17 Year Old American Oak (TD0229)

- **Age**: 17 years, 7 months
- **Cask**: TD0229 – single‑cask American oak ex‑tawny refill
- **Maturation**: Minimum 16 years in Australian barrels
- **Bottle count**: 431
- **ABV**: 46.2 %
- **Price**: AUD 800 (regular)
- **Tasting notes**: Bright, creamy nose with orchard fruit, soft spice and a savory thread; full palate of honeyed sweetness, baked fruit, warming spice; long finish with vanilla, light spice and pastry‑like sweetness.

# Double Cask – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove’s **Double Cask** range blends French and American oak‑aged single‑malt whisky to create a creamy, fruit‑forward spirit that showcases the breadth of age and cask types in their bondstore. Each release is crafted by Distillery Manager Heather Tillott and her Sensory Team.

Key points:
- **Cask blend**: French Oak + American Oak.
- **Release example**: Batch DC122 (2026) – $250 AUD per bottle.
- **Availability**: Sold out or available via waitlist, ballot, or cellar‑door visit.
- **Awards**: 2018 World Whisky Awards Best Australian Single Malt; 2017 Luxury Spirits Masters Gold Medal for Double Cask (DC30TH).

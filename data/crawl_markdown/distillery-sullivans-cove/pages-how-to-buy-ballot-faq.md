# Ballot FAQ – Sullivans Cove Whisky

Sullivans Cove uses a ballot system to distribute its highly‑demanded Old & Rare single‑cask releases. The page explains why ballots are used, how the technology works, and what customers need to know about entering, winning, and payment.

Key points:
- Ballots ensure fair access for genuine supporters and prevent bots or resellers from snatching bottles.
- Each ballot entry is verified through a multi‑step security process (human check, 3D Secure card verification, one entry per person/household).
- Winners are notified by SMS/email; they must pay the retail price plus shipping if selected.
- Entry does not guarantee purchase; withdrawal before draw is possible but not after confirmation.
- Payment methods: credit/debit cards (including AmEx); PayPal not supported.

The page also includes standard legal notices, contact links and a brief overview of Sullivans Cove’s product categories.

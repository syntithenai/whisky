# 20 Year Old American Oak (TD0015)

- **Age & Maturation**: Filled Jan 2005, decanted Feb 2025 – 20 yrs 1 mo.
- **Cask**: 200 L American‑oak second‑fill; 171 bottles at 47.8 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 1 000 (regular & sale).
- **Tasting Notes**: Creamy, sweetened condensed milk, marshmallow, toasted coconut, warm biscuits; spice (incense, cinnamon quill, peppercorn, coriander seed); fruit (cherry, plum, banana, fig, agave nectar). Palate: oak grip, spiced warmth, crème caramel, strawberry jam, butterscotch frosting, ginger ale. Finish: juicy fruit loaf, Boston bun, blood plum, candied orange, sage, pistachio paste, toffee, marzipan.
- **Packaging**: Handmade solid‑oak box with a letter from the Distillery Manager.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Lamb Place, Cambridge, Tasmania, Australia.

> *“Deliciously warming from start to finish, with seamless flow between sweetness, fruit and oak spice. Balanced, structured and quietly complex.” – Sensory Team*

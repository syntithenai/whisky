# 17 Year Old American Oak Ex‑Bourbon (TD0129)

- **Age & Cask**: 17 years, 3 months in a 200 L American oak ex‑bourbon cask (filled Aug 2006, decanted Mar 2024). 180 bottles at 47.5 % ABV.
- **Price**: AUD 800 per bottle.
- **Tasting notes**: Coconut‑raspberry, eucalyptus bark, apple juice; sweet berry, lychee, blackberry, citrus peels; grain, maple bacon, cardamom; lemon sherbet, mandarin, apricot‑coconut; white pepper, roasted artichokes; tropical fruits, banana; finish with vanilla musk, coriander seeds, mint.
- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia – long‑term maturation since 1994.

> *“A beautifully balanced whisky, showcasing vibrant spirit and bold first‑fill cask influence. Sweet and savoury layers interplay seamlessly…”* – Sullivans Cove Sensory Team.

# American Oak Second‑Fill (TD0462)

- **Distillery**: Sullivans Cove, Tasmania, Australia
- **Product**: Single‑cask American Oak second‑fill whisky, cask No. TD0462
- **Age**: 10 years 2 months
- **ABV**: 49.2 %
- **Price**: AUD 400 (regular and sale price)
- **Bottles produced**: 265
- **Key tasting notes**: bright fruit (pineapple, apricot, pear, banana), warm spices (cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg), vanilla‑rich American oak, buttery crumpets, sourdough, lemon curd, dark dates/rum, sherbet‑like tangy finish.
- **Production**: aged in a 200 L American oak cask previously used for an older batch of Sullivans Cove single malt; unflocked and unfiltered.

The whisky is described as radiant, complex, and balanced, with a vibrant fruit‑spice character that evolves through the palate to a zippy citrus finish.

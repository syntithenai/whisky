# Sullivans Cove Whisky Collection
Sullivans Cove offers a range of single‑cask Australian whiskies, including Double Cask blends, American Oak and French Oak expressions, and an Old & Rare line featuring some of the country’s oldest bottles. Prices vary from AUD 250 for a standard double‑cask to AUD 2,500 for 17‑year‑old French Oak cask‑strength releases.

## Key product categories
- **Double Cask** – creamy, fruit‑forward style.
- **American Oak** – award‑winning single‑cask whiskies.
- **French Oak** – world‑champion expressions (World’s Best 2014 & 2019).
- **Old & Rare** – long‑matured bottles from full‑size barrels since 1994.

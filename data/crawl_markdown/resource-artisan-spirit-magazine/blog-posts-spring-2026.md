# Spring 2026 Issue of Artisan Spirit Magazine

The March 25, 2026 issue introduces a range of industry topics—from building new distilleries to sensory vocabulary—and highlights featured distilleries such as Greenbar, Hollywood, and J. Rieger & Co.

Key article themes:
- Avoiding costly mistakes when building a new distillery
- Stillage processing with centrifugation
- The science behind the new alcohol guidelines

# Spring 2025 Issue of Artisan Spirit Magazine

The March 20, 2025 edition celebrates the magazine’s 50th issue and 12‑year anniversary. It highlights industry resilience amid challenges such as new single‑malt standards, waste management, and legal changes. Featured content includes award winners (Ted Huber, Starlight Distillery), articles on alternating proprietorships, cream liqueur FAQs, rum lessons, opposition to identity standards, and fall protection strategies. The issue spotlights several distilleries—Copper Fox, Frey Ranch, Glenglassaugh Hillrock Estate, JAZ Spirits, Los Poblanos’ Town & Ranch Tasting Room, Minden Mill, Port Ellen, Root Shoot Rosebank, Rustic Farm, Split Spirits, Starlight, and Del Bac. The magazine remains independent, supported by sponsors such as the American Spirits Exchange, CF Napa Brand Design, Lallemand Biofuels, and others.

The page also lists subscription options (physical, digital, complimentary for craft distillers) and provides links to past issues, advertiser index, and industry events calendar.

# A Yarn with Morris Head Distiller Darren Peck

The article is a profile of **Darren Peck**, the head distiller at **Morris Whisky** in Australia. It traces his journey from a Yorkshire upbringing during Thatcher’s Britain, through an environmental science degree and early beer‑fermentation work, to a global career that has taken him across six continents. Peck’s adventures include working for a large Dublin brewery, making rum in Venezuela, and a memorable encounter with Russian police on a train near Moscow. The piece highlights how his diverse experiences have shaped his approach to whisky at Morris, where he is credited with pioneering the brand’s **Tokay Barrel** release.

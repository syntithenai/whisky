# The Whisky Club Australia

The Whisky Club is a free‑to‑join subscription service that delivers exclusive, award‑winning single‑malts and other rare whiskies straight to members’ doors each month. Members receive a *Whisky of the Month* (typically $120–$150 per bottle) plus optional extras, with no minimum spend or contract. The club boasts over 38 000 members and partners with top distilleries worldwide to create bespoke releases.

Key features:
- **Membership**: Free sign‑up; choose one‑bottle or two‑bottle plans.
- **Monthly delivery**: Receive the Whisky of the Month, with a 3‑day window to add extras or opt out.
- **Exclusive releases**: Limited‑edition bottlings (e.g., Loch Lomond 2010 Sauternes Cask, Glen Scotia Single Cask #3787, Meikle Tōir Turbo Peated).
- **Member perks**: Access to events, discounted whisky at partner bars, masterclasses, and travel prizes.
- **Community**: Over 38 000 members share reviews and experiences on the site.

The page also lists upcoming releases (Ardbeg Dolce, Sullivans Cove Double Cask, Macallan Harmony Amber Meadow) with tasting notes, ABV, pricing, and availability dates.

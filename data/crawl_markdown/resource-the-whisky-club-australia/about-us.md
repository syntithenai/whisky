# About The Whisky Club

The Whisky Club is the world's largest community of whisky lovers, serving over 38,000 active Members worldwide. Established in 2014, the club works directly with distilleries across Scotland, Ireland, Japan and beyond to create multi-award winning whiskies exclusively for its Members.

## Key Facts
- **Membership**: Exclusive access to specially crafted whiskies from renowned distilleries
- **Distribution**: Whiskies delivered monthly to Members' doorsteps
- **Exclusivity**: Only available to Club Members, with collectors bidding up to 20x the original Member price at auctions
- **History**: Founded in 2014, serving over 38,000 active Members today

## Production Signals
- **Distillery Partnerships**: Collaborations with top distilleries like Drs Bill Lumsden (Glenmorangie), Kelsey McKechnie (Balvenie), Alex Thomas (Bushmills) and Graeme Cruickshank (Aberlour)
- **Exclusive Creations**: Whiskies created exclusively for The Whisky Club, akin to a limited-edition sports car made by Ferrari for its club members

## Commercial Signals
- **Value Appreciation**: Collectors often bid up to 20x the original Member price at auctions
- **Membership Benefits**: Discounted drams and cellar door purchases at global Clubhouse network locations
- **Whisky Education**: Regular online masterclass tastings with world-class whisky makers

## Risks/Unknowns

- **Distillery Relationships**: Maintaining strong partnerships with distilleries to secure exclusive releases

# Australia’s Coolest Whisky Distillery Experiences

The article lists seven Australian distilleries worth visiting, highlighting their unique features and locations:

- **Lark Distillery** – Tasmania’s birthplace of Australian whisky, uses local barley and peat.

- **Joadja Distillery** – New South Wales; on‑site barley, solar‑powered still, Spanish cask heritage.

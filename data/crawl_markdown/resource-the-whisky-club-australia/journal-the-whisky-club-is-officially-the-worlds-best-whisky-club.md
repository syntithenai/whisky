# The Whisky Club is officially the world’s best whisky club
The article announces that The Whisky Club has been ranked 31st in the International Wine & Spirit Competition (IWSC) Top 50 producers, making it the highest‑ranked whisky club globally and the top Australian‑based producer. It highlights the club’s partnership with over 2,300 distilleries, its focus on creating exclusive whiskies for members rather than independent bottling, and the recognition of partner distilleries such as William Grant & Sons, Chivas Brothers, Irish Distillers, The Glenmorangie Company, and Campari. The piece also notes media coverage in outlets like Broadsheet, Food & Drinks Business, and Melbourne Insider.

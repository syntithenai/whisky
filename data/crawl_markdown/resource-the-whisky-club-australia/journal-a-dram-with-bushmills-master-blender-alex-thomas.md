# An interview with Bushmills' Master Blender Alex Thomas

- **Interview focus**: Alex Thomas, Master Blender at The Old Bushmills Distillery.
- **Background**: Born in Ballymoney, joined Bushmills in 2004, became Master Blender in 2021.
- **Key product highlighted**: *Bushmills 2012 Burgundy Cask* – first whiskey overseen by Alex from lay‑down to final maturation in Burgundy wine casks.
- **Distillery highlights**: 400‑year history, located near the Giant’s Causeway, uses water from Saint Columb's Rill, operates as a grain‑to‑glass distillery.
- **Personal note**: Alex expresses pride in the 2012 Burgundy Cask and invites readers to taste it at the club.

# A dram (or two…) with GlenDronach’s whisky queen

The Whisky Club Australia features an interview with **Dr Rachel Barrie** – Master Blender at GlenDronach, BenRiach and Glenglassaugh. The piece traces her career from a research scientist to Scotch‑whisky master blender, highlights her honorary doctorate, and showcases her views on women in whisky, brand strategy for female consumers, and the future of Scotch whisky.

Key points:
- 27 years’ experience at Glenmorangie, Ardbeg, Bowmore, BenRiach.
- Became a Master Blender in 2003; honorary doctorate (Hon. DSc.).
- Emphasises confidence, opportunity and diversity for women in the trade.
- Advises brand managers to focus on individuality rather than stereotypes.
- Looks forward to growth, enrichment and celebrating whisky character.

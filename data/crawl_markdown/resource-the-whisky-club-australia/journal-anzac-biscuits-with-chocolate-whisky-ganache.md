**Anzac Biscuits with Chocolate Whisky Ganache**

A simple recipe that turns classic Australian Anzac biscuits into a whisky‑infused treat. The biscuit base uses plain flour, caster sugar, desiccated coconut, rolled oats and sunflower seeds, bound together with butter, golden syrup, baking soda and hot water. After baking, the biscuits are sandwiched with a chocolate ganache made from dark chocolate, double cream, golden syrup, whisky and butter.

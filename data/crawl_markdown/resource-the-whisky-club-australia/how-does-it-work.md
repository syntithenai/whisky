# The Whisky Club Australia – How It Works

The page explains the club’s subscription model: free sign‑up, choose a one‑ or two‑bottle membership, receive an exclusive whisky each month, and have the option to add extra bottles or skip a delivery. Prices are $120–$150 per bottle, with no minimum spend or contracts.

It lists several upcoming **exclusive releases** (e.g., The Macallan Harmony Collection Amber Meadow, Loch Lomond 2010 Vintage Sauternes Cask, Ardbeg Dolce – Ardbeg Day 2026, and Sullivans Cove Batch‑Strength Double Cask). ## Key Facts
- Membership is free to join; payment occurs when you order.
- One‑bottle membership: $120–$150 per bottle.
- Two‑bottle membership: $240–$300 per month.
- No contracts or minimum spend.
- Members receive a single, exclusive whisky each month.
- Option to add extra bottles or skip a delivery before billing.
- Launch dates for featured releases are all 1 May 2026.

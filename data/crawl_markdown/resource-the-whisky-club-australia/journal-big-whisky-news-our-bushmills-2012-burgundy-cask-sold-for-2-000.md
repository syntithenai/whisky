# Big Whisky News: Our Bushmills 2012 Burgundy Cask Sold for $2,000

- The **Bushmills 2012 Burgundy Cask** was released exclusively for The Whisky Club in October 2022.
- It sold on the auction site Uniquely Irish on 12 Feb 2023 for €1,350 (≈$2,000) after four bids.
- Australian members received it for only $135.
- This cask is the final release of Bushmills’ Heritage Casks trilogy, following the Marsala (2020) and Banyuls (2021) editions.
- Master Blender Alex Thomas calls it “one of the best whiskeys that Bushmills have produced to date.”
- The club highlights auction prices to demonstrate collective buying power rather than encouraging resale.
- Subsequent releases include the 2010 Double Moscatel Cask (Whisky Club’s Whisky of the Year 2024) and the Christmas Causeway 2012 Vintage, available only to members.

## Key Takeaways
1. **Exclusive release** – Only for The Whisky Club members.
2. **High auction value** – €1,350 ($2,000) overseas; $135 locally.
3. **Heritage Casks trilogy** – Marsala → Banyuls → Burgundy.
4. **Master Blender endorsement** – Alex Thomas praises the cask.
5. **Member‑only pricing strategy** – Demonstrates club’s value proposition.

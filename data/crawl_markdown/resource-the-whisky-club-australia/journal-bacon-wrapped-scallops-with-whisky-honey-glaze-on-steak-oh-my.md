# Bacon‑Wrapped Scallops with Whisky‑Honey Glaze

A quick, indulgent dish that pairs sweet whisky‑honey glaze with savory scallops wrapped in bacon and served atop a juicy steak. The recipe calls for Scotch fillet steak, Tullibardine The Murray Quinterris whisky, honey, butter, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and optional vegetables.

**Key steps**
1. Soak toothpicks to prevent burning.
2. Cook bacon until flexible (not crispy) and drain on paper towel.
3. Make glaze: melt butter & brown sugar, add whisky, let alcohol evaporate (~1 min), stir in vanilla and honey.
4. Wrap scallops with bacon, secure with toothpicks, brush with glaze.
5. Sear scallops in the same pan, then cook steak to desired doneness (medium‑rare used).
6. Plate steak, place scallops on top, drizzle remaining glaze, pour a glass of whisky.

The dish is described as “super easy” and suitable for two people.

---

## Ingredients
- Bacon strips (streaky)
- 12 scallops
- 2 Scotch fillet steaks
- 15 ml Tullibardine The Murray Quinterris whisky
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- ¼ cup raw honey
- 2 Tbsp butter
- 2 Tbsp brown sugar
- Splash olive oil
- Vegetables (e.g., broccoli, carrots)

## Equipment
- Cutting board, knife, small saucepan, cooking spoon, tongs, large flat‑bottomed frying pan, BBQ/grill, toothpicks, bowl of water, plates, paper towel, whisky glass.

---

### Notes
- Bacon should not be overcooked; it needs to stay flexible for wrapping.
- The glaze can be doubled if desired; keep some reserve for serving.
- Serve with a glass of the same whisky used in the glaze.

# Kitty Hawk Distillery

Handcrafted boutique spirits from Queensland, Australia.

## Key Facts
- Distilled in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia
- Small-batch production focusing on quality over quantity
- Unique Australian flavor profile

## Production Signals
- **Ingredients**: Local Queensland grains and water sources
- **Process**: Traditional copper pot stills for distillation
- **Maturation**: Aged in reclaimed oak barrels from other spirits industries

## Commercial Signals
- Limited release expressions highlighting rare casks
- Collaborations with Australian artists and chefs
- Premium pricing reflecting boutique status

## Risks / Unknowns
- Dependence on Queensland's climate for grain quality
- Small production scale limiting economies of scale
- Brand awareness in international markets

# Kitty Hawk Distillery Overview

Kitty Hawk Distillery is a Brisbane‑based craft distillery that specialises in small‑batch copper pot still products. Their portfolio includes:
- **Single‑malt whiskies** such as *The Kitty Hawk ‘Byron Double’*, aged in ex‑Bourbon oak and finished in a rum cask from Lord Byron Rum Distillery, and *Stratus*, blended with an Oloroso sherry cask and ex‑Bourbon American oak.
- **Branded brandy** – the *Grandom Apple Brandy (eau de vie de cidre)* is made from Queensland Granite Belt apples, fermented by Martin Rellstab at Sunshine Coast Cider, double‑distilled on Kitty Hawk’s copper pot stills and aged in new French oak plus ex‑Cognac casks.
- **Gin range** – the flagship *Kitty Hawk Original Hand‑crafted Gin* uses 10 botanicals distilled on Australian copper pots, while the *Finger Lime Gin* showcases local Queensland finger limes from the Scenic Rim with lime‑caviar pearls.

All spirits are produced in Brisbane and sold through an online store that offers direct purchase links. The distillery emphasises collaboration (e.g., with Diviner’s Distillery), unique cask finishes, and the use of regional ingredients to create distinct flavour profiles.

## Key Product Highlights
- **Byron Double** – 47 % ABV, rich & spicy with warm rum notes.

- **Finger Lime Gin** – 70 % ABV, citrus with lime caviar pearls.

## Production & Distillation
All products are distilled on copper pot stills built in Australia. The distillery uses a combination of traditional techniques (double‑distillation for brandy) and modern finishing methods (ex‑Bourbon oak, sherry, rum, rye casks). They collaborate with other Australian distilleries to source unique cask finishes.

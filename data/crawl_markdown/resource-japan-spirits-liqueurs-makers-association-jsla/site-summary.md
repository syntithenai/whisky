## Long Description
The Japan Spirits & Liqueurs Makers Association (JSLA) is the primary industry body that compiles production data, publishes legal frameworks, and offers support services for distillers in Japan. Its website hosts a range of documents: annual production statistics for whisky, rum, gin, shochu, and other spirits; regulatory reports covering labeling, packaging, quality control, and environmental compliance; and practical guides on navigating government assistance programs such as IT investment aid and tax services. JSLA also collaborates with international bodies like WHO to set safety standards and shares best‑practice resources for both small craft distillers and large commercial producers.

The resource serves a broad audience: craft distillers seeking market data, students studying Japanese spirits production, industry professionals needing regulatory updates, and policymakers monitoring the sector. It offers downloadable PDFs, Excel tables, and public reports that are freely accessible, making it an essential reference for anyone involved in Japan’s whisky and spirits ecosystem.

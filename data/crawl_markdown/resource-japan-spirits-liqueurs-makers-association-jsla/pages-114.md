# Japanese Whisky‑Related Legal Framework

This page lists the statutes, regulations and guidelines that govern the production, sale, and marketing of whisky in Japan. It covers:
- **Licensing and registration** for distilleries and related businesses.
- **Quality control** requirements (raw materials, production processes, labeling).
- **Advertising and consumer protection** rules.
- **Environmental and safety standards** for the industry.

The information is presented as a public‑policy reference rather than product data.

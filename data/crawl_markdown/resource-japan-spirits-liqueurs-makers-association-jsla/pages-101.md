# Japan Spirits & Liqueurs Makers Association (JSLA) – Overview of the Japanese Whisky Production Organization

- **Purpose**: The page outlines the role and responsibilities of the *Japan Whisky Production Organization* (日本ウイスキー製造組合), a public‑body entity that coordinates activities related to whisky production, regulation, and promotion in Japan.
- **Key Themes**:
  - Legal framework for whisky manufacturing, including safety, environmental protection, and quality control.
  - Standards for ingredients, production processes, labeling, and age statements (e.g., VO, VSO, VSOP, XO).
  - Guidance on marketing, distribution, and consumer information.
  - Collaboration with international bodies such as WHO and the Alcoholic Beverage Association.

The page is primarily a regulatory and informational resource rather than a product catalogue or review site.

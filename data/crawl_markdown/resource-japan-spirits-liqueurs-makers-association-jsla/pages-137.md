# Japan Spirits & Liqueurs Makers Association (JSLA) – Home Page

The JSLA website provides a comprehensive overview of the Japanese whisky industry, including:
- **Industry statistics and legal framework**: annual reports, regulatory updates, and statistical data on production, consumption, and trade.
- **Policy and environmental initiatives**: information on sustainability measures, CO₂ reduction, and public‑private cooperation.
- **Industry resources**: links to research papers, guidelines for whisky production, and glossary terms.
- **Organizational structure**: details about the association’s history, governance, and member services.

The page is primarily a hub of navigation links to PDFs, news articles, and internal sections such as *Activity Reports*, *Statistics & Law*, and *Know & Enjoy*.

---

## Key Points
- JSLA was established in 1953 under the *Whisky Act*.
- The association publishes annual activity reports and statistical data on whisky production and trade.
- It promotes environmental initiatives, including CO₂ reduction and sustainable packaging.
- Resources include guidelines for whisky production, terminology glossaries, and legal references.

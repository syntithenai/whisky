# Limeburners - Great Southern Distilling Company

## Key Facts
- Located in Southland, New Zealand
- Established in 2015 by Duncan and Fiona MacLeod
- Produces single malt whisky using locally sourced barley
- Focuses on creating unique expressions that showcase the region's terroir

## Production Signals
- Small-batch distillery with limited production capacity
- Uses traditional copper pot stills for fermentation and distillation
- Maturation takes place in ex-bourbon barrels, imparting distinct flavors
- Limited availability of releases due to small-scale production

## Commercial Signals
- Emphasis on storytelling and heritage through whisky expressions
- Collaborations with local artists and musicians to create unique experiences
- Focus on sustainability and environmental stewardship
- Strong brand identity built around the concept of "Great Southern"

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited market presence due to small-scale production
- Dependence on local barley supply for consistent quality
- Potential challenges in scaling up production while maintaining quality standards

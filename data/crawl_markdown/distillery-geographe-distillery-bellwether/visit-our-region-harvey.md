# Explore Harvey in the Geographe Wine Region

## There's a lot more to Harvey than meets the eye!
- Stirling Cottage, located in the town of Harvey, is the first home of May Gibbs, the celebrated children's author, where the inspiration for Snugglepot and Cuddlepie developed.
- Harvey is also home to some incredible street art. You'll discover murals in the main street, shovel sculpture at the Diversion as you enter Harvey and a painted concrete seat in middle of town – it's worth taking a look!

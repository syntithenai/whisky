# Geographe Wine Association
The Geographe Wine Association (GWA) is dedicated to showcasing the rich diversity of the Geographe Bay wine region in Western Australia. The website serves as a hub for growers, producers, and visitors, offering comprehensive resources such as a wineries directory, regional map, blog posts, events calendar, and a toolkit for industry stakeholders.

## Key Highlights
- **Region**: Spanning from Harvey in the north through Capel to Busselton, extending eastwards to Donnybrook and Ferguson Valley.
- **Diversity**: Home to 57 growers, 26 producers, and 36 grape varieties, making it WA’s most varied wine region.
- **Resources**:
  - Wineries directory with contact details
  - Interactive regional map
  - Blog covering grape variety diversity, wine show navigation, and regional promotion strategies
  - Events calendar featuring the annual Geographe Wine Show
  - GWIA Toolkit for growers and producers
- **Wine Show**: An annual competition that publishes results online; the latest 2025 results are available.

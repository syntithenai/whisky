# Geographe Wine Region Overview

## Ferguson Valley
- **Growing Conditions**: Ideal for medium to full-bodied wines including Shiraz, Tempranillo, Cabernet Sauvignon, Semillon and Chardonnay.
- **Climate**: Heat Degree days: 1700; Sunshine hours per day: 8.5; Annual Rainfall: 700-900
- **Growing Season Rainfall**: Mean Jan temp: 22; Harvest: Early Feb to Early April
- **Topography & Soil**: Hilly, Gravelly sandy loams.
- **Water**: Surface water.

## Donnybrook
- **Growing Conditions**: Known for superb wines especially Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc and Cabernet Sauvignon.
- **Climate**: Heat Degree days: 1700; Sunshine hours per day: 8.5; Annual Rainfall: 700-900
- **Topography & Soil**: Hilly and coastal plain, Gravelly sandy loams.
- **Water**: Surface water.

## Capel
- **Growing Conditions**: Mediterranean climate with hot dry summers and cool wet winters.
- **Climate**: Heat Degree days: 1700; Sunshine hours per day: 8.5; Annual Rainfall: 790-900
- **Topography & Soil**: Flat coastal plain rising to an average elevation of 170 metres through the Whicher Range, Gravelly ironstone, free draining loamy soils and sandy soils on the coastal plain.
- **Water**: Spring and ground water in the Whicher Range; Groundwater on the coastal plain.

## Busselton
- **Growing Conditions**: Ideal for full-bodied table wines due to high ripening temperatures and sunshine hours offset by cooling afternoon breezes from Geographe Bay.
- **Climate**: Heat Degree days: 1700; Sunshine hours per day: 8.5; Annual Rainfall: 790-900
- **Topography & Soil**: Flat coastal plain rising to an average elevation of 170 metres through the Whicher Range, Gravelly ironstone, free draining loamy soils and sandy soils on the coastal plain.
- **Water**: Spring and ground water in the Whicher Range; Groundwater on the coastal plain.

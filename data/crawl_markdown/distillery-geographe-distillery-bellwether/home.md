# Geographe Wine Association

The Geographe Wine Association (GWA) promotes the diverse wine region of Geographe Bay in Western Australia. The site highlights the region’s geography, its 57 growers, 26 producers and 36 grape varieties, and offers links to wineries, events, a toolkit, and the annual Geographe Wine Show.

## Key Points
- **Region**: Extends from Harvey (north) through Capel to Busselton, eastwards to Donnybrook and Ferguson Valley.
- **Diversity**: 57 growers, 26 producers, 36 grape varieties – the most diverse WA wine region.
- **Resources**: Wineries directory, regional map, blog, events calendar, GWIA Toolkit, Wine Show information.
- **Wine Show**: Annual competition with results posted online; 2025 results available.

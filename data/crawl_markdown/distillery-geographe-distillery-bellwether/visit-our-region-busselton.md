# Geographe Distillery (Bellwether) - Busselton, Western Australia

## Key Facts
- Located in the heart of the Geographe Wine Region near Busselton, WA
- Focus on premium single malt whisky production
- Small batch distillery with a focus on quality and craftsmanship

## Production Signals
- Limited edition releases showcasing unique flavour profiles
- Use of traditional copper pot stills for handcrafted spirit
- Emphasis on oak maturation in American white oak barrels

## Commercial Signals
- Collaborations with local wineries and producers in the region
- Participation in regional wine shows and events
- Strong brand positioning as a premium, locally crafted whisky

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited distribution beyond the Geographe Wine Region
- Dependence on tourism and visitor numbers to Busselton area

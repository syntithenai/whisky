# Buffalo Trace Distillery Overview

- **Location**: Frankfort, Kentucky
- **Founded**: 1775 (Leestown)
- **Distillery Type**: Bourbon Whiskey
- **Awards**: World's most award-winning distillery with over 600 employees
- **Mission**: Create exceptional spirits, share them globally, and preserve bourbon heritage

## Key Facts
- Oldest continually operating distillery in America
- Crafted bourbon on the same Kentucky grounds for over 200 years
- Home to over 20 spirit brands including Buffalo Trace, W.L. Weller, Eagle Rare, Blanton's, etc.
- National Historic Landmark since 2013
- Distillery open daily (except holidays) with tours and visitor center

## Production Signals
- Hand-selected grains from Kentucky & Indiana farms
- Pure limestone water used in distillation
- Copper stills for traditional distillation
- Aging in charred white oak barrels (70-80 years old)
- Over 1,000 awards for premium spirits

## Commercial Signals
- Consistently top-ranked in international whiskey competitions
- Strong brand positioning around heritage and craftsmanship
- Widely distributed globally with multiple bourbon brands
- Successful partnerships with Sazerac (e.g., Eagle Rare)

## Risks / Unknowns
- Dependence on bourbon market trends
- Potential regulatory changes affecting distillation practices
- Supply chain risks for grain inputs
- Competition from new craft distilleries and premium spirits

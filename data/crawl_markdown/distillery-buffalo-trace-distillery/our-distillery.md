# Buffalo Trace Distillery – Our Distillery

- **Location & Heritage**: Frankfort, Kentucky; oldest continually operating distillery in the U.S., founded 1775 as Leestown.
- **Historic Milestones**: E.H. Taylor Jr. (1870), George T. Stagg (1904), renamed Buffalo Trace (1999). National Historic Landmark (2013).
- **Production**: Hand‑selected grains from Kentucky & Indiana, limestone water, copper stills, 70–80 yr old white oak barrels charred to perfection, aging in warehouses dating back to 1881.
- **Brands**: Buffalo Trace, W.L. Weller, Eagle Rare, Sazerac Rye, Traveller, E.H. Taylor Jr., Blanton’s Single Barrel, George T. Stagg.
- **People**: Col. Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr., George T. Stagg, Albert B. Blanton, William L. Weller.
- **Awards**: 1,000+ awards; recent accolades include Double Platinum ASCOT (2022) and San Francisco World Spirits Competition Silver (2019).

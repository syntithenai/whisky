# Benchmark Old No. 8

**Benchmark Old No. 8** is the flagship Kentucky Straight Bourbon from Buffalo Trace Distillery. It offers a caramel‑laden nose, a robust palate of leather, tobacco and oak with hints of dried cherries, and a smooth, calming finish. The spirit is 80 proof (40 % ABV) and made from Mash Bill #1.

## Tasting Notes
- **Nose**: Caramel, stone‑fruit backdrop
- **Palate**: Leather, dry tobacco, oak, dried cherries
- **Finish**: Smooth, calming

## Key Facts
- 80 proof (40 % ABV)
- Straight Bourbon
- Mash Bill #1
- Awarded Silver at multiple competitions in 2024‑25

# Blanton's Gold Edition

Blanton’s Gold is a limited‑edition, 103‑proof (51.5 %) single‑barrel bourbon released by Buffalo Trace Distillery. It builds on the original Blanton’s line with higher proof and deeper, spicier complexity aimed at international markets.

**Tasting notes** – spicy rye & tobacco nose; caramel, honey, dark fruit and citrus on the palate; oak and vanilla finish.

**Key facts** – each bottle has a unique horse‑jockey stopper (letters from “Blanton’s”), barrel dump date, and is highly collectible. The original Blanton’s was launched in 1984 as the world’s first single‑barrel bourbon.

**Serving suggestion** – neat or on the rocks.

## Distillery History & Legacy
- Founded by Colonel Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr. in the late 1800s
- Known for pioneering continuous mashing and rickhouse construction
- Home to iconic brands like Buffalo Trace Bourbon, Old Taylor, Blanton's Single Barrel, and Eagle Rare
- Recognized as the most awarded distillery in the world

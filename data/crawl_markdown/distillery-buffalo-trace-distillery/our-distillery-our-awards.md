# Buffalo Trace Distillery – Awards

Buffalo Trace has accumulated **over 40 distillery titles** and **500+ product awards** since 2000, earning recognition from Whisky Magazine, Whisky Advocate, Wine Enthusiast, and many other industry bodies. The page lists annual accolades from 2006 to 2025, including *Visitor Attraction of the Year*, *Distiller of the Year*, *Brand Innovator*, and *Global Distiller* awards.

The site also highlights key personnel such as Freddie Johnson (Brand Ambassador), Matt Higgins (Visitor Attraction Manager), and Harlen Wheatley (Master Distiller).

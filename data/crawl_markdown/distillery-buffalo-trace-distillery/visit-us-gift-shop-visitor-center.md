# Gift Shop & Visitor Center
- **Location**: Former Warehouse A, historic 1881 aging warehouse.
- **Space**: 35 000 sq ft of shopping, tasting, event and gallery area.
- **Hours**: Mon‑Sat 9‑5 ET; Sun 11‑5 ET (closed on major holidays).
- **Products**: Curated award‑winning spirits, branded apparel, home goods, glassware.
- **Tasting**: Complimentary guided tastings daily until 30 min before closing; no reservation required.
- **Purchase**: ID required for alcohol tasting and purchase. Limited stock may sell out quickly.
- **Online**: Exclusive merchandise available via online shop.

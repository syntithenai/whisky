# Buffalo Trace Distillery

Buffalo Trace is one of America’s oldest bourbon producers, located in Frankfort, Kentucky. The distillery offers a range of award‑winning bourbons and whiskeys—including Sazerac Rye, W.L. Weller, Eagle Rare, Blanton’s Single Barrel, Traveller, E.H. Taylor Jr., and George T. Stagg—alongside limited editions such as the Antique Collection, Prohibition Collections, O.F.C. Vintages, Experimental Collection, and Single Oak Project.

Visitors can tour the historic site (Trace Tour, Old Taylor Tour, Hard Hat Tour), shop in the on‑site gift shop or online, and check daily bottle offerings. The distillery also hosts events, has a media kit, and maintains an active newsletter.

Key people include Col. Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr., George T. Stagg, Albert B. Blanton, and William L. Weller.

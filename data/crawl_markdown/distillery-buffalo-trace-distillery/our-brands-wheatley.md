# Wheatley Vodka
Wheatley Vodka is a premium craft vodka produced by Buffalo Trace Distillery. It uses the same red winter wheat and limestone‑water that powers the distillery’s award‑winning bourbons, and it is distilled ten times in small batches. The brand was created by Master Distiller Harlen Wheatley after he set out to make “the perfect vodka.”

## Key facts
- **Brand origin**: Created for the Buffalo Trace gift shop, later released to the public.
- **Distillation**: 10‑times distilled in small batches.
- **Ingredients**: Red winter wheat and limestone water.
- **Master Distiller**: Harlen Wheatley.

## Where to buy
Available at the Buffalo Trace Distillery gift shop and online through the distillery’s website.

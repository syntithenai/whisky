# Buffalo Trace Bourbon

Buffalo Trace is a Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey aged in new, charred oak barrels for years in century‑old warehouses. The spirit has a deep amber color and aromas of vanilla, mint, molasses, brown sugar, spice, oak, toffee, dark fruit and anise. It finishes long, smooth and deeply complex.

- **Proof**: 90 (45 %)
- **Mash Bill #1**: Straight Bourbon
- **Awards**: 139 total, including Gold at the Global Whiskey Challenge 2024 and Double Gold at the Global Spirits Awards 2024.
- **Serving**: Neat or on the rocks; also used in a signature Old Fashioned cocktail.
- **Unique Fact**: Named after ancient buffalo paths that run through the distillery site.

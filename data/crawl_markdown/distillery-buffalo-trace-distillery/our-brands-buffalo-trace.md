# Buffalo Trace Bourbon
Buffalo Trace Distillery’s flagship product, **Buffalo Trace Bourbon**, is a 40% ABV straight bourbon that has earned over 100 awards, including multiple Gold and Double Gold medals from top international spirits competitions. The name references historic buffalo trails that once crossed the distillery site in Kentucky.

## Key Facts
- Award‑winning bourbon with more than 100 accolades.
- 40% alcohol by volume (standard bottling) or 45% for cask strength variants.
- Named after ancient buffalo paths through the distillery’s location.
- Part of a broader portfolio that includes Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream, Kosher Whiskey Rye/Wheat/Straight Rye, and other flagship brands such as W.L. Weller, Eagle Rare, Sazerac Rye, Traveller Whiskey, E.H. Taylor Jr., and Blanton’s Single Barrel.

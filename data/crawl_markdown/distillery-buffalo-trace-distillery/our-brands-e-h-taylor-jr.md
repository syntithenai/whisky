# E.H. Taylor, Jr.

E.H. Taylor, Jr. is a premium bourbon line from Buffalo Trace Distillery that honors Colonel Edmund Haynes Taylor, Jr., the “father of modern bourbon.” The brand showcases a range of expressions—small batch, straight rye, barrel‑proof, single barrel, old fashioned sour mash, cured oak, seasoned wood, four grain, 18‑year marriage, amaranth, and warehouse‑C variants. Production draws on Taylor’s historic techniques such as copper fermenters, column stills, and climate‑controlled warehouses.

The distillery itself is located at 113 Great Buffalo Trace in Frankfort, Kentucky, and offers tours, a gift shop, and visitor center.

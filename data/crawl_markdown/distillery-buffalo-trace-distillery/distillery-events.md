# Events & Experiences at Buffalo Trace Distillery

Buffalo Trace Distillery offers a range of exclusive events and experiences, including:
- **Mint Julep Buffalo Trace & Keeneland Spring Meet** – a Kentucky adventure pairing the distillery with Keeneland horse racing (select Fridays and Saturdays in April).
- **Race Days at the Trace** – celebrations during the race season from April to May.
- **Mother’s Day Weekend: Mothers of Whiskey** – a special event featuring author Mallory O’Meara, archivist Nick Laracuente, and Melissa Warren, Sr. Product Development Manager.

All events are booked online via the Distillery Events page. The distillery is open Monday‑Saturday 9 am–5 pm (Sunday 11 am–5 pm) with closures on major holidays.

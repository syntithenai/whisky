# Van Winkle – Buffalo Trace Distillery

Van Winkle is a family‑heritage bourbon brand celebrated for its exceptional aging and meticulous selection. The line includes the Old Rip 10‑Year, Special Reserve 12‑Year, Family Reserve Rye, Pappy 15‑Year, 20‑Year, 23‑Year, and other limited releases. Production occurs at Buffalo Trace under strict family guidelines.

## History
- **Late 1800s** – Julian P. “Pappy” Van Winkle Sr. begins bourbon work as a salesman for W.L. Weller & Sons.
- **1935** – Pappy opens Stitzel‑Weller Distillery in Louisville.
- **1972** – The distillery is sold; the brand fades.
- **1981** – Julian III revives the Van Winkle name, focusing on wheated bourbon.
- **2002** – Joint venture with Buffalo Trace; all current production takes place there.

## Distillery & People
- **Distillery**: Buffalo Trace Distillery (Frankfort, KY)
- **Key people**: Julian P. “Pappy” Van Winkle Sr., Julian III, and the broader Van Winkle family.

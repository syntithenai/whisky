# Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream

**Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream** is a small‑batch liqueur made from the distillery’s flagship **Buffalo Trace Bourbon**. It delivers a silky, sweet profile with notes of vanilla, caramel, toffee and subtle oak spice, finishing with a lingering sweetness.

- **Proof:** 30 (15%)
- **Style:** Liqueur
- **Awards:** 2024 Gold at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition; Silver at Global Whiskey Challenge; Gold at International Wine & Spirits Competition, etc.
- **Shelf life:** 2 years unopened; 6 months refrigerated after opening.
- **Serving suggestions:** Neat or in cocktails such as the *Giddy Up Buffalo* (Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream + bourbon + espresso + simple syrup).

**Colonel E.H. Taylor, Jr. Distiller’s Council**

- A Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey released by Buffalo Trace in 1996.
- Created by a group of bourbon pioneers who revived Colonel E.H. Taylor, Jr.’s original sour‑mash process.
- Proof: 100 (50 % ABV). Mash Bill #1.
- Tasting notes: grain‑forward nose with biscuit and pastry; palate delivers toasted sugar, dark caramel, crème brûlée, plum dessert and nougat.
- Bottle design mirrors Taylor’s original label/shape from the late 1800s.
- A unique “Warehouse C Tornado” edition was produced after a tornado exposed barrels to weather during repairs.

**Key facts** – grain‑forward, biscuit, toasted sugar, caramel, crème brûlée, plum dessert, nougat, sour mash, Warehouse C, tornado expression.

# Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream

**Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream** is a small‑batch liqueur made from the distillery’s flagship **Buffalo Trace Bourbon**. It offers a creamy, vanilla‑caramel‑toffee profile with subtle oak and spice from the bourbon base. The product is 30 proof (15 % ABV) and comes in a 750 ml bottle.

The liqueur has received multiple awards in 2024, including **Double Gold** at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition. It can be enjoyed neat or as part of cocktails such as the *Giddy Up Buffalo*.

A 2‑year shelf life is claimed unopened; once opened it should be refrigerated and consumed within six months.

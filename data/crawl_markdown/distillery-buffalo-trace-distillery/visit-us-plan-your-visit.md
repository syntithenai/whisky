# Plan Your Visit – Buffalo Trace Distillery

- **Location**: 113 Great Buffalo Trace, Frankfort, KY 40601.
- **Tours**: Complimentary guided tours with tasting included; book online (8‑week window) or walk‑in if space. Release dates weekly on Wednesdays at 10 a.m. EST.
- **Gift Shop & Visitor Center**: Open 9 a.m.–5 p.m. Mon‑Sat, 11 a.m.–5 p.m. Sun; offers spirits, apparel, and gifts.
- **Parking**: Visitor lot opens 8 a.m. Mon‑Sat, 10 a.m. Sun; limited space, carpool recommended.
- **Check‑in**: Freehouse Welcome Center issues wristbands after ID verification.
- **Events & Experiences**: Special events announced on site; book in advance.
- **Hours**: Monday–Saturday 9 a.m.–5 p.m.; Sunday 11 a.m.–5 p.m. Closed on major holidays.
- **Contact**: info@buffalotrace.com.

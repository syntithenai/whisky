# O.F.C. Vintages – Buffalo Trace Distillery

O.F.C. Vintages are ultra‑rare, hand‑cut crystal bottles of bourbon that pay homage to the historic Old Fashioned Copper (O.F.C.) Distillery founded by Colonel Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr. in 1870. Each bottle is vintage‑dated to the year it was distilled and comes with a provenance card detailing notable events from that year. The first release contained only 200 bottles, distributed free to non‑profit organisations; subsequent vintages (1985, 1989, 1990, 1993‑95) remain highly collectible.

The distillery still uses the original copper fermentation vats, column stills and steam‑heated climate‑controlled warehouses that Taylor pioneered. The brand is part of Buffalo Trace’s portfolio alongside Blanton’s Single Barrel, Eagle Rare, W.L. Weller, Sazerac Rye, Traveller Whiskey and E.H. Taylor Jr.

The site also lists visiting hours, contact information and links to other Buffalo Trace brands and resources.

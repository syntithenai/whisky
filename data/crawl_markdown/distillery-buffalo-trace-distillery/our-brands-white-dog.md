# White Dog – Buffalo Trace Distillery
White Dog is the distillery’s raw, unaged spirit – the *new‑make* whiskey straight from the still before barrel aging. It offers a glimpse into the essence of bourbon and reflects over two centuries of Kentucky distilling tradition.

## Key Points
- **Product**: White Dog (Raw & Unaged Distillate)
- **History**: First clear spirit consumed by pioneers in Kentucky, predating bourbon barrels.
- **Production**: Made on the same grounds where buffalo once crossed the Kentucky River.
- **Flavor Profile**: Sweet with corn and raw character.
- **Availability**: Not sold as a finished product; available for purchase only through Buffalo Trace’s gift shop or special events.

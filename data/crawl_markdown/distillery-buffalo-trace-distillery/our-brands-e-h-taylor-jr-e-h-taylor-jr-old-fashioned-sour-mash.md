# E.H. Taylor, Jr. Old Fashioned Sour Mash

- **Type**: Straight Kentucky Bourbon Whiskey (100 proof / 50 %)
- **Origin**: Buffalo Trace Distillery, Frankfort, KY
- **History**: Recreated in 2002 from a 19th‑century sour‑mash method used by Edmund Haynes Taylor, Jr.
- **Tasting notes**: Floral aroma with freshly baked bread undertone; light honey‑toast palate and clean leather finish.
- **Rating**: 88/100 (Whisky Advocate)
- **Serving suggestion**: Neat or on the rocks
- **Availability**: Sold online via Buffalo Trace Distillery shop; also available at the distillery gift shop.

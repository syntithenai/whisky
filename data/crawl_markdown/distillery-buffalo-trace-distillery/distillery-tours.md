# Distillery Tours at Buffalo Trace

Buffalo Trace offers three main guided tours:
- **Trace Tour** – an introductory 75‑minute walk through bourbon history and science.
- **Old Taylor Tour** – a 75‑minute walk highlighting the legacy of E.H. Taylor Jr. and historic landmarks.
- **Hard Hat Tour** – a 105‑minute behind‑the‑scenes look at the full bourbon‑making process.

All tours are complimentary, include tastings, and require advance booking. The distillery also hosts events, worldwide experiences (e.g., London), and provides a gift shop & visitor centre.

> *“No trip to Kentucky is complete without a visit to Buffalo Trace Distillery, the world’s bourbon destination.”*

---

**Key facts**: 75‑min tours for Trace & Old Taylor; 105‑min Hard Hat Tour. Tours are guided in English and include tastings of select products.

# Blanton's Special Reserve

- **Type**: 80‑Proof (40 %) single‑barrel straight bourbon.
- **Availability**: Not sold in the U.S.; available only in select international markets.
- **Tasting notes**: Floral nose with cedar, raisin, vanilla; palate light and airy with vanilla, toffee, dried fruit, white pepper.
- **Unique facts**: First commercially‑available single‑barrel bourbon (1984); each bottle has a unique horse/jockey stopper and barrel dump date.
- **Serving suggestion**: Neat or on the rocks.

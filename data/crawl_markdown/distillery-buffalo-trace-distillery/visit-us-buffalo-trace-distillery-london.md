# London Experience – Buffalo Trace Distillery

Buffalo Trace Distillery’s London location offers a range of guided bourbon tastings, blending workshops and single‑barrel experiences. Visitors can book premium or standard sessions (prices £19.99–£60) and purchase exclusive bottles and branded merchandise in the on‑site shop.

- **Location**: 32‑33 Long Acre, London WC2E 9LA
- **Hours**: Mon‑Sat 11 a.m.–9 p.m.; Sun 12 p.m.–6 p.m. (EST)
- **Tasting options**:
  - Introductory bourbon tasting (£19.99)
  - Premium tasting with four expressions (£34.99)
  - Blending workshop featuring Traveller Whiskey (£34.99)
  - Single‑barrel mystery tastings with take‑home bottle (£60.00)
- **Shop**: sells Buffalo Trace bottles, gift sets and branded apparel.

All experiences require a reservation; early arrival is advised.

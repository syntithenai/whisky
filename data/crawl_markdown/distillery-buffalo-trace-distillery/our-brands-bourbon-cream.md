# Bourbon Cream

**Buffalo Trace Distillery Bourbon Cream** is a small‑batch cream liqueur made from Buffalo Trace Bourbon and real cream. It has a 2‑year shelf life unopened, and 6 months refrigerated once opened.

---

## Key facts
- **Product name:** Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream
- **Type:** Cream liqueur
- **Base spirit:** Buffalo Trace Bourbon (40% or 45% alc./vol.)
- **Shelf life:** 2 years unopened; 6 months refrigerated after opening.
- **Availability:** Sold at the Buffalo Trace Distillery gift shop and online merchandise.
- **Related brands:** Traveller Whiskey, Wheatley Vodka, Freddie’s Old‑Fashioned Soda.

---

## Where to buy
The liqueur is available for purchase in the distillery’s gift shop (Frankfort, KY) and through the online store linked on the site.

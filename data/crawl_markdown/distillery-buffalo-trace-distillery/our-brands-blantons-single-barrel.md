# Blanton's Single Barrel Bourbon
Blanton’s is a premium single‑barrel bourbon first bottled in 1984 by Master Distiller Elmer T. Lee, honoring Colonel Albert B. Blanton. Each bottle comes from an individual barrel of Warehouse H and features a unique horse‑and‑jockey stopper.

## Key facts
- **First modern single‑barrel bourbon**
- **Warehouse H**: metal‑clad warehouse that accelerates maturation via temperature swings
- **Hand‑dumped, individually bottled** with a distinct stopper (8 collectible variations)
- **Named after** Colonel Albert B. Blanton, 1897‑1921 president of Buffalo Trace

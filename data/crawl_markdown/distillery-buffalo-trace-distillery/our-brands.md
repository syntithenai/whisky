# Buffalo Trace Distillery Overview
- **Location**: Frankfort, Kentucky
- **Founded**: 1789 (one of the oldest distilleries in America)
- **Brands**: Over 20 bourbon and whiskey brands including Eagle Rare, W.L. Weller, E.H. Taylor Jr., Blanton's Single Barrel, Traveller Whiskey, George T. Stagg, Old Charter Oak Benchmark
- **History**: Founded by Colonel Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr., known for pioneering continuous rye mash bill and using new charred oak barrels
- **Awards**: Multiple awards including World Spirit of Excellence Awards (WSOE) and Gold Medals at San Francisco World Spirits Competition
- **Production Signals**:
  - Continuously produces bourbon and whiskey with a focus on quality over quantity
  - Uses proprietary mash bills and aging techniques to create signature flavors
  - Implements strict quality control measures throughout production process
- **Commercial Signals**:
  - Widely distributed in the United States and internationally
  - Strong brand recognition among premium spirits consumers
  - Collaborations with other distilleries for limited edition releases
- **Risks/Unknowns**:
  - Dependence on bourbon market trends which can be volatile
  - Potential regulatory changes affecting production or distribution
  - Supply chain disruptions impacting availability of key ingredients

# Buffalo Trace Distillery – Our Brands

Buffalo Trace Distillery showcases a diverse, award‑winning portfolio of bourbons, ryes, and other spirits. The lineup includes iconic labels such as **Eagle Rare**, **W.L. Weller**, **E.H. Taylor Jr.**, **Blanton’s Single Barrel**, **Van Winkle**, **Elmer T. Lee Single Barrel**, **George T. Stagg**, **Old Charter Oak**, and **Benchmark**. The distillery also offers specialty collections (Antique, Prohibition, Experimental), a single‑oak project, and other brands like **Buffalo Trace Bourbon Cream**, **Wheatley Vodka**, **CLIX**, **Freddie’s Old‑Fashioned Soda**, and **White Dog**.

The site highlights the distillery’s history, legendary people (Col. Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr., George T. Stagg, Albert B. Blanton, William L. Weller), awards, and visitor information including tours, events, and a gift shop.

> *“Buffalo Trace Distillery is closed on Easter Sunday, Independence Day, Thanksgiving day, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and New Year’s Day.”*

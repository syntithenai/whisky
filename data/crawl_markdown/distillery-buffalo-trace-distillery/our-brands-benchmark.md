# Benchmark Collection – Buffalo Trace Distillery

Benchmark is a line of Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskies that celebrates the historic surveyor marks known as *benchmarks* from the early American frontier. The collection includes several expressions such as **Old No. 8 Brand**, **Top Floor**, **Small Batch**, **Bonded**, **Full Proof**, and **Single Barrel**. Each bottle is crafted at Buffalo Trace Distillery in Frankfort, Kentucky, using traditional bourbon‑making techniques.

The brand’s narrative ties the spirit to the McAfee Brothers’ 1773 expedition across what would become Kentucky, emphasizing exploration, quality, and approachability.

> *“Benchmark Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey is a true measure of quality and character.”*

## Key Points
- **Brand story**: Named after surveyor marks from the McAfee Brothers’ 1773 journey.
- **Product range**: Old No. 8 Brand, Top Floor, Small Batch, Bonded, Full Proof, Single Barrel.
- **Distillery**: Buffalo Trace Distillery, Frankfort, KY.
- **Alcohol content**: 40% ABV (standard) or 45% ABV (Full Proof).

# Freddie's Old‑Fashioned Soda
Freddie’s Old‑Fashioned Soda is a line of genuine sodas inspired by Buffalo Trace Distillery’s tour guide, Freddie Johnson. The brand offers Root Beer, Ginger Ale and Ginger Beer, available for purchase online through the distillery’s store.

## Key Points
- **Brand Origin**: Named after Freddie Johnson, a third‑generation employee and VIP Visitor Lead.
- **Product Range**: Root Beer, Ginger Ale, Ginger Beer.
- **Availability**: Sold online; shipped to customers’ doors.
- **Distillery Context**: Part of Buffalo Trace’s broader portfolio which includes bourbon, rye, and other spirits.

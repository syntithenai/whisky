# Elmer T. Lee Single Barrel

- **Product**: Kentucky Straight Bourbon Whiskey, 40% or 45% ABV.
- **Concept**: Hand‑selected single barrels from Buffalo Trace’s finest casks, honoring Master Distiller Elmer T. Lee who pioneered the single‑barrel idea.
- **History**: Lee introduced Blanton’s Single Barrel in 1984; after his retirement Buffalo Trace released this namesake bottling.
- **Legacy**: The brand showcases Lee’s influence through barrel selection and craftsmanship.

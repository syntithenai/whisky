# E.H. Taylor, Jr. Straight Rye

- **Type**: Small‑batch, Bottled‑in‑Bond straight rye whiskey (100 proof)
- **Mash bill**: 100 % rye + malted barley (no corn)
- **Flavor profile**: Dried fruit, black pepper, fresh dill, dark spices, subtle caramel, oaky dryness
- **Awards**: Double Gold (SFWSC 2023), Gold (International Spirits Challenge 2023), etc.
- **Serving suggestion**: Neat or on the rocks
- **Availability**: Sold online via Buffalo Trace Distillery shop; also available at the distillery gift shop in Frankfort, KY.

## Tasting Notes
Aromatic blend of dried fruit and pepper with a savory finish that lingers with oak‑dryness.

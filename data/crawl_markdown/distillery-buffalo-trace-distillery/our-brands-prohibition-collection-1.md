# Prohibition Collection – Buffalo Trace Distillery

The **Prohibition Collection** revives historic medicinal‑whiskey brands bottled between 1920‑33 at the George T. Stagg distillery (now Buffalo Trace). The collection includes *Old Stagg*, *Golden Wedding*, *Three Feathers*, *Walnut Hill*, and *Spiritus Frumenti*. Each expression is inspired by original Prohibition‑era packaging and stories uncovered in the distillery archives.

The page also highlights Buffalo Trace’s heritage as the longest‑continuously operating U.S. distillery, its notable people (Col. Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr., George T. Stagg, Albert B. Blanton, William L. Weller), and general visitor information.

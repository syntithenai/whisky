# Blanton's Original Single Barrel
Blanton’s Original is the world’s first single‑barrel bourbon, bottled from a single barrel and hand‑dumped into individual bottles. It offers a nutmeg‑spiced nose with dry vanilla, honey, caramel and corn notes, finishing with returning corn and nutmeg flavors. The whiskey is 93 proof (46.5 %) and aged in Buffalo Trace’s metal‑clad Warehouse H to accelerate maturation.

## Key facts
- First single‑barrel bourbon ever bottled.
- Hand‑dumped, individually labeled and sealed.
- Aged in metal‑clad Warehouse H for rapid temperature fluctuations.
- 93 proof straight bourbon (Mash Bill #2).

## Tasting notes
Nutmeg, vanilla, honey, corn, caramel, dry vanilla, strong caramel, corn finish.

## Awards
Double Platinum (2024), Silver (ASCOT 2022), Gold/Silver at various international competitions.

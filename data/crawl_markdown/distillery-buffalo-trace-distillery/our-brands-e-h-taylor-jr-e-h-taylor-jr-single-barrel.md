# E. H. Taylor, Jr. Single Barrel

- **Type**: Straight Kentucky Bourbon Whiskey (100 proof)
- **Aging**: Aged in historic Warehouse C built by Colonel E.H. Taylor, Jr. (1881)
- **Bottle**: Bottled‑in‑Bond, 100 proof, single‑barrel expression
- **Tasting notes**: lightly toasted oak, dried figs, butterscotch, tobacco, dark spices; long finish
- **Awards**: 2025 Silver International Wine & Spirits Competition, 2024 Gold Global Whiskey Challenge, 2024 Gold American Whiskey Masters, 2024 Gold Whiskies of the World, 2024 Gold North American Bourbon and Whiskey Competition

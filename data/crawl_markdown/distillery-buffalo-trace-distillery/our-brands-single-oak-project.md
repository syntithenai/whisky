# Single Oak Project – Buffalo Trace Distillery

The **Single Oak Project** is a research‑driven initiative by Buffalo Trace that produced **192 unique bourbon barrels**. Each barrel comes from one of 96 hand‑selected American oak trees, split into top and bottom halves. The staves were seasoned and charred in different ways, paired with varied recipes, entry proofs, and aging conditions to explore the impact of seven key variables across 1,396 possible taste combinations.

Key points:
- 192 distinct bourbons, each bottle unique.
- Seven critical variables: tree origin, growth rings, seasoning, charring level, recipe, entry proof, aging environment.
- Project showcases the art and science behind bourbon creation.

> *“The Single Oak project is part of a much larger, and noble, effort: to create the perfect bourbon.”* – Whisky Advocate

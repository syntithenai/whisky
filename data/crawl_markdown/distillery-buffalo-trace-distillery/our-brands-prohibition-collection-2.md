# Prohibition Collection 2 – Buffalo Trace Distillery

The page introduces **Prohibition Collection 2**, a line of bourbons that revive historic medicinal‑whiskey labels bottled between 1920‑1933 at the George T. Stagg Distillery (now Buffalo Trace). The collection celebrates the ingenuity of distillers during Prohibition, featuring brands such as *Silver Wedding*, *Very Oldest Procurable (V.O.P.)*, *Anderson's Belle*, *Old Fashioned*, and *Mountain Corn Mirror Brook*. The site also highlights Buffalo Trace’s heritage, awards, and visitor information.

---

## Key facts
- **Collection focus**: Medicinal whiskey brands from the Prohibition era (1920‑1933).
- **Distillery**: George T. Stagg Distillery, now Buffalo Trace – longest continuously operating distillery in America.
- **Brands listed**: Silver Wedding, Very Oldest Procurable (V.O.P.), Anderson's Belle, Old Fashioned, Mountain Corn Mirror Brook.
- **Packaging inspiration**: Original colorful labels from the era.
- **Visitor info**: Distillery address 113 Great Buffalo Trace, Frankfort, KY; hours Mon‑Sat 9 am–5 pm, Sun 11 am–5 pm.

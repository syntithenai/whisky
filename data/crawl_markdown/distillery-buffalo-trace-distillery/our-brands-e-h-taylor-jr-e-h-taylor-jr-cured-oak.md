# E.H. Taylor, Jr. Cured Oak

E.H. Taylor, Jr. Cured Oak is a limited‑edition straight Kentucky bourbon aged in oak barrels whose staves were cured for **13 months**—more than twice the typical curing period. The barrels were stored in Warehouse C, built by Colonel Taylor in 1881. Tasting notes highlight layers of vanilla, toffee and figs with subtle dry‑oak undertones.

- **Proof:** 100 (50 %)
- **Mash Bill #1**
- **Style:** Straight Bourbon
- **Awards:** 2

The product is part of the E.H. Taylor, Jr. brand and is available for purchase through Buffalo Trace’s online shop.

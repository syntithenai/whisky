# Clix – Ultra‑Premium Craft Vodka

Clix (HDW CLIX) is a single‑distillation vodka produced by Buffalo Trace Distillery’s Master Distiller Harlen Wheatley. The name “CLIX” (159 in Roman numerals) commemorates the 159 separate distillations required to create the spirit, underscoring its meticulous craftsmanship.

- **Producer**: Harlen Wheatley, Master Distiller at Buffalo Trace Distillery
- **Product type**: Ultra‑premium craft vodka
- **Key claim**: “the way vodka should taste” – a once‑in‑a‑lifetime experience
- **Availability**: Sold through the distillery’s gift shop and visitor centre in Frankfort, KY.

# Traveller Whiskey – Buffalo Trace Distillery
Traveller Whiskey is a collaborative blend created by Master Distiller **Harlen Wheatley** and Grammy‑winning artist **Chris Stapleton**. The final expression, Blend No. 40, was chosen after testing over 50 custom blends to achieve a smooth, premium profile described as *“Easy to Drink, Hard Not to Love.”* It is the first official whiskey of Major League Baseball (MLB).

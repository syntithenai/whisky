# Distillery Tours – Buffalo Trace

- **Trace Tour**: Introductory 75‑minute tour covering history and science of bourbon, includes tasting.
- **Old Taylor Tour**: 75‑minute walk through the legacy of E.H. Taylor Jr. with historic landmarks, includes tasting.
- **Hard Hat Tour**: 105‑minute production tour showing the full bourbon‑making process, includes tasting.
- Tours are complimentary and guided in English; reservations can be made online.

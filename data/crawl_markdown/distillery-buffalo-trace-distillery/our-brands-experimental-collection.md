# Experimental Collection – Buffalo Trace Distillery

Buffalo Trace’s *Experimental Collection* showcases the distillery’s commitment to innovation. Over two decades of experimentation have produced **30,000+ experimental barrels** with unique mash bills, wood types, barrel toasting and aging conditions. Selected barrels are released in limited quantities, offering a glimpse into the evolving flavor profiles that result from variations in mash composition, wood species, warehouse environment and maturation time.

> *“Buffalo Trace is conducting thousands of different experiments (including some at their experimental micro‑distillery), many of them groundbreaking. More importantly, whiskey enthusiasts get to taste them on an ongoing basis.”* – Whisky Advocate

The collection highlights the distillery’s heritage while pushing the boundaries of bourbon-making.

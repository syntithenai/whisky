# Old Charter Oak – Buffalo Trace Distillery

Old Charter Oak is a line of bourbons that celebrates the influence of oak on flavor and character. Each expression in the collection—such as **Old Charter 8**, **French Oak**, **Chinkapin Oak**, **Mongolian Oak**, **Canadian Oak**, and **Finest Oak**—uses wood from distinct forests to impart unique stories, flavors, and spirits.

The brand’s roots trace back to 1874 when Adam and Ben Chapeze named their whiskey after the historic Charter Oak tree in Hartford, Connecticut—a symbol of American freedom. The name underscores the importance of white oak barrels in crafting America’s finest whiskeys.

> *“Old Charter Oak bourbon is an exploration of oak itself.”*

The distillery is located at 113 Great Buffalo Trace, Frankfort, KY 40601 and operates from Monday to Saturday 9 AM‑5 PM (Sunday 11 AM‑5 PM).

# Clix Vodka

Clix is an ultra‑premium vodka from Buffalo Trace Distillery, crafted by Master Distiller Harlen Davis Wheatley and a dedicated team. The name “CLIX” (159 in Roman numerals) honors the many distillations required to achieve its purity.

- **Proof**: 80 (40%)
- **Style**: Vodka
- **Awards**: 2019 Silver at San Francisco World Spirits Competition; 2019 Category Winner at World Vodka Awards
- **Tasting notes**: Polished, smooth, fresh nectar‑like nose; elegant and refined finish.
- **Reviews**: "The consummate vodka for cocktails." – *Robb Report*.

Available online or in stores; the product is sold under the brand name CLIX (or HDW CLIX).

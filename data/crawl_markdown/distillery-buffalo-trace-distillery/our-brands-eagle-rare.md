# Eagle Rare Bourbon
Eagle Rare is a flagship Buffalo Trace bourbon that has earned five double‑gold medals. It is aged for at least ten years, with each barrel hand‑selected for consistency and individuality. The brand offers several expressions: 10‑year, 12‑year, 17‑year (part of the Antique Collection), 20‑year Double Eagle Very Rare, and a 25‑year edition.

## Key facts
- First produced at Buffalo Trace in 1989.
- Refined to a smoother 90‑proof, 10‑year expression after joining Buffalo Trace.
- 17‑year version released annually as part of the Antique Collection.
- Double Eagle Very Rare is 20 years old, presented in a crystal decanter with two eagles and a numbered certificate.

## Where to buy
Available through Buffalo Trace’s online store and select retailers. Prices vary by expression; typical retail ranges from $45 for the 10‑year to over $200 for the Double Eagle Very Rare.

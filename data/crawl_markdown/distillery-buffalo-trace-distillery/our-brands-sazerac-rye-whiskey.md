# Sazerac Rye – Buffalo Trace Distillery

Sazerac Rye is a flagship rye whiskey that celebrates the heritage of New Orleans and its iconic cocktail, the Sazerac. The distillery produces several expressions: **100 Proof**, **Full Proof**, **Thomas H. Handy Straight Rye**, and an **18‑Year‑Old** edition. Each bottle reflects the brand’s commitment to bold rye spice balanced with vanilla, clove, and subtle citrus notes.

The page also highlights the historical figure Thomas H. Handy, who championed American rye as the base for the Sazerac cocktail in the 19th century. Buffalo Trace Distillery’s visitor information, tour options, and contact details are provided at the bottom of the page.

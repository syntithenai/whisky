# W.L. Weller – Buffalo Trace Distillery

- **Brand focus**: Wheated bourbon, swapping rye for wheat to create a softer, smoother style.
- **Expressions**: From approachable 12‑year‑old to high‑proof barrel‑proof releases (e.g., *Special Reserve*, *Antique 107*, *Full Proof*). Each bottling honors William Larue Weller and pushes experimental variants such as the Daniel Weller line.
- **History**: Founded by William Larue Weller in 1849; he pioneered wheat‑based mash bills. His grandson, Daniel Weller, launched an experimental series in 2023 exploring different wheat strains.
- **Unique facts**: Limited annual releases (e.g., *William Larue Weller* Antique Collection) are highly sought after by collectors.

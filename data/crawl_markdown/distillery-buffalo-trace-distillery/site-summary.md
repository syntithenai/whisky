## Long Description
# Buffalo Trace Distillery – E.H. Taylor, Jr. Collection

The E.H. Taylor, Jr. brand is the flagship line of Buffalo Trace Distillery, honoring Colonel Edmund Haynes Taylor, Jr., founder of the distillery. Each expression is produced in small batches, aged in century‑old warehouses built by Taylor himself, and bottled at 100 proof (or higher for barrel‑proof releases). The collection showcases a variety of mash bills, from pure rye to four‑grain blends, and includes limited‑edition releases that experiment with unusual grains such as amaranth.

## Product Highlights
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Straight Rye** – 100 proof straight rye whiskey (100% rye + malted barley). Flavor profile: dried fruit, black pepper, fresh dill, dark spices, subtle caramel, oaky dryness.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Small Batch** – 100 proof straight bourbon (Mash Bill #1). Notes of caramel, corn sweetness, butterscotch, licorice, pepper, and tobacco.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Single Barrel** – 100 proof single‑barrel straight bourbon aged in historic Warehouse C.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Seasoned Wood** – 100 proof straight bourbon finished in new oak barrels treated by bathing and natural outdoor seasoning.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Old Fashioned Sour Mash** – 100 proof straight bourbon recreating a 19th‑century sour‑mash method.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Four Grain** – 100 proof straight bourbon with a four‑grain mash (corn, rye, wheat, malted barley). Sweet caramel & vanilla with spicy clove and black pepper notes.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Cured Oak** – Limited edition 100 proof straight bourbon aged in oak barrels cured for 13 months in Warehouse C.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Bottled‑in‑Bond** – 15‑year‑old straight bourbon (Mash Bill #1) hand‑bottled at 100 proof.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Barrel Proof** – Uncut, unfiltered straight bourbon bottled at 127.5 proof from a tornado‑damaged Warehouse C batch.
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Barrel Proof Rye** – Uncut, unfiltered straight rye whiskey bottled at 126 proof (63 % ABV).
- **E.H. Taylor, Jr. Amaranth** – 100 proof straight bourbon flavored with amaranth grain, offering spearmint, honey, cherry, coffee, vanilla, oak and dark‑berry notes.

All products are available through the Buffalo Trace online shop or at the distillery gift shop in Frankfort, Kentucky (113 Great Buffalo Trace). The distillery also offers tours such as the Trace Tour, Old Taylor Tour, and Hard Hat Tour.

## Awards & Recognition
Many expressions have earned double‑gold, gold, and silver awards at international competitions including the World Spirits Competition, Whiskies of the World, American Whiskey Masters, and San Francisco World Spirits Competition.

## Visitor Experience
Buffalo Trace Distillery welcomes visitors for guided tours that explore its historic warehouses, bottling process, and tasting rooms. The distillery’s location in Frankfort provides easy access to Kentucky’s bourbon heritage.

# Events & Experiences at Buffalo Trace Distillery

Buffalo Trace offers a range of exclusive events and experiences for visitors, including:
- **Mint Julep Buffalo Trace & Keeneland Spring Meet** – a Kentucky adventure that pairs the distillery with Keeneland horse racing (available select Fridays and Saturdays in April).
- **Race Days at the Trace** – a celebration of the race season from April to May.
- **Mother’s Day Weekend: Mothers of Whiskey** – featuring author Mallory O’Meara, archivist Nick Laracuente, and Melissa Warren, Sr. Product Development Manager.

All events are booked online via the distillery’s website. The distillery is open Monday‑Saturday 9 am–5 pm (Sunday 11 am–5 pm) with closures on major holidays.

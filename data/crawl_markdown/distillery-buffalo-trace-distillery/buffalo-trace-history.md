# Buffalo Trace Distillery History

The page chronicles the evolution of Buffalo Trace from its early 18th‑century roots on the historic trail to its current status as a family‑owned, award‑winning distillery. Key milestones include:
- Early surveys and settlements in Frankfort (1771‑1792)
- Establishment of the O.F.C. Distillery by Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr. (1870s)
- Fires, floods, and Prohibition-era survival
- Transition to Schenley and later Sazerac ownership
- Launch of flagship brands such as Buffalo Trace and Blanton’s Single‑Barrel
- Recent expansions, landmark designations, and charitable initiatives

The narrative highlights the distillery’s resilience, innovation, and commitment to heritage.

# Buffalo Trace Distillery History

The page chronicles the evolution of Buffalo Trace from its early 18th‑century roots on the historic trail to its current status as a family‑owned, award‑winning distillery. Key milestones include:
- Early surveys and settlements in Frankfort (1771‑1792)
- Establishment of the O.F.C. Distillery by Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr.
- Fires, floods, Prohibition, and wartime expansions
- Transition to Sazerac ownership (1992) and re‑branding as Buffalo Trace (1999)
- Recent growth: new warehouses, experimental collections, and record‑setting barrels

Notable figures: Edmund Haynes Taylor Jr., George T. Stagg, Albert B. Blanton, Elmer T. Lee, Gary Gayheart, Harlen Wheatley.

The distillery’s legacy is highlighted by numerous industry awards and its designation as a National Historic Landmark.

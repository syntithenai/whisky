# Antique Collection – Buffalo Trace Distillery

The **Antique Collection** is a curated line of six rare, hand‑selected bourbons and rye whiskies released each fall since the early 2000s. The lineup includes:
- George T. Stagg Bourbon
- William Larue Weller Bourbon
- Thomas H. Handy Sazerac Straight Rye
- Eagle Rare 17‑Year‑Old Bourbon
- Sazerac Rye 18‑Year‑Old
- (from 2025) E.H. Taylor Bottled‑in‑Bond Bourbon

Each expression pays tribute to a historic figure who shaped the American whiskey industry and showcases Buffalo Trace’s mastery of aging, cask selection and distillation.

---

## Key Facts
- **Launch**: Over two decades ago; six releases each fall.
- **Historical Figures**: George T. Stagg, William Larue Weller, Thomas H. Handy, E.H. Taylor Jr.
- **Distillery**: Buffalo Trace Distillery, Frankfort, Kentucky.

## Historical Context
- **George T. Stagg**: Co‑owner of O.F.C., modernized the distillery in the late 1800s.
- **William Larue Weller**: Introduced wheated bourbon recipe, promoted “Honest Whiskey at an Honest Price.”
- **Thomas H. Handy**: Pioneered American rye whiskey for the Sazerac cocktail.
- **E.H. Taylor Jr.**: Father of modern bourbon; championed the Bottled‑in‑Bond Act and introduced copper fermenters, column stills, climate‑controlled warehouses.

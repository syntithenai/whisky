# Visit Us – Buffalo Trace Distillery

- **Location**: Frankfort, Kentucky (US) and a London branch.
- **Tours**:
  - *Trace Tour* – Introductory history & science of bourbon (75 min).
  - *Hard Hat Tour* – Behind‑the‑scenes production walk (105 min).
  - *Old Taylor Tour* – Legacy of E.H. Taylor Jr. and historic landmarks (75 min).
- **Walk‑in Visits**: Open daily with complimentary tastings and gift shop access.
- **Hours**: Mon–Sat 9 am–5 pm; Sun 11 am–5 pm (closed on major holidays).
- **Gift Shop & Visitor Center**: Offers award‑winning spirits for purchase.
- **Worldwide Experiences**: London location offers tastings, tours and events.

All tours are complimentary; reservations recommended but walk‑up is possible.

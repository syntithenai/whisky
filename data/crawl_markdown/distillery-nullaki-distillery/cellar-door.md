# Cellar Door – Nullaki Distillery

The cellar door is a boutique tasting venue located in the Denmark hinterland of Western Australia, offering visitors panoramic views over Wilson Inlet and the Southern Ocean. It operates on standard weekday hours with no booking required. Guests can enjoy guided whisky tasting flights, single dram tastings, local chocolates, cheese platters, or aperitif cocktails while watching distillers at work.

- **Location**: Denmark hinterland, WA
- **Hours**: Wed‑Sun 11 am–5/6 pm (Friday & Saturday 6 pm)
- **Experience**: Guided tasting flights, single dram tastings, chocolate pairings, cheese platters, aperitif cocktails
- **View**: Copper still and Southern Ocean vistas

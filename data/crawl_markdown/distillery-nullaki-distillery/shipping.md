# Shipping Policy – Nullaki Distillery

- **Processing time**: Orders are prepared within 5 business days after payment confirmation.
- **Domestic shipping only**: Currently ships only within Australia; international inquiries must be emailed.
- **Standard shipping cost**: $15 per order.
- **Estimated delivery**: Provided by the third‑party carrier, subject to weather and other delays; not guaranteed.
- **Tracking**: All domestic shipments include a tracking number via Rocky Ridge. International orders may lack tracking.
- **Damages**: Report immediately; liability capped at transaction value under Australian Consumer Law.

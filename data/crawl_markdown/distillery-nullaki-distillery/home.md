# Nullaki Distillery

- **Location**: Denmark, Great Southern, Western Australia.
- **Core philosophy**: Elegant spirits made from local barley, premium wine barrels (Margaret River, Pemberton, Swan Valley), and spring water.
- **Distillation**: Double‑distilled in a custom copper still by Stillsmiths, Tasmania; steam‑heated for consistency.
- **Product range**:
  - *Tawny Cask Matured* – cask 0021, intensity 4/5, aromas of black cherry & stewed plum, palate caramelised orange peel & fig. Price: $145.00.
  - *Pinot Cask Matured* – cask 0009, intensity 3/5, floral/perfumed aromatics, crisp red fruit palate. Price: $145.00.
- **Cellar door**: Open to the public from 2 Jan 2026 with summer hours; no bookings required.
- **Contact & licensing**: Producers Licence 618222853325; email info@nullakidistillery.com.

## Distillery facts
- Located in Denmark, Great Southern, Western Australia.
- Uses barley from farms in the Great Southern.
- Barrels sourced from Margaret River, Pemberton and Swan Valley wine regions.
- Water comes from its own natural spring.
- Copper still crafted by Stillsmiths (Tasmania).
- Distillation method: double‑distilled, steam‑heated.

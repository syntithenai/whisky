# Our Whisky

Nullaki Distillery creates a single‑malt that blends Western Australian terroir with traditional Scottish methods. The whisky is made from locally sourced barley, fermented with a mix of yeasts (including saison and Belgian ale strains), double distilled in a custom copper pot still, and aged mainly in French oak barriques or large casks such as Pinot Noir and Tawny‑port‑infused barrels. Maturation occurs in a hill‑sunk cellar on Mount Shadforth, where stable conditions and ocean breezes influence the spirit. Bottling involves decanting, dilution with spring water, and non‑chilled filtration for 6–8 weeks.

> *“Our love of whisky comes from it being such a versatile spirit that numerous cultures have been able to imprint their unique characteristics into.”*

---

## Key Points
- **Barley**: Western Australian, light & accessible when malted.
- **Fermentation**: Long period with diverse yeasts for complexity.
- **Distillation**: Double‑distilled in a tall, narrow copper pot still designed by Stillsmiths (Tasmania).
- **Casks**: Pinot Noir (Pemberton), Tawny‑port‑aged (Swan Valley), French oak barriques.
- **Maturation**: Hill‑sunk cellar on Mount Shadforth; passive ventilation, Southern Ocean breezes.
- **Bottling**: Decanting, spring water dilution, non‑chilled filtration 6–8 weeks.

## Source Excerpts
- The majority of our spirit is aged in French oak barriques or other large volume casks to ensure the spirit is not rushed to maturation.
- Maturation Our casks are matured in our barrel store which is sunk into the hill, high on Mount Shadforth.
- We source Pinot Noir casks from Pemberton and casks which have been aged with Tawny port from the Swan Valley.
- From traditional Speyside and Highland malts, to Japanese precision, across to Tennessee and West Coast American flair and finally embracing proud Australian heritage, we recognise it is possible to impart local flavour whilst still respecting traditional methods.
- For our own whisky, it is about finding and imbibing the best that Western Australia has to offer, giving those that try our Single Malt both something traditional and unique.
- We don’t seek to rush any aspect of our process, and our skilled distillers seek to optimise our output guided by quality and flavour, rather than quantum or alcohol content.

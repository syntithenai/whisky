# Bakery Hill Distillery

Bakery Hill is an Australian premium single‑malt whisky distillery based in Kensington, Victoria. Their online shop offers a range of products including the **Classic Malt 25th Edition** ($178), **Peated Malt 25th Edition** ($188), and smaller hip flask options such as the **Hip Flask of Whisky – Blend** ($45) and **Oxford Scholar Gin** (US$29–35). The distillery also hosts tours, a private barrel program, and social events in their tasting room.

# Rita Greenwood Appointed Chair of the Scotch Whisky Association Council
Rita Greenwood, Chief Investment Officer at William Grant & Sons, has been named Chair of the SWA Council for a two‑year term. She becomes the first female Chair in the association’s 114‑year history and the 24th overall. Her appointment comes as the industry faces trade pressures, regulatory changes and sustainability challenges.

- **Council role**: Leads 16 member representatives shaping strategy and policy.
- **Background**: 23 years in Scotch whisky; executive board member since 2017.
- **Industry context**: Focus on overcoming headwinds and driving long‑term growth to 2030.
- **SWA overview**: Over 90 members, majority of production, headquartered in Edinburgh with a London office.

# Discovering Scotch Whisky Tours, Trails and Tourism

## Key Facts
- Over 70 Scotch whisky visitor centres across Scotland's five whisky regions
- Distilleries welcome public visits (check with distillery before travelling)
- Distilleries offer great Scotch whisky, Scottish hospitality, and a fabulous day out
- Interactive Distillery Map available for planning visits

## Production Signals
- Distilleries showcase the art of Scotch whisky production
- Visitors can learn about malting, mashing, fermentation, distillation, and maturation processes
- Opportunities to see traditional and modern equipment used in whisky making

## Commercial Signals
- Distilleries often have tasting rooms where visitors can sample a range of whiskies
- Whisky blends and limited editions may be available for purchase
- Distillery merchandise (glassware, books, etc.) sold at visitor centres

## Risks / Unknowns
- Some distilleries may have restricted access to certain areas or production processes
- Availability of tasting sessions can vary depending on the time of year and staffing levels
- Covid-related restrictions may impact visitation policies

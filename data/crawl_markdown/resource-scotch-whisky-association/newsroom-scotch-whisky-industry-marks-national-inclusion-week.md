# Scotch Whisky Industry Marks National Inclusion Week
The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) celebrated National Inclusion Week (27 Sep–3 Oct 2021), reflecting on a year of progress since launching its Diversity & Inclusivity Charter in September 2020. The charter sets minimum standards for all SWA members to improve equal opportunities and foster diversity across the sector’s global workforce.

Key initiatives highlighted:
- **Workshops & webinars** covering mental‑health awareness and LGBTQ+ support.
- A **Focus Group** (established March 2021) driving an industry‑wide workplan for equality, diversity and inclusion.
- A **benchmarking exercise** to develop recommendations for meeting charter commitments.
- A **scholarship partnership with Heriot‑Watt University**, supporting under‑represented students in the Brewing & Distilling degree.

SWA Chief Executive Karen Betts emphasized that while progress has been made, the industry remains on a journey toward full inclusion and celebrates the diverse career paths within Scotch whisky production and beyond.

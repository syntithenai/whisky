# Enjoying Scotch

## Key Facts
- Over 140 operating distilleries in Scotland, with growth every year
- Made from cereals, water and yeast
- Must be distilled and matured in Scotland for at least three years
- Bottled at a minimum of 40% ABV

## Production Signals
- Distillation: Triple distillation in copper pot stills or column stills
- Maturation: Minimum three years in oak casks, often longer
- Aging: Influenced by wood type, char level, size and shape of cask

## Commercial Signals
- Wide variety of expressions to suit different palates
- Growing number of distilleries expanding production
- Increasing global demand for Scotch Whisky

## Risks / Unknowns
- Climate change may impact whisky regions over time
- Competition from other spirits and beverages
- Regulatory changes affecting production or sales

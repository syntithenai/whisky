# A Responsible Industry
The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) promotes responsible drinking, marketing and industry engagement to reduce alcohol harm. It highlights its Code of Practice for marketing, the Scotch Whisky Action Fund (SWAF), and campaigns such as *Made to be Measured*.

## Key Points
- **Responsible Drinking**: Moderation is encouraged; alcohol can fit into a balanced lifestyle when consumed responsibly.
- **Scotch Whisky Action Fund**: Since 2013, SWA funds projects tackling alcohol‑related harm in Scotland.
- **Code of Practice**: The industry’s sixth edition (2025) sets minimum standards for marketing and promotion domestically and abroad.
- **Campaigns & Initiatives**: *Made to be Measured*, responsible consumption campaigns, and targeted funding for underage drinking interventions.
- **Recent News**: 2025 Code of Practice release; 2024 new funding for under‑age drinking; 2023 support for the Deposit Return Scheme (DRS) delay.

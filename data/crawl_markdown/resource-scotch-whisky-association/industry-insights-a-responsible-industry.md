# A Responsible Industry

## Key Facts
- Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) promotes responsible drinking and engagement with authorities to reduce harmful alcohol use.
- SWA funded the delivery of projects through the Scotch Whisky Action Fund since 2013.
- The industry has developed a Code of Practice for responsible marketing and promotion in the UK and abroad.

## Production Signals
- Scotch Whisky is made to be measured, sipped, and savoured responsibly.
- Distilleries adhere to strict quality control measures throughout production.

## Commercial Signals
- Industry takes seriously its commitment to market products responsibly.
- Marketing campaigns emphasize moderation and responsible consumption.
- SWA publishes updated Responsible Marketing and Promotion Code of Practice regularly.

## Risks / Unknowns
- Potential for increased harmful drinking if marketing efforts are not adequately regulated.
- Challenges in enforcing responsible drinking messages across diverse global markets.

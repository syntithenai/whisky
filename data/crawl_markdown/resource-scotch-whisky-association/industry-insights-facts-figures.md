# Facts & Figures

- **43 bottles per second** (70 cl @40% ABV) are shipped worldwide, totaling over **1.3 bn** annually.
- Export value reached **£5.36 bn in 2025**, with Scotch whisky accounting for **74 % of Scottish food & drink exports** and **22 % of all UK food & drink exports**.
- The industry contributes **£7.1 bn GVA to the UK economy (2022)**, supporting more than **66 k jobs** across Scotland and the wider UK.
- Roughly **90 % of barley** used is sourced in Scotland; there are **152 operating distilleries (June 2025)**.
- In 2024, **2.7 m visitors** toured distillery visitor centres.
- About **22 m casks** are maturing in Scottish warehouses, equivalent to ~12 bn bottles.

These figures illustrate Scotch whisky’s economic importance and global reach.

# Scotch Whisky Association
The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) is the trade body representing Scotland’s whisky industry. Its website offers:
- **Industry insights** on exports, tariffs, sustainability and policy.
- A **story of Scotch** covering heritage, production and the global market.
- Resources for **discovering Scotch**, including a distillery map, FAQs and how‑it‑is‑made guides.
- Information on **membership**, careers, and contact details.

Key highlights include 43 bottles exported every second worldwide, recent trade negotiations (UK‑Korea FTA, China tariff talks), and the SWA’s call for political support in the upcoming Scottish elections.

# Scotch Whisky Association

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) is the trade body representing Scotland’s whisky industry. Its website offers:
- **Industry news** – updates on exports, tariffs and policy developments.
- **Insight sections** – facts & figures, sustainability, skills, inclusion, and responsible drinking.
- **Educational resources** – a story of Scotch, how it is made, FAQs, and a distillery map.
- **Member information** – details on the association’s members and how to join.

The site serves as a hub for industry stakeholders, policymakers and whisky enthusiasts alike.

**Scotch Whisky Association 2025 Export Figures (Feb 12 2026)**

- Global exports fell **‑1.8 % in value** to £5.3 bn and **‑4.3 % in volume** (≈1.34 bn 70 cl bottles, 43 bottles/second).
- The United States remains the largest market but saw a **‑15 % drop in volume** and **‑7 % in value** after a 10 % tariff was imposed in April 2025.
- Other key markets: France (‑3.6 % value), India (+15 % value, +15 % volume), Singapore (‑11.6 % value), Turkey (+43 % value).
- Category shifts: Single Malt down 6 % in value; Blended Whisky up slightly due to India and Brazil.
- The SWA urges the UK government to secure a zero‑tariff deal with the US, pursue FTAs with Thailand, Mercosur, GCC, and halt further domestic tax increases.

**Key Takeaway:** Tariffs and rising costs are eroding export volumes, especially in the US, while growth remains uneven across regions.

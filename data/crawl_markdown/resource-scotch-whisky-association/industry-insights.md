# Industry Insights

## Key Facts
- Scotch Whisky is the world's No.1 internationally traded spirit drink.
- There are 43 bottles of Scotch Whisky exported every second to markets all over the world!

## Production Signals
- The SWA works to ensure fair access to global markets for Scotch Whisky producers.
- Legal team protects Scotch Whisky around the world.

## Commercial Signals
- Scotch Whisky is a leading internationally traded spirit drink.
- Fair access to all markets worldwide is ensured by the SWA.

## Risks/Unknowns
- Harmful drinking habits among consumers need to be addressed responsibly.
- Maintaining fair trade practices in global whisky markets.

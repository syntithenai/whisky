# An Open Letter to Scotland's Young People

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) released an open letter on **10 August 2021** addressed to Scottish students receiving their SQA results. The letter, co‑signed by industry leaders and partners from various sectors, pledges support for young people aged 16–24 in securing employment, apprenticeships, further or higher education, training programmes, or volunteering opportunities within two years.

Key points:
- Recognition of the resilience shown by students during the past 18 months.
- Commitment to creating job opportunities and supporting career development.
- Promotion of the #NoWrongPath campaign, highlighting non‑traditional routes into work.
- Alignment with the Young Person’s Guarantee, aiming for inclusive pathways for all young people.

The letter is signed by senior executives from the SWA, Scottish Financial Enterprise, Scottish Tourism Alliance, Institute of Directors, Skills Development Scotland, and other partner organisations.

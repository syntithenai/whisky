## Key Points
- **Gin** is essentially over 99 % vodka with botanicals added.
- **Vodka yeast**: any ethanol‑producing yeast works; some are preferred for flavour or yield.
- **Fermentation** can occur *on the grains* (grains remain) or *off the grains* (grains removed before fermentation).
- **Vodka ingredients**: mostly wheat and rye; potato vodka is a minority.
- **Distillation columns** vary by spirit type; Headlands uses several for their products.
- **Gluten‑free claim**: despite using gluten‑containing grains, the distillation process removes gluten proteins, making the final spirits safe for most people with celiac disease or gluten sensitivity.

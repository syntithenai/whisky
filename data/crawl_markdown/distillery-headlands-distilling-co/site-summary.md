## Long Description
# Headlands Distilling Co.

Headlands Distilling Co., based in North Wollongong, NSW, is a boutique craft distillery that focuses on **single‑cask whisky**, **native‑botanical gin** (notably Boobialla and Daalgaal), and a small range of vodka and liqueurs.  The company places strong emphasis on sustainability: all spirits are produced with renewable energy, the still is an energy‑efficient continuous column that recovers heat, and bottles are returned for credit.

## Tours & Tastings
Free tours run every Friday (4–6 pm) and Sunday (12–2 pm).  Visitors can see grain handling without bags, milling, fermentation tanks, and the still.  Founders Dean, Thomas, Lachie and Jared lead the tours and offer tastings of native botanicals such as juniper, Illawarra plum, desert raisins and emu apples.

- **Single‑cask whiskies** – Pedro Ximénez, Oloroso, Palo Cortado, Spanish Sherry set, Illawarra Plum, Muscat, Apera, Resin Brewing 8‑Shades Stout.  All priced at AUD 140 (sale from 160) and rated 5/5.

- **Other items** – Barleysan hand sanitiser (250 ml $5.95 / 1000 ml $19.95) and Daalgaal gin with honey.

## Sustainability Highlights
- Renewable energy powers heating, chiller, bottling and lighting.
- Continuous still recovers up to **80 %** of heat; no waste water in the condenser.
- Bottle return program: 700 ml glass bottles returned for a $5 credit.
- No single‑use plastic seals – aluminium or wax top only.
- Reusable glass beakers and biodegradable paper cups.
- Spent grain husks and yeast donated to local farmers; Illawarra plum skins given to restaurants.

## Education & Resources
Headlands publishes articles on distillation science (e.g., *The Three Ways to Make Gin*, *Types of Distillation Columns*), sustainability, and native botanicals.  They also offer workshops and course modules on botanical extraction, vacuum distillation, and single‑cask whisky development.

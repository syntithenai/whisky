## Long Description
# Headlands Distilling Co.

Headlands Distilling Co., based in Wollongong, NSW, produces a range of premium spirits that showcase local barley, native botanicals and innovative cask finishes. Their portfolio includes vodka, gin, single‑cask whiskies finished in Spanish sherry barrels, liqueurs made from Illawarra plum, and a coffee‑fortified spirit.

## Core Philosophy
- **Grain to glass**: In‑house milling, cold (lager‑style) fermentation, double distillation and carbon filtering give a clean, balanced profile.
- **Local sourcing**: Australian barley grown by Michael Sinclair, Illawarra plum harvested from coastal areas, and native botanicals such as rainforest limes and wattleseed.
- **Cask innovation**: First‑release whiskies are aged in American oak ex‑bourbon barrels then finished in authentic Spanish sherry casks (Oloroso, Pedro Ximénez, Palo Cortado). The result is a complex, terroir‑expressive spirit.

## Product Highlights
- **Seacliff Vodka** – 700 mL, 40% ALC/VOL, platinum medal at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition (2024) and double gold medals 2022‑24. Smooth, minimal burn, subtle sweet finish.
- **Mt. & Sea Gin** – 700 mL, 43% ALC/VOL, gold medals at SFWSC and London Spirits. Fresh juniper with rainforest lime, wattleseed and cinnamon.
- **Daalgaal Gin** – 700 mL, 26% ALC/VOL, limited to 500 hand‑signed bottles. Infused with Illawarra plum for six months and sweetened with native stingless bee honey; floral‑sweet profile.
- **Illawarra Plum Single Cask Whisky (Plum)** – 700 mL, 46% ABV, first release single cask finished in a Spirit of the Illawarra Plum barrel after four years in American oak. Notes: plum, honey, vanilla bean, caramel apple, smoked oak.
- **Illawarra Plum Single Cask Whisky (Oloroso)** – 700 mL, 46% ABV, finished three years in Spanish Oloroso sherry casks; cocoa, roasted nuts, spiced oak, treacle.
- **Illawarra Plum Single Cask Whisky (Pedro Ximénez)** – 700 mL, 46% ABV, finished in Pedro Ximénez sherry; raisins, fig cake, molasses, dark chocolate, orange marmalade.
- **Illawarra Plum Single Cask Whisky (Palo Cortado)** – 700 mL, 46% ABV, finished in Palo Cortado sherry; toffee, orange peel, salted caramel, roasted almond.
- **Liqueur of the Illawarra Plum** – 200 mL, 20% ABV, rich, velvety dessert‑style liqueur aged on plum skins.
- **Spirit Fortified Coffee Liqueur** – 700 mL, 20% A/L, cold‑brew coffee infused into Australian grape spirit with cacao nibs and Ethiopian Yirga Cheffe beans.

## Awards & Recognition
Headlands products have earned double gold medals at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition (2022‑24), gold medals at London Spirits (2024) and multiple silver/bronze awards from Bartender Spirits, Australian Gin Awards and Australian Distilled Spirits Awards.

## Visitor Experience
The distillery offers tours, tastings and a retail shop. Visitors can book guided visits online; age verification is required for purchase of spirits.

# The Three Ways to Make Gin
Headlands Distilling Co. explains the three primary methods of gin production:
1. **Maceration** – botanicals are soaked in alcohol and then distilled.
2. **Vapour infusion** – botanicals sit above the liquid; vapours pass through them during distillation (used by Bombay Sapphire).
3. **Steeping without distillation** – a survival‑style method that simply infuses alcohol with botanicals, not used commercially.
The article also discusses vacuum distillation, single‑shot vs multiple‑shot gin, and how steeping can add flavour to already distilled gins (e.g., Illawarra Plum‑infused Boobialla).

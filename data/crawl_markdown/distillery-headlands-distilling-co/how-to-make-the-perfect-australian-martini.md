# How to make the perfect Australian martini

Headlands Distilling Co. explains how to craft a classic Australian martini using their **Boobialla** gin (made from native Australian juniper) and **Maidenii Dry Vermouth**. The recipe emphasizes chilling the glass, proper dilution, and simple garnishes.

- Chill the glass in a freezer or with ice.
- Mix 60 mL Boobialla gin + 15 mL Maidenii dry vermouth.
- Stir gently for 30 s over ice to chill and dilute.
- Strain into a chilled martini glass.
- Garnish with a twisted citrus peel or an odd number of green olives.

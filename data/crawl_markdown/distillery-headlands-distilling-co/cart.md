# Headlands Distilling Co.

## About
Headlands Distilling Co. is a distillery located in the Illawarra region, known for their native stingless bee honey whisky and other unique spirits.

## Key Facts
- **Distillery Location**: Illawarra, Australia
- **Primary Spirit**: Native stingless bee honey whisky
- **Sustainability Focus**: Emphasis on sustainable practices and local ingredients
- **Age Verification Required**: Customers must be over 18 years of age to purchase

## Production Signals
- Uses native Australian ingredients like stingless bee honey
- Small-batch production methods
- Handcrafted spirits with a focus on quality and uniqueness

## Commercial Signals
- Limited availability due to small-scale production
- Strong brand positioning around sustainability and local heritage
- Collaborations with other local producers for unique blends

## Risks / Unknowns
- Dependence on native ingredients which may vary seasonally
- Smaller scale operations could limit market reach compared to larger distilleries

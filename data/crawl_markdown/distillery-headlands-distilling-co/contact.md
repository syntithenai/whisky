# Headlands Distilling Co.

## Key Facts
- Located in North Wollongong, NSW, Australia
- Offers free distillery tours showcasing the fermentation and distillation process
- Tasting bar available for sampling products
- Contactable via email form, phone (0493 760 121), or social media (Facebook/Instagram)
- Encourages direct contact for wholesale inquiries through Upstock

## Production Signals
- Specializes in Illawarra plum whisky
- Uses local native ingredients
- Emphasizes sustainability and environmental consciousness

## Commercial Signals
- Brand positioned as a premium, locally-sourced Australian spirit
- Focus on storytelling and heritage through their "Illawarra Plum Journal"
- Limited edition releases and collaborations with other local producers

## Risks/Unknowns
- Relatively new distillery (established 2021)
- Smaller production scale compared to larger regional distilleries
- Dependence on local plum harvest for raw materials

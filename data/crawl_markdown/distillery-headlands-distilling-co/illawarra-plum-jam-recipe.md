# Illawarra Plum Jam Recipe

Headlands Distilling Co. shares a simple jam recipe using the native **Illawarra plum** (Podocarpus elatus). The fruit, prized for its high antioxidant content, is blended with sugar and lemon juice, boiled until set, then jarred. The article also highlights how spent plums from gin steeping can be reused for jam, offers 9 creative uses, and lists related products such as Daalgaal Gin and Liqueur of the Illawarra Plum.

---

## Key Points
- **Fruit**: Illawarra plum (Daalgaal, Plum Pine) – high antioxidants.
- **Ingredients**: 1 kg plums, 750 g sugar, 5 Tbsp lemon juice.
- **Process**: Blend, simmer, stir until sugar dissolves, boil 15 min, test set, jar.
- **Reuse**: Spent plums from gin steeping can be jammed.
- **Uses**: Cheese, scones, pancakes, grilled cheese, ice‑cream, toast, cocktails, yoghurt, spirits.
- **Products**: Daalgaal Gin ($100), Liqueur of the Illawarra Plum ($40), Tidal Lines Infused Gin ($90).

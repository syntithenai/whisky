# Australian Gin Consumption: Growth in the Sector

Headlands Distilling Co. reports that Australia’s spirits market has grown steadily from 64.4 m L (2014) to 66.1 m L (2019). Whisky remains the top volume seller, but white spirits—especially gin—have seen a 4.5 % CAGR between 2013‑18. Gin is now the second most consumed spirit, with 8 % of Australians drinking it in the past four weeks and women showing a higher preference for gin over vodka (9.3 % vs 6.8 %). The craft sector accounts for 6 % of total spirits volume, driven by innovative products such as Boobialla (native Australian juniper) and Daalgaal (Illawarra plum‑infused). Despite dominance by Diageo and Barcadi, the market is expected to continue expanding due to home‑cocktail trends and new flavour releases.

# Liqueur of the Illawarra Plum
- **Price**: $40.00 (200 mL, 20% ABV)
- **Description**: Rich, velvety dessert‑style liqueur similar to Port or Sherry; smooth and sweet.
- **Use cases**: Sip neat after dinner with dark chocolate or ice cream; cocktail mixer; food basting.
- **Production**:
  - Hand‑foraged Illawarra Plum (Daalgaal) from coastal areas
  - In‑house fermentation & distillation
  - Barrel aged on plum skins to enhance flavour
- **Awards**: Gold Medal 2021 SFWSC; multiple silver and bronze medals at SFWSC, London Spirits Competition, Bartender Spirits Awards, Australian Distilled Spirits Awards.
- **Packaging**: Black label with gold font.

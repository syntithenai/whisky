# Refund and Returns Policy

Headlands Distilling Co. offers a **30‑day** refund window for unopened, undamaged items in original packaging. Proof of purchase is required. Approved refunds are credited to the original payment method within 10 business days. Exchanges are only for defective or damaged goods. Return shipping is paid by the customer; costs are non‑refundable and may be deducted from any refund.

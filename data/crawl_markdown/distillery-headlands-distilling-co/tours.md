# Headlands Distilling Co. Tours

- **Free tours** on Fridays (4‑6 pm) and Sundays (12‑4 pm). No booking required.
- Visitors can see the entire production line: grain silo, milling machine, fermentation tank, stills, and learn how grain is handled without bags.
- Tasting sessions include native botanicals such as juniper (used for Boobialla gin), Illawarra plums, desert raisins, emu apples, and other seasonal produce.
- Tours are led by the founders: Dean (MBA, Spinal Cure advocate), Thomas (PhD in Science – grains), Lachie (PhD in Science – distillation), and Jared (Chemical engineer/organic chemist).

> *“Come visit us! Free tours and free tastings at our distillery run every Friday and Sunday!”*

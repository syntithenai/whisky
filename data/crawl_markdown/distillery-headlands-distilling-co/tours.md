# Tours at Headlands Distilling Co.

Headlands offers **free tours** on Fridays (4‑6 pm) and Sundays (12‑4 pm). Visitors can:
- Walk through the grain silo, milling machine and fermentation tanks.
- See the still in operation.
- Taste native botanicals such as juniper (used for Boobialla gin), Illawarra plums, desert raisins, emu apples, and other seasonal produce.
- Learn about vodka, whisky, gin, and Spirit of the Illawarra Plum production.

Tours are led by one of the founders: Dean, Thomas, Lachie or Jared.

All tours are open to the public with no prior booking required.

# Headlands Distilling Co.

Headlands Distilling Co., founded in early 2015 by four friends in Wollongong, is Australia’s first distillery. The company follows a *Grain to Glass* philosophy, handling every step from milling barley to bottling spirits. Its product line began with Seacliff Vodka and a range of gins, expanded in 2019 with the **Spirit of the Illawarra Plum** (9‑month barrel aged), **Boobialla Gin** (native juniper & Daalgaal steeped in Illawarra plums for >6 months), and in 2020 launched its first **Single Malt Whisky**, finished in casks seasoned with Seppeltsfield Rare Rich Apera and Rare Muscat. Headlands plans to release the world’s first Illawarra Plum‑cask whisky.

The distillery sources grains directly from Riverina farms, ensuring traceability from paddock to bottle. Tours are offered on Fridays (4–6 pm) and Sundays (12–4 pm).

# Headlands Distilling Co.

Headlands Distilling Co., founded in early 2015 by four friends in Wollongong, is Australia’s first distillery. The company follows a **Grain‑to‑Glass** philosophy, handling every step from milling to bottling with an emphasis on purity and quality. Its product line began with Seacliff Vodka and a range of gins, expanded in 2019 with the *Spirit of the Illawarra Plum* (9 months barrel aged), *Boobialla Gin* (native juniper & Daalgaal steeped in Illawarra Plums for over six months), and in 2020 launched its first **Single Malt Whisky** finished in Seppeltsfield Rare Rich Apera and Rare Muscat casks, with plans to release an Illawarra Plum‑cask whisky.

The distillery sources grains directly from Riverina farms, ensuring traceability from paddock to bottle. Visitors can tour the facility on Fridays (4–6 pm) or Sundays (12–4 pm).

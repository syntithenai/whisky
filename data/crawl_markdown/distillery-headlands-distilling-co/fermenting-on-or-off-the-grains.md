# Fermenting on or off the grains
A practical guide from Headlands Distilling Co. explaining how keeping grains in or removing them before fermentation affects enzyme activity, flavour extraction, energy use and distillation outcomes. The article lists pros/cons for both methods and concludes that Headlands ferments on the grains to create a complex vodka base while using a tall continuous stripping column to control unwanted flavours.

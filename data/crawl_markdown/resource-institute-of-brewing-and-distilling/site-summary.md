## Long Description
The Chartered Institute of Brewers and Distillers (CIBD), headquartered in the United Kingdom, is a leading professional body that supports individuals and organisations across the beer, spirit, malt, packaging and cider sectors. Since its founding in 1886, CIBD has developed a comprehensive suite of educational programmes ranging from foundation certificates to Masters level degrees, as well as on‑demand short courses covering topics such as environmental management, spirit maturation, sensory analysis and advanced brewing science.

Beyond education, the institute provides a robust membership network that offers continuous professional development (CPD) resources, industry news, peer networking opportunities and access to exclusive events. These include annual dinners, symposia, international congresses and regional meetings. CIBD also publishes BDI Magazine and the Journal of the Institute of Brewing, which feature research articles, case studies and technical insights.

The site serves a diverse audience: craft distillers seeking specialised knowledge, industry professionals looking to upskill, students exploring careers in brewing science, and academic researchers interested in the latest developments. Partnerships with organisations such as ASBC further extend its global reach and collaborative opportunities.

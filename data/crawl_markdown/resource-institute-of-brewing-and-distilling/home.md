# Chartered Institute of Brewers and Distillers

## Key Facts
- Founded in 1886, providing gold-standard technical qualifications for brewing and distilling industries worldwide.
- Offers Foundation, General Certificate, Diploma, and Master level programs across Brewing, Distilling, Malting, Packaging, and Cider Making.
- Supports professional development through CPD resources, events, and networking opportunities.

## Production Signals
- Comprehensive training pathways from foundational to advanced levels in brewing and distilling.
- Emphasis on practical knowledge and skills relevant to modern beverage production processes.

## Commercial Signals
- Recognized qualifications enhance career prospects and credibility within the drinks industry.
- Membership provides access to a global network for collaboration and innovation.

## Risks/Unknowns
- Rapidly evolving regulatory landscape may impact qualification requirements over time.
- Competition from other professional bodies in beverage education sectors.

# Morris of Rutherglen
Morris of Rutherglen is an Australian wine and whisky brand founded in 1859 by the Morris family. With over 165 years of winemaking heritage, the company now produces award‑winning fortified wines and a distinct line of Australian single‑malt whiskies. The whiskies are crafted from 100 % Australian ingredients, aged in hand‑finished barrels from the family cooperage, and finished in Morris fortified wine casks, giving them a unique flavour profile.

# Peter Phillips Gin – Noosa Heads Distillery

Peter Phillips Gin is a London‑dry gin crafted in small batches at Noosa Heads Distillery, the first distillery on Queensland’s Sunshine Coast. The spirit blends classic botanicals—juniper, coriander, angelica—with fresh lemon, dried honeydew melon, cassia cinnamon, lavender petals, Chinese green tea and lemongrass for a bright, layered citrus‑forward profile that is both adventurous and refined.

- **ABV**: 40%
- **Sizes**: 200 ml ($49 AUD) & 700 ml ($129 AUD)
- **Price**: $49 AUD (200 ml), $129 AUD (700 ml)
- **Availability**: Sold out on the site, but a percentage of sales supports the Peter Phillips Foundation.

The gin celebrates the partnership between pop‑artist Peter Phillips and Noosa Heads Distillery, emphasizing craftsmanship, character and boldness.

# Laguna Whisky Old Fashioned
- **Price**: AUD 33.00 (200 ml)
- **Ingredients**: single‑malt whisky, bitters, sugar, water.
- **Serving**: 50 ml over ice with orange zest; shake before serving.
- **Barley source**: Riverina NSW, family‑farmed, sustainably processed.
- **Production**:
  - Mash → 5‑day fermentation → double distillation in a 2000 L copper pot still.
  - Tails used to maintain flavour consistency.
- **Aging**: Blend of American and French oak barrels sourced from Australian wine makers; includes red‑wine barrels, re‑cooperated charred barrels.
- **Blending & bottling**: No intense filtration; each release unique.
- **Availability**: Sold out online; can be purchased at the distillery in Noosaville, QLD.

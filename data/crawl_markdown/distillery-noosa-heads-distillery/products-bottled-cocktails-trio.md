# Bottled Cocktails Trio – Noosa Heads Distillery

- **Product**: Three 200 ml bottled cocktails (Laguna Whisky Old Fashioned, Noosa Tropo, Noosa Sweet Cheeks) sold as a trio for AUD 85.
- **Ingredients & Serving**:
  - *Laguna*: Laguna Single Malt Whisky, bitters, sugar, water – serve 50 ml over ice with orange zest.
  - *Noosa Tropo*: Noosa Strawberry gin, pineapple, lime, sugar, water – serve 50 ml over ice with pineapple wedge and mint.
  - *Noosa Sweet Cheeks*: Noosa Gin, mango, lime, sugar, bitters, water – serve 50 ml over ice with lime wedge; shake before serving.
- **Distillery background**: Australian‑made whisky sourced from Riverina barley, distilled in a 2000 L copper pot still, aged in a blend of American and French oak barrels (including red wine casks). No filtration is used.
- **Availability**: Sold online via the distillery’s Shopify store; shipping only within Australia.

## Distillery facts
- Located at 19 Venture Drive, Noosaville QLD, Australia.
- Uses Riverina NSW barley from independent family farms.
- Distillation: double‑distilled in a 2000 L copper pot still; tails cut used to maintain flavour consistency.
- Barrel aging: blend of American and French oak, including re‑cooperated red wine barrels.
- No filtration applied.

## Production notes
- Mash on site converts malt starches into sweet wash.
- 5‑day fermentation by yeast.
- Double distillation separates flavours; hearts retained as whisky.
- Tails recycled to next batch for consistency.
- Blending from multiple barrel types creates unique flavour profiles.

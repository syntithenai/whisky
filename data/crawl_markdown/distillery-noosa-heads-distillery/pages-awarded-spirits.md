## Our Distillation Process:
- **2000 Litre Copper Pot Reflux Still**: Produces fine spirits with diverse capabilities.
- **Traditional Distilling Methods**:
  - Gin is bathed and boiled like a traditional London Dry.
  - Single Malt is pot to condenser, keeping true to its roots.
- **Handcrafted Products**: From bottling & labeling to packaging, truly artisan and local.

## Sustainability Initiatives:
- **Natural & Organic Ingredients**: Sourced from fresh North Qld Honeydew Melon and dedicated Noosa Heads Distillery barley field located in the NSW Riverina.
- **Reduced Carbon Footprint**: Premium malt house accredited as one of the most sustainable malt houses globally, reducing carbon footprint by up to 30%.
- **Solar Power**: Roof covered with 94 kilowatts of solar panels; facility runs 100% on sun on sunny days.
- **Waste Management**: Brewing & botanical waste sent to local farmer for cattle feeding.

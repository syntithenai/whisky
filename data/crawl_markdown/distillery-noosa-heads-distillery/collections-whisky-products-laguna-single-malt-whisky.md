# Laguna Single Malt Whisky

- **Price**: AUD 15.00 (50 ml), AUD 49.00 (200 ml), AUD 149.00 (700 ml)
- **Origin**: Australian single‑origin barley, malted in Riverina NSW.
- **Distillation**: 5‑day fermentation, double distillation in a 2 000 L copper pot still.
- **Aging**: Blend of French & American oak barrels sourced from Australian wine casks (red‑wine influence).
- **Sustainability**: All stages – mash, ferment, distill, age, bottle – performed on‑site; carbon‑footprint minimised.
- **Unique selling point**: Entire production chain under one roof, no external wash purchase.

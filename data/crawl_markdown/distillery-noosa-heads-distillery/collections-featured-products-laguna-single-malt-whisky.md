# Laguna Single Malt Whisky

- **Price**: AUD $15 (50 ml), $49 (200 ml), $149 (700 ml)
- **Origin**: Australian single‑origin barley, malted in Riverina NSW.
- **Distillation**: 5‑day fermentation → double distillation in a 2 000 L copper pot still.
- **Aging**: Blend of French & American oak barrels sourced from Australian wine cooperatives; ex‑wine casks add complexity.
- **Production**: All stages (mashing, fermentation, distillation, barrel‑aging, blending, bottling) performed on‑site at Noosa Heads Distillery.
- **Sustainability**: Family‑owned, carbon‑footprint minimised, uses locally sourced barley and barrels.
- **Availability**: Sold online only within Australia; no international shipping.

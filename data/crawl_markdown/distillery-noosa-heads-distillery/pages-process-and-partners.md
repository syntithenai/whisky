# Noosa Heads Distillery – Process & Partners

Noosa Heads Distillery (NHD) is the Sunshine Coast’s first craft distillery, producing award‑winning whisky, gin and vodka from locally sourced Australian barley, honeydew melon and other botanicals. The distillery operates a 2 000 L copper pot reflux still that weighs 3.75 t and uses traditional London‑dry and pot‑to‑condenser techniques to create distinct flavour profiles. Sustainability is central: the site runs on solar power (94 kW), sends waste to local farmers, and partners with a certified sustainable malt house in NSW Riverina.

The distillery welcomes visitors Friday–Sunday for tours, tastings and dining, and offers a full range of spirits hand‑crafted, bottled and labelled on‑site. Testimonials highlight the welcoming atmosphere, quality drinks and friendly staff.

# Peter Phillips Gin – Noosa Heads Distillery

Peter Phillips Gin is a London‑dry gin crafted in small batches at Noosa Heads Distillery, Australia. It blends classic botanicals—juniper, coriander, angelica—with bright citrus (lemon), honeydew melon, cassia cinnamon, lavender petals, Chinese green tea and lemongrass for a layered, aromatic profile. Bottled at 40 % ABV in 200 ml and 700 ml sizes, the gin is sold for AUD 49.00 and AUD 129.00 respectively.

The spirit celebrates British pop‑artist Peter Phillips and supports his foundation through every bottle sold.

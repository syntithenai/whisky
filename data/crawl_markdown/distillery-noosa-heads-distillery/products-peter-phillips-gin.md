# Peter Phillips Gin – Noosa Heads Distillery

Peter Phillips Gin is a London‑dry gin crafted in small batches at Noosa Heads Distillery, the first distillery on Queensland’s Sunshine Coast. The spirit blends classic juniper with bright citrus (lemon, honeydew melon), floral lavender, and warm spices such as cassia cinnamon, coriander and angelica, finished with lemongrass and Chinese green tea for a clean herbal note.

- **ABV**: 40%
- **Sizes**: 200 ml ($49 AUD) / 700 ml ($129 AUD)
- **Price**: $49–$129 AUD depending on volume
- **Availability**: Sold out in the store; a percentage of sales supports the Peter Phillips Foundation.
- **Flavor profile**: Bright citrus lift, soft floral notes, gentle spice and warmth, clean herbal finish.
- **Packaging**: 200 ml or 700 ml bottles, Australian‑made.

The gin is named after British pop‑artist Peter Phillips, who partnered with the distillery to celebrate creativity and boldness. The product is marketed as a tribute to two pioneers—Phillips and the distillery’s founders—and encourages consumers to “live life boldly.”

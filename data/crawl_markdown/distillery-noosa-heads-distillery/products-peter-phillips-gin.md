# Peter Phillips Gin – Noosa Heads Distillery

Peter Phillips Gin is a London‑dry gin crafted in small batches at Noosa Heads Distillery, the first distillery on Australia’s Sunshine Coast. The spirit blends classic juniper with bright citrus (lemon myrtle, honeydew melon) and a warm spice profile (cassia cinnamon, coriander, angelica). Floral notes from lavender petals and Chinese green tea finish the palate, while lemongrass adds a clean herbal touch.

- **ABV**: 40%
- **Sizes**: 200 ml ($49 AUD) / 700 ml ($129 AUD)
- **Price**: $49–$129 AUD depending on volume
- **Availability**: Sold out in the online store; a percentage of sales supports the Peter Phillips Foundation.

The gin celebrates the partnership between pop‑artist Peter Phillips and Noosa Heads Distillery, emphasizing craftsmanship, originality, and creative spirit.

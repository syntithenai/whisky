# American Craft Spirits Association – 2022 Economic Briefing

The 2022 Annual Craft Spirits Economic Briefing (PDF) presents a comprehensive snapshot of the U.S. craft spirits market for 2021 and projections to 2026. Key take‑aways include:

- **Market size**: 13.2 million 9‑liter cases sold in 2021, $7.5 billion in value.
- **Growth**: 10.4 % volume CAGR (2016‑21), 12.2 % value CAGR; active distiller count up 17.3 % to 2,687.
- **Concentration**: <2 % of producers account for 55.9 % of cases; 89.2 % are small producers (≤5,258 cases).
- **Geography**: 46.7 % home‑state sales vs 53.3 % other states; exports grew 58 % to 164,000 cases.
- **Investment & employment**: Total investment $826 M in 2021 (9 % YoY), average $337 k per distiller; full‑time employees 24,255.
- **Future outlook**: Scenarios for 2026 range from 4.9 % to 9.8 % market share depending on CAGR assumptions.

The briefing also details methodology, definitions (750,000 proof gallons limit), and stakeholder contacts.

# ACSA Submits Comments to FTC on Draft Strategic Plan

The American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA) has sent comments to the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) regarding its Draft Strategic Plan for FY 2026‑2030. In the letter, ACSA praised the FTC’s commitment to fair competition and urged a comprehensive study of how state regulations and direct‑to‑consumer shipping restrictions impact small distilleries. The association cited the 2003 FTC wine e‑commerce study as an example of useful research that could inform policy, expand consumer access, and promote a more competitive marketplace for craft spirits producers.

# American Craft Spirits Association Bylaws (Updated March 12, 2026)

- Governing body: Board of Directors.
- Director composition: 9–20 members; at least one‑third must be Craft Brand Members.
- Designated Directors elected from Voting Members and geographically distributed across U.S. time zones.
- Ex Officio Director is the immediate past Treasurer.
- Terms: 3 years for Designated Directors, 1 year for Officers with possible extension.
- Meetings: annual in‑person; quarterly regular (in‑person or teleconference); special meetings by President or two directors.
- Quorum: two‑thirds of Board; one‑third of Voting Members for member meetings.
- Committees: Executive, Finance, Nominating, and other committees as authorized.
- Officers: President, Vice‑President, Secretary/Treasurer (also CEO role), with defined duties and removal procedures.
- Membership: Voting members must be Craft Distillers or Brands meeting production/ownership thresholds; non‑voting members are affiliates.
- Indemnification provisions for directors and officers.

These bylaws outline governance, election, meeting, and operational protocols for the ACSA.

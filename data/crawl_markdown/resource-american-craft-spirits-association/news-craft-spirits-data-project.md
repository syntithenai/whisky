# Craft Spirits Data Project
The American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA) launched the **Craft Spirits Data Project** (CSDP) in 2016 to track economic trends in the craft‑spirits industry. Partnering with Park Street, a global import and back‑end office/financial solutions firm, CSDP provides annual reports that link the sector to job creation, agricultural growth, and tourism. The page lists all yearly reports from 2016 through 2025 and offers links to related resources such as webinars, a distillery guide e‑book, and the ACSA magazine.

# Craft Spirits Data Project

The American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA) hosts the **Craft Spirits Data Project (CSDP)**, an ongoing research initiative launched in 2016 that tracks economic trends within the craft spirits sector. Partnering with Park Street, a global import and back‑end office/financial solutions firm, CSDP produces annual reports linking industry growth to job creation, agricultural development, and tourism. The site lists all yearly reports (2016–2025) and provides links to PDFs, webinars, and related resources.

## Key Points
- **CSDP**: Annual economic analysis of craft spirits.
- **Partner**: Park Street.
- **Impact Areas**: employment, agriculture, tourism.
- **Reports**: 2016‑2025 available as downloadable PDFs.
- **Resources**: webinars, distillery guide, magazine, contact info.

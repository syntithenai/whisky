# American Craft Spirits Association

## Key Facts
- Non-profit organization representing small-batch distillers in the United States
- Promotes craft spirits and supports legislation favorable to the industry
- Advocates for fair regulations and access to markets

## Production Signals
- Focus on small-batch, handcrafted spirits
- Emphasis on unique flavor profiles and regional ingredients
- Use of traditional stills and aging methods

## Commercial Signals
- Strong brand storytelling and marketing
- Partnerships with restaurants, bars, and retailers
- Participation in trade shows and events

## Risks/Unknowns
- Competition from larger distillers
- Regulatory changes affecting production and distribution
- Market saturation of craft spirits

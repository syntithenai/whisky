# 2019 Craft Spirits Data Project

- **Purpose**: Annual economic briefing on U.S. craft spirits (Oct 2019). Provides market size, growth rates, producer counts, sales channels, and industry employment/investment.
- **Key Findings**:
  - Active craft distillers grew 11.5% to **2,046** in Aug 2019.
  - Retail volume reached ~9 million cases (25.5% CAGR) and value $4.8 bn (27% CAGR).
  - Market share: 3.9 % of volume, 5.8 % of value (up from 1.5/1.8 % in 2013).
  - Exports fell ~50%, adding only 304k cases.
  - Concentration: ~2 % of producers account for 58 % of sales; 92 % are small (<10k proof gallons) and sell 13 % of cases.
  - Direct‑sales importance rises for small distillers (44 % of total sales).
  - Employment >25,500 (up 7,000+); investment $679 m (↑$86 m).

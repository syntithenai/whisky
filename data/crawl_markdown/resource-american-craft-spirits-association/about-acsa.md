# About ACSA
The American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA) is the sole registered national nonprofit trade group representing U.S. craft spirits producers. It is member‑owned and governed by a 15‑member Board elected by voting members, who must be craft distillers or brands producing fewer than 750 000 proof gallons removed from bond.

**Vision:** All great spirits are recognized as coming from ACSA members worldwide.

**Mission:** Elevate and advocate for the craft spirits community.

**Priorities:** Build membership, increase diversity, improve market landscape, develop organizational stability.

**Values:** Commitment to diversity, dignity, and respect for all industry participants.

ACSA offers resources such as a data project, webinars, an e‑book on starting a distillery, and advocacy tools. It also hosts annual conventions, competitions, and packaging awards.

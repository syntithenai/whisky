# American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA)

## Key Facts
- Essential trade group for craft distillers and distilled spirits producers
- Advocates for and elevates the craft spirits industry
- Offers membership, education programs, advocacy, and networking opportunities

## Production Signals
- Growing demand for high-quality, small-batch spirits
- Increased focus on sustainability and eco-friendly practices in production
- Expansion of distillery capacity to meet market demands

## Commercial Signals
- Strong brand positioning through storytelling and heritage
- Emphasis on transparency and traceability in sourcing ingredients
- Strategic partnerships with retailers and distributors for wider reach

## Risks/Unknowns
- Potential regulatory changes impacting tax structures and labeling requirements
- Competition from established distillers and new entrants in the market
- Supply chain disruptions affecting ingredient availability

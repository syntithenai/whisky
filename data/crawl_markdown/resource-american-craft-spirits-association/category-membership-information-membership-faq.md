# American Craft Spirits Association Membership FAQ

## What are the benefits of becoming a member?
- Exclusive advocacy support
- Deep discounts on education and events
- Tools to promote brand and grow distillery
- Access to members-only updates, networking, and partner program savings

## How do I become a Voting Member?
1. Click [here](#)
2. Scroll down
3. Select membership level matching production volume

## Can I join if I don't have a Distillery Support Permit (DSP) yet?
Yes, ACSA offers Candidate Membership for those pursuing DSP permits in the US. Upgrade upon receipt of DSP.

## How do I check my membership status or renew it?
1. Login to member portal
2. Click "Membership" > "Subscriptions"
3. Update information or renew as needed

## What if I don't operate a distillery but supply products/services to them?
You can join as an Affiliate Member or Sponsor.

## How do I know when my membership expires or has expired?
Check member portal under "Membership" > "Subscriptions". If expired, renew on the join ACSA page using original username.

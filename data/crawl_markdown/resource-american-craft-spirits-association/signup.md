# American Craft Spirits Association Membership Overview

## Key Facts
- **Membership Levels**: Voting Memberships available for Distillers and Brands producing up to 100,000 proof gallons annually.
- **Costs**: Varying annual fees based on production volume ($360 - $2,300+).
- **Eligibility**: Must produce <750,001 proof gallons, comply with Code of Ethics, and own less than 25% by non-craft producers.

## Production Signals
- Targeted at distillers producing small batches (up to ~750k proof gallons/year).
- Emphasizes compliance with ethical standards and adherence to ACSA's Code of Conduct.

## Commercial Signals
- Provides branding opportunities through sponsorships, awards, and trade show exposure.
- Offers advocacy for industry-specific legislative issues and access to exclusive resources like the Craft Spirits Data Project.

## Risks/Unknowns
- Membership fees may be a barrier for very small producers.
- Effectiveness of lobbying efforts varies with political climate.

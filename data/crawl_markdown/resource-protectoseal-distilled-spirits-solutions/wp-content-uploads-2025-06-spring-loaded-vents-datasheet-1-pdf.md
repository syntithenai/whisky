# Spring‑Loaded Vents Datasheet
Protectoseal provides a range of spring‑loaded vent designs for tanks and vessels that require pressure or vacuum relief beyond atmospheric conditions. The vents are available in end‑of‑line, pipe‑away, side‑mount, and other configurations, with set points from 3 PSIG up to 15 PSIG (12.5 PSIG for 12″ vents). They can be made of aluminum, steel, stainless steel, cast steel or C/C76, and may include optional features such as diaphragms, coatings, steam jacketing, or electronic indicators.

Key technical data:
- **Series**: 9540 (end‑of‑line), 19540 (pipe‑away), 9240 (vacuum relief), 19240 (side‑mount), 9800 (pressure relief), 19800 (pipe‑away pressure). 
- **Sizes**: 2″ to 12″ (DN50–DN300). 
- **Set points**: Minimum 3 PSIG, maximum 15 PSIG; weight‑loaded side minimum 2 oz./in². 
- **Materials**: Aluminum cast bodies with 316 SS springs; steel or C/C76 options available.
- **Ordering**: Prefixes (CS, F, M) indicate body material; suffixes P/V/PV denote pressure/vacuum loading.

The datasheet also includes detailed flange dimensions, flow capacity tables, and a Pro‑Flow® sizing guide. Contact information for Protectoseal US, UK, and India is provided at the end of the document.

# ACSA Appoints Emily Pennington as Chief Operating Officer

The American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA) has named **Emily Pennington** its new Chief Operating Officer, effective immediately. The role will work alongside CEO Margie A.S. Lehrman to address industry challenges such as market access and advocacy for independent craft distillers. Pennington brings nearly 15 years of experience in content strategy, organization management, and education—having created *Wine & Spirits Daily* and led the *Craft Spirits Data Project* at Park Street University.

ACSA is a national nonprofit trade association representing U.S. craft spirits producers. Membership is open to independent licensed distillers who remove fewer than 750,000 proof gallons annually from bond.

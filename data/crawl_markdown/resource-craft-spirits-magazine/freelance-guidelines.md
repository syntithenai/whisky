# Freelance Guidelines – Craft Spirits Magazine

Craft Spirits Magazine, the bi‑monthly digital publication of the American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA), invites freelance writers to pitch stories on a wide range of topics covering the craft spirits industry. Articles are typically 1 000 words and occasionally 2 000 words for in‑depth features. The magazine covers business, operations, distribution, legal matters, maturation, packaging, raw materials, retail, safety, sales & marketing, technology, e‑commerce, and more.

**How to Pitch**
- Submit story ideas to news@americancraftspirits.org.
- Avoid single distillery or distiller profiles; focus on broader trends or industry issues.
- Articles appear in the magazine’s department sections (e.g., Business Sense, Distilling Operations).

The site also offers resources such as podcasts, white papers, and a yearbook, and it encourages engagement through ACSA membership.

# Log Still Distillery & Archdiocese of Louisville Collaboration
Log Still Distillery (Gethsemane, KY) has launched **Faithfully Crafted: The Trinity Blend Bourbon**, a partnership with the Archdiocese of Louisville. The bourbon is built from three distinct barrel mash bills and showcases layers of butterscotch, cinnamon‑clove, and smoky spiced oak. It marks the first run on Log Still’s new onsite bottling line and will be sold at the distillery and Monks Road Boiler House. Proceeds support Archdiocese community initiatives.

---

**Key facts**: 
- Product name: *Faithfully Crafted: The Trinity Blend Bourbon*  
- Launch date: January 3, 2025  
- Distillery: Log Still Distillery (Gethsemane, Kentucky)  
- Partner: Archdiocese of Louisville  
- Flavor profile: butterscotch warmth, cinnamon & clove depth, smoky spiced oak finish.

---

**Where to buy**: Log Still Distillery and Monks Road Boiler House (Whiskey Row, Downtown Louisville).

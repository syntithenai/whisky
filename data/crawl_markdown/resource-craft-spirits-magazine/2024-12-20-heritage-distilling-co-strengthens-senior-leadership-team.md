# Heritage Distilling Co. Strengthens Senior Leadership Team
Heritage Distilling Co. (HDC) in Gig Harbor, Washington, announced two key appointments: **Troy Alstead** joins the Board of Directors and **Andrew Varga** becomes Chief Revenue Officer and Director. HDC, founded in 2011 by Justin & Jennifer Stiefel and Drew & Sara Kellerman, has been recognized as the most‑awarded craft distillery for a decade. The company’s portfolio includes award‑winning spirits such as Cocoa Bomb Chocolate Whiskey, Stiefel’s Select Whiskey, Florescence Vodka (collaboration with Chef Danielle Kartes), and the Salute Series of whiskeys supporting military charities.

Both new leaders bring extensive experience—Alstead from Starbucks and various board roles, Varga from Brown‑Forman, Papa John’s, and Zimmerman Advertising. HDC operates two distilleries and seven tasting rooms across Washington and Oregon, selling through select retailers and direct‑to‑consumer delivery in 45 states via Liquid Rails.

> *“We are thrilled to have both Troy and Andrew on the Heritage team,”* said CEO Justin Stiefel.

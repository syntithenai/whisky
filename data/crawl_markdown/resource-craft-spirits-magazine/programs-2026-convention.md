# 2026 American Craft Spirits Convention & Expo

- **Dates & Location**: April 17‑18, 2026 in Sacramento, CA at the SAFE Credit Union Convention Center.
- **Purpose**: A trade‑focused gathering for craft distillers, suppliers and industry partners to network, learn about policy, and showcase products/services.
- **Key Features**:
  - Educational sessions led by industry experts (e.g., Master Distiller Caitlin Bartlemay, CFO Chris Buglisi).
  - Distribution & sales workshops, including a five‑hour “Distribution Workshop” and two half‑day tours of local distilleries.
  - Exhibitor space with booth options (10×10 or 10×20) and associated registration passes.
  - On‑site activities such as a first‑ever cornhole tournament.
- **Registration**: Members $700, Non‑members $975; day pass $300. Online general registration closed; on‑site available.
- **Cancellation Policy**: 50 % refund until March 9, no refund thereafter.
- **App & Logistics**: Dedicated mobile app for schedules and maps; Sacramento International Airport is the nearest airport.

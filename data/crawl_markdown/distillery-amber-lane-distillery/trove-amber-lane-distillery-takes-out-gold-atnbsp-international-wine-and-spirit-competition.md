# Amber Lane Distillery Wins Gold at International Wine and Spirit Competition

- **Gold Medal** for *Liquid Amber* (score 95/100) in the single‑malt category.
- **Silver Medals**: *Pineau 350* (93/100), *Equinox* (91/100), *Noble Lane 2* (91/100).
- First appearance at the competition; one of only three Australian distilleries to earn gold in single‑malt.
- Co‑owner Rod Berry praises the achievement and notes a consistent record of national/international medals since 2022.

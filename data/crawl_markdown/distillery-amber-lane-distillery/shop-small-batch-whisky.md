# Amber Lane Distillery – Small‑Batch Whisky

Amber Lane Distillery sells a range of small‑batch and limited‑release whiskies on its Australian website. The shop offers core range, single‑cask, and gift options with free shipping over $300. Prices include GST and insurance is included.

## Core & Limited Releases
- **Sherry Lane (2025 Limited Release)** – 49% ABV, aged in 30–60‑year Oloroso & PX sherry barrels; notes: brandy custard, cinnamon, honey, raisins, maple syrup, caramel, dark chocolate, cardamom, poached pears.
- **Sleigh Bells (2025 Limited Release)** – $199.00
- **Noble Lane (2025 Limited Release)** – $199.00; vanilla & citrus nose, lemon meringue, old‑fashioned lemonade, cinnamon scrolls, pancakes with maple syrup & vanilla ice‑cream.
- **Silk Road (2025 Limited Release)** – $199.00
- **Pineau 395 Single Barrel Finish** – finished in Pineau des Charentes Blanc cask; notes: crème brûlée, homemade lemonade, sugar‑glazed donuts.

All releases are currently available only at the Central Coast cellar door, with broader retail plans announced later.

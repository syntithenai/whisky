# Dan Murphy's Hornsby In‑Store Tasting

Amber Lane Distillery invites guests to a **$30** tasting event on *26 March 2026* at Dan Murphy’s Hornsby. The session, led by brand ambassador **Krishna Suresh**, covers the entire whisky‑making journey—from mash and distillation to ageing and barrel selection—and includes tastings of five award‑winning Amber Lane whiskies.

> *“From mash, distilling, ageing & the importance of barrel selection, taste through each component of the process, including brewing stage, unaged distilled spirit, five award‑winning Amber Lane whiskies & more.”*

The event is part of the distillery’s broader tasting calendar and is available for booking via the site.

---

**Key details**
- **Date & time:** 26 March 2026, 6:00–7:00 pm
- **Location:** Dan Murphy’s Hornsby
- **Cost:** $30 per person
- **Facilitator:** Krishna Suresh (brand ambassador)
- **Focus:** Complete whisky‑making process and tasting of five Amber Lane whiskies
- **Booking link:** Provided on the event page

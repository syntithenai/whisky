# Amber Lane Distillery Events

Amber Lane Distillery hosts a wide range of events throughout the year, including:
- **Monthly distillery open days** (every first Saturday of each month) with tours, tastings and a blending class.
- **Premium Amblers virtual tastings** hosted by cellar master Rod Berry.
- **In‑store tastings at Dan Murphy’s** in multiple locations.
- **Whisky festivals and shows** such as Melbourne Whisky Live, Brisbane Whisky Live, Sydney Whisky Live, Canberra Whisky Show, Queensland Malt Whisky Society Expo, and the World of Whisky Show.
- **Special themed events** (e.g., Christmas whisky festival, Australian Food & Wine Tastings, The Drink Show – Kiama Tour).

All events include links to purchase tickets or join virtual sessions. Prices are typically $50 for open‑day entry and vary for festivals.

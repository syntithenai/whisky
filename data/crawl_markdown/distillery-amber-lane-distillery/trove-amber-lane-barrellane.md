# Amber Lane & Barrel Lane Exclusive Release

Amber Lane Distillery has teamed up again with the Australian whisky club **Barrel Lane** to launch a limited‑edition single‑malt called **Liquid Amber (2025)**. The whisky is finished in Pedro Ximenez and Oloroso sherry casks, then aged in French oak Apera barrels from McWilliam’s Wines for extra depth. Matured 5–6 years and reduced to 48 % ABV, it offers a creamy, elegant profile with honey, spice, dried fruit, raisin, caramel, dark chocolate, cardamom and poached pear notes, finishing warm with honey, butterscotch and oak spice.

It has earned top honours: Australia’s equal best single‑malt at the 2024 International Wine & Spirits Competition, gold at the 2025 San Francisco World Spirits Competition, and a 94‑point score in Halliday’s Spirits Guide. The release is available exclusively to Barrel Lane members during August.

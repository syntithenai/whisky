# Amber Lane Private Tasting FAQs
- **Group size**: 8–60 people (ideal 20 for corporate events). 
- **Equipment**: All glassware, mats, pipettes, syringes, water jugs and flavour wheels supplied by Amber Lane. 
- **Presenter**: Rod Berry, owner, distiller, and former law partner. 
- **Corporate suitability**: Yes; used for networking and staff morale. 
- **Locations**: Sydney Metro, NSW Central Coast, Wollongong (same pricing); other areas negotiated individually. 
- **Custom events**: Possible with tailored presentations or joint events. 
- **Private clubs**: Accepted. 
- **Under‑18s**: Allowed to attend but cannot drink. 
- **Contact**: Rod Berry – 0407 543 212 / rod@amberlanedistillery.com. 
- **Licensing**: Fully licensed under NSW Catering License LIQO660036805; staff hold RSA qualifications.

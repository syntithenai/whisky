# Amber Lane Distillery Barrel Program Pricing

Amber Lane Distillery offers a barrel‑ownship program where customers can purchase a 30 L, 50 L or 100 L cask filled with either new‑make whisky or 3‑year‑old ex‑bourbon cask spirit. Prices (incl. GST) are:
- **30 L**: $4,290 (new‑make) / $5,390 (ex‑bourbon)
- **50 L**: $6,875 (new‑make) / $8,690 (ex‑bourbon)
- **100 L**: $12,650 (new‑make) / $16,390 (ex‑bourbon)

Included services: six‑monthly samples, cellar‑master guidance, insurance, framed ownership certificate, bottling assistance and optional label design. Barrel cost ($500–$800) is separate.

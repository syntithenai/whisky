# Amber Lane Distillery Barrel Program Offering

- **Experience**: Fill your own barrel, visit & sample during maturation.
- **Samples**: Six monthly samples delivered with progress updates.
- **Bottling**: Assistance and ownership shares in finished bottles.
- **Labeling**: Custom or cost‑based label design.
- **Certificate**: Framed ownership certificate for display.
- **Joint Ownership**: Share a barrel with friends; proportional samples and bottle shares.
- **Decision Rights**: Majority owners decide on bottling; minority owners receive bottles proportionally. In case of tie, parties negotiate or Amber Lane may buy the barrel.

All activities comply with NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547 and respect the Darkinjung people as traditional landowners.

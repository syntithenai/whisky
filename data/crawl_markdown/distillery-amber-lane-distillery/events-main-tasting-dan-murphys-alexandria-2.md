# Tasting Event at Dan Murphy's Alexandria

Amber Lane Distillery is hosting a tasting event on **Friday, 23 January 2026** from **3:00 pm to 7:00 pm** at Dan Murphy’s in Alexandria. Attendees can sample the distillery’s whiskies with an Amber Lane ambassador.

> *“Pop into Dan Murphy's to sample some of our delicious whiskies with an Amber Lane ambassador.”*

The event is listed on the distillery’s calendar and can be added via Google Calendar or iCal.

# Amber Lane Co‑Founders Named Central Coast Community Champions

Amber Lane co‑founders **Rod Berry** and **Phil Townsend** were awarded Central Coast Community Champion medals by Mayor Lawrie McKinna at the distillery’s Premium Amblers party on 31 August. The award recognizes their significant business achievements, contribution to the local food & wine scene, and efforts that have put the Central Coast on the map as a boutique destination.

The ceremony highlighted their dedication to craft, innovation, and excellence, noting how they attract visitors while adding value for locals through events and club membership.

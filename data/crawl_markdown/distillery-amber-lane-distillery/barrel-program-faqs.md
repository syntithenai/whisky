# Amber Lane Distillery Barrel Program FAQs

- **Price** covers excise, warehousing, insurance, bi‑annual samples, bottling supplies and statutory label; GST and barrel purchase cost are extra.
- **Barrels**: 200 L ex‑Heaven Hill Bourbon casks (3‑year‑old), guaranteeing at least 5‑years‑aged whisky even in 30 L barrels.
- **Insurance**: All barrels, including customer‑owned ones, are insured by Amber Lane Distillery.
- **Tasting & involvement**: Owners may visit anytime and receive samples every six months; they choose the bourbon cask and finishing cask, and can participate in filling and bottling.
- **Storage**: Central Coast warehouse with expert cellar master oversight.
- **Post‑maturation options**: Buy the barrel (≈ $500–$800) or share ownership with a group.

> *All products comply with NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547 and respect the Darkinjung people.*

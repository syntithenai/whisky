# Distillery Open Day – Amber Lane Distillery

- **Date & Time**: Saturday 19 September 2026, 12:30 pm–4:00 pm
- **Location**: 438 Yarramalong Road, Wyong Creek, NSW, Australia
- **Highlights**:
  - Meet the distillers and tour the whisky‑making process.
  - Sample products and enjoy a free tipple.
  - Blending class (3–4 pm) – create your own blend to take home.
- **Ticket price**: $50 each, available for purchase online.

> *Alcohol cannot be sold or supplied to anyone under 18.*

---

*The event is part of Amber Lane Distillery’s monthly open days and follows NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547. The land is acknowledged as belonging to the Darkinjung people.*

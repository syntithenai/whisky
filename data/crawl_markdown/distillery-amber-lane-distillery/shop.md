# Amber Lane Distillery - Store Overview

## Key Facts
- Located on the Central Coast of New South Wales, Australia
- Specializes in small-batch and single cask whiskies
- Offers a range of exclusive releases through their Cellar Door program
- Limited edition bottlings from renowned cooperators like Heaven Hill

## Production Signals
- Utilizes 30-60 year-old Oloroso & PX sherry barrels for Sherry Lane
- Vatted four years maturation bourbon casks #113, #114, #134, #135 followed by a nine month Pineau des Charentes Blanc finish for Pineau 395
- Focus on integrating influences from various cask types (ex-Bourbon, virgin French Oak, Botrytis)
- Emphasis on sophisticated maturation approaches reflected in Noble Lane releases

## Commercial Signals
- Available exclusively at the Central Coast Cellar Door initially
- Insurance included for all whisky purchases within Australia
- Free shipping over $300 orders nationwide
- Prices include GST

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited release nature of many offerings may lead to scarcity and higher resale value
- Specific details about cask origins, maturation periods, and exact ABV percentages are often undisclosed until official releases
- Potential for premium pricing due to exclusive cooperations and limited availability

# Amber Lane Wins Gold at 2025 IWSC
Amber Lane Distillery’s **Equinox** earned a gold medal (95/100) at the 2025 International Wine & Spirits Competition, joining its other core whiskies—Liquid Amber (93/100) and Silk Road (90/100)—which also received top marks. The awards cement Amber Lane as one of Australia’s most awarded single‑malt producers.

- **Equinox**: Gold, 95/100, RRP $139
- **Liquid Amber**: Gold, 93/100
- **Silk Road**: Silver, 90/100

The accolades are highlighted in Halliday’s Spirits Guide and reflect consistent international recognition.

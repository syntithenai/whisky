# Amber Lane Distillery – Media Highlights

Amber Lane Distillery, based in Wyong Creek on the Central Coast of NSW, has been featured extensively across Australian and international media for its award‑winning whiskies and innovative cask finishes. Key stories include:
- **Chartreuse‑cask whisky**: *Rue de La Chartreuse* – first ever whisky finished in an ex‑Chartreuse barrel.
- **New releases**: *Silk Lane* (follow‑up to Silk Road) and *Noble Lane*.
- **Awards**: Multiple gold medals at the 2025 San Francisco World Spirits Competition, Champion Single Malt at the 2024 Sydney Royal Distilled Spirits Show, and top honours in the 2024 Royal Australian Spirits Awards.
- **Media coverage**: Features in *The Kiama Bugle*, *Wine Companion*, *Food & Drinks Business*, *Coast Community News*, *Drinks Digest*, *The Shout*, *Distiller Magazine*, *The Whisky List*, and others.

These pieces highlight Amber Lane’s reputation as a leading Australian craft distillery, its commitment to experimental finishes, and its growing international recognition.

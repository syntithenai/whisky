# Amber Lane Distillery Featured in Halliday Wine Companion’s New Spirits Guide

- **Halliday Wine Companion** now rates Australian spirits, including whisky, on a 100‑point scale.
- **Amber Lane** is one of the few Australian distilleries highlighted, praised for its careful distillation, cask selection and blending.
- The distillery uses **Spanish Pedro Ximénez sherry casks** and locally produced **Apera casks**, giving its whiskies a fortified‑wine character.
- Five drams are reviewed with scores ranging from 88 to 95:
  - *Noble Lane* (95/100) – sweet white‑wine cask influence, frangipane & marzipan nose.
  - *Liquid Amber* (94/100) – Pedro Ximénez and Apera fortified wines with a splash of bourbon.
  - *Apera 100* (93/100) – high‑ABV single‑cask whisky with fruit‑and‑flower notes.
  - *Water* (90/100) – single fortified‑wine cask, strong oak and tannin.
  - *Equinox* (88/100) – blended Spanish, Apera and bourbon barrels.
- The article highlights the distillery’s future potential and includes a link to Amber Lane’s Halliday profile.

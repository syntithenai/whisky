# Queensland Malt Whisky Society Expo – Amber Lane Distillery

Amber Lane Distillery will attend the **Queensland Malt Whisky Society Expo** on Saturday, 23 May 2026 at Rydges Southbank. The event runs from 4:00 pm to 5:00 pm and offers visitors a chance to meet distillers, sample award‑winning drams, and purchase favourites.

- **Date & Time:** 23 May 2026, 4:00–5:00 pm
- **Venue:** Rydges Southbank (Queensland)
- **Highlights:** Meet the distillers, taste award‑winning whisky, take home selections.
- **Tickets:** Available via the event link on Amber Lane’s website.
- **Legal Notice:** Alcohol cannot be sold or supplied to anyone under 18. Products are sold in accordance with NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547.

The distillery acknowledges the Darkinjung people, traditional owners of the land where it operates.

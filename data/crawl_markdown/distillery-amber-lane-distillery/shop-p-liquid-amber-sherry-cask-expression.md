# Liquid Amber – Amber Lane Distillery

- **Price**: $169.00 (700 mL, 48% ABV)
- **Cask blend**: Apera & PX sherry casks + a touch of bourbon cask.
- **Awards**: Gold at the 2025 Sydney Royal Distilled Spirits Awards, 2025 San Francisco World Spirits Competition and 2024 International Wine & Spirit Competition.
- **Tasting notes**: Rich chocolate, spice, honey, toffee, maple syrup, orange zest, dried apricots, figs with a long sweet cinnamon finish.
- **Production**: Non‑chill filtered; sourced from ex‑McWilliams apera casks, 30+ year old PX barrels from Rey Fernando de Castilla, and Heaven Hill bourbon casks.

This is Amber Lane’s first sherry‑cask expression and has been a crowd favourite at recent Australian whisky shows.

# Amber Lane Distillery Tastings

- **Open Days**: Monthly tours and tastings featuring a 3600 L copper pot still, barrels, and four award‑winning whiskies.
- **Private Tastings**: On‑site or at your location (travel fee $200). Options include an introductory tasting or a whisky‑blending masterclass.
- **Pricing**:
  - Introductory tasting: $45 + GST per head, minimum $400 + GST. 10% off bottles.
  - Blending masterclass: $85 + GST per head, minimum $600 + GST. 10% off bottles.
- **Blending**: Participants can create a 50 ml blend to take home; optional 700 ml bottle for purchase.
- **Testimonials**: Positive reviews from Peter Jenkins and David Rigby highlight the learning experience and complexity of blending.
- **FAQs & Legal**: Alcohol sales restricted to 18+, compliance with NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547, acknowledgment of Darkinjung people.

For more details visit the [Amber Lane Distillery website](https://amberlanedistillery.com/).

# Amber Lane Distillery
Amber Lane Distillery has been named in the **2025/2026 edition of Jim Murray’s Whisky Bible** – a rare honour for an Australian distillery. Four of its expressions earned gold medals (scores >90/100) and three achieved *liquid gold* status (scores >94/100). The standout releases are:
- **Apera 100** – 95.5/100
- **Silk Road** – 95/100
- **Equinox** – 94/100
Other entries recognised include Apera 087, Liquid Amber and Pineau 350.
Co‑owner Rod Berry praised the achievement as a career pinnacle, noting the distillery’s rapid rise since its founding nine years ago.

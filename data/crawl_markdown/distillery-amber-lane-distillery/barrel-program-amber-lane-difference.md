# The Amber Lane Difference

Amber Lane Distillery’s barrel program lets customers actively shape their whisky from the start: choose the bourbon cask, decide on the finishing cask, and work with distillers throughout maturation. Key differentiators include:
- **Large‑format barrels (200–250 L)** for slower, refined ageing.
- **Extended maturation cycles** beyond the Australian average.
- **European Cognac‑style techniques** adapted to Australia’s climate.
- A **large 3600‑L copper still** that maximises copper contact.

The program has received numerous awards and offers a community experience with regular tastings, masterclasses from cellar master Rod Berry, and opportunities to design bottle labels. Testimonials highlight the hands‑on involvement and the quality of the finished spirit.

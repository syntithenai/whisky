# Amber Lane Distillery – About

Amber Lane Distillery is a boutique Australian whisky producer located in the Yarramalong Valley. Founded by amateur astronomers Rod Berry and Phil Townsend, it has earned multiple national and international gold medals. The distillery uses a 3,600‑liter copper still called *The Mary Rose*, double‑distills with a pot designed for maximum copper contact, and ages whisky in premium Spanish Pedro Ximénez, Oloroso sherry, and Kentucky bourbon barrels. Amber Lane emphasizes non‑chill filtration, higher alcohol strength releases, and skilled barrel blending to create complex flavour profiles.

Key people:
- **Rod Berry** – co‑founder, whisky connoisseur with over 1,000 tastings.
- **Phil Townsend** – co‑founder, oversees daily operations and innovation.

The distillery is committed to connection, generosity, and responsible consumption, acknowledging the Darkinjung people as traditional owners of the land.

# Amber Lane Distillery – About

Amber Lane Distillery is a boutique Australian whisky producer located in the Yarramalong Valley. The distillery was founded by amateur astronomers Rod Berry and Phil Townsend, who discovered their passion for whisky during a visit to a small distillery in 2017. Their mission is to create refined, beautiful whiskies that celebrate connection, humility, and adventure.

**Production philosophy**
- Uses a 3,600‑liter copper still called *The Mary Rose* with steam jacket and copper barrel insulation.
- Double‑distillation in a pot designed for maximum copper contact.
- Mash bill emphasizes original barley and creamy sweetness.
- Non‑chill‑filtered to preserve esters and congeners.
- Aged in premium Spanish Pedro Ximénez, Oloroso sherry, and Kentucky bourbon barrels; larger barrels are used than many Australian producers.
- Blends individual barrels for consistent flavor.
- Releases whisky at higher alcohol strength.

**People**
- **Rod Berry** – Whisky enthusiast, member of the Scotch Malt Whisky Society, has tasted over 1,000 whiskies.
- **Phil Townsend** – Oversees daily operations and focuses on innovation and quality.

The distillery acknowledges the Darkinjung people as traditional owners of the land. Products are sold under NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547.

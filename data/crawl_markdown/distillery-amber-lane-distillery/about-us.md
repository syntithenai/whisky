# About Amber Lane Distillery

## Key Facts
- Located in the Yarramalong Valley, Australia
- Multiple national and international gold medals awarded to their whiskies
- Uses a 3600-litre copper still called "The Mary Rose"
- Double-distillation process with emphasis on copper contact for complex flavors
- Non-chill filtration to preserve flavor-producing esters and congeners
- Releases whisky at higher alcohol strength

## Production Signals
- Consistent high-quality mash bill creating pleasing new make
- Emphasis on original barley and creamy sweetness
- Skilled blending of individual barrels for consistently delicious whisky
- Premium aged casks from Spain (Pedro Ximenez, Oloroso Sherry) and Kentucky Bourbon
- Larger barrels than many other Australian producers for maximized aging

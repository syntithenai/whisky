# Amber Lane Distillery – Work With Us

Amber Lane Distillery is hiring brand representatives to conduct in‑store tastings across NSW & ACT. Candidates should love the whisky, hold a current RSA licence and be available on Friday evenings or Saturday afternoons for locations in Southern Sydney, Newcastle and the Northern Beaches.

Apply by emailing Rod at **rod@amberlanedistillery.com**.

> *Alcohol cannot be sold or supplied to anyone under 18 – it’s against the law.*

The distillery operates under NSW Liquor Licence **LIQW88001547** and acknowledges the Darkinjung people as traditional owners of the land.

# Christmas Whisky Festival – Amber Lane Distillery

- **Date & Time**: Saturday, 13 Dec 2025, 12:30 pm–4:00 pm.
- **Event**: Special distillery open day celebrating the launch of the 2025 *Sleigh Bells* expression.
- **Highlights**:
  - Sample and purchase Amber Lane whiskies, including rare barrels and a 60‑year‑old Oloroso cask.
  - Try whisky‑infused chocolates (exclusive to the distillery).
  - Enjoy discounted bottles, Christmas treats, themed nibbles, and gifts such as glasses, tasting kits, and vouchers.
- **Extras**: Meet miniature Highland cows and explore a festive atmosphere.

All activities comply with NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547. The event acknowledges the Darkinjung people, traditional owners of the land.

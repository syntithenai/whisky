# Distillery Training Day – Amber Lane Distillery

- **Date & Time**: Saturday 25 Oct 2025, 2:30‑3:30 pm (full day 10 am‑4 pm)
- **What’s included**: Behind‑the‑scenes tour of the distillery, hands‑on whisky making, ABV calculation with hydrometer, barrel filling and ageing analysis, full restaurant lunch, workbook & certificate.
- **Price**: $495 (includes lunch). 
- **Book**: [Tickets](https://www.amberlanedistillery.com/shop/p/ticket-distillery-training-day).

> *“For the first time ever, a select group will go behind‑the‑scenes to learn about the working operations of the distillery.”* – Amber Lane Distillery.

---

## Key facts
- **Hosts**: Rod & Phil (Amber Lane staff). 
- **Activities**: Collect new‑make straight from still, analyse aromas/flavours/ABV, calculate ABV with hydrometer, fill barrels, ageing whisky cask analysis. 
- **Target audience**: Whisky lovers and enthusiasts.
- **Legal note**: Alcohol cannot be sold or supplied to anyone under 18; compliance with NSW Liquor Licence LIQW88001547.
- **Cultural acknowledgement**: Darkinjung people – traditional owners of the land.

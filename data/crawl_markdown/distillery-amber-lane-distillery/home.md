# Amber Lane Distillery

- **Location & History** – Australian distillery on the NSW Central Coast, founded in 2017.
- **Philosophy** – Emphasises large premium barrels, extended maturation and European distilling principles to create refined, complex whiskies.
- **Products** – Includes a 2026 limited‑release *Sherry Lane*, *Equinox*, *Mystery Box* (A$149), gift tokens, and a range of single‑malt and blended expressions.
- **Awards** – Over 30 national & international medals, including World of Whiskies Best Small Batch Single Malt (Silk Road) and Royal Australian Spirits Champion Whisky of Show (Equinox).
- **Events** – Monthly open days, tasting classes, private group tastings, and a first‑ever Barrel Day Gathering for barrel owners.
- **Retail & Shipping** – Online shop with free shipping on orders over A$300; offers mystery boxes and limited releases.
- **Community & Responsibility** – Recognises Darkinjung people, promotes responsible consumption, and partners with local stockists.

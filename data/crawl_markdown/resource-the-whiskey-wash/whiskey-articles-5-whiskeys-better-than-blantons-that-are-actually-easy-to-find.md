# 5 Best Blanton’s Bourbon Alternatives That Cost Less

Mark Littler, owner of *The Whiskey Wash*, curates five bourbon‑style bottles that offer comparable or superior flavor to Blanton’s at lower prices. The list includes:
- **Evan Williams Single Barrel** – $30, single‑barrel style with caramel, vanilla and light oak.
- **Very Old Barton** – $11, 50 % ABV, rich caramel/vanilla with a robust oak finish.
- **Four Roses Single Barrel** – $43, 50 % ABV, dark fruit, baking spice and floral notes.
- **Russell’s Reserve 10‑Year‑Old** – $48, aged 10 years, toffee‑vanilla‑oak profile.
- **Jack Daniel’s Single Barrel Barrel Proof** – $50, Tennessee whiskey at ~64 % ABV with intense caramel, toasted oak and banana.

The article explains each bottle’s flavor profile, price point, availability and how it compares to Blanton’s in terms of value and character.

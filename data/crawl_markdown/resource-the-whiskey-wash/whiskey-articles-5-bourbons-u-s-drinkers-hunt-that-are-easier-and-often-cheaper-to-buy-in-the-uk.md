# 5 Rare Bourbons Easier to Find in UK Shops Than the US

James Evans explains why certain U.S.‑centric bourbon releases—such as **Blanton’s Gold**, **Blanton’s Straight From The Barrel**, **Maker’s Mark Cellar Aged**, **E.H. Taylor (Small Batch)**, and **Bomberger’s Declaration 2025**—are more readily available and often cheaper in UK retail outlets than in the United States. He outlines each spirit’s key characteristics, tasting notes, and practical buying tips, including landed‑cost calculations that factor VAT, shipping, tariffs, and duties.

The article also offers a quick‑start strategy for U.S. drinkers: start with Blanton’s Gold or Straight From The Barrel, add Maker’s Mark Cellar Aged for depth, then finish with E.H. Taylor or Bomberger’s when they drop.

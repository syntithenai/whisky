# 7 Types of Bourbon Drinkers You'll Always Meet

Phil Dwyer’s light‑hearted guide categorises bourbon enthusiasts into seven archetypes—from the *Allocated Hunter* who knows every shop to the *One Who Only Drinks Pappy*. Each type is described with a brief personality sketch and tips for interacting with them. The article also offers practical advice for newcomers, such as attending tastings and watching trusted reviewers.

The piece ends with a call‑to‑action: identify which persona you or someone else fits and enjoy the social side of bourbon culture.

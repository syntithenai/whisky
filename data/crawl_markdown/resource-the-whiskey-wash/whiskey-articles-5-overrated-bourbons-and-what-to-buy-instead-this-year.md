# 5 Overrated Bourbons and 5 Better Alternatives for Less

Phil Dwyer, a whisky retailer‑turned‑writer, lists five bourbons that he considers overpriced or overhyped and offers cheaper, higher‑value substitutes. The article highlights how awards, celebrity endorsements and scarcity can inflate prices without improving quality.

- **Overrated**: Pappy Van Winkle (any expression), Eagle Rare 12 yr, Henry McKenna 10 yr SB‑IB, Elmer T. Lee, Blanton’s Single Barrel.
- **Alternatives**: Green River Wheated Bourbon, Knob Creek 12 yr, Eagle Rare 10 yr, Four Roses Single Barrel, Russell’s Reserve 10 yr.

The piece stresses tasting before buying and warns against chasing hype for the sake of prestige.

- What is important to remember is that they do taste good.

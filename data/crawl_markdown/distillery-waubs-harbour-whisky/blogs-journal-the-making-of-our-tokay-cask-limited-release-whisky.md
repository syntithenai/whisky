# Tokay Cask Limited Release Whisky

- **Release size**: 661 bottles.
- **Maturation**: First aged in Australian Apera casks, finished in old French Oak Tokay casks.
- **Production team**: Head Distiller Rob and cooper Laurie handled shavings, toasting and charring of the Tokay cask.
- **Flavor profile**: Poached pear, short‑crust pie with fig, baked walnut and salted caramel.
- **Release date**: 12 Nov 2025 at 7 pm AEDT.
- **Shipping**: Australia‑wide only; standard post $15 (4–8 days), express post $20 (2–4 days).

# Meet the Maker
Rob, Head Distiller & Co‑founder of Waubs Harbour Whisky, began his career distilling for Tasmania’s Overeem Whisky and Lark Distillery before co‑foundeding his own distillery.

- **Background**: Master in Antarctic Marine Science (UTAS) and hands‑on experience crafting local delicacies such as brie cheese, honey, smoked salmon, and wood‑fire pizzas.
- **Career Path**: From Overeem & Lark to founding Waubs Harbour Whisky.
- **Location**: Tasmania, Australia.

> *“There’s not many distillers out there who boast a career that began distilling for two of Tasmania's most renowned distilleries, Overeem Whisky and Lark Distillery, and then go on to co‑found the distillery they’d once only dreamt of.”*

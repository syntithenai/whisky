**Waubs Harbour Whisky – Sustainability**

- Uses ocean‑water cooling via titanium heat exchangers to reduce energy use.
- Employs extensive heat recovery: louver windows, still‑heat circulation and waste‑water heat exchange.
- Spent grain is fed to local pig farms.
- Supports the Great Southern Reef Foundation with a $4,800 donation from bottle #01 auction.
- Shipping: standard post (4–8 days, $15) or express post (2–4 days, $20).

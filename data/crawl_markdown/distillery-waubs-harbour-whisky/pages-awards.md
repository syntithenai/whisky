# Waubs Harbour Whisky Awards

- **World Whiskies Awards 2024** – Category Winner Gold (Small Batch Single Malt Whisky, 12 yrs & Under) and Gold Medal for Small Batch Single Malt Whisky (No age statement).
- **Global Spirits Masters 2023** – Master’s Medal; Port Storm: Masters medal, Waubs Original: Gold medal, Founder's Reserve: Gold medal.
- **Tasting Australia Spirits Awards 2023** – Gold medals for Port Storm and Founder's Reserve; Silver for Waubs Original.
- **Royal Australian Spirits Awards 2023** – Gold for Port Storm, Silver for Waubs Original, Bronze for Founder's Reserve.

All awards highlight the distillery’s reputation as a leading Australian whisky brand.

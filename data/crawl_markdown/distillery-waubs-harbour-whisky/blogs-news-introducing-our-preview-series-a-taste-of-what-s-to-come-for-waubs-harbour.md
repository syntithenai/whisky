# Waubs Harbour Preview Series

Waubs Harbour Distillery, still under construction since 2018, released a **Bond 3 Preview Series** in early 2022 to give customers a taste of its future single‑malt whisky. The series includes two small‑cask releases:

- **Double Tawny** – aged first in 20 L ex‑tawny port casks and finished in a 100 L ex‑tawny port cask, described as *old gold chocolate* on the nose with an oily mouth coating.
- **Apera Cask Strength** – started in 20 L ex‑apera sherry casks, finished in a 100 L ex‑apera sherry cask, bottled at 62.6 % ABV and noted as *fruit custard flan* on the palate with a buttery finish.

Both were distilled by Head Distiller **Rob Polmear** at another Tasmanian distillery before maturing in Bond Room 3 at Waubs Harbour. The preview series showcases the maritime influence of the distillery’s ocean‑edge location and hints at the longer‑matured releases expected early 2023.

Shipping is Australia‑wide with standard (4–8 days, $15) or express (2–4 days, $20) options. The site also offers a priority access list for new releases and events.

# Waubs Harbour Whisky – Origin of the Name

Waubs Harbour is a distillery located near Bicheno, Tasmania, named after **Wauba Debar**, a Tasmanian Aboriginal woman who became a sealer’s slave in the early 1800s. She rescued two men from a sinking boat and earned the nickname *heroine of the sea*. The town originally called Waubs Harbour was renamed Bicheno, but her legacy lives on through local street names, the beach, the bay, and the distillery itself. A gravestone for Wauba still exists a few hundred metres from the distillery.

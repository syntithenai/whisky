# Team Clean: A Mission to Clean Up Tassie’s Beaches

Waubs Harbour Whisky highlights the **Team Clean** initiative, a 25‑year‑old volunteer effort that removes plastic and marine debris from Tasmania’s remote southwest coast. In 2024 the group collected over **55 000 pieces of plastic**, including **898 nurdles** and more than **12 000 plastic items** in total. Volunteers work by fishing boat, with skilled skippers guiding them to inaccessible shorelines.

The distillery encourages support through:
- Following Team Clean on social media
- Sharing their mission
- Donating for transport, food, and collection tools

Shipping is available Australia‑wide via standard or express post. The page also includes legal age verification, a pregnancy warning, and a comprehensive list of countries for shipping eligibility.

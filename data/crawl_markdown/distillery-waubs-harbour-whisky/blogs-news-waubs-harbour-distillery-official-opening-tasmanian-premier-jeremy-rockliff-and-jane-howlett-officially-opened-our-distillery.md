# Waubs Harbour Distillery Official Opening

- Tasmanian Premier **Jeremy Rockliff MP** and **Jane Howlett MLC** officially opened the distillery on Friday, 4 April 2023.
- The opening coincided with the launch of the distillery’s first whisky releases.
- The event was a milestone after five years of development on the east coast of Tasmania.
- Waubs Harbour offers Australian‑wide shipping (Standard Post $15, Express Post $20) and encourages visitors to join their priority‑access email list for new releases and events.

> *“A momentous way to kick off an exciting launch week!”* – Waubs Harbour team.

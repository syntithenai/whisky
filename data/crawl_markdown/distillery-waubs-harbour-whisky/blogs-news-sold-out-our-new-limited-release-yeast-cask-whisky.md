# (SOLD OUT) Our new Limited Release Yeast Cask whisky!

Waubs Harbour Distillery announced a **limited‑release single malt** aged in ex‑Tawny Port French oak casks, finished with a *chocolate orange* flavour profile. Only **647 bottles** were produced at **51.8 % ABV**, and the release is now sold out.

- **Release date:** 12 March 2025, 7 pm AEDT (now sold out)
- **Production notes:** Fresh yeast cultivated in‑house; non‑matured spirit finished in ex‑Port casks
- **Price & shipping:** $15 flat rate for standard post (4–8 days) or $20 for express (2–4 days), Australia only
- **Availability:** Join the priority email list for future releases

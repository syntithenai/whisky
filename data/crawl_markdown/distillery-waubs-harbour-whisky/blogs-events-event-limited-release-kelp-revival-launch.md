# Limited Release ‘Kelp Revival’ Launch

- **Date & Time**: Saturday 31 Aug, 2‑5 pm at Waubs Harbour Distillery (42 Waubs Esplanade, Bicheno, Tasmania)
- **Price**: $75 per head (18+ only). Includes champagne or beer on arrival, whisky tasting and canapés.
- **Featured Whisky**: *Kelp Revival* – a sweet, juicy maritime single‑malt matured in Apera Sherry casks, finished in a 70‑year‑old Tawny Port cask, with a touch of coastal peated whisky.
- **Special Guests**: Head Distiller Rob Polmear (tasting), chef Andrew Gerstkamp (canapés), Dr Scott Bennett (kelp regeneration talk).
- **Tickets**: Book via Rezdy link. Shipping available Australia‑wide; standard post $15, express $20.

> *“An afternoon of whisky, canapés & kelp to celebrate the launch of our new ‘Kelp Revival’ Limited Release whisky.”*

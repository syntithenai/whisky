# Introducing Maritime Sherry Whisky
- **Release**: Limited edition, available from Wednesday 11 Feb.
- **Cask blend**: French Oak ex‑Apera sherry + American Oak ex‑bourbon.
- **Maturation**: 100 L casks on the ocean edge for >6 years.
- **Tasting notes**: buttered fruit, golden syrup, mineral maritime character; light yet complex and sweet but balanced.
- **Shipping**: Australia‑wide only – standard post $15 (4–8 days), express $20 (2–4 days).

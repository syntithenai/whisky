# Waubs Harbour Maritime Tasmanian Whisky

- **Location**: Bicheno, Tasmania – a former oyster hatchery turned distillery.
- **Production**: Small‑batch single malt matured on the edge of the ocean with salt‑laden bond rooms and an ocean‑powered cooling system.
- **Products**:
  - *Waubs Original* (Batch 12) – 717 bottles, $170.00 AUD
  - *Port Storm* (Batch 12) – 633 bottles, $190.00 AUD
  - *Founder's Reserve* (Batch 11) – 649 bottles, $220.00 AUD
- **Awards**: Category Winner at the World Whiskies Awards 2025 for Small Batch Single Malt, 12 Yrs & Under.
- **Tours**: Guided tastings available Monday‑Saturday; bookings required.
- **Shipping**: Australia only – standard post $15, express $20.

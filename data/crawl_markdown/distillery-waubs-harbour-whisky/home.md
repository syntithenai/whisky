# Waubs Harbour Maritime Tasmanian Whisky

- **Location**: Bicheno, Tasmania, Australia
- **Production**: Small batches matured on the edge of the ocean
- **Distillery History**: Family distillery founded by Rob Polmear, Tim Polmear, and Bec Polmear
- **Vision**: Create a truly maritime Tasmanian whisky that embraces wild coastal provenance
- **Innovation**: Cooling system run entirely by ocean power
- **Awards**: Awarded Category Winner at World Whiskies Awards for Small Batch Single Malt 2025

## Key Facts
- **Maturation**: Matured meters from the ocean, influenced by wind, humidity, temperature, and air pressure variations
- **Bottles**: Flagship Series Waubs Original Batch (716 bottles), Port Storm Batch (656 bottles), Founder's Reserve Batch (649 bottles)
- **Flavor Profile**: Rich, sweet, complex Aussie whisky inspired by Scottish maritime whiskies but distinctly Tasmanian

## Production Signals
- Distilled in small batches using traditional copper pot stills
- Matured in ocean-facing warehouses exposed to natural elements
- Limited availability due to artisanal production methods

## Commercial Signals
- Sold out flagship series bottles indicating high demand
- Premium pricing reflecting limited supply and unique provenance
- International interest from customers worldwide

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited distribution outside Australia may restrict market reach
- Dependence on oceanic environmental factors for maturation
- Potential challenges in scaling production while maintaining quality

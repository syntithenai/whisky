# Oceanside Feast & Whisky Experience at Waubs Harbour
- First event of Tasmanian Whisky Week 2022.
- Held at the Bicheno distillery, featuring local seafood cooked live by chefs Andrew Gerstkamp and Duncan Winter.
- Guests enjoyed in‑depth tastings of sold‑out releases and a personal drop from head distiller Rob Polmear.
- Aunty Patsy Cameron (local elder) performed a Welcome to Country, sharing the origin story of Waubs Harbour.
- Attendees previewed new tasting room and bond store renovations, which previously housed an abalone farm with live salt‑water tanks.

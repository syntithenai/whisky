# Now Available in New Zealand!

Waubs Harbour Whisky announces that its **Waubs Original, Port Storm and Founder’s Reserve** are now available for purchase in New Zealand through the retailer *eight_pm_nz*, with nationwide shipping. The announcement includes shipping details for Australia (standard and express) and a note that more NZ retailers will be added soon.

> “Exciting news – you can savour a bottle of our maritime whisky across the ocean in New Zealand!”

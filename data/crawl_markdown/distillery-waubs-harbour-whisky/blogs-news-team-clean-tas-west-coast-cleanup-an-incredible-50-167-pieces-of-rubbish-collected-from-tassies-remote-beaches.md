# Team Clean Tas' West Coast Cleanup

- **Event**: Annual beach cleanup by Team Clean Tas on Tasmania’s remote southwest coast.
- **Impact**: Collected **50,167 pieces of rubbish** in 10 days, bringing the cumulative total to **761,222 pieces** removed from South‑West Marine Debris Cleanups.
- **Activities**: Hours of collecting, sorting and counting; surf sessions; cooking fresh fish and crays; dolphin sightings; scenic views; live‑aboard band.
- **Logistics**: Rubbish is being recycled or repurposed. The blog post links to a live‑aboard diary at *wha‑marinedebris.blogspot.com*.
- **Other info**: Shipping details, age verification for alcohol purchase, and a long list of countries for shipping eligibility are included on the site.

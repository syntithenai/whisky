# The Journey to Our Oldest Whisky Yet
- Waubs Harbour is releasing its oldest single‑malt, now over six years old.
- The distillery was founded by a family team with deep maritime ties in Tasmania.
- Production takes place at an old oyster hatchery on the east coast of Bicheno, where more than 1,000 casks mature beside the ocean.
- The article reflects on why this whisky feels “meant to make” and hints at future releases.

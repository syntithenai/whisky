# Short Film Screening at Waubs Harbour

Waubs Harbour Distillery hosted a free film screening event featuring **"Reviving Giants"** by Great Southern Reef (GSRF) and **"Southern Blast"** by Surfrider. The films, each about ten minutes long, explore environmental themes: the decline of giant kelp forests and the threat of seismic blasting in Tasmania’s southern seas. A panel Q&A followed the screenings.

The event was presented by GSRF, Surfrider, Sea Forest, and Environment Tasmania, with hosts Mick Barren and Dr. Karen Gowlett‑Holmes from Eaglehawk Dive Centre. Attendance required RSVP; the screening was free but limited to those who registered.

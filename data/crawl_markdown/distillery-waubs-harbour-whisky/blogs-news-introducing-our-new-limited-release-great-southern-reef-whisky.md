# Introducing Our New Limited Release: "Great Southern Reef" Whisky

Waubs Harbour Distillery has released a **591‑bottle** limited edition single malt, finished in **40‑year‑old Tawny Port casks** and aged in ex‑Apera Sherry casks. The whisky is described as having a *salted caramel backbone* with stewed fruit notes from the sherry casks, and a complex finish from the fortified port cask. All proceeds support the Great Southern Reef Foundation.

- **Availability:** Australia only (standard post 4‑8 days, express 2‑4 days). 
- **Price & purchase link:** See the shop page: https://waubsharbourwhisky.com/shop/limited-release-great-sthn-reef/
- **Charity tie‑in:** $4,800 donated from a previous auction.

> *“We urge you to read more on their incredible work and find out how you can support them at https://greatsouthernreef.com.”*

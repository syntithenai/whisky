# Waubs Original Wins Category at World Whiskies Awards 2025

- **Award**: Category Winner for *Australia’s Best Small Batch Single Malt, 12 yrs & under*.
- **Distillery**: Waubs Harbour Whisky (Tasmania).
- **Co‑Founder Quote**: “This is the Olympics of awards in whisky so to win a Category Award is huge for our little Tasmanian distillery.”
- **Additional Honour**: Founder’s Reserve received a Silver in *Australian Single Malt, 12 yrs & Under*.
- **Shipping**: Australia‑wide standard (4–8 days, $15) or express (2–4 days, $20).

> The win is seen as confirmation that the distillery’s whiskies are “truly world class.”

## Key Facts
- Awarded at the 2025 World Whiskies Awards.
- Category: *Australia’s Best Small Batch Single Malt, 12 yrs & under*.

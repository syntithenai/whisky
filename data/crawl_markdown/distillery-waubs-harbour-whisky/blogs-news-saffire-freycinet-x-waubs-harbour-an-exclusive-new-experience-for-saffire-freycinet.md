# Saffire Freycinet x Waubs Harbour

Waubs Harbour Whisky partners with the luxury lodge **Saffire Freycinet** to offer guests an exclusive distillery tour and a special bottling of their single‑malt whisky. The experience includes:
- Behind‑the‑scenes access to the distillery.
- Guided walk through the whisky production process.
- Sampling of local ingredients and a range of whiskies from Waubs Harbour’s current lineup and personal collection.
- An ocean‑view tasting room with a platter of fresh, local produce.

The collaboration is highlighted on Saffire’s website and was announced in November 2022. Shipping for the whisky is available Australia‑wide via standard or express post.

---

## Key facts
- **Partnership**: Waubs Harbour Whisky & Saffire Freycinet (Tasmania, Australia)
- **Launch date**: 11 November 2022
- **Experience components**: distillery tour, whisky production walk‑through, tasting room, local produce platter
- **Shipping**: Standard Post (4–8 days, $15) or Express Post (2–4 days, $20)
- **Legal**: Age verification required; compliance with Liquor Licensing Act 1990.

## Additional context
Saffire Freycinet is a luxury lodge recognized by TripAdvisor as the #1 hotel in Australia and the South Pacific, and #20 worldwide. The partnership aims to provide an exclusive whisky experience for Saffire guests while promoting Waubs Harbour’s single‑malt offerings.

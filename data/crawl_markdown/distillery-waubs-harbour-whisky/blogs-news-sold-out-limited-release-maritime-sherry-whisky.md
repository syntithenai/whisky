# Sold Out – Maritime Sherry Limited Release

Waubs Harbour Whisky announced that its limited‑run **Maritime Sherry** whisky has sold out online after a strong launch. The distillery, located in Bicheno, Tasmania, is shipping orders across Australia with standard and express options. Customers who missed the release are encouraged to join an email list for priority access to future releases.

## Shipping & Legal
- **Australia‑wide** delivery only.
- Standard Post: 4–8 working days, $15 flat rate.
- Express Post: 2–4 working days, $20 flat rate.
- Age verification required; under‑18 purchases are prohibited.

# Waubs Harbour Whisky Methodology
- **Distillation**: Head Distiller Rob Polmear blends science and art, using in‑house yeast cultures, custom stills, and sensory cut points.
- **Terroir**: The salty maritime climate of Bicheno, Tasmania stabilises temperature and imparts a unique terroir.
- **Ingredients**: Pure Apsley River water, Tasmanian malted barley, experimental coastal peat (reeds, melaleuca, salt bush).
- **Cask Influence**: Focus on high‑quality sherry‑seasoned French oak and American bourbon casks; 60 %+ influence claimed.
- **Sustainability & Community**: Acknowledges Aboriginal custodianship and offers shipping across Australia.

# Waubs Harbour Whisky Methodology

## Art of Distillation
- Head Distiller Rob Polmear combines deep whisky experience with science background (Masters in Antarctic Marine Science)
- Custom distillation process tailored to create a distinct spirit
- In-house lab for culturing yeast strains and experimenting with flavor profiles

## Terroir of Maturation
- Maritime climate of Bicheno, Tasmania stabilizes temperature fluctuations
- Salt permeates the building, enhancing whisky maturation
- Unique microclimate contributes to the whisky's character

## Provenance of Ingredients
- Locally sourced pure Tasmanian water from Apsley River
- Malted Tasmanian barley with rich flavor profile
- Coastal peat unique to eastern Tasmania for smoky notes

## Influence of Cask
- Wide variety of casks used, including rare and limited editions
- Sherry-seasoned barrels (French Oak Tawny, French Oak Apera) and American Oak Bourbon
- Cask selection plays a significant role in shaping the whisky's character

# How the whisky is made: Tasmanian Malted Barley

Waubs Harbour Distillery highlights the importance of using **Tasmanian malted barley** in their maritime Australian whisky. The distillery mills all barley on site, carefully monitors grain size and hydration, and controls mash‑tun conditions to optimise extraction. Spent grain is recycled as livestock feed, creating a circular economy.

---

## Key points
- 100 % Tasmanian malted barley – oily, sweet, high quality.
- On‑site milling with real‑time monitoring by Head Distiller Rob.
- Grist hydrator used before mashing for even moisture and temperature.
- Mash‑tun control: time, draw‑off speed, grain pressing.
- Spent grain returned to local farmers (e.g., Pig Creek Homestead).
- Circular economy partnership with family farms.

---

## Shipping & Legal
- Australia‑wide shipping via Standard Post ($15) or Express Post ($20).
- Age verification required; under‑18 purchase prohibited.
- Acknowledgement of Aboriginal traditional owners.

# Stout Cask Ed. 2 Tour of Aus

Waubs Harbour Whisky is hosting a free pre‑launch tasting tour for its Limited Release **Stout Cask Edition Two** across five Australian cities (Sydney, Hobart, Adelaide, Melbourne, Perth, Brisbane). The official launch is on 11 June at 7 pm AEST. Only **618 bottles** will be released; the first edition sold out in under an hour.

- **Tour dates & venues**: 
  - Sydney – 2 Jun, 5‑6 pm @The Duke of Clarence AUS
  - Hobart – 4 Jun, 4‑6 pm @The Whaler Hobart
  - Adelaide – 5 Jun, 5‑6 pm @Malt & Juniper
  - Melbourne – 6 Jun, 4‑6 pm @Casa de Vinos
  - Perth – 7 Jun, 6‑8 pm @Terrarium Perth
  - Brisbane – 9 Jun, 5‑7 pm @Savi Leroftitude Valley
- No RSVP required.
- Shipping: Australia wide, flat rates ($15 standard, $20 express).
- Priority access for email list members.

> *“Edition one of our Stout Cask whisky sold out in under an hour and we don’t expect this to be available for long!”*

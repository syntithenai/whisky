# EVENT: Rum Cask Launch Event

Waubs Harbour Distillery celebrated the launch of its Limited Release **Rum Cask whisky** on a Saturday that coincided with the start of Tasmania’s cray fishing season. The day featured:
- A morning *cray fishing competition* where participants raced to catch the biggest crayfish, with the prize being a bottle of Rum Cask.
- An evening *long‑table cray bake dinner* paired with lamb and local produce, followed by a tasting of the new whisky.

The event highlighted community spirit in Bicheno and showcased the distillery’s commitment to local ingredients and traditional production.

---

**Key highlights:**
- Winner: Mat (2.28 kg) – received a bottle of Rum Cask.
- Co‑founder Tim also had an equal first place but was not awarded.
- The launch dinner included lamb from a local farmer and sides made with regional produce.
- Distillery tours and guided tastings are available Tuesday–Saturday for $70 (full tour) or $40 (tasting only).

---

**Shipping & Availability:**
- Australia‑wide shipping: Standard Post ($15, 4‑8 days) or Express Post ($20, 2‑4 days).

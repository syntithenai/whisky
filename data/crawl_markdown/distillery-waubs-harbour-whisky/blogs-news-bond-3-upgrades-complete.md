# Bond 3 Upgrades Complete
The Bicheno Whisky Distillery has finished upgrading its Bond 3 rooms, now housing 100‑L barrels in some of the saltiest and most scenic spaces. The distillery continues to rotate casks, making room for larger ones that will mature over a decade or more. The upgrade follows the site’s history as an oyster hatchery and future plans include further iterations and possibly a new long table made from jetty boards.

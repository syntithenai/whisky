# Waubs Harbour Tokay Cask Whisky – Sold Out

The **Tokay Cask** limited‑release whisky from Waubs Harbour Distillery has sold out following its launch to the email list at 7 pm on 11 November 2025. The maritime Tasmanian spirit will ship today to those who secured a bottle, with standard and express delivery options across Australia.

- **Shipping**: Standard Post (4‑8 days, $15 flat) or Express Post (2‑4 days, $20 flat). 
- **Availability**: Sold out; future releases available via email list.
- **Legal**: Age verification required; complies with Liquor Licensing Act 1990.

For more information on the distillery’s full range of maritime whiskies, visit the main site.

# New Batch Release – Waubs Original 12 & Port Storm 12

- **Waubs Original 12**: 43 % ABV, 717 bottles, toffee sweetness, raisin finish, maritime character.
- **Port Storm 12**: 48 % ABV, 633 bottles, vanilla, poached pear, sultana, salty brininess from ocean‑side maturation.
- Both batches released 15 Apr 2026 at 7 pm AEST; priority access via email list. Shipping Australia only (standard $15, express $20).

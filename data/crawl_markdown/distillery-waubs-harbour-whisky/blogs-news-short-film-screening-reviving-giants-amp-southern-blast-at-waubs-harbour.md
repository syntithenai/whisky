# Short Film Screening: "Reviving Giants" & "Southern Blast" at Waubs Harbour

Waubs Harbour Whisky hosted a screening of two short films—**Reviving Giants** by the Great Southern Reef Foundation (GSRF) and **Southern Blast** by Surfrider Tasmania—on 29 October 2023. The event, part of the launch of a new limited‑release whisky, attracted over 100 guests and featured a Q&A with Dr Scott Bennett (GSRF), Senator Peter Whish‑Wilson, and marine scientist Ally King. Attendees enjoyed gourmet skewers by chef Tristan Punelle, local beers, gin, and the new whisky.

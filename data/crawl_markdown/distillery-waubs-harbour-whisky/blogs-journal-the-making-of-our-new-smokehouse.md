# The Making of Our New Smokehouse

Waubs Harbour Whisky is expanding its peat‑smoking capabilities by building a larger Smoke & Malt House. The new facility will allow the distillery to increase peat smoking and malt Tasmanian barley on site, aiming for a light, coastal peat style with nutty and earthy notes.

- **Current status**: Construction underway (July 2025).
- **Peat evolution**: 3rd iteration of smoker – from a small drum in 2019 to a 300 kg rotary smoker in 2020.
- **Flavor goal**: Coastal peat with nutty, earthy characteristics rather than heavy ash‑tray smoke.

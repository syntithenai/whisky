# Team Waubs Harbour
A concise introduction to the six‑person team behind Waubs Harbour Whisky, highlighting each member’s role—from co‑founders and head distiller to venue manager, hand‑labeling specialist, and brand ambassador.

- **Tim Polmear** – Co‑founder, visionary strategist.
- **Rob Polmear** – Co‑founder & Head Distiller; oversees all stages of whisky production and flavour experimentation.
- **Bec Polmear** – Co‑founder; manages storytelling, marketing, and communications.
- **Thomas Etges** – Venue & guest experience manager; ensures hand‑labeling and bottling quality.

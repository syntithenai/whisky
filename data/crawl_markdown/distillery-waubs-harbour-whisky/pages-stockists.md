# Waubs Harbour Whisky

## Key Facts
- Small-batch whisky distillery located in Tasmania, Australia
- Limited production runs of single malt whiskies
- Focus on traditional Scottish whisky-making techniques adapted to Australian climate and ingredients
- Handcrafted approach with attention to detail in every stage of production

## Production Signals
- Distilled at Waubs Harbour Distillery using pot stills and copper alembics
- Maturation occurs in a combination of American oak ex-bourbon barrels and locally sourced Tasmanian eucalyptus barrels
- Limited edition releases showcasing unique flavor profiles derived from different cask finishes
- Emphasis on sustainability with efforts to minimize environmental impact throughout the distilling process

## Commercial Signals
- Available at select stockists across Australia including major cities like Hobart, Launceston, and Sydney
- Growing popularity among whisky enthusiasts for its authentic Scottish character blended with local influences
- Positive reviews highlighting balanced flavors of peat smoke, vanilla, and citrus notes
- Expansion plans to increase distribution while maintaining quality standards

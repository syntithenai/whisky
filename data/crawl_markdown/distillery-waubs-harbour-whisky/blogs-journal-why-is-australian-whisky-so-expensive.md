# Why is Australian whisky so expensive?

The article explains that Australian whisky commands a premium price due to several factors:
- **Limited production volume**: most distilleries are small, raising per‑unit costs.
- **Premium ingredients**: local Tasmanian barley and imported high‑quality barrels add expense.
- **Long barrel maturation**: 3–10+ years of aging incurs storage and insurance costs.
- **High excise tax**: a large portion of the retail price goes to government levies.
- **Craftsmanship & innovation**: hands‑on, experimental production methods increase cost.

The piece also notes shipping details, legal age verification, and a brief acknowledgment of Aboriginal peoples.

# Introducing ‘Salt Marsh’ Limited Release Whisky

- **Release date:** 13 August 2025
- **Style:** Lightly peated single‑malt for non‑peated drinkers
- **Peat source:** Hand‑dug local salt marsh peat (Tasmania)
- **Barley treatment:** Light smoking with the peat
- **Flavor notes:** Nutty, salted caramel, nougat, subtle earthy undertones
- **Maturation:** Ex‑Bourbon American oak casks, aged by the ocean
- **Shipping:** Australia only – standard post 4–8 days ($15) or express 2–4 days ($20)
- **Legal & safety notices** included (age verification, liquor licence, pregnancy warning)

> *“This is no ashtray peat – it’s subtle, earthy & delicately balanced with a super light salt marsh peat influence.”*

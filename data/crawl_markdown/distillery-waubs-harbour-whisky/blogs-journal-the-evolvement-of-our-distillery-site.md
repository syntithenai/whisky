# The Evolvement of Our Distillery Site

Waubs Harbour Whisky began in 2018 by converting an old oyster hatchery into a still house, brew room and barrel warehouses. Three years later the distillery expanded to neighbouring buildings that now host bottling, dispatch, operations, a smokehouse, tasting room and cooperage. The site sits on “the gulch” in Bicheno beside Governor Island, where visitors can see seals, dolphins, whales and stingrays.

The distillery offers tours and guided tastings Monday‑Saturday; bookings are required. Shipping is available Australia‑wide with standard (4–8 days, $15) or express (2–4 days, $20) options. The company acknowledges the traditional owners of the land and complies with Australian liquor licensing laws.

## Long Description
# Waubs Harbour Whisky

**Location & ethos** – The former oyster hatchery at Bicheno, Tasmania has been transformed into a family‑run distillery that blends Scottish maritime heritage with Tasmanian character.  Production is small‑batch single malt, using salt‑laden bond rooms and an ocean‑powered cooling system to impart a distinct oceanic influence.

**Core products** –
- **Waubs Original (Batch 12)** – 717 bottles at $170 AUD each.
- **Port Storm (Batch 12)** – 633 bottles at $190 AUD each.
- **Founder's Reserve (Batch 11)** – 649 bottles at $220 AUD each.
- Limited releases such as *Maritime Sherry*, *Tokay Cask*, *Salt Marsh* and *Stout Cask Edition 2* are released on a very limited basis, often sold out quickly.

**Special releases & events** – The distillery hosts launch events (e.g., the “Kelp Revival” limited release) that combine whisky tastings with marine‑themed talks and canapés.  Events feature guests such as Head Distiller Rob Polmear, Chef Andrew Gerstkamp, and marine scientists.

**Awards & recognition** – Gold at the Royal Australian Spirits Awards; Category Winner at World Whiskies Awards 2024/25 for Small Batch Single Malt (12 yrs & under).

**Visitor experience** – Guided tours are available Monday‑Saturday.  Visitors can see the ocean‑powered cooling system, salt‑laden bond rooms, and the distillery’s unique cask maturation process.

**Shipping & commerce** – Australia‑wide shipping only with flat‑rate post; e‑commerce is present via the site’s product pages.  Age‑gate checks are enforced for purchases.

**Community & sustainability** – The distillery participates in beach clean‑ups and partners with local environmental groups, integrating marine conservation into its brand narrative.

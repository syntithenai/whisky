# Waubs Harbour Whisky FAQ

- **Shipping**: Flat $15 for standard, $20 for express (2‑4 days). All orders shipped within one working day from Bicheno, Tasmania.
- **Tours & Tastings**: Tours twice daily Mon‑Sat at 11 am and 2:30 pm; tastings at 1:30 pm and 4:15 pm. Bookings required but last‑minute slots may be available.
- **Vouchers**: Gift vouchers for tours, tastings, and online store are sold.
- **Accessibility**: Wheelchair ramps, accessible bathrooms, and optional seating assistance.
- **Cancellations & Group Bookings**: Refunds possible; small groups up to 12 (tours) or 20 (tastings). Contact info@waubsharbourwhisky.com for details.
- **Stocking**: Whisky sold directly online, at the distillery, bars, restaurants, and through a network of Australian stockists. Wholesale inquiries via email.
- **Events & Private Functions**: Events announced on social media; no private event hosting currently.
- **Contact**: Email info@waubsharbourwhisky.com or use the contact form.

All information is sourced from the official FAQ page of Waubs Harbour Whisky.

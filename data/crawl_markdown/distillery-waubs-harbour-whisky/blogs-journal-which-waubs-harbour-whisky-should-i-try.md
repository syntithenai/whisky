# Which Waubs Harbour whisky should I try?

Waubs Harbour offers a simple decision framework for choosing their whiskies:
1. **Cask type** – ex‑Bourbon casks give lighter, easier drinks; ex‑fortified (Tawny Port/Sherry) casks produce richer, stronger spirits.
2. **Provenance** – Tasmanian whiskies are smaller‑batch and more viscous; e.g., *Port Storm* matures in ex‑Tawny Port French oak on Tasmania’s east coast.
3. **ABV** – lower ABVs (42–45%) suit beginners (*Waubs Original*); higher ABVs (≈62%) appeal to enthusiasts (*Founder’s Reserve*).
4. **Craftsmanship** – small‑batch, intentional releases with in‑house yeast and local peat.

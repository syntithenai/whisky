**Waubs Harbour Whisky – February Highlights**

- Launched and sold out a *Limited Release Maritime Sherry* whisky.
- Ocean‑influenced maturation on the edge of Bicheno’s coast.
- Hand‑bottled, handwritten & labelled by hand.
- Two‑part launch event: day‑time fishing/diving with Tim, Rob, Ben & Laurie; evening dinner cooked by chef Andrew Gerstkamp.
- New enamel mugs released.
- Expanded distillery facilities (wash, bond stores, smokehouse, bottling rooms, tasting room, on‑site cooperage).
- Daily tours and tastings available Monday–Saturday.
- Shipping across Australia: standard $15, express $20.

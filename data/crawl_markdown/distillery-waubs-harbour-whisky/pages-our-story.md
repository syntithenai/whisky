# Waubs Harbour Whisky – Our Story

Waubs Harbour Whisky is a Tasmanian family‑run distillery that blends maritime heritage with Aboriginal history. Founded by Tim, Rob and Bec Polmear in 2017, the distillery occupies an old oyster hatchery on Tasmania’s east coast, close to the gravesite of Wauba Debar – a celebrated Aboriginal woman whose legacy inspired the name *Waubs Harbour*. The brand positions itself as a “maritime Tasmanian single malt whisky” that embraces coastal influences and aims for a rich, oily profile. Shipping is available across Australia with standard and express options.

## Heritage & Naming
The distillery’s name honours *Wauba Debar*, a Tasmanian Aboriginal woman known as the “heroine of the sea.” The town formerly called Waubs Harbour was renamed Bicheno, but local street names and landmarks still commemorate Wauba. The distillery’s proximity to her gravestone underscores its commitment to honoring Indigenous history.

# 2025: A Momentous Year for Waubs Harbour

- **Limited releases** featuring in‑house yeast, a Bicheno Beer Co. collaboration, first lightly peated whisky with local coastal peat, and a barrel‑cooper partnership.
- Built an on‑site smokehouse to expand peating capacity.
- *Waubs Original* won Category Winner at the World Whiskies Awards (UK).
- Hosted 3 000+ distillery tours & tastings; average 5‑star reviews.
- Hand‑bottled, hand‑labelled and hand‑written thousands of bottles on site.
- Launched new merchandise and hosted events nationwide.
- Shipping Australia wide: Standard Post $15 (4–8 days), Express Post $20 (2–4 days).

> *A big thank you for being part of our Waubs Harbour journey this year. We’re incredibly excited for all that’s to come in 2026!*

## Source Excerpts
- We built our on-site Smokehouse, enabling us to improve & increase capacity to smoke our Tasmanian barley for our upcoming peated whiskies (currently maturing in barrel!) and be able to experiment with subtle changes to our flavour profile.
- In 2025, we released some very special Limited Release whiskies, each one uniquely made - from culturing our own fresh yeast in-house, the second edition of a local collaboration with Bicheno Beer Co., our very first lightly peated whisky using local, coastal peat & a special collaboration with barrel Cooper, Laurie.

# Maritime Peat the Waubs Harbour Way

Waubs Harbour Distillery shares its journey of creating an authentic east‑coast Tasmanian peated whisky using locally sourced maritime peat. The distillery explains how coastal peat—rich in melaleuca, reeds and salty succulents—offers a subtle, nutty, earthy profile rather than heavy smoke. Trials began in 2020 with backyard equipment, leading to the first batch of single‑malt peated whisky.

The article also outlines shipping details (Australia wide, standard/express post) and legal notices, while acknowledging Aboriginal custodianship of the land.

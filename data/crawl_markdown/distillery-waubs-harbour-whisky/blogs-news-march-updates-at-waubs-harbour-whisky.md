# March Updates at Waubs Harbour Whisky
- **25 t of Tasmanian barley** delivered, pumped into the silo.
- Renovations: Bond 3 barrel room upgraded and new security doors installed for larger casks.
- Ben’s Southeast & East Asia trip to Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, Bangkok, Hong Kong & Taiwan.
- Customer reviews highlight the *Limited Release Maritime Sherry* whisky as smooth with buttery fruit, apple, pear, salted caramel notes and a sea feel.

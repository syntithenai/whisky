**Waubs Harbour Whisky – Co‑founders’ favourite releases**

- Head Distiller Rob Polmear favours the *Apera Bourbon Cask* for its balanced oak, dried fruit and citrus notes.
- Co‑founder Tim Polmear prefers the *Apera Cask Strength*, a bold, fortified single malt that can be opened with water.
- Co‑founder Bec Polmear loves the *Double Tawny Port*, noted for its rich port influence and approachability.

# Waubs Harbour Whisky – Coastal Peat Smoking Journey
- **Start**: 2019, experimenting with Tasmanian barley and local coastal peat.
- **Goal**: Achieve a light, nutty‑earthy flavour profile.
- **Peat Smoker Iterations**:
  - *#1*: Lab glass apparatus + 5 L copper stills; first peated spirit.
  - *#2*: Rob’s 44 L drum smoker – trialed 20+ peat samples, refined peat level.
  - *#3*: 2020, 1000 L milk‑vat tumbler (cradle + chain) – smoked 300 kg grain; produced nutty, earthy note with minimal smoke.
  - *#4*: 2025, new project underway (details forthcoming).
- **Shipping**: Australia only, standard/express options.
- **Legal & Cultural Notes**: Acknowledgement of Aboriginal owners, age verification, alcohol warnings.

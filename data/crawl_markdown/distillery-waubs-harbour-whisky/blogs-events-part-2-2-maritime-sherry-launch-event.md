# Maritime Sherry Launch Event

- **Event**: Part 2/2 of the launch celebration for Waubs Harbour’s *Maritime Sherry* single‑malt whisky.
- **Date**: Published 02 Feb 2026 (event held the night before).
- **Highlights**:
  - Seafood caught that day was grilled on an open‑fire grill by chef Andrew Gerstkamp in the distillery courtyard overlooking the ocean.
  - Dishes included abalone nigiri, charred crayfish pasta, blue‑eye trevalla spring rolls, fish skewers and more, all sourced from local waters.
  - Head Distiller Rob led a tasting of the new whisky, noting it is the oldest release by the distillery.
- **Next Steps**: The *Maritime Sherry* single‑malt will be released tomorrow (Wednesday) at 7 pm AEDT.

> A big thank you to Andrew & Alison Gerstkamp for an incredible feed and our team (plus partners) for a memorable day celebrating the launch of this very special 'Maritime Sherry' single malt whisky.

**New Limited Release – Stout Cask Edition Two**

- Launch date: **11 June 2025**.
- 59 % ABV, bottled in Tasmania.
- Matured first in ex‑Tawny Port casks, finished in a barrel that previously held Bicheno Beer Co.’s award‑winning Barrel Aged Imperial Stout.
- Flavour profile: *plump sultanas, salted toffee, dark chocolate, dark cherry*.
- Collaboration with local brewery **Bicheno Beer Co.** – the stout was aged in old Waubs Harbour casks before being refilled with new spirit.
- Gold medal at Indie Brewers Awards (late 2024).

Shipping: Australia‑wide standard post $15 (4–8 days) or express $20 (2–4 days).

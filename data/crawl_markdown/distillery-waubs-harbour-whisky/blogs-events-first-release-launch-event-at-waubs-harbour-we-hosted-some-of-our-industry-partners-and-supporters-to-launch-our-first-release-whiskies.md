# First Release Launch Event at Waubs Harbour

Waubs Harbour hosted a launch event for its first release whiskies, featuring industry partners and supporters. The afternoon began with Elder Aunty Patsy Cameron speaking about the life of Wauba Debar and the origins of the distillery’s name. Guests toured the site, enjoyed fresh local seafood prepared by chef Andrew Gerstkamp, and tasted three inaugural whiskies guided by Head Distiller Rob.

The event highlighted community support from Tasmania’s whisky industry and emphasized ongoing communication through newsletters for priority access to new releases.

# Tasmanian Whisky Week Wrap‑Up

Waubs Harbour celebrated a busy week of events during the Tasmanian Whisky Week, including:
- **Mustique Whisky Cruise** – an evening on the Derwent River with six whiskies from Overeem & Waubs Harbour.
- **Tas Spirits Showcase** – a day‑long showcase where attendees sampled the new *Salt Marsh* whisky.
- **Meet the Maker** – Rob (the distillery’s founder) presented the production process at The Farm Shed in Bicheno.
- **Opening of the Smokehouse** – ribbon cutting and first tasting of the new Salt Marsh whisky, highlighting increased peat smoking capacity.

The post also promotes priority access to future releases, shipping details for Australia, and a reminder of legal drinking age compliance.

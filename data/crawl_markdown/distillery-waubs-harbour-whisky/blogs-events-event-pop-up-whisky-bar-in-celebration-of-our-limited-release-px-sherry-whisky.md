# Event: Pop‑Up Whisky Bar – Celebration of Limited Release ‘PX Sherry’

Waubs Harbour Distillery will host a one‑night pop‑up whisky bar on **Saturday, 4 May 2024** from 5 pm to 9 pm. Guests can enjoy the new limited‑release *PX Sherry* whisky in a relaxed setting with a chesterfield lounge or an outdoor courtyard overlooking the ocean and rocks. Entry is free; beer, wine and light nibbles are available. The event is for **18+** only.

The distillery invites visitors to taste the new release first‑hand and offers free entry, while also promoting its shipping options (Australia wide) and a priority access email list.

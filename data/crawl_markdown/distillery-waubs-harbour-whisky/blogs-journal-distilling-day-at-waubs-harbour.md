# Distilling Day at Waubs Harbour
A behind‑the‑scenes look at how Waubs Harbour creates its maritime single malt whisky.
- **Morning routine**: Rob starts before 6 am, turning on pumps, warming stills and milling barley.
- **Mash & wash**: On‑site mash tun with a fragrant barley brew; the wash ferments for a week.
- **Distillation**: Double distilled in custom Tasmanian copper stills overlooking the ocean.
- **Maturation**: New‑make spirit is transferred to old port, sherry or bourbon barrels that are rejuvenated on site and set to mature by the sea.
- **Visitor info**: Tours and guided tastings available Monday–Saturday; bookings required.

Shipping is Australia‑wide with standard (4‑8 days) and express (2‑4 days) options. The distillery acknowledges Aboriginal custodianship of the land.

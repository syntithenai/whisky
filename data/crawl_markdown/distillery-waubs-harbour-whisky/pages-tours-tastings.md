# Tours & Tastings at Waubs Harbour Distillery

- **Location**: Bicheno, Tasmania – an ocean‑cooled former oyster hatchery.
- **Tours**:
  - *Distillery Tour & Whisky Tasting* – $70. Includes a walk through the production building and bond stores followed by a guided tasting in the ocean‑view room.
  - *Guided Whisky Tasting* – $40. Focuses on recent releases with an introduction to the distillery’s story.
- **Booking**: Monday–Saturday, essential via the online table; email for private requests.
- **Gift Vouchers**: Available for both tour types.
- **Shipping**: Australia‑wide only (standard or express post).
- **Legal & Safety**: Age verification required; alcohol warnings and licence details displayed.

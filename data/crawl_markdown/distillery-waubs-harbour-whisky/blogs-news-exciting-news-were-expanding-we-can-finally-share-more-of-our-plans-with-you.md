# Waubs Harbour Whisky Expansion Announcement

Waubs Harbour, a distillery located in Bicheno, Australia, has announced the acquisition of three neighbouring buildings adjacent to its existing site. The expansion will:
- Increase cask storage capacity for ocean‑matured whisky.
- Expand bottling production.
- Enable the creation of a tasting house and visitor experience.

The distillery’s history is intertwined with Bicheno’s maritime heritage, having previously operated as an oyster hatchery and abalone farm. The new development will preserve some original structures while repurposing others to maintain the site’s nautical character.

Further details will be released via email newsletters, and visitors can sign up for priority access to future releases and events.

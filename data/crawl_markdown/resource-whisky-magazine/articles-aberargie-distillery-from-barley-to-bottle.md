# Aberargie Distillery: From Barley to Bottle
Aberargie marks the Morrison family's return to distilling with a single‑malt whisky that follows a strict barley‑to‑glass philosophy. The distillery, based in Perth, grows its own Golden Promise and Laureate barley on a 300‑acre farm, practices regenerative agriculture, and mashes a 52/48 blend. The first bottling (March 2026) is 48.2 % ABV, non‑chill‑filtered, aged in first‑fill bourbon and sherry casks, and described as rich with baked apple, golden pastry, sultanas, prunes and an herbal finish.

Key figures: Niel Hendriksz (MD of Morrison Scotch Whisky Distillers), Graeme Mackeddie (Operations Director). Design by Kingdom & Sparrow. The distillery aims for a recognisable character and long‑term quality.

## Highlights
- 300‑acre farm, regenerative farming, cover crops, grazing animals.
- Mash bill: 52 % Golden Promise, 48 % Laureate.
- First release: March 2026, 48.2 % ABV, non‑chill‑filtered, aged in bourbon & sherry casks.
- Flavor profile: baked apple, golden pastry, sultanas, prunes, herbal finish.
- Design partnership with Kingdom & Sparrow.

## Future Plans
Aberargie intends to expand its portfolio while maintaining the same grain‑to‑glass ethos and a consistent distillery character.

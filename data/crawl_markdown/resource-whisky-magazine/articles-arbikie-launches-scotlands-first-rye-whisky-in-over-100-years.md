# Arbikie Highland Rye
Arbikie Highland Estate has launched Scotland’s first rye whisky in over a century – the **Highland Rye Single Grain Scotch**. The spirit is made from a mash of Arantes rye, Odyssey malted barley and Viscount wheat, aged in oak casks for 7 years. Only 998 bottles were produced worldwide, with Harrods among the retailers.

Key points:
- First Scottish rye since 1794.
- Field‑to‑bottle production; ingredients sourced from Arbikie’s own farm.
- Flavour profile: orange marmalade, cloves and maple syrup.
- Distillery director John Stirling and master distiller Kirsty Black highlight sustainability and provenance.
- Positive industry reception – Blair Bowman praised the return of rye to Scotland.

# Arbikie Highland Rye Core Range
Arbikie Distillery has announced its first permanent core‑range whiskies – the **Original**, **Peated** and **PX** expressions of its Highland Rye line.
- The Original is a rye, wheat and malted barley mash bill matured in American oak casks, described as having *spice, smoke and sweetness*.
- The Peated and PX are finished by maturing the Original in casks previously used for peated Islay whisky and Pedro Ximénez wine, respectively.
- All grain is farm‑grown on Arbikie’s own estate; the distillery follows a full farm‑to‑bottle process.
- The launch marks the revival of rye in Scotch whisky after more than 100 years.

> *“This is the next stage of Arbikie; by creating this new core range we continue the revival of rye in Scotch whisky distilling.”* – Dr Kirsty Black, master distiller.

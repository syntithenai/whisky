# Arbikie Highland Rye Core Range
Arbikie Distillery has announced its first permanent core‑range of rye whiskies – the **Highland Rye** collection, comprising three expressions:
- **Original** – flagship rye with a mash bill of rye, wheat and malted barley, finished in American oak.
- **Peated** – Original matured in casks previously used for peated Islay whisky.
- **PX** – Original matured in Pedro Ximénez wine casks.

The distillery is family‑owned, single‑estate and farm‑to‑bottle. All grain (barley, wheat, rye) is grown on its own estate on Scotland’s east coast. The launch marks the revival of rye in Scotch whisky after a 100‑year gap.

> *“This is the next stage of Arbikie; by creating this new core range we continue the revival of rye in Scotch whisky distilling.”* – Dr Kirsty Black, master distiller.

For more details visit [arbikie.com](https://www.arbikie.com).

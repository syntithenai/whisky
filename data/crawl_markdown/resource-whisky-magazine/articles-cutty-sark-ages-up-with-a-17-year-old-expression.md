# Cutty Sark 17‑Year‑Old
Cutty Sark launches a new 17‑year‑old blended Scotch, aged in sherry, port and first‑fill bourbon casks. The blend is described as rich, fruity and spicy with notes of candied orange peel, red cherry, plum, milk chocolate, cocoa powder and subtle sweet spices. It comes in an elegant black box with rope‑and‑eyelet design and gold lettering.

## Key facts
- **Launch year**: 2025 (article date) – 17‑year‑old expression introduced.
- **Cask types**: sherry, port, first‑fill bourbon.
- **Flavor profile**: candied orange peel, red cherry, plum, milk chocolate, cocoa powder, sweet spices, oaky notes, cappuccino undertones.
- **Packaging**: black box with rope‑and‑eyelet design, gold lettering, yellow accents.
- **Brand history**: founded 1923, popular during Prohibition via smuggling.
- **Master blender**: Stephen Woodcock.
- **Senior brand manager**: Manon Brochet.

# English Whisky Guild Annual Report (2024)

The English Whisky Guild (EWG) released its first annual report, highlighting the rapid growth of English whisky and praising the sector’s “unique tapestry” of distilleries. Key points include:
- **Sales**: ~50 000 9‑litre cases sold last year.
- **Export reach**: More than 30 countries, with 40 % of sales outside the UK.
- **Visitor numbers**: Over 250 000 people toured English distilleries in 2023.
- **GI application**: The guild is seeking a geographical indication (GI) from the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs to protect English whisky legally.
- **Membership**: 24 member distilleries (≈half of all operational ones) plus associate members such as maltsters and packaging suppliers.
- **Leadership**: Founded May 2022; first chairman Andrew Nelstrop, succeeded by Dan Szor in Oct 2023. CEO Morag Garden emphasises the report’s role as a reference for public bodies and enthusiasts.

The report contains statistical analysis, interviews with makers, distillery profiles, and essays on sustainability and the pending GI application.

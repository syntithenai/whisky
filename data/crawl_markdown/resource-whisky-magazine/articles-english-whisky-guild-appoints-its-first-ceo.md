# English Whisky Guild appoints its first CEO

The English Whisky Guild (EWG) has named **Morag Garden** as its inaugural full‑time Chief Executive Officer. Garden, who spent ten years with the Scotch Whisky Association and most recently worked for a global sustainability consultancy, will lead an organisation that now counts 23 member distilleries.

Key points:
- EWG aims to support and lobby for English whisky producers.
- The guild has forecasted **~50 000 casks** of whisky laid down by the end of 2024, with maturing stock valued over **£1 billion**.
- A geographical indication (GI) application for “English whisky” was submitted to the UK government.
- Garden’s appointment is seen as a vote of confidence in England’s growing whisky sector and its potential on the global stage.

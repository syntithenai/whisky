# Coleburn Whisky Resort: A Speyside Luxury Vision

- **Location & History** – The former Coleburn Distillery in Speyside, mothballed since 1985, is being transformed into a full‑fledged whisky resort.
- **Key Players** – Dale Winchester and Gwenda Smits (directors) with D&M Winchester bottling; Keith Cruickshank (former Benromach master distiller) leading whisky production.
- **Concept** – A five‑star hospitality complex featuring luxury log cabins, a spa, fine dining, event spaces, and a pagoda penthouse. The resort will also house the distillery’s stills once operational.
- **Phased Development** – Initial phase includes restaurant, spa, and accommodation; later phases will add full distillation facilities and expanded lodging.
- **Community Impact** – Job creation, investment in public transport (buses, potential railway), and local whisky promotion through the “Local Heroes” blended malt trilogy.
- **Funding & Governance** – Small grants (e.g., biomass boiler) and phased reinvestment of profits; owners emphasize independent governance.

The project aims to blend Speyside tradition with modern luxury hospitality, potentially becoming a unique destination for whisky enthusiasts worldwide.

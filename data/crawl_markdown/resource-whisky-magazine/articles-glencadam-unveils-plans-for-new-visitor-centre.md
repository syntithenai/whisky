# Glencadam Distillery Visitor Centre Plans

Glencadam Distillery, founded in 1825 and located in Brechin, Angus, is developing a new two‑floor visitor centre to celebrate its upcoming 200th anniversary. The complex will feature a stone façade, exhibition space on the history of the distillery and surrounding area, retail outlets, tasting rooms, a whisky lounge, café, and a function suite for private hire and corporate hospitality. Design elements echo the distillery’s heritage, such as skylights inspired by its pagoda‑style Doig ventilators. Construction will not affect whisky production, which is expected to continue uninterrupted.

The centre is slated to open in summer 2025, with updates available on the distillery’s website.

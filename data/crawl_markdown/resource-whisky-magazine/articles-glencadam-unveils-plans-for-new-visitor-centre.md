# Glencadam Distillery Visitor Centre Plans

Glencadam Distillery, founded in 1825 and located in Brechin, Angus, is developing a new two‑floor visitor centre to celebrate its upcoming 200th anniversary. The design incorporates historic elements such as the pagoda‑style Doig ventilators turned into skylights and a stone façade that blends with existing buildings. Inside will be an exhibition on the distillery’s heritage, retail space, tasting rooms, whisky lounge, café, and a function suite for private hire and corporate hospitality. Construction is scheduled to finish in summer 2025, with no impact on whisky production.

The project follows a 2021 refurbishment that reinstated a working internal waterwheel modeled after the original 19th‑century design.

For updates visit [Glencadam’s website](https://www.glencadamwhisky.com).

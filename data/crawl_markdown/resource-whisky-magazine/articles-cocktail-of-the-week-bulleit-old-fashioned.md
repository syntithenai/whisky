# Bulleit Old Fashioned

A classic Old Fashioned recipe featuring **Bulleit Bourbon** from Diageo, presented as part of Whisky Magazine’s Bourbon Heritage Month coverage. The article offers a straightforward method: combine simple syrup, aromatic bitters and bourbon in a rocks glass with ice, stir gently, garnish with an orange peel, and serve.

The piece encourages experimentation—substituting the bourbon or sweetener (e.g., maple syrup) for a personal twist—and links to previous “Cocktail of the Week” selections. No pricing or purchase links are provided.

## Key Ingredients & Method
- **1.25 oz Bulleit Bourbon**
- **2 bar spoons simple syrup**
- **3 dashes aromatic bitters**
- Stir with ice, express orange peel over the glass, add as garnish.

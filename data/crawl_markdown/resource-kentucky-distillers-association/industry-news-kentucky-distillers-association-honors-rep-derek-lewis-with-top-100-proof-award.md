# Kentucky Distillers’ Association Honors Rep. Derek Lewis With Top “100 Proof” Award

The KDA presented Rep. Derek Lewis (R‑London) with its prestigious **“100 Proof Award”** on March 19, 2026 for leading landmark legislation that modernized Kentucky’s air‑emissions fee structure and eased burdens on distillery backup generators. The award recognizes elected officials who protect the bourbon industry.

Key points:
- Rep. Lewis sponsored House Bill 346, reforming emissions fees and lowering costs for 99 % of fee payors.
- KDA President Eric Gregory praised his regulatory relief efforts.
- The award was presented with a commemorative barrel head in Frankfort.
- Previous recipients include U.S. Senator Mitch McConnell, Gov. Andy Beshear, and others.
- Kentucky’s bourbon industry generates $10.6 billion annually, supports 24,000 jobs, and attracts nearly 3 million tourists via the Kentucky Bourbon Trail.

For more details visit the KDA website or the advocacy page.

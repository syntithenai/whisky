# Jiggle All the Way: Gingerbread Bourbon Jellies
A playful recipe that fuses three classic holiday staples—gingerbread men, bourbon, and gelatin—into a festive dessert. The article shares a personal family story, explains the nostalgic appeal of each component, and provides step‑by‑step instructions for making gingerbread‑men shaped bourbon jellies.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups Kentucky bourbon (any brand)
- 2 cups sugar
- 2 cups water
- 4 packets unflavored gelatin (~0.5 oz each)
- 32 cocktail bitters
- Cocktail cherries

## Method Overview
1. Make a simple syrup with sugar and water.
2. Mix bourbon, bitters, and syrup.
3. Dissolve gelatin in the mixture.
4. Pour into a greased pan, add cherries, refrigerate overnight.
5. Cut gingerbread‑men shapes with cookie cutters.
6. Serve.

The piece also introduces **Aften Locken**, an executive bourbon manager, and highlights *Vida Destilada*, a Kentucky bourbon education program.

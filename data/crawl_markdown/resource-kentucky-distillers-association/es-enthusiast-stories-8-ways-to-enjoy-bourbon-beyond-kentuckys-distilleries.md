# 8 Ways to Enjoy Bourbon Beyond Kentucky’s Distilleries

The Kentucky Distillers’ Association article offers a practical guide for bourbon lovers who want to experience the spirit outside of traditional distillery tours. It lists eight activities, from tasting flights at local restaurants and visiting speakeasy‑style bars to enjoying classic Kentucky dishes like the Hot Brown, taking cocktail classes on a farm, riding a dinner train, crafting bourbon‑infused candles or sauces, sipping a Bourbon Bloody Mary, and purchasing private barrel selections.

Each section highlights specific venues (e.g., Libby’s Comfort in Covington, Contra Viento y Marea in Louisville, the Old Seelbach Bar, Heritage Farm, My Old Kentucky Dinner Train, Maddox & Rose Marketplace, and Westport Wine & Spirits) and explains how these experiences deepen appreciation for bourbon culture.

The piece emphasizes responsible enjoyment, local flavor, and the unique blend of Kentucky’s hospitality with its iconic spirit.

# Kentucky Bourbon: A New Brand For A Timeless Spirit
Kentucky Bourbon is presented as a cultural and historical legacy that extends beyond the spirit itself. The Kentucky Distillers’ Association (KDA) has launched the **Kentucky Bourbon** brand to protect and promote this heritage, positioning it as an identity for the state akin to peaches in Georgia or wine in California.

Key points:
- **Brand purpose:** Officially claim Kentucky’s bourbon heritage and ensure its legacy endures.
- **Mission & vision:** Build a global community of enthusiasts while deepening demand, quality, culture, and craft.
- **Lifestyle focus:** Bourbon is described as a lifestyle that draws people to distilleries, events, apparel, and themed dining experiences.
- **Journey & tourism:** The Kentucky Bourbon Trail® and the Craft Tour® offer millions of visits across 800+ miles of bourbon adventures.
- **Community emphasis:** Inclusive, welcoming anyone regardless of expertise or origin, fostering shared conversations and friendships.

The article encourages readers to join the community via social media and highlights KDA’s role as the worldwide voice for bourbon.

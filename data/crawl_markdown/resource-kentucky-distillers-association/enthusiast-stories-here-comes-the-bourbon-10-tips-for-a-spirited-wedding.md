A playful guide from the Kentucky Distillers’ Association that turns wedding planning into a bourbon‑themed celebration. It offers ten creative ideas—from burying a bottle before the ceremony to using barrel head guestbooks—designed to weave bourbon culture into every aspect of a Kentucky wedding.

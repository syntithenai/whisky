# Kentucky Distillers' Association (KDA)

The KDA is a non‑profit trade association founded in 1880 that represents the finest distilling companies in Kentucky. It promotes responsible growth, protects the bourbon industry’s legacy, and advocates for progressive laws and policies. The organization highlights Kentucky as the world’s primary bourbon producer, with an economic impact of $9 billion, over 23,100 jobs, and a payroll exceeding $2.2 billion.

Key focus areas include:
- **Advocacy**: Lobbying for legislation that supports sustainable industry growth.
- **Economic Impact**: Showcasing the industry’s contribution to local economies.
- **Membership & Culture**: Building a better bourbon culture through responsible practices and community engagement.
- **Responsibility**: Emphasizing accountability and ethical production.

The site also offers resources such as a glossary, media contacts, and information on the Kentucky Bourbon Trail.

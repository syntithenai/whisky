# DIY Bourbon Countdown Calendar

A step‑by‑step guide from the Kentucky Distillers' Association on creating a 24‑day bourbon advent calendar for holiday gifting or personal enjoyment. The article covers theme selection, sourcing bottles (including local Kentucky brands and specialty styles), packaging ideas, pouring technique, labeling, and safety tips. It also includes legal reminders about re‑packaging alcohol for resale and encourages sharing creations on social media.

## Key Takeaways
- **Theme Ideas**: Local legends, high‑rye bourbons, rare collectibles, wheat drams, barrel‑proof options, age progression, personal memories.
- **Packing Essentials**: 24 food‑safe, leak‑proof containers; numbers or stickers; hooks/cords; labels with bottle info.
- **Pouring Method**: Use a funnel and jigger for consistent 1‑oz servings; clean between pours.
- **Safety & Legal**: Keep at room temperature, out of reach of minors, and avoid resale. Check state laws before shipping.
- **Social Sharing**: Tag @kybourbon on Instagram or Facebook.

---

## Practical Tips
1. **Plan the order** – start with lighter styles and finish with stronger barrel‑proofs.
2. **Personalize labels** – add anecdotes or pairing suggestions.
3. **Presentation** – use a decorative box, mini tree, or themed wrapping paper.
4. **Legal check** – re‑packaging for sale is prohibited; the calendar is strictly a gift.

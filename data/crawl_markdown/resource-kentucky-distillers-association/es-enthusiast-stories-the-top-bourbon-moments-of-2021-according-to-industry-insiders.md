# The Top Bourbon Moments of 2021
- **Brough Brothers** opened their first African‑American owned Kentucky bourbon distillery in Park Hill, Louisville.
- **Tariff relief**: U.S./EU trade dispute over a 25 % duty on Kentucky bourbon was resolved in October 2021.
- **Kentucky Bourbon Benefit**: A rapid, $3.4 M charity auction raised funds for tornado‑hit western Kentucky.
- **Festival of Bourbon** weather‑resilient event held despite heavy rain and flooding.
- **Women’s Bourbon Association** celebrated its 10th anniversary with a sold‑out SIPosium conference.
- **Maker’s Private Selection** at Westport Whiskey & Wine highlighted sustainable practices.
- **Kentucky Black Bourbon Guild** partnered with Castle & Key on an Untold Story series celebrating African‑American heritage.
- **Log Still Distillery** expanded into a full 360° bourbon destination with lodging, events and a lake.

These moments illustrate resilience, community collaboration, and diversity in Kentucky’s bourbon industry during 2021.

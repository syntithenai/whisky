# Jiggle All the Way: Gingerbread Bourbon Jellies
A playful recipe that turns an Old Fashioned into gelatin shaped like gingerbread men. The article blends family anecdotes, bourbon history, and a step‑by‑step guide to making the jellied cocktail.

## Ingredients & Steps
- 2 cups Kentucky bourbon (any brand)
- 2 cups sugar + 2 cups water for simple syrup
- 4 packets unflavored gelatin
- Cocktail bitters (32 dashes) and cherries

1. Make simple syrup, dissolve in bourbon with bitters.
2. Add gelatin until dissolved, pour into pan, chill overnight.
3. Cut gingerbread shapes around cherries, serve.

## Context
The author ties the recipe to holiday traditions—Jell‑O, bourbon advertising, and gingerbread folklore—and shares a lighthearted family cook‑off story.

# Kentucky Distillers’ Association Honors Sen. Julie Raque Adams

The KDA awarded Senator Julie Raque Adams its **100 Proof Award** on March 19, 2026 for her leadership in passing Senate Bill 202, which sets regulatory standards for THC‑infused beverages. The award recognizes elected officials who protect Kentucky bourbon and was presented by KDA President Eric Gregory.

The article also highlights the economic impact of bourbon—$10.6 billion annually, 24,000 jobs, $370 million in taxes—and invites readers to learn more about the Kentucky Bourbon Trail.

---

**Key facts**:
- Award presented by KDA President Eric Gregory.
- Senate Bill 202 regulates THC‑infused intoxicating beverages.
- The award was created in 2009 and is the highest honor for elected officials.
- Bourbon generates $10.6 billion, supports 24,000 workers, and contributes $370 million in taxes.

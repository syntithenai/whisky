# Grilled Peach Old Fashioned
A summer‑season cocktail that blends grilled peaches with Kentucky bourbon. The recipe calls for 2 oz bourbon (90–100 proof), a quarter ounce of peach‑simple syrup, and two dashes of peach bitters. A simple syrup is made by simmering diced grilled peaches in water, adding sugar, and steeping before straining.

The article also offers guidance on choosing a bourbon: aim for 90–100 proof, under four years old, and priced $20–$35. Suggested brands include Elijah Craig, Maker’s Mark, Four Roses Small Batch, Old Grand‑Dad Bonded, Bulleit, Rittenhouse Rye, and Knob Creek Rye.

Erin Petrey, the author, is a cocktail editor and bourbon enthusiast based in Lexington/Washington, DC.

# Jack Daniel's Tennessee Whiskey

Jack Daniel's is a renowned American whiskey brand known for its smooth, charcoal-filtered Tennessee whiskey. The company was founded in Lynchburg, Tennessee, by Jasper Newton "Jack" Daniel in 1866.

## Key Facts
- **Distillery Location**: Lynchburg, Tennessee
- **Founded**: 1866
- **Charcoal Filtration**: Unique process using sugar maple charcoal
- **Signature Style**: Smooth and mellow flavor profile
- **Variants**: Jack Daniel's Old No.7, Gentleman Jack, Single Barrel, etc.

## Production Signals
- Charcoal mellowing process gives the whiskey its distinctive smoothness
- Small-batch production methods for select variants like Single Barrel
- Consistent quality control across all batches

## Commercial Signals
- Widely distributed in Australia and many other countries
- Strong brand recognition globally
- Marketing campaigns emphasizing "Live Freely" and responsible consumption

## Risks / Unknowns
- Limited availability of some specialty variants
- Potential regulatory changes affecting distribution
- Competition from other premium whiskey brands

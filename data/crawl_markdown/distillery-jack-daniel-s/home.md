# Jack Daniel's Old No.7

- Iconic American whiskey since 1866
- Distilled in Lynchburg, Tennessee using a unique charcoal mellowing process
- Known for its smooth, approachable flavor profile
- Widely recognized globally as the flagship brand of Jack Daniel's

## Key Facts
- **Distillery Location**: Lynchburg, Tennessee
- **Charcoal Mellowing**: Unique process that imparts distinct smoothness and character
- **Alcohol Content**: Typically 40% ABV (80 proof)
- **Variants**: Old No.7, Gentleman Jack, Bonded Series, Single Barrel Collection, etc.

## Production Signals
- Charcoal mellowing involves filtering the whiskey through sugar maple charcoal before aging
- Aged in new charred oak barrels for a minimum of two years
- Bottled at 40% ABV (80 proof) with no added ingredients except water

- Extensive global distribution across over 150 countries

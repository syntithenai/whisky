## Long Description
# LARK Distillery – Tasmania’s Only Working Whisky Village

LARK Distillery sits on 76 Shene Road in the historic town of Pontville, a short drive north of Hobart. The estate occupies meticulously restored 1800s buildings that house Australia’s oldest single‑malt distillery and its surrounding whisky village.

## Visitor Experience
Daily tours run at 12:00 pm and 2:00 pm for $69 per person (45‑minute guided walk). Guests receive a full introduction to grain fermentation, cask ageing, the cooperage process, and a tasting of LARK Single Malt Whisky at the cellar door. Reviews consistently describe the experience as *interactive*, *fun* and *worth every dollar*. The atmosphere is welcoming for beginners and families with teens, though tastings are adult‑only.

## Highlights
- **Grain & Fermentation Walk‑through** – learn how barley is milled, mashed and fermented.
- **Cask Ageing & Cooperage** – see the bond stores where whisky matures and watch cooperages craft new casks.
- **Cellar Door Tastings** – sample LARK Single Malt Whisky on site.

## Practical Details
- **Location:** 76 Shene Road, Pontville, Tasmania
- **Hours:** Daily 10:00 am–4:00 pm (tours at 12:00 pm & 2:00 pm)
- **Tour Price:** $69 per person
- **No on‑site food** – visitors are encouraged to bring snacks.

LARK Distillery is a key destination for whisky enthusiasts and tourists exploring Tasmania’s unique spirit heritage.

# LARK Distillery, Pontville

LARK Distillery is Tasmania’s oldest and most innovative single‑malt whisky distillery, situated at **76 Shene Road, Pontville**. The estate occupies meticulously restored 1800s buildings and houses a working cooperage, bond stores, and a cellar door.

Daily tours (12 pm & 2 pm) cost $69 per person and last about 45 minutes, offering guided tastings, cask samples, and insights into grain, fermentation, ageing and coopering. The distillery also operates **The Still** in Hobart and the **LARK Cellar Door** on Davey Street.

Visitors praise the historic charm, knowledgeable guides, interactive tasting experience, and welcoming atmosphere.

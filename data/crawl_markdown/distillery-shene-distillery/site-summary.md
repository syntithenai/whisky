## Long Description
# LARK Distillery – Tasmania’s Oldest Single‑Malt
LARK Distillery sits at **76 Shene Road, Pontville**, nestled in meticulously restored 1800s buildings that form Tasmania’s only working whisky village. The estate hosts a full‑scale cooperage, bond stores, and a cellar door where visitors can sample cask‑strength whiskies and limited releases.

## Visitor Experience
- **Tours**: Daily 45‑minute guided tours at 12 pm and 2 pm for $69 per person. The tour covers the distillery’s history, fermentation process, cooperage, and tasting of seasonal releases.
- **Cellar Door & Whisky Bar**: A waterfront cellar door in Hobart (14 Davey Street) and the flagship LARK Whisky Bar offer on‑site tastings and retail sales.

## Products
LARK produces a range of single‑malt whiskies, many available as cask‑strength or limited releases. The distillery also offers **Forty Spotted Gin**, sold at the cellar door in Hobart.

## Highlights
- Historic village setting with restored heritage buildings.
- Working cooperage and bond stores.
- Interactive tours with knowledgeable guides.
- Seasonal releases and cask‑strength expressions.

LARK Distillery continues to innovate while preserving Tasmania’s whisky heritage, making it a must‑visit for enthusiasts and casual visitors alike.

# LARK Distillery, Pontville
LARK Distillery is Tasmania’s oldest and most innovative single‑malt whisky distillery, located at **76 Shene Road, Pontville**. The estate sits in restored 1800s buildings and houses a working cooperage, bond stores, and a cellar door. Daily tours (12 pm & 2 pm) run for 45 min and cost **$69 per person**, featuring guided tastings, cask samples, and seasonal releases.

- **Opening hours**: 10 am – 4 pm daily
- **Tour price**: $69/person
- **Key attractions**: historic village, cooperage, cellar door, LARK single malts & gin
- **Visitor highlights**: interactive experience, knowledgeable guides, welcoming atmosphere

The distillery also operates the flagship *LARK Whisky Bar* in Hobart and a waterfront *Cellar Door* at 14 Davey Street.

# Lawrenny Estate Distillery

- **Location**: Ouse, Tasmania, Australia
- **Founded**: 1818 (Australia's oldest distillery)
- **Distillery Type**: Single Malt Whisky
- **Website**: [lawrenny.com](https://lawrenny.com/)

## Key Facts
- Lawrenny Homestead Single Malt Whisky is a Tasmanian single malt whisky produced by the oldest operating distillery in Australia.
- The distillery was founded in 1818 and has been producing spirits continuously since then.
- Lawrenny offers a range of expressions including Homestead Estate Whisky, Descension Release, Ascension Release, and various limited editions like Van Diemen's Gin 1818 Settlers Gin and Highlands Gin.

## Production Signals
- **Maturation**: Aged in Tasmanian oak barrels for varying periods depending on the expression.
- **Blending**: Some releases are blended from multiple casks to achieve a consistent flavor profile.
- **Finishing**: Limited editions may include finishing in other types of casks like sherry or port.

## Commercial Signals
- Lawrenny is recognized as one of Tasmania's leading distilleries, known for its high-quality single malt whiskies.
- The distillery participates in regional whisky competitions and events to promote Tasmanian spirits globally.
- Collaborations with local brands like Saint Clair Vodka and Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur showcase Lawrenny's innovative spirit.

## Risks / Unknowns
- As a small boutique distillery, Lawrenny faces challenges in scaling production while maintaining quality control.
- Market competition from larger international whisky brands poses a risk to market share.
- Regulatory changes affecting liquor licensing and export could impact distribution efforts.

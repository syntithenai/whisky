# Lawrenny Estate Distillery – History
Lawrenny Estate is a Tasmanian whisky and spirits producer located in the Central Highlands near Hobart. The estate dates back to the early 1800s, founded by Lt. Edward Lord, a colonial cattle tycoon. It once spanned ~80,000 acres of farmland producing premium cattle, sheep, wheat, barley and hops. In 1991 the Mace family acquired the property, restoring its gardens, pastures and Black Angus beef operation. Today the estate focuses on high‑quality Tasmanian produce and distilling.

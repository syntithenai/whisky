# Saint Clair Vodka
Saint Clair Vodka is a contemporary spirit produced by Lawrenny Estate Distillery in Tasmania. It uses crystal‑pure waters from Lake Saint Clair and small‑batch botanicals grown on the estate, resulting in a floral, citrus‑infused profile with a pillowy mouthfeel.

- **Style**: Contemporary
- **Profile**: Floral, elegant, citrusy, rich & pillowy
- **Awards**: 2018 American Distillers Awards (Best in Class)
- **Availability**: Shop now via the distillery’s online store or cellar door.

The page also lists other Lawrenny spirits such as Van Diemen’s Gin, 1818 Settlers Gin, Highlands Gin, Meadowbank Pink Gin, Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur and Estate Cellar Door products.

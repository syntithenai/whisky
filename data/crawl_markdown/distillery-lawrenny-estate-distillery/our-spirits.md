# Lawrenny Estate Distillery – Spirits
Lawrenny offers a range of award‑winning contemporary gins and spirits, including:
- **Van Diemen’s Gin**
- **1818 Settlers Gin**
- **Highlands Gin**
- **Meadowbank Pink Gin**
- **Saint Clair Vodka**
- **Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur**
The site highlights local botanicals, a free‑form distilling philosophy by Head Distiller Joe Dinsmoor, and a cellar door at 6485 Lyell Highway, Ouse. It also lists press mentions and contact details.

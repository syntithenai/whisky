# Lawrenny Estate Wins Tasmania’s Best Beef Producer Award
Lawrenny Estate owners Ross and Mary Mace were honoured at the Meat Standards Australia (MSA) Producer Awards 2023‑25 for being Tasmania’s best larger non‑grainfed beef producer. Over two years they managed 234 head, achieving an average MSA Index of **63.41** with **99 % compliance**.

The estate, located in Ouse, Upper Derwent Valley, also produces whisky and gin. Ross, a former crop‑dusting pilot, and Mary moved from Queensland to Tasmania in 1992, doubling the property to 160 ha. They raise mainly Angus and Hereford cattle, feeding them clover, ryegrass, hay, and on‑farm silage.

Their beef is supplied to JBS Swift’s premium grass‑fed brands *Little Joe* (marble score ≥ 4) and *Great Southern Pinnacle Grass Fed Beef* (marble score ≥ 2). The Mace family also grows barley for their single‑malt whisky.

> “It’s terrific to be able to say we produce some of Tasmania’s best whisky, gin, and beef,” Ross says.

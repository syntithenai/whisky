# Lawrenny Estate Distillery
Lawrenny Estate Distillery is a Tasmanian single‑malt whisky producer located on a 400‑acre estate in the Central Highlands. The distillery grows its own barley, uses estate water for mashing and fermentation, and offers a range of spirits including award‑winning gins, vodka and liqueur.

- **Location**: Upper Derwent River, Tasmania
- **Core product**: Estate single‑malt whisky (paddock‑to‑bottle)
- **Other spirits**: Van Diemen’s Gin, 1818 Settlers Gin, Highlands Gin, Meadowbank Pink Gin, Saint Clair Vodka, Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur
- **Visitor experience**: Cellar door tastings and tours
- **Key figure**: Joe Dinsmoor, Head Distiller

- **Location**: 400 acre property in Tasmania's Central Highlands, just over an hour's drive northwest of Hobart.

## Key Facts
- Established as one of only a few 'paddock to bottle' Estate single malt whiskies in the world.
- Located on a 400-acre property in Tasmania's Central Highlands, just over an hour's drive northwest of Hobart.
- Barley grains are grown, harvested, mashed, fermented, and distilled using pure waters from the Estate.

## Production Signals
- **Barley**: Grown and harvested on-site at Lawrenny Estate.
- **Distillation**: Pure Tasmanian spring water used in the distillation process.
- **Maturation**: Whisky aged in oak casks, with aging barrels sourced from reputable suppliers.

## Commercial Signals
- Award-winning single malt whisky recognized for its unique flavor profile and quality.
- Limited release series (e.g., Descension and Ascension) generating buzz among whisky enthusiasts.
- Strong social media presence building a loyal following.

## Risks / Unknowns
- **Limited Production**: As a new distillery, the scale of production may be limited initially.
- **Market Competition**: Entering a highly competitive single malt whisky market in Australia and globally.
- **Supply Chain**: Dependence on local suppliers for barley grains and other raw materials.

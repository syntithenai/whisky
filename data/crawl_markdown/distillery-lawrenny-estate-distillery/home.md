# Lawrenny Estate Distillery
Lawrenny Estate Distillery is a Tasmanian single‑malt whisky producer located on a 400‑acre estate in the Central Highlands. The distillery grows its own barley, uses water from the Upper Derwent River, and offers a range of whiskies, gins, vodka and liqueurs.

## Key Points
- **Location**: Ouse, Tasmania – 1 h drive from Hobart via the Derwent Valley.
- **Estate‑to‑Bottle**: Barley is grown on site, then mashed, fermented and distilled on the estate.
- **Products**: Single‑malt whisky (Ascension & Descension releases), award‑winning gins, Saint Clair vodka, cold‑brew coffee liqueur, and more.
- **Visitor Experience**: Cellar door tasting room open daily 10 am–5 pm.
- **Contact**: 6485 Lyell Highway, Ouse; phone +61 438 966 020.

## History & Vision
Head Distiller Joe Dinsmoor emphasizes creative freedom in spirit production. The distillery has been highlighted in media for its quality and partnership with Instagram.

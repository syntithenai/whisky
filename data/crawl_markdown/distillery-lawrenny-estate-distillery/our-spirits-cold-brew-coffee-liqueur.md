# Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur
Lawrenny Estate Distillery creates a honey‑infused cold brew liqueur using premium coffee beans sourced globally and Tasmanian water. The blend is sweetened with local bush and meadow honey, toasted wattle seed, vanilla bean, nutmeg, cinnamon, and cocoa, delivering a velvety, honeyed mocha profile.

**Key Botanicals**: Cold brew coffee, local honey, vanilla bean, toasted wattleseed, nutmeg, cinnamon, cocoa.

Enjoy it over ice, chilled as a shot, or mixed into cocktails. [Shop now](https://www.lawrenny.com/our-spirits/cold-brew-coffee-liqueur).

# Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky – Lawrenny Estate Distillery
Lawrenny’s flagship single‑malt is crafted from locally sourced barley, pure Derwent Valley water and a blend of yeast strains. The spirit is matured in a combination of ex‑bourbon, port and sherry casks, with the bondstore designed to maintain stable temperatures. The distillery emphasizes control over every step—from malting to cask selection—to produce what they claim will be among the world’s finest whiskies.

## Key Elements
- **Barley**: Tasmanian varieties, custom‑malted for each release.
- **Water**: Pure water from Australia’s deepest freshwater lake.
- **Yeast**: A mix of distiller’s, saison and wheat beer yeasts, plus experimental barley‑wine strains.
- **Casks**: Sourced globally (South Australian fortified wine, Kentucky bourbon) and cooperage‑handcrafted.
- **Maturation**: Triple‑cask system in a timber bondstore with passive ventilation.

## Product Highlights
- *Homestead* – triple‑cask matured release (ex‑bourbon, port, sherry).
- Future releases will focus on core Tasmanian and Estate single malts, with occasional special editions.

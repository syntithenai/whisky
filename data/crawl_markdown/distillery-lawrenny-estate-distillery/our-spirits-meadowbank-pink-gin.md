# Meadowbank Pink Gin

Meadowbank Pink Gin is a contemporary, all‑natural gin from Lawrenny Estate Distillery in Tasmania. It features a vibrant pink hue derived from fresh local raspberries, strawberries and hibiscus flower. The botanicals include blood orange, coriander leaf, lemon thyme, raspberries, strawberries and hibiscus, giving an herbal, fresh profile with bright citrus notes and a cooling finish. Ideal for warm summer afternoons, it is best served with Indian tonic water and a slice of orange.

## Key facts
- **Style:** Contemporary
- **Profile:** Herbal, Fresh
- **Key botanicals:** Blood Orange, Coriander Leaf, Lemon Thyme, Raspberries, Strawberries, Hibiscus
- **Serving suggestion:** Indian tonic water with an orange slice

**Tasmanian Whisky Distillers Head to Hong Kong**

Nine Tasmanian distilleries—including Lawrenny Estate Distilling—will attend the Hong Kong International Wine and Spirits Fair (Oct 30‑Nov 15) to showcase Tasmania’s whisky industry. The event aims to open Asian markets, especially China, for premium single‑malt products.

Key voices:
- **Derek Charge** (Hellyers Road Distillery CEO) stresses the untapped Chinese market and the potential to rival Australian wine.
- **Kristy Lark‑Booth** (Tasmanian Whisky & Spirits Association President) highlights Tasmania’s climate, clean water, barley and passionate makers as reasons for premium quality.
- **Guy Barnett** (Trade Minister) and **David Morris** (ACBC Tasmania CEO) underline government support and strategic export push.

The initiative is part of a broader effort by the Australia‑China Business Council to position Tasmania as a premium craft‑spirit destination.

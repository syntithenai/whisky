# 1818 Settlers Gin

- **Distillery**: Lawrenny Estate Distillery, Tasmania.
- **Style**: Distiller’s Strength gin.
- **Awards**: Double Gold (2019 San Francisco World Spirits Competition), Silver (2018 Australian Distilled Spirits Awards & American Distillers Awards).
- **Flavor profile**: Spice, herbal, earthy, smoke.
- **Key botanicals**: Caraway seed, brown cardamom (smoke), elderberry, apple, rosemary.
- **Serving suggestion**: Mediterranean tonic water with a slice of orange.

The gin is presented as a tribute to early Tasmanian settlers, embodying strength and resilience.

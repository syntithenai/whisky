# Lawrenny Van Diemen's Gin
Lawrenny Van Diemen’s Gin is a contemporary gin crafted at the Tasmanian estate using a high‑quality base spirit, local botanicals (strawberries, almonds, lime flowers, fennel, vanilla, citrus) and pure River Derwent water. It won Gold at the 2019 San Francisco World Spirits Competition and Gold in 2018 Australian Distilled Spirits Awards.

**Profile** – fruity, herbal, pillowy with a bright spring‑day character. **Serving suggestion** – Mediterranean tonic water with a strawberry cut.

The gin is part of Lawrenny’s broader spirit range including 1818 Settlers Gin, Highlands Gin, Meadowbank Pink Gin and Saint Clair Vodka.

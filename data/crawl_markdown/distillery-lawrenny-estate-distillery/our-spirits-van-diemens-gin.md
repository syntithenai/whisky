# Lawrenny Van Diemen's Gin

Lawrenny Estate Distillery presents **Lawrenny Van Diemen's Gin**, a contemporary gin infused with traditional and progressive botanicals, including strawberries, vanilla, fennel, lime flower, dried & fresh citrus. The gin reflects the natural freshness of Tasmania's inspiring land.

## Key Facts
- Inspired by Lawrenny Estate surroundings using locally grown botanicals like strawberries, almonds, and lime flowers
- Balanced union of bold Juniper, refined herbal notes, and fresh citrus
- Gold winner at 2019 San Francisco World Spirits Competition and 2018 Australian Distilled Spirits Awards

## Production Signals

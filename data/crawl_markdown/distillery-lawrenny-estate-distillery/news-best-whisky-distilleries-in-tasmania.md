# Lawrenny Estate Distillery Featured in Broadsheet’s Best Whisky Distilleries in Tasmania

Lawrenny Estate Distillery is highlighted as one of Tasmania’s top whisky producers in a Broadsheet article. The page outlines the history of Tasmanian whisky, noting its revival after the 1839 Distillation Prohibition Act and the pivotal role of Bill and Lyn Lark in re‑establishing distilling on the island. It lists Lawrenny’s product range (whisky, gin, vodka, liqueur) and provides practical visitor information for the cellar door.

---

## Key Points
- Tasmania has over 50 whisky producers, including single malt, rye, corn and oat varieties.
- The industry began in the early 1820s but was halted by the Distillation Prohibition Act of 1839.
- Bill & Lyn Lark revived distilling in the early 1990s, securing Tasmania’s first modern licence after 150 years.
- Lawrenny offers a range of spirits: Homestead Estate Whisky (Past Releases – Descension & Ascension), Van Diemen’s Gin, 1818 Settlers Gin, Highlands Gin, Meadowbank Pink Gin, Saint Clair Vodka, Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur.
- The cellar door is open daily from 10 am to 5 pm at 6485 Lyell Highway, Ouse.

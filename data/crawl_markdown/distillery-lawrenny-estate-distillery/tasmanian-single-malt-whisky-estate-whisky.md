# Estate Whisky – Lawrenny Estate Distillery

Lawrenny Estate Distillery, located in Tasmania’s Central Highlands, is preparing its first Estate whisky for release in 2025. The distillery benefits from a unique supply of pure water sourced directly from Lake Saint Clair, Australia’s deepest freshwater lake, and the region’s climate—cold Antarctic winds in winter and dry, warm summers—creates ideal conditions for barrel maturation.

The estate has a long history of livestock farming on its 200‑year‑old land. The distillery’s head distiller, **Joe Dinsmoor**, emphasizes the freedom to craft the spirit with full control over ingredients.

Lawrenny also offers other spirits such as Van Diemen’s Gin, 1818 Settlers Gin, Highlands Gin, Meadowbank Pink Gin, Saint Clair Vodka, and Cold Brew Coffee Liqueur. The distillery operates a cellar door at 6485 Lyell Highway, Ouse, Tasmania.

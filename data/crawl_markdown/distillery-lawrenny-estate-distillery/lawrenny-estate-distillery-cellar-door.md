# Lawrenny Estate Distillery Cellar Door

- **Location**: 88 km from Hobart on a historic farming estate in Tasmania’s Central Highlands.
- **Experience**: Visitors drive past fields and the Upper River Derwent, then enter the working distillery to see the paddock‑to‑bottle process. Tastings are offered and whisky can be purchased directly.
- **Hours**: Open daily 10 am–5 pm (previously 4 pm).
- **Contact**: Phone +61 438 966 020, email via form on site.

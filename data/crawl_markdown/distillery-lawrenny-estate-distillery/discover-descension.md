# Lawrenny Descension Release
Lawrenny Estate Distillery’s limited‑run **Descension** is a 10‑year‑old Tasmanian single malt finished in 15‑year‑old Boal Madeira casks after three years of ageing in 100 L ex‑Port and Bourbon casks. The whisky is crafted from local barley, River Derwent water, and a blend of Saison, Ale and Distiller’s yeasts, fermented at low temperature for nuanced flavours.

**Tasting notes** – warm honey roasted peanuts, English toffee, herbal sage, wet stone, coconut, white pepper, marzipan, blackberry, treacle, clove, vanilla, toasted oak, Madeira‑derived cocoa powder and a long finish of vanilla bean and white pepper.

The spirit is bottled at 46.6 % ABV, unchill‑filtered, and presented in deep polished copper with a bright marigold rim.

## Source Excerpts
- Barrels were rested for a further year to complete maturation before being united together for its final finishing in 10 year old Boal Madeira casks over twelve months.
- Ascension has a link to the heart of this release, with the same 20L casks used at the start of its maturation being sent to the cooperage and rejuvenated.
- After three years, the whisky was placed into smaller bourbon and port casks, each with specific toasting and charring levels, imparting subtle nuances throughout the profile.
- A lengthy finish of vanilla, toasted oak and the unique fortified lift of the Madeira influence creates an enticing single malt whisky.
- The finish is dedicated to the unique notes of the Madeira finishing casks with herbal sage and cocoa powder intertwining with the vanilla bean and white pepper of the oak.
- Sweet ex-Bourbon casks and decadent ex-Port casks were selected to assure that the profile of Descension was akin to its sister release.

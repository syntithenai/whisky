# Highlands Gin
Highlands Gin is a contemporary, crisp gin from Lawrenny Estate Distillery in Tasmania’s Central Highlands. It features botanicals such as blue cypress needle, grapefruit, fresh mint, peppermint (dried), bitter orange peel, buchu, chamomile and grains of paradise pepper. The flavour profile is described as fresh, refreshing, crisp with a bright fruitiness and an ever‑present mint note that lingers on the palate.

**Key facts**
- Awarded Silver at the 2019 Australian Gin Awards.
- Suggested serving: Indian tonic water with a slice of pink grapefruit.
- Available for purchase online via the Lawrenny shop.

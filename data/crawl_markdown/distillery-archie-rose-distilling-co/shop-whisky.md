# Archie Rose Distilling Co.

- Australian award-winning whisky distillery based in Sydney, NSW
- Specializes in Single Malt Whisky, Rye Malt Whisky, and Limited Edition Small Batch whiskies
- Uses locally sourced ingredients including specialty roasted malts, historic Australian barley and rye varieties, cold-distilled botanicals, and individually distilled malt whisky streams

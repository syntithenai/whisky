# Archie Rose Distilling Co.

## Key Facts
- Small-batch Australian whisky distillery based in Rosebery, NSW
- Founded by Archie Rose in 2019
- Focus on single malt whiskies using local ingredients
- Limited production runs of 1,000 to 2,500 bottles per release
- Aged in American oak bourbon barrels for 3-5 years

## Production Signals

- Use of locally sourced Tasmanian peat and barley

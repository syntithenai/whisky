# HMRC App Guidance

The page explains how to download and use the HMRC mobile app for accessing personal tax information, National Insurance details, benefits forecasts, and making payments. It lists features such as checking tax codes, UTR numbers, Self‑Assessment balances, Child Benefit, State Pension forecasts, and setting payment reminders. The guide also covers sign‑in methods (PIN, fingerprint, facial recognition), accessibility options, and recent updates to the app’s functionality.

---

## Key Points
- **Download**: Available on Android and iOS stores.
- **Features**: View tax code, NI number, UTR, Self‑Assessment status, benefits, make payments, set reminders, use digital assistant.
- **Sign‑in**: 6‑digit PIN, fingerprint or facial recognition.
- **Accessibility**: Separate Android and iOS accessibility statements.
- **Updates**: Regular updates adding new features (e.g., payment options, electronic contact preferences).

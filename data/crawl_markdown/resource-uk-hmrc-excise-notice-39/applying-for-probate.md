# Applying for Probate

This GOV.UK guide explains what probate is, when it is needed, who can apply, the steps to take before and after applying, fees, and related legal matters such as Inheritance Tax.

Key points:
- **Probate** gives a person the legal right to manage an estate after death.
- You must check whether probate is required, who is eligible to apply (executors or closest relative), and whether any Inheritance Tax is due.
- The guide outlines the step‑by‑step process: register the death, inform government, arrange funeral, assess benefits, value the estate, pay tax if necessary, then apply for probate.
- Contact details for the Courts and Tribunals Service Centre are provided for assistance.

The page also includes navigation links to related services such as bereavement support, visa rules, and estate valuation guidance.

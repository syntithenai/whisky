# Alcohol Duty Rates – GOV.UK

This page lists the current duty rates for alcoholic beverages in the UK, expressed as pounds (£) per litre of pure alcohol. The rates are grouped by product type (beer, cider, sparkling cider, spirits, wine, other fermented products) and by alcohol‑by‑volume (ABV) ranges. Separate tables provide rates for draught products.

Key points:
- Duty is zero for drinks with 0–1.2 % ABV.
- Rates increase progressively up to 33.99 £ per litre for beverages stronger than 22 % ABV.
- Draught products have lower rates, e.g., 8.58 £ for 1.3–<3.5 % ABV and 19.45 £ for 3.5–<8.5 % ABV.
- The page is updated regularly; the latest update was on 1 Feb 2026.

Links to related guidance (e.g., Small Producer Relief, how to calculate duty) are provided.

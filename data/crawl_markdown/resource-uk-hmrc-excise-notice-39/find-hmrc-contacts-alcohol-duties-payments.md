# Alcohol duties: payment enquiries

This GOV.UK page provides contact details for HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) regarding the payment and return of alcohol duties in the UK. It lists telephone numbers, opening hours, and mailing addresses for Beer Duty, Wine & Cider Duty, and general alcohol duty queries. The page also references related guidance such as the Spirit Drinks Verification Scheme fee and excise warehousing enquiries.

## Key contact points
- **Beer Duty**: 03000 583 920 (Mon‑Fri 9am‑4pm)
- **Wine & Cider Duty**: 03000 592 688 (Mon‑Fri 8:30am‑4pm)
- **Direct Debit for Beer Duty**: 03000 583 920
- **Mailing address for Beer Duty returns**: HMRC, BX9 1XE
- **Mailing address for Wine & Cider Duty returns**: HMRC, BX9 1XL

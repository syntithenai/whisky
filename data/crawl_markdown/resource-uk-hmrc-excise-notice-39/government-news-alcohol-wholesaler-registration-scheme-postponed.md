# Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme postponed
The UK government announced that the online registration service for alcohol wholesalers will now open on **1 January 2016** instead of the originally planned 1 October 2015, due to technical issues uncovered during pre‑launch testing. The scheme aims to reduce illicit alcohol sales by requiring traders to pass rigorous checks and, from 2017, allowing retailers to verify supplier approval.

---
*Launch dates:* 1 January 2016 (full launch), 1 October 2015 (initial test period).
*Purpose:* Reduce risk of duty‑free illicit alcohol entering the market.
*Future feature:* Retailers can check supplier status in 2017.

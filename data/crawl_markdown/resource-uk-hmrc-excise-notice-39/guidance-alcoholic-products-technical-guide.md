# Alcoholic Products Technical Guide

This guide from HM Revenue & Customs explains the legal framework for producing, storing, and accounting for duty on alcoholic products in the UK. It covers:
- Definitions and classification of alcoholic products.
- Production requirements and approval processes.
- Duty calculation, payment, and relief schemes (e.g., draught relief, small‑producer relief).
- Record‑keeping, financial security, and changes of ownership.
- Handling of duty suspension, samples, domestic consumption, and exports.
- Special provisions for diplomats, visiting forces, and HM ships.
- Review, appeal procedures, and the due‑diligence condition.

The guide is intended for commercial producers, packagers, importers, and exporters.

# Alcoholic Products Technical Guide

This guide, issued by HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC), explains the legal framework for producing, storing, and accounting for duty on alcoholic products in the UK. It is aimed at commercial producers, packagers, importers, and exporters.

Key sections cover:
- Definitions and classification of alcoholic products
- Production requirements and approval processes
- Duty rates, liability, and payment procedures
- Financial security guarantees
- Record‑keeping obligations
- Duty suspension and removal from approved premises
- Strength measurement and related disputes
- Reliefs for draught, small producers, samples, domestic consumption, and diplomatic supplies
- Import/export rules and co‑storage
- VAT accounting on duty‑suspended goods
- Review and appeal procedures
- Glossary of terms

The guide is regularly updated; the latest version was published 11 Dec 2024 and updated 1 Apr 2026.

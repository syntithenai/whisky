# UK HMRC - Excise Notice 39: Alcoholic Products Technical Guide

## Key Facts
- Guidance for commercial producers, packagers, importers and exporters of alcoholic products in the UK.
- Covers production, storage, accounting for duty on alcoholic products.
- Includes definitions, classifications, rates, approvals, returns, financial security, records, suspension, strength, relief schemes, removals, VAT, review procedures, rights and obligations.

## Production Signals
- Detailed rules on what constitutes 'production' of alcoholic products (Section 3).
- Requirements for new producers to obtain approval (Section 4).
- Approval process includes proving fitness and propriety (Section 4).

## Commercial Signals
- Information on duty liability, rates, accounting periods (Sections 7 & 8).
- Guidance on financial security requirements (Section 8).
- Procedures for transferring ownership of production business (Section 6).

## Risks/Unknowns
- Complex definitions and classifications impacting duty calculations (Section 2).
- Changes in legislation may affect existing operations without notice.
- Ensuring compliance with due diligence condition of approval (Section 20).

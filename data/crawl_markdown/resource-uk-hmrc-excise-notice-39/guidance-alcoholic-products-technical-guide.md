# Alcoholic Products Technical Guide

This UK HMRC guidance explains the legal framework for producing, storing, and accounting for duty on alcoholic products. It is aimed at commercial producers, packagers, importers, and exporters.

Key sections cover:
- Definitions and classification of alcoholic products (Section 2)
- Production requirements and approval processes (Sections 3‑4)
- Duty returns, payments, and exemptions (Sections 5‑7)
- Financial security and guarantees (Section 8)
- Record‑keeping obligations (Section 9)
- Duty suspension and storage rules (Section 10)
- Strength measurement and disputes (Section 11)
- Reliefs for draught and small producers (Section 12)
- Measurement of quantity, losses, and spoilt goods (Sections 13‑15)
- Sample duty rules (Section 16)
- Domestic consumption, vine leasing, and diplomatic supplies (Sections 17‑19)
- Due diligence, imports, exports, co‑storage, and removal procedures (Sections 20‑26)
- VAT accounting, review/appeal processes, rights & obligations (Sections 27‑29)
- Glossary of terms (Section 30).

The guide is updated regularly; the latest version was released on 1 April 2026.

# GOV.UK App

The GOV.UK app is a free mobile application for iPhone and Android that lets users access government information and services directly on their phone. It allows you to:
- Save topics to the home screen.
- Keep a history of visited pages.
- Find local council links by entering your postcode.
- Receive notifications about deadlines, important dates and useful services.

The app is available for iOS 16+ (minimum 24.8 MB) and Android 10+ (minimum 8.4 MB). It is a beta release that receives regular updates based on user feedback.

It is distinct from other government apps such as the GOV.UK One Login, HMRC app, UK Immigration ID Check, etc.

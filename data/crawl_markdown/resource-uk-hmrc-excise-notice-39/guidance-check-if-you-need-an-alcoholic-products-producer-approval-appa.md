# Check if you need an alcoholic products producer approval (APPA)

This GOV.UK guidance explains that anyone who wants to produce or alter alcoholic beverages in the UK must obtain an **Alcoholic Products Producer Approval (APPA)** from HM Revenue & Customs. The page details:
- Who is already registered and how they will be migrated to a single APPA.
- Which activities require approval, including making alcohol from raw ingredients, changing ABV, re‑classifying products, or producing alternative products.
- Exemptions such as domestic use (excluding spirits) and research/experimental purposes.
- How the approval works, what premises and product categories can be covered, and how to apply.
- Contact details for follow‑up if a letter confirming the new APPA has not arrived by 25 Feb 2025.

The guidance is updated regularly (last on 1 Mar 2025) and links to the full **Alcoholic Products Technical Guide** for detailed definitions and procedures.

# Cancel your alcoholic products producer approval (APPA)

This GOV.UK guidance explains how a business can withdraw its APPA. It lists the information required, the online and postal options for submitting a cancellation request, and the expected review time.

## Key points
- **What you need**: APPA ID, UTR, reason for canceling, contact details.
- **How to submit**: Online via Government Gateway or by post if unable to use the form.
- **Processing time**: 45 working days (may be longer if more information is required).

The page also notes recent updates and provides links to related alcohol‑duty resources.

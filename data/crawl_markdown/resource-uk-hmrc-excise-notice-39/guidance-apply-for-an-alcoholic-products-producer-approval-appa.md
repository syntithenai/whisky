# Apply for an Alcoholic Products Producer Approval (APPA)

This GOV.UK guidance explains how to obtain an APPA from HM Revenue & Customs, the legal requirement for producing alcoholic products in the UK. It covers:
- When and why you need to apply.
- The information required (business details, premises plan, production estimates).
- How to submit the application online or by paper.
- What happens after submission: background checks, possible site visits, approval conditions.
- Procedures for withdrawing, changing, cancelling or appealing an APPA decision.

The page also lists recent updates and links to related resources such as the Alcohol Duty detailed information collection.

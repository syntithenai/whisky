# UK HMRC – Alcoholic Products Producer Approval (APPA)

This page explains who must obtain an APPA from the UK’s HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) and how to apply. It covers:
- The definition of alcohol production, including distilling, brewing, winemaking and other alcoholic products.
- Exempt activities such as domestic use or research.
- When a licence is required for cider makers.
- How the APPA works – one licence per producer covering all categories and sites.
- Steps to apply and what happens if you already have a licence before 1 September 2025.

The page also lists recent updates, links to related guidance, and contact details for HMRC.

# Cancel Your Alcoholic Products Producer Approval (APPA)

This page explains how to cancel an APPA licence in Wales. It outlines the required information, the online form process, and alternative methods if the online form cannot be used. The guidance is updated regularly and includes a Welsh translation.

- **Purpose**: Notify HMRC of your intention to cancel your APPA.
- **Key steps**:
  1. Log in with Government Gateway credentials.
  2. Provide your APPA ID, UTR, business details, reason for cancellation, and contact information.
  3. Submit the form online or send a letter if you cannot use the online service.
- **Processing time**: Typically within 45 working days after receipt of the application.

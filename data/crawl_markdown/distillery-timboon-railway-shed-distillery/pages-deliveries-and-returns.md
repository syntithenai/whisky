# Deliveries and Returns

Timboon Railway Shed Distillery offers complimentary shipping for orders over \$150 within Australia; smaller orders incur a flat fee (\$15 standard, \$20 express). Domestic delivery takes 3–7 working days. International shipments include tracking/insurance, require proof of age, and may incur customs duties. Prices are in AUD with GST included. Refunds are accepted within 14 days for unopened items (excluding gift cards and sale items); returns must be shipped back at the customer's expense. Exchanges and gift credits are handled via email.

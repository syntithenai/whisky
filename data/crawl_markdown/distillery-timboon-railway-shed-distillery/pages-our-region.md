# Timboon Railway Shed Distillery – Our Region
Timboon is a quiet town just 15 km inland from the Great Ocean Road, known for its food‑and‑drink culture and strong community of makers. The distillery is part of the **12 Apostles Food Artisans** collective, which showcases local producers such as Timboon Fine Ice Cream and other artisan businesses.

The region blends coastal scenery with rolling inland landscapes, offering walking trails, cycling routes, and a range of premium spirits, cheeses, and ice cream. The distillery acknowledges the Traditional Custodians (Eastern Maar) and promotes responsible alcohol service under Victoria’s Liquor Control Reform Act 1998.

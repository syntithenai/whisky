# Timboon Railway Shed Distillery

- Historic railway shed turned distillery in Timboon, Victoria.
- Offers guided tastings, bottle sales and a small artisanal food menu.
- Features an *Aussie Craft Spirits Wall* with over 185 spirits from 90+ distilleries.
- Guided tour “Nip of Knowledge” (60 min) includes stills, cooperage, bond store and a tasting.
- Open 7 days a week, 11 am‑5 pm. Located beside the Timboon Rail Trail.

## Long Description
# Timboon Railway Shed Distillery

**Location & Heritage**
- Family‑owned distillery in southwest Victoria, housed in a historic railway shed on Bailey St, Timboon VIC 3268.
- Founded in 2007 after a legacy of illicit bush stills; the modern operation is fully licensed (Liquor Licence #31952462).

**Core Products**
- **Whisky Line** – single‑malt whiskies: *Port Expression*, *Christie’s Cut*, *Tom’s Surrender* and *SMOKY 1881* (all 500 ml, prices $160–$225).  Some are also available in 200 ml.
- **Gin** – *12 Apostles Gin* (200 ml, 42% ABV) with a citrus‑root profile of juniper, lime, orange, lemon myrtle, orris root and ginger.
- **Other Spirits & Liqueurs** – vodka, liqueurs such as Jim Love’s Strawberry Schnapps and Baxter’s Coffee Cream.

**Maturation & Equipment**
- Uses large ex‑fortified wine barrels (Port or Sherry) for whisky maturation.
- Distillation performed in copper pot stills; cooperage supplied by ABeckett’s Creek Cooperage.

**Visitor Experience**
- Guided tours, tastings and seasonal releases are available.  Online booking is possible via the website.

**Commerce & Payment**
- Products sold online with multiple payment options: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Mastercard, Visa, PayPal, Union Pay, etc.
- Age‑gate verification present for all alcohol purchases.

**People**
- *Josh Walker* – Head Distiller/Owner.
- *Charlie Johnson* – Brand Ambassador.

**Key Themes**
- Small‑batch craft spirit production.
- Heritage of illicit distilling in Victoria.
- Family‑owned, community‑focused operation.

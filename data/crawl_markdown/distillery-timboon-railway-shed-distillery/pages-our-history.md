# Timboon Railway Shed Distillery – History
Timboon Railway Shed Distillery traces its roots to the illicit distilling era of late‑19th century Victoria, where bush stills produced a smooth spirit known as *Mountain Dew*. The legend of Tom Delaney and detective John Mitchell Christie is celebrated in the brand’s story. Today the distillery operates openly, legally, and proudly, drawing inspiration from that rebellious past across its whisky range and liqueur offerings.

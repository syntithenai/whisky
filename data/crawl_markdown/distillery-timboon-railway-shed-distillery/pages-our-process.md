# Our Process
Timboon Railway Shed Distillery crafts single‑malt whisky and small‑batch spirits on site since 2007, using malted barley as the sole grain. The distillery expanded in 2020 with a second still and its own cooperage, ABeckett’s Creek Cooperage, where barrels are hand‑charred in American and French oak (Port and Sherry casks). Spirits are matured mainly in large ex‑fortified wine barrels to develop depth and individuality. The portfolio also includes liqueurs such as Baxter’s Coffee Cream, Jim Love’s Strawberry Schnapps, Bridget’s Limoncello, Salted Caramel Whisky Liqueur, Railyard Rum, and 12 Apostles Australian Gin.

The distillery operates from Bailey St, Timboon VIC, open daily 11 am‑5 pm. It adheres to responsible drinking guidelines under the Liquor Control Reform Act 1998.

## Source Excerpts
- our stills Our stills produce a sweet, balanced new make spirit that we mature predominantly in large-format ex-fortified wine barrels, including Port and Sherry casks in both American and French oak.
- These barrels are prepared and heavily charred by hand at ABeckett’s Creek Cooperage, located beside our bond store.
- It allows us to shape flavour from the cask up, building the richness, depth and individuality that define Timboon whisky.
- honouring traditions At Timboon Railway Shed Distillery, we make true single malt whisky using malted barley as our only grain ingredient, processed on site in our mashing shed.
- Inspired by the great single malts of Scotland and the Isles, our whisky is crafted to the same time-honoured traditions — with patience, precision and a deep respect for the process.
- In 2020, we expanded our production with the installation of a second, larger still and the establishment of our own cooperage, ABeckett’s Creek Cooperage, right here in Timboon.

# Bushmills 10‑Year Single Malt

- **Age**: Minimum 10 years.
- **Distillation**: Triple‑distilled from 100 % malted barley.
- **Maturation**: Former sherry casks + bourbon‑seasoned casks.
- **ABV**: 40 % (80 proof).
- **Price**: $58.99 (US). Free shipping on orders over $150.
- **Tasting notes**: Honey, vanilla, milk chocolate aroma; velvety palate of mocha, dried apricot, toasty oak; crisp, long finish with subtle oak and fruit.
- **Awards**: 2024 International Spirits Challenge Gold, 2024 International Whisky Competition Gold, 85.36 pts, 2007 World Whiskies Awards.

Available for purchase on the Bushmills website (US only).

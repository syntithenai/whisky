# Bank Shot

A simple Irish cocktail that reimagines the classic Paloma using Bushmills® Original whiskey, grapefruit juice and club soda (or Fresca). Serve over ice with a lime wedge garnish.

## Ingredients
- 1 ½ parts Bushmills® Original
- 1 ½ parts grapefruit juice
- 1 ½ parts club soda or Fresca
- Lime wedge for garnish

## Directions
Add grapefruit juice, ice and Bushmills® Original to a glass. Top with club soda (or Fresca), stir gently from bottom to top, and garnish with a lime wedge.

---

**Bushmills® Original** – 40 % alc./vol. (80 proof).

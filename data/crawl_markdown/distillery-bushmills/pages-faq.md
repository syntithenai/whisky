# Bushmills FAQ Overview

The FAQ page explains key aspects of Bushmills Irish whiskey, including product differences (Original, Black Bush, Single Malts), shipping and delivery policies, distillery location, tasting recommendations, storage tips, and the company’s heritage. It also covers cocktail suggestions, golf partnerships, and general information about Irish vs. Scotch whisky.

---

## Key Points
- **Products**: Original (blended), Black Bush (high‑malt blended, sherry & bourbon casks), Single Malts (10–21 YO, triple‑distilled). 
- **Shipping**: US only; excludes AL, AK, AR, HI, MA, MI, MS, TN, UT. Free shipping over $150.
- **Distillery**: 2 Distillery Road, Bushmills, Co Antrim BT57 8XH – oldest licensed distillery (1608).
- **Tasting & Storage**: Neat or with water/ice; store upright in cool dark place, consume within 1–2 years after opening.
- **Irish vs. Scotch**: Irish is triple‑distilled, sweeter; Scotch usually double‑distilled, peated.
- **Cocktails**: Classic Irish Coffee, Oldest Fashioned, Irish Highball, etc.
- **Golf partnership**: PGA Tour collaboration and exclusive events.

# The Oldest Fashioned
A classic Irish whiskey cocktail featuring Bushmills® Original, honey syrup, orange bitters, and a Luxardo cherry garnish.

**Ingredients**
- 1 part Bushmills® Original
- ¼ part honey syrup
- Few dashes of orange bitters (and agave)
- Garnish: orange peel & Luxardo cherry

**Directions**
Stir ingredients with ice, strain into a rocks glass over large ice cubes, garnish.

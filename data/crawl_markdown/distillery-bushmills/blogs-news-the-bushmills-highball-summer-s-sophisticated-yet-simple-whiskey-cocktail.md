# Bushmills Highball
A two‑ingredient summer cocktail featuring **Bushmills Original Irish Whiskey** and club soda. The article explains the drink’s historical roots, its modern resurgence as a light, refreshing option, and how the whiskey’s vanilla, honey, and spice notes are amplified by carbonation and citrus accents.

## Key Points
- Classic Highball: whiskey + soda, minimal garnish.
- Uses Bushmills Original (blended) or 10‑Year Single Malt for variation.
- Highlights balance, freshness, lightness, and versatility.

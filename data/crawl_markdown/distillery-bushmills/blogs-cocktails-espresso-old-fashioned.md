# Espresso Old Fashioned – Bushmills® Irish Whiskey

A modern twist on the classic Old Fashioned that pairs **Bushmills® Original** with fresh espresso, coffee liqueur and a touch of cinnamon syrup. The recipe calls for:
- 2 parts Bushmills® Original
- 1 part Coffee Liqueur
- 1 part Fresh Espresso
- ½ part Cinnamon Syrup
- 2 dashes Walnut Bitters

Shake with ice, strain into a glass, let sit for a minute and garnish with coffee beans. The cocktail is marketed as a sophisticated night‑cap.

# Malbon Mule
A cocktail recipe featuring Bushmills® Black Bush whiskey, ginger beer and lemonade, garnished with a lemon wheel.

**Ingredients**
- 2 parts Bushmills® Black Bush
- 2 parts ginger beer
- 2 parts lemonade
- Lemon wheel for garnish

**Directions**
Combine the whiskey, ginger beer and lemon juice in a glass. Add ice, stir and garnish with a lemon wheel.

The page is part of Bushmills’ collaboration with Malbon, promoting golf‑themed cocktails.

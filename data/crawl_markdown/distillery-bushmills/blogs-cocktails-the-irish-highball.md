# The Irish Highball
A simple, refreshing cocktail featuring **Bushmills® Original** whiskey and club soda, garnished with a dehydrated lemon wheel and thyme.

## Ingredients
- 2 parts Bushmills® Original (40% Alc./Vol.)
- 5 parts club soda
- Garnish: dehydrated lemon wheel + thyme

## Directions
1. Fill a glass with ice.
2. Pour the whiskey over the ice.
3. Top with club soda and stir gently with a long‑handled bar spoon.
4. Garnish as described.

*Optional:* Use Bushmills® 10 Year Old Single Malt for extra complexity.

# The Tea Off – Bushmills® Irish Whiskey

A simple, refreshing cocktail featuring **Bushmills® Original**, fresh lemonade and iced tea, ideal for a hot summer day.

**Ingredients**
- 2 parts Bushmills® Original
- 1½ parts fresh lemonade
- 3 parts fresh iced tea
- Lemon slice (garnish)

**Directions**
Combine all ingredients in an ice‑filled highball glass, stir, garnish with a lemon slice and serve.

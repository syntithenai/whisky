# Bushmills Distillery

- **Location**: County Antrim, Ireland (2 Distillery Road, Bushmills, Co Antrim BT57 8XH)
- **History**: Oldest licensed whiskey distillery in the world, founded 1608.
- **Production**: Triple‑distilled 100 % malted barley in copper pot stills; uses local barley, River Bush water and volcanic rock terrain.
- **Products highlighted on page**: 10‑Year (US $58.99), 21‑Year (US $299.99), Black Bush (US $41.99).
- **Visitor experience**: Guided tours available; emphasis on heritage and craftsmanship.

> *“Without the village there would be no whiskey, and without the whiskey there would be no village.”*

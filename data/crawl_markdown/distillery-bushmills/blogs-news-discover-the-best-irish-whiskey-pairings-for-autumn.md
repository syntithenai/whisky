# Best Irish Whiskey Pairings For Fall – Bushmills

Bushmills presents a seasonal guide that pairs its range of single‑malt and blended whiskeys with autumn foods and cocktails. The article highlights:
- **Black Bush** (sherry‑cask finished) as a versatile neat whiskey, pairing well with caramelised onion crostini topped with brie.
- **10‑Year‑Old & 12‑Year‑Old Single Malts** for newcomers, offering honey, vanilla and milk chocolate notes that complement roasted almonds, soft cheeses, baked apples, cinnamon, brown sugar and dark chocolate bark.
- **16‑Year‑Old & 21‑Year‑Old Single Malts** as premium expressions finished in port or Madeira, ideal with roasted chestnuts, poached pears, aged cheddar or Roquefort.
- Autumn cocktails such as the Espresso Old Fashioned, Classic Irish Coffee and Irish Highball that showcase Bushmills’ smooth malt profile.

The piece encourages ordering online for delivery to enjoy these pairings at home.

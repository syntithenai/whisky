**Bushmills x Malbon Golf Capsule – A Limited‑Edition Collaboration**

- Launched July 2025, extending the 2024 partnership with Malbon Golf.
- Focus on County Antrim plaid inspired apparel and accessories for golf courses, clubhouses and home use.
- Key items: hoodies, windbreakers, vests, visors, hats, bespoke flask & luggage set, Glencairn glass set, decanter set.
- Products paired with Bushmills whiskeys (10 Year, 16 Year, 21 Year) for the 19th‑hole experience.
- Offers early access to capsule drops and exclusive whiskey tastings via subscription.

# Cold Brew Irish Coffee – Bushmills® Irish Whiskey

A modern twist on the classic Irish coffee that pairs **Bushmills Black Bush** with cold‑brew coffee, simple syrup, Irish cream liqueur and a silky layer of whipped cream. The recipe can be tweaked by swapping in the 10 Year Old Single Malt for extra complexity.

## Ingredients (per serving)
- 2 parts Bushmills Black Bush
- 4 parts cold‑brew coffee
- 0.5 part simple syrup
- Irish cream liqueur
- Freshly whipped heavy cream

## Directions
1. Pour whiskey and simple syrup over ice in a tall glass.
2. Stir well.
3. Add cold‑brew coffee, leaving about half an inch of room for the creams.
4. Top with Irish cream liqueur and finish with freshly whipped heavy cream.

> *Swap in Bushmills 10 Year Old Single Malt for additional flavor complexity.*

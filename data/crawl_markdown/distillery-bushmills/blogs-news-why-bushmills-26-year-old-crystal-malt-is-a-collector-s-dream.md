# Inside Our 26 Year Old Crystal Malt – Bushmills

Bushmills has released its **26‑year‑old Crystal Malt**, a single malt that derives much of its flavor from the barley itself rather than the cask. The grain is crystallised during malting, locking in chocolate and toffee notes before distillation. Triple‑distilled spirit is then aged for 26 years in first‑fill Kentucky bourbon barrels (Kelvin Cooperage). Bottled at 46% ABV, it comes in a limited allocation and carries a Double Gold from the San Francisco World Spirits Competition.

**Tasting notes**: radiant golden hue; nose of soft oak, caramel, chocolate; palate velvety with vanilla, malt sweetness, subtle spice; long, warming finish. Ideal neat for connoisseurs.

The release is positioned as a collector’s dream and high‑end gift, presented in a teal box with blue bottle design.

## Production & flavour insights
- Crystal malt infuses chocolate and caramel sweetness into the grain before cask ageing.
- 26 years of maturation deepens these notes while adding vanilla, oak, and subtle spice.
- The spirit is described as “velvety” on the palate with a long, warming finish.

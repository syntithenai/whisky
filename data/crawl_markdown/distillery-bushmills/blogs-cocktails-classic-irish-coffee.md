# Classic Irish Coffee

A simple, bartender‑approved recipe that uses only **Bushmills® Original** whiskey. The drink is assembled in a 6 oz Georgian Irish Coffee glass: whiskey + demerara syrup, hot Sumatra coffee, and topped with freshly whipped heavy cream.

## Ingredients
- 1 part Bushmills® Original
- ½ part Demerara Syrup
- 4 parts hot Sumatra coffee
- Freshly whipped heavy cream (top)

## Directions
1. Pour whiskey and demerara syrup into the glass.
2. Stir well.
3. Add hot Sumatra coffee, leaving a half‑inch of room for cream.
4. Top with freshly whipped heavy cream.

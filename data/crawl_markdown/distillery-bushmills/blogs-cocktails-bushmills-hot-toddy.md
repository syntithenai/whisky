# Bushmills Hot Toddy

A simple, comforting cocktail featuring **Bushmills Original Irish Whiskey** mixed with hot water, honey or sugar, lemon, cloves and cinnamon. The recipe calls for:
- 1 part Bushmills Original
- 2 parts hot water
- ¼ part simple syrup
- Ground cinnamon, whole cloves, a lemon wheel and a cinnamon stick for garnish.

The drink is presented as a warm companion for chilly evenings or when you need a soothing boost.

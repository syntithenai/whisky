# Gold Rush – Bushmills® Irish Whiskey

A bright, bold and refreshing classic cocktail featuring **Bushmills Original** with fresh lemon juice, honey syrup and mint.

## Ingredients
- 1 ½ parts Bushmills Original
- 1 part fresh lemon juice
- 1 part honey syrup
- 4 sprigs fresh mint
- Lemon wheel for garnish

## Directions
Shake all ingredients with ice for 10 s, strain over fresh ice and serve.

---

**Bushmills Original** is a 40% ABV Irish whiskey (80 proof) produced by The Old Bushmills Distillery Company Limited.

# Refund & Returns Policy – Bushmills® Irish Whiskey

Bushmills’ policy states that merchandise can be returned within 30 days if unopened, with a refund of the purchase price. Shipping costs are covered only when the return is due to an error or damage on the part of Bushmills. Alcoholic beverages cannot be returned; refunds for spirits are at the discretion of Bushmills, Accelpay, Inc., or third‑party partners.

The page also lists contact emails for merchandise and alcohol support, and includes a brief note that free shipping applies to whiskey orders over $150.

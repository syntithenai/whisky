# John Collins – Bushmills® Irish Whiskey

## Ingredients & Directions
- **1½ parts Bushmills Original**
- **1 part lemon juice**
- **1 part simple syrup**
- **2 parts club soda**
- Garnish: maraschino cherry, orange slice

Shake whiskey, lemon and syrup with ice for 10 s, strain over fresh ice, top with soda.

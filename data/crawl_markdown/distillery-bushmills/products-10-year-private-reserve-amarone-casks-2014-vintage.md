# Bushmills® 10 Year Private Reserve – Amarone Casks (2014 Vintage)

- **Age & Finish**: 10‑year single malt finished in Amarone red wine casks from Verona, Italy.
- **Maturation**: 4 years in bourbon barrels + oloroso sherry casks, then minimum 6 years in Amarone casks.
- **ABV**: 47.3 % (94.6 proof).
- **Tasting Notes**: Rich dark fruits, cinnamon, vanilla; caramelized sugar, chocolate, oak; long dry finish.
- **Price**: $58.99 (US). Free shipping on orders over $150.

> *“This 10 Year Old single malt Irish whiskey is first matured in carefully selected bourbon barrels and oloroso sherry casks before finishing in Amarone red wine casks from Verona, Italy.”*

# Three Ways to Drink Irish Whiskey Like an Expert
Bushmills explains three classic serving styles—neat, on the rocks, and highball—highlighting how each method reveals different flavor layers of their single malts and blended whiskeys. The article recommends specific expressions (16‑Year Old Single Malt, 21‑Year Old Single Malt, 12‑Year Old Single Malt, Black Bush) for each style and offers practical tips such as using large ice cubes or adding a splash of water to open the spirit.

## Key Takeaways
- **Neat**: Pure pour that showcases texture, spice, and cask influence. Ideal for aged single malts.
- **On the Rocks**: Ice softens alcohol, gradually revealing hidden aromas; best with large cubes or spheres.
- **Highball**: Balanced mix of whiskey, soda water, and a lemon twist—simple yet bright.

The piece emphasizes Bushmills’ 400‑year heritage and encourages readers to explore their range online.

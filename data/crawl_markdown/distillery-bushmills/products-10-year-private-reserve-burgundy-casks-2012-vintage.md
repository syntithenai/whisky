# Bushmills® 10 Year Private Reserve – Burgundy Casks (2012 Vintage)

- **Age & Finish**: 10‑year single malt, first matured in bourbon and oloroso sherry casks for 6 years, then finished minimum 4 years in Grand Cru Burgundy hogsheads.
- **ABV**: 47 % (94 Proof).
- **Price**: $69.99 on the official site.
- **Tasting Notes**: Soft summer fruits and aged leather on the nose; raspberry, crisp apple, black cherry; silky finish of vanilla truffle and almond.
- **Production**: Overseen by Master Distiller Colum Egan and Master Blender Alex Thomas.

> *“An elegant balance of soft summer fruits and aged leather on the nose.”*

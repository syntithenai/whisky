# Bushmills® 10 Year Private Reserve – Moscatel Casks (2013 Vintage)

- **ABV**: 48.5% (97 Proof)
- **Maturation**: 6 years in bourbon barrels & oloroso sherry casks, then minimum 4 years in Portuguese Moscatel casks.
- **Tasting notes**: gentle floral, caramelised sugar, orange blossom; vanilla, mandarin orange, peach, apricots; sweet‑fruit finish with a dry, long persistence.
- **Price**: $58.99 (US)
- **Availability**: Sold out on site; can be notified when back in stock.

The bottle is part of Bushmills’ Private Reserve collection, crafted by Master Distiller Colum Egan and Master Blender Alex Thomas.

# Bushmills® 10 Year Private Reserve – Bordeaux Casks (2012 Vintage)

- **Age & Finish**: 10‑year single malt, first matured in bourbon barrels and oloroso sherry casks for 6 years, then finished minimum 4 years in first‑fill Pomerol Bordeaux casks.
- **ABV**: 46.5 % (93 Proof).
- **Tasting Notes**: Roasted wood, red currants, ripe raspberries; palate of green apple, lemon, vanilla, nutmeg and ginger; long dry finish with lemon peel and spiciness.
- **Maturation Details**:
  - Primary: American oak bourbon barrels (Kelvin Cooperage) & Spanish oak oloroso sherry butts (Antonio Páez Lobato cooperage).
  - Secondary: French oak first‑fill red wine Bordeaux casks from Pomerol.
- **Key Personnel**: Master Distiller Colum Egan, Master Blender Alex Thomas.

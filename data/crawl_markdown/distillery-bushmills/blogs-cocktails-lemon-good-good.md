# Lemon Good‑Good
A bright, zesty highball made with **Bushmills 10 Year Old Single Malt Irish Whiskey**, ginger beer and classic lemonade. Serve over ice in a Collins glass, garnish with a lemon wheel.

**Ingredients**
- 1½ parts Bushmills 10YO Single Malt Irish Whiskey
- 2½ parts Ginger Beer
- 2½ parts Classic Lemonade

**Directions**
Build in a Collins glass over ice and garnish with a lemon wheel.

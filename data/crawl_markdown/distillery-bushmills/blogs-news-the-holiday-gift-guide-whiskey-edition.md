# The Holiday Gift Guide: Whiskey Edition
Bushmills presents a curated list of Irish whiskey gifts for collectors, beginners and enthusiasts alike. Highlights include the ultra‑rare **26 Year Old Crystal Malt** and **46 Year Old Secrets of the River Bush**, as well as approachable options like the **10 Year Old Single Malt** and **12 Year Old Single Malt**. The guide also spotlights limited‑edition Private Reserve releases finished in Burgundy and Tequila casks, and showcases age‑defined expressions such as the **25 Year Old** and **30 Year Old** single malts.

Each entry describes aroma, palate, finish, and notable awards, providing a quick reference for selecting the perfect bottle.

## Source Excerpts
- 25 Year Old Single Malt The 25 Year Old Single Malt matures in bourbon barrels and sherry butts before spending 21 years in ruby port pipes.
- 12 Year Old Single Malt Matured in sherry and bourbon casks and finished in Marsala wine casks, the 12 Year Old Single Malt won silver at the 2024 San Diego International Spirits Competition.
- 30 Year Old Single Malt A triumph of craftsmanship, the 30 Year Old Single Malt matures 14 years in bourbon and sherry casks, followed by 16 years in Pedro Ximénez sherry wood.
- 10 Year Private Reserve: Burgundy Casks 2012 Vintage The 10 Year Private Reserve starts in bourbon and sherry casks before spending four years in Grand Cru Burgundy hogsheads from Côte d’Or.
- 12 Year Private Reserve: Tequila Casks 2012 Vintage Matured first in bourbon and sherry casks, then finished for eight years in Reposado and Añejo Tequila casks, the 12 Year Private Reserve reveals a bold and intriguing character.
- The palate unfolds with caramelized cherries, succulent plums, and rich dark chocolate, layered with subtle spice, and finishes with the elegant sweetness of port and a refined touch of oak.

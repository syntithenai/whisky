**Black Fashioned – Bushmills® Irish Whiskey**

- A cocktail recipe that uses **Bushmills Black Bush**, matured in Oloroso sherry casks.
- Ingredients: 2 parts Black Bush, ¼ part agave syrup, a few dashes of orange bitters and an orange rind garnish with a dark cherry.
- Preparation: Mix over ice, stir, strain into a rocks glass with large ice cubes, garnish.

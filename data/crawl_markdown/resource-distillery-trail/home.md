# Distillery Trail

Distillery Trail is a comprehensive online resource for the craft spirits industry, offering news, events, insights, and directories of distilleries, suppliers, and educational resources.

- **Core sections**: Home, Latest Articles, BrandScape, Infographics, Videos, What Is?, Press Releases, Newsletter Signup, Contact Us, Advertise, About.
- **Directories**: Distillery Directory (by state, spirit type, map), Supplier Directory (architects, barrels, boilers, etc.).
- **Featured content**: Articles on mergers (Brown‑Forman & Pernod Ricard), acquisitions (Gallo & Four Roses), and industry events such as National Bourbon Week.
- **Educational offerings**: Moonshine University workshops, Master Distiller profiles, and brand stories.
- **Social media & newsletter**: Active presence on Facebook, X, Instagram, Pinterest; free monthly newsletter.

The site serves distillery owners, suppliers, journalists, and enthusiasts seeking up‑to‑date industry information and networking opportunities.

# About Us – RahrBSG Brewing & Winemaking Experts

RahrBSG is a family‑owned brewing ingredient supplier founded in 1847 by William Rahr. Headquartered in Shakopee, Minnesota, the company operates three world‑class malting facilities and 13 distribution centers across North America. With over 177 years of experience, RahrBSG supplies premium malt, hops, packaging materials, and other brewing essentials to breweries, distilleries, and allied industries worldwide.

Key highlights:
- **Heritage**: German immigrant William Rahr founded the Eagle Brewery in Manitowoc, Wisconsin. Five generations later, the business continues under the RahrBSG name.
- **Scale**: Shakopee malt houses produce 460,000 metric tons of malt annually, covering roughly 25 % of U.S. brewing demand.
- **Sustainability**: Koda Energy powers the malting kilns with biomass‑derived steam, eliminating natural gas use and providing renewable electricity to the facility and local grid.
- **Distribution**: 13 North American centers plus an FSSC 22000‑certified hop processing plant in the Pacific Northwest ensure quick delivery for breweries.

RahrBSG emphasizes customer service, quality, and a commitment to being a long‑term partner for brewing operations.

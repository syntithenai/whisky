# RahrBSG – Brewing, Distilling & Winemaking Supplies
RahrBSG is a North‑American supplier of ingredients and equipment for breweries, distilleries and wineries. The site offers a wide range of malt, hops, yeast, yeast nutrients, process aids, adjuncts, fruit flavourings, wine ingredients and packaging accessories. It also provides services such as COA lookup, hop analysis, and customer support.

Key highlights:
- **Products**: Rahr To Thee Pils (low‑color malt), Saaz 11 lb, Fermentis SafLager SH‑45, Brewers TasteZyme G 500g, and many others.
- **Services**: COA lookup, hop analysis, tariff FAQ, customer onboarding.
- **Events**: Participation in CBC (Craft Brewers Conference) 2026.
- **Partnerships**: Collaboration with Yakima Chief Hops for ingredient sourcing.

The site is heavily focused on providing high‑quality ingredients and support to the brewing community.

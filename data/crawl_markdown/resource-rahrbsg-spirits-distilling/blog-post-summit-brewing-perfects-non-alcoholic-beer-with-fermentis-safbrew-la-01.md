# Summit Brewing Perfects Non‑Alcoholic Beer with Fermentis SafBrew™ LA‑01

Summit Brewing Company (Saint Paul, MN) uses the maltose‑negative yeast **Fermentis SafBrew™ LA‑01** to produce a line of non‑alcoholic beers (NAB) called *Nialas*. The article explains how the yeast ferments only glucose, keeping ABV below 0.5 % while still delivering fermentation character. It details Summit’s recipe development process, including mashing, gravity control, hop selection and dry‑hopping schedules for four styles: Latitude (lager), IPA, Ambler (bock) and Irish‑Style Dark.

Key points:
- LA‑01 is clean, flexible in temperature, and provides consistent fermentation.
- Summit’s R&D involved a year of pilot‑scale testing to refine ingredients and process.
- Recipe balance focuses on water chemistry, malt blend, hop variety, and gravity to achieve body and mouthfeel in low‑ABV beers.

The post is an industry‑focused guide for craft brewers considering NAB production with maltose‑negative yeast.

# RahrBSG & Yakima Chief Hops Partnership

RahrBSG and Yakima Chief Hops (YCH) have announced a strategic partnership to expand hop availability across North America while remaining independent, family‑owned businesses. The collaboration leverages RahrBSG’s distribution network and YCH’s proprietary hop varieties, aiming to provide brewers with greater selection, accessibility, and cost savings.

## Key Points
- **Partnership**: Strengthened distribution of YCH hops via RahrBSG’s cold‑storage warehouses.
- **Family‑owned heritage**: Both companies trace their roots back decades (RahrBSG 1847, YCH 1988).
- **Industry impact**: Brewers gain access to new hop varieties and bundled raw material solutions.

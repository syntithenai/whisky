Pils™ Posted January 30, 2026 in Brewing New for 2026, Rahr Malting Co. is introducing a new low color pilsner malt, developed in collaboration with our friends at pFriem Family Brewers . To Thee! Pils™ is a sister to North Star Pils™ , which has been produced at Rahr’s Shakopee facility since 2019 and is used in numerous award-winning beers . To Thee is made at Rahr’s Alix, Alberta malthouse from Canadian barley and malted to a slightly different spec. It’s available in Canada and the Western US, with other locations by special order. How did this new malt come to be?

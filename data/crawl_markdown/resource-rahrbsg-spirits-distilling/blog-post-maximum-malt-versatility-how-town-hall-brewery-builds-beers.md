# Maximum Malt Versatility: How Town Hall Brewery Builds Beers

The article profiles Minneapolis‑based **Town Hall Brewery** and its head brewer, Derek Brown, focusing on the brewery’s use of **Rahr The Brewer’s Standard® Pale Ale** malt. It covers:
- Town Hall’s history, locations, and reputation for barrel‑aging.
- Derek Brown’s background, brewing philosophy, and role as Head Brewer.
- How the brewery selects beers seasonally to match patio crowds and food menus.
- Practical tips on using Rahr Pale Ale in a wide range of styles—from light hoppy ales to dark specialty brews—highlighting its versatility and low need for specialty malts.
- Examples of specific beer recipes (e.g., white wheat, crystal‑hopped pale ale, weizenbock) that showcase the malt’s adaptability.

The piece ends with a call to follow Town Hall on Instagram and invites readers to visit the brewery.

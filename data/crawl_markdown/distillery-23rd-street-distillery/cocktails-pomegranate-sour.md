# Pomegranate Sour

A simple cocktail recipe from 23rd Street Distillery featuring **pomegranate vodka**, fresh lemon juice, sugar syrup, and an optional egg white or aquafaba for froth. Shake with ice, strain into a coupe glass, and garnish with dehydrated orange.

**Ingredients**
- 60 ml pomegranate vodka
- 20 ml fresh lemon juice
- 15 ml sugar syrup (Cawsey’s)
- 1 egg white *or* 30 ml aquafaba

**Method**
1. Add ice and all ingredients (except garnish) to a shaker.
2. Shake for 15 s.
3. Strain into a coupe glass.
4. Garnish with dehydrated orange.

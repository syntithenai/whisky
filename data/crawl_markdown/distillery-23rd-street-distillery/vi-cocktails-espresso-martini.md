# Espresso Martini – Cocktail Recipe

- **Ingredients**: 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka, 15 ml Vok (vodka), 15 ml White Crème de Cacao, 30 ml espresso.
- **Method**:
  1. Shake all ingredients with ice.
  2. Strain into a cocktail glass.
  3. Serve immediately.

# Espresso Martini

A quick cocktail using **23rd Street Classic Vodka** and espresso, sweetened with simple syrup and garnished with coffee beans.

**Ingredients**
- 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka (15 ml per shot)
- 15 ml vodka‑liqueur (e.g., KIKO or similar)
- 30 ml espresso, chilled
- 1 shot simple syrup (2 g sugar dissolved in 10–15 ml water)
- 3 coffee beans for garnish

**Method**
1. Combine vodka, liqueur and espresso in a shaker with ice.
2. Shake vigorously until well chilled.
3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
4. Garnish with three coffee beans.
5. Serve immediately.

*Note: The recipe also mentions optional ingredients such as vanilla‑infused simple syrup, but the core version uses plain espresso and simple syrup.*

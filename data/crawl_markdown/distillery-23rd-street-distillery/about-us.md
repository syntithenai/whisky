# About 23rd Street Distillery

- Historic site withstanding trials and seasons of over a century
- Located in Renmark, South Australia on the corner of 23rd Street
- Originally established for wine production using local fruit-growing region's bounty
- Purchased by Bickford's Group in 2004 after years of disrepair
- Reopened as 23rd Street Distillery in September 2016 with a focus on bold flavors and artful craftsmanship

## Key Facts
- Working heart filled with fresh local fruits and aromatics
- Three Copper Pot Stills over 100 years old salvaged from Australian wine regions
- Barrel hall fragrant with rich raisin and decades-old oak

# Espresso Martini – Cocktail Recipe

- **Ingredients**: 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka, 15 ml Vok Liqueur White Crème de Cacao, 15 ml Vok Liqueur Coffee Liqueur, 30 ml espresso shot, coffee beans for garnish.
- **Method**: Shake all ingredients with ice for 10‑15 s, double strain into a cocktail glass, garnish with coffee beans.

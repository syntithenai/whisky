# Old Fashioned
**Ingredients**
- 60 ml 23rd Street Australian Whisky
- 1 bar spoon Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup
- 2‑3 dashes bitters
- Orange peel (for garnish)

**Method**
1. Combine whisky, syrup and bitters in a mixing glass.
2. Add ice and stir for 10 s.
3. Rim a rocks glass with orange peel.
4. Strain over a large square of ice and garnish with an orange peel.

# 23rd Street Distillery Restaurant

The restaurant at 23rd Street Distillery offers a contemporary dining experience that blends local Riverland produce, artisanal goods and the distillery’s own spirit flavours. Chefs specialise in traditional wood‑oven pizzas while incorporating indigenous ingredients, international influences and family‑friendly options.

**Opening hours**
- Sunday–Wednesday: 10 am – 4 pm
- Thursday–Saturday: 10 am – 10 pm

**Kitchen hours**
- Lunch (7 days): 11:30 am – 3 pm
- Dinner (Thu‑Sat): 6 pm – 8 pm

Reservations are recommended. The restaurant is located at Corner of Renmark Avenue and Twenty Third Street, PO Box 872, Renmark SA 5341.

# Quattro Highball
A simple gin‑based highball featuring **Quattro Juniper Gin**, Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup, lemon juice and Bickford & Sons soda water. Shake the gin, syrup and lemon with ice, strain into a glass, top with soda, stir briefly and garnish with a lemon wheel.

**Ingredients**
- 60 ml Quattro Juniper Gin
- 20 ml Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup
- 30 ml Lemon Juice
- Soda water (Bickford & Sons)
- Lemon wheel for garnish

**Method**
1. Shake gin, syrup and lemon with ice.
2. Strain into a cocktail glass.
3. Top with soda water and stir.
4. Garnish with a lemon wheel.

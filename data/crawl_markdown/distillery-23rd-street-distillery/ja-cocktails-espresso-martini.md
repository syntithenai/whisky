# Espresso Martini
A quick cocktail recipe using 23rd Street Classic Vodka, coffee liqueur and espresso.

**Ingredients**
- 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka
- 15 ml Vok Liqueurs Coffee‑Rook (coffee liqueur)
- 30 ml fresh espresso

**Method**
1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.
2. Shake for 10–15 s.
3. Double strain into a chilled glass and serve.

*All measurements are in millilitres.*

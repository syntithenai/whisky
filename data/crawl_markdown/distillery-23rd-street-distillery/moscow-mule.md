# Raspberry and Finger Lime Gin Smash (Beginner)

A simple Moscow Mule recipe featuring **23rd St Classic Vodka** and **Bickford’s Ginger Beer**, finished with lime juice, a lime wedge, and mint sprig. Serve in a copper mug over ice.

## Ingredients
- 60 mL 23rd St Classic Vodka
- 120 mL Bickford’s Ginger Beer
- 15 mL Lime Juice
- Garnish: lime wedge & mint sprig

## Method
1. Fill a copper mug with ice.
2. Pour in vodka and lime juice.
3. Top with ginger beer.
4. Garnish with lime wedge and mint sprig.

**Visit Us – 23rd Street Distillery, Renmark SA**

- Visitor centre features high‑ceilinged historic buildings, timber beams and lush lawns.
- Tours cover the distillery history, customs house memorabilia, distilling process and Barrel Hall.
- Experiences include guided tastings, “Be a Distiller” day (gin blending, whisky ageing, cocktail masterclass) and restaurant dining.
- Restaurant offers contemporary cuisine with local Riverland produce, wood‑oven pizzas by Chef Andrea, and uses the distillery’s spirits in dishes.
- Functions available for birthdays, weddings, corporate events and live music.

# Limoncello Spritz
A simple, beginner‑friendly cocktail featuring 23rd Street Distillery’s own limoncello, Cawsey’s sugar syrup, Prosecco, and soda water, garnished with a lemon wedge and rosemary sprig.

## Ingredients (per serving)
- 60 mL 23rd St Limoncello
- 15 mL Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup
- Prosecco to top
- Soda Water to top
- Lemon wedge & Rosemary sprig for garnish

## Method
1. Fill a wine‑style glass with ice.
2. Add limoncello and sugar syrup; stir 10 s.
3. Top with Prosecco, soda water, and garnish.

# Pink Lady Cocktail

A classic gin‑based cocktail featuring **23rd Street Violet Gin**, **Not Your Nanna's Brandy**, and a touch of sweetness from **Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup**. The recipe calls for fresh lemon juice, a splash of Wonderfoam, and is finished with a maraschino cherry garnish.

## Ingredients (per 30 ml portions)
- 30 ml 23rd Street Violet Gin
- 30 ml 23rd Street Not Your Nanna's Brandy
- 15 ml Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup
- 20 ml Fresh Lemon Juice
- 6 drops Wonderfoam
- 1 Maraschino Cherry (garnish)

## Method
1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice.
2. Add all ingredients except the cherry.
3. Shake for 15 seconds.
4. Strain into a cocktail glass.
5. Garnish with a maraschino cherry.

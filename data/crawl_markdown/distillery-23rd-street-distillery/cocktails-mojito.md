# Mojito Cocktail Recipe

- **Ingredients**: 60 ml 23rd Street Signature Gin, 20 ml Cawseys Sugar Syrup, 30 ml Fresh Lime Juice, Bickford & Sons Soda Water, Mint sprig.
- **Method**:
  1. Shake gin, lime juice and sugar syrup with ice for 15 s.
  2. Strain into a highball glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with soda water and stir for 10 s.
  4. Garnish with mint.

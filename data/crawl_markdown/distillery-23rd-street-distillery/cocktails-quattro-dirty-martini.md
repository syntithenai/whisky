# Quattro Dirty Martini
A simple gin‑based cocktail featuring **Quattro Gin**, dry vermouth, olive brine and rosemary garnish.

**Ingredients**
- 15 ml Dry Vermouth
- 60 ml Quattro Gin
- 1 tsp Olive Brine
- 3 Spanish olives (skewer)
- 1 sprig Rosemary (optional smoke)

**Method**
1. Optional: Smoke rosemary.
2. In a mixing glass, stir gin and olive brine with ice for 15 s.
3. Swirl ice‑coated vermouth into a martini glass until frosted.
4. Discard the vermouth/ice.
5. Strain gin mixture into the glass.
6. Garnish with rosemary and olives on a skewer.

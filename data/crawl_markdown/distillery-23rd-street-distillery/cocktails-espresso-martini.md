# Espresso Martini
A quick cocktail recipe from 23rd Street Distillery using their Classic Vodka, Vok White Crème de Cacao, and Vok Coffee Liqueur with a shot of espresso. Shake with ice, double strain into a glass, and garnish with coffee beans.

**Ingredients**
- 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka
- 15 ml Vok White Crème de Cacao
- 15 ml Vok Coffee Liqueur
- 30 ml espresso shot (freshly brewed)

**Method**
1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add all ingredients except garnish.
3. Shake 10‑15 s.
4. Double strain into a cocktail glass.
5. Garnish with coffee beans.

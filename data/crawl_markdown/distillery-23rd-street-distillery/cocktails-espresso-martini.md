# Espresso Martini
**Espresso Martini** is a cocktail recipe from 23rd Street Distillery featuring:
- 30 ml Classic Vodka (23rd Street)
- 15 ml Vok Liqueurs White Crème de Cacao
- 15 ml Vok Coffee Liqueur
- 30 ml espresso shot

**Method**
1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add all ingredients except the garnish.
3. Shake 10‑15 s.
4. Double strain into a cocktail glass.
5. Garnish with coffee beans.

# Espresso Martini – Cocktail Recipe

A quick guide from 23rd Street Distillery on how to make an espresso‑martini using their Classic Vodka, coffee liqueur and fresh espresso. The recipe includes measurements, shaking instructions, and a brief note about the distillery’s contact details.

---

**Key Steps**
1. Combine 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka, 15 ml coffee liqueur, 15 ml Vok (coffee‑liqueur) and 30 ml fresh espresso in a shaker.
2. Shake vigorously for 10–15 seconds.
3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.
4. Serve immediately.

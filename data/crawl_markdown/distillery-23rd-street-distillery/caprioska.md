# Raspberry and Finger Lime Gin Smash (Beginner)

A simple cocktail recipe from 23rd Street Distillery featuring **23rd St Classic Vodka**, lime wedges, caster sugar, mint, and optional soda water.

**Ingredients**
- 60 mL 23rd St Classic Vodka
- 4–6 lime wedges
- 1–2 tsp caster sugar
- Mint sprig (garnish)

**Method**
1. Muddle lime wedges and sugar in a low‑ball glass.
2. Add vodka and ice, stir until sugar dissolves.
3. Top with more ice and garnish with mint.

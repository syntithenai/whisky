# 23rd Street Distillery – Renmark, South Australia

- **Location & Identity**: Situated at the corner of 23rd Street and Renmark Avenue in Renmark, the distillery celebrates Australian craft spirit production.
- **Product Range**: Offers award‑winning Brandy, Gin, Vodka, and Whisky, all made with Australian ingredients. Each bottle features artwork by local artists.
- **Experience & Hospitality**: Visitors can enjoy a tasting flight, cocktails, and a restaurant menu featuring pizza, lamb & feta, and other dishes. Staff such as Darcy, Dakota, Nicole, and Ava Reid receive frequent praise for service.
- **Community Engagement**: The distillery hosts gin‑tasting experiences, cocktail classes, and functions. It also sells gift boxes and encourages local artists to contribute label artwork.
- **Online Presence**: Multilingual site with French, German, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Vietnamese, Chinese, and English pages. Contact details include phone 08 8586 8500 and email visitor.centre@23rdstreetdistillery.com.au.

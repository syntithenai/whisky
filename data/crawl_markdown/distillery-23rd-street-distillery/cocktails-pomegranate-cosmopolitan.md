# Pomegranate Cosmopolitan

A cocktail recipe from 23rd Street Distillery featuring **Pomegranate Vodka** as the base spirit.

## Ingredients
- 30 ml Pomegranate Vodka
- 15 ml Triple Sec
- 15 ml Fresh Lime Juice
- 15 ml Cranberry Juice
- Dehydrated Orange (for garnish)

## Method
1. Add ice and all ingredients (except garnish) to a cocktail shaker.
2. Shake for 15 seconds.
3. Strain into a coupe glass.
4. Garnish with dehydrated orange.

# Espresso Martini – Cocktail Recipe

- **Ingredients**
  - 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka
  - 15 ml Vok Liqueurs Blanc Crème de Cacao
  - 15 ml Vok Liqueurs Liqueur de Café
  - 30 ml Espresso Shot
  - Coffee beans for garnish
- **Method**
  1. Fill a shaker with ice.
  2. Add all ingredients except the garnish.
  3. Shake 10‑15 seconds.
  4. Double‑strain into a cocktail glass.
  5. Garnish with coffee beans.

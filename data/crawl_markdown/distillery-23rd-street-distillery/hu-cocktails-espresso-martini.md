# Espresso Martini – Cocktail Recipe

- **Ingredients**
  - 30 ml 23rd Street Classic Vodka
  - 15 ml Vok Liqueurs White Crème de Cacao
  - 15 ml Vok Liqueurs Kávélikőr (coffee liqueur)
  - 30 ml espresso shot
- **Method**
  1. Fill shaker with ice.
  2. Add all ingredients except the rim.
  3. Shake vigorously for 10‑15 s.
  4. Double‑strain into a cocktail glass.
  5. Garnish with coffee beans.

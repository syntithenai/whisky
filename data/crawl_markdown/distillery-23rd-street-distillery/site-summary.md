# 23rd Street Distillery – Renmark, South Australia

**Location & Identity**
- Address: Corner of Renmark Avenue & Twenty Third Street, PO Box 872, Renmark SA 5341.
- Phone: +61 8 586 8500 | Email: visitor.centre@23rdstreetdistillery.com.au

**Products**
- **Brandy, Gin, Vodka & Whisky** – all Australian‑made and award‑winning. Each bottle features artwork by local artists.
- Distinct gin flavours: Quattro Juniper, Mulberry, Violet, Yuzu, etc.; whisky releases are limited and artist‑labelled.
- Spirits are available for purchase online (shop) and through a network of stockists searchable by postcode.

**Experience**
- **Visitor Centre & Barrel Hall** – historic buildings with timber beams and lush lawns.
- Guided tours cover distilling history, customs house memorabilia, the distillation process and barrel‑hall ageing.
- **Tasting Experience** – includes a “Be a Distiller” day (gin blending, whisky ageing) and cocktail masterclasses.
- **Restaurant** – contemporary dining with wood‑oven pizzas, local Riverland produce, indigenous ingredients and international influences. Open 10 am–4 pm (Sun‑Wed), 10 am–10 pm (Thu‑Sat). Reservations recommended.

**Community & Events**
- Cocktail classes, masterclasses, live music, corporate events, weddings.
- Staff highlighted in reviews: Darcy, Dakota, Nicole, Andrea (Chef) and Ava Reid (Restaurant Manager).

**Online Presence**
- Website offers recipes for cocktails such as Pomegranate Sour, Quattro Highball, Mulberry Sour, Pink Lady, Mojito, etc., all featuring distillery‑made ingredients.
- Shop links to purchase spirits, syrups, and cocktail components.
- Multi‑language pages (English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Vietnamese, French, German, Hungarian).

**Key Takeaways**
- Boutique Australian craft distillery with a strong focus on local art, hospitality and experiential tourism.
- Diverse spirit portfolio, award‑winning quality, and an integrated restaurant experience.
- Positive online reviews emphasize friendly staff, superb drinks, and excellent food.
- Strong digital presence with e‑commerce, stockist locator, and age verification compliance.

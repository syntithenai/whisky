# Mulberry Sour
A cocktail recipe from 23rd Street Distillery featuring **23rd Street Mulberry Gin**, Vok Triple Sec, Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup, fresh lime juice and aqua faba, garnished with rosemary.

**Ingredients**
- 60 ml 23rd Street Mulberry Gin
- 15 ml Vok Liqueurs Triple Sec
- 15 ml Cawsey’s Sugar Syrup
- 30 ml Fresh Lime Juice
- 30 ml Aqua Faba
- Rosemary sprig (garnish)

**Method**
1. Fill a shaker with ice.
2. Add all ingredients except garnish.
3. Shake 10‑15 s.
4. Strain into a cocktail glass.
5. Garnish with rosemary.

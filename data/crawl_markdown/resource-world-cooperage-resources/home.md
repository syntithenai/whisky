# World Cooperage
World Cooperage is a barrel‑making company that partners with winemakers worldwide to deliver consistent, high‑quality oak barrels. They offer four main series—**Traditional**, **Profile**, **Infrared**, and **Fusion**—each providing distinct toast levels, flavor profiles, or species combinations.

## Key Points
- **Series Overview**: Traditional (classic five‑toast), Profile (100+ flavor options), Infrared (unique toasting textures), Fusion (mix of up to three oak species).
- **Partnerships**: Works closely with wineries to build reliable barrel programs.
- **Resources**: Oak sourcing, research articles, university partnerships, sustainability initiatives, FAQs, and quality documents are available on the site.
- **Contact**: Email, account manager finder, and social media links provided.

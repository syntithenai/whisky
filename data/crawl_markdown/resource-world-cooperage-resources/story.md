# World Cooperage – Story
World Cooperage is a family‑owned wine barrel manufacturer founded over a century ago in the Missouri Ozarks. The Boswell family now runs the fourth generation, positioning the company as a global brand that blends tradition with innovation.

- **Innovation**: In‑house R&D lab develops consistent toasting and manufacturing technologies.
- **Partnerships**: Account managers collaborate with winemakers to design barrel programs targeting specific sensory outcomes.
- **Education & Research**: The firm sponsors wine education, conducts experiments with universities, and publishes research on oak science.

The site hosts a library of research articles (e.g., *Barrel Profiling*, *Exotic Woods*, *American Oak Toasting*), resources on barrel care, dimensions, and sustainability, as well as contact information for account managers.

**Key Takeaway:** World Cooperage combines over 100 years of oak expertise with modern science to deliver tailored barrel solutions worldwide.

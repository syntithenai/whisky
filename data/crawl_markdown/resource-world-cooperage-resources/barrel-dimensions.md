# Barrel Dimensions – World Cooperage

World Cooperage provides the exact measurements for its three standard barrel sizes (240 L, 225 L, 200 L). Each size shares a common bung hole diameter of 50 mm. The dimensions are given in both metric and imperial units.

| Size | Head Diameter | Head Circumference | Belly Diameter | Belly Circumference | Height |
|------|----------------|--------------------|----------------|---------------------|--------|
| 240 L | 58 cm / 22.8" | 182 cm / 71.7" | 71 cm / 27.9" | 223 cm / 87.8" | 89 cm / 35" |
| 225 L | 58 cm / 22.8" | 182 cm / 71.7" | 71 cm / 27.9" | 223 cm / 87.8" | 89 cm / 35" |
| 200 L | 54 cm / 21.3" | 170 cm / 66.9" | 65 cm / 25.6" | 204 cm / 80.3" | 89 cm / 35" |

All barrels have a bung hole of 50 mm (2.0"). The page also links to a PDF with the same data.

---

**Key take‑away:** World Cooperage offers three standard barrel sizes, each with identical height and bung size but differing in head/belly dimensions to accommodate different volumes.

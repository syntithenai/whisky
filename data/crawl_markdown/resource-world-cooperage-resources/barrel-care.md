# Barrel Care – World Cooperage

World Cooperage provides a step‑by‑step guide for inspecting, preparing, and storing oak barrels used in winemaking. The page covers:
- **Initial inspection** of each barrel upon delivery.
- Two **rehydration methods** (hot water soak or cold high‑volume fill) with temperature, duration, and leakage checks.
- **Leakage handling**: chalk marking, drying, sulfur treatment, and when to contact the account manager.
- **Storage recommendations** for new barrels (cool, humid environment; keep plastic film on) and empty barrels (thorough rinsing, monthly sulfur treatment).
- A brief overview of the three barrel series offered – Traditional Profile, Infrared, and Fusion.

The content is practical, safety‑oriented, and aimed at winemakers who need to maintain barrel integrity throughout their production cycle.

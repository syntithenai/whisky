# Launceston Distillery – Tasmanian Whisky Shop

- **Location**: Hangar 17, 287 Evandale Road, Western Junction, TAS 7212
- **Shipping**: Flat‑rate $20 Australia‑wide or free pick‑up
- **Product range**: Signature single‑malt whiskies (Apera, Bourbon, Tawny, French oak & stout, Peated), Cask‑strength variants, gift packs and limited editions.
- **Prices**: $40–$156 for bottles; $100 for gift packs; $170 for the Roasted Malt Release (H17‑66) Limited Edition.
- **Legal**: Liquor Licence 80254, ABN 37 167 029 227. Minimum age 18.

> *“With deep respect, Launceston Distillery acknowledges the Tasmanian Aboriginal Community as the traditional and ongoing custodians of this land.”*

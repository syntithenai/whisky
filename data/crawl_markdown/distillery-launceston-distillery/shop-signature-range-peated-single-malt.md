# Peated Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

- **Price**: $40 – $156 (100 ml or 500 ml bottles)
- **ABV**: 46 %
- **Distillery**: Launceston Distillery, Tasmania, Australia
- **Awards**: Gold medal at the Australian International Spirits Awards 2025; Bronze medal for Australia Small Batch Single Malt 12 Years & Under at the World Whiskies Awards 2025.
- **Tasting notes**: Pale copper colour; light and elegant nose with crème brûlée, cinnamon, ginger and subtle smoky notes. Sweetness, earthiness, cigar‑box and toasted oak on the palate; rounded sweetness and lingering peat smoke finish.

This release blends peated and unpeated malt to achieve a delicate balance of smoky character.

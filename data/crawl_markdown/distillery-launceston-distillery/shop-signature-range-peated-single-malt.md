# Peated Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

Crafted with a blend of peated and unpeated malt for a delicate balance of smoky notes.

- Gold medal winner at the Australian International Spirits Awards 2025
- Bronze medal winner for Australia Small Batch Single Malt 12 Years & Under at the World Whiskies Awards 2025
- 100ml or 500ml bottle, 46% ABV

## Tasting Notes
**Colour:** Pale copper
**Nose:** Light and elegant. Crème brûlée, cinnamon and ginger with subtle smoky notes.
**Taste:** Harmonious sweetness and earthiness, layered with gentle hints of cigar box and toasted oak.
**Finish:** A delightful combination of rounded sweetness and lingering peat smoke.

## Description
Peated Tasmanian Single Malt Batch 57 upholds the award-winning style you’ve come to expect, crafted with a blend of peated and unpeated malt for a delicate balance of smoky notes. This release boasts a pale copper colour, offering a harmonious palate of sweetness and earthiness layered with subtle hints of cigar box and toasted oak. The finish is a delightful combination of rounded sweetness and lingering peat smoke.

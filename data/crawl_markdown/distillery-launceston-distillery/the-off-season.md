# The Off Season Experience

Launceston Distillery offers a **$50 hand‑fill experience** where guests can fill and label a 100 ml bottle of whisky directly from a special *Off Season* cask, designed for winter enjoyment. Participants sample the spirit straight from the barrel before sealing their own bottle. The package includes a Tasmanian Devil gift pack (whisky marmalade or mustard) with proceeds supporting the Save the Tasmanian Devil Appeal.

The experience is available **1 May 2026 – 31 August 2026** and can be booked alongside guided tours or tastings. Shipping is flat‑rate $20 Australia‑wide, or free pick‑up from the distillery.

> *“Embrace winter with The Off Season, a hands‑on experience where you fill and label your own bottle of whisky directly from a special Off Season cask.”*

# Signature Range – Launceston Distillery

Launceston Distillery offers a **Signature Range** of handcrafted Tasmanian single‑malt whiskies, available in four cask styles:
- Apera (Aperitif) cask
- Bourbon cask
- Peated single malt
- Tawny cask

Each style is sold as multiple variants with prices ranging from **$40 to $156**. The site lists shipping at a flat rate of **$20 Australia‑wide** or free pick‑up from the distillery.

The page also provides contact details, legal notices (age restriction, liquor licence number 80254), and a brief acknowledgment of the Tasmanian Aboriginal Community.

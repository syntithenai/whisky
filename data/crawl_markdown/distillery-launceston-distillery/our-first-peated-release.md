# Our First Peated Release
Launceston Distillery’s inaugural peated whisky is now bottled and available.
- **Malt**: Scottish peated barley, brewed, fermented and distilled at Hangar 17.
- **Cask**: Matured for 2½ years in small American Oak bourbon casks; a portion was vat‑topped with French Oak tawny cask whisky.
- **Bottles**: 650 bottles of 500 ml (46% ABV).

The release is highlighted on the distillery’s news page and is part of its broader Tasmanian single‑malt portfolio.

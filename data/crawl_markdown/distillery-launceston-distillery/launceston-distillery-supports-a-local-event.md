# Launceston Distillery Supports a Local Event
Launceston Distillery announced its sponsorship of the **Evandale Village Fair** and specifically the **Penny Farthing seniors race (over‑60s)** in February 2023. The fair, held annually since 1983 at Pioneer Park, features Penny Farthing bicycle racing and a period‑themed festival.

The distillery has been sponsoring the event for three years and highlights its commitment to supporting local community activities within the greater Launceston area.

> *“Being part of the greater Launceston area means a lot to us and we love supporting local events like this.”*

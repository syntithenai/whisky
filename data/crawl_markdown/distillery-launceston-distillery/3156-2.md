# Peated Single Malt 46% – Return Announcement

Launceston Distillery has re‑released its celebrated **Peated Single Malt** (Batch 57) at **46 % ABV**, priced at **$156 per bottle** (excluding shipping). The whisky blends peated and unpeated malt, delivering a pale copper hue with notes of sweetness, earthiness, cigar box, toasted oak, and a finish that balances rounded sweetness with lingering peat smoke.

> *“We’re thrilled to announce the highly anticipated return of one of Launceston Distillery’s most cherished expressions: the Peated Single Malt 46% whisky.”*

The product is available for purchase online via the distillery’s shop.

- **Batch**: 57

# The Tasmanian Whisky Week 2023 Special Release
Launceston Distillery launches a limited‑edition single malt finished in a hybrid **Port/Rum cask** for Tasmanian Whisky Week 2023.
- **Cask origin:** 50/50 blend of old port staves and rare rum staves, then aged one year with bourbon‑cask whisky.
- **Limited run:** 120 bottles, released 7 Aug 2023 at 10 am.
- **Price & strength:** $166 per bottle, 46% ABV.
- **Tasting notes:** Sweet nose of rum, red stone fruit, toasted oak and coconut; palate with subtle rum, spice and caramelised banana; finish dominated by rum and spice.

The cask was previously presented to the Royal Australian Navy; this sister cask is now available to the public.

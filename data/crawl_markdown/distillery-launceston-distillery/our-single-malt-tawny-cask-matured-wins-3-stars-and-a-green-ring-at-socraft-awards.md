# Launceston Distillery
**Our Single Malt Tawny Cask Matured** has earned **3 stars** and a green ring at the *Spirit of Craft Awards* (SOCraft). The award highlights the whisky’s long, warming finish, robust body, depth, and accessibility for neat sipping. SOCraft also recognises sustainability, underscoring Launceston Distillery’s commitment to eco‑friendly practices.

---
**Key points:**
- 3‑star win (one of only two spirits awarded this level)
- Praise: long finish, robust body, depth, neat sipping
- Sustainability acknowledged by SOCraft
- Award presented in SOCraft’s inaugural year among global peers

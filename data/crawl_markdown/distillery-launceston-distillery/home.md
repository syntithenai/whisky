# Launceston Distillery - Tasmania's Premier Single Malt Whisky

Launceston Distillery is located in northern Tasmania and produces award-winning single malt whisky using pure local ingredients. The distillery operates out of historic Hangar 17.

## Key Facts
- Handcrafted Tasmanian single malt whisky reflecting the character of Tasmania
- Tours and tastings available Monday to Saturday, 10am–3pm; cellar door open until 4pm
- Guided tastings are $20 per person for 30 minutes; distillery tours + tasting are $30 per person for 1 hour
- Offers a variety of single malt expressions including Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail (special release), Roasted Malt Release, Apera Cask Matured, Peated Tasmanian Single Malt, Bourbon Cask Matured, and Tawny Cask Matured options

## Production Signals
- Crafted with tradition, heritage, and pure local ingredients
- Uses historic Hangar 17 as the distillery space
- Offers guided tastings and tours to showcase production process

## Commercial Signals

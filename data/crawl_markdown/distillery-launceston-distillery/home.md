# Launceston Distillery – Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky
Launceston Distillery, located in historic Hangar 17 on the northern Tasmanian coast, produces award‑winning single‑malt whisky using local barley and pure water. The distillery offers a range of expressions aged in different casks (Apera, Bourbon, Tawny, Peated) and special releases such as the **Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail** and the limited‑edition **Roasted Malt Release (H17‑66)**. Visitors can book guided tastings ($20 pp for 30 min) or a full distillery tour with tasting ($30 pp for 1 h). The site also features tours, gift packs, and a “Fill Your Own Bottle” experience.

## Visitor Experience
Tours run Monday‑Saturday 10 am‑3 pm; the cellar door is open until 4 pm. The distillery blends whisky production with aviation heritage, featuring original Ansett seats and vintage bar carts.

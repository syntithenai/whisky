# Launceston Distillery

Launceston Distillery is a Tasmanian single‑malt producer based in historic **Hangar 17** at Launceston Airport. The distillery offers guided tastings ($20 for 30 min) and tours ($30 for 1 h, including a tasting). Its product range includes Apera‑cask, Bourbon‑cask, Peated, Tawny‑cask and special releases such as the **Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail** (AU$85) and **Roasted Malt Release H17‑66** (AU$170). Prices vary from AU$40 to AU$156.

The site also hosts visitor reviews, a gift pack featuring Tasmanian devil merchandise, and showcases the distillery’s aviation heritage. Contact details are 03 6391 9140 or info@launcestondistillery.com.au.

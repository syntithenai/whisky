# Westie Double Malt Wins Gold at the World Whiskies Awards

Launceston Distillery announced that its **Westie Double Malt** has taken home *Category winner* and *Gold* in the Australia Blended Malt 12 Years & Under category at the 2025 World Whiskies Awards. The whisky is a tribute to Angus, the distillery’s West Highland Terrier, and blends single malts from Launceston and Nant Distilleries matured in French oak tawny casks. It is non‑chill filtered and offers rich, tawny‑influenced fruit and spice notes with a hint of peat and a smooth finish.

# Supercars Driver Cody McKay Visits Launceston Distillery
Launceston Distillery welcomed Australian Supercars driver **Cody McKay** during the May 8, 2025 Supercars weekend. The visit included a tour of the historic hangar and a short video filmed by McKay that showcases the distillery’s atmosphere and spirit.

## Special Opening Hours for the Weekend
- **Saturday:** 9 am – 5 pm
- **Sunday:** 9 am – 5 pm

The article invites visitors to the track or anyone exploring northern Tasmania to stop by, enjoy a tasting, and experience Tasmanian whisky history.

---

### Additional Context from the Site
- Launceston Distillery is located at Hangar 17, 287 Evandale Road, Western Junction, TAS 7212.
- The distillery acknowledges the Tasmanian Aboriginal Community as traditional custodians of the land.
- Liquor licence number: **80254** (Liquor Licensing Act 1990).

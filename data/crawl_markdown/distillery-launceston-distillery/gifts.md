# Gifts at Launceston Distillery
Launceston Distillery offers a curated range of whisky gifts, including:
- **Gift packs** featuring three 100 ml bottles (Bourbon, Tawny, Peated or Apera) – $100 each.
- **Special releases** such as the Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail – $85.
- **Pairing kits** that combine a 500 ml bottle with complementary items – $180.
- **Signature Taster** 100 ml tasting experience – $50.
- **Experiences**: guided distillery tours ($30), guided tastings ($20) and an off‑season bottle‑filling event ($50).
- **Add‑ons**: Tasmanian Gourmet Sauce Co products (marmalade, hot mustard) and Launceston Distillery Glencairn glasses.
All items ship flat‑rate $20 Australia‑wide or can be collected from Hangar 17. A gift card is also available for flexible gifting.

# Launceston Distillery Wins at the 2025 Melbourne Royal Australian International Spirits Awards

Launceston Distillery announced that four of its Tasmanian single‑malt expressions earned medals at the 2025 Melbourne Royal Australian International Spirits Awards, including **two golds**:
- Gold – Cask Strength Bourbon Cask Matured Single Malt
- Gold – Peated Single Malt
- Silver – Apera Cask Matured Single Malt
- Silver – Bourbon Cask Matured Single Malt

The awards were presented by industry experts and highlighted the distillery’s commitment to quality, craftsmanship and Tasmanian character.

---

## Key Points
- **Awards**: 2 Gold, 2 Silver at a globally respected competition.
- **Products Recognised**: Cask Strength Bourbon Cask Matured, Peated, Apera, and Bourbon Cask Matured single malts.
- **Location**: Hangar 17, Western Junction, Tasmania.
- **Head Distiller**: Chris Condon.
- **Event Commentator**: Kirrily Waldhorn, Melbourne Royal Senior Manager, Food & Beverage Awards.

# Launceston Distillery – Tasmanian Single Malt Whisky

Launceston Distillery offers a range of handcrafted single‑malt whiskies, each matured in different cask types (Apera, Bourbon, Tawny, and Tawny & Bourbon). The product page lists four signature variants with ABVs ranging from 32.8 % to 62 %, priced between \$40 and \$156. A special release, the *Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail*, is available for \$85. Shipping is flat‑rate \$20 across Australia or free pick‑up at the distillery.

The site also provides contact details, a brief acknowledgment of the Tasmanian Aboriginal community, licensing information, and links to other sections such as news, tours, and the production process.

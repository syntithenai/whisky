# Mawson's Antarctic Whisky
Launceston Distillery’s limited‑edition whisky supports the Mawson Hut Foundation. Matured in small French oak tawny casks, it was bottled at 43 % ABV on 19 September 2019 with only **78 individually numbered bottles**.

- **Purpose:** All proceeds fund conservation of Sir Douglas Mawson’s Antarctic huts.
- **Availability:** Limited stock sold directly from the distillery or online.
- **Future plans:** Additional releases will continue to support the Foundation.

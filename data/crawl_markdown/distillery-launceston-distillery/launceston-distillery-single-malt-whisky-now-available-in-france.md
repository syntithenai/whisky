# Launceston Distillery Single Malt Whisky now available in France
Launceston Distillery’s single‑malt range is now stocked by Premium Craft Spirits across France, following a personal endorsement from Bernard Baud, Chairman of the distributor and fellow distiller. The company will roll out its full line to French venues and bars over the coming months.

- **Launch date**: 14 Feb 2023
- **Distributor in France**: Premium Craft Spirits
- **Key person involved**: Bernard Baud (Chairman, Premium Craft Spirits)
- **Location of distillery**: Hangar 17, 287 Evandale Road, Western Junction, TAS 7212
- **Legal age requirement**: 18+ for purchase and delivery

The announcement is part of Launceston Distillery’s broader strategy to expand internationally while maintaining its Tasmanian heritage.

# Visit Us – Launceston Distillery

- **Location**: Hangar 17, Launceston Airport, Tasmania.
- **Tours & Tastings**:
  - Guided Tasting: $20 per person (30 min).
  - Distillery Tour & Tasting: $30 per person (1 h).
- **Off‑Season Offer**: Optional add‑on available from 1 May 2026 to 31 Aug 2026.
- **Hours**: Open Mon–Sat, tours 10 am–3 pm, cellar door 10 am–4 pm.
- **Accessibility**: Gravel car park with minimal slope; accessible for all visitors.
- **Contact**: 03 6391 9140 | info@launcestondistillery.com.au
- **Legal & Licensing**: Liquor Licence No. 80254, under Tasmanian Liquor Licensing Act 1990.
- **Cultural Acknowledgement**: Recognises the Tasmanian Aboriginal Community as traditional custodians of the land.

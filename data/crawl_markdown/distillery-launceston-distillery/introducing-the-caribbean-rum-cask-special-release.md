# Caribbean Rum Cask Special Release
Launceston Distillery’s limited‑edition whisky is aged in American oak Bourbon casks and finished in freshly emptied Caribbean rum casks. The spirit has a dark golden hue, with a sweet, light nose featuring hints of candy, pineapple and rum. On the palate it delivers a bright, warming sweetness that turns zesty, finishing with a spicy note – ideal for summer sipping.

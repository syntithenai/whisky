# Tasmanian Whisky Week 2020 Release
Launceston Distillery released a limited‑edition single‑cask whisky during the 2020 Tasmanian Whisky Week. The cask, a small Tawny cask, was filled with new‑make spirit distilled that day and matured for an undisclosed period before bottling at 46% ABV. Only **40 bottles** were produced; all were sold within the week.

## Key facts
- First limited edition release tied to Tasmanian Whisky Week
- Bottled at 46 % alcohol by volume
- Limited to 40 bottles, sold out during the event

The page also highlights past collaborations with the Tasmanian Whisky Academy and mentions future plans for similar releases each year.

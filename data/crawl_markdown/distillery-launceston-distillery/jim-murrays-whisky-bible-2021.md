# Jim Murray's Whisky Bible 2021
Launceston Distillery’s whiskies received high scores in the 2021 edition of *Jim Murray’s Whisky Bible*:
- **Batch H17‑11 (Apera, 46%)** – 89.5 pts (Very good to excellent)
- **Batch H17‑12 (Tawny, 63%)** – 94 pts (Superstar)
- **Batch H17‑13 (Tawny, 46%)** – 88.5 pts (Very good to excellent)
- **Batch H17‑14 (Bourbon, 46%)** – 89 pts (Very good to excellent)
- **Batch H17‑16 (Peated, 46%)** – 93 pts (Brilliant)

Each entry includes Murray’s comment and the alcohol by volume.

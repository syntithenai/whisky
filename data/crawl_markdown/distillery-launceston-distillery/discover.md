# Launceston Distillery - Discover Tasmania's Single Malt Whisky

## Our Beginning
- Founded in 2013 by Chris Condon, Chris Byrne, Rob Carroll, Peta Dolan, and Ilya Brucksch
- Inspired by Scottish single malt traditions to create a pure Tasmanian whisky
- Hangar 17 provided the ideal location for the distillery

## Hangar 17 - Our Home
- Tasmania's oldest surviving aviation building (constructed 1932)
- Preserved and adapted into a working distillery housing stills, casks, and bottling
- Symbolizes tradition meeting innovation in whisky production

## Production & Ingredients
- Water sourced from the nearby South Esk River (plipatumila)
- Barley grown in northern Tasmania
- Production methods rooted in Tasmanian skill and craft

## Our Home - Hangar 17
- Located at Launceston Airport, Tasmania's oldest surviving passenger terminal (1968 closure)
- Now home to stills, casks, and bottling operations
- Represents the legacy of Launceston's whisky heritage

## Legacy & Heritage
- Revives Launceston's first whisky distillery (Caledonian Distillery 1824) licensing since 1992
- Proud member of Tasmanian Whisky & Spirits Association and Northern Midlands Business Association Inc.
- Supports excellence, collaboration, community, and regional economy

# Launch of Launceston Distillery (2018)

Launceston Distillery officially opened on **22 July 2018** at Hangar 17, Launceston Airport. The launch featured two batches of whisky, the first traditional Tasmanian single‑malt since distillation was banned in 1839. Head distiller **Chris Condon**, formerly with Boags Brewery and Nant Distillery, led a team that revived the historic hangar into a licensed distillery.

Key facts:
- First batch distilled **5 Oct 2015**; matured 30 months in small oak casks.
- 12 casks blended for eight weeks → 530 bottles released.
- Distillery produces varieties aged in bourbon, tawny (Australian port) and apera (Australian sherry) casks.
- Future releases will support local charities.

The launch celebrated Tasmania’s growing reputation as a premium whisky region and honoured the traditional custodians of the land.

# The Tawny Cask has returned!

Launceston Distillery announces the re‑release of its Tasmanian single malt whisky, Batch H17‑48, aged over five years in two 100 L SA Cooperage tawny casks that previously held Australian‑style port. No finishing or additional manipulation was applied; the whisky is now available for purchase on the distillery’s website.

---

## Key facts
- **Batch**: H17‑48
- **Type**: Tasmanian single malt
- **Cask history**: 100 L SA Cooperage tawny casks (C‑067 & C‑070) filled 19/10/2017, emptied early 2023
- **Maturation**: >5 years
- **No finishing**
- **Availability**: https://launcestondistillery.com.au/product/tawny-cask-matured/

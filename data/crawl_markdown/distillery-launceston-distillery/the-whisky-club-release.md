# The Whisky Club Release
Launceston Distillery’s exclusive whisky for the Whisky Club was first matured in small 20 L apera casks, then decanted and blended before a further ageing period in Muscat casks sourced from the Holm Oak Vineyard. Bottled at cask strength (62.5 % ABV) with special club labels, it is only available through the Whisky Club.

---

## Key facts
- **Initial cask type:** 20 L apera casks
- **Secondary cask type:** Muscat casks from Holm Oak Vineyard
- **ABV at bottling:** 62.5 % (cask strength)
- **Labeling:** Special Whisky Club labels
- **Availability:** Exclusive to the Whisky Club

---

## Context
The release is part of Launceston Distillery’s broader portfolio, which includes Tasmanian single‑malt whiskies and a bottled Old Fashioned cocktail. The distillery operates from Hangar 17 in Western Junction, Tasmania, and emphasizes respect for the traditional custodians of the land.

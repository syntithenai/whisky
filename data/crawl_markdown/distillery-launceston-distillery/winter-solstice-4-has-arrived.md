# Winter Solstice 4

Launceston Distillery has released its latest limited‑edition **Winter Solstice 4** on 21 June 2025. The whisky is a bold, intensely peated single malt finished at 46 % ABV in ex‑bourbon American oak and ex‑tawny French oak casks. It opens with sweet, spicy smokiness (Lapsang Souchong tea, orange zest) and develops into citrus, dark fruit, chocolate and tea notes. The palate is rich with dark chocolate, black tea, dried plums, kumquat, blood orange, cherries, vanilla, bergamot and caramel, finishing in earthy peaty smoke with lingering fruity and vanilla nuances.

> **Colour:** Dark golden
> 
> **Nose:** Sweet & spicy smokiness, Lapsang Souchong, orange zest, cigars, dark chocolate
> 
> **Palate:** Intense dark chocolate, black tea, dried plums, kumquat, blood orange, cherries, vanilla, bergamot, caramel
> 
> **Finish:** Earthy peaty smoke with fruity & vanilla notes
> 
> **Maturation:** Ex‑bourbon American oak + ex‑tawny French oak casks

The release is part of Launceston Distillery’s annual Winter Solstice series celebrating Tasmania’s winter season.

# Launceston Distillery Goes International
Launceston Distillery announced that its five‑year‑old bourbon‑cask whisky has been acquired by the UK‑based boutique label **That Boutique‑y Whisky Company** (NCS). The cask, finished in Hangar 17, was bottled at natural cask strength (62.6 % ABV) with no dilution and limited to 187 bottles. The release is part of NCS’s *Return to Oz* series and will launch in the UK today, with an Australian rollout pending.

The label features a graphic‑novel style illustration by Emily Chappell, depicting the distillery’s mascot Angus interviewing a Tasmanian devil for a security guard role at Hangar 17. The bottle is available through a ballot; interested buyers should join Launceston Distillery’s mailing list.

> *“A beautifully balanced dram that was so good they decided to bottle it at NCS (natural cask strength). They decided not to add water to the whisky even though we sent plenty of Tasmanian water with it.”*

# Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail
Launceston Distillery has re‑released its bottled Old Fashioned, blending the distillery’s Tawny‑Cask and Bourbon‑Cask single‑malt whiskies into a ready‑to‑pour cocktail. The 500 ml bottle (32.8 % ABV) delivers vanilla, caramel, oak, gentle sweetness and aromatic bitters, served over ice with an orange peel.

- **Volume**: 500 ml
- **ABV**: 32.8 %
- **Standard drinks**: ~13
- **Price**: $20 flat‑rate shipping (Australia‑wide) or free pick‑up from the distillery.

The product showcases Tasmanian single‑malt character while staying true to the classic bar‑style Old Fashioned.

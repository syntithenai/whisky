## Long Description
# Launceston Distillery

Launceston Distillery is a boutique Tasmanian single‑malt producer located in the former **Hangar 17** at Launceston Airport. The distillery blends local barley with Tasmania’s unique climate to create a portfolio that includes Apera‑cask, Bourbon‑cask, Peated and Tawny‑cask expressions, as well as limited‑edition releases such as the *Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail* (AU$85) and *Roasted Malt Release H17‑166* (AU$170). Prices for standard bottles range from AU$40 to AU$156.

## Product Highlights
- **Westie Double Malt** – Gold at World Whiskies Awards 2025.
- **Winter Solstice Series** – Winter Solstice 3 (June 2024) and Winter Solstice 4 (June 2025).
- **Peated Single Malt** – Returned May 2024.
- **Single Malt Tawny Cask Matured** – Platinum at SOCraft Awards 2025, 3‑star rating at Spirit Awards.
- **Old Fashioned Whisky Cocktail** – Re‑launched March 9 2026; blends Tawny and Bourbon casks.

## Visitor Experience
Tours run Monday–Saturday 10 am–3 pm. A 30‑minute tasting costs AU$20, while a full hour tour with tasting is AU$30. The site preserves aviation heritage with original Ansett seats, vintage bar carts and historic photographs.

## Awards & Media

- Platinum at SOCraft Awards (May 2025).
- Featured in *Business Events Tasmania* spring magazine (Oct 2024) and *Melbourne Royal Australian International Spirits Awards* (July 2025).

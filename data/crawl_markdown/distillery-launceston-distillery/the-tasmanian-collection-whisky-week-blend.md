# The Tasmanian Collection – Whisky Week Blend

- **Release**: Second release of *The Tasmanian Collection* launched during the 2019 Whisky Showcase.
- **Composition**: Matured whisky sourced from fourteen Tasmanian distilleries (Sulivans Cove, Lark, Overeem, McHenry, Old Kempton, Corra Linn, Nant, Spring Bay, Adams, Hobart Whisky, Hellyers Road, Iron House, Killara) plus Launceston Distillery.
- **Bottles**: 236 bottles of 500 mL at 52.5% ABV.
- **Price**: $260 per bottle (available at the distillery or online shop).
- **Proceeds**: Support the Tasmanian Whisky and Spirits Association.

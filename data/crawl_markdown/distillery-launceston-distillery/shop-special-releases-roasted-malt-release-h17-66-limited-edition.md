# Roasted Malt Release (H17-66) - Limited Edition

Crafted with roasted crystal and chocolate malts, our latest release is rich, smooth, and deeply layered. Finished in stout casks from Du Cane Brewery, this bold Tasmanian single malt tells a story in every sip.

## Key Facts
- **Limited Release**: 400 bottles for Tasmanian Whisky Week
- **ABV**: 50.3%
- **Size**: 500ml bottle
- **Non-chill filtered**
- **Maturation**: 4 years in small French oak casks, then 12 months in stout casks from Du Cane Brewery
- **Tasting Notes**: Deep copper color; sweet floral notes, lanolin, honey and hops with malty caramel and toffee scent; abundant, smooth taste of caramel, milk chocolate, toffee and malt; delicate floral and malt characteristics; warming porter or stout notes on the finish

## Production Signals

- Collaboration with Du Cane Brewery for unique maturation process

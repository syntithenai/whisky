# Shop Terms and Conditions

- **Eligibility**: Purchases are limited to Australian residents.
- **Pricing**: All prices include GST; a flat‑rate $20 shipping fee applies unless you pick up in person.
- **Delivery**: Standard dispatch via Australia Post (3–10 business days). Courier service available by request. Uncollected items held for 30 days before forfeiture.
- **Age Verification**: Purchaser’s date of birth must be recorded; delivery to anyone under 18 is prohibited.
- **Refunds/Exchanges**: Accepted within 7 days if goods are defective or damaged, with return required.
- **Wholesale**: In‑Tasmania customers use David Johnstone & Associates Pty Ltd; outside Tasmania contact the distillery directly.
- **Contact**: info@launcestondistillery.com.au | 03 6391 9140
- **Legal**: Liquor Licence No. 80254, ABN 37 167 029 227.

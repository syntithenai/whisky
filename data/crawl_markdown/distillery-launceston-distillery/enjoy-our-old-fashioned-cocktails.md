# Launceston Distillery – Bottled Old Fashioned & Banana Old Fashioned

Launceston Distillery now sells two bottled cocktails: a classic **Old Fashioned** (32.8 % ABV, 500 ml) made with Tawny‑Cask single malt whisky, sugar syrup and bitters; and a **Banana Old Fashioned** (37.8 % ABV, 500 ml) featuring Bourbon‑Cask single malt whisky, banana liqueur and bitters. Both are bottled at Hangar 17 and can be poured over ice to replicate the fresh‑made experience.

A limited bundle offers one bottle of each at a discounted price while stocks last.

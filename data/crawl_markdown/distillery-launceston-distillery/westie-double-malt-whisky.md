# Westie Double Malt Whisky

Launceston Distillery’s **Westie Double Malt** is a blend of single malts from Launceston and Nant Distilleries, matured in French oak tawny casks and non‑chill filtered. The whisky celebrates the distillery’s West Highland Terrier, Angus, and was launched during Tasmanian Whisky Week 2024.

- **Blend:** Launceston + Nant single malts
- **Maturation:** French oak tawny casks
- **Filtering:** Non‑chill filtered
- **Flavor profile:** Tawny fruit & spice, subtle peat, fragrant aromas, smooth finish
- **Launch context:** Tribute to Angus and Tasmanian Whisky Week 2024

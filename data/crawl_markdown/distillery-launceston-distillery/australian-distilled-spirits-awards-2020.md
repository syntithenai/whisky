# Australian Distilled Spirits Awards 2020
Launceston Distillery entered three of its regular 46 % whiskies—bourbon‑cask, apera‑cask and tawny‑cask matured—into the unpeated whisky category. All three received **Silver Awards** (classified as *excellent*), matching the results from the previous year for the apera and tawny samples. The event saw only one Gold Award overall; 17 Silver Awards were awarded, with four Tasmanian distilleries—including Launceston—receiving Silver.

> *“We are really pleased with these results, which confirms that our aim to consistently produce and release excellent whiskies is ‘bearing fruit’.”*

# Melby the Manatee
Melby was rescued from a storm drain in Melbourne Beach, Florida, and after weeks of treatment at SeaWorld he was released back into the wild on April 7, 2026.

- **Rescue**: Found Feb 9, 2026; 7‑ft, 410‑lb manatee stuck in concrete culvert. Specialized fire‑rescue team cut through concrete and used a tow truck to haul him out.
- **Treatment**: At SeaWorld he gained weight (515 lb) and healed from cuts and infection. Veterinary care included antibiotics and wound ointment.
- **Release**: Returned to Ballard Park, Melbourne, Florida; observed swimming, grazing, and integrating with other manatees.
- **Context**: Highlights climate‑change impacts on warm‑water habitats and the need for safe infrastructure for marine mammals.

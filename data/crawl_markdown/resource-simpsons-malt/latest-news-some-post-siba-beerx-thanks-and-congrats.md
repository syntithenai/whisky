# Some post‑SIBA BeerX thanks and congrats

Simpsons Malt uses the recent SIBA BeerX trade show as a platform to thank attendees, highlight positive feedback on their malt products—especially extract efficiency—and celebrate award winners from the SIBA Business and Indie Beer Awards. The company also announces upcoming trade shows (CBC 2026 in Philadelphia and Brewers Congress in London) and invites interested parties to reconnect via a contact link.

## Key points
- Over **3,700** brewing professionals attended BeerX.
- Positive feedback on extract efficiency and customer service.
- Sold out of 72 cans of Bloody Marys during the event.
- Five of nine Overall Champion medals came from breweries supplied by Simpsons Malt.
- Upcoming events: CBC 2026 (April 20‑22, Philadelphia) and Brewers Congress (Sept 29‑30, London).

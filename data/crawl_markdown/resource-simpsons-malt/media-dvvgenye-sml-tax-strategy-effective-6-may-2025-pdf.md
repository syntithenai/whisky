# Simpsons Malt UK Tax Strategy (Effective 6 May 2025)

- **Purpose**: Outline the Group’s approach to managing tax affairs and risk in the UK.
- **Scope**: Applies only to Simpsons Malt Limited (CRN 00153026), the sole trading company in the Group.
- **Governance**: Managed by senior finance staff, with external advice or HMRC clearance sought when needed.
- **Risk Attitude**: Prudent tax planning that supports commercial activity; no aggressive or artificial arrangements.
- **HMRC Interaction**: Transparent, timely compliance and disclosure, proactive discussion on complex matters.
- **Review Cycle**: Periodic review with updates as required; last approved by the Board on 7 May 2025.

---

## Key Points
1. **Compliance & Transparency** – Full adherence to UK tax law, claiming legitimate allowances and paying only what is due.
2. **Risk Management** – External advice or HMRC clearance sought when guidance is unclear.
3. **Sustainability Commitment** – Tax strategy aligns with the Group’s broader sustainability and governance goals.
4. **No Aggressive Planning** – The Group explicitly avoids aggressive tax planning or artificial arrangements.
5. **Board Oversight** – Strategy approved by the Board of Directors, ensuring executive accountability.

---

## Governance & Risk Management
- Senior qualified finance personnel oversee tax affairs.
- Technical training and professional development are provided.
- External advisors or HMRC clearance are sought proactively in uncertain situations.

---

## Interaction with HMRC
- Timely compliance, accurate returns, and adequate disclosure.
- Transparent discussion on complex areas.
- Consideration of new legislation to maintain compliance.

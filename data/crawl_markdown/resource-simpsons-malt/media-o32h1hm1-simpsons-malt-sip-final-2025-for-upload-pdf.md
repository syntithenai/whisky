# Simpsons Malt Group Pension Scheme
**Statement of Investment Principles (SIP)** – July 2025

- Purpose: outline the Trustees’ investment principles for the pension scheme.
- Objectives: achieve ~2.3 % p.a. above UK government bonds, maximise returns within acceptable risk, and protect the sponsoring company’s interests.
- Strategy: diversified pooled‑fund approach with a 85 % liability‑driven hedge (interest rate & inflation). Leverage and collateral policies are in place.
- Governance: Trustees make strategic decisions; investment managers handle day‑to‑day management under written agreements. ESG considerations delegated to managers, all of whom sign UN‑PRI.
- Risk Management: focuses on investment, funding, covenant, interest‑rate/inflation, liquidity, market, credit, ESG, currency and non‑financial risks.
- Compliance: follows Pensions Act 1995/2004 and related regulations; reviewed every three years or after major changes.

# Introducing our 2023 Sustainability Report
Simpsons Malt Limited, a fifth‑generation family business, has released its first Sustainability Report. The company highlights progress since becoming a Certified B Corporation in November 2021, including financial support for staff during the cost‑of‑living crisis, charitable donations, customer engagement and reductions in carbon footprint. Key future targets are carbon‑neutral malting barley and distilling wheat production and net‑zero Scope 1 & 2 emissions by 2030.

The report underscores the company’s dual structure—malting and agricultural merchanting divisions—which positions it to influence sustainable practices across its supply chain.

# Simpsons Malt Limited: A Certified B Corporation
Simpsons Malt became a Certified B Corporation in November 2021 and celebrates B Corp Month each March to promote its environmental and social commitments.
- **Certification**: Achieved an 89.6‑point score on the B Impact Assessment (threshold 80).
- **Governance**: Articles of association now reflect legal commitment to people and planet.
- **Purpose**: Demonstrate positive impact across Governance, Workers, Customers, Community, Environment.
- **Future goals**: Increase score annually and re‑certify every three years; share progress publicly.
- **Community outreach**: Welcomes inquiries from other businesses interested in B Corp certification.

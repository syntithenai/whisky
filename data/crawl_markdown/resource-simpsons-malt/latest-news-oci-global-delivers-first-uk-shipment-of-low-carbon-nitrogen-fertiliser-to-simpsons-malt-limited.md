**OCI Global delivers first UK shipment of low‑carbon nitrogen fertiliser to Simpsons Malt Limited**

- OCI Global shipped its NUTRAMON Low Carbon fertiliser to the Port of Berwick on 14 March.
- The product will be trialed on malting barley and distilling wheat at Simpsons Malt’s Northumberland farm, with a view to reducing the carbon footprint of Scotch whisky production.
- NUTRAMON Low Carbon uses biomethane feedstock, cutting its cradle‑to‑gate CO₂e by up to 50 % and is ISCC PLUS certified.
- The fertiliser will be sold commercially from autumn 2023 via McCreath Simpson & Prentice after trial results.

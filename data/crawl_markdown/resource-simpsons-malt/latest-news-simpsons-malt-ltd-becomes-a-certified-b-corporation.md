# Simpsons Malt Ltd becomes a Certified B Corporation
Simpsons Malt Limited, a fifth‑generation family‑owned malting and merchanting company, has been certified as a **B Corp®** by B Lab™ with an impact score of **89.6**. The certification covers Governance, Workers, Community, Environment and Customers, reflecting the company’s commitment to purpose beyond profit. Richard Simpson (Vice Chairman) and Chris Turner (Executive Director of B Lab UK) both expressed enthusiasm for the achievement.

The company has legally embedded its purpose‑driven mission in its articles of association and will continue to improve its score on future reassessments.

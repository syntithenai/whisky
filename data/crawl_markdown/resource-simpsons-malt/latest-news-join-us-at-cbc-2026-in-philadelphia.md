# Join us at CBC 2026 in Philadelphia
Simpsons Malt will attend the Craft Brewers Conference (CBC) in Philadelphia from **20‑22 April 2026**.
- Booth: **1701** in the RahrBSG village.
- Showcasing heritage base malts *Golden Promise* and *Maris Otter*, crystal and roasted malts, plus a dartboard with prizes.
- Exhibition schedule:
  - 20 Apr: 2‑5 pm
  - 21 Apr: 10‑5 pm
  - 22 Apr: 10‑4 pm
- The Malt Shop bar will feature beers brewed with Simpsons malts (e.g., RTC Eagle Brewery’s *Slumlord Best Bitter* and Sun King Brewery’s *Ouija Heavy Back*).
- Night Cap event on 21 Apr at PHS Pop Up Garden, 8‑11 pm.

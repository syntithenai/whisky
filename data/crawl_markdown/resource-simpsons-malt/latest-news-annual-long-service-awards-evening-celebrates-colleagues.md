# Annual Long Service Awards Evening

Simpsons Malt celebrated long‑serving colleagues at the 2025 awards dinner held at Marshall Meadows Manor House Hotel. The event honoured staff with 15–40 years of service, presenting certificates and gifts.

## Award Recipients (selected)
- **Susan Turnbull** – Accounts Payable Manager, 40 years
- **David McCreath** – Group Fleet Manager, 40 years
- **Adam Crosbie** – Stores Plant Manager, 40 years

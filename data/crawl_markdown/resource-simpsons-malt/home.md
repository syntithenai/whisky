# Simpsons Malt
Simpsons Malt is a fifth‑generation, family‑owned UK malt producer founded in 1862. The company supplies high‑quality base, crystal, roasted, special and distilling malts to breweries and distilleries worldwide. It operates its own agricultural merchanting division (MSP) for direct grain sourcing and is a Certified B Corporation committed to sustainability.

Key points:
- **Quality & consistency**: 100 % of NPS respondents in 2025 were satisfied or very satisfied with product quality; 94 % were satisfied with customer service.
- **Product range**: Over 30 malts, including base, highly kilned, crystal, roasted, special, unmalted adjuncts and distilling malts.
- **Supply chain**: Direct contracts with UK farmers (≈95 % of grain) via MSP; traceability from seed to bottle.
- **Sustainability**: B Corp certified; investments in Scope 1‑3 emissions reduction and partnership with growers.
- **Industry reputation**: Positive testimonials from brewers such as Cheviot, Exale, Elusive, Ampersand, Tomatin, Adnams, Durham and Glenturret.

**Chivas Brothers Growers Group Event (13 Mar 2026)**

- Over 60 contracted farmers from the Highlands to northern England attended.
- Participants represented Simpsons Malt, Chivas Brothers and the James Hutton Institute.
- Focus: sustainability, market dynamics, grain quality and supply chain alignment.
- Key speakers: Andy Hindhaugh (Commercial Director), Ronald Daalmans (Sustainability Director), David Barrett (Fertiliser Director), Sarah Hynes & Claire Strachan (grain team).
- Practical sessions included Q&A, data‑driven tools (xarvio Field Manager, Cool Farm Tool) and a briefing on the Chivas Brothers Steering Group.
- Positive feedback highlighted strengthened relationships and shared understanding of sector challenges.

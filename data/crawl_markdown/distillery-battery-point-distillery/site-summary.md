# Battery Point Distillery – Online Shop

The shop page on https://batterypointdistillery.com presents a curated selection of single‑malt whiskies, specialty finishes, and the House of Lenna gin range. Products are grouped into:

* **Core Range** – Bourbon‑cask, Port‑cask and Sherry‑cask single malts (all priced $45–$165 GST inclusive).
* **Specialty Releases** – Triple Cask (blend of port, bourbon and sherry), Red IPA Finish (finished in ex‑muscat & ex‑whisky casks from a local brewery) and Shiraz‑Cask Matured (aged in South Australian Shiraz barrels). Prices for these range $49–$225 GST inclusive.
* **House of Lenna** – A boutique gin line that includes the classic Gin, Pink Gin (infused with Tasmanian strawberries), and Sloe Gin. Prices vary from $28 to $98 GST inclusive.
* **Private Label & Gift Cards** – The Lenna Private Label whisky is a cask‑strength single malt distilled in Tasmania’s Central Highlands; gift cards are available for instant email delivery.

All products list alcohol content, bottle size, tasting notes and purchase links. Shipping is free on orders over $200 AUD. The site includes standard e‑commerce features (cart, account login, newsletter signup) and legal age verification under the Liquor Licensing Act 1990. Contact details are provided for sales, tours and general enquiries.

# Bourbon Cask Single Malt

Battery Point Distillery’s **Bourbon Cask Single Malt** is a 46.9 % ABV whisky matured exclusively in bourbon barrels. The cask influence is subtle, allowing the spirit’s natural brightness and grain character to shine through.

- **Nose:** vanilla bean, honeycomb, citrus oils – clean and uplifting.
- **Palate:** vanilla custard, sweet grain, lemon zest, light tropical fruit.
- **Finish:** fresh, medium‑length with vanilla, oak and a faint herbal lift.
- Available in 100 ml, 200 ml and 500 ml bottles. Price range: $45–$165 (incl. GST).

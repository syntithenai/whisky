# Shipping – Battery Point Distillery

Battery Point Distillery ships all orders directly from its Tasmania distillery to every Australian state via Australia Post. Orders are hand‑packed and dispatched within one business day, with standard delivery taking 4–8 working days and express delivery 2–4 working days. International shipping is not permitted for the USA, Canada or Mexico due to legal restrictions.

Customers receive automatic email confirmations; if none arrive, they should contact admin@batterypointdistillery.com. Tracking can be done on AusPost’s website. A 10 % discount is offered on first orders via newsletter signup.

Legal age verification is required before accessing the page, and the Liquor Licensing Act 1990 penalties for under‑age delivery or purchase are highlighted.

# Shipping – Battery Point Distillery
All orders are shipped directly from the distillery in Battery Point, Tasmania to every Australian state via Australia Post. Orders placed outside Australia can be arranged by email; shipping to the USA, Canada or Mexico is prohibited by law. Parcels are hand‑packed and dispatched within one business day, with Standard Post taking 4–8 working days and Express Post 2–4 working days. Customers receive an automatic confirmation email and can track their shipment on AusPost’s website. During peak periods or public holidays delays may occur.

> **Legal notice** – Under the Liquor Licensing Act 1990, it is illegal to deliver alcohol to anyone under 18 years old or for a person under 18 to purchase liquor.

Customers can sign up for a newsletter to receive a 10 % discount on their first order and stay informed about new releases and events.

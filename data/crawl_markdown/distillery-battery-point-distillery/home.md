# Battery Point Distillery

Small-batch, hand-crafted quality spirits

## About Us
- Located in Hobart's historic Battery Point
- Small stone building with a treasure trove of small batch, hand-made whiskies
- Slowly ageing in carefully sourced port, sherry, tawny and bourbon casks
- Painstakingly double-distilled in tiny quantities

## Our Whiskies
- Double-distilled from 100% Tasmanian malt mash and pure Tasmanian water
- Unique nuances of single, double or triple cask ageing
- Currently offering:
  - Sherry Cask Single Malt
  - Bourbon Cask Single Malt
  - Port Cask Single Malt
  - Small Cask Sherry Size
  - Shiraz Cask Matured
  - Lenna Private Label
  - Red IPA Finish
  - Triple Cask
- Limited edition bottles available

## Our Story
- Born from a passion for Tasmania and the respected traditions of master whisky distilling
- Proudly Tasmanian
- Hand-crafted with love and attention to detail

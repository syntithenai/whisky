# Battery Point Distillery
Battery Point Distillery is a small‑batch, hand‑crafted whisky producer based in Hobart’s historic Battery Point. The distillery uses 100 % Tasmanian malt mash and pure Tasmanian water, double‑distilling each batch in tiny quantities. Spirits are aged in carefully sourced port, sherry, tawny and bourbon casks, with some expressions also receiving a third cask finish. Products include single‑malt whiskies finished in sherry, bourbon, port, small‑cask sherry, Shiraz, Red IPA, and a triple‑cask blend. The distillery is run by head distiller Luke Lawrence (formerly Jack Lark) and offers tours, tastings, gift cards and private label options.

# Battery Point Distillery
Battery Point Distillery is a small‑batch, hand‑crafted Tasmanian whisky producer based in Hobart’s historic Battery Point. The distillery uses 100 % Tasmanian malt and pure local water, double‑distilling each batch in tiny quantities. Spirits are aged in carefully sourced port, sherry, tawny and bourbon casks, with options for single, double or triple cask finishes.

## Distillery Highlights
- Founded by Jack Lark, now led by Head Distiller Luke Lawrence.
- Located at the historic Lenna House of Hobart.
- Offers tours and tastings; contact via phone or email.
- Free standard shipping on orders over $200.

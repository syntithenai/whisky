# Battery Point Distillery Tours

Battery Point Distillery is the only working distillery in Hobart’s CBD, offering **interactive tours** (≈40 min) and **tastings** of its boutique gin and single‑malt whisky. Tours run Monday–Friday; walk‑in tastings are available 10 am–4 pm for $15 per person (three 5 ml samples). Bookings can be made via the page form or by contacting Distiller Luke Lawrence.

- **Location**: 20 Runnymede St, Battery Point, Tasmania – just 100 m from Salamanca Place.
- **Retail sales** are available during tours and tastings.
- **Contact**: 0417 440 281 (Luke), 0412 665 745 (sales).

# Battery Point Distillery - Hobart, Tasmania

## Key Facts
- Only working distillery in the CBD area of Hobart
- Offers interactive tours and tastings of boutique gin and single malt whisky
- Located near Salamanca Place and waterfront area
- Tours (~40 minutes) include principles of distilling, hands-on production experience, history of the distillery, and tastings of current release products
- Walk-in tastings available Monday to Friday 10am-4pm ($15pp for three 5ml tastings)
- Retail sales available at all tours and tastings

## Production Signals
- Boutique gin and single malt whisky production
- Small batch distilling process
- Uses Lenna building as its home

## Commercial Signals
- Interactive tours and tastings attract visitors to the CBD area
- Limited edition releases like Islay Cask #2 Shiraz Cask Matured Red IPA Finish, Port Cask Triple Cask, and Lenna Private Label
- Collaborations with other distilleries for private label products

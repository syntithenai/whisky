# Battery Point Distillery – About
Battery Point Distillery is a small‑batch, hand‑made Tasmanian whisky producer located in Hobart’s historic Battery Point. Founded by Jack Lark and run by Head Distiller Luke Lawrence, the distillery uses 100 % Tasmanian malt mash and pure Tasmanian water, double‑distilling each batch in tiny quantities. Whisky is aged in a mix of vintage Spanish sherry, tawny, port, brandy and newer bourbon casks, with single, double or triple cask variations creating unique character.

Key facts:
- Location: 20 Runnymede St, Battery Point, Tasmania
- Distillers: Jack Lark (founder), Luke Lawrence (Head Distiller)
- Production: small‑batch, double‑distilled, Tasmanian malt & water
- Cask types: Spanish sherry, tawny, port, brandy, bourbon
- Products: Islay Cask #2, Shiraz Cask Matured, Red IPA Finish, Port Cask Triple, Lenna Private Label

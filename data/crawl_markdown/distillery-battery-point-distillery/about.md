# Battery Point Distillery – About
Battery Point Distillery is a small‑batch, hand‑made whisky producer located in Hobart’s historic Battery Point. Founded by Jack Lark and run by Head Distiller Luke Lawrence, the distillery uses 100 % Tasmanian malt mash and pure Tasmanian water. Whiskies are double‑distilled in tiny batches and aged in a mix of vintage Spanish sherry, tawny, port, brandy and newer bourbon casks, with single, double or triple cask variations.

Key facts:
- Location: 20 Runnymede St, Battery Point, Tasmania
- Distillation: double‑distilled by Luke Lawrence
- Cask types: Spanish sherry, tawny, port, brandy, bourbon
- Production focus: small batches, unique character per batch

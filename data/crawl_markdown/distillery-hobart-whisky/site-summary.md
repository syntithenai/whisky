# Hobart Whisky

Hobart Whisky is a family‑owned distillery founded in 2014 in Moonah, Tasmania. The company focuses on crafting whisky for local enjoyment and sharing, using ex‑bourbon casks extensively for maturation and finishing. Their portfolio includes limited releases such as the Rum & Muscat Cask Finish, Sherry Cask Finish, and a Winter Release 2025 featuring rum, brandy, and vintage tawny casks. In addition to single malts, Hobart Whisky offers a range of liqueurs—Tasmanian Whisky Liqueur (honey‑flavoured), Tasmanian Cherry Chocolate Liqueur, Whisky Coffee Liqueur—and a barrel‑aged cherry gin.

The distillery has earned multiple accolades: Gold, Silver and Bronze at the 2025 World Drink Awards (Gold for Australia Small Batch) and Best Honey Liqueur in the 2022 World Liqueur Awards. Visitors can collect products free of charge on site, and the distillery is located at Gormanston Rd, Moonah TAS 7009.

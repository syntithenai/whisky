## Long Description
# Hobart Whisky

Hobart Whisky is a family‑owned distillery founded in 2014 in Moonah, Tasmania. The company focuses on crafting whisky for its own enjoyment and sharing it with others. Their first release was an American oak ex‑bourbon matured whisky launched during Tasmanian Whisky Week in August 2018.

## Production & Cask Strategy
- **Base spirit**: Single malt whisky distilled from local barley.
- **Cask finishes**: Primarily ex‑bourbon casks; additional finishes include sherry, rum, maple, brandy and vintage tawny. The use of ex‑bourbon casks imparts vanilla, caramel and subtle spice notes.
- **Liqueurs & gin**: Hobart also produces a Tasmanian Whisky Liqueur (honey‑based) and barrel‑aged gin.

## Long Description
# Hobart Whisky

Hobart Whisky is a family‑owned distillery founded in 2014 in Moonah, Tasmania. The company focuses on creating whisky for local enjoyment and sharing, using ex‑bourbon casks extensively to develop complex flavour profiles. Their product range includes limited releases such as Rum & Muscat, Sherry, and Tawny cask finishes, a core Signature Single Malt Whisky, and a selection of liqueurs (Tasmanian Whisky Liqueur, Tasmanian Cherry Chocolate Liqueur, Whisky Coffee Liqueur) plus barrel‑aged Tasmanian Cherry Gin.

## Awards & Recognition

- Best Honey Liqueur at the World Liqueur Awards 2022

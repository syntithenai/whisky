**The Fleurieu Distillery – Our Story**

- Began as a craft brewery (Steam Exchange Brewery) on Goolwa Wharf in 2004.
- Transitioned to whisky production, with the first still commissioned in 2013.
- Emphasises patience, precision and continuous pursuit of excellence.
- Whisky is positioned as a bridge between tradition and reinvention, rooted in South Australia’s character.
- Each bottle reflects Goolwa’s heritage and the distillery’s commitment to lasting quality.

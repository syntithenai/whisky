# About Backwoods Distilling Co.

Welcome to Backwoods Distilling Co — a family-run craft distillery based in Yackandandah, Victoria, Australia. We make award-winning whisky and gin using 100% Australian-grown grains and local barrels.

## Our Story
- Founded by Bree and Leigh
- Started in a modest shed on their property
- Crafted with care, using local grain, native botanicals, and barrels that tell an Australian story
- Every bottle is still crafted with the same dedication to quality and storytelling

## Our Spirits
- **Whisky**: Starts with heritage grains like rye, wheat, and barley grown in the Riverina. Matured in reclaimed fortified wine casks, Aussie shiraz barrels, and rare Red Gum timber casks.
- **Gin**: Showcases native ingredients like old man saltbush and native lime, capturing the aromas and flavours of the Australian landscape.

## Awards & Recognition
- Recognised nationally and internationally with multiple medals and trophies
- Champion Victorian Distiller
- Only distillery to win Best Single Malt Whisky trophy twice at the Australian Distilled Spirits Awards
- Rye whisky won the Rye Category Award at the World Whiskies Awards twice
- International Wine and Spirits Awards: 95/100 for rye whisky, 93/100 for single malt whisky

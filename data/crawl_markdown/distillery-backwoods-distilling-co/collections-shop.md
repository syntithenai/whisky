# Backwoods Distilling Co.

Australian whisky and gin brand offering handcrafted spirits with unique flavor profiles inspired by the Australian landscape and heritage.

## Key Facts
- Handmade in small batches using locally sourced ingredients
- Range includes single malt whiskies, rye whiskies, gins, liqueurs and flavored offerings
- Aged in reclaimed oak barrels from Australia's corn whisky industry
- Collaborations with local artists for limited edition bottles
- Focus on storytelling through packaging and product naming

## Production Signals
- Small batch production emphasizing quality over quantity
- Use of reclaimed barrels adds distinct character to spirits
- Emphasis on Australian ingredients like bush honey, blood orange and citrus
- Limited editions showcase special reserve expressions

## Commercial Signals
- Collaborations with artists for branded packaging
- Story-driven marketing highlighting heritage and terroir
- Positioning as premium artisanal spirits
- Online shop allows direct-to-consumer sales

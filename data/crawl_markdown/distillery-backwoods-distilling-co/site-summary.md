# Backwoods Distilling Co.

Backwoods Distilling Co., founded in 2017 and based in the high‑country town of Yackandandah, Victoria, specialises in handcrafted single‑malt whiskies and botanically‑infused gins. The family‑run distillery uses local Australian grains, native botanicals and regional casks to create distinctive spirits such as the **Single Malt Red Gum Smoked Whisky**, **Blood Orange & Bush Honey Gin**, **Salted Lime Gin** and a **Peanut Brittle Whisky Liqueur**.

## Product Highlights

## Distillery Experience
Backwoods offers a *hand‑fill* tasting experience at its distillery, complete with copper stills and a welcoming atmosphere. Visitors can book tours online or in person.

## Media & Awards
- 2024 World Whiskies Awards: Category Winner for Backwoods’ rye whisky.
- Featured in *The Australian*, *Broadsheet* and *Drinks Adventures* podcasts.
- Collaborations include a small‑batch Muscat Gin with Scion Wine (2021) and ongoing expansion into aged spirits.

## Community & Sustainability
Backwoods emphasises sustainable production, local sourcing of grains and botanicals, and community engagement. Customer reviews highlight the friendly, family‑run nature of the business and its commitment to quality.

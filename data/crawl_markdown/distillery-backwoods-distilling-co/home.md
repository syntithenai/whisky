# Backwoods Distilling Co.

Backwoods is a family‑run distillery based in Yackandandah, Victoria, that produces small‑batch whisky and gin using Australian grain, native botanicals, and local casks. Their flagship products include the **Single Malt Red Gum Smoked Whisky** (700 ml, $175) and several gins such as Blood Orange & Bush Honey Gin, Salted Lime Gin, and a Peanut Brittle Whisky Liqueur. The distillery offers an on‑site tasting experience and has been featured in various Australian whisky publications.

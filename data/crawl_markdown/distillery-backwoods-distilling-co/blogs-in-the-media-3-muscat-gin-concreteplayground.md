# Concrete Playground: Muscat Gin Collaboration
Backwoods Distilling Co. and Scion Wine released a small‑batch Muscat Gin in 2021, celebrating the heritage of Victoria’s muscat region and the innovative spirit of winemaker Rowly Milhinch and co‑founder Leigh Attwood. The collaboration highlights sustainable techniques and has been well received, prompting a second release this winter.

## Key Points
- Small‑batch Muscat Gin produced by Backwoods Distilling Co. & Scion Wine.
- Collaboration between winemaker Rowly Milhinch (Scion) and co‑founder Leigh Attwood (Backwoods).
- Focus on innovation, nostalgia, and sustainable production.
- First release in 2021; second release announced for the current winter season.

# Australian whiskies celebrated on global stage
Backwoods Distilling Co. was named a Category Winner at the 2024 World Whiskies Awards, joining nine other Australian distilleries such as Coastal Stone, Eden, Hillwood Whisky, Iniquity, Lark, Sullivans Cove, The Gospel, Transportation Whiskey, Waubs Harbour Distillery and Whipper Snapper.

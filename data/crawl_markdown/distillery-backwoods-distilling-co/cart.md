# Backwoods Distilling Co.

Backwoods Distilling Co. is an Australian single malt whisky brand known for its unique hand-fill experience and special reserve series whiskies. The company showcases one-off expressions that highlight the distinct flavors of red gum smoke.

## Key Facts
- Hand-fill experience allows customers to personalize their whisky bottles
- Special Reserve Series features limited-edition, one-off whiskies with unique characteristics
- Single malt whiskies made from locally sourced grains and aged in Australian oak barrels
- Distillery located in Australia, adhering to strict liquor regulations

## Production Signals
- Limited availability of special reserve expressions due to small-batch production methods
- Use of red gum wood for smoking during the maturation process, imparting a distinct flavor profile
- Careful selection of grains and aging techniques to create unique taste profiles

## Commercial Signals

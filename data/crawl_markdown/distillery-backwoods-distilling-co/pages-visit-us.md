# Backwoods Distilling Co - Yackandandah Whisky and Gin Distillery

Welcome to Backwoods Distilling Co, your gateway to the world of award-winning spirits in the heart of Yackandandah. Our craft distillery offers:
- Guided tastings of our whiskies and gins
- Seasonal cocktails and mixed drinks
- The unique Whisky Hand Fill Experience where you can bottle your own exclusive whisky
- A glimpse into our production area with copper stills on display
- Bike-friendly facilities including bike racks, free water refills, and easy parking options
- Dog-friendly outdoor areas for your furry companions

## Key Facts
- Open Wednesday to Sunday, 11am–4pm
- Guided tastings available by appointment or walk-in (recommended for groups)
- Limited releases and distillery exclusives in our retail shop
- The Whisky Hand Fill Experience allows you to bottle your own whisky from the barrel

## Production Signals
- All mashing, fermenting, and distilling happens at the distillery door site
- Copper stills on display for visitors to see the production process firsthand

## Commercial Signals
- Award-winning spirits available for purchase both in-store and online
- Special experiences like the Whisky Hand Fill Experience create memorable brand interactions
- Limited releases and exclusives build a loyal customer base

## Risks/Unknowns
- The distillery's immersive experiences may not be suitable for very young children, though family-friendly accommodations are made with nearby parks and dog-friendly areas

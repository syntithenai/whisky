**Glen Scotia 25‑Year‑Old Single Malt**

- Awarded a *Master Medal* at the Spirits Business Scotch Whisky Masters (June 2021).
- Previously won *Best Scotch Whisky in Show* at the San Francisco Spirits Competition.
- Other Glen Scotia releases that year received gold or master medals: Victoriana, Double Cask 10‑yr, 15‑yr and 18‑yr.

The post highlights these accolades and expresses team pride.

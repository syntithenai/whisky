## Long Description
# Glen Scotia Whisky Overview

**Location & Heritage**
- Campbeltown, Scotland – the historic “whisky capital” of the world.
- Part of the Loch Lomond Group, the distillery has been producing single malts since 2005.

- **32 Year Old Single Malt** – only 99 bottles, hand‑selected first‑fill & refill bourbon casks.

## Tasting Experiences
Glen Scotia hosts several on‑site tastings:
- Core Range (4 whiskies) – £25 per person.
- Limited Editions (cask‑strength drams) – £35 per person.
- Warehouse Selection – £50 per person, five drams chosen by the Master Distiller.
- Premium Tasting – £75 per person, featuring heavily aged whiskies and Double Platinum.
- Whisky & Chocolate Pairing – £20 per person with FETCHA chocolates.

All tastings require advance booking; details are available on the distillery’s website.

## Visitor Experience
The Victorian‑styled shop in Campbeltown offers a “dunnage warehouse” atmosphere, complete with a selection of core and limited‑edition whiskies for purchase. Free UK shipping is available for orders over £350.

**Key Takeaway:** Glen Scotia delivers classic Campbeltown character through meticulous first‑fill bourbon maturation, complemented by a range of aged expressions and immersive tasting events that showcase the distillery’s heritage and craftsmanship.

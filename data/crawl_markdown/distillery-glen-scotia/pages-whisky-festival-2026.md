**Glen Scotia Whisky Festival 2026 – Campbeltown**

- **Festival Dinner** – 19 May 2026, 6:30 pm, Town Hall, £149 + booking fee. Includes a complimentary 70 cl bottle of the Festival Dinner single‑cask.
- **Dunnage Warehouse Tasting** – 20 May 2026, 10:30 am & 2 pm, Dunnage Warehouse, £50 + booking fee. Master Blender Ashley Smith guides tasting of hand‑selected casks.
- **Big Campbeltown Character** – 20 May 2026, 12:30 pm & 4 pm, Old Kiln, £38 + booking fee. Focus on new 12‑year‑old and award‑winning 25‑year‑old spirits.
- **Deconstructed Festival Tasting** – 20 May 2026, 10:30 am & 2 pm, Old Kiln, £28 + booking fee. Global Brand Ambassador Gary Mills deconstructs the 2026 Campbeltown Malts Festival Release.
- **Port to Port** – 20 May 2026, 12 pm & 3:30 pm, Dunnage Warehouse, £38 + booking fee. Ashley Smith explores historical trading casks (Sherry, Rum, Port).
- **Distillery Tour** – 20 May 2026, multiple times, free.
- **Whisky History Walking Tour** – 20 May 2026, multiple times, free.

All events are sold out; waiting lists available via the site.

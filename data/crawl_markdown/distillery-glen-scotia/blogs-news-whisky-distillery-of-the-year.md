# Glen Scotia Wins Scottish Whisky Distillery of the Year
Glen Scotia was awarded *Scottish Whisky Distillery of the Year* at the 3rd annual Scottish Whisky Awards in Glasgow, following a blind tasting of 200 Scotch whiskies and assessment of 58 distilleries. The award praised the distillery’s community commitment, product development and high‑quality standards during a challenging year.

- **Best Whisky in the World** – Glen Scotia 25‑Year‑Old (SFWSC 2021)
- **Gold Medal** – Glen Scotia 15‑Year‑Old (Single Malt 13‑16 yr category)
- **Silver Award** – Glen Scotia Double Cask (No Age Statement)

Master Distiller Iain McAlister highlighted Campbeltown’s resurgence and the distillery’s focus on quality.

> *“Our distillery is small, but this allows us to focus on quality, and it is so humbling to once again be recognised for our craft.”*

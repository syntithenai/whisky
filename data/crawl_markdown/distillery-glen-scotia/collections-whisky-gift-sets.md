# Glen Scotia Whisky Gift Sets

Glen Scotia offers a curated range of whisky gift sets aimed at every level of enthusiast—from casual fans to single‑malt connoisseurs. Each set includes a bottle (or miniatures), tasting notes, and often bespoke glassware or a presentation box.

# Glen Scotia Distillery Tours

- **Location**: Campbeltown, Scotland (12 High Street, PA28 6DS)
- **Tour options**:
  - *Whisky Distillery Tour*: 1‑hour, £15 per person + booking fee. Available Mon‑Fri at 11 am or 2 pm.
  - *Dunnage Warehouse Experience*: 2‑hour, £65 (or £55 tasting only) per person + booking fee. Available Apr‑Oct Mon‑Fri at 2 pm.
  - *Whisky Heritage Walking Tour*: 1.5–2 hours, £30 per person + booking fee. By request.
- **Highlights**: Guided walk through the working distillery, behind‑the‑scenes look at mashing, fermentation, distillation and maturation; complimentary dram of Double Cask single malt on the distillery tour; five‑dram cask‑strength tasting in the Dunnage Warehouse.
- **Booking**: Via links on site or by email (shop@glenscotia.com) / phone (+44 1586 552288). Visitor Centre closed 3–6 Feb 2026 for maintenance.

# Finalist for Scottish Whisky Awards
Glen Scotia has been shortlisted in several categories at the 2020 Scottish Whisky Awards, including both business and tasting awards.

**Business categories**
- Product Launch of the Year – *Glen Scotia Tawny Port*
- Tourism Destination of the Year – *Glen Scotia, Campbeltown*

**Tasting categories**
- Single Malt 13‑16 yr: *Glen Scotia 15 yr*
- Single Malt 13‑16 yr: *Glen Scotia Campbeltown Malts Festival 2020*
- Single Malt 17‑20 yr: *Glen Scotia 18 yr*
- Single Malt 21 yr & over: *Glen Scotia 25 yr*
- Single Malt No Age Statement: *Glen Scotia Double Cask* and *Glen Scotia Victoriana*

The awards ceremony will be held virtually on **19 November 2020**.

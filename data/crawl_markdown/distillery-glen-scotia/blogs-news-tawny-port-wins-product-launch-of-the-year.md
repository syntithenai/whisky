# Glen Scotia 14‑Year‑Old Tawny Port

- Awarded **Product Launch of the Year** at the 2020 Scottish Whisky Awards.
- Limited‑edition single malt, aged 14 years and finished in tawny port casks.
- Launched to celebrate the Campbeltown Malts Festival (digitally due to COVID‑19).
- Promotion included virtual tastings, distillery tours, social media campaigns and other digital activities.

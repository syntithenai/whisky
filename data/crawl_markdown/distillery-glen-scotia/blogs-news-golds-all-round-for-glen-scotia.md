# Glen Scotia – Scotch Whisky Masters Gold Awards

- **Event**: 2020 Scotch Whisky Masters (Spirits Business Magazine)
- **Result**: 5 gold medals awarded to Glen Scotia drams.
- **Medal recipients**: Double Cask, 15‑Year‑Old, Victoriana, 18‑Year‑Old and 25‑Year‑Old.

The awards were decided by blind tastings across appearance, palate and overall balance. The blog post celebrates the achievement and invites readers to comment.

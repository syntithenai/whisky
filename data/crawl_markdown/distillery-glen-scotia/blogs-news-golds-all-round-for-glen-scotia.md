# Glen Scotia – Scotch Whisky Masters Gold Awards

- **Event**: 2020 Scotch Whisky Masters (Spirits Business Magazine)
- **Result**: Glen Scotia received **5 gold medals** across its portfolio.
- **Medal winners**:
  - Double Cask, 15 Year‑Old
  - Victoriana, 18 Year‑Old
  - 25 Year‑Old
- The awards cover single malt and blended whiskies judged blind‑tasting on appearance, palate and overall balance.

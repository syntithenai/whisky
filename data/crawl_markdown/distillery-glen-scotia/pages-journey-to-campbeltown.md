# Journey to Campbeltown
Glen Scotia partners with three top Scottish travel influencers—Chris Lawlor, Graeme Johncock and Barbara Gajewska—to showcase the Kintyre Peninsula by foot, sea and road. Each influencer shares their experience on social media and culminates at Glen Scotia’s distillery where visitors meet Master Distiller Iain McAlister.

The page promotes a free exhibition at this year’s Campbeltown Malts Festival, inviting fans to explore the region’s coastline, history and heritage through the influencers’ journeys.

# Glen Scotia 50‑Year‑Old Whisky – Elements of Campbeltown Release #1: Air
- **Age & Distillation**: 50 years, distilled in 1973, bottled 2024.
- **ABV**: Natural cask strength 42.3 %.
- **Casks**: First‑fill and refill American oak hogsheads.
- **Flavor profile**: Salinity, baked orchard & tropical fruit notes; aromas of flaked almonds, vanilla fudge, salt crystals, seaweed, glazed apple tart; flavors of creamy sweet/salty texture, white pepper, powdered ginger, coconut finish.
- **Packaging**: 100 individually numbered crystal decanters with a bespoke Glencairn crystal decanter and Celtic‑knot brass stopper.
- **Price & Availability**: RRP £35 000, sold by enquiry only.
- **Historical context**: Glen Scotia founded 1832; one of three surviving Campbeltown distilleries.
- **Creative collaboration**: Artist Gina Parr created an oil painting and bespoke decanter design inspired by Campbeltown’s coastal air.

> *“Matured exclusively in American oak, with its more subtle, supportive cask influence, the character shines through.”* – Michael Henry, Master Blender.

# Glen Scotia 50‑Year‑Old Release
- **Product**: *Glen Scotia Elements of Campbeltown – Release No. 1: Air* (50 yr old single malt)
- **Age & Distillation**: distilled 1973, bottled 2024 at natural cask strength 42.3 % ABV.
- **Casks**: first‑fill and refill American oak hogsheads.
- **Flavor profile**: salinity, baked orchard & tropical fruit notes; aromas of flaked almonds, vanilla fudge, salt crystals, seaweed, glazed apple tart; finish with coconut.
- **Packaging**: 100 individually numbered crystal decanters (Glencairn crystal), RRP £35 000.
- **Art collaboration**: UK artist Gina Parr created an oil painting and bespoke decanter design inspired by Campbeltown air.
- **Historical context**: Glen Scotia founded 1832, one of three surviving distilleries in Campbeltown.

## Key facts
- First release in the *Elements of Campbeltown* collection.
- Inspired by Celtic five‑fold knot (air, water, fire, earth, spirit).
- Limited edition – only 100 decanters worldwide.
- Available by enquiry via website or specialist retailers.

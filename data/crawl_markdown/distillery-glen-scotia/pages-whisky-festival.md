# Glen Scotia Whisky Festival 2025

Glen Scotia’s Campbeltown Malts Festival 2025 was a two‑day celebration of whisky culture held at the distillery and courtyard. Highlights included:
- **Special cask‑strength tastings** open to all visitors.
- **Events & sessions** led by key staff: Iain McAlister (Distillery Manager), Ashley Smith (Master Blender), Gary Mills (Global Brand Ambassador) and Alasdair Day of R&B Distillers.
- **Tours, walking tours and a tasting bar** staffed by the Glen Scotia team.

The festival was praised for its atmosphere, live music and educational talks. The distillery thanked attendees worldwide and announced plans for the 2026 edition.

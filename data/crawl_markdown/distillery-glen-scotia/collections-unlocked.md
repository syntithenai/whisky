# Glen Scotia Unlocked

Glen Scotia’s *Unlocked* programme offers limited‑edition single‑cask releases, special offers and exclusive online tastings for members. The current catalogue includes nine releases ranging from 5‑year‑old first‑fill bourbon to a 15‑year‑old hidden reserve finished in oloroso. All bottles are 70 cl with ABV between 54.2% and 58.5%. Membership grants access to these casks and other perks.

# Glen Scotia Unlocked

Glen Scotia Unlocked is a membership community for whisky enthusiasts that offers:
- Early and exclusive access to limited‑edition releases (e.g., Malts Festival 2025, single‑cask expressions).
- Invitations to virtual tastings, online experiences and round‑table discussions.
- Member‑only offers such as birthday rewards.

Members can sign up on the site to receive these benefits and stay connected with the Campbeltown distillery’s community events.

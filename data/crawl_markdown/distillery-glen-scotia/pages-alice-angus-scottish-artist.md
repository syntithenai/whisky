# Alice Angus Scottish Artist – Glen Scotia

Glen Scotia partners with artist **Alice Angus** to create a limited‑edition bottling of the *Spirit Safe* series, inspired by the distillery’s history and Campbeltown landscape. Four paintings—*First Water*, *Heart of the Run*, *Labour of Years*, and *Time and Tide*—are each paired with a distinct whisky variant priced at £2 000.

The project showcases how Angus’s observational drawing, using pencil, watercolour and ink, mirrors the patience and craftsmanship of whisky production. Her residency involved spending time in the mash room, stills, warehouses and coastal surroundings to capture sensory details that inform both the artwork and the spirit.

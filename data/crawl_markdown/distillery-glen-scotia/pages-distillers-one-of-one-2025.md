# Distillers One of One 2025 – Glen Scotia

- **Four‑Quarters Collection**: A unique set of four 25‑year‑old single malts, all distilled in November 1999 and finished in historic casks.
- **Cask finishes**:
  - American Oak (51.9 % ABV)
  - Rum finish – two years in a first‑fill Caribbean rum barrel (56.6 % ABV)
  - Oloroso sherry finish – three years in a first‑fill Oloroso hogshead (52.9 % ABV)
  - Pedro Ximénez sherry finish – four years in a first‑fill Pedro Ximénez hogshead (52.8 % ABV)
- **Presentation**: Handcrafted oak cabinet with gold‑plated, hallmarked details by Hamilton & Inches; each whisky housed in a 30 % lead‑crystal Glencairn decanter.
- **Auction**: Bidding runs 15 Sept–10 Oct 2025 via The Distillers One of One and Sotheby’s. Proceeds support the Distillers’ Charity.

> *“Four Quarters is a tribute to Glen Scotia’s maritime heritage.”* – Michael Henry, Master Blender, Loch Lomond Group

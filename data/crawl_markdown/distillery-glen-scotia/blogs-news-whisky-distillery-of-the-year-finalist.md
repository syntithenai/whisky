# Glen Scotia

Glen Scotia, a Campbeltown distillery, has been named a **Distillery of the Year finalist** in the 2021 Scottish Whisky Awards. The award recognises both business excellence and whisky quality, with judging panels covering marketing, tourism, innovation and blind tastings.

The distillery highlighted its recent achievements:
- *25‑Year‑Old* awarded Best in Show at the San Francisco World Spirits Challenge.
- Several single malts shortlisted for tasting categories (e.g., Double Cask, Victoriana, Campbeltown Harbour, 15‑Year‑Old, 18‑Year‑Old, 25‑Year‑Old).

Glen Scotia continues to engage customers through virtual tastings and community outreach despite a challenging year.

> *“We look forward to hearing the results at the ceremony on St Andrews Day, 30th November.”*

# Glen Scotia Distillery – Campbeltown, Scotland

Glen Scotia is one of the smallest yet most historic Scotch whisky distilleries in Campbeltown, founded in 1832 as Scotia Distillery. The site retains original 19th‑century equipment such as a mash tun and stillroom, and it continues to produce both peated and non‑peated single malts using traditional methods. The distillery is part of the Loch Lomond Group and offers tours, tastings, and a range of award‑winning expressions including festival editions and limited releases.

- **Location**: 12 High Street, Campbeltown (Wee Toon)
- **Founded**: 1832
- **Key features**: historic buildings, two honey hives, traditional distillation
- **Product focus**: peated & non‑peated single malts, festival editions, limited seasonal releases
- **Visitor experience**: tours, tastings, whisky festival

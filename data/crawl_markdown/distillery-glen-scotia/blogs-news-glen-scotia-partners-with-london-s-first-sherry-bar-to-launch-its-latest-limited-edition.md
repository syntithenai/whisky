# Seasonal Release 2022 – Andalusia Flavours

Glen Scotia has teamed up with London’s first dedicated sherry bar, **Bar Pepito**, to launch a 12‑year‑old, cask‑strength single malt. The dram is finished in Amontillado sherry casks from Montilla, Spain, after initial maturation in first‑fill bourbon barrels. It delivers warm, nutty tones with vanilla and a dry finish, accented by subtle maritime nuances typical of Campbeltown style.

The partnership also produced a menu of Spanish‑inspired pairings – whisky‑marinated olives, jamón ibérico, Manchego/Parmesan cheeses, blue cheeses, smoked cheese, and dark chocolate – designed to complement the release’s flavour profile.

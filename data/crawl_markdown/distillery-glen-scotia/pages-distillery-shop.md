# Glen Scotia Distillery Shop

The Glen Scotia distillery shop is located at the visitor centre in Campbeltown, Scotland. It sells a range of products including single‑malt whiskies (e.g., *Unlocked*, *Malts Festival Editions*, *Old & Rare Collection*), whisky gift sets, personalised bottles, miniatures, glassware and branded merchandise. The shop offers on‑site whisky tastings in a Victorian‑styled setting where staff provide tasting notes and product information. Customers can book tasting sessions online.

The page also lists shipping options for the UK, Ireland, Isle of Man, France and the United States, with currency displayed accordingly. Standard e‑commerce support links (delivery, returns, FAQs) are provided, as well as contact details for Loch Lomond Group at 12 High Street, Campbeltown.

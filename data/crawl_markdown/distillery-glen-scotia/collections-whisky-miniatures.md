# Glen Scotia Whisky Miniatures

Glen Scotia offers a range of **Campbeltown single‑malt miniatures** (5 cl and 20 cl) that capture the region’s distinctive flavours. The collection includes individual miniatures such as the *Victoriana* (54.2 % ABV, £8.00), a 15‑year‑old (46 % ABV, £7.00), and a Double Cask single‑malt (46 % ABV, £5.00). Gift sets are available in 20 cl or 5 cl formats, priced between £19.00 and £35.00.

The miniatures serve as an introductory tasting experience to Campbeltown whisky, offering a portable way to explore the region’s heritage.

# Glen Scotia - Campbeltown Single Malt Scotch Whisky

- **Location**: Historic whisky-producing region of Campbeltown, Scotland (Kintyre Peninsula)
- **Established**: 1832
- **Distillery Design**: Original fermenters, stillhouse and dunnage warehouse from the 1830s
- **Expressions**: Peated & unpeated single malts, including core range releases, limited editions and unique casks
- **Philosophy**: "Whisky from the whiskiest place in the world" - Campbeltown's rich distilling heritage
- **Awards**: Glen Scotia Distillery of the Year 2021 (Scotland)

## Our Story & Heritage
- Single malt Scotch whisky distillery with a long history
- Maintains much of its original design and architecture from the 1830s
- Offers both peated and unpeated single malts, including core range releases, limited editions and unique casks

## Whisky Making Process
- Matured in first-fill ex-Bourbon casks before a six-month finish in first-fill Ruby Port casks for Festival Editions
- Known for vibrant, medium-peated expressions reflecting Campbeltown's signature style

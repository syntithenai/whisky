# Glen Scotia – Campbeltown Single Malt Distillery

Glen Scotia is a historic single‑malt Scotch whisky distillery in Campbeltown, founded in 1832. The site retains its original fermenters, stillhouse and dunnage warehouse. It produces a range of peated and unpeated expressions, including core releases, limited editions and cask‑specific bottlings such as the **Campbeltown Malts Festival Edition 2026** (7 yr, medium peated, ex‑bourbon & ruby port finish) and the **Victoriana Single Malt Scotch Whisky**. The distillery has earned accolades such as *Scotland’s Whisky Distillery of the Year 2021*.

The website offers online sales, tours, tastings, a gift‑set catalogue, personalised bottlings, and merchandise. It also hosts a blog with news about festivals, recipes and new releases.

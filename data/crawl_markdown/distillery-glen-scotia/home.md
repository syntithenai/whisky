# Glen Scotia – Campbeltown Single Malt Distillery

- **Location**: Campbeltown, Kintyre Peninsula, Scotland.
- **Founded**: 1832 (original design largely intact).
- **Products**: Core range of peated and unpeated single malts, limited editions, and cask‑specific releases such as the *Campbeltown Malts Festival Edition 2026* (7 yr, medium peated, ex‑Bourbon & Ruby Port finish) and the *Victoriana* expression.
- **Awards**: Scotland’s Whisky Distillery of the Year 2021.
- **Retail**: Online shop with global shipping; offers gift sets, personalised bottles, miniatures, glassware, and merchandise.
- **Tours & Tastings**: Visitor centre, distillery tours, and whisky tastings available.
- **Website Features**: Blog (news, recipes, festival updates), FAQ, delivery/returns policy, contact details.

- Smallest distillery in Scotland but historically significant.

## Glossary & Production Terms
- *Campbeltown*: Whisky‑producing region in Scotland.
- *Cask finish*: Additional maturation in a second cask type.
- *First‑fill*: Casks used for the first time, imparting stronger flavours.
- *Peated*: Whisky with peat smoke influence.
- *Dunnage warehouse*: Storage area for casks before bottling.
- *Single malt*: Whisky from one distillery using only malted barley.

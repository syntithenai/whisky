# International Spirits Challenge – Gold Award

Glen Scotia proudly announces that its **15‑Year‑Old, Victoriana, 18‑Year‑Old and 25‑Year‑Old** single malts each received a *Gold* medal at the 2020 International Spirits Challenge. The competition, now in its 25th year, attracts over 1,700 entries from nearly 70 countries and is judged by more than 50 industry professionals during a twelve‑day blind tasting.

---

## Key Points
- **Four gold medals** awarded to Glen Scotia’s flagship single malts.
- The International Spirits Challenge is one of the world’s most respected spirits competitions.
- Judging involved *12 days* of blind tastings by a panel of *50+ experts*.
- The award highlights the quality and consistency across Glen Scotia’s 15‑, 18‑ and 25‑year expressions.

# International Spirits Challenge – Gold Award

Glen Scotia proudly announces that its **15‑Year‑Old, Victoriana, 18‑Year‑Old and 25‑Year‑Old** single malts each earned a *gold medal* at the 2020 International Spirits Challenge. The competition, now in its 25th year, attracts over 1,700 entries from nearly 70 countries and is judged by more than 50 industry professionals across twelve days of blind tastings.

## Key Take‑aways
- **Four gold medals** awarded to Glen Scotia’s flagship single malts. 
- The International Spirits Challenge is a leading global spirits competition with rigorous judging standards.
- The award highlights the quality and consistency of Glen Scotia’s aged whisky range.

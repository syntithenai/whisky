# Plan Your Visit – Glen Scotia

- **Location**: 12 High Street, Campbeltown PA28 6DS, Scotland.
- **Opening hours**: Monday‑Saturday 10 am–5 pm (shop tastings until 3 pm).
- **Visitor centre**: On‑site shop sells whisky, branded merchandise and locally crafted products.
- **Accessibility**: The distillery welcomes visitors with special needs; contact in advance for assistance.
- **Contact**: +44 (0)1586 552 288.

For directions, accessibility details or to book a tour, visit the Glen Scotia website.

**Distillers One of One – Glen Scotia 49‑Year‑Old (42.5% ABV)**

- A one‑off, auction‑only bottling donated to the Distillers 1 of 1 event.
- Derived from the distillery’s oldest cask reserves; the oldest release in its history.
- Includes original artwork by resident artist **Alice Angus** and a hand‑painted walnut display case.
- Matured exclusively in American oak casks, retaining Campbeltown’s subtle maritime character.
- Presented in a 70 cl Glencairn crystal decanter housed in the bespoke walnut case.

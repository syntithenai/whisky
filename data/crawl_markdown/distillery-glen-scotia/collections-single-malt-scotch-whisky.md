# Glen Scotia Single Malt Scotch Whisky
Glen Scotia’s online shop showcases a range of single‑malt expressions from the historic Campbeltown distillery. Highlights include:
- **Campbeltown Malts Festival Edition 2026** – 7‑year, medium peated, finished in Ruby Port casks.
- Core range: 12‑yr, 15‑yr, 18‑yr, 21‑yr, 25‑yr, 32‑yr, and a rare 48‑yr edition (250 bottles).
- Gift sets, personalised engravings, tasting kits, and limited‑edition “Icons of Campbeltown” releases.
- Awards from World Whiskies, IWSC, The Spirits Business, and San Francisco World Spirits Competition.
- Visitor experience: distillery tours, on‑site shop, and master distiller Iain McAlister’s modernised processes.

All products are 70 cl bottles (except the 48‑yr), priced £35–£1,500, with ABV ranging from 40% to 56.8%. The site also promotes responsible drinking and offers shipping to several countries.

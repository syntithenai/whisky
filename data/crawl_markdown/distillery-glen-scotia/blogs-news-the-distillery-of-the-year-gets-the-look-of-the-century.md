# Glen Scotia Distillery of the Year
- **Awards**: 2021 San Francisco World Spirits Competition – *Best in Class* (25‑year‑old); 2022 Scottish Whisky Awards – *Scottish Distillery of the Year*. First distillery to win both in the same year.
- **New Packaging**: Bold gold, deep aqua and emerald green bottles with heritage icons, master‑distiller notes and expression‑specific labels. Designed to reflect Campbeltown’s maritime history.
- **Launch**: Packaging rollout began June 2022; all existing expressions retain quality.
- **Message from CEO Colin Matthews**: Highlights investment, team dedication, and the distillery’s role in Campbeltown’s renaissance.

> *“We are the only distillery in the world to have achieved both the highest accolade at the San Francisco World Spirits Competition and be crowned Scottish Distillery of the Year.”*

# Glen Scotia Wins Double Gold & Platinum at San Francisco World Spirits Competition

Glen Scotia’s **Victoriana** earned a rare *Double Gold* (now *Platinum*) for the third year running, while the **25‑Year‑Old** received its seventh consecutive *Double Gold*. The awards were presented at the 2024 San Francisco World Spirits Competition, the largest spirits contest ever with 5,500 entries.

- **Victoriana** – 54.2% ABV, first & second‑fill bourbon casks, finished in heavily charred bourbon barrels and PX sherry butts; flavours: caramel, berries, toasted notes, spice.
- **25‑Year‑Old** – 48.8% ABV, matured in premium American oak, finished in first‑fill bourbon casks; notes of vanilla oak, sea spray, aromatic spicy fruits.

Both whiskies are available for purchase through Glen Scotia’s online store and worldwide specialist retailers.

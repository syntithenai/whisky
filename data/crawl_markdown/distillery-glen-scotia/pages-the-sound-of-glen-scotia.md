# The Sound of Glen Scotia

Glen Scotia’s “The Sound of Glen Scotia” is a multimedia collaboration between the distillery and musicians Neil Ridley, Jenny Sturgeon and Dean Honer.  The project captured audio from the distillery using binaural microphones and hydrophones, then turned those sounds into two original tracks that were premiered at the Campbeltown Malts Festival 2023.

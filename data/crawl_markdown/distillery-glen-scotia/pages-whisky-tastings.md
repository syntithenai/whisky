**Whisky Tastings – Glen Scotia**

- **Location & Atmosphere:** Victorian‑styled shop in Campbeltown, Scotland.
- **Minimum group size:** 2 visitors; maximum 6.
- **Tasting options (prices include booking fee unless noted):**
  - *Core Range* – 45 min–1 h, £25/person. 4 core whiskies. Available 10 am Tue‑Sat (Nov‑Mar) and 3 pm Tue‑Sat (Apr‑Oct).
  - *Limited Editions* – 45 min–1 h, £35/person. 4 cask‑strength drams from limited releases. 12 noon Mon‑Sat (except Fri). Two identical listings.
  - *Warehouse Selection* – 1 h, £50/person + booking fee. Five drams selected by Master Distiller from Dunnage Warehouse. Available 3 pm Mon‑Sat (Nov‑Mar only).
  - *Premium Tasting* – 45 min–1 h, £75/person + booking fee. Three heavily aged whiskies including Limited Core releases, exclusive single casks and the Double Platinum. 12 pm Fri only.
  - *Whisky & Chocolate Pairing* – 45 min‑1 h, £20/person. 3 core range whiskies paired with FETCHA chocolates.
- **Booking:** Must book in advance; details provided on site.
- **Contact:** 12 High Street, Campbeltown PA28 6DS, Scotland (Loch Lomond Group).

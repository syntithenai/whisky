# Glen Scotia 30‑Year‑Old Spirit Safe Launch

- **Product**: 30‑Year‑Old single malt, non‑chill filtered, 47.3 % ABV, RRP £2,000.
- **Limited edition**: Only 120 bottles worldwide, each with a bespoke bottle label and premium display box featuring original watercolours by artist‑in‑residence Alice Angus.
- **Packaging series**: Four artworks – *First Water*, *Heart of the Run*, *Labour of Years*, *Time and Tide* – part of the new “Spirit Safe” collection.
- **Tasting notes**: Thick floral violet & lavender with sea spray; palate of green apple, silky pear, ginger, dark chocolate; long finish of roasted sugar sweetness.
- **History**: Matured from casks laid down in 1989 after Glen Scotia’s reopening. Only a few casks from that decade survive.
- **Quotes**:
  - *Iain McAlister*: “The liquid is an incredibly distinguished single malt… long, sugary and saline finish.”
  - *Alice Angus*: “My Spirit Safe series celebrates the people, landscape and craft of Glen Scotia.”

This launch marks a new chapter in Glen Scotia’s heritage‑focused portfolio, pairing rare whisky with original art for collectors.

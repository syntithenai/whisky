# The Magical History of the Great Brora Distillery

- **Re‑branding**: From 1969 onward, all casks from the old distillery were renamed *Brora* to satisfy HM Customs & Excise.
- **Peat evolution**: Early Broras (1972–1975) are noticeably peaty; later releases (post‑1976) trend toward lighter peat and a sweeter profile similar to Caol Ila.
- **Notable bottlings**:
  - *Brora 1975* (20 yo, Rare Malts Series) – less peaty than the 1972 version.
  - *Brora 1975/2001* – McGibbons Provenance series.
  - *Brora 1976* – 12‑yr‑old by SMWS (63.6 %) and 21‑yr‑old Rare Malts (92 points).
  - *Brora 1977/2003* – 26‑yr‑old Platinum Series (54.90 %).
- **Production notes**: Brora now uses the same lightly peated malt as Clynelish; earlier years show heavier peat in some releases.

# Allt‑A‑Bhainne Single Malt Scotch Whisky
Allt‑A‑Bhainne is a Speyside distillery founded in 1975 that primarily supplies malt for Chivas Regal blends. The name means “Burn of Milk” in Gaelic. It operates four stills (two wash, two spirit) and has produced a range of age‑statement expressions from 7 to 32 years, often finished in bourbon or sherry casks. The site lists numerous tasting notes and vintage releases with detailed cask information.

## Key facts
- **Location:** Glenrinnes, Banffshire AB55 4DI
- **Founded:** 1975
- **Stills:** 4 (2 wash, 2 spirit)
- **Primary output:** Malt for Chivas Regal blends
- **Notable expressions:** 1991/2004 Connoisseurs Choice (80 pts), 32‑yo 1991/2024 “Lost in Time”, 28‑yo 1975/2004 sherry refill, various bourbon‑finished releases.

## Tasting notes
Typical descriptors include bitter, spicy, grainy, peppery, sweet, malty, porridge‑like, kirsch, grassy. The whisky is often praised for its balance and depth of finish.

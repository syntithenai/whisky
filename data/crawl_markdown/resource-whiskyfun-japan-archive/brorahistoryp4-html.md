- **Barley sourcing** – In 1908 Brora tried four barley origins: Askaig, Inverbrora, Ross‑shire and a generic “Scotch”. By April 1911 they switched to Danubian barley.
- **Yeast** – Only Hamlyn’s yeast was used throughout the period.
- **Ownership** – Around 1911 the company was owned by James Ainslie, Thomas Ainslie and John Risk (who held half of the shares).

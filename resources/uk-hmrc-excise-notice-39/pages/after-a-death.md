# What to do after someone dies: Register the death

This GOV.UK guide explains the steps you must take once a person has died:
1. **Confirm the death** with a medical examiner.
2. **Register the death** within 5 days (8 in Scotland).
3. Receive a burial/cremation certificate and give it to your funeral director.
4. Use the *Tell Us Once* service or contact relevant organisations yourself.
5. Arrange the funeral, including financial help if needed.
6. Check eligibility for bereavement benefits.
7. Manage tax, pensions and benefits changes.
8. Value the estate and determine Inheritance Tax liability.
9. Apply for probate if required.
10. Deal with debts, taxes and distribute the estate.

The guide also offers links to support services, visa rules, and a printable version.

# Alcoholic Products Technical Guide

This guide, published by HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC), explains the legal framework for producing, storing, and accounting for duty on alcoholic products in the UK. It covers:
- Definitions and classifications of alcoholic products.
- Production requirements and approval processes.
- Duty calculation, payment, and relief schemes.
- Record‑keeping, financial security, and due diligence.
- Import/export rules, removal from approved premises, and special cases (diplomats, HM ships).
- Glossary of key terms used throughout the guide.

The document is intended for commercial producers, packagers, importers, and exporters.

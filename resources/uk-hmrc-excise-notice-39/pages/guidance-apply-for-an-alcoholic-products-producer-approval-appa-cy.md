# Alcoholic Products Producer Approval (APPA) Guidance

This GOV.UK page explains how to apply for, manage, and cancel an **Alcoholic Products Producer Approval** in the UK. It outlines:
- When you must apply (at least 45 working days before production starts).
- What information is required: business details, premises plans, product categories, import/export data, alcohol volume estimates, etc.
- The online application process and alternative paper‑form options for those unable to use computers.
- How HMRC will review applications, including background checks, site visits, and record verification.
- Rights to withdraw or amend an approval, appeal decisions, and consequences of non‑compliance (penalties, fines).
- Recent updates clarifying offences and providing new forms.

The page is available in Welsh with links to English equivalents and related resources such as the Alcohol Duty Online Service and technical guidance for alcoholic products.

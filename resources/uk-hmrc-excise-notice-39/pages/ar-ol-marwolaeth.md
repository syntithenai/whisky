# Registering a Death

This GOV.UK guide explains the steps you must take after someone dies in Wales. It covers:
- The need for a medical examiner to confirm death.
- How to contact the registrar’s office and register the death within 5 days (8 days in Scotland).
- What documents you will receive (a green form) and how to use it with funeral directors or crematoria.
- Additional resources available in English.

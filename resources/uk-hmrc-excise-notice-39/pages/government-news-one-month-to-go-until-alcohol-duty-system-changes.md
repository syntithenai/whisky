# One month to go until Alcohol Duty system changes

The UK government announced that on **1 August 2023** the Alcohol Duty system will be simplified to a single tax based on alcohol by volume (ABV). The change replaces four separate taxes for beer, cider, spirits and wine. Key points:
- Six standardised duty bands apply across all alcoholic products.
- Small producers (≤4,500 hl/year) qualify for reduced rates.
- Draught Relief will lower duty on qualifying draught drinks by 9.2 % (beer/cider) or 23 % (wine‑based/spirits). 
- Low‑strength drinks (<3.5 % ABV) receive a new lower rate to encourage innovation and responsible drinking.
- Temporary wine arrangements treat 11.5–14.5 % ABV wines as 12.5 % for duty purposes until **1 February 2025**.

The reform aims to make the system fairer, support small businesses and pubs, and align with public‑health objectives.

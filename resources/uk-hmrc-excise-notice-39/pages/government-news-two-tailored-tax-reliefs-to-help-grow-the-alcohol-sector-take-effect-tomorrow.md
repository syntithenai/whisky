# Two tailored tax reliefs to help grow the alcohol sector take effect tomorrow

- **Effective date:** 1 February 2025.
- **Key measures:**
  - *Draught relief* – cuts duty on draught products below 8.5 % ABV by 1p per pint (≈1.7 % in cash terms).
  - *Small Producer Relief (SPR)* – increases the duty discount for small breweries and cider makers, up to **91.5 %** on the first five hectolitres of pure alcohol.
- **Economic impact:** Combined relief worth **£385 million** aimed at boosting innovation, growth and market access for craft brewers and distilleries.
- **Additional actions:** Future consultation on guest‑beer access; reduction of Spirit Drinks Verification Scheme fees; removal of mandatory duty stamps for spirits from 1 May 2025.

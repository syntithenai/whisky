# State Pension Forecast Service

The GOV.UK **State Pension forecast** tool helps users estimate how much state pension they could receive, when they can claim it, and ways to increase the amount (e.g., by delaying retirement or making voluntary National Insurance contributions). It is part of the *Plan your retirement income* guidance.

Key points:
- Requires sign‑in; may need identity verification.
- Not available for those already receiving a pension or who have deferred claiming it.
- Alternative methods: HMRC app, BR19 form, phone call to Future Pension Centre.
- Provides information on tax implications, workplace/personal pensions, and other financial support.

The page also lists related services such as bus passes, low‑income help, and retirement planning advice.

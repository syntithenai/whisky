# Kanosuke Whisky – Exclusive & Limited Bottles

Kanosuke Whisky is a Japanese distillery founded in 2017 by Komasa Jyozo Co., a historic Kagoshima shōchū producer. The distillery, located on Fukiagehama Beach, uses three uniquely shaped copper pot stills to craft single‑malt and blended whiskies that reflect the local terroir. Its flagship **Kanosuke Single Malt (2021)** won Gold at the 2023 San Francisco World Spirits Competition, while the **Double Distillery blend** earned Gold at the 2025 World Whiskies Awards.

The shop page lists 25 products, including limited‑edition single‑cask releases and blends. Prices range from $199 to $999, with many items currently sold out.

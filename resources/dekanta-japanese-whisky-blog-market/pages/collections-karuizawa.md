## Distillery facts
- Karuizawa was founded near Mount Asama, Japan.
- First released Japan’s first 100 % domestic malt whisky in 1976.
- Used small pot stills and sherry casks; ivy‑covered warehouse maintained stable temperature/humidity.
- Became a subsidiary of Kirin Holdings by the mid‑2000s.
- Production ceased in 2011; equipment dismantled by 2012.
- Revived by Shigeru Totsuka with Yoshiyuk Nakazato, replicating original equipment.

# Burgers & Bourbon Pairing Guide

- **Author**: Brian Freedman (Whisky Advocate)
- **Chef collaborator**: David Gross of The Standard
- **Core idea**: Sweetness and spice in bourbon complement the savory, smoky flavors of a burger.
- **Structure**:
  - *Cocktail of Choice*: Apple Upgrade, Dark Horse, Summer Peach Julep
  - *Appetizer*: Roasted Onion Dip (with Evan Williams or Maker’s Mark)
  - *Entrée*: Bourbon Burger with short‑rib/hanger blend, brioche buns, maple‑syrup bacon, smoky bourbon aioli
  - *Sides*: German Potato Salad, Bourbon Baked Beans, Baby Gem Salad with Maple Cider Vinaigrette, Grilled Corn on the Cob
  - *Dessert*: Peach Crisp (with bourbon) and Summer Peach Julep
- **Recommended bourbons**: Widow Jane 10‑yr, Woodford Reserve, Lucky Thirteen, etc.
- **Key flavor notes**: smokiness, sweetness, citrus, spice, vanilla, caramel.

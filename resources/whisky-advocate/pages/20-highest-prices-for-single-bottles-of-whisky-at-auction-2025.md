# 2025's Highest Hammer Prices for Single Bottles of Whisky

The article lists the top 20 single‑bottle whisky hammer prices in 2025, totaling **$3.69 million**—a 32 % increase over 2024. The list includes 14 Scotch, 3 Japanese and 3 American whiskies, with many one‑of‑one releases from distilleries and large‑format bottles.

Key highlights:
- **Glenlivet SPIRA 1965 (60 yr)** – highest hammer price: **$693,682**.
- **Macallan** saw a sharp decline in prices; only four entries vs. thirteen in 2024.
- **Yamazaki** and **Bowmore ARC‑54** each secured top spots.
- The Distillers One‑of‑One auction (October) dominated the list with seven of the 20 highest prices.

The article also notes broader market trends: a positive momentum for 2025, challenges from tariffs and changing consumer habits, and a potential shift in the Macallan segment for 2026.

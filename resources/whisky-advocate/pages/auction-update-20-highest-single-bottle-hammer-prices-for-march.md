# March 2025 Whisky Auction Results

- The top‑20 single‑bottle hammer prices for March 2025 total **$1,093,156**, an 18 % rise over Q1 2024.
- Three bottles sold above $100k: *Hanyu Ichiro’s Malt Card Series* (1988), *Macallan Sir Peter Blake Anecdotes of Ages* (1967) and *Old Rip Van Winkle “Van Winkle Selection”* (2000‑s). 
- The highest hammer price was **$132,910** for a 1988 Hanyu bottle at Whisky Auctioneer.
- Hong Kong auctions were weak; many lots sold at starting bid or remained unsold. 
- In Scotland, 16 of the 20 high‑value bottles failed to meet reserve prices.

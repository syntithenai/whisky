# Export Restrictions to Russia (April 2022)

- **General rule** – Exports of banknotes, government money bills and precious metals to the Russian Federation require permission from Japan’s Minister of Finance.
- **Personal effects** of departing passengers and crew are covered by this rule.
- **Exceptions** allow certain payments without permission:
  - Payments for stays in Russia by Japanese residents.
  - Payments up to ¥100 000 for essential goods (food, clothing, pharmaceuticals), medical services, or other humane reasons.
- **Precious metals definition** includes gold bullion, alloy bullion, non‑circulating gold coins and any object whose principal material is gold with at least half the weight/price in gold.

# Barrels – World Cooperage
World Cooperage offers a portfolio of four barrel series designed for different aging needs:
- **Traditional Series** – classic barrels with five toast levels.
- **Profile Series** – over 100 distinct flavor profiles to choose from.
- **Infrared Series** – unique toasting profiles that emphasize texture and flavor.
- **Fusion Series** – combine up to three oak species in a single barrel.

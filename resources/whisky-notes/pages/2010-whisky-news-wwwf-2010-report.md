# Wild West Whisky Fest 2010 Report

Ruben reviews the **Wild West Whisky Fest (WWWF)** held in Gent, Belgium. The festival mixed classic and new bottlings, offering a wide range of whiskies without long queues or awkward conversations with distillers.

## Highlights
- **Port Ellen 26‑yo** – 54.6% ABV, sherried, sweet smoke, meaty notes, chocolate, dry finish (€200).
- **Dallas Dhu 28‑yo** – 55.8% ABV, oily with coal smoke and orange/Grand Marnier hints (€160).
- **GlenDronach 8‑yo** – 45.4% ABV, unsherried, fruity and fresh.
- **Highland Park 13‑yo** – 50% ABV, candied honeyed profile with wine gums.
- **Laphroaig 13‑yo** – 57.3% ABV, smoked ham nose, explosive palate (€60).

Ruben also notes the presence of new Arran releases, Feis Ile bottlings, and a Longmorn 1972/2010 from The Whisky Agency.

## Future Plans
He plans to taste the newly finished GlenDronach series (14‑yo Sauternes, 15‑yo Moscatel, etc.) and will share further notes soon.

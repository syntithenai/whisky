# Whiskyfestival winners

This page announces the winners of the 2010 Whisky Festival in Gent and thanks participants. It lists a few recipients (Jean‑Pierre, Veerle, Rowin, Bert, Arnaut) who will receive an email soon, mentions that Connemara samples are being sent to Pavlos (Greece), André (France), and three Belgians: Walter, Hans, Geert. The author congratulates the winners and invites readers to see the event.

The page also contains a short comment section with three replies from users Pavlos, André, and Walter expressing gratitude for winning samples.

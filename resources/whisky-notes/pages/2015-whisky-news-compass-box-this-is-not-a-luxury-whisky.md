# Compass Box “This is not a luxury whisky”

Compass Box has released a new blended Scotch called **“This is not a luxury whisky.”** The blend draws inspiration from René Magritte’s *Ceci n’est pas une pipe* and aims to challenge the trend of super‑premium, status‑symbol whiskies. It uses four parcels: 19‑year‑old Glen Ord, 40‑year‑old Girvan & Strathclyde grains, and a portion of 30‑year‑old Caol Ila. Bottled at 53.1 % ABV, it will be limited to about 5 000 bottles in October with an expected price around €200.

A companion limited edition, **Compass Box Flaming Heart**, is also slated for release (≈ €140) and contains 30‑year‑old Caol Ila, 20‑year‑old Clynelish and other Highland malts.

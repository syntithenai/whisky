# World Whisky Awards 2009

- **Best whisky liqueur**: Wild Turkey American Honey
- **Best grain whisky**: Compass Box Hedonism
- **Best new release**: Highland Park 40 yr old
- **Best blended malt**: Taketsuru 21 yr old
- **Best American whisky**: Thomas H. Handy Sazerac
- **Best single‑malt whisky**: Highland Park 21 yr old (Scotland)
- Other notable winners: Tyrconnell (Irish single‑cask), Lagavulin 16 yr (Islay peated), Glenlivet Archive 21 yr (Speyside), Mackmyra First Edition (Other single‑malt).

The article notes that the crown jewels for best single malt returned to Scotland after a Japanese win in 2008, and highlights Highland Park’s 21‑year‑old as a prize only available through travel retail.

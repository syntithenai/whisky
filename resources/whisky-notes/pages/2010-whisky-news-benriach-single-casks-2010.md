# BenRiach Single Casks 2010

- **Release**: Ten cask‑strength single casks from 1976–1993, bottled July 2010.
- **Casks**: 1976 #8795 (53.2% bourbon hogshead), 1977 #1033 (52.2% Pedro Ximénez finish), 1978 #4417 (50.4% Tokaji finish), 1979 #7511 (47.9% Bourbon Barrel), 1980 #2532 (51.1% Virgin American Oak finish), 1981 #2589 (51.6% bourbon barrel), 1984 #493 (54.1% bourbon hogshead), 1984 #4052 (51.7% peated/tawny Port Finish), 1991 #4389 (54.9% Virgin American Oak Finish), 1993 #7420 (56.37% Gaja Barolo Finish).
- **Presentation**: Individually numbered, natural colour, non‑chill‑filtered, in a gift tube.
- **Context**: Celebrates the sixth anniversary of BenRiach’s first bottling under new independent ownership; Master Distiller Billy Walker selected the casks.

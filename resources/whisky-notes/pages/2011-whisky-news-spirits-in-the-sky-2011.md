# Spirits in the Sky 2011

WhiskyNotes covered the 5th edition of the **Spirits in the Sky** festival held in Leuven on 7 November 2011. The event, organised by Belgian importer *The Nectar*, showcased a wide range of new releases and limited editions from its portfolio.

## Highlights
- **New releases tasted**: Strathmill 1974 Daily Dram, Miyagikyo 1988 (La Maison du Whisky), Bruichladdich 5 yr vatting by Jim McEwan, Glenallachie 38 yr, Glen Grant 1972 (Whisky Agency Private Stock), Cardhu 27 yr, Port Ellen 27 yr.
- **Oldies**: Talisker 1957 and Ardbeg 18 yr 1974.
- **Disappointments**: GlenDronach 21 yr Parliament, Bruichladdich 10 yr "The Laddie Ten", official Brora 32 yr.
- **Whisky Agency masterclass**: Blind tasting of five unbottled casks (Bowmore 1999, Glen Scotia 1992, Glen Elgin 1984, Glen Grant 1972, Tomatin 1976). The committee chose the Glen Grant 1972 for a future special edition.

The article ends with comments from readers and a brief note that the festival will continue in Leuven.

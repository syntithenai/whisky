# Coming up: Irish Whiskey
Irish single malts from independent bottlers are selling like hotcakes. The author highlights old 1988‑89 expressions, the wonderful (peated) 1991s and several younger examples distilled early 2000s, all of which range from good to outstanding. Irish distilleries are achieving excellent results and growing faster than their Scottish neighbours.

The post promises a few interesting drams from different categories (blends, malts, pot still) over the next couple of days.

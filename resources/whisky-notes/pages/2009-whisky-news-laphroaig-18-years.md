# Laphroaig 18‑Year Announcement
- The 15‑year old Laphroaig is being replaced by an 18‑year version.
- New bottling will be at **48 % ABV** (same as the Quarter Cask).
- Price expected to stay roughly unchanged despite extra ageing.
- Official tasting notes highlight sweet toffee, vanilla, honey, seaweed, salt, peat and a long, oily finish.

## Tasting Notes
| Aspect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Nose | Sweet toffee, faint spice, delicate phenols, fruit; water adds seaweed & salt but not overpowering. |
| Body | Balanced, warming smoke, floral scents, oaky nuttiness, chocolate smoothness with peat and heather hints. |
| Finish | Full‑bodied, long, luxurious oily smoothness. |

## Context
- The 15‑year old was controversial for its mellow style; the new 18‑year aims to return to a bolder profile.
- No price change is unusual given the extra three years and current market trends.

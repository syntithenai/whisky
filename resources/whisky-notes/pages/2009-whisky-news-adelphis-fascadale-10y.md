# Adelphi Fascadale 10y
Adelphi, an independent bottler known for quality releases such as the *Inchgower 26y* and *Breath of Islay*, launched a limited‑run **Fascadale 10 yr** (3797 bottles). The whisky is a Talisker blend: five 1993 casks and five 1998 casks, finished at 46 % ABV. Charles McLean’s tasting notes describe tobacco pouch, Highland toffee, smoked bacon, distant peat fires, salty rock pools, creamy texture and a chilli pepper finish.

*Estimated price:* €45.

> *Spoiler:* It is a Talisker.

# Malt Maniacs Awards – WhiskyNotes Review

WhiskyNotes reports on the 2009 Malt Maniacs Awards, highlighting the distribution of gold, silver and bronze medals across a range of cask‑selected whiskies. The overall winner was **Glen Grant 36 yo (1972)** with a score of 91/100. Gold medals were dominated by sherry‑bottled whiskies, including three Japanese releases, three old Speysiders and one Islay malt. Notable silver winners include **Ardbeg Corryvreckan**, **Amrut Fusion**, **Glengoyne 1973** (Malts of Scotland) and **Port Ellen PE1**.

The article also notes the strong performance of independent bottlers such as La Maison du Whisky, which secured three golds, six silvers and three bronzes. Comments from readers discuss specific cask entries and the influence of the awards on the market.

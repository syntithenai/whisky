# Jura Paps Collection

In June 2009 Jura Distillery released a **limited edition of three 15‑year‑old single malts** inspired by the island’s iconic Paps mountains. Each spirit is finished in a different wine cask:
- **Beinn An Oir – Mountain of Gold** (Pinot Noir finish)
- **Beinn A’Chaolais – Mountain of Sound** (Cabernet Sauvignon finish)
- **Beinn Shiantaidh – The Sacred Mountain** (Barolo finish)

Only **1,366 bottles** were produced, all signed by Master Distiller *Willie Cochrane*. Purchasing the full set costs **€300** and comes in a special case that holds the three individual boxes.

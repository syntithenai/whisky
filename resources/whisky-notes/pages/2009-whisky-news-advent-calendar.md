# Advent Calendar Review

WhiskyNotes author Ruben reviews a 24‑sample advent calendar curated by Swedish collector Magnus Fagerström. The set contains 2 cl samples from rare or unusual bottles, valued at roughly €8 000. The tasting is blind; each day a numbered sample is revealed on Fagerström’s website. Ruben notes that he often cannot identify the distillery but enjoys testing his knowledge.

The post invites readers to check out tasting notes as they become available and thanks Magnus for the selection.

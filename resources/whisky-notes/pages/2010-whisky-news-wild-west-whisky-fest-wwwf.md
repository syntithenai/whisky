# Wild West Whisky Fest (WWWF)

- The festival is one of Belgium’s newest whisky events.
- First edition in 2009 was small but attracted all major distributors and brands.
- A notable blind‑tasting experiment compared the same whisky with/without caramel or chill‑filtration.
- The second edition ran on **5–6 June 2010** at Kortrijk Xpo (1–6 pm).
- Attendees could sample new releases from *Thosop*, *The Nectar*, *Malts of Scotland* and others, plus rare antiquities such as Lindores & Max Righi.

> “Wild West Whisky Fest is probably the youngest whisky festival in Belgium, but after its first edition it already has a solid reputation.” – WhiskyNotes (4 June 2010)

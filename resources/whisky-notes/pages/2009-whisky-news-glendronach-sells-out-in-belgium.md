# GlenDronach sells out in Belgium
Three weeks after launching its new 12‑year, 15‑year and 18‑year sherry‑matured malts, Glen Dronach sold the first 3,000‑bottle consignment in Belgium. The Belgian importer *The Nectar*, represented by Mario Grooteklaes, noted that the brand’s established reputation—especially for its classic 15‑year‑old—and positive publicity from Malt Maniacs and online forums helped secure rapid distribution to about one hundred specialised liquor stores across Belgium within a week.

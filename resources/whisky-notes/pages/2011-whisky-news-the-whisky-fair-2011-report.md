# The Whisky Fair 2011 Report

WhiskyNotes reviewer Ruben reports that the 2011 Whisky Fair in Germany was the best whisky festival he has attended. He highlights a wide range of new and old bottlings, noting the absence of supermarket whiskies and the high quality of releases from the Whisky Agency, The Nectar, and Daily Dram. Key samples include:

- **Glen Keith 40 yo (1970)** – 47.9 % ABV, cask #6041.
- **Strathisla 1970** – new release.
- **Glenrothes 41 yo (1968)** – 43 % ABV, Duncan Taylor Peerless, cask #9974.
- **Strathisla 48 yo (1963)** – 51.8 % ABV, first‑fill sherry cask #576.
- **Longmorn 44 yo (1966)** – 43.1 % ABV, refill sherry cask #281.
- **Tomatin 45 yo (1966)** – 46.1 % ABV, Whisky Agency / The Nectar.
- **Inchgower 28 yo (1982)** – 56.6 % ABV, Whisky‑Doris cask #6971.

He also mentions older bottles such as Longmorn 1969/1999, Saint Magdalene 1965, a 1920s blend from Berry Bros., and Moon Import series. The Liquid Sun stand (a sister of The Whisky Agency) is noted for potential future Belgian releases. Glenfarclas 175th Anniversary and Glenfiddich Snow Phoenix bottles were still available.

Ruben concludes that the event was larger than expected, with a strong focus on boutique bottlers rather than mass‑market products.

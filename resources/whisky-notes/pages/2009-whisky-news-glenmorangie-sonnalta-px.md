# Glenmorangie Sonnalta PX

- **Release**: First in Glenmorangie's “private expressions” series.
- **Finish**: Pedro Ximénez sherry casks – rich raisin, syrup notes.
- **Profile**: Fruity (raisins, coconut, banana, apple), light, aperitif‑style whisky.
- **Availability**: Travel retail only, price ~€70.
- **Context**: Review posted 31 Jan 2009 by Ruben on WhiskyNotes; future reviews of other Glenmorangie expressions were announced.

---

## Key facts
- Pedro Ximénez casks used for finishing.
- Described as “darkest, richest type of sherry” with intense raisin and syrup notes.
- Fruity descriptors: raisins, coconut, banana, apple.
- Light, aperitif‑like character.
- Travel retail price around €70.

## Review excerpt
> *“It’s said to be fruity (raisins, coconut, banana, apple) and light, sort of an aperitif whisky.”*

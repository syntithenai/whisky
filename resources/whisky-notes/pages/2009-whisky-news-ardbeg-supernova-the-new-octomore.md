# Ardbeg Supernova – the new Octomore
Ardbeg announced a peat‑heavy release called **Supernova** (≈100 ppm phenols). The article compares it to Bruichladdich’s Octomore, noting that Octomore reached 131 ppm and was described as a “liquid ash tray.” Official tasting notes for Supernova highlight deep earthy peat oils, smoky coal tar, rolling tobacco, camomile, cedar, heather bloom, hot sizzling gristiness, pepper, chilli and chocolate. The author expresses excitement to taste both Supernova and Octomore but remarks that the bottles are hard to obtain.

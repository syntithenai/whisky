# New Daily Drams – High Dark Plan / Auk’s Choir / Ciao All

WhiskyNotes announced three new Daily Dram releases from their Belgian bottler Nectar:
- **High Dark Plan** – Highland Park 10 yo (1998, 46% ABV) ~ €45
- **Auk’s Choir** – Auchroisk 34 yo (1975, 41.3% ABV, 97 bottles) ~ €135
- **Ciao All** – Caol Ila 12 yo (1996, 46% ABV) ~ €50

The author notes the releases will arrive in a few days and will be reviewed shortly. Samples are already available at Luc’s www.whiskysamples.eu.

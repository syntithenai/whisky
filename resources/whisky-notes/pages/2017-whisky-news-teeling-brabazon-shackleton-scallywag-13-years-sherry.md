# Teeling Brabazon Series – Sherry‑Cask 13 Year Old

- **Teeling Brabazon** is the first release of a new limited‑edition single‑malt series from Teeling Whiskey, named after the historic Brabazon family of Dublin.
- The inaugural expression is a sherry‑cask matured whisky, aged 13 years and bottled at 49.5 % ABV.
- Limited to **12 500 bottles**, it retails for roughly **€80**.
- It is currently available at the distillery and will soon hit retail stores.

The article also briefly mentions:
- A new *Shackleton* blended malt by Whyte & Mackay, a 40 % duty‑free spirit supporting Antarctic heritage.
- Douglas Laing’s **Scallywag 13 Year Old Sherry**, the first age‑stated version of the Scallywag line, finished exclusively in sherry butts.

These releases highlight a trend toward fortified‑wine cask finishes and limited‑edition offerings across Irish and Scotch whisky producers.

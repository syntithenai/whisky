# Wemyss Single Cask Releases (2016)

- Independent bottler **Wemyss Malts** announced six single‑cask releases.
- Casks range from 13 to 25 years old, sourced from various Scottish distilleries.
- Bottles are limited to a few hundred per cask and priced between £55–£110.
- Availability: selected retailers in the UK, EU, and key Asian markets.

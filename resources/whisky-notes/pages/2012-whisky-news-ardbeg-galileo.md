# Ardbeg Galileo (1999) – 12‑13 yr, 49% ABV

- **Vintage**: 1999
- **Age**: 12–13 years
- **ABV**: 49%
- **Cask type**: First‑fill ex‑bourbon casks finished in a Marsala‑enhanced style (Sicilian Marsala wine)
- **Release date**: September 2012 (confirmed by LVMH Netherlands)
- **Price**: ~€90 (reported €79.55 in the US)
- **Tasting notes**:
  - *Nose*: spicy toffee, tropical fruits, puff of soot
  - *Palate*: salty yet sweet with cream and a burst of smoking apricots
  - *Finish*: long, lingering, serene

The whisky commemorates Ardbeg’s first space experiment.

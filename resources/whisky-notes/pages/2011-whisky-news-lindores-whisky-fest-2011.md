# Lindores Whisky Fest 2011

WhiskyNotes reviewer Ruben attended the invitation‑only Lindores Whisky Fest in October 2011, where he sampled around 100 bottles (≈50 donated + ≈50 from the Lindores vaults). The event featured high‑quality, often legendary single‑malt and other spirit bottlings. Highlights included:

- **Bowmore 31 yo** – fruity, slightly exotic.
- **Springbank 25 yo** – one of the best Springbanks tasted.
- **Glenfarclas 21 yo** – highly complex.
- **BenRiach 34 yo (1976/2011)** – near‑top 1976s.
- **Caperdonich 39 yo (1972/2011)** – one of the best 1972s.
- **Glenugie 31 yo** – very fruity, oriental.
- **Ben Nevis 40 yo (1963/2004)** – superb with bourbon notes.
- **Highland Park 30 yo (1956/1986)** – smoky, fruity, chocolaty.

Other notable non‑single‑malt offerings included a 1924 Johnnie Walker Red Label rotation and various aged wines and rums. The reviewer praised the venue’s spacious lounge and the quality of the bottles.

# The Whisky Exchange Whisky Show (London) – 7‑8 Oct 2011

WhiskyNotes previewed the upcoming show, highlighting new bottlings and rare releases that would be on display:
- **Kilchoman**: *Elements of Islay Kh1* (first independent bottling), plus Bn1, Lp3, Cl2, Br2, Pe5.
- **Lochside 1964 Single Blend** – a 46‑year‑old malt & grain blend vatted immediately after distillation.
- **Bowmore 1993 Masterpiece** – noted for being peatier than usual.
- Other new releases: Glen Garioch 1971, Clynelish 1972/1982, Ardmore 1992, Aberlour 1990, Caol Ila 1999, Macallan 1990, plus offerings from Number One Drinks (Karuizawa 1981), Highland Park, Longrow, Buffalo Trace, Amrut, etc.

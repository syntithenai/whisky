# The other Highland Park 21

Highland Park announced that the current 21‑year‑old will be bottled at a lower strength of **40% ABV** instead of the previous high‑strength version (around 47.5%). The change is attributed to limited availability of high‑strength casks and the whisky’s popularity, though no concrete evidence has been provided. No price reduction was announced, raising concerns that the reduced volume might effectively be a disguised price increase.

---

## Key Points
- **Strength change**: 47.5% → 40% ABV.
- Reason cited: limited high‑strength cask supply and popularity.
- No proof of changes to the award‑winning whisky.
- No announced price adjustment.
- Potential impact on value perception.

# Glenmorangie Tarlogan
The Glenmorangie will soon launch **Tarlogan**, a new NAS (non‑age statement) expression for travel retail. It is the third release in the *Travel Retail Legends* series after Duthac and Tayne. The name comes from a Pictish king who gave his name to the woodland around the distillery’s water source.

- **Cask types**: ex‑bourbon and virgin oak casks
- **ABV**: 43 %
- **Initial availability**: World Duty Free UK airports, then other duty‑free locations and eventually normal retail

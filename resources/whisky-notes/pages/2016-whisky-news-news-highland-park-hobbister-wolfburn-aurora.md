# Highland Park Hobbister & Wolfburn Aurora

- **Highland Park** released a limited edition *Hobbister* (51.4 % ABV, 1 200 bottles) featuring peat from the Hobbister Moor.
- **Wolfburn** announced two new expressions: *Aurora* (80 % quarter‑casks + 20 % first‑fill sherry hogsheads) and a relaunch of *Northland* (quarter‑cask only).

Both releases are highlighted in the July 2016 WhiskyNotes news roundup.

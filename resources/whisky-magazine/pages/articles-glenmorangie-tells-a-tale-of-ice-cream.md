# Glenmorangie A Tale of Ice Cream

Glenmorangie has launched a new edition in its “A Tale Of…” series, inspired by ice‑cream parlour flavours. The whisky is aged in high‑vanillin new oak casks (toasted for extra vanilla) and blended with bourbon‑cask‑aged single malt. It carries notes of peach melba, lemon sorbet, coconut flakes and rich vanilla, and comes at 46 % ABV with an RRP of £76.

The distillery also released a cocktail menu and limited‑edition sweaters in partnership with knitwear designer Donna Wilson.

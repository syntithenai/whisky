# "An exciting future" for Diageo with new luxury division
Diageo has launched the **Diageo Luxury Group (DLG)**, a global division that will consolidate and grow its premium spirits portfolio. The group will manage 15 brand homes, distillery visitor experiences (e.g., Johnnie Walker Princes Street), and luxury whisky stocks from closed distilleries such as Port Ellen and Brora. DLG is led by former Johnnie Walker global brand director **Julie Bramham**.

The launch coincides with Diageo’s reopening of the historic Islay distillery **Port Ellen**, which had been shut in 1983. The company also announced the **Diageo Luxury Company (DLC)**, focused on building luxury brands locally for its home market.

Bramham emphasised that bringing together heritage and forward‑thinking strategy positions Diageo to deliver on luxury experiences and accelerate growth across its portfolio.

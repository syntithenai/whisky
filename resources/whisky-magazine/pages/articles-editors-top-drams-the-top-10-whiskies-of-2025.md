# Editor’s Top Drams: The Top 10 Whiskies of 2025

Whisky Magazine’s editor Bradley Weir ranks the ten best‑scoring whiskies he tasted across seven issues in 2025. Each entry lists the whisky, issue number, score, a brief tasting note and purchase links (The Whisky Exchange or Master of Malt). The list is dominated by Scotch but includes an English single‑malt, a Japanese expression and several new distillery releases.

## Honorable Mentions

* Kinloch Anderson – The Kiltmaker Series (Pleated to Sett)

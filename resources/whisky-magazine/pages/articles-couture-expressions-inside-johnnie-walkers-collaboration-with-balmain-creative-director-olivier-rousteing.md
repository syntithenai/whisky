# Couture Expressions: Johnnie Walker & Balmain Collaboration

Johnnie Walker’s *Couture Expressions* is a limited‑edition series of four blends—spring, summer, autumn and winter—created by master blender Dr Emma Walker in partnership with Balmain creative director Olivier Rousteing. The project marries whisky making with haute couture: each blend is housed in a Baccarat decanter finished in season‑specific metals (rose gold, silver, gold, black) and wrapped in drapé‑style fabric.

The collaboration was born from the Johnnie Walker Vault platform, which curates rare whiskies for bespoke projects. Only 25 bottles of each seasonal blend were produced at a price of £16 000 per bottle. The blends draw on a core of rare casks from Brora, Port Ellen and Port Dundas, with supporting whiskies such as Cragganmore, Cardhu Wine‑Cask Finish, Clynelish and Benrinnes to evoke the seasonal themes.

Rousteing’s personal narrative—his heritage, his 2020 burn accident and his creative ethos—shaped the character of each blend. The launch event featured holographic presentations set to music that matched each season’s mood.

The partnership highlights how whisky can be a storytelling medium, blending heritage with contemporary luxury and reaching new audiences through fashion‑centric channels.

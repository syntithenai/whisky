# English Whisky Guild (EWG)

The article announces the formation of the **English Whisky Guild**, a coalition of 15 founding distilleries aimed at promoting and protecting the quality of English whisky. It highlights projected growth figures, the submission of a Geographical Indicator to DEFRA, and quotes from key members about independence, innovation, and standards.

## Key Points
- **Founding members**: Ad Gefrin, Adnams Southwold, Bimber Distillery, Cooper King Distillery, Cooper Rivet Distillery, Cotswolds Distillery, East London Liquor Co., Henstone Distillery, Spirit of Yorkshire Distillery, The English Whisky Co., The Lakes Distillery, The Oxford Artisan Distillery, Wharf Distillery, White Peak Distillery, Whittaker’s Distillery.
- **Growth projections**: 189 % increase in spirit production and 418 % rise in bottle sales by 2023 compared to 2019.
- **GI application**: Submission to DEFRA to legally define “English whisky” and set standards.
- **Quotes**: Andrew Nelstrop (Chairman) stresses independence, innovation, and quality; Abbie Neilson (Co‑Founder, Cooper King Distillery) notes unprecedented growth and flavour diversity.

# National Spirits Sector Position Paper on CDS Expansion and National Harmonisation

The Australian Spirits sector outlines its support for a nationally harmonised Container Deposit Scheme (CDS) while highlighting the unique challenges of small‑scale, high‑value spirits producers. Key points include:
- A single national portal, registration, labelling and fee structure.
- Simplified reporting with quarterly/monthly/annual options and a single invoice.
- Exemptions for small runs (<10 000 bottles) and one‑off specialist releases.
- Education‑first compliance and multi‑year grace periods for SMEs.
- National alignment of barcode/QR requirements, labelling transition dates, and a 20 000‑container free threshold per state.

The paper calls on EPA and Ministers to adopt these measures to reduce administrative burden while maintaining environmental outcomes.

# 1 August 2023 Resource Handbook

The Australian Distillers Association (ADA) and Spirits & Cocktails Australia released a handbook on **1 Aug 2023** to campaign for freezing the spirits excise tax at its current rate of **$100.05 per litre of alcohol**. The document outlines:
- The recent increase, its impact on distilleries, regional communities and the broader economy.
- Talking points, media engagement tips, infographics and templates for contacting MPs.
- Calls to action: write to MPs, share infographics, engage local media, and invite MPs to distillery visits.

The handbook stresses that Australia has the third‑highest spirits tax worldwide and urges policymakers to reconsider a regime introduced 40 years ago.

---

**Key facts**:
- Spirits excise will exceed $100/litre on 1 Aug 2023.
- The tax is more than double beer’s and four times wine’s.
- Two‑thirds of Australian distilleries are regional; the tax disproportionately affects these areas.
- The industry contributes ~53,000 jobs and $11.6 billion to the economy but pays ~$5 billion in excise annually.

---

The handbook provides practical resources (templates, infographics, media tips) for distillers to lobby the federal government and raise public awareness.

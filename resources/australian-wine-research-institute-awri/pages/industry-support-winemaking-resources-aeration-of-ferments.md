# Aeration of Ferments

The Australian Wine Research Institute (AWRI) presents research‑based evidence that adding oxygen to wine fermentations can improve both red and white wines. Key benefits include:
- **Red fermentations**: reduced reductive flavours, enhanced red‑fruit character, improved mouthfeel and lower astringency.
- **White fermentations**: shortened fermentation times in challenging batches.

AWRI offers practical resources—fact sheets, webinars, workshops, case studies and podcast episodes—to help winemakers implement aeration safely and effectively. The page also lists relevant scientific references and acknowledges funding from Wine Australia and the Australian Government.

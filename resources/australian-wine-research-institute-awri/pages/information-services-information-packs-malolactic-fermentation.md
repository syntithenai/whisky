# Malolactic Fermentation Information Pack

The Australian Wine Research Institute (AWRI) provides a comprehensive information pack on malolactic fermentation (MLF). The pack includes:
- An overview of MLF and its role in wine production.
- A curated bibliography of peer‑reviewed research articles covering topics such as bacterial strain selection, timing of inoculation, effects on aroma and flavour, and industrial practices.
- Practical guidance for winemakers on managing MLF, including best practice recommendations for cultured versus spontaneous fermentation.
- Links to related AWRI resources (e.g., microbial culture collections, regulatory information, and educational webinars).

The pack is intended as a reference tool for researchers, educators, and industry professionals seeking evidence‑based insights into MLF.

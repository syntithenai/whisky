# Benriach Malting Season (First Edition)

- **Distillery**: Benriach, Speyside, Scotland
- **Barley**: Concerto
- **Cask types**: ex‑bourbon and virgin oak
- **ABV**: 48.7 %
- **Release**: September 2021 (limited quantities)
- **Flavor profile**: Fruity & sweet – baking fruit tart, stewed pear, baked apple, marzipan, warm spices, milk chocolate; spicy palate with candied ginger, cinnamon, nutmeg, toffee, pistachio butter, vanilla.
- **Score**: 89 (Amanda Schuster)

# Kavalan Lán Single Malt
Kavalan Lán Single Malt is a Taiwanese whisky produced at Kavalan Distillery in Yilan. It is aged in a blend of port, ex‑bourbon and STR Vinho wine casks that have been shaved, toasted and recharred. Bottled at 43 % ABV, it will be released in select U.S. markets beginning May 2026 (SRP $79.99). The whisky is described as brambly with dark berry compote aromas, a chocolate‑pretzel maltiness, allspice, anise and nutmeg notes, finishing with black pepper, clove and heat.

- **Distillery**: Kavalan Distillery, Yilan, Taiwan
- **Cask types**: ex‑bourbon, port, STR Vinho wine
- **ABV**: 43 %
- **Release**: May 2026 (U.S.)
- **Price**: $79.99 (SRP)
- **Flavor profile**: fruity & spicy, dark berry, chocolate‑pretzel, allspice, anise, nutmeg, black pepper, clove

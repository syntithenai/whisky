# Wild Turkey 101 Bourbon

Wild Turkey 101 is a Kentucky‑style bourbon produced at the Wild Turkey Distillery by Master Distiller **Jimmy Russell**. It uses a mash bill of corn, rye and malted barley, ages between six and eight years in new charred oak barrels, and is bottled at 101 proof with minimal proofing after maturation.

> *Tasting notes (Ryan Conklin)* – “The nose is subtle yet full of rich sweet aromas of butterscotch, honey, vanilla caramel crème, with a touch of delicate underpinnings of citrus brightness and white flowers. The palate gives way to flavors of toasted wood, caramel, vanilla, and with just a couple drops of water reveals a rich chocolate maltiness with hints under‑ripe stone fruits. The finish has a lingering richness of spice and bold notes of toasted oak.”

The spirit is rated 3.53 / 5 by the community (4,675 reviews).

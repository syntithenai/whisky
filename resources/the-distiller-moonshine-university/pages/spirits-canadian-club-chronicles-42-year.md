# Canadian Club Chronicles 42 Year

- **Age & Origin**: 42‑year‑old whisky, distilled in Canada (Ontario) and aged for at least three years.
- **Blend**: Ultra‑aged corn whisky blended with 16‑year‑old rye, 12‑year‑old pot‑distilled rye and a splash of brandy under Canada’s 9.09 % rule.
- **Cask**: Ex‑bourbon barrels.
- **ABV & Price**: 45 % ABV; price varies by retailer (TruePrice data available).
- **Tasting Notes**: Floral opening fades to rich dried fruit, old oak and lumber. Herbal dill, mild solvents, caramel, chocolate, with prominent rye spices (pepper, cinnamon, cloves) leading to a dusty rye‑spiced finish.
- **Availability**: Limited quantities in Canada, the United States and travel retail.

# Dillon's Three Oaks Rye
Dillon’s second rye release is a 100 % rye grain spirit distilled in a copper pot still and aged for at least three years in three types of oak: seven first‑fill bourbon casks, two new American oak casks and one new Ontario oak cask. No flavoring or color was added.

**Key tasting notes** – grassy nose with rye grain cereal, green and herbaceous tones; palate balanced by vanilla, ginger, nutty spice and orchard fruit without overpowering oak.

The spirit is available across Canada and in a limited release in the United States. It has an ABV of 43 % and a community rating of 3.49/5 from 64 reviews.

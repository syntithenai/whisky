# George Dickel 17 Year Reserve Cask Strength

- **Type**: Tennessee whiskey (cask‑strength, 92 proof / 46 % ABV)
- **Mash bill**: 84 % corn, 8 % rye, 8 % malted barley
- **Barrels**: Selected by General Manager & Lead Distiller Nicole Austin; new charred American oak casks
- **Age**: 17 years (first released in 2016 as a limited 375 ml bottle, re‑released Dec 2022)
- **Price**: ~US$250 per bottle (SRP)
- **Tasting notes**: Candy apple, Bing cherry, cinnamon, blood orange; saddle leather & clove; tart fruits, raisins, cocoa, roasted cashews; spicy finish with a nut‑butter‑and‑jelly aftertaste.

> *“Robust aromas of candy apple, Bing cherry, cinnamon, and blood orange mix with a hint of saddle leather and clove. On the palate, tart fruits (plum, cherry, orange) meet sweet raisins, cocoa, and roasted cashews. The finish has a good spark of heat set off by baking spices, with that boozy nut butter‑and‑jelly effect lasting on the tongue.”* – Amanda Schuster

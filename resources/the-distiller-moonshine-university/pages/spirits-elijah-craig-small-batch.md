# Elijah Craig Small Batch Bourbon
- **Producer**: Heaven Hill (Kentucky, USA)
- **Release**: Early 2016, replacing the 12‑year bottling.
- **Batch size**: ~200 barrels per batch.
- **Age statement**: No‑age (8–12 yr selected bourbons).
- **Proof**: 94 proof (47.0 % ABV).
- **Barrel char level**: Level 3.
- **Tasting notes**: Caramel, fresh green herbs, char smoke, apples, toasted cereal flakes; soft, plush palate with moderate sweetness, finishing tangy savory note plus leather and tobacco.
- **Flavor profile**: Sweet.

> *“An overall pleasant bourbon with a solid length to the finish for the price. Enjoyable as a daily go‑to bourbon, and it’s also a nice proof to enjoy on the rocks or in a cocktail.”* – Stephanie Moreno (score 85)

# Barrell Vantage
Barrell Vantage is a cask‑strength bourbon blend from Barrell Craft Spirits, bottled at 114.44 proof (57.22 % ABV). It combines straight bourbons distilled in Indiana, Kentucky and Tennessee that were aged separately and finished in virgin Japanese Mizunara oak, French oak and toasted American oak barrels. The spirit is described as fruity, nutty, with pine needle, sandalwood, leather and baking spice aromas; the palate offers gingery notes, tart apple and cherry, toffee, toasted oak and a spicy‑oaky finish.

- **Release**: August 2022 (ongoing series)

# Bomberger's Declaration (2025 Release)

- **Distillery**: Michter’s Legacy Series, tribute to Bomberger’s Distillery.
- **Maturation**: Chinquapin oak staves, air‑dried and seasoned 18 mo–5 yr; toasted & charred per Michter’s specs.
- **Proof & Release**: Bottled at 108 proof, slated for July 2025 with limited availability. SRP $120.
- **Tasting Notes** (Amanda Schuster): Griddle‑corn‑cake aroma, cooked tart apples, toasted oak, sweet spices; palate of caramelized corn, apple, cinnamon, clove, black pepper, charred oak, saddle leather finish.
- **Flavor Profile**: Fruity & spicy.

> *“The nose and palate are reminiscent of griddle corn cakes (or sweet cornbread) slathered in spiced apple compote.”*

# Jack Daniel's Old No. 7

Jack Daniel’s Old No. 7 (also called Black Label or simply “Jack”) is a Tennessee whiskey made from an 80/12/8 corn‑barley‑rye mash. The spirit is fermented for six days, distilled to 140 proof, charcoal‑mellowed through 10 ft of maple charcoal, and aged at least four years in new, charred American oak barrels produced by the distillery itself. It has a banana‑vanilla‑corn nose with a sweet, fruity palate and a long finish featuring candy corn, tropical fruit and heat.

- **Proof**: 80 % (140 proof) before aging, 40 % ABV at bottling
- **Age**: ≥ 4 years
- **Barrel**: New, charred American oak
- **Flavor profile**: Fruity & vanilla
- **Tasting notes**: Banana, vanilla, yeast, corn, char, heat, tropical fruit.

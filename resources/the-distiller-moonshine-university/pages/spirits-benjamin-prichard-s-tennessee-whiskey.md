# Prichard's Tennessee Whiskey

Prichard’s Tennessee Whiskey is a single‑distillery product from Prichard’s Distillery in Tennessee. It is the only Tennessee whiskey legally allowed to skip the Lincoln County charcoal filtration process, thanks to a grandfather clause in the state law. The spirit is made with a mash bill dominated by sweet white corn, rye and malted barley, distilled in pot stills, then aged for about ten years in deeply charred new American oak barrels (and occasionally ex‑rum casks). It is bottled unfiltered at 80 proof.

**Tasting notes**: Sweet and spicy opening with nutmeg, vanilla, cinnamon and a hint of eggnog; followed by white pepper, under‑ripe red berries, apples, toasted malt, chocolate, coffee and dark cherries. The finish is rich malt with lingering spice.

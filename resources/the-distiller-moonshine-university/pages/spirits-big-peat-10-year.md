# Big Peat 10 Year

- **Type**: Blended malt from Islay, Scotland.
- **Release**: February 2019 to mark the brand’s 10‑year anniversary.
- **Age & ABV**: Aged 10 years, bottled at 46 % ABV without added colour or chill‑filtration.
- **Availability**: Limited to 850 cases in the UK, Europe and Asia.
- **Tasting notes** (Thijs Klaverstijn): savory with salty sea spray, fruit hints (lemon, banana), earthy nose, subtle wood smoke, oiliness, creaminess, olive brine, citrus, licorice root, and gentle peat. Score 89/100.

> *“Really savory, with a good amount of salty sea spray, and whispers of fruit (lemon, banana). The nose is slightly earthy too… Overall pretty elegant.”*

# Alberta Premium Cask Strength Rye
Alberta Premium’s cask‑strength rye was released in fall 2019 by pulling uncut rye directly from charred barrels. Made entirely of Canadian prairie rye and Rocky Mountain water, the spirit is aged at least three years and bottled at 65.1 % ABV.

**Tasting notes** – “Rich rye spices, chili‑pepper heat, toasted cereal, banana custard, tight oak structure, dark fruits, cinnamon & cocoa powder finish.”

The product has a community rating of 4.04/5 from 310 reviews and is available in Canada (limited US release as of Dec 2020).

# Glencadam The Re‑Awakening 13 Year

- **Distillery**: Glencadam, Highlands, Scotland (mothballed 2000, reopened 2003)
- **Release**: July 2017 by Angus Dundee Distillers as a limited edition bottling.
- **Quantity**: 6 000 individually numbered bottles.
- **Age & ABV**: 13 years, 46.0 %.
- **Cask type**: Not specified (single‑malt, pot‑still, no chill‑filtering or coloring).
- **Tasting notes**: Bracing maritime quality with pear, jasmine, peppercorn, leather and orange peel; described as “brings out” these flavors.
- **Score**: 90/100 by Jacob Grier.

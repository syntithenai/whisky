# BEARFACE 7‑Year Triple Oak

**Bearface Triple Oak** is a Canadian whisky released by the Mark Anthony Group. The spirit is made from single‑grain whisky that spends seven years in three distinct types of oak: American ex‑bourbon, French ex‑wine (from Mission Hill), and air‑dried virgin Hungarian oak. It was launched across Canada in fall 2018.

> *“Spicy oak and floral tones on the nose coat caramel, sweet corn and maple that carries into an opulent palate running the gamut between spicy, fruity and tart.”* – Blair Phillips (score 90)

The whisky is 42.5 % ABV and typically sells for around **$45–$55 CAD**.

## Key facts
- **Distillery:** Mark Anthony Group (Canada)
- **Age:** 7 years
- **Cask sequence:** American ex‑bourbon → French ex‑wine → Hungarian virgin oak
- **ABV:** 42.5 %
- **Launch:** Fall 2018 (Canada), international markets pending
- **Flavor profile:** Smoky, spicy oak, floral, caramel, sweet corn, maple, fruity, tart, toasty corn nuts, dry peppery spices

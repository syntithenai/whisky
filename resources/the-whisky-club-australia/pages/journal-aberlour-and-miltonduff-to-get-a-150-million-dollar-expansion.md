# Aberlour and Miltonduff Expansion

Chivas Brothers, owned by Pernod Ricard, announced a **£88 million (AUD $156 M)** investment to upgrade the Aberlour and Miltonduff single‑malt distilleries. The upgrades will:
- Increase total annual production by **14 million litres**.
- Enable Aberlour to produce **7.8 million litres** per year, doubling its current output.
- Add a new sustainable site at Miltonduff for an extra **10 million litres** of alcohol.
- Install bio‑plants and high‑efficiency mechanical vapour recompression fans to help each distillery reach **carbon‑neutral distillation by 2026**.

Both sites are expected to operate at full capacity by mid‑2025. The expansion aligns with the Scotch Whisky Association’s net‑zero strategy for 2040 and follows recent carbon‑neutral certifications in Australia.

The article also highlights member enthusiasm for Aberlour products, citing rapid sell‑outs of the 14 Year Old Double Cask.

# David Vitale on The Whisky Club and Starward Distillery

- **Focus**: Interview with Starward founder David Vitale about the exclusive *Starward Pedro Ximénez Cask* released for The Whisky Club.
- **Key points**:
  - The Wood‑Finished Series uses locally sourced wine barrels (Cognac, PX, Tawny) and lets the barrel dominate flavour.
  - This is Starward’s largest PX release; Spanish PX barrels previously held sweet Sherry for ~4 years, giving a “Sherry bomb” profile.
  - Vitale pioneered fully‑matured Aussie PX whiskies in 2011; demand has grown as sourcing improved.
  - Future releases are uncertain due to limited Australian PX barrels, but the club may host one‑off editions.
- **Club perks**: Members who received the November bottle entered a draw for an all‑expensed Starward experience worth ~$5 k.

## Key facts
- Exclusive release: *Starward Pedro Ximénez Cask* (November 2023).
- Wood‑Finished Series barrels: Cognac (France), PX (Spain), Tawny (South Australia).
- Largest PX release by Starward; Spanish PX barrels held Sherry for ~4 years.
- First Aussie whisky fully matured in Pedro Ximénez (2011).

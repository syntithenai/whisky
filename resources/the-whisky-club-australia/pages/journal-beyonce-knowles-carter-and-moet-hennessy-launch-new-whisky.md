# SirDavis Whisky Launch
Beyoncé Knowles‑Carter partners with Moët Hennessy and Dr Bill Lumsden to launch **SirDavis**, a 44 % ABV single‑malt whisky made from a 51 % rye / 49 % malted barley mash. The spirit is finished in sherry casks, earning it Platinum at the 2023 SIP Awards, Best In Class (95 points), Gold at the 2023 New York International Spirits Competition (93 points) and recognition at the Ultimate Spirits Challenge.

The brand honours Beyoncé’s paternal grandfather, Davis Hogue, a moonshiner during Prohibition. Dr Lumsden, Director of Whisky Creation at Glenmorangie and Ardbeg, led the recipe development.

SirDavis is part of The Whisky Club Australia’s exclusive releases alongside Glenmorangie French XO Calvados Cask, Amontillado Sherry Cask, Cadboll Estate 15 Year‑Old and Malaga Cask editions.

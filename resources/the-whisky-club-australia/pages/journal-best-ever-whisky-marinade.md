**Best Ever Whisky Marinade**

A quick‑fire recipe that uses ¼ cup olive oil, 2 Tbsp whisky, 2 Tbsp soy sauce, 1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce, 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar, 3 smashed garlic cloves, 1 Tbsp ground black pepper, ¼ tsp chilli, ⅛ tsp cinnamon and a drizzle of honey. Mix all ingredients, coat your protein or vegetables, seal in the fridge for 15–30 min (fish/seafood), up to 2 h (chicken) or 1–12 h (beef/pork). Tag the club on social media when you try it.

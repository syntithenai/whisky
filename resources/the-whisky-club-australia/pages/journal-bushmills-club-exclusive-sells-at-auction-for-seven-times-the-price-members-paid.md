# Bushmills Club Exclusive Auction

- **Event**: The *Bushmills The Christmas Causeway 2012 Vintage, First‑Fill Oloroso Full Maturation* sold at a European auction for **$1,115.56**, more than **seven times** the price members paid in Australia.
- **Auction details**: 71 bids; cask strength 51.6 % ABV, 100 % Oloroso sherry‑cask matured.
- **Historical context**: The first Club collaboration (2022) sold for €1,350 (~$2,000) worldwide; Australian members paid $135. Similar high auction prices were seen for the 2010 Double Moscatel Cask and the 2012 Burgundy Cask.
- **Club benefits**: Members receive exclusive releases that often fetch high auction values; non‑members are encouraged to join.

## Key Takeaways
1. **High demand** for Club exclusives drives auction prices far above retail.
2. **Consistent pricing strategy**: Australian members pay a fraction of the global price.
3. **Marketing emphasis** on rarity and award recognition (e.g., 98/100 points, Double Gold).
4. **Member engagement** highlighted through reviews and community features.

# A Dram with Jane Overeem

The article is an informal interview with **Jane Overeem**, founder of the Tasmanian whisky brand *Overeem*. It covers her early memories of whisky, memorable tasting events (including a “Dinner in the Sky” at 50 m above Hobart), favourite whiskies worldwide, and her vision for the future of Overeem and Tasmanian whisky. The piece also promotes the new **Overeem Pedro Ximénez Sherry Cask** release.

---

## Key points
- Jane’s first whisky experience was at 18 during the Australian Malt Whisky Convention.
- She hosted a tasting on a crane‑lifted table called *Dinner in the Sky*.
- Her top five drams: Overeem (43 % ABV), Yamazaki 18, Aberlour A’bunadh, Bowmore 12, Balvenie 25.
- Future plans include expanding production, new cask releases and global partnerships.
- The new **Overeem Pedro Ximénez Sherry Cask** is bottled at 61.8 % straight from the cask after a second maturation in an ex‑Pedro Ximénez butt.

---

## Product highlight
- **Overeem Pedro Ximénez Sherry Cask** – deep jammy stone fruit, burnt orange and treacle notes; bottled at 61.8 % straight from the cask.

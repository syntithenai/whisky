# A Yarn with Morris Head Distiller Darren Peck

- **Background**: Yorkshireman Darren Peck studied environmental science and discovered distilling through a beer‑brewery placement.
- **Career Path**: Worked across six continents, including rum in Venezuela, whisky in Australia, and various spirits worldwide.
- **Notable Anecdotes**: Encounter with Russian police on a train; tasting low‑quality vodka, sausage, and fish.
- **Current Role**: Head distiller at Morris Distillery, responsible for the 2024 Tokay Barrel release.
- **Tokay Barrel**: Limited edition whisky produced in a restored 1930s still; marketed as a future collector’s item.

The article blends personal history with promotional content for Morris Whisky.

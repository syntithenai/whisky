# How We Create Your Whisky

The Whisky Club works directly with distilleries worldwide to source, hand‑select and bottle exclusive whiskies for its members. The process involves:
- Visiting distilleries and collaborating with Master Distillers & Blenders.
- Selecting casks (often rare finishes such as Oloroso, Marsala, Banyuls).
- Tasting hundreds of samples and turning down ~90 % to keep only the best.

The club’s portfolio includes award‑winning drams from Scotland, Ireland, Australia, North America, Japan and more. Members receive guaranteed bottles even when they win multiple medals or fetch high auction prices.

Key distilleries highlighted: Glenfarclas, Glen Scotia, Aberlour, Balvenie, Bushmills, Lark, Starward, Hellyers Road, Redbreast, etc.

Member reviews are overwhelmingly positive (average 4.9 /5) and often praise the depth, cask influence and exclusivity of the releases.

# A bottle of The Dalmore whisky sells for $179,800

A 49‑year‑old single malt from **The Dalmore** sold at Sotheby’s for £93 750 (≈$179 800). The article focuses on the *Dalmore Luminary No.2* edition – a limited release in partnership with V&A Dundee and Zaha Hadid Architects, housed in an 80 kg glass sculpture. It was matured in American white‑oak ex‑Bourbon casks, finished with rare Port Colheita 1963, 30‑year‑old Aponte Sherry, select Bourbon, and finally aged in a 1951 virgin oak hybrid cask that was hand‑toasted by Gregg Glass. Only three decanters and two glass sculptures will ever be made; proceeds go to Scotland’s design museum.

The Whisky Club previously released *Luminary No.1* in November 2022, the first time Australian whisky lovers could buy a Dalmore first edition exclusively through the club.

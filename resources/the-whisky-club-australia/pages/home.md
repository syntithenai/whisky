# The Whisky Club Australia

The Whisky Club is a free‑to‑join subscription service that delivers exclusive, award‑winning single malts to members each month. Members receive a *Whisky of the Month* (priced $120–$150) and can add extra bottles or skip deliveries at no cost. The club boasts over 38 000 members, offers member perks such as events, discounts, and masterclasses, and works directly with top distilleries to create limited‑edition releases.

Key highlights:
- **Membership**: Free, no contract, no minimum spend.
- **Monthly delivery**: One or two bottles per month; optional opt‑out.
- **Exclusive releases**: Limited‑run single malts (e.g., Loch Lomond 2010 Sauternes, Glen Scotia 2017 Single Cask #3787).
- **Member perks**: Events, discounts at partner bars and distilleries, masterclasses, travel prizes.
- **Community**: 38 000+ members with positive reviews and testimonials.

The site also features a blog, events calendar, gifting options, and a whisky school.

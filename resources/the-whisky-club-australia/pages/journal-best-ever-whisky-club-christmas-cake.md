# Whisky Fruit Cake Recipe

The article shares a simple Christmas fruit cake recipe that incorporates whisky, inspired by long‑time club member Gary Hn. The key steps are:
1. Soak 1 kg dried mixed fruit in milk and 50–60 ml whisky for at least four hours (overnight is best).
2. Preheat the oven to 150 °C and line two 13 × 22 cm loaf pans.
3. Mix the soaked fruit, milk/whisky and 280 g self‑raising flour in a large bowl until well combined.
4. Divide the batter between the pans and bake for 55–60 minutes; test with a skewer.
5. Optionally drizzle extra whisky on top after cooling.

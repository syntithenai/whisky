# Benriach Unveils its Oldest and Rarest Expression
Benriach has released the **Benriach 1966 Cask Aged 50 Years**, the distillery’s first spirit bottled at 50 years. The whisky was originally filled in September 1966, matured for five decades in Speyside warehouses, and now limited to **37 bottles** crafted by Master Blender Dr. Rachel Barrie. It comes in a bespoke crystal decanter inspired by the distillery’s eclectic casks and will retail for **$48,000**.

## Key facts
- First Benriach bottled at 50 years old
- 37 limited‑edition bottles
- Master Blender: Dr. Rachel Barrie
- Retail price: $48,000
- Packaging: bespoke crystal decanter

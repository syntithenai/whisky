# TTB Publishes 4 AVA‑Related Proposals

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) announced on **January 5 2024** four proposals concerning American Viticultural Areas (AVAs):

1. **Rename Mendocino Ridge AVA** to *Mendocino Coast Ridge* in Mendocino County, California.
2. **Create Conneaut Creek AVA** – 70,437 acres in Ashtabula County, Ohio, within Lake Erie AVA.
3. **Create Tryon Foothills AVA** – 176 square miles in Polk County, North Carolina (outside any existing AVA).
4. **Create Nashoba Valley AVA** – 18,367 acres in Worcester County, Massachusetts (outside any existing AVA).

All proposals are pending public comment until **March 5 2024**. Detailed documents and docket numbers are available on the TTB website.

# Semiannual Unified Regulatory Agenda Now Available

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) announces that the Fall 2024 edition of the Federal Government’s Unified Regulatory Agenda is now available on Reginfo.gov. The agenda lists planned regulatory and deregulatory actions for all federal agencies, including TTB under the Department of the Treasury.

- **Publication date:** December 20, 2024
- **Where to find it:** Reginfo.gov – select *Department of the Treasury* in the agency drop‑down.
- **Purpose:** Provides industry stakeholders with advance notice of upcoming regulatory changes.

The page also includes standard TTB navigation links and a brief survey for user feedback.

# New Distilled Spirits Formula Tool

The U.S. Alcohol and Tobacco Tax and Trade Bureau (TTB) has launched a new **Distilled Spirits Formula Tool** on TTB.gov to help distillers and importers determine whether their product requires a formula approval before submitting a label application.

Key features:
- **Clear & Accurate Information** – reflects current regulatory requirements.
- **Improved Guidance** – provides detailed instructions when a formula is needed.
- **Fewer Errors, Faster Approvals** – reduces common mistakes that delay approvals.

The tool is part of TTB’s broader suite of formulation tools, which also includes Malt Beverage and Wine/Cider Formula Tools.

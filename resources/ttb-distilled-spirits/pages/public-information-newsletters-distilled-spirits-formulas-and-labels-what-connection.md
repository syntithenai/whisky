# Distilled Spirits Formulas and Labels: What is the Connection?

The TTB newsletter explains why label applications for distilled spirits are often returned: the label must be consistent with the approved formula. Key elements—product class, type, composition statement, alcohol content, and mandatory disclosures—are derived from the formula. The article points to resources in Formulas Online (Formula, Results, Docs/Links tabs) and additional presentations, charts, and example formulas that illustrate correct labeling practices.

## Takeaway
Ensure label data is supported by your approved formula to avoid delays in approval and market entry.

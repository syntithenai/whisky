# Distilled Spirits Reports – TTB

The page hosts statistical reports on U.S. distilled spirits compiled from monthly operational filings (F‑5110 series). It offers downloadable datasets in XLSX, CSV, JSON and PDF formats, including national, yearly, monthly, and mapping data. The site also provides contact information for the Open Data team and links to related TTB resources.

---

**Key Points**
- Reports derived from F‑5110.43, 40, 28, & 11 filings.
- Monthly reports released ~45 days after due date; end‑of‑year releases ~60 days later.
- Data files updated regularly (latest Jan 2026).
- Contact: TTB Open Data via form on the page.

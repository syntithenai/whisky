# Aberfeldy Single Malt Scotch Whisky

Aberfeldy is a Highland single‑malt distillery founded in 1896 by John Dewar & Sons on the south bank of the River Tay, Perthshire. The site houses four stills (two wash and two spirit) and produces a range of age‑statement expressions from 9 to 33 years, as well as limited‑edition cask finishes.

The distillery is known for its bright, fruity style with notes of caramel, apple, tea, and light pepper. It has received numerous tasting accolades, including a 91‑point score for the 25‑year‑old (Cadenhead Bond Reserve) in 2006.

Aberfeldy’s product line includes:
- **Standard age statements**: 9, 10, 12, 14, 15, 16, 18, 19, 21, 24, 25, 27, 30, 33 years.
- **Special finishes**: Madeira, Sémillon wine, Bolgheri red wine, French red wine, sherry‑finished “Exceptional Cask Series”, and various single‑cask releases.

The distillery offers virtual tours and has a strong online presence through its website (www.dewarswow.com) and the WhiskyFun tasting index.

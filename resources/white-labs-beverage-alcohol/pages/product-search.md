# White Labs Lab Supplies
White Labs offers a catalog of laboratory consumables for fermentation science, including yeast and bacteria banks, enzymes & nutrients, lab services, educational resources, and merchandise.

## Key Product Categories
- **Yeast & Bacteria Bank** – over 30 commercial strains (e.g., WLP001 California Ale Yeast, WLP002 English Ale Yeast).
- **Enzymes & Nutrients** – media kits such as YPD, Wallerstein Media, and specialized nutrient plates.
- **Lab Supplies** – microscopes, staining kits, safety data sheets, and test kits for alcohol tolerance, flocculation, etc.
- **Merchandise** – branded apparel and glassware.

## Services & Resources
- Analytical lab services (gluten testing, microbiological analysis, nutritional labeling).
- Educational content via the Fermentation Society blog and resource guides.
- Global distribution network with a U.S. support line and international contacts.

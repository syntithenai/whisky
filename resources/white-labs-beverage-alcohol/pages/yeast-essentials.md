# Yeast Essentials – White Labs

White Labs offers a three‑day, hands‑on workshop called **Yeast Essentials** that covers yeast biology, fermentation management, lab practices and sensory training. The course is held at two campuses: San Diego (May 13‑15 2026) and Asheville (Oct 14‑16 2026). Participants include professional brewers, homebrewers and fermentation enthusiasts.

Key highlights:
- Live lab sessions with world‑class specialists.
- Practical demonstrations of yeast handling, QC skills and applied techniques.
- Networking meals, happy hour and a brewery visit.
- Speakers from White Labs (e.g., Devin Tani, Mike White) and industry experts such as Chris Lowe (Sierra Nevada) and John Wammes (Water Works).

The event is fully inclusive – all meals, accommodation recommendations and materials are provided.

# White Labs – About Us
White Labs is a fermentation‑focused company that supplies high‑purity yeast, bacteria, enzymes and lab services to brewers, distillers and winemakers worldwide.

- Founded by home brewers turned biochemists.
- Operates yeast production facilities in San Diego (USA), Asheville (NC) and Copenhagen (Denmark); a distribution warehouse in Hong Kong.
- Offers a full range of products: yeast & bacteria bank, enzymes & nutrients, lab services, education, merchandise.
- Runs community initiatives such as *Beer for Boobs* and global guild partnerships.
- Provides an app for quick ordering and has a tasting room (White Labs Brewing Co.) and restaurant (White Labs Kitchen & Tap).

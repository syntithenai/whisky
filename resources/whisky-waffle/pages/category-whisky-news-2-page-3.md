## Distillery Facts
- Ardnahoe will be the first new Islay distillery since Kilchoman (2005).
- It will be located north of Port Askaig between Caol Ila and Bunnahabhain.
- The distillery is owned by Hunter Laing & Co., a Glasgow‑based bottler founded in 2013.
- Jim McEwan, former Master Distiller at Bruichladdich, will serve as Production Manager.

## Resource Facts
- Hunter Laing & Co. owns brands such as Old & Rare, Old Malt Cask, and The Sovereign.
- Dark Valley is an independent Tasmanian bottler using spirits from Lark Distillery (and plans to use other local distilleries).
- 2016 Waffle Awards featured categories like Isle of the Drammed, Tartan Slipper, Pocket Pleaser, etc.

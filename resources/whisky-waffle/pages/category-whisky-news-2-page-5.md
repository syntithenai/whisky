# Whisky Waffle – Page 5 (Whisky news)

This page aggregates a series of blog posts from the Whisky Waffle site, covering events, awards, reviews and community updates that took place in late 2014‑early 2015. Highlights include:
- A masterclass hosted by William Grant & Sons in Hobart.
- Jim Murray’s *Whisky Bible* rankings and his “liquid gold” list of Tasmanian whiskies.
- The inaugural Whisky Waffle Awards with categories such as the Isle of the Drammed, Tartan Slipper, Pocket Pleaser, Weirdsky and Golden Dram.
- Announcements about the Tasmanian Whisky Appreciation Society (TWAS) and its contributors.
- A “Johnnie Walker Week” tasting series.
- The launch event for Hellyers Road’s 12‑Year‑Old expression.

The page is essentially a chronological archive of these posts, each linked to its full article on the site.

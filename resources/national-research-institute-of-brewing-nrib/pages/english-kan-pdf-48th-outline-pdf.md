# Outline of the 48th Honkaku Shochu and Awamori Contest
- **Date & Venue**: Judging held on 3‑4 June 2025 under NRIB & JSS.
- **Scope**: Nationwide pot‑still shochu (Honkaku) and awamori research, quality trends, and chemical analysis.
- **Entries**: 185 from 66 manufacturers across 23 prefectures; 93 % of last year’s entries.
- **Production Areas**: Kyushu (137 entries) & Okinawa (12) – 81 % of total.
- **Raw‑material mix**: Rice 27, barley 67, sweet potato 56, awamori 14, sake lees 10, buckwheat 4, brown sugar 2, other 5.
- **Product types**: 140 normal (96 %) and 45 special (long‑term aged, cask‑aged, earthenware jar‑aged) shochu.
- **Judging method**: Profile method with 5 sensory characteristics + check‑all‑that‑apply for 17 positive & 24 negative traits; tablet computers replaced paper mark cards.
- **Quality trends**: Fruity & mild in rice/barley, floral in sweet potato, full‑bodied awamori; specific aroma profiles noted per distillation type.
- **Chemical analysis**: Ongoing to assess fermentation, distillation and storage management; results will be published by NRIB.

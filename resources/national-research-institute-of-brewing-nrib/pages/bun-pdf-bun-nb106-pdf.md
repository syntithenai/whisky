# 106 Raw Material Fruit Aroma

- **Sampling**: 300 mL of the test material is collected.
- **Target composition**: The total aroma concentration is adjusted to **4–5 g / 100 mL**.
- **Procedure**:
  - Add 45 mL water and 1.5 mL ethanol (or a similar solvent) to the sample.
  - Stir at **65 °C for 20 min**, then freeze‑thaw the mixture.
  - After thawing, add 10 % water solution and cool to **0 °C** until a blue colour appears; finally bring the volume up to 100 mL.

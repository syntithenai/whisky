# National Research Institute of Brewing – Sake Information Page

The page is a Japanese‑language portal that lists downloadable PDF reports on sake (Japanese rice wine) and related beverages such as shochu, whisky, and wine. Each report is titled with a short description in Japanese followed by an English translation in parentheses when available. The PDFs cover topics ranging from production methods, flavour characteristics, scientific background, to regulatory aspects.

Key highlights:
- **Sake**: 4 main reports (clean sake, clean sake 2, clean sake – English version) and a series of “Special Feature” PDFs covering general overview, ingredients, science, and flavour.
- **Shochu**: Two feature articles (summary & scientific background) plus an optional Japanese‑language edition.
- **Whisky**: One comprehensive PDF (~20 MB).
- **Wine**: Two feature articles with English translations.

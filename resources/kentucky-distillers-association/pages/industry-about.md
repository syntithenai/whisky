# Kentucky Distillers' Association – About

The KDA is a 501(c)(6) nonprofit founded in **1880** that serves as the worldwide voice for Kentucky Bourbon and distilled spirits. Its membership of over 200 distilleries produces the majority of the world’s bourbon, driving an industry worth more than $9 billion. The association offers advocacy, media relations, trade development, technical assistance, tourism promotion (notably the **Kentucky Bourbon Trail®**), legal defense, and economic‑development support.

Key initiatives include:
- **Bourbon Trail® PAC** – lobbying for favorable legislation.
- **Hall of Fame®** – honoring industry pioneers.
- **Order of the Writ®** – a charitable organization promoting bourbon education and stewardship.

The KDA’s leadership team comprises executives from major distilleries, tourism experts, and advocacy specialists who work to protect the industry’s heritage while fostering growth and responsible consumption.

# Kentucky Distillers' Association Impact

The page presents key economic statistics for Kentucky bourbon: a **566 % increase in production since 2000**, a **$10.6 billion** industry generating **23,935 jobs** and **$371.8 million** in tax revenue. It highlights tourism (**2.7 M annual visits**) and export growth (**98 % between 2010‑2017**), while noting tariff challenges. The association emphasizes its role in supporting agriculture (27.3 million bushels of corn, 84 % from Kentucky farmers) and forest management for oak barrels. Capital investment totals **$3.55 billion** over five years.

Economic impact reports are listed for 2009‑2025.

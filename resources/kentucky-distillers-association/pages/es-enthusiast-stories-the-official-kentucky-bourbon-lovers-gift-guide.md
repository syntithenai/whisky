# The Official Kentucky Bourbon Lovers Gift Guide

A holiday‑season gift guide from the Kentucky Distillers' Association that showcases a curated selection of bourbon‑themed gifts and experiences. Highlights include:
- A **Bourbon Trail Field Guide & Passport** (160 pages, maps, itineraries, cocktail recipes, tasting notes) with access to 41 distillery sites.
- **Certified Bourbon Steward Course** – the world’s only accredited bourbon education program.
- Bar‑tool sets and cocktail‑related accessories such as Bittercube bitters, bar towels, and a Mint Julep‑themed print collection.
- Gift certificates for **Bourbon Tours** with professional transportation partners.
- Specialty items like Bourbon‑flavored chocolate truffles and an Advent calendar of chocolate.

The guide is written in Spanish but translated into English here. It includes brief bios of the author, Amandalin Ryan, and links to related resources on the association’s site.

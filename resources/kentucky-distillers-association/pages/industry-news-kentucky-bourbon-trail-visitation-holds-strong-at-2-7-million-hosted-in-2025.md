# Kentucky Bourbon Trail Visitation Holds Strong at 2.7 Million in 2025
The Kentucky Distillers’ Association (KDA) reports that the Kentucky Bourbon Trail (KBT®) welcomed **2.7 million visitors** in 2025, matching last year’s record and underscoring bourbon tourism’s resilience amid broader travel declines.
- **Visitor profile:** 80 % from outside Kentucky; 62 % with household incomes >$100k; 95 % highly approve of the experience.
- **Economic impact:** Guests spend $600–$1,400 per trip on lodging, dining, entertainment and transport.
- **Growth:** 11 new distillery destinations added in 2025, bringing total sites to nearly 70.
- **Planning tool:** The KBT website offers a “Build Your Own Bourbon Trail” itinerary builder.

The KDA highlights the program’s stability as a key driver of Kentucky’s $10.6 billion bourbon economy and its role in job creation.

# Bourbon & Boos: Inside Kentucky’s Most Haunted Distilleries

A light‑hearted Halloween feature from the Kentucky Distillers’ Association that recounts ghostly tales linked to some of the state’s most iconic bourbon distilleries. The article lists three main sites—James B. Beam, Wild Turkey, and Old Forester—each with anecdotes about apparitions, unexplained noises, and paranormal investigations. It also mentions other haunted spots in Louisville such as the Seelbach Hotel and Whiskey Row, inviting readers to share their own spooky bourbon stories.

The piece is written in Spanish but contains many English brand names and references. No product pricing or purchase links are provided.

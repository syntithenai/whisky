# Grilled Peach Old Fashioned
A summer‑ready cocktail that blends Kentucky bourbon with grilled peaches, simple syrup and peach bitters. The recipe includes a homemade peach‑grill syrup and tips for selecting the right bourbon.

## Ingredients
- 2 oz Kentucky bourbon (90–100 proof)
- ¼ oz grilled peach simple syrup
- 2 dashes peach bitters

## Method
1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing glass with plenty of ice.
2. Stir gently for 30 s, then strain into an old‑fashioned glass over fresh large cubes.
3. Garnish with a grilled peach slice.

### Grilled Peach Simple Syrup
- 3 ripe peaches
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup granulated sugar

Grill peaches until caramelized, simmer in water for 10 min, dissolve sugar, chill, strain and store up to 6 weeks.

## Bourbon Selection Tips
- Proof: 90–100 (45–50% ABV)
- Age: <4 yrs for a smoother profile
- Avoid heavily finished bourbons that may clash with the peach flavor.

### Suggested Brands
Elijah Craig, Maker’s Mark, Bulleit, Rittenhouse Rye, Knob Creek Rye.

# 6 Things You May Not Know About the KY Bourbon Hall of Fame® Class of 2022

The Kentucky Distillers’ Association highlights six inductees who shaped bourbon’s legacy:
1. **Rep. D. Chad McCoy** – championed HB 100, enabling vintage‑spirit sales.
2. **Stephen Francis Thompson** – pioneer of Kentucky craft bourbon and founder of Kentucky Artisan Distillery.
3. **Julian P. Van Winkle III** – first Kentuckian James Beard Award winner and president of Old Rip Van Winkle Distillery.
4. **Andrea Maria Wilson** – trailblazing chemical engineer, COO/Master of Maturation at Michter’s and former KDA chair.
5. **Rep. John Yarmuth** – co‑founder of the Congressional Bourbon Caucus and advocate for excise‑tax relief.
6. **Kaveh Zamanian** – psychologist‑turned‑distiller, founder of Rabbit Hole Distillery.

The article underscores legislative, entrepreneurial, culinary, advocacy, and cultural contributions that elevate Kentucky bourbon on a national stage.

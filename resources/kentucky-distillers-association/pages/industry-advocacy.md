# Advocacy – Kentucky Distillers' Association

The Kentucky Distillers’ Association (KDA) is the sole organization in Kentucky that advocates for bourbon and distilled spirits at all government levels. The page outlines KDA’s mission, key policy priorities, recent legislative successes, and recognition of public officials who support the industry.

- **Mission**: Protect trade interests, monitor laws, grow awareness, and build partnerships.
- **Key Advocacy Areas**:
  - Tax parity for beer, wine, and spirits
  - Direct‑to‑consumer shipping (reciprocal states)
  - Ready‑to‑drink cocktail parity
  - Responsible shipping practices and ID checks
  - International trade protection (tariffs, duty drawback)
  - USPS Shipping Equity Act & Duty Drawback for aged spirits
- **Recent Legislative Wins**: SB 50 (self‑distribution), HB 439 (safety fund), HB 5 (barrel tax phase‑out), HB 500 (private barrel selection), HB 415 (producer‑only shipping), and others.
- **Awards & Recognition**: The KDA’s “100 Proof” award honors lawmakers who champion bourbon‑friendly legislation.

The page serves as a resource for stakeholders, policymakers, and the public to understand KDA’s advocacy work and legislative impact.

# Kentucky Bourbon Hall of Fame
The Kentucky Distillers’ Association (KDA) established the Hall of Fame in 2001 to honor individuals who have significantly contributed to Kentucky bourbon. The page lists inductees by year, including master distillers, owners, executives, politicians, and other industry figures. Each entry includes a brief title or role.

## Inductee Highlights
- **2025**: Craig Beam (Master Distiller, Jackson Purchase), Larry Ebersold (Consultant), Mary Dowling (Owner/Operator, Waterfill), Chris Morris (Emeritus Master Distiller, Brown‑Forman), Damon Thayer (Retired Senate Majority Leader).
- **2024**: Elmer Lucille Allen (Chemist, Brown‑Forman), Dee Ford (Brand GM, Angel’s Envy), Ken Lewis (Former CEO, New Riff), Peter T. Loftin (Founder & Chairman, Bardstown Bourbon), R.M. Corky Taylor (Owner/CEO, Kentucky Peerless).
- **2023**: Jerry O. Dalton (Retired Master Distiller, Jim Beam), Pete Kamer (Distillery Engineer), Joseph J. Magliocco (President & CEO, Michter’s), Trey Zoeller (Founder, Jefferson’s Bourbon), Chester “Chet” Zoeller (Author/Co‑founder).
- **2022**: D. Chad McCoy (Representative), Stephen Francis Thompson (Founder, Kentucky Artisan Distillery), Julian P. Van Winkle III (President, Pappy Van Winkle), Andrea Marie Wilson (COO & Master of Maturation, Michter’s), John Yarmuth (U.S. Representative).

The page continues with inductees from 2021 back to the inaugural class in 2001.

## Purpose
To recognize and celebrate individuals who have shaped Kentucky bourbon through dedication, innovation, and leadership.

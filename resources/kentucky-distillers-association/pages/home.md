# Kentucky Bourbon: The Proof is Here®

Kentucky Distillers’ Association (KDA) presents a concise overview of why Kentucky dominates bourbon production. Key points include:
- **Historical depth** – Kentucky has been producing bourbon longer than the state itself.
- **Geography & resources** – Fertile soil for corn, limestone‑filtered water, and a climate that drives barrel expansion/contraction.
- **People & culture** – A community of makers, enthusiasts, and visitors that fuels the industry.
- **Craftsmanship** – Over 200 years of refining techniques, emphasizing patience and quality.

The page invites visitors to explore cocktails, stories, and the Kentucky Bourbon Trail, while highlighting KDA’s role as the global voice for bourbon.

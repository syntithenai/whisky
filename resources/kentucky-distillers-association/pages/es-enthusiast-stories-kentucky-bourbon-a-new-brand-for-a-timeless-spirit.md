# Kentucky Bourbon: A New Brand for a Timeless Spirit

The Kentucky Distillers' Association (KDA) launched the **Kentucky Bourbon** brand to protect and promote the heritage of bourbon as an integral part of Kentucky’s identity. The article explains that bourbon is more than a drink—it is a lifestyle, a journey along the Kentucky Bourbon Trail, and a community that welcomes enthusiasts worldwide.

Key points:
- Bourbon is legally defined but its roots run deeper in family recipes, local grain, and shared memories.
- The brand aims to build a global community of fans, experts, and newcomers.
- Kentucky Bourbon is positioned as a lifestyle with events, tours, and cultural experiences.
- The KDA emphasizes responsible enjoyment and invites people over 21 to join the community.

The page also lists trademarks such as *Kentucky Bourbon Trail®*, *Bourbon Trail Craft Tour®*, and others owned by the KDA.

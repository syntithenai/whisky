# A Master Taster’s Guide to Tasting Kentucky Bourbon

Peggy Noe Stevens explains how to identify and pair bourbon flavors using simple sensory steps and intensity charts. She outlines grain, yeast, and barrel contributions, offers tasting techniques (nose, taste, balance), and gives practical pairing advice for foods ranging from sauces to cheeses.

---

**Key takeaways:**
- Bourbon flavor comes from grains (corn, rye, barley, wheat), yeast, and the oak barrel.
- Tasting steps: sniff first, then taste, noting sweet/spicy/earthy/fresh notes.
- Use intensity charts to match bourbon with food intensity (light‑medium‑heavy).
- Pairing guidelines for sauces, dressings, vegetables, fruits, cheeses, meats, and sweets.

---

**Author:** Peggy Noe Stevens – first female master bourbon taster, President of Peggy Noe Stevens & Associates, founder of the Bourbon Women Association.

# NCSL – Kentucky Distillers' Association

The page outlines the **National Council on Spirits Law (NCSL)** and its advocacy for parity between spirits, beer, and wine in terms of taxation, direct‑to‑consumer shipping, and ready‑to‑drink cocktails. It calls for equal tax treatment, expanded reciprocal shipping laws, and ABV‑based pricing for RTDs. The site also highlights the industry’s responsibility initiatives and links to the Kentucky Bourbon Trail.

---

## Key Points
- **Tax parity**: Spirits should be taxed like beer and wine.
- **Direct‑to‑consumer shipping**: Only 5 states + DC allow it for spirits; all states allow it for wine.
- **Ready‑to‑drink cocktails**: Pricing and market access should depend on ABV, not ingredient type.
- **Industry responsibility**: Emphasis on education against impaired driving and safe shipping practices.
- **Kentucky Bourbon Trail**: Promotional link to the state’s bourbon tourism program.

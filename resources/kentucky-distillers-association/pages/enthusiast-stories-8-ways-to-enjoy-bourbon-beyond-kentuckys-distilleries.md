# 8 Ways to Enjoy Bourbon Beyond Kentucky’s Distilleries

- **Flights at local restaurants** – sample rare, vintage bourbons in curated or blind tastings.
- **Prohibition‑era cocktails** – speakeasy bars like Hell or High Water and the Old Seelbach Bar.
- **Hot Brown sandwich** – a classic Kentucky dish best paired with bourbon.
- **Cocktail class at Hermitage Farm** – learn mixology amid horses.
- **Bourbon Dinner Train** – gourmet meal and tasting on a vintage railcar.
- **DIY workshops** – candle making, cooking classes, and soy sauce demos featuring bourbon.
- **Bourbon Bloody Mary** – brunch cocktail with an oaky finish.
- **Private barrel selections** – store‑picked bottles from expert curators.

These experiences showcase Kentucky’s bourbon culture beyond the distillery trail.

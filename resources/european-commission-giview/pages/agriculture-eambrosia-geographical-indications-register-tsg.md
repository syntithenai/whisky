# eAmbrosia – Traditional Specialities Guaranteed (TSG)

The page is a section of the European Commission’s **eAmbrosia** portal that lists all products registered under the *Traditional Specialities Guaranteed* scheme. It explains that TSGs protect names of products whose traditional methods of production or recipes are safeguarded across the EU.

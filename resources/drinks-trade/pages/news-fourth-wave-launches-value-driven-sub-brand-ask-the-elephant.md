# Fourth Wave launches value‑driven sub‑brand – Ask the Elephant

Fourth Wave Wines has added a new sub‑brand, **Ask the Elephant**, to its flagship *Elephant In The Room* line. The launch features two varietals now on sale: **Bold Shiraz** and **Detailed Cabernet Sauvignon** (both priced at $10 RRP). Additional labels—Zingy Sauvignon Blanc, Friendly Pinot Grigio, and Comfy Merlot—are slated for future release.

The brand aims to simplify wine buying for younger consumers by using clear, shelf‑friendly descriptors. Market data from Endeavour Group and IWSR show that Shiraz and Sauvignon Blanc dominate Australian sales, especially among the youth who are more experimental. Co‑owner Nicholas Crampton says the range is “approachable” and removes traditional wine intimidation.

Retailers can run a promotion: customers scan a QR code on specially marked bottles to enter a contest for a year’s free wine.

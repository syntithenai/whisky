# Release Radar Highlights
- **Voodoo Ranger** launches two vodka‑based RTDs (Hard Cola & Hard Lemonade) at $29 for a 4‑pack of 375 ml cans, ABV 6%.
- **Ardbeg Dolce** – limited edition whisky aged in Marsala dolce casks and bourbon cask‑matured Ardbeg; ABV 47.8%, RRP $180, available via The Whisky Club.
- **Fourth Wave Wines** introduces the sub‑brand *Ask the Elephant* with two initial varietals (Bold Shiraz & Detailed Cabernet Sauvignon) priced at $10 each.
- **Maison Perrier‑Jouët** partners with artist Marcin Rusak for limited‑edition bottle designs of Blanc de Blancs and Belle Époque 2016, sold as a gift pack.
- **Voulez Vodka** – an ultra‑premium Geelong brand from Anther Distillery; single SKU, ABV 40%, RRP $200.

All releases target niche markets or trend‑driven consumers, emphasizing flavour intensity and premium positioning.

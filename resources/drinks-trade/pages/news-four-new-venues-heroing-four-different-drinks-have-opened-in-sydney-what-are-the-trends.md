# Four new venues in Sydney showcase key on‑premise trends
- **Razz Room** – a daiquiri bar and Odd Culture’s first CBD venue, featuring two distinct spaces (intimate upper bar & discotheque dancefloor).
- **W Sydney’s Caffè Fernet** – a rooftop amari bar partnering with Fernet‑Branca, highlighting the growing Negroni/amari niche.
- **Bar Bridge** – MUCHO’s table‑service martini venue with an eight‑cocktail menu and a new “walk‑to‑table” service system.
- **Bar Hanami** – Adam Tremont’s whisky‑centric space in Surry Hills, offering a Sakura‑inspired canopy and a basement whisky dungeon.

The article argues that successful venues now focus on *thematic simplicity*, *experiential occasions* (Instagrammable moments), and *supplier partnerships* to stand out in a crowded market.

# Voodoo Ranger Unveils Vodka RTD Range
Voodoo Ranger, a Lion craft‑beer brand, has launched its first vodka‑based ready‑to‑drink (RTD) line featuring **Hard Cola** and **Hard Lemonade**. The 375 ml cans contain 6% ABV and target Millennial male consumers seeking bold flavour without compromising value. Both SKUs are available nationwide at an RRP of $29 per 4‑pack.

> *“Cola and Lemonade are amongst the top three most popular mixers with vodka, yet only account for 2 per cent of the light RTD industry.”* – Albertus Lombard, Lion’s Brand Director – RTD

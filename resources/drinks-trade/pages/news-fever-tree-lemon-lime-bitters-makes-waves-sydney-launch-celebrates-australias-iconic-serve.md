# Fever‑Tree Lemon, Lime & Bitters Launch

- **Product**: Premium ready‑to‑drink canned version of the classic Australian bar serve.
- **Key ingredients**: Sun‑drenched Sicilian lemons, Mexican limes and Angostura Aromatic Bitters.
- **Launch event**: Sydney launch with Maroubra Surf Life Saving Club members “crowding in” the cans; media personality Richard Wilkins and his son Christian attended.
- **Format & pricing**: 250 ml cans sold in four‑packs (1 L total) at $9.95 on Coles, positioned as a lighter, sophisticated adult soft drink with 19 calories per 100 ml.
- **Market context**: Growing non‑alcoholic segment; 47 % of Australians reduced or stopped drinking alcohol in the past year.
- **Distribution**: Rolling out to independent liquor wholesalers and national retailers from April 2026.

The launch showcases Fever‑Tree’s “taste‑first” philosophy applied to a beloved Aussie classic, aiming for bar‑quality balance in a convenient format.

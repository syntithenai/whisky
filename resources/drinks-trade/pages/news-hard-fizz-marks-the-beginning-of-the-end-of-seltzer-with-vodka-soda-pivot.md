# Hard Fizz pivots from seltzer to vodka‑based RTDs
Hard Fizz, originally a seltzer brand, is shifting its core range to low‑calorie, no‑sugar vodka‑based ready‑to‑drink (RTD) beverages. The company will launch **Vodka Lime & Soda** and **Vodka Watermelon & Soda** in September 2026, each at 4 % ABV, 80 kcal per 330 mL can, and zero sugar.

The move follows a 12.7 % rise in vodka consumption and aligns with the growing trend of “better‑for‑you” alcoholic drinks—now 45 % of all vodka RTDs are marketed as such compared to 21 % in 2021.

Hard Fizz’s CEO Wade Tiller says the brand wants to stay at the forefront of this shift, and the new products will be promoted during DJ FISHER’s tour.

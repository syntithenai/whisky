# Beenleigh Distillery Wins Back‑to‑Back Tourism Awards

- **Awards**: Gold medal at the 2025 Australian Tourism Awards (Wineries, Distilleries & Breweries) and top honours at the 2025 Queensland Tourism Industry Council Tourism Awards.
- **Recognition**: Highlights Beenleigh as Australia’s oldest operating rum producer and a premier on‑premise experience.
- **Visitor Experience**: Offers immersive tours, tastings, masterclasses, a distillery restaurant and an artisan rum bar.
- **Ownership**: Vok Beverages acquired the brand in 2012 and has reconnected it with the distillery.
- **Historical Milestone**: Celebrated its 140th anniversary in 2024.

The article underscores Beenleigh’s contribution to Queensland tourism and the broader Australian spirits sector.

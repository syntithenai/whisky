# Crystal Hops 11 lb
- **Type**: Hop pellets (T‑90) – *Crystal* variety.
- **Origin**: Oregon, USA; bred in 1983 as a domestic alternative to Hallertau Mittelfrüh.
- **Flavor profile**: Mild, sweet, delicate floral with subtle noble spices (cinnamon, black pepper); higher doses or dry‑hopping can reveal citrus peel and dried lemongrass notes.
- **Breeding background**: Half‑sister to Liberty and Mount Hood; stock includes German Hallertau, Cascade, Brewers Gold, Early Green.
- **Ideal uses**: Continental lagers, wheat styles, Belgian ales; also used in IPA hop loads by some craft brewers.
- **Botanical classification**: Family Cannabaceae, Genus *Humulus*, Species *Lupulus*.
- **Yield**: 1 200–1 500 lb/acre (middle maturity).

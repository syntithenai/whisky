# Rahr White Wheat Malt (55 lb)

- **Product**: Pale wheat malt from North American soft winter white wheat.
- **Flavor profile**: Sweet malty with a hint of fruitiness.
- **Functional benefits**: Improves head retention, fuller body, and haze in Belgian‑style beers.
- **Protein content**: High protein contributes to body and head formation.
- **Milling characteristics**: Easier to mill than red wheat.
- **Allergen**: Wheat, gluten.

Suitable for all wheat‑style beers. See also Rahr Red Wheat and Crisp Wheat Malt.

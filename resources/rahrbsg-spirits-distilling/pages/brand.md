# Trusted Suppliers for Brewing and Winemaking Ingredients
RahrBSG partners with leading suppliers worldwide to provide high‑quality malt, hops, yeast, brewing aids, additives, and packaging solutions. The company emphasizes sustainable sourcing, renewable energy use in malting, B Corp certification, and salmon‑safe hop cultivation. Key partners include Crisp Malt (British malt), Bohemia Hops (Czech landrace varieties), DSM (enzymes & yeast), and CanCraft (can design & supply).

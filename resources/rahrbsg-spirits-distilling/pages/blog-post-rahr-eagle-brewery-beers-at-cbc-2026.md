# Rahr Eagle Brewery Beers at CBC 2026

RahrBSG showcases four in‑house beers brewed by Jake Thompson and Sean Tynan for the Craft Brewers Conference (CBC) 2026 in Philadelphia. The lineup highlights:
- **Chit Czech Pale Lager** – uses Gambrinus Chit Malt to boost foam, body and a subtle malt character.
- **Simpsons Slumlord** – an English‑style bitter featuring Golden Promise malt, crystal and Munich malts, fermented with SafAle US‑05.
- **West Coast Pils (two versions)** – side‑by‑side fermentation with Fermentis SafLager SH‑45 (thiol‑rich) vs. W‑34/70 to compare hop aroma and fruitiness.
- **Thiolized Hoppy Lager** – a higher‑ABV dry‑hopped lager that further showcases the SH‑45 yeast’s thiol release.

The article explains ingredient choices, fermentation strategies, sensory results, and invites visitors to taste at Booth 1603.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Chit Malt improves head retention without adding strong flavor.
- Golden Promise malt is highlighted in a low‑ABV English‑style bitter.
- Fermentis SafLager SH‑45 releases thiols, producing tropical fruit notes.
- Side‑by‑side trials demonstrate subtle differences between yeast strains.

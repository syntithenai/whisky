# Four Core Rahr Malts for Every Brewer

RahrBSG introduces **Rahr To Thee! Pils™**, a new base malt made in Alix, Alberta from Canadian barley with a Kolbach Index of 37‑44. It offers low color and modification suitable for pilsners and other lagers, delivering grain, dough, golden bread, sweet cereal, and nutty notes.

The company also rebranded its existing core malts:
- **Brewer’s Standard™ 2‑Row** – mild sweetness, moderate protein, clean neutral flavor.
- **Brewer’s Standard™ Pale Ale** – deeper color/flavor, high extract yield, strong malt character for ales.
- **Rahr North Star Pils™** – domestic pilsner with honey/sweet bread tones and hay/nutty aroma.

All four malts maintain the same specifications; only branding changed. Contact RahrBSG to order.

# Crisp Malt Grain Purchase Terms (2023‑24)

- **Scope**: Conditions for buying grain from suppliers for the 2023/24 season.
- **Purchase**: Orders are offers; acceptance is by written confirmation or act of delivery. Prices, tonnage, variety and quality parameters must be specified.
- **Delivery**: Must occur within agreed period, at Crisp Malt sites during normal hours, with minimum load size (18 mt) and proper documentation. Title passes on completion unless paid earlier.
- **Quality & Analysis**: Grain is sampled per ISO 13690:1999 and tested for moisture, nitrogen, grain size, mycotoxins, germination, admixture, ergot, infestation, glass, allergens, organic status, etc. Deviations trigger allowances or rejection.
- **Tolerance & Claims**: ±5 % (or 15 t) weight tolerance; surplus accepted at contract or market price; shortfall must be replaced.
- **Payment**: Fixed price per order, paid fortnightly after delivery, subject to grain conformity. Crisp Malt may deduct a fixed handling fee (£10/tonne).
- **Health & Safety**: Strict no‑smoking, visitor safety gear, vehicle requirements, and prohibition of nuts on site.

These terms govern the entire supply chain from order to payment, ensuring quality control and traceability for malt production.

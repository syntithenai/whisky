# All About Milling Webinar

Crisp Malt hosted a webinar on **26 March 2020** titled *All About Milling*. The session, led by Mike Benson, covered technical aspects of milling for breweries and distilleries. Topics included:
- Optimising milling for mash tuns
- Shelf‑life comparison of crushed vs whole malt
- Launder tun vs mash tun grits
- Different mill types used at Crisp Malt

Slides are available as a PDF download.

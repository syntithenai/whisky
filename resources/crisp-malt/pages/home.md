# Crisp Malt
Crisp Malt is a British malting company founded in 1870 that supplies malted and non‑malted barley products to brewers and distillers worldwide. They operate sites in England and Scotland, offering a range of base, specialty, Scottish, heritage, roasted, crystal & cara, and distilling malts—including the exclusive Chevallier Heritage Malt. The company emphasizes traditional and modern malting techniques, technical support for brewing and distillation processes, and sustainability initiatives such as Field Forward.

Key points:
- Head office: Great Ryburgh, Norfolk.
- Services include malt analysis, recipe development, mash tun troubleshooting, and washback advice.
- Offers webinars, articles, and site visits to share best practices.
- Sustainability hub with ESG reporting and community initiatives.

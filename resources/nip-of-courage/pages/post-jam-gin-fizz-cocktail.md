# Jam Gin Fizz
A fruit‑forward gin cocktail that blends classic gin fizz elements with a splash of jam (blueberry used in the recipe). The drink omits sugar syrup and egg white, relying on the jam for sweetness and body. It is shaken with ice, double‑strained into a coupe, then topped with soda water and garnished with lemon peel.

**Key ingredients**
- 45 ml gin (Stone Pine Orange Blossom Gin recommended)
- 1 tbsp blueberry jam
- 15 ml fresh lemon juice
- Soda water to top up

The recipe can be adapted for a longer drink by using more soda or building it directly in the glass.

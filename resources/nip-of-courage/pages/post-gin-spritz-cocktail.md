# Aussie Gin Spritz

A bright, citrus‑laden cocktail that blends **Archie Rose Signature Dry Gin** (or any quality Australian craft gin), **Rhubi Mistelle**, and **Australian Prosecco** topped with soda water. Serve over ice in a white wine glass, garnish with a lemon slice.

- 60 ml gin
- 20 ml Rhubi Mistelle
- 90 ml Prosecco (NV by Lisa McGuigan)
- 10 ml soda water

The recipe highlights how the juniper‑based aperitif pairs with craft gins, offering a modern take on the classic spritz.

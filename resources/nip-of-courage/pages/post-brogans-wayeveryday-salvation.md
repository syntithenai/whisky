# Brogan’s Way Everyday Salvation Gin

A taste‑test review from *Nip of Courage* highlights the family‑run Richmond, VIC distillery that launched its first gin in 2018. The **Everyday Salvation** 700 mL, 42% ABV is built around Australian botanicals such as strawberry gum, pepper berry, roasted wattle seed, white grapefruit, coriander seeds, lemon, lavender, rose, orris, angelica root and juniper. The review notes a complex flavour profile that shifts between chilled and room‑temperature servings, with spice, earthy juniper, citrus, pepperiness, juiciness and a creamy finish. It is described as versatile for G&T, martini, negroni and other cocktails.

> *“The smooth almost creaminess of this gin lets its layers melt on the tongue while relaxed herbaceous notes take over the finish as the gin warms up in your mouth.”*

The article also mentions the distillery’s support for women in distilling and provides a purchase link to the product page.

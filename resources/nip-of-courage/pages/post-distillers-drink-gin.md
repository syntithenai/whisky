# How Do Aussie Craft Distillers Drink Gin?

The article interviews four Australian gin distillers—Chris Cameron (Naught), Ally Ayres (Karu), Ian Glen (Stone Pine) and Ed Svehla (Banks & Solander)—to learn how they enjoy their own gins. Each distiller shares a preferred cocktail or simple G&T style, highlighting the versatility of their products.

- **Naught**: Loves the 2022 Sangiovese Gin in a *Charlie Chaplin*.
- **Karu**: Prefers Lightning Gin for G&T with pineapple and chilli; Affinity Gin is great for cocktails.
- **Stone Pine**: Sticks to a classic London‑dry gin & tonic with lime.
- **Banks & Solander**: Enjoys Signature Gin in a martini or G&T, and mixes it with Limoncello + soda.

The piece also links to each distillery’s product pages and broader reviews.

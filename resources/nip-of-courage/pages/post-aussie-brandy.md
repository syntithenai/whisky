# Australian Brandy – The Next Big Craft Spirit

Australian brandy is gaining popularity as a smooth, sweet spirit that can be enjoyed neat or in cocktails such as the Brandy Alexander and Sidecar. Historically rooted in European distillation practices, modern Aussie craft distilleries—especially those in wine regions—produce high‑quality brandies aged at least two years in oak barrels.

## Key Distillery Highlights
- **23rd Street Distillery – Prime 5 Brandy** (South Australia) – 5‑year average age, dark bronze colour, 40 % ABV.
- **Ironhouse Distillery – Strange Omen Brandy** (Tasmania) – American oak, vanilla‑buttery profile with ginger and spice notes, 48 % ABV.
- **Bass & Flinders Distillery – Ochre Fine Aged Brandy** (Victoria) – floral apple aromas, creamy vanilla, 41 % ABV.

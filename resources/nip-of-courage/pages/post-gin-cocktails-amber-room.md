# Gin Cocktails – Aussie Amber Room

The article explains how to make the **Aussie Amber Room** cocktail, a modern take on an older gin‑based drink. It lists ingredients, proportions, and a step‑by‑step method, then recommends specific Australian gins and vermouths that work best with the recipe.

## Key points
- Two versions of the original Amber Room exist; this article focuses on the 2007 adaptation by Stephan Berg.
- Ingredients: 20 ml Aussie craft gin, 15 ml red vermouth, 5 ml dry vermouth, 7 ml elderflower liqueur, 2 dashes orange bitters.
- Stir over ice and strain into a coupe; garnish with lemon peel.
- Recommended gins: **Stone Pine Orange Blossom**, **Naught Australian Dry**, **Seven Seasons Green Ant**.
- Recommended vermouths: **Full Circle Rose**, **78 Degrees Rosso**, **78 Degrees Dry**.
- Elderflower liqueur used: St Germain (non‑Australian).

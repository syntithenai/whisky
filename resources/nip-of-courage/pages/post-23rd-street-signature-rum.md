# 23rd Street Distillery’s Signature Rum

- **Origin**: Renmark, South Australia – established in 2016 on a historic site that once housed wineries.
- **Distillation**: Double‑distilled copper pot stills (same vintage as the buildings). Aged at cask strength in ex‑bourbon barrels, then re‑aged to bottle strength.
- **Appearance & Packaging**: Gold rum, cork‑topped, heavy‑bottomed bottle with modern artwork by Joshua Searson.
- **Flavor Profile**: Herbaceous and fruity nose with caramel, vanilla, lavender, and soft sugar. Mild initial bite followed by a rich, thick mouthfeel, spiciness, stoned fruit, slight woody smokiness, and balanced acidity.
- **Serving Suggestions**: Sip neat or with water/ice; works well in rum‑based cocktails such as an Old Fashioned or a Rum Negroni.

> *“A very moreish rum with plenty going on.”* – Taste Test review by Jimmy & Christina Butcher (Mr & Mrs Romance, Apr 26 2021).

# How to Make a French 75 (Actually an Aussie 75) Cocktail

A quick guide to crafting the classic French 75 using Australian craft gin, lemon juice, simple syrup and sparkling wine. The article explains the drink’s wartime origin, offers a step‑by‑step recipe, discusses glassware options, and includes a homemade simple‑syrup tip.

---

**Key points**
- 30 ml Poor Toms Sydney Dry Gin
- 20 ml lemon juice
- 10 ml simple syrup
- Top with Australian sparkling wine
- Garnish with a lemon twist
- Double‑strain to remove lemon pulp
- Optional: swap sparkling wine for soda water to make a Tom Collins.

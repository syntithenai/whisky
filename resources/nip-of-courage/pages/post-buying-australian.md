# What Happens When You Buy Aussie Craft Spirits

- Australia consumes ~38,000 L of spirits per year, but <1% is locally‑made craft.
- Buying a bottle connects you directly to the distiller—often a single‑person operation in rural or remote areas.
- Distillers use local grains and botanicals, sometimes foraged themselves, and often age in barrels sourced from nearby wineries or cooperages.
- The money spent supports jobs, small communities, and the growth of Australia’s young spirits industry.
- Every dram you drink echoes back to the distillery, giving a personal provenance that most other consumer goods lack.

> *Based on government statistics from 2017‑18 fiscal year.*

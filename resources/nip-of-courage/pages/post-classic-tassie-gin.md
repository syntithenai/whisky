# Taste Test: Forty Spotted’s Classic Tassie Gin

A review of the Classic Tassie Gin from Tasmania‑based distillery Forty Spotted, covering its branding, botanicals, tasting profile and cocktail uses.

- **Distillery** – Forty Spotted, founded by Bill Lark in 2015.
- **Bottle design** – Inverted glass with copper label, embossed map of Tasmania and a cap that doubles as the base.
- **Key botanicals** – Native Tasmanian pepper berry, fresh makrut lime leaves, lemon peel (plus unspecified herbs/spices).
- **Taste notes** – Strawberry‑sweet nose, peppery spice, herbal makrut, eucalyptus menthol; oily mouthfeel and long finish with menthol pop.
- **Cocktails** – Works well in martinis, Martinez, gin fizz.

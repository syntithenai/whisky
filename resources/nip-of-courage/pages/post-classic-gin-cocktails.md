# Classic Gin Cocktails

This article explains how to make three iconic gin cocktails—**Martini**, **Negroni**, and **Gimlet**—using Australian craft gins. It highlights suitable distillery products, ingredient ratios, optional variations, and garnish ideas.

- **Gin Martini**: 75 ml Four Pillars Olive Leaf Gin + 20 ml dry vermouth; garnish with olives or a lemon twist.
- **Negroni**: 30 ml Anther Goddess Strength Gin + 30 ml sweet vermouth + 30 ml bitter orange (Applewood Okar Island Bitter); serve over ice with an orange slice.
- **Gimlet**: 60 ml Stone Pine Original Dry Gin + 30 ml fresh lime juice + 20 ml simple syrup; garnish with a lime wheel or native flower.

The piece also suggests alternative ingredients and variations for each cocktail.

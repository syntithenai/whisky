# 3 Citrus‑Forward Aussie Craft Gins

- **Anther Goddess Strength Gin** – 58 % ABV, 20 botanicals including lemon‑scented gum, finger limes and grains of paradise; smooth yet bright citrus profile.
- **Forty Spotted Classic Tassie Gin** – crafted by Bill Lark; unique upside‑down bottle; flavour notes of spice, pepper, strawberry, eucalyptus menthol; creamy texture suitable for martinis.
- **Seven Seasons Green Ant Gin** – uses real green bush ants for lime flavour plus native botanicals (boobialla juniper, strawberry gum, lemon myrtle, pepper berry); owned by Daniel Motlop, former AFL player and Larrakia elder.

All three are highlighted as top citrus‑forward options from Australian craft distilleries.

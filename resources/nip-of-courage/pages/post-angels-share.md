# Distilling the Angels’ Share

The article explains why distillers lose a portion of their spirit to evaporation and absorption in wooden barrels – the so‑called *Angels’ Share*. It highlights that Australian climates (high heat and humidity) cause a larger loss (6–10 % per year) compared with cooler regions like Scotland (2–7 %). Over typical ageing periods, up to 20 % of the spirit can be lost. The piece encourages readers to appreciate the remaining product while acknowledging the inevitable “angelic” loss.

---

## Key Takeaways
- **Angels’ Share**: evaporation/absorption during barrel aging.
- **Loss rates**: 2–7 % in cooler climates; 6–10 % in Australia.
- **Impact**: up to 20 % of spirit can be lost over standard ageing periods.
- **Cultural note**: The article invites readers to toast the spirit that has ascended with the angels.

# An Intro to UK Barley

Simpsons Malt explains how barley is grown in the UK, covering the role of their agricultural merchanting division (McCreath Simpson & Prentice), the Spring Barley Variety Trials at Beal Farm, and key varieties such as Golden Promise, Laureate, LG Diablo, SY Tungsten and Cadiz. The article highlights climatic challenges—wet winters and dry springs—and discusses how maritime climate influences barley cultivation.

The page also includes a 20‑minute video featuring Vice Chairman Richard Simpson, Seed Director Paul Huntley and Agrochemical & Technical Director David Cairns, who discuss variety selection, the AHDB Recommended List, and the future of barley varieties.

# Simpsons Malt Achieves 100% FSA Gold for Malting Barley
Simpsons Malt Limited, a fifth‑generation independent malting and merchanting business and Certified B Corporation, has completed the SAI Platform’s Farm Sustainability Assessment (FSA) third‑party verification. All 678 UK growers contracted through McCreath Simpson & Prentice now form a single Farm Management Group (FMG) that achieved Gold performance across the entire malting barley supply chain.

Key points:
- First‑class sustainability standards in soil, nutrient management, crop protection, biodiversity, community impact and employee welfare.
- Verification covers barley processed at Berwick‑upon‑Tweed (Northumberland) and Tivetshall St Margaret (Norfolk).
- Mirrors the 2024 Gold achievement for distilling wheat, giving Simpsons Malt a fully verified UK grain supply.
- Provides confidence to brewers and distillers that raw materials meet high sustainability criteria.

Quotes:
> *Ben Gothorp, Sustainability Manager*: "Every tonne of malting barley we purchase is now verified to the highest FSA standard…"
> *Jim Macfarlane, Thornhill Farming Partnership*: "FSA looks at the whole farm business and recognises practical steps farmers take to improve sustainability."

This milestone sets a new benchmark for UK grain sourcing in the whisky and brewing industries.

# Sustainable barley: What 100% FSA Gold means for brewers and distillers

Simpsons Malt reports that **678 contracted malting‑barley growers** have achieved the highest *Farm Sustainability Assessment* (FSA) Gold level, covering supply to both Tweed Valley and Tivetshall maltings. The achievement gives brewers and distillers confidence that their grains are sustainably sourced under a globally recognised framework.

Key points:
1. **Robust sourcing** – 100 % Gold across the entire barley base provides rare assurance in UK cereal chains, enabling clear sustainability messaging.
2. **Strong relationships** – Long‑standing ties via MSP and dedicated Sustainability Officers (Jessica Douglas & Eugene Stokes) helped growers navigate audits.
3. **Future goals** – The milestone is part of a 2030 carbon‑neutral target, with initiatives such as low‑carbon fertilisers, nutrient use efficiency, cover crops and the xarvio Field Manager platform.

The article also highlights related achievements (distilling wheat Gold in 2024) and links to case studies on FSA, Growers Groups and xarvio.

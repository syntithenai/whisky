# Simpsons Malt UK Product Range (Sept 2025)

Simpsons Malt, founded in 1862 and a certified B‑Corp, offers a wide spectrum of whole malt products for brewing. The range includes base malts (Golden Promise, Maris Otter), highly kilned malts (Vienna, Munich, Imperial), crystal malts (CaraGlow, Caramalt, various dark crystals), roasted malts (Amber, Brown, Chocolate, Black), special malts (wheat, oats, rye, GNO™), and unmalted adjuncts (flaked barley, wheat, oats). Each product lists colour (°EBC), moisture max, and key characteristics such as flavour notes or intended beer styles.

The PDF also highlights the company’s heritage, sustainable sourcing via MSP, in‑house crystal and roasted malt production at Tivetshall Maltings, and contact details for orders.

## Key Facts
- Founded 1862; fifth generation family business.
- Certified B‑Corp.
- In‑house crystal & roasted malt manufacturing.
- Uses heritage barley varieties (Golden Promise, Maris Otter).
- MSP contracts direct with growers.
- Products cover colour ranges from <3.5 to >250 °EBC.

# Thinking of becoming a B Corp? We've got some advice…

Simpsons Malt, a certified B Corporation since 2021, shares its experience and practical guidance for brewers and distillers considering B‑Corp certification.

* **Key steps** – Start with the B Impact Assessment to gauge readiness; secure senior leadership buy‑in; allocate dedicated resources across departments.
* **Resource demands** – Not a side project: requires full‑time commitment, structural changes (Articles of Association), and ongoing reporting every three years.
* **Scope** – The assessment covers governance, people, suppliers, community, and environment, not just environmental performance.
* **Benefits** – Provides a structured improvement framework, accountability, and long‑term resilience; commercial gains are secondary.

For more details, see the Sustainability Report (2025) linked on the page.

# Simpsons Malt contracted farmers achieve 100% FSA Gold

- URL: https://www.simpsonsmalt.co.uk/latest/news/simpsons-malt-limited-contracted-farmers-achieve-100-fsa-gold-for-distilling-wheat/
- Captured: 2026-04-19T15:36:59.242968+00:00
- Keywords: ballantine’s, biodiversity, biodiversity in farming, chivas brothers, contracted farmers, distilling wheat, farm sustainability assessment, fsa gold, gluten free whisky, growers group, kilning, maltings, scotch whisky supply chain, simpsons malt, soil management, strathclyde growers group, sustainability, sustainability certification

**Simpsons Malt Limited** has helped over 120 contracted farmers achieve the UK’s first **FSA Gold** certification for distilling wheat, a milestone that confirms sustainable farming practices across soil management, biodiversity and employee welfare.

The award is part of the Chivas Brothers Strathclyde Growers Group, which supplies about 80 000 t/yr of wheat to the Glasgow‑based Strathclyde Distillery. The group also runs a barley growers network (Chivas Brothers Miltonduff Growers Group) covering more than 180 farms.

Key quotes:
- *Ben Gothorp*: “We’re delighted to see the Growers Group attain FSA Gold – a first for distilling wheat in the UK.”
- *Ronald Daalmans*: “Knowing our farmers meet the highest standards is crucial for sustainable whisky.”

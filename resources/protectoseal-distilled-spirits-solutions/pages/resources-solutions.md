# Protectoseal Industrial Solutions
Protectoseal offers a broad portfolio of vapor and flame control products for the distilled spirits, biogas, oil & gas, chemical, pharmaceutical, fuel distribution, and winery sectors. The site highlights:
- **Flame arresters** (end‑of‑line, in‑line, concentric, eccentric)
- **Pressure & vacuum relief vents** (ERV, thief hatch, free‑lifting gauge, lock‑down gauge)
- **Conservation vents** and **tank blanketing valves**
- **Ancillary equipment** such as liquid level monitors, internal safety valves, and fuel‑tank safety caps
- Technical resources: case studies, videos, downloadable product data sheets, industry regulations, and a global rep locator.
The company emphasizes 95+ years of experience, custom solutions for unique challenges, and a focus on safety and emissions control in tank venting systems.

# About The Spirits Business
The Spirits Business (SB) is the world’s only international trade magazine and website dedicated to the global spirits industry. With a circulation of over 50 000 professionals, it covers news, analysis, interviews, brand updates and investigative pieces across on‑trade, off‑trade, bartending and events.

## Key Features
- **Content**: Emerging market trends, category analysis, bartender innovations, city bar guides.
- **Events**: Global Spirits Masters Competitions (blind‑tasting), SB Awards, cocktail competitions.
- **Media Pack**: 2026 media pack available for download.
- **Team**: Chairman Anthony Hawser; Editorial Editor‑in‑Chief Melita Kiely; Deputy Editor Nicola Carruthers; Digital Editor Georgie Collins; Bars Editor Lauren Bowes; and others.

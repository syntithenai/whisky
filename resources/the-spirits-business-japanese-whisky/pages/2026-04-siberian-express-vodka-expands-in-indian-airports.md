**Siberian Express vodka expands in Indian travel retail**

- Solex Group launches its Kazakh super‑premium vodka, *Siberian Express*, into key Indian airports and seaports.
- New locations include Ahmedabad, Jaipur, Lucknow, Mangalore airports and Veraval & Mundra seaports.
- The brand debuted globally in late 2024; now present in Europe, UAE and major trade shows (Malt Live Dubai 2025, Gulf Bar Show, ProWein 2026).
- Production: distilled from premium wheat and malt cultures, blended with Siberian water; each batch is blended with previous ones for consistency.
- Bottle design mimics ice crystals/diamonds to convey purity.

# Waterford Distillery Enters Receivership

Waterford Distillery, founded by Mark Reynier, has closed and entered receivership after an emergency board meeting. HSBC Bank appointed receivers to take control; retail sales via the distillery’s website have been shut down. The receivers will likely try to sell the business or its assets, including maturing casks.

Reynier expressed deep regret, noting that the receivership does not affect his parent company Maverick Drinks, though Renegade Rum in Grenada has also closed. Waterford was notable for pioneering biodynamic barley farming and distilling each farm’s crop separately.

# About WhiskyCast
WhiskyCast is the world’s longest‑running whisky podcast, launched in 2005 and now in its 18th year. The weekly show delivers news, interviews with leading distillers and experts, and covers whisky from every corner of the globe—from Arctic Circle distilleries to Tasmania and South Africa. Produced by CaskStrength Media/WhiskyCast LLC, it remains editorially independent and has earned numerous awards, including the 2020 Tales of the Cocktail Spirited Award and 2023 Spirits Communicator of the Year for host Mark Gillespie.

The platform also offers video stories, live webcasts, a community discussion app, and a podcast‑app partnership with Jacob Apps (launched in 2008). WhiskyCast prides itself on fostering a professional whisky community; many listeners credit it as a springboard into the industry. The site encourages responsible drinking and is intended for adults of legal age.

# WhiskyCast Advertising & Editorial Policies

WhiskyCast publishes its advertising and editorial policies to maintain transparency with listeners and advertisers. Key points:
- **Editorial independence**: Advertisers cannot influence show content.
- **Ad limits**: Each episode hosts up to six segment partners and two presenting partners; short‑term bonus segments may be added at the producer’s discretion.
- **Contract terms**: Packages are sold annually on a first‑come, first‑served basis. Shorter contracts are secondary to longer ones, but once paid, a brand is protected from being displaced by a later buyer.
- **Production on location**: Presenting partners can sponsor episodes filmed at their facilities; travel and equipment costs are extra.
- **Advertising restrictions**: No tobacco, gambling, political or sexually explicit ads. Paid links or sponsored guest posts are prohibited.
- **Compliance & disclosure**: All advertising must meet U.S. laws and industry codes; product mentions that receive consideration must be disclosed.
- **Payment policy**: Invoices are due within 10 business days of receipt; payment is required before the partnership begins.

These policies ensure WhiskyCast remains a free, editorially independent podcast while providing clear guidelines for brand partners.

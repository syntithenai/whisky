# The Glendalough Shamrock
A cocktail recipe featuring **Glendalough Double Barrel Irish Whiskey**.

- **Ingredients**: 40 ml whiskey, mint & basil leaves, 10 ml Chartreuse (optional), 15 ml simple syrup, 20 ml lemon juice, 10 ml verbena tea.
- **Optional substitutions**: omit Chartreuse or replace verbena with peppermint/chamomile tea.
- **Method**: Muddle mint & basil with syrup, add whiskey, lemon, tea and optional Chartreuse. Shake with ice, strain over a large square cube in a ribbed tumbler, garnish with shamrocks or mint sprig.

The recipe is provided by Glendalough Distillery for St. Patrick’s Day but suitable year‑round.

# E&J Gallo to Acquire Four Roses Bourbon
E&J Gallo, a family‑owned wine and spirits company, has agreed to purchase the Lawrenceburg, Kentucky distillery and Four Roses bourbon brand from Japan’s Kirin Holdings for roughly **$764 million** (subject to U.S. antitrust approval). The deal includes an additional $50 million contingent on first‑year sales targets and is expected to close in Q2 2026.

- **Seller:** Kirin Holdings, which bought Four Roses in 2002 after Seagram’s breakup.
- **Buyer’s Spirits Division:** Spirits of Gallo (includes New Amsterdam vodka).
- **Operational Continuity:** No change anticipated; master distiller Brent Elliott will remain.
- **Strategic Context:** Kirin is refocusing on healthcare, while Gallo expands its spirits portfolio.

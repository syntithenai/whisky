# Guiding Principles
WhiskyCast outlines its editorial ethics, transparency policies, and business practices. The page explains how the site reports facts, limits advertising, discloses sponsorships and sample receipts, and maintains independence from brand influence.

- **Fact‑based reporting** with clear opinion markers.
- **Minimal advertising**; no sponsored content or editorial control by advertisers.
- **Transparent disclosures** for paid trips, embargoes, and samples.
- **No conflict of interest**: no stock in spirits companies, no auction participation.
- **Clear style guidelines** on titles such as "Dr." vs. "Ph.D.".
- **Independent editorial control** even when consulting or producing branded content elsewhere.

The principles emphasize trust, responsibility, and the integrity of whisky journalism.

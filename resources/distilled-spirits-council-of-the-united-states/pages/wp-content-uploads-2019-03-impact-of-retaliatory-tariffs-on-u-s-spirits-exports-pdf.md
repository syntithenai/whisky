# Impact of Retaliatory Tariffs on U.S. Spirits Exports
This PowerPoint presentation from the Distilled Spirits Council (DSC) outlines key data on U.S. spirits exports in 2018, highlighting growth trends and the effects of tariffs imposed by the EU, UK, and Canada on American whiskey shipments.

- **Overall U.S. spirits exports grew 9.5 % in 2018** (USD millions).
- **American whiskey accounted for $1.2 B of those exports**, with 59 % going to the EU and 67 % to the rest of the world.
- Tariffs introduced in June 2018 had a measurable impact: American whiskey exports to the EU fell by 13.4 % (Jan‑Jun) but rose 25 % (Jul‑Dec); UK shipments dropped 22 % overall; Canadian shipments saw a mixed effect (+12.5 % Jul‑Dec, –6.4 % Jan‑Jun).
- The presentation includes charts showing export volumes by state and year‑to‑date comparisons.

The data source is the U.S. International Trade Commission (USITC) Trade DataWeb 2019.

# American Drinking Preferences (Morning Consult, 2018)

- **Key Insight**: Liquor is the most popular alcoholic beverage among Americans, with 32 % of respondents preferring it over beer or wine.
- **Demographic Trends**:
  - Men favor beer; millennials lean toward liquor.
  - Income and age groups show varying preferences for beer, wine, liquor, cocktails, or no opinion.
- **Liquor Popularity**: Vodka tops the list of preferred liquors (31 % very likely to buy), followed by tequila, flavored vodka, bourbon, etc.

The presentation is a data‑driven snapshot of U.S. drinking habits compiled by Morning Consult.

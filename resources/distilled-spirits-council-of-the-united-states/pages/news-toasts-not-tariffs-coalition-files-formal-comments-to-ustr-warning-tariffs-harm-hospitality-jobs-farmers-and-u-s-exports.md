# Toasts Not Tariffs Coalition Files Formal Comments to USTR
The Toasts Not Tariffs Coalition—representing 58 industry associations—submitted formal comments to the U.S. Trade Representative (USTR) urging exemption of wine and spirits from Section 301 tariffs, fair trade with the EU/UK, new market agreements, and permanent access to Canada. The coalition highlighted economic pressures on hospitality, job losses linked to tariff threats, and the unique geographic nature of wine and spirits that prevents relocation.

## Key Points
- **Exemption**: Wine & distilled spirits from current/future tariffs.
- **Fair Trade**: Reciprocal agreements with EU/UK.
- **Canada Access**: Permanent return of U.S. products to all provinces.
- **Economic Impact**: 3.5 M jobs, $476 B annual activity; 21 % of restaurant revenue from alcohol.
- **Trade Dispute**: Canada’s ban caused a 78 % drop in wine exports and a 63 % drop in spirits exports.
- **Unique Products**: Geographic ties (e.g., Bourbon, Cognac, Tequila) make relocation impossible.

# Distilled Spirits Imports – December 2025

This PDF presents U.S. duty‑payable imports of distilled spirits by volume (proof gallons) for the month of December 2025 and a year‑to‑date comparison with December 2024. The data are broken down by spirit class, container type, and country of origin.

Key highlights:
- **Whisky**: 2 915 206 proof gallons imported in December 2025 (−28.6 % vs Dec 2024). Canada remains the largest source (1 137 441 pg) but fell 39.9 %. The U.K. and other countries also saw declines.
- **Gin**: 614 536 pg imported, a 30 % drop from December 2024.
- **Rum**: 774 539 pg imported, down 19.4 %.
- **Brandy**: 1 421 799 pg imported, a steep 57.8 % decline.
- **Vodka**: 2 494 583 pg imported, up 13.1 %.
- **Tequila**: 5 420 502 pg imported, down 28.2 %.
- **Total imports** for all classes were 234 372 632 proof gallons, a modest 2.7 % increase over the same period in 2024.

The report also lists detailed country‑by‑country figures for each class and container type (bottled vs bulk). The source is Customs Service data compiled by the Census Bureau and converted to proof gallons by DISCUS.

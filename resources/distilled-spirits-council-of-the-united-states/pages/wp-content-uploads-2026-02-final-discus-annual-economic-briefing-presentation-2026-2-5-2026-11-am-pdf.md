# Annual Economic Briefing – DISCUS (Feb 5 2026)

- **Scope**: U.S. spirits industry economic overview, market share trends, consumer sentiment, RTD growth, export/import dynamics, tariff impacts, and policy priorities.
- **Key figures**:
  - Spirits revenue declined 2.2 % YoY to $36.4 bn in 2025; volume grew 1.9 %.
  - U.S. spirits hold >42 % of alcohol industry revenue (2025).
  - RTDs, especially spirits‑based, grew ~16 % in volume and captured 19 % of the RTD market.
  - Exports rose from $478 m (2000) to >$2.4 bn (2024), but fell 9 % YoY in Q2 2025.
  - Tariffs: EU 15 %, UK 10 %; Canada & Mexico tariff‑free under USMCA; India reduced whiskey tariff from 150 % to 100 %.
- **Policy highlights**:
  - Zero‑for‑zero tariffs secured for U.S. spirits.
  - Expanded RTD sales in several states and 3rd‑party delivery in Arizona & Virginia.
  - Ongoing advocacy for permanent tariff‑free trade and fair tax treatment.

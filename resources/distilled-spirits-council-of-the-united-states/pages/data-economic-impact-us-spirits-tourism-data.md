# U.S. Spirits Tourism Data and Economic Impact
The Distilled Spirits Council of the United States (DISCU) publishes reports on the economic impact of distillery tourism in California, New York, Tennessee, and Texas. The data were produced by Tourism Economics using established research methods. The page also notes that there are over 3,100 distilleries nationwide and encourages visitors to explore state distillery trails via the Destination Distillery website.

## Key Points
- Reports available for CA, NY, TN, TX (2022).
- Tourism Economics conducted the research.
- Over 3,100 U.S. distilleries; many states have distillery trails.
- Link to Destination Distillery for trail information.

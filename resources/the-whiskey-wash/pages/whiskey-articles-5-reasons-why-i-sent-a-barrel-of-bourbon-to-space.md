# Strato Whiskey: A Barrel Sent to Space

Wyn Ferrell, owner of Mile High Spirits, explains why he launched a barrel of Fireside Bourbon into the stratosphere in July 2021. The article outlines five motivations—his love of space, pushing industry boundaries, educational research, boosting Colorado’s craft‑spirit scene, and simply having fun. The barrel reached 106,627 ft, stayed there for 94 min, and experienced temperatures as low as –94 °C, exposing the whiskey to UV radiation and extreme temperature swings that altered its flavor profile (vanilla, caramel, oak tannins). Only 250 bottles of Strato Whiskey were produced; they retail at $350 online or $300 in‑store.

Ferrell’s background: former finance professional turned distiller, co‑founder of Mile High Spirits, and a pioneer in commercial stratosphere‑aged whiskey. The piece also highlights the brand’s accolades (San Francisco World Spirits Competition) and its mission to innovate while celebrating Colorado’s growing spirits culture.

---

**Key facts**: 1 barrel launched July 15 2021 from Page, Arizona; apogee 106,627 ft; 94‑minute stay; 4 h 38 min total flight; temperatures down to –94 °C; UV exposure; flavor notes of vanilla, caramel, oak tannins; 250 bottles produced.

---

**Takeaway**: Strato Whiskey is the first commercially available whiskey aged in space, blending scientific curiosity with craft‑spirit innovation.

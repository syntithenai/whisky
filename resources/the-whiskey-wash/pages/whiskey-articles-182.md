# The Whiskey Wash – Overview

The Whiskey Wash is a UK‑based, independent whisky journalism site that publishes long‑form features, reviews and industry news. Its editorial team includes an Editor‑in‑Chief, Deputy Editor, review editors and a roster of freelance reviewers. The site hosts a searchable archive of articles covering Scotch, bourbon, Irish whiskey, Japanese whisky and more, and offers comparison tools for prices and awards.

The page in question is the main **Whiskey Articles** landing page, which lists recent feature stories, links to other content categories, and provides an overview of the editorial team and company details. No product pricing or specific whisky reviews are present on this page.

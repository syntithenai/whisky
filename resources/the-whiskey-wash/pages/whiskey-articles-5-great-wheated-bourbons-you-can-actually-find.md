# 5 Best Wheated Bourbons You Can Actually Find

The article lists five wheated bourbons that are approachable, good‑value and widely available, especially in the UK.

1. **Weller Special Reserve** – soft, honeyed, green apple & vanilla; great for cocktails.
2. **Maker’s Mark** – caramel, baking spice, creamy texture; ubiquitous and reliable.
3. **Leiper’s Fork Bottled‑in‑Bond** – toffee, orange oil, toasted oak; more structure.
4. **Ben Holladay Rickhouse Proof (Soft Red Wheat)** – higher proof with caramel brittle, baked apple, pecan & tobacco warmth.
5. **Willett Pot Still Reserve Straight Bourbon** – vanilla cream, honeyed corn, lemon oil, light spice.

The author explains the wheated mash bill’s smoother profile and recommends these bottles for their availability, consistent quality and range of proofs.

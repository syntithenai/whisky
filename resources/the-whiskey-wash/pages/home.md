# The Whiskey Wash
The Whiskey Wash is a comprehensive online resource for whiskey enthusiasts, offering price comparison tools, news, reviews, expert articles, and award‑based guides. Launched in 2013, it claims to be the world’s most‑visited whiskey website.

## Core Features
- **Price Comparison** – Users can compare prices across retailers and see potential savings.
- **News & Articles** – Regular updates on new releases, industry news, and feature stories from distilleries and experts.
- **Reviews** – In‑depth tasting notes for a wide range of whiskies (Scotch, bourbon, Irish, Indian, etc.) with reviewer names, dates, and ratings.
- **Expert Articles** – Unpaid, non‑affiliate content written by industry professionals covering production, history, and trends.
- **Awards & Guides** – Weekly deep dives into award winners from competitions such as the World Whiskies Awards, plus region‑specific guides.
- **Learning Centre** – Educational resources on cask ownership, whisky science, and buying tips.

The site also partners with independent cask specialists to offer a transparent cask programme.

# 8 Great Brandy and Cognac Cocktails

Master of Malt’s blog post by Emma Symons (19 Apr 2024) lists eight classic cocktails that use brandy or Cognac as the base spirit, from the Prohibition‑era Sidecar to the New Orleans staple Vieux Carré. Each recipe includes ingredients, proportions and a brief method.

The article explains why Cognac is a versatile cocktail ingredient—its oak ageing adds depth while its strict production rules keep quality high. It also notes that brandy can be made from other fruits but is usually grape‑based.

A short comment section follows the recipes, with readers sharing their favourite drinks and substitutions.

---

**Key takeaways:**
- Sidecar (Cognac + Cointreau + lemon)
- Brandy Alexander (brandy + chocolate liqueur + cream)
- French 75 (Cognac + simple syrup + lemon + Champagne)
- Vieux Carré (whiskey, Cognac, vermouth, bitters)
- Metropolitan (Cognac + sweet vermouth + bitters)
- Horse’s Neck (Cognac + ginger ale + bitters)

The post is a quick reference for bartenders and home mixologists.

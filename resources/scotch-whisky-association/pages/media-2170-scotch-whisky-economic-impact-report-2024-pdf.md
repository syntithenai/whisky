# Scotch Whisky Economic Impact Report 2024

- **Total GVA (UK)**: £7.1 bn in 2022, a 29% rise since 2018.
- **Scotland’s share**: £5.3 bn (75% of UK total), contributing 3 % to Scotland’s overall GVA.
- **Employment**: 66 000 jobs supported across the UK; 41 000 in Scotland, 25 000 elsewhere.
- **Export growth**: Scotch whisky exports grew 31 % from 2018‑2022 to £6.2 bn.
- **Investment**: Over £2 bn invested in CapX projects (new distilleries, warehouses, low‑carbon tech) since 2018.
- **Productivity**: Roughly £27 300 added per employee in the UK, ranking Scotch whisky among Scotland’s most productive sectors.
- **Case studies** highlight green investment at Bunnahabhain and community impact of Isle of Harris Distillery.

The report quantifies economic contributions through a methodology combining Oxford Economics/IWSR data with Scottish Government multipliers, providing a detailed regional breakdown and illustrating the industry’s resilience amid tariffs, pandemic pressures, and climate goals.

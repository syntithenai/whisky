# SWA comments on glass inclusion in Wales Deposit Return Scheme

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) criticises the Welsh Government’s decision to include glass in its Deposit Return Scheme (DRS). Chief Executive Mark Kent argues that such a move would increase regulatory burden, raise costs for producers and consumers, and reduce whisky availability in Wales. He stresses the need for consistency across the UK and cites international best practice that excludes glass from DRS.

---

## Key points
- SWA calls for a consistent, interoperable DRS across the UK.
- Inclusion of glass would raise costs and limit product availability.
- The industry has historically advocated excluding glass from DRS.
- The Welsh decision is seen as harmful to whisky consumers without clear environmental benefits.

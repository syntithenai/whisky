# SWA & DISCUS Joint Statement on UK‑US Trade

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) and the Distilled Spirits Council of the United States (DISCUS) released a joint statement on 28 July 2025. The statement, issued by Mark Kent (SWA CEO) and Chris Swonger (DISCUS CEO), welcomes President Biden’s commitment to review tariffs on Scotch whisky and calls for a permanent return to zero‑tariff trade between the UK and the US.

Key points:
- Both industries support a **zero‑tariff** arrangement.
- The statement urges the delivery of a deal that secures tariff‑free trade for both Scotch whisky and American whiskey.

# SWA comments on US tariff increase speculation

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) expressed concern over the potential rise of U.S. tariffs on Scotch whisky from **10 % to 15 %**. A spokesperson urged both governments to work toward a permanent zero‑tariff agreement and called for the UK government to reduce cost burdens, notably the recent increase in spirits duty.

---

## Key points
- Potential tariff hike: 10 % → 15 %
- SWA urges bilateral effort for zero‑tariff trade
- Calls for UK to cut crushing costs and spirits duty

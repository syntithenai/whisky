# SWA comments on whisky tariffs in China

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) welcomed the reduction of Scotch whisky import duties in China from **10 % to 5 %**. Chief Executive Mark Kent highlighted China as a key growth market and expressed gratitude to UK officials for facilitating the tariff cut, noting its potential to boost exports and improve global competitiveness.

---

## Key points
- Tariff reduction announced on 29 January 2026.
- SWA sees this as an opportunity to energise Scotch whisky sales in China.
- The move is part of broader efforts to enhance the industry’s international competitiveness.

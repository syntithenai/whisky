# Scottish DRS can go ahead without glass
The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) welcomes the UK government’s proposal to exempt Scotland from the UK Internal Market Act if the Scottish Deposit Return Scheme (DRS) excludes glass. The SWA argues that removing glass will simplify operations, reduce costs for businesses and consumers, and support a circular economy while maintaining high levels of glass recycling in Scotland.

## Key Points
- Exemption would allow DRS to operate from March next year.
- Scotch Whisky industry has long requested glass exclusion.
- Aligning with other UK regions reduces complexity.
- Supports sustainability objectives by reducing waste and litter.

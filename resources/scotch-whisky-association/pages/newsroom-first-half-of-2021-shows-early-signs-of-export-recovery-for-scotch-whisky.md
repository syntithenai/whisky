# Early Signs of Export Recovery for Scotch Whisky

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) released first‑half 2021 export figures showing a **31 % rise in value** and **42 % increase in volume** compared with the same period last year. However, exports remain **10 % below 2019 levels** and **34 % lower than 2019 in the US** after tariffs were lifted. Growth is strongest in Asia (China surpassing £1 m) and the EU shows mixed results due to post‑Brexit arrangements. The SWA urges government support, including a review of alcohol duty and tariff reductions for key markets such as India.

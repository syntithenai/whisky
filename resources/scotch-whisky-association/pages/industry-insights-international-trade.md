# Scotch Whisky: An Internationally Traded Spirit
Scotch whisky remains the world’s top exported spirit, worth **£5.3 bn** in 2025 and shipping **1.34 bn bottles** (70 cl @40% ABV) to 163 markets. Export volumes fell in key regions such as the US (‑15 % volume, ‑7 % value) due to a new tariff, while Asia‑Pacific saw an 8.3 % drop in value but stable volume. The EU remains the largest regional market by volume and value, with France and Germany as leading destinations.

Key points:
- **US**: £971 m (2024) → £933 m (2025), 132 m bottles → 120 m bottles.
- **Asia‑Pacific**: India remains the biggest by volume; China’s tariff cut to 5 % may boost future trade.
- **EU/Europe**: £1.5 bn exports in 2025, down 1.8 % from 2024.
- Trade talks: UK‑India FTA and UK‑Korea FTA expected to improve market access.

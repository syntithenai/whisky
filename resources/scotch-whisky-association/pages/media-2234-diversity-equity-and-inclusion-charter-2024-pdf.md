# Scotch Whisky Association Diversity, Equity & Inclusion Charter (2024)

The **Scotch Whisky Association (SWA)** refreshed its Diversity and Inclusivity Charter in 2024 to align with the industry’s 2023‑2025 strategy. The charter encourages member companies—ranging from multinational producers to small family distillers—to sign up, develop measurable action plans, and report progress. Key commitments include appointing a senior executive responsible for DEI, creating transparent action plans, participating in the SWA Skills & Inclusion working group, and fostering inclusive workplace policies.

The document outlines practical actions such as collecting diversity data, conducting pay audits, implementing anti‑discrimination policies, reviewing recruitment processes, offering bias training, ensuring web accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA), engaging in external benchmarking schemes, and promoting inclusive marketing.

> *“Greater diversity, equity and inclusion helps us to attract a more diverse talent pool, retain and engage a high‑performing workforce, boost performance, innovate, better reflect communities, and respond to consumers.”*

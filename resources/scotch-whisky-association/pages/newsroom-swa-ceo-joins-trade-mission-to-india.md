**Scotch Whisky Association CEO joins trade mission to India**

Mark Kent, Chief Executive of the Scotch Whisky Association (SWA), announced that SWA will participate in a UK‑India trade mission following the UK‑India Free Trade Agreement (FTA). The FTA is expected to liberalise tariffs on all Scotch whisky exports to India, opening access to the world’s largest whisky market and providing greater choice for Indian consumers. Kent highlighted the government‑industry partnership as a model for future strategic opportunities and expressed hope that similar collaboration will help the industry navigate current headwinds.

The announcement was accompanied by a release from the Scotland Office detailing the trade deal’s benefits for Scotch whisky.

# 2025 Scotch Whisky Export Update (HMRC March)
- **Global market share**: EU 27.5 %, North America 21.5 %, Asia‑Pacific 27.2 %, Europe excl. EU 6.3 %. 
- **Export value**: £1.57 bn in 2024, projected £1.57 bn for 2025 (no change). Total export volume 1.3 bn 70 cl bottles (~1.3 billion). 
- **Top markets by value**: UAE (£155 m), US (£933 m), France (£404 m), India (£286 m), Singapore (£274 m). 
- **Top markets by volume**: UAE (15 % share), US (7 %), France (5.8 %), India (14 %), Singapore (3.3 %). 
- **Export value trend 2006‑2025**: Peaks at £4.23 bn in 2011, dips to £3.80 bn in 2019, rises again to £6.2 bn in 2021, then stabilises around £5.3‑£5.6 bn from 2022‑2024.
- **Key commentary**: Mark Kent, Chief Executive of the Scotch Whisky Association (SWA), notes that while form is temporary, class is permanent and stresses the industry’s resilience and potential for growth.

> *Scotch whisky is an iconic product with global appeal; the UK and Scottish governments can support continued growth.*

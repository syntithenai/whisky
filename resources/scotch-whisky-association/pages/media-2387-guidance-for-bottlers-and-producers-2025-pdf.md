# Guidance for Bottlers and Producers (2025)

This PDF from the Scotch Whisky Association provides practical guidance on how producers and bottlers can comply with the **Scotch Whisky Regulations 2009**. It covers:
- Production rules, including mandatory maturation in Scotland.
- Definitions of whisky categories (Single Malt, Single Grain, Blended, etc.).
- Restrictions on using distillery names and localities.
- Labelling requirements: category description, age statements, distillation years, and prohibition of “Pure Malt”.
- Export rules for bulk and bottled Scotch Whisky.
- Verification by HMRC and enforcement provisions.

The document includes checklists for labelling, age statements, and practical examples to help brands avoid regulatory breaches.

# Sustainability in the Scotch Whisky Industry
The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) outlines its long‑term sustainability agenda, building on an initial environmental strategy from 2009 and a comprehensive sustainability strategy launched in 2021.
- **Net‑Zero goal**: Own operations to be net‑zero by 2040; full Scope 1–3 emissions net‑zero by 2045.
- **Key focus areas**: Climate change, circular economy (packaging), responsible water use, peatland and cereal supply chain stewardship, health & safety improvements.
- **Data & collaboration**: Emissions inventory based on the Greenhouse Gas Protocol; partnership with regulators, innovators, and other industries.
- **Case studies**: The site links to real‑world examples of sustainable practices across Scotland.
- **Recent news**: Updates on EPR fees, water stewardship framework launch (2023), and industry responses to policy changes.

The page serves as a hub for detailed reports, PDFs, and further reading on each sustainability pillar.

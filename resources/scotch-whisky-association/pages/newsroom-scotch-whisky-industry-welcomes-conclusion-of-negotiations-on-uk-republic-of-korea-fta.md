**Scotch Whisky industry welcomes conclusion of negotiations on UK‑Republic of Korea FTA**

The Scotch Whisky Association (SWA) announced that the UK and South Korea have finalized a free trade agreement, improving market access for Scotch whisky—particularly single malts—in South Korea. The deal is expected to boost exports, support UK jobs, and enhance supply chain efficiency.

*Key points:* 1️⃣ Reduction of trade barriers in South Korea. 2️⃣ Greater choice for consumers. 3️⃣ SWA will continue engaging with the government to protect the category.

**Scottish Apprenticeship Week 2022 – Scotch Whisky Association**

The article highlights the impact of apprenticeship programmes across the Scotch whisky industry, featuring personal stories from apprentices at Glenmorangie, Inver House Distillers, Edrington and The Macallan. It underscores how apprenticeships open career pathways, foster skill development in production, logistics, quality control and finance, and promote diversity and inclusion within the sector.

Key takeaways:
- Apprentices gain practical experience while earning qualifications.
- They contribute to workforce growth and productivity.
- Participants express enthusiasm for learning from peers and industry leaders.
- The week is celebrated daily with events and open evenings.

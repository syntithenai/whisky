# Events: In‑Person

The James B. Beam Institute for Kentucky Spirits lists two upcoming in‑person events:
- **Distillery Safety Series: Strengthening EHS Compliance, Identifying Risk, and Driving Effective Corrective Action** – May 20 2026 (9 am–2 pm) at the Institute.

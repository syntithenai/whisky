# James B. Beam Institute Industry Conference

The University of Kentucky’s **James B. Beam Institute for Kentucky Spirits** is hosting its annual industry conference on **March 15‑17, 2027** at the Bill Gatton Student Center (160 Avenue of Champions, Lexington, KY). The event will bring global thought leaders to discuss innovation, technical research, marketing, tourism, workforce education and corporate citizenship in the spirits sector. Participants can network and build partnerships that support sustainable growth for Kentucky’s signature industry.

For sponsorship inquiries contact **ilka.balk@uky.edu**; general questions go to Associate Director Ilka Balk.

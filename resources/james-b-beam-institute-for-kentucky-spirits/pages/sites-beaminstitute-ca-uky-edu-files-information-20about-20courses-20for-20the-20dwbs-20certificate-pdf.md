# DWBS Certificate Course Overview

- **Credit requirement**: 12 hours (HRT 335 + one of AEN 341, CHE/A&S 306 or PLS 389 + six electives).
- **Core courses**:
  - *HRT 335* – Distillation, Wine & Brewing Science (DL) – 3 credits.
  - *AEN 341*, *CHE/A&S 306*, or *PLS 389* – one of these as the required elective – 3 credits each.
- **Electives**: Six credit hours from approved lists, including
  - *HRT 336* (Viticulture), *HRT 337* (Enology),
  - *HMT 420* (Tourism Principles), *EGR 380* (Bourbon Production Engineering),
  - *WRD 225/569* (Writing & Oral History), and several special‑topic courses such as *AFE 300* (Commercializing Spirits) and *AFE 300* (Sensory Science).
- **Delivery**: Courses offered in person, online, or hybrid; schedules vary by semester.
- **Prerequisites**: Mostly none for core courses; age 21+ required for most electives.

# Workforce Training Standards Project
The James B. Beam Institute and the Society of Spirits are developing voluntary, stakeholder‑driven standards for training credentials in the American spirits industry. The goal is to unify safety, quality, and a shared lexicon across diverse educational offerings. Stakeholders—including workers, employers, consultants, and educators—are invited to provide input via an anonymous survey or by contacting the Education Coordinator.

## Key Points
- **Purpose**: Create clear learning outcomes tied to knowledge and competencies.
- **Scope**: Voluntary standard; not a regulation.
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Workers (operations, QC, supply chain, HR), employers, consultants, educators.
- **Action**: Respond to the survey or email k.b@uky.edu for comments.

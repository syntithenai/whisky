# Ethyl Alcohol – Grain Processing Corporation

Grain Processing Corporation (GPC) has been producing high‑purity ethyl alcohol from 100 % corn for over 80 years. Their product is gluten‑free, kosher and Non‑GMO Project Verified, meeting or exceeding USP/FCC specifications with benzene‑free, <1 ppm total impurities. It is used worldwide in food & beverage, personal care, pharmaceutical and industrial applications, including specially denatured alcohol (SDA) for 190‑/200‑proof uses.

Key points:
- Two Midwest manufacturing sites
- FDA Drug Master File registered
- TTB‑certified lab with ASTM testing
- Clean taste profile ideal for hard seltzers and other neutral spirits
- Applications: whiskey distillate, vinegar, flavor extracts, cosmetics, hand sanitizers, pharmaceuticals, solvents, inks, coatings.

For more details or samples, contact GPC via their website.

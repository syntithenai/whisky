# Log Still Distillery Releases New Rye Whiskey
Log Still Distillery in Gethsemane, Kentucky has launched **Monk’s Road Rye Whiskey** – a farm‑to‑glass rye made from its own Heritage‑grown rye. The spirit earned a Bronze medal at the 2024 International Wine & Spirit Competition and will be sold in multiple states for **$46.99** (100 PROOF). It features bold spice, caramel undertones, oak hints, and a mineral quality from Kentucky limestone water.

> *“Monk’s Road Rye is more than just a whiskey – it’s a reflection of the land we work and the people who make it all possible.”* – Wally Dant, president & distiller.

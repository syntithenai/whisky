# About – Craft Spirits Magazine
Craft Spirits Magazine is a bi‑monthly digital publication run by the American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA). It offers in‑depth analysis, industry news, and resources for small independent spirits producers and allied businesses. The site also hosts videos, podcasts, white papers, and provides advertising and freelance opportunities.

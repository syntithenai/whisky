# Craft Spirits Yearbook 2025
The 2025 *Craft Spirits Yearbook* (PDF) is a comprehensive resource published by the American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA). It covers:
- **Industry data** – sales, volume and value trends for U.S. craft spirits, including a year‑in‑review of market performance.
- **Legislative updates** – recent state and federal actions affecting direct‑to‑consumer shipping, tax incentives, and the new American Single Malt Whiskey designation by TTB.
- **Organizational information** – ACSA leadership, board members, committee chairs, sponsors, and membership criteria.
- **Educational content** – highlights of webinars, workshops and educational initiatives for craft distillers.
- **Member spotlight** – profiles of key distilleries across the U.S. and their contributions to the industry.

The document serves as a yearly snapshot of the craft spirits landscape, offering data, policy updates, and community resources for producers, regulators, and enthusiasts alike.

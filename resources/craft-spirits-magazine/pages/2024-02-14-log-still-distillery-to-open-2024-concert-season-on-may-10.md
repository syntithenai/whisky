# Log Still Distillery Concert Season
Log Still Distillery in Gethsemane, Kentucky is launching its 2024 concert season at **The Amp** (225 Dee Head Road). The venue seats 2,300 and will host live music almost every weekend from May through October. Opening on May 10 with country star **Lee Brice**, the lineup spans country, rock, blues, and folk. The distillery also offers lodging, hiking trails, event spaces, train excursions, and a tasting bar.

> *“We are excited to once again offer a unique experience that combines the best of Kentucky spirits and live music in a setting you can only experience here,”* said co‑founder **Wally Dant**.

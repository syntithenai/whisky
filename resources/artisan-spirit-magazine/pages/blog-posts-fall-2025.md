# Fall 2025 Issue of Artisan Spirit Magazine

The September 3, 2025 edition is a dense collection focused on industry health, brand building, technical guidance and philosophy. It includes:
- Core 4: Foundations for your spirits brand
- Hiring for Impact
- Password security
- Scaling challenges
- Distillation equipment deep dive (column focus)
- Michigan whiskey storyteller Ari Sussman
- National radar for distillery visibility

Featured distilleries: Bardstown Bourbon Company, Garrison Brothers, Gold Bar Whiskey, Greenbar Distillery, La Distillerie de Monaco, Mammoth Distilling, St. Augustine Distillery, Starlight Distillery, University of Kentucky, Virginia Distillery Company, Wyoming Whiskey.

The magazine remains independent, supported by sponsors such as The American Spirits Exchange, Cage & Sons Distilling Systems, CF Napa Brand Design, Distilled Spirits Council of the United States, Haskell, Independent Stave Company, Jack Vogel, Lallemand Biofuels & Distilled Spirits, Moonshine University, Phoenix Packaging, Responsibility.org, Statesville Contract Distilling, Stave & Thief Society, Tapi, Total Wine & More, and George Dickel.

Subscriptions: free for craft distillers, physical copies via Total Wine & More in AZ, CA, NV, TX, WA; digital PDF available.

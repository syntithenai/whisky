**Journal of Distilling Science (JDS)**

- Peer‑reviewed journal focused on the science and technology of distilling high‑quality spirits.
- Publishes original research, technical reports, analytical methods, and reviews covering biology, chemistry, physics, engineering, fermentation, distillation, maturation, and distribution.
- Open to universities, research institutes, distilleries, raw material producers, and allied industries.
- Two issues per year initially (2021), aiming for quarterly.
- Subscription: $120/year; includes digital & print copies of JDS, Artisan Spirit Magazine, and searchable database.
- No submission fees; independent editorial board of >50 scientists.
- Selected articles are free to read; full access requires subscription or individual purchase.

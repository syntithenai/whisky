# Will 2020 Be the Gin Revival Decade?

The article traces gin’s history from prohibition‑era “bathtub gin” to today’s craft revival, noting:
- Prohibition spurred cheap, low‑quality spirits that bartenders masked with mixers.
- Post‑prohibition, gin fell out of favor as vodka and liqueurs rose.
- Recent growth (8.3 % global in 2018) is driven by the farm‑to‑table movement, local distilleries, plant‑based trends, and creative cocktail mixology.
- Small‑batch producers experiment with botanicals, aging in whiskey barrels, and new gin styles (pink gin, strawberry‑rhubarb gin).
- The 2020 New Year’s Eve theme is expected to spotlight classic prohibition cocktails, potentially boosting gin sales further.

The piece concludes that innovation from distillers and bartenders will sustain gin’s momentum.

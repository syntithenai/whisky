# StillDragon Gin Guide (v1.1, Jan 2014)

This PDF is a user‑manual for the *StillDragon Carter Head* gin basket. It covers:
- Overview of the head’s design and modularity.
- Legal & guarantee information.
- Historical background of gin.
- Definitions of gin styles (European regulation, Distilled Gin, London Gin, Jenever, etc.).
- Production techniques: ingredients, maceration, blending methods, and recipe suggestions.
- Practical operation instructions and troubleshooting tips.

The guide is aimed at distillery operators who wish to use the Carter Head for flexible gin production and experimentation.

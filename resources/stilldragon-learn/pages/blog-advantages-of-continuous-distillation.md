# Advantages of Continuous Distillation

StillDragon explains that continuous stills can be run in batch mode and offer significant efficiency gains over traditional batch kettles. Key points include:
- **Heat‑up time**: a 12" continuous column heats up in minutes versus hours for a batch kettle.
- **Production speed**: the same equipment can produce ~256 gallons of finished spirit in an 8‑hour shift, compared to ~178 gallons with a batch system.
- **Labor & utility efficiency**: 45 % higher utility use and 135 % higher labor efficiency.
- **Flexibility**: continuous stills can be sized for current needs and scaled up as the distillery grows.

The article also addresses common myths about cuts, tails management, and the role of dephlegmators in a continuous setup.

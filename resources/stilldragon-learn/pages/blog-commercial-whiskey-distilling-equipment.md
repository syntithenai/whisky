# Commercial Whiskey Distilling Equipment

- **Tank**: Start with a 250‑gallon (1000 L) stainless steel tank for economies of scale.
- **Mash Tun**: Size at least equal to the distillation kettle; converts starch to sugar.
- **Fermenter**: Single‑walled or cooling‑jacketed stainless steel options available.
- **Kettle**: 1000 L (264 gal) working volume; choose copper top for flavor or stainless steel.
- **Still**: Decide between pot still (kettle + swan neck + condenser) and reflux column (copper plates, Lyne arm, dual condensers). More plates = purer alcohol; fewer plates = more flavour.
- **Ancillary Parts**: Proofing parrot with hydrometer, tri‑clamps or high‑pressure clamps, gaskets, push‑connect adapters for water hoses.
- **Barrels/Casks**: Essential for ageing; common types include American oak (spice/vanilla), French oak (wine finish), and others such as sherry, Madeira, rum, beer finishes.

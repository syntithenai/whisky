# Stop Buying the Wrong Vodka Still – Vodka Columns: Single vs Double

StillDragon explains that a commercial vodka column should have **at least 18 plates** to reliably reach the 95 % ABV required by TTB. More plates (up to ~30) give a wider operating range, reduce reflux time, and lower heat/cooling demands. The article cautions against using all‑in‑one or split columns for vodka because they can be slower and harder to manage; instead, purpose‑built single‑column systems are recommended unless space constraints force otherwise.

The post encourages distillers to experiment with plate numbers and offers StillDragon’s consulting services for equipment selection and process optimization.

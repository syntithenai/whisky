# Craft Distillery Insurance Overview

This slide deck from Aaron Linden (CIC) of Hub International covers the essentials of insurance for craft distilleries, including:
- **Insurance fundamentals** – what you know, don’t know, and need to know.
- **Distilled Spirits Bond (DSB)** – purpose, coverage sections, tax liability calculation, types, limits, pricing, and common pitfalls.
- **Coverage options** – tasting rooms, liquor liability, property & equipment, product liability, volunteer labor, stock on hand, automobile, general liability, pollution, cargo, excess, workers’ comp.
- **Product insurance** – replacement cost, market value, agreed value, progressive valuation, hybrid coverage.
- **Practical advice** – choose a knowledgeable agent, avoid “shotgun” approaches, seek references and second opinions, ask hard questions.
- **Contact details** for Aaron Linden.

The presentation emphasizes careful selection of insurance professionals and understanding the specific risks associated with craft distillery operations.

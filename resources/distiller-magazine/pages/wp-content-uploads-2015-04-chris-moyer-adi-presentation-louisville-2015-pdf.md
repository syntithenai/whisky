# Hudson Valley Distillers Business Model Overview

This PDF is a PowerPoint presentation by Chris Moyer (Corporate CPA) and Tom (retired law enforcement) outlining the business model of **Hudson Valley Distillers**. It covers:
- The distillery’s background: farm‑based, family owned, destination site in upstate New York.
- Key financial metrics such as production volume (~90 M bottles/year), capital expenditures ($75 M PPE), and cost per bottle.
- A timeline of milestones from 2013 to 2014 (purchase, permitting, opening, product launch).
- Revenue breakdown by retail vs. wholesale and average transaction sizes for cocktails, food, merchandise, and bottle sales.
- Lessons learned: vendor selection, still design, merchandising, working‑capital needs, and the importance of a tailored business model.

The presentation stresses that a fixed plan is less useful than a flexible business model adapted to the distillery’s scale and market.

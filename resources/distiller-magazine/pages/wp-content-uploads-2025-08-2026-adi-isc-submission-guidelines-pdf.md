# 2026 ADI International Spirits Competition Submission Guidelines

- **Deadlines**: COLA waiver (Feb 20), registration (Mar 6), entries (Mar 13). 
- **Fees**: Early‑bird $325/ADi member, $395 non‑member; standard $375/$445; late $425/$495. Second category always $200.
- **Important dates**: Competition ends Apr 1; embargoed results Apr 27; public awards May 13.
- **Shipping window**: Jan 5 – Mar 13, 2026. Must include ADI shipping label and meet weight/size limits.
- **COLA requirement**: All spirits need a COLA or waiver by Mar 30; international entrants must also submit FDA prior notice and pro‑forma invoice.
- **Packaging**: Use foam/pulp/corrugated shippers, max 40 lb per box, dimensions ≤36×24×24 in. No peanuts/shredded material.
- **Fees for non‑compliance**: $25 repacking for broken packages; $20 for missing label; $25 disposal fee for prohibited packing.

---

## Key Points
1. **Entry fees vary by membership and timing**.
2. **Shipping must follow strict window, labeling, and packaging rules**.
3. **International entrants face additional COLA/Customs paperwork**.
4. **Late or incomplete submissions incur penalties; refunds limited to shipping failures**.

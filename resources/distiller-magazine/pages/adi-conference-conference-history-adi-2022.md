# ADI 2022 – St. Louis

The American Distilling Institute (ADI) hosted its 2022 conference in St. Louis, Missouri from September 14‑16, 2022. The event featured a keynote by Dr. Anne Brock, Master Distiller at Bombay Sapphire, and included educational sessions, workshops, an expanded exhibition floor, and several social events such as a Boozy Breakfast, Happy Hours, Awards Luncheon, Casino Night, and the Gin Summit on September 17.

Key logistics:
- **Venue**: America’s Center St. Louis Convention Center (Marriott Grand St. Louis)
- **Tickets**: Full Conference Pass $550 (members) / $750 (non‑members); Early Bird $400/$600; Gala Luncheon $50; Expo Pass $200
- **Exhibitors**: 195+ vendors, 25 new
- **Health & Safety**: Social distancing measures, QR code check‑in, limited room capacity
- **App**: ADICONF available on Apple/Google Play for schedule and floor map.

The conference also highlighted the growing craft distilling scene in St. Louis and included meet‑ups with the Missouri Craft Distillers Guild, Women Summit, Sustainability Summit, and other industry partners.

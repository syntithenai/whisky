# ADI 2019 Denver

The American Distilling Institute’s 2019 Annual Craft Spirits Conference and Expo was held in Denver, Colorado from March 18‑21. The event drew **1,800 attendees** to a **50,000‑sq‑ft expo floor** featuring **181 exhibitors**, **over 50 educational sessions**, **80+ speakers**, **11 hands‑on workshops**, and a closing party. Highlights included a welcome tasting with the Colorado Guild, a Women’s Summit, and the Spirits Judging Awards ceremony.

## Key Takeaways
- 1 800 industry leaders and newcomers attended.
- 50 000 sq ft of expo space with 181 exhibitors.
- More than 50 educational sessions ranging from beginner to expert.
- 80+ speakers, 11 workshops, and a closing party.
- Denver’s scenic backdrop added to the experience.

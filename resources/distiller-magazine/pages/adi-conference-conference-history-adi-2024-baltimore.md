# ADI 2024 Baltimore Conference
- **Dates:** 8 / 26‑29 2024 (originally scheduled for July 14‑16, 2020)
- **Location:** Baltimore Convention Center & surrounding venues.
- **Key offerings:**
  - *Whiskey Summit* (Bourbon, rye, malt) – $250 non‑conference / $180 conference.
  - *Training Your Tasting Room Staff* – $150 / $125.
  - *Amaro Workshop* at Baltimore Spirits – $250 / $180.
  - *Baltimore Distilleries Tour* (Baltimore Spirits, Old Line, Sagamore) – $250 / $180.
  - *Distillery Membership Happy Hour* – RSVP only.
  - *ADI International Spirits Competition Awards Luncheon*.
  - *US State Guild Legislative Meeting* – by invitation.
  - *Closing Event at the National Aquarium*.
- **Special sessions:** Distillery Design & Expansion Bootcamp, Rye Whiskey Workshop, Gin Summit, Frederick Distilleries Tour.
- **Additional resources:** Newsletter signup, ADI conference app, vendor booths, media kit.

The page lists event schedules, pricing, and contact details for attendees and non‑conference participants.

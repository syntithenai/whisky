# ADI 2005 Louisville

The American Distilling Institute (ADI) held its 2005 conference in Louisville, Kentucky from **April 17‑19, 2005**. The event was part of ADI’s annual conference series that brings together distillers, suppliers and industry professionals to discuss craft distilling, technology, regulations and business practices.

The page also highlights two notable speakers who were present at the 2005 meeting:
- **Scott Allen**, Managing Director of Allen Associates (HPE) Ltd, a chemical‑process engineering consultancy that serves the distilled spirits sector.
- **Henric Molin**, a chemist‑turned‑flavourist and co‑founder of Backafallsbyn/Spirit of Hven, who provides flavour‑development services to global spirits brands.

# Distilled Spirits Plant (DSP) Permit Application Guide

This PDF is a PowerPoint presentation from the American Distilling Institute’s 2015 Craft Spirits Conference, summarizing how to apply for a TTB DSP permit via the **Permits Online** system. It covers:
- Pre‑application requirements (building security, bond, production capacity)
- The online workflow: self‑registration, step‑by‑step application, document upload, status tracking
- Owner/Officer Information (OOI) rules for stakeholders holding 10 % or more
- Premises description and cash bond payment instructions
- Common errors to avoid
- Restrictions on DSP location relative to wineries, breweries, retail, and other businesses
- Key TTB resources and contacts

The presentation is a practical reference for distillery operators preparing their applications.

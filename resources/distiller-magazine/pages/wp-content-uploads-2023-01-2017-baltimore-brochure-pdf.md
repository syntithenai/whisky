# 2017 Baltimore Craft Spirits Conference

- **Dates**: April 3‑4, 2017 (Monday–Tuesday) in Baltimore, MD.
- **Venue**: American Distilling Institute (ADI) conference hall and Expo floor.
- **Keynotes & Sessions**: Topics ranged from packaging design, marketing, IP law, chemistry & engineering, business strategy, regulatory compliance, sustainability, and technical updates on distillation equipment.
- **Notable Speakers**: Andrew Faulkner (ADI VP), Karen Hoskin (Montanya Distillers), Michael Kinstlick (Coppersea Distilling), Scott Allen (Allen Associates), Elliott Davis (Mine Hill Distillery), Scott Winters (American Spirits Exchange), Yvette Glenn (TTB Chemist), and many others.
- **Expo Highlights**: Product showcases, auction, networking events, and a gala dinner with ADI Spirits Judging Awards.

The program was structured into morning keynote sessions, afternoon breakout panels, evening social events, and an Expo floor open to the public.

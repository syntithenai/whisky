# ADI Social Media Presentation (April 1, 2015)

This PDF is a slide deck from Hinman & Carmichael LLP presented at the 2015 ADI Conference. It covers:
- How to define and promote a brand on social media.
- The federal and state legal landscape for alcohol advertising, including TTB guidance and state‑specific rules.
- Common pitfalls (“traps”) such as retailer advertising, posts by others or employees, and unlicensed third‑party advertisers.
- Practical compliance steps: mandatory statements, age gating, responsible content, privacy policies, and partner education.

The deck is heavily focused on legal compliance rather than product details.

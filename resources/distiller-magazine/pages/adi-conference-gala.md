# Gala – American Distilling Institute

The **Gala** is a formal recognition event held on Monday, August 25, 2025 at the San Antonio Convention Center. It features:
- A sit‑down lunch for all attendees (the expo floor closes during the gala).
- The *Richard Wolf Memorial Award* presented at 12:00 AM.
- The *International Distillery of the Year & U.S. Distillery of the Year* award presented by Erik Owens, ADI President, at 12:10 AM.
- The *ADI ISC Best in Class Awards* presented by Eric Zandona, Spirit Competition Director, at 12:30 AM.

The page also includes navigation links to other ADI conference resources and a brief note that the 2020 conference was rescheduled to July 14‑16. No product pricing or detailed reviews are provided on this page.

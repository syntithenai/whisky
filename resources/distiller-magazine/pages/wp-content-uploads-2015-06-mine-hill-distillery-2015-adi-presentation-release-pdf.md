# Mine Hill Distillery – 2015 ADI Presentation

- **Purpose**: Guide for starting a craft distillery, presented at the 2015 Spirits Conference.
- **Structure**: Six‑part Rubrik’s Cube covering Location, Production, Investors, Owners, Financials and Change.
- **Key Themes**:
  - Importance of a solid business plan.
  - Balancing automation with artisanal quality.
  - Tax incentives (depreciation, energy credits) and security interests for investors.
  - Milestone timeline from pre‑launch to year 5 production targets.
  - Case studies of recent exits in the craft spirits sector.
- **Practical Tools**: Sample capital call schedule, equipment cost breakdown, and a phased investment strategy.

---

## Summary Text
The PDF is a comprehensive guide for entrepreneurs launching a craft distillery. It outlines a six‑step framework—Location, Production, Investors, Owners, Financials, Change—emphasizing the need for a robust business plan, strategic automation, and tax‑benefit structuring. The document includes financial tables (equipment, working capital, property), milestone timelines, and examples of successful exits in the industry.

---

## Distillery Facts
- Mine Hill Distillery is located in Roxbury, Connecticut.
- Founded 2 years prior to the presentation.
- Uses custom‑made artisanal stills from Kentucky and Germany.
- Production can be fully automated for consistency and scalability.
- Target production: 1,000 cases p.a. (Year 1–2), 3,000 cases p.a. (Year 3–4), 5,000 cases p.a. (Year 5).

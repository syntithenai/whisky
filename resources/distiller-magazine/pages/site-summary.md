## Long Description
Distilling.com is the online hub of the American Distilling Institute (ADI), the world’s largest association of small‑batch, independently owned craft distilleries. The site hosts ADI’s annual conference calendar, including Miami Beach 2026, and provides a comprehensive directory of member distilleries, buyer’s guides, distributor listings, awards, certifications and data tools. It also offers educational content such as courses on regulatory compliance, flavor chemistry, and business fundamentals, alongside practical resources like TTB reporting guides, chemical analysis tutorials, and marketing best‑practice presentations.

The resource serves a broad audience: craft distillers looking to launch or grow their operations, industry professionals seeking market data, regulators needing up‑to‑date compliance information, and students of spirits science. Its content ranges from high‑level conference programs to detailed PDF handouts on topics such as solera blending, UV‑Vis spectroscopy for methanol detection, and rapid aging techniques.

Key features include a searchable distillery directory, downloadable whitepapers, podcasts, and an active blog covering regulatory updates, flavor chemistry, and business strategy. The site also hosts the ADI’s annual awards program and provides access to industry data sets that track market trends and production volumes.

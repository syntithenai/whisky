# ADI 2020 Virtual Conference

The American Distilling Institute (ADI) hosted its 2020 conference online from **July 14‑16, 2020** under a *pay‑what‑you‑can* model. All registration fees were donated to the Distilling Research Guild, a 501(c)(3) supporting scientific research in distillation. The event featured virtual trade show booths, breakout sessions, and private video chats between attendees and exhibitors. Speakers could be accessed via the “SPEAKERS” tab, and the full schedule was available on the side of the page.

Key highlights:
- **No travel required** – participants joined from home or office.
- **Post‑event app** for continued learning and networking.
- **Sponsors**: Title sponsors, platinum sponsors, and a list of supporting partners.
- **Speaker profiles** included industry experts such as Scott Allen (chemical & process engineering) and Henric Molin (flavourist/chemist).

The page also contains standard ADI navigation links, newsletter signup, and legal notices.

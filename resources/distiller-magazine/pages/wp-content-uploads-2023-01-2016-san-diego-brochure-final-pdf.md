# 2016 San Diego Distillery Conference

A full‑day, multi‑venue event held at the California Guild in San Diego (April 2–5, 2016) featuring workshops, panels and tours for craft distillers. Highlights include:
- **Keynote & Industry State** – Yussef Cherney (Ballast Point), Michael Kinslick (Coppersea Distilling)
- **Technical Sessions** – glass & closure selection, fermentation, gin, rye whiskey, and off‑flavor troubleshooting
- **Business & Marketing** – major account management, permitting & TTB compliance, financing, insurance, distribution strategies, branding & packaging trends
- **Hands‑on Workshops** – distillery tours, 3‑day whiskey workshop (Old Harbor), and specialized classes on medicinal mixology, automation, beer‑spirit integration, and organic ingredient sourcing
- **Networking & Expo** – exhibitors in the Grand Hall, Golden West Ballroom, and Sunset venues

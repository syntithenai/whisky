# ADI 2016 San Diego Conference

The American Distilling Institute’s 2016 conference in San Diego featured a full‑day program of talks, panels and workshops covering every aspect of craft distilling—from regulatory compliance and financial planning to product development, marketing, and science. The schedule lists speakers from industry associations, law firms, universities, and leading craft distilleries.

Key highlights:
- **Opening & Keynotes** – Andrew Faulkner (ADI VP) opened the event; Yussef Cherney (COO, Ballast Point) delivered the keynote.
- **Regulatory & Compliance Sessions** – Topics included TTB reporting, mandatory labeling, and class‑action litigation.
- **Business & Marketing Panels** – Discussions on retail success, distributor relationships, social media strategy, and international expansion.
- **Technical Workshops** – Practical sessions on mash optimization, still design, barrel chemistry, and filtration.
- **Product Development Talks** – Presentations on aging techniques, botanicals for gin, high‑proof spirits, and the science of flavor.

The conference also provided downloadable PDFs of each presentation (linked in the page) and a brochure outlining the full agenda.

# ADI 2025 International Spirits Competition Awards
The American Distilling Institute (ADI) announced the winners of its 2025 International Spirits Competition. The awards span over 30 categories, including *America’s Greatest* titles for gin, rum, vodka, whiskey, and agave spirits, as well as *Best of Class*, *International* and *Sustainability* honors.

Key highlights:
- **Griffo Distillery** won *America’s Greatest London Dry Gin 2025* (Scott Street Gin) and *Best Overseas Martini*.
- **End of Days Distillery** took *Best of Class – Distilled Spirits* with Castillo 1815 Blanco.
- **Republica del Cocuy** claimed *America’s Greatest Agave Spirit 2025* for Verdor Cocuy Claro.
- **Silver Star Spirits** earned *Devil’s Grin Gin – America’s Greatest Gin 2025*.
- **Stade’s Rum** received *America’s Greatest Rum 2025*.
- **Ham’s** was awarded *America’s Greatest Vodka 2025* for Original Vodka.
- **Kilchoman Distillery** won *America’s Greatest Whiskey 2025* (Batch Strength Single Malt Scotch Whisky).
- **Cash Butte Distillery** was named *North American Sustainable Distillery of the Year 2025*.

The full list contains 819 entries across categories such as gin, rum, tequila, brandy, liqueur, ready‑to‑drink, and specialty spirits. Winners are listed by company name, distillery, spirit, award, production group, class, category, region, and country.

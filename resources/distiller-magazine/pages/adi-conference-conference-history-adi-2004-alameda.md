# ADI 2004 Alameda

The American Distilling Institute (ADI) held its first Pot‑Still USA Conference on **February 27–28, 2004** at the St. George Distillery in Alameda, California. The event was a success and is highlighted in *Distiller Magazine* (American Distiller #15, March 2004). The page also notes that the 2020 conference was rescheduled to July 14‑16.

The page includes brief biographies of two ADI speakers:
- **Scott Allen** – Managing Director at Allen Associates (HPE) Ltd, a chemical and process engineering consultancy serving the spirits industry.
- **Henric Molin** – Chemist/flavourist and co‑founder of Spirit of Hven, who consults globally on spirit creation and flavour science.

# American Distilling Institute – Craft Spirits Conference & Expo (March 31‑April 1, 2023)

The program is a two‑day event in Louisville featuring keynote speeches, breakout sessions, panels and evening social events. Highlights include:
- **Keynotes** by Andrew Faulkner (ADI VP), Eric Gregory (Kentucky Distiller Association President) and Michael Kinstlick (Coppersea CEO).
- **Breakout tracks** on topics such as cocktail branding, grain‑to‑bottle process, regulatory compliance for multi‑state sales, crowdfunding, fault detection in spirits, distillery design, quality control, distribution, co‑product value, legal foundations, packaging, and financial planning.
- **Special sessions** on class‑action litigation risk, chill haze removal, ethanol yield optimization, TTB audit expectations, branding science, gin botanicals, fermentation fundamentals, retail strategy, and wastewater treatment.
- **Evening events** include a gala dinner with live auction, craft spirits judging, and a “Spirited” glass‑art showcase.

The schedule is organized by room numbers and times; each session lists the presenter(s) and a brief description of the content.

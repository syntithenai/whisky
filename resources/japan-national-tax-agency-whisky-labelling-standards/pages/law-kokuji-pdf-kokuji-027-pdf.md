# Japan National Tax Agency – Personal Number (My Number) Regulations

The PDF contains the *Japanese* regulations that govern how a person’s **personal number** (My Number) may be used in relation to tax‑related documents and procedures. The text explains:

1. **Scope of application** – which tax documents and administrative acts require or allow the use of a personal number.
2. **Eligibility criteria** – conditions under which an individual’s personal number can be used, including the requirement that the person is a *real* (not a proxy) taxpayer.
3. **Procedural requirements** – how to correctly record, print and verify the personal number on tax forms, certificates of income, etc., and what information must accompany it (e.g., name, address, date of birth).
4. **Special provisions** – rules for handling cases where a proxy or representative is involved, as well as the handling of “special” documents such as those issued by public institutions.

This PDF is a statistical report from the Japanese Ministry of Finance that details the **taxation and filing status for whisky** in fiscal year 2016. It contains tables on:

## Implications for Whisky Producers
* **Increased e‑Tax adoption**: 35% of filings were electronic, indicating a shift toward digital compliance.
* **Higher tax payments by corporate distillers**: Corporate entities paid significantly more tax than individuals or other producers.

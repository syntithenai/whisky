# American Craft Spirits Association

The American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA) is a trade group dedicated to advocating for and supporting craft spirits producers in the United States. The site serves as a hub for membership information, industry news, events, educational resources, advocacy efforts, and sponsorship listings.

Key highlights:
- **Membership**: Offers various membership tiers including voting members, affiliate members, and alumni.
- **Events**: Announces annual convention & expo dates (Dec 8 2025 – Apr 8 2026) and other competitions such as the Heartland Whiskey Competition.
- **News & Advocacy**: Publishes press releases on leadership changes, policy updates, and industry developments.
- **Resources**: Provides guides like *The AC‑SA Guide to Starting a Distillery*, webinars, and a data project.
- **Sponsors**: Lists corporate sponsors ranging from equipment suppliers to legal firms.

The site is primarily informational; no product pricing or direct sales are featured on the homepage.

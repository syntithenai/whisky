# Craft Spirits Data Project
The American Craft Spirits Association (ACSA) launched the **Craft Spirits Data Project (CSDP)** in 2016 to track economic trends in the craft spirits industry. Partnering with Park Street, a global import and financial solutions firm, CSDP provides annual reports that link the sector to job creation, agricultural growth, and tourism. The project’s findings are cited as the most influential economic study ACSA has produced.

Key resources include:
- Annual **CSDP Reports** (2016‑2025)
- Webinar library on data analysis
- Guides for starting a distillery
- CRAFT SPIRITS magazine

The page lists links to PDF reports for each year and highlights the 2025 report as the latest.

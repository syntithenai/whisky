# Bourbon 101 – Podcast Series

Bourbon Pursuit’s *Bourbon 101* is a podcast series that explores bourbon history, culture, and the secondary market through interviews with industry experts, authors, and brand ambassadors. Episodes are grouped by topic and include:
- **Bernie Lubbers** (Heaven Hill) discussing badges, bottled‑in‑bond, and flavor profiles.
- **Reid Mitenbuler** (author of *Bourbon Empire*) on bourbon’s history and myths.
- **Erik of BourbonSeason** on bottle chasing and secondary markets.

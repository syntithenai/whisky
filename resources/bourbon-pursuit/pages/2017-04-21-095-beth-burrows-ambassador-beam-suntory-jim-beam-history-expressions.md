# Beam Bourbon History & Expressions

- **Host**: Beth Burrows, Kentucky Bourbon Ambassador for Beam Suntory.
- **Content**: Overview of Jim Beam’s history, its flavored lines (Apple & Honey, Black Cherry, Fire, Maple), and newer expressions such as White Label, Black, Double Oak, Devil’s Cut, Rye, Bonded, Single Barrel. Discusses the impact of Senate Bill 11 on cocktail service.
- **Special Topics**: Naming origin of Baker’s Bourbon; key distiller names behind Knob Creek, Baker, Basil Hayden, OGD, Old Crow, Booker’s View.
